11:52 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in an event on the occasion of the Day of Ukrainian Statehood held on Mykhailivska Square in Kyiv. Today, I had the honor to congratulate doctors and nurses, frontline medics on their professional holiday; I am grateful to everyone on whom the life of Ukraine depends - address by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy 27 July 2023 - 22:54 An important conversation also took place in the presence of the Minister of Health with Commander of the Medical Forces of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Tetiana Ostashchenko. I expect the commander to work together with the community of combat medics, with volunteers. In Odesa, President viewed the damage to Spaso-Preobrazhensky Cathedral caused by Russian terrorist shelling 27 July 2023 - 22:04 The Head of State was told about the extent of the destruction of the church and its current condition. The President was informed that experts are currently working to assess the possibility of restoring the building. In Odesa, the Head of State visited rehabilitation departments where defenders of Ukraine are undergoing rehabilitation treatment 27 July 2023 - 21:27 Volodymyr Zelenskyy talked to the defenders who are undergoing rehabilitation in the institution, and wished them health and a speedy recovery. Ihor Zhovkva met with Deputy Secretary-General of the European External Action Service 27 July 2023 - 20:00 During the meeting, the parties discussed key issues on the agenda of cooperation between Ukraine and the EU, in particular, they reviewed Ukraine's further steps towards European integration and noted the progress made by our country in implementing seven recommendations of the European Commission. On the Day of Medical Worker, Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited hospitals in Mykolaiv region 27 July 2023 - 18:29 Volodymyr Zelenskyy talked to medical workers and congratulated them on their professional holiday. Ihor Zhovkva met with the Ambassador of India to Ukraine 27 July 2023 - 17:26 The interlocutors exchanged views on preparations for the next meeting at the level of national security advisors on the implementation of the Ukrainian Peace Formula, as well as on the organization of the Global Peace Summit. President congratulated military medics who save lives of Ukrainian defenders in Dnipropetrovsk region 27 July 2023 - 11:08 During a working trip to Dnipropetrovsk region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited a medical facility where Ukrainian defenders are undergoing treatment. Congratulations of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy on the Day of Medical Workers of Ukraine 27 July 2023 - 09:01 I thank you, dear Ukrainian medics, for your professionalism, for your service, and for every life you have saved. For all these infinite worlds that are seen in the eyes of children, adults, soldiers, and civilians - all those whom you have saved.
