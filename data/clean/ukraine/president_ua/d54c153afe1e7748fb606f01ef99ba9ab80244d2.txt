20:57 At their meeting in Tirana on 28 February 2024, the President of Ukraine and the Prime Minister of the Republic of North Macedonia adopted the following declaration. In Tirana, the President of Ukraine and the Prime Minister of North Macedonia signed the Joint Declaration on Euro-Atlantic integration of Ukraine 28 February 2024 - 20:47 The document reaffirms North Macedonia's support for Ukraine's Euro-Atlantic aspirations and invitation to join NATO when the conditions are met. In the framework of the Ukraine – South East Europe Summit, Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with Aleksandar Vučić 28 February 2024 - 20:20 The President expressed gratitude for the humanitarian and financial assistance, as well as for providing shelter to Ukrainian citizens who fled to Serbia due to Russian aggression. President of Ukraine met with President of Moldova in Tirana 28 February 2024 - 19:43 The Heads of State discussed recent events in the Transnistrian region of Moldova, Russia's attempts to destabilize the situation there and effective ways to counteract the influence of the aggressor country. Head of State met with the President of North Macedonia 28 February 2024 - 19:31 The Head of State praised North Macedonia's support for Ukraine at the political level. Volodymyr Zelenskyy thanked the interlocutor for a clear position on the future membership of our country in NATO, which was confirmed in the Joint Declaration on Ukraine's Euro-Atlantic Integration. President of Ukraine met with the President of Montenegro in Tirana 28 February 2024 - 18:27 Volodymyr Zelenskyy spoke about the overall architecture of Ukraine's efforts to restore a just and lasting peace. Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with Andrej Plenković in Tirana 28 February 2024 - 18:18 On the sidelines of the Ukraine – South East Europe Summit in Tirana, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with Prime Minister of the Republic of Croatia Andrej Plenković. President: Ukraine will support all initiatives that will strengthen its ability to fight the aggressor 28 February 2024 - 17:54 Ukraine will welcome the initiatives of world leaders aimed at strengthening the Ukrainian army on the battlefield and the country's resilience in the fight against the aggressor – Russia. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a conversation with media representatives following the Ukraine – South East Europe Summit in Tirana. President following the Ukraine – South East Europe Summit in Tirana: Everyone in Europe must be ready to defend their sovereignty, and Ukraine offers joint work, co-production in the sphere of defense 28 February 2024 - 16:45 The Head of State also emphasized that every nation that aspires to be a part of the European and Euro-Atlantic communities and shares common European and Euro-Atlantic values deserves full integration into the respective alliances.
