Andriy Yermak had a phone call with Jake Sullivan 11 March 2024 - 23:29 The interlocutors discussed the situation on the battlefield and the urgent needs of Ukrainian defenders. In particular, Andriy Yermak emphasized the importance of the U.S. House of Representatives approving an aid package for Ukraine as soon as possible, as it is badly needed by Ukrainian forces. Andriy Yermak met with Sanna Marin 6 March 2024 - 12:48 Andriy Yermak thanked Sanna Marin for her efforts to support Ukraine, in particular within the International Working Group and the Tony Blair Institute for Global Change, with a delegation of which she arrived on a visit to Ukraine.
