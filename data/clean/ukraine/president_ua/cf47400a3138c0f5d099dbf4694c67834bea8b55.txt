implementation of Ukrainian Peace Formula with Ambassador of India 1 September 2023 - 19:55 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak held a meeting with Ambassador of India to Ukraine Harsh Kumar Jain. At the meeting, Yermak thanked the Indian side for participating in the consultations regarding the implementation of the Ukrainian Peace Formula. "It is very important for us to continue this open dialogue, to know the opinions and understand the position of all our friends and partners," the head of the President's Office said. The interlocutors emphasized mutual interest in deepening bilateral cooperation between Ukraine and India. They also exchanged views on the eve of important international events, in particular in the context of the opening of a new session of the UN General Assembly.
