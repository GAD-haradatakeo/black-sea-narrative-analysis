19 September 2023 - 02:19 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Head of President's Office had a telephone conversation with US President's national security advisor 2 September 2023 - 08:41 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak, as part of the continuous dialogue with the allies, had a telephone conversation with National Security Advisor to the President of the United States Jake Sullivan. Andriy Yermak briefed the interlocutor on the situation on the battlefield, the defense forces' successful advance in certain areas of the front, and also pointed to the needs of the Ukrainian army to continue liberation of the territories of our country from the Russian invaders. The head of the Office of the President raised the notion of creating working groups to develop proposals for the implementation of each of the 10 points of the Peace Formula. In addition, Andriy Yermak emphasized the importance of continuing the work on signing bilateral agreements on security commitments with Ukraine within the framework of the G7 Joint Declaration of support for our state. The head of Ukraine's Presidential Office thanked US President Joseph Biden, Congress and the entire American people for their unwavering leadership in defense, political, economic and humanitarian support for our state in defending freedom and common democratic values.
