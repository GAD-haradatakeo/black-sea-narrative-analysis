19:55 Go to all galleries Home News Current events We Must Act Together to Fully Implement the Peace Formula – Volodymyr Zelenskyy Addressed the Participants and Guests of the Prayer Breakfast 29 June 2024 - 14:43 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in the National Prayer Breakfast, held for the first time under the patronage of the Head of State. The event was attended by representatives of all denominations and churches, as well as the Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada, Ruslan Stefanchuk, and the Prime Minister, Denys Shmyhal. Volodymyr Zelenskyy thanked the participants and guests of the Prayer Breakfast for their support, for praying for Ukraine, for spreading the truth about the war, and for uniting for the sake of our country and common human values. "Our Prayer Breakfast is attended by representatives of different countries and different communities. But there is one thing we are all undoubtedly united in, and that is our desire to preserve the brightest side of people and our country – to withstand this terrible time of war and to ensure for Ukraine a real, tangible and, from the perspective of international security, exemplary victory of good over evil, so that other aggressors will not attack their neighbors," the Head of State emphasized. The President urged all those present to fight to restore normal life and security in all Ukrainian cities and communities and to act together to fully implement the Peace Formula. "We have proved more than once that there are no impossible things – when you really believe in your strength, when you really know that human and God's truth is on your side, when you act, and when you do everything to keep people united. And I believe that we can bring peace back to Ukraine," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said. The attendees prayed for Ukrainian warriors and all our people, for the unification of the world against evil, for victory, peace and recovery of Ukraine. Prayer Breakfasts are held all over the world. In Ukraine, they have been held for 20 years in 90 cities and communities.
