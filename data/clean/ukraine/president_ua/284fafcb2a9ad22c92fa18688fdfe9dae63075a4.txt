Head of State met with the delegation of the global parliamentary network "United for Ukraine" 23 February 2023 - 20:39 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with a delegation of the global parliamentary network United for Ukraine, which included representatives of the parliaments of 13 European countries and the European Parliament. Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Pedro Sánchez unveiled a plaque dedicated to the Prime Minister of Spain on the Walk of the Brave in Kyiv 23 February 2023 - 18:38 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Spain Pedro Sánchez visited the Walk of the Brave on Constitution Square in Kyiv and took part in the unveiling of a plaque in honor of the head of the Spanish government. The more countries are involved in the implementation of the Peace Formula, the sooner the war in Ukraine will end - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 23 February 2023 - 18:26 Ukraine is interested in the support of our Peace Formula by as many countries as possible, as this will help to end the unjust war unleashed by Russia. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a conversation with media representatives following a meeting with Prime Minister of Spain Pedro Sánchez in Kyiv. Launch of negotiations on Ukraine's accession to the EU could become a historic achievement of Spain's Presidency of the Council of the European Union - Volodymyr Zelenskyy following his meeting with Pedro Sánchez in Kyiv 23 February 2023 - 17:55 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Spain Pedro Sánchez, who arrived in our country on a visit. Air defense equipment provided by Italy is what really saves the lives of our people - Volodymyr Zelenskyy following talks with Giorgia Meloni in Kyiv 21 February 2023 - 22:23 "Since the beginning of Russia's full-scale invasion, we have been receiving principled assistance and support from Italy at all levels. Both defense and political, humanitarian, economic, and support for our energy system. Rome played a significant role in resolving the issue of Ukraine's EU candidate status. We are grateful to Italy for this," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said. Joint Declaration of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and President of the Council of Ministers of the Italian Republic Giorgia Meloni 21 February 2023 - 20:58 The President of Ukraine and the President of the Council of Ministers of the Italian Republic note the readiness to remain fully committed to the policy of non-recognition of illegal annexation of Ukraine’s territories by Russia.
