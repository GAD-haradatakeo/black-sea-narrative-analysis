18:47 During a working visit of the President of Ukraine to the Republic of Turkey, First Lady Olena Zelenska launched two new Ukrainian-language audio guides in Turkey - at the Galata Tower in Istanbul and at the Museum of Anatolian Civilizations in Ankara. The launch ceremony was attended by First Lady of Turkey Emine Erdoğan. The ninth meeting of the High-Level Strategic Council between Ukraine and Turkey will be held under the co-chairmanship of the Presidents of the two states. Government representatives must support Ukrainian warriors on the frontline - President 8 April 2021 - 20:27 Government representatives must regularly visit the frontline positions of the Armed Forces of Ukraine in the Donetsk and Luhansk regions to support our defenders, especially when there is an aggravation on the touch line, said President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a working trip to Donbas.
