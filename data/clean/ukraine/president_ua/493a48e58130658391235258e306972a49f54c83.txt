President of Ukraine: We understand all the challenges for Kharkiv region and are working on strengthening its defense 1 March 2024 - 19:13 Volodymyr Zelenskyy said that the partners are well aware of what Ukraine lacks to be even stronger. This applies not only to Kharkiv but also to populated areas on the border near the front line. Ukraine and the Netherlands together defend our people, our values, and together we must win, defending them – Volodymyr Zelenskyy after meeting with Mark Rutte in Kharkiv 1 March 2024 - 18:58 Ukraine and the Netherlands have concluded a bilateral Agreement on Security Cooperation, which formalizes the full spectrum of interaction between the two countries for the next 10 years. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy following talks with Prime Minister Mark Rutte of the Kingdom of the Netherlands in Kharkiv. Volodymyr Zelenskyy, Mark Rutte visited Ukrainian defenders at a military hospital in Kharkiv 1 March 2024 - 18:47 During their trip to Kharkiv, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Dutch Prime Minister Mark Rutte visited a clinical center where Ukrainian servicemen wounded in battles in Kharkiv region are undergoing treatment. Volodymyr Zelenskyy, Mark Rutte in Kharkiv met with Ukrainian investigators, experts involved in investigation into shooting down Malaysia Airlines flight MH17 1 March 2024 - 17:59 Rescuers and investigators briefed Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Mark Rutte on the facilitation of the work of international experts in examining and transporting the bodies of the deceased. They said that this work was complicated by the actions of Russia-backed fighters. Agreement on security cooperation between Ukraine and the Netherlands 1 March 2024 - 17:07 The Participants have decided to continue their cooperation to assist Ukraine in defending itself against Russian aggression for as long as it takes. Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Mark Rutte visited the metro school in Kharkiv 1 March 2024 - 16:49 The leaders attended math, Ukrainian and English classes and talked to the children. They asked about their favorite classes, hobbies and dreams and wished the children success. President of Ukraine and the Prime Minister of the Netherlands saw the buildings damaged by Russian shelling of Kharkiv and honored the memory of children killed by Russian aggression 1 March 2024 - 16:38 Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Mark Rutte saw the building of the Kharkiv Regional State Administration on Sumska Street, which was heavily damaged by Russian shelling on March 1, 2022. Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Mark Rutte met in Kharkiv and signed an agreement on security cooperation between Ukraine and the Netherlands 1 March 2024 - 16:30 In Kharkiv, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Prime Minister of the Netherlands Mark Rutte held negotiations in narrow, expanded formats and in the format of a military cabinet meeting. They also signed an agreement on security cooperation between Ukraine and the Netherlands.
