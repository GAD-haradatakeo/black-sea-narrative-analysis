21:00 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy demands that the Prosecutor General of Ukraine and the Minister of Internal Affairs ensure effective coordination of work to prevent the escalation of violence on the streets of the country. The Head of State stressed that the disturbance of public peace, illicit trafficking and use of weapons, and numerous attacks on citizens, in particular with the use of violence, were a matter of serious concern. "I demand that the Prosecutor General and the Minister of Internal Affairs ensure systematic and coordinated work aimed at preventing the escalation of violence on the streets of the country, take clear and effective preventive measures to ensure a peaceful life of Ukrainian citizens," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said.
