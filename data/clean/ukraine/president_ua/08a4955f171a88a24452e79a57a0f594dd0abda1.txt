- 14:35 Go to all galleries Home News Current events President visited the frontline positions of the military in the Donetsk region 17 February 2022 - 15:28 During a working trip to the Donetsk region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited the positions of Ukrainian servicemen on the front line of defense, where intense shelling continues. The Head of State observed the samples of modern weapons and military equipment transferred to the units of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, including FGM-148 Javelin and NLAW portable anti-tank missile systems, as well as M141 grenade launcher. Volodymyr Zelenskyy also observed the frontline positions and got himself acquainted with the operational and tactical situation in the area of responsibility of the Armed Forces of Ukraine. The President spoke with the servicemen and presented gifts to them. "I am proud of your heroism. You defend Ukraine's independence and decently rebuff the enemy. We feel constant pride in our defenders," he said.
