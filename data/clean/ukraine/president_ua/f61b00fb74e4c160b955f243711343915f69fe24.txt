Those Who Survived Were Robbed of Decades after 1944 – President of Ukraine on the Remembrance Day of the Genocide of the Crimean Tatar People 18 May 2024 - 19:41 The President of Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, took part in the unveiling of a memorial stone, which will be replaced by a Memorial to the Victims of the Crimean Tatar Genocide. Today, the world commemorates the 80th anniversary of the deportation from the Crimean Peninsula organized by the Soviet authorities. A Consolidation of Countries from All the Continents at the Global Peace Summit Will Show Who Really Wants to End the War – the President of Ukraine in an Interview for AFP 18 May 2024 - 14:51 Ukraine is ready to engage in dialogue with all the states that support its territorial integrity. That is why the President and his team are actively working to involve representatives of the Global South in the Summit. After all, nuclear security is very important for China, while food security is important for Africa and Asia, and prisoner exchange is important for the Arab world. Ukraine Has Done Everything Necessary to Launch Negotiations on Accession to the EU – President at the Meeting with Journalists 17 May 2024 - 12:54 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with media representatives before the Global Peace Summit in Switzerland, the start of EU accession negotiations and the NATO Summit. He spoke about Ukraine's steps to restore a just peace and his expectations from these international events. In Kharkiv, Volodymyr Zelenskyy Presented State Awards to Warriors of the 92nd Separate Assault Brigade Named after Kish Ataman Ivan Sirko 16 May 2024 - 15:10 The Head of State also heard a report by Brigade Commander Pavlo Fedosenko on the operational situation in their area of responsibility. In Kharkiv, the President Visited the Hospital Where the Wounded Soldiers are Treated and Thanked Them for Defending Ukraine 16 May 2024 - 14:07 The President awarded the servicemen with the Orders of Bohdan Khmelnytsky, III class, the Orders "For Courage", III class, and the Medals "For Military Service to Ukraine". Operational Situation, Tasks and Needs of Each Unit: The President Held a Meeting of the Staff in Kharkiv 16 May 2024 - 12:44 The President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a meeting of the Staff of the Supreme Commander-in-Chief in Kharkiv. Georgia, Liechtenstein, and Malawi will Participate in the Global Peace Summit 15 May 2024 - 19:36 The President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held telephone conversations with the President of Georgia Salome Zourabichvili, the President of Malawi Lazarus Chakwera, and the Prime Minister of Liechtenstein Daniel Risch, he also thanked them for confirming their participation in the Global Peace Summit. Housing for IDPs and Restoration of Affected Communities: The President Met with American Philanthropist Dell Loy Hansen 15 May 2024 - 19:16 The Head of State emphasized that Ukraine greatly appreciates the people who have been helping our country since the first days of full-scale Russian aggression.
