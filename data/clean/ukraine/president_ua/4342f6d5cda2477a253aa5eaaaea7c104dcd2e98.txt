President of Ukraine: Justice reform and anticorruption reform will soon be accelerated 18 October 2021 - 21:50 President Volodymyr Zelenskyy is convinced that in the near future the anticorruption bodies of Ukraine will resume their work in full, as well as the implementation of judicial reform will be accelerated and the Constitutional Court will be rebooted. The President said this in an interview for ICTV in the framework of the Freedom of Speech program. There is enough gas in Ukraine today to provide consumers with at a stable price - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 18 October 2021 - 21:39 "I want to reassure everyone. I believe that the authorities have taken many important, substantive and timely actions. Today there is enough gas for society, enough gas for consumers. Despite the gas shortage, which arose for obvious reasons in Europe, we have not just enough gas, but enough gas at normal prices, compared to European ones," said the President.
