New Defense Package and Strengthening Ukraine's Air Defense: Volodymyr Zelenskyy Met with Joseph Biden 11 July 2024 - 22:32 As part of the 75th NATO Summit in Washington, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with US President Joseph Biden.
