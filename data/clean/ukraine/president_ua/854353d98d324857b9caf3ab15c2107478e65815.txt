Jacob Sullivan and Mark Milley 1 August 2022 - 16:49 As part of the continued dialogue, Head of the President's Office Andriy Yermak, Minister of Foreign Affairs Dmytro Kuleba and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine General Valerii Zaluzhny had a phone conversation with US Secretary of State Antony Blinken, US President's National Security Advisor Jacob Sullivan and US Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff General Mark Milley. The representatives of Ukraine informed the American side of the operational situation and the needs of our army. Andriy Yermak, Dmytro Kuleba, and Valerii Zaluzhny noted a considerable contribution of the United States to the strengthening of Ukraine’s defense capability. Andriy Yermak and Jacob Sullivan agreed on the need to organize a conversation between the leaders of Ukraine and the United States - Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Joseph Biden - in the near future.
