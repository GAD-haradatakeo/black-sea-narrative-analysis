- 13:02 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak Discussed Support for the Peace Summit with the President of the World Boxing Council 31 May 2024 - 10:02 The Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak held a video conference call with the President of the World Boxing Council Mauricio Sulaimán. Andriy Yermak thanked the World Boxing Council and Mauricio Sulaimánfor supporting Ukraine and condemning Russian aggression against our country. The Head of the President's Office spoke about President Volodymyr Zelenskyy's Peace Formula and the preparation of the Peace Summit. He noted that the participants of this event will consider concrete steps to establish a just peace in Ukraine, such as free navigation in the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov, nuclear safety, the release of prisoners of war and the return of illegally deported Ukrainian children. Andriy Yermak called on the President of the World Boxing Council to support the Peace Summit and spread the word about it. “I am sure that such an influential and respected person in sports as you will be able to help us. Your public statements in support of the Peace Summit will be very important,” said the Head of the Presidential Office. Mauricio Sulaimán noted that the World Boxing Council will continue to stand with Ukraine. He also emphasized that he plans to involve famous athletes in supporting the Peace Summit. “The World Boxing Council and all its members continue to support all the champions of Ukraine because you are all heroes. And our hearts are with Ukraine,” Mauricio Sulaimán emphasized.
