11:16 Go to all galleries Home News Current events Volodymyr Zelenskyy: The state will solve urgent problems of the veterans 23 August 2019 - 12:10 The state will definitely solve all urgent problems of the veterans. It was stated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy during the opening of the II International Forum of Volunteers and Veterans “Ukraine is where we are”. “We are aware of all the pressing issues - healthcare, rehabilitation, social adaptation, land acquisition, housing acquisition, preferential customs clearance of the vehicles. The state must do all this for you,” Volodymyr Zelenskyy stressed.
