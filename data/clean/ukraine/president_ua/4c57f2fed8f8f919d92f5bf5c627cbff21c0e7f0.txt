Ukrainian volunteers fight for our country every day and will definitely emerge victorious - Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with warriors who defend Ukraine in various units 14 March 2023 - 19:13 On the Day of Ukrainian Volunteer, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with warriors who stood up for Ukraine as part of various combat units. We will do everything possible and impossible to strengthen our warriors - address by the President of Ukraine 13 March 2023 - 21:42 In particular, the government has approved and proposes to the Verkhovna Rada to adopt amendments to the state budget, with more than half a trillion hryvnias allocated for defense additionally. All funds will be used to provide financial support to the military, to support our defense sector. Head of the Presidential Office held a meeting with the Polish delegation 13 March 2023 - 16:02 The parties discussed a wide range of issues related to defense support for Ukraine, European and Euro-Atlantic integration, and Poland's participation in the reconstruction of our country. Now is the time when Ukraine can overcome all historical difficulties if we preserve our unity - address by the President of Ukraine 12 March 2023 - 20:11 I thank everyone who cares about Ukraine and Ukrainians!
