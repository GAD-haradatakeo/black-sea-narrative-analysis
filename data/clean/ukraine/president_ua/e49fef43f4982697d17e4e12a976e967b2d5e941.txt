Ihor Zhovkva: The 23rd Ukraine-EU Summit was one of the most successful in the history of our country's relations with the European Union 12 October 2021 - 19:56 The 23rd Ukraine-EU Summit held in Kyiv on October 12 with the participation of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, President of the European Council Charles Michel and President of the European Commission Ursula von der Leyen was an important stage in developing political dialogue and deepening integration between our country and the European Union.
