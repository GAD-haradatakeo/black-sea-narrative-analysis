Head of the Presidential Office had a phone call with National Security Advisor to the Prime Minister of India 25 January 2023 - 16:14 The interlocutors discussed a number of topical issues in the context of deepening Ukraine-India cooperation in bilateral and multilateral formats. Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine had a phone call with the U.S. President's National Security Advisor 23 January 2023 - 21:26 Andriy Yermak briefed Jake Sullivan on the current situation on the frontline and the tasks facing the Ukrainian Defense Forces. The importance of providing the Ukrainian army with weapons and equipment to effectively respond to the aggressor's actions was emphasized. Andriy Yermak discussed with representatives of partner countries the acceleration of the supply of equipment necessary for Ukraine to restore the energy system 23 January 2023 - 20:21 On the instructions of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, work with international partners continues to support the restoration of the country's energy system after the missile attacks by Russia. On Monday, Head of the President's Office Andriy Yermak held a regular meeting via video conference to discuss the supply of equipment needed to repair Ukraine's power grid. In the process of rebuilding Ukraine, American business will once again be able to demonstrate the power of free entrepreneurship - Andriy Yermak at the meeting of the National Association of State Chambers 23 January 2023 - 19:52 Head of the Presidential Office Andriy Yermak is convinced that the post-war reconstruction of Ukraine will be a challenge and an opportunity for American entrepreneurs to show their best well-known qualities. He said this during an online speech at a meeting of the American National Association of State Chambers (NASC). Andriy Yermak briefs US senators on situation at front, strengthening of Ukrainian army 21 January 2023 - 14:26 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak organized a briefing for a delegation of US senators visiting our country. Peace Formula proposed by the President of Ukraine is a real way to achieve it - Andriy Yermak at the Davos Forum 19 January 2023 - 22:05 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak notes that President Volodymyr Zelenskyy proposed the Ukrainian Peace Formula as a way to end the war, as it is impossible to achieve peace acceptable to our country with the current Russian leadership. The first ambassadors, experts on the African continent, joined the Grain from Ukraine initiative - Andriy Yermak 19 January 2023 - 14:47 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak held a regular meeting of the working group on providing assistance to countries suffering from an acute food crisis, during which they discussed the expansion of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy's Grain from Ukraine initiative and the involvement of international experts familiar with the issues of the African continent in its implementation.
