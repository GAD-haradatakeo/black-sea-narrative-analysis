President of the European Council Charles Michel and President of the European Commission Ursula von der Leyen have started the 23rd Ukraine-EU Summit in Kyiv. President of Ukraine discussed with the Chancellor of Germany and the President of France the issue of achieving peace in Donbas 11 October 2021 - 19:08 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a video conference with Federal Chancellor of Germany Angela Merkel and President of the French Republic Emmanuel Macron. The incidence of COVID-19 is growing, but lockdown can be avoided through vaccination and compliance with quarantine restrictions - meeting with the President 11 October 2021 - 16:00 The situation with the spread of COVID-19 in Ukraine, accelerating the vaccination process and combating forgery of PCR tests and vaccination certificates was discussed by participants of the conference call chaired by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy on combating the COVID-19 pandemic. The 23rd Ukraine-European Union Summit will take place on October 12 in Kyiv 11 October 2021 - 14:05 The 23rd Ukraine-European Union Summit will take place on October 12 in Kyiv. The event will be chaired by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, President of the European Council Charles Michel and President of the European Commission Ursula von der Leyen. Members of the competition commission for the election of the head of the SAP must perform their duties - President 9 October 2021 - 15:37 "There can be no adequate explanation why the competition for the election of a new head of the SAP is delayed. All members of the competition commission must do their job and elect a new head of the SAP, as it should be in accordance with the best international practices," said President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy.
