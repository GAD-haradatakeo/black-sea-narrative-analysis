President 12 February 2022 - 17:43 Ukraine is aware of the risks of escalation of the situation on the part of the Russian Federation, is preparing for any developments and is in constant consultation with international partners in order to resolve the conflict through political and diplomatic means. This was stated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy in a conversation with media representatives during a working trip to the Kherson region. In the Kherson region, Volodymyr Zelenskyy observed the drills of the Ministry of Internal Affairs 12 February 2022 - 15:21 During a working trip to the Kherson region, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy observed the conduct of tactical and special exercises of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine. President held an offsite meeting of the National Security and Defense Council in Kharkiv, at which the Maritime Security Strategy of Ukraine was approved and sanctions were imposed against a number of companies 11 February 2022 - 21:49 Under the chairmanship of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, an offsite meeting of the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine was held in Kharkiv to discuss the formation of territorial defense, impose restrictive measures against a number of legal entities and approve the Maritime Security Strategy of Ukraine. President of Ukraine met with the OSCE Chairman-in-Office, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Poland 11 February 2022 - 20:16 During a working trip to the Kharkiv region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with the OSCE Chairman-in-Office, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Poland Zbigniew Rau. This year, 715 facilities in 24 regions of Ukraine will be implemented under the Large Construction program 11 February 2022 - 18:38 At a meeting of the Presidium of the Congress of Local and Regional Authorities held in Kharkiv, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy heard a report on plans to implement the Large Construction program for the current year. President of Ukraine: Medicine should not only stabilize the human condition, but also restore its vital functions 11 February 2022 - 17:49 During his working trip to the Kharkiv region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a meeting of the Congress of Local and Regional Authorities, during which they discussed issues of improving the quality of medical services and ensuring decent wages of medical workers. Thanks to the program of thermal modernization more than 1.5 million citizens will be able to pay less for heating next season - President 11 February 2022 - 16:51 In Kharkiv, at a meeting of the presidium of the Congress of Local and Regional Authorities chaired by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, the Energy Independence Program, which envisages "green" transformation of the economy and increasing energy efficiency, in particular through thermal modernization of residential buildings, was presented. Volodymyr Zelenskyy: An effective model of territorial defense is a reliable rear for the army, and together they form the basis of a strong Ukraine 11 February 2022 - 15:57 To build an effective model of territorial defense and national resistance, there must be full cooperation between the authorities at all levels and an understanding of their area of responsibility. This was stated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy at a meeting of the Presidium of the Congress of Local and Regional Authorities in Kharkiv. Ihor Zhovkva had a phone conversation with the diplomatic adviser to the Prime Minister of Denmark 11 February 2022 - 15:01 Ihor Zhovkva informed the interlocutor in detail about the current security situation in Donbas, in the temporarily occupied Crimea, as well as along the state border of Ukraine.
