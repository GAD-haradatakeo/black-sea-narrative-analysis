Home News Presidential Office – Ihor Zhovkva and Advisor to the Prime Minister of the Netherlands discussed Ukraine's European and Euro-Atlantic integration by phone 9 June 2021 - 19:31 Ihor Zhovkva informed about Ukraine's steps towards Euro-Atlantic integration. The Advisor to the Prime Minister of the Netherlands agreed on the importance of deepening cooperation between Ukraine and the Netherlands in this direction. Andriy Yermak: The United States shares our position that it is impossible to solve any issues related to Ukraine without Ukraine 9 June 2021 - 18:24 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak is convinced that the United States will adhere to the principled position of supporting Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity during a meeting between US President Joe Biden and Russian President Vladimir Putin on June 16 in Geneva. Ihor Zhovkva and representatives of EU institutions discussed key issues on the agenda of relations between Ukraine and the European Union 8 June 2021 - 21:30 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva met with Managing Director for Russia, Eastern Partnership, Central Asia, Regional Cooperation and the OSCE at the European External Action Service Michael Siebert and Deputy Director General of the Directorate for European Neighborhood and Enlargement Negotiations of the European Commission Katarína Mathernová. Deputy Head of the Office of the President and EU officials discussed ways to resolve issues in judicial reform 8 June 2021 - 19:58 During the discussion of the draft laws on the activities of the High Qualification Commission of Judges of Ukraine (HQCJ) and the High Council of Justice (HCJ), Andriy Smyrnov stressed that the main issue remains open - the principles of decision-making by competition commissions. Roman Mashovets discussed the defense reform in Ukraine with the High-Level Advisor from Lithuania in the Reform Committee of the Ministry of Defense and the Armed Forces of Ukraine 8 June 2021 - 17:53 Roman Mashovets, Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine, had a meeting with Vitalijus Vaikšnoras, High Level Strategic Advisor from Lithuania in the Reform Committee of the Ministry of Defense and the Armed Forces of Ukraine. The time of declarations should be a thing of the past, a clear plan for Ukraine's integration into NATO is needed - the results of the conversation between Presidents Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Joe Biden 8 June 2021 - 17:34 A meaningful conversation between the leaders of Ukraine and the United States of America made it possible to coordinate in detail the positions of our states and testifies to the close and partnership relations between Ukraine and the United States. This was stated by Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak. The situation with COVID-19 in Ukraine continues to improve - meeting at the Office of the President 7 June 2021 - 14:18 There is a positive trend in the incidence of COVID-19 in Ukraine - all indicators are stabilizing. This was discussed at a conference call at the Office of the President of Ukraine. Ihor Zhovkva discussed development of political dialogue with the diplomatic adviser to the Prime Minister of Hungary 3 June 2021 - 18:42 The interlocutors praised the results of recent bilateral contacts, including the recent meeting of the Foreign Ministers of Ukraine and Hungary and the second meeting of the working interagency group on education in May. Ihor Zhovkva took part in a working meeting of advisers to the Presidents of Lithuania, Estonia, Latvia and Poland 1 June 2021 - 20:27 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva took part in a working meeting of foreign policy and national security advisers to the Presidents of the Baltic States and Poland, which took place in Vilnius. Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council Oleksiy Danilov also took part in the event on behalf of the Ukrainian side. In a conversation with his Austrian counterpart, Ihor Zhovkva stressed the need for further monitoring of the withdrawal of Russian troops from the eastern border of Ukraine 27 May 2021 - 19:45 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva had a phone conversation with Head of the Department for European and Foreign Policy of the Office of the Federal Chancellor of the Republic of Austria Barbara Kaudel-Jensen.
