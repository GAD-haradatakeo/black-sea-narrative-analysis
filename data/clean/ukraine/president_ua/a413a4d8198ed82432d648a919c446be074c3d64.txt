meeting of Staff of Supreme Commander-in-Chief chaired by President takes place 23 December 2022 - 20:29 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a regular meeting of the Staff of the Supreme Commander-in-Chief on Friday. Information regarding the current situation on all front lines was heard. Intelligence leaders reported on possible enemy actions in the near future. Members of the Staff reviewed the pace of restoration of damaged military equipment and the rhythm of supplying ammunition to the hottest areas of the front. In addition, the meeting participants heard information about the assistance that Ukraine will receive to restore the power system and provide citizens with electricity. The meeting was attended by: Head of the Office of the President Andriy Yermak, Secretary of the National Security and Defense Council Oleksiy Danilov, Minister of Defense Oleksiy Reznikov, Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Valeriy Zaluzhny, Chief of Defense Intelligence Kyrylo Budanov, Head of the Foreign Intelligence Service of Ukraine Oleksandr Lytvynenko, Commander of the Ground Forces of the Ukrainian Armed Forces Oleksandr Syrsky and the commanders of the task forces. The Staff meeting was also attended by: Deputy Prime Minister for the Reconstruction of Ukraine, Minister of Communities, Territories and Infrastructure Development Oleksandr Kubrakov, Minister of Internal Affairs Denys Monastyrsky, Minister of Foreign Affairs Dmytro Kuleba, other members of the government, including acting Head of the Security Service of Ukraine Vasyl Maliuk and other heads of security and law enforcement agencies.
