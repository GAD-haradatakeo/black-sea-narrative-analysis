Independence” 27 August 2024 - 15:53 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak Held a Telephone Conversation with the Minister of Enterprises and Made in Italy 21 August 2024 - 16:36 Head of the Office of the President Andriy Yermak held a telephone conversation with Minister of Enterprises and Made in Italy Adolfo Urso. First Deputy Prime Minister of Ukraine and Minister of Economy of Ukraine Yuliia Svyrydenko also took part in the conversation. Andriy Yermak spoke about the development of the Made in Ukraine economic platform initiated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. During the conversation, the parties agreed to intensify cooperation within the framework of this project and to hold a Ukraine-Italy conference aimed at strengthening interaction between Ukrainian and Italian businesses. The Head of the Presidential Office thanked the Italian side for confirming its delegation's participation in the thematic conference dedicated to the Energy Security point of the Peace Formula and for the continued support of the Ukrainian people in their fight against Russian aggression. Andriy Yermak and Adolfo Urso agreed to continue discussing areas of cooperation within the framework of Italy's G7 presidency this year.
