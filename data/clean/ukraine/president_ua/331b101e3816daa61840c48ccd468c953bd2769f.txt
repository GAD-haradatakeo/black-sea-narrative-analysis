President: I believe those guilty of war crimes in Ukraine will be justly punished 24 February 2023 - 21:21 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy is convinced that the military and political leadership of Russia will be brought to justice for war crimes committed in Ukraine, and he hopes for the help of the Netherlands in this process. Ukraine grateful to Japan for its support, in particular within G7 – Volodymyr Zelenskyy 24 February 2023 - 21:05 Ukraine is grateful to Japan for the support it provides to our country, in particular for its central role within the G7 presidency. It was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy at a press conference for representatives of Ukrainian and foreign media entitled "February. The year of Invincibility." My task is to do everything so that the world does not weaken its support for Ukraine – President 24 February 2023 - 20:59 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy considers it necessary to make every effort so that the world preserves unity around Ukraine and continues supporting our country in its struggle for freedom and independence. President of Ukraine takes part in meeting of G7 leaders via video link 24 February 2023 - 19:21 Ukraine withstood the full-scale invasion of Russia one year and started to liberate its lands from occupation, so it is necessary to make efforts so that the aggression is defeated this year and peace returns to our country. It was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during his speech via a video link at the meeting of the G7 leaders. Implementation of Ukrainian Peace Formula to make it possible to proceed to diplomatic fixation of end of war – President 24 February 2023 - 18:55 The implementation of all points of the Ukrainian Peace Formula will make it possible to restore international law and move to the diplomatic documenting of the end of the war in Ukraine. President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said this at a press conference for Ukrainian and foreign media entitled "February. The year of Invincibility."
