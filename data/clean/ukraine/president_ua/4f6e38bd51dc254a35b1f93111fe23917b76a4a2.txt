23:54 Go to all news pg Photo gallery 31 October 2024 - 00:00 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak Met with Jake Sullivan 30 October 2024 - 09:00 The Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine, Andriy Yermak, held a meeting with the National Security Advisor to the President of the United States, Jake Sullivan, in Washington. The discussion focused on the situation on the battlefield. Andriy Yermak stressed that the United States' support for the Victory Plan and the strengthening of Ukrainian warriors is crucial for Ukraine. Special attention was paid to increasing sanctions pressure on the Russian Federation. The Head of the Presidential Office noted that the restrictions imposed should really deprive the aggressor state of the opportunities to obtain funds for financing the war. Andriy Yermak thanked U.S. President Joseph Biden and his administration, both houses and parties of Congress, and all the American people for supporting Ukraine in its fight against full-scale Russian aggression.
