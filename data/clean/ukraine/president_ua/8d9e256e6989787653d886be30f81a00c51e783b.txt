Andriy Yermak had a phone call with the British Prime Minister's National Security Adviser 18 April 2023 - 21:39 The parties discussed the current situation at the front and the importance of supplying Ukraine with the necessary weapons for the successful liberation of territories occupied by Russian troops. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with the Speaker of the U.S. House of Representatives 18 April 2023 - 21:02 Volodymyr Zelenskyy informed the interlocutor about the course of hostilities in the main frontline areas and about the urgent defense needs of our country, in particular in armored vehicles, long-range weapons, artillery, air defense and aircraft. President visited a hospital in the Donetsk region and awarded servicemen 18 April 2023 - 15:13 The President also spoke with the medical staff of the hospital and awarded representatives of the medical service of the Armed Forces of Ukraine with Orders of Danylo Halytsky for saving the lives of Ukrainian defenders. President visited the frontline positions of the Ukrainian defenders in the Donetsk region 18 April 2023 - 14:33 During a working trip to the Donetsk region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited the frontline positions of the Ukrainian defenders in Avdiivka. Olena Zelenska talked about the All-Ukrainian mental health program on the pages of Vogue Ukraine Edition and NV magazines 18 April 2023 - 14:05 The wife of the President of Ukraine, Olena Zelenska, in an exclusive interview for the first issue of Vogue Ukraine Edition after the start of the full-scale invasion, spoke about the All-Ukrainian mental health program she initiated, the goal of which is to make psychological care as accessible and universal as general medical care. Andriy Yermak held an online briefing on security issues for representatives of the British Parliament 18 April 2023 - 13:52 On the instructions of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, Head of the Office of the President Andriy Yermak held an online briefing on security issues for members of the defense and foreign affairs committees of the House of Commons of the Parliament of Great Britain. A ship with Ukrainian grain arrived in Yemen as part of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy's Grain from Ukraine humanitarian food program 18 April 2023 - 12:29 As part of the Grain from Ukraine initiative of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, the ship Negmar Cicek delivered humanitarian cargo – 30,000 tons of wheat – to the Port of Salif in the Republic of Yemen. Andriy Yermak had a phone call with Jake Sullivan 18 April 2023 - 10:11 The interlocutors agreed on further coordination in the issue of providing security assistance to Ukraine and discussed next steps in this direction.
