13:56 President Volodymyr Zelenskyy signed Decree № 28/2021 extending the visa-free regime for entry into Ukraine and transit through the territory of Ukraine for citizens of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland until January 30, 2022. Foreign citizens will be able to take advantage of the visa-free regime if their period of stay in Ukraine does not exceed 90 days within 180 days.
