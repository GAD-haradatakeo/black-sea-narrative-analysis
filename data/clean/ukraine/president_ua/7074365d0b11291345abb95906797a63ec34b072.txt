Kyiv 20 April 2023 - 16:49 Go to all galleries Home News Current events In the Poltava region, the President paid a visit to the wounded servicemen in the hospital and presented awards 18 April 2023 - 21:50 During a working trip to the Poltava region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited one of the medical facilities where Ukrainian servicemen undergo treatment. The President talked to the warriors and presented them with state awards. For the personal courage and selfless actions shown in the defense of the state sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine, the defenders were awarded medals "For Military Service to Ukraine". "Thank you for your service. Get well soon. Victory will be ours," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said. The Head of State also inspected the X-ray and neurosurgery department of the hospital and observed the work of the rehabilitation medicine center. The President was shown modern equipment that helps carry out high-quality rehabilitation of patients. Volodymyr Zelenskyy presented the representatives of the medical service of the Ukrainian Armed Forces with the Orders of Danylo Halytsky, Princess Olga of the III degree, medals "For Military Service to Ukraine", and also conferred the "Honored Doctor of Ukraine" titles upon them. "I want to thank you. You are doing a great job, which is appreciated not only by the boys and girls who undergo treatment here, but also by all citizens of Ukraine. I wish you the health that you give to our soldiers and civilians.
