all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak met with the delegation of the Hudson Institute 8 March 2024 - 09:43 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak met with a delegation of the Hudson Institute, a US think tank headed by its President and CEO John Walters. Andriy Yermak thanked the representatives of the Hudson Institute for their visit and their consistent support for our country in the fight against Russian aggression. "It is important that you see with your own eyes that Ukrainians remain strong and continue to fight for their freedom," he said. The Head of the President's Office emphasized that it is important that the United States' support for Ukraine does not stop, as it is essential for the continuation of the fight against Russian aggression. Andriy Yermak also emphasized Ukraine's hope for fair decisions at the NATO Summit in Washington in July 2024. In this context, the Head of the President's Office mentioned the launch of the International Working Group on Security Issues and Euro-Atlantic Integration of Ukraine, which, in particular, drafts relevant proposals for bringing Ukraine closer to the Alliance. The participants of the meeting agreed to maintain a regular exchange of views and coordinated steps for further cooperation.
