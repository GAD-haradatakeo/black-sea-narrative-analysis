13:35 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak had a phone conversation with the Advisor to the Federal Chancellor of Germany 4 August 2022 - 21:45 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a phone conversation with Foreign Policy Advisor to the Federal Chancellor of Germany - Director of the Department of Foreign Policy, Security and Development of the Federal Chancellery of Germany Jens Plötner. The parties discussed the security and economic support for our state, as well as the formation of a system of security guarantees for Ukraine. Expressing gratitude to Germany for helping Ukraine in the face of Russia's full-scale aggression, Andriy Yermak emphasized the need for our country to quickly receive modern weapons. The interlocutors also discussed the issue of further macro-financial support for Ukraine from Germany and the EU. Separately, the Head of the President's Office focused on the issue of increasing sanctions pressure on Russia. Andriy Yermak also expressed hope for a high level of participation of the German side in the online summit of the international Crimea Platform on August 23.
