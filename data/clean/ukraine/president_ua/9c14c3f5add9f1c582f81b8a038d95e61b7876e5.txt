visited wounded defenders of Ukraine in the hospital and presented state awards to the military and medical personnel 31 May 2023 - 19:54 During his working visit to Odesa region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited the hospital of the Armed Forces of Ukraine wounded in the fighting with the Russian occupiers, particularly in the Bakhmut sector. The Head of State talked to the defenders. They told where they were wounded and how they are undergoing treatment and rehabilitation. "I wish you a speedy recovery. Thank you from all Ukrainians for your service. You are real heroes," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said. The President presented the Orders "For Courage" of the third degree to the servicemen who are undergoing treatment in the hospital. Volodymyr Zelenskyy also met with the medical staff of the institution and presented state awards to the military medics. "These are awards from the state for your service. For putting seriously wounded heroic people back on their feet. And you are our heroes too. I want to thank you from the bottom of my heart for your golden hands.
