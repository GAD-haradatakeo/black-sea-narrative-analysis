Representatives 28 March 2024 - 18:33 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with Speaker of the U.S. House of Representatives Mike Johnson. President of Ukraine met with the President of the National Assembly of France 28 March 2024 - 18:16 According to the Head of State, the visit of the President of the National Assembly of France is a sign of support for Ukraine, which he hopes will continue. The interlocutors also discussed cooperation in the defense sector and the priority needs of the Armed Forces of Ukraine. President introduced newly appointed Head of the Foreign Intelligence Service of Ukraine Oleh Ivashchenko 28 March 2024 - 13:53 The President emphasized that the newly appointed FIS Head, General Oleh Ivashchenko, is a professional who perfectly understands the intelligence activities and the main goal of our state. Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with the student community of Sumy University 27 March 2024 - 21:39 The Head of State emphasized that even during the war, many young people continue to study at this university, which means that life goes on, and that is the most important thing. President met with entrepreneurs – members of the "Made in Ukraine" platform in Sumy region 27 March 2024 - 19:23 The Head of State called on all regions of the country to join the "Made in Ukraine" platform to support Ukrainian manufacturers of goods. In Sumy region, Volodymyr Zelenskyy awarded people who are keeping the region alive during a full-scale war 27 March 2024 - 19:13 "I am grateful to the rescuers, medical workers, and power engineers who are overcoming severe challenges. I am grateful to the police. I am grateful to all those who live in Sumy region and fight with all their hearts and souls," Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasized. President held a coordination meeting on security, social and economic situation in Sumy region 27 March 2024 - 18:36 The participants of the meeting paid special attention to the situation in the five-kilometer border zone, evacuation of the population from dangerous areas and maintaining order in these areas. President inspected the construction of fortifications in Sumy region 27 March 2024 - 17:15 Volodymyr Zelenskyy was briefed on a standard project for the construction of a platoon stronghold. It includes, among other things, reinforced concrete structures, firing positions for tanks and infantry fighting vehicles, and shelters made of corrugated steel. Volodymyr Zelenskyy paid a visit to the 117th Separate Territorial Defense Brigade in Sumy region 27 March 2024 - 16:58 During the meeting, the President and the commanders discussed the necessary means of countering this type of weapon. President visited a military hospital in Sumy and met with wounded defenders of Ukraine 27 March 2024 - 16:41 The President was briefed on the course of treatment and recovery of the Ukrainian warriors, including those who were seriously wounded.
