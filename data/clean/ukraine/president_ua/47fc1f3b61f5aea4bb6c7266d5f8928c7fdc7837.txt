athletics arena in the sports center "Olympus" in Kreminna - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 23 October 2020 - 18:29 While on a working trip to the Luhansk region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited the Luhansk Regional Sports Center "Olympus" in Kreminna. Director of the institution Yuriy Ishchenko showed the Head of State the work of the Center and the course of reconstruction in it. Volodymyr Zelenskyy inspected the stadium for training athletes, a swimming pool, and a gym for weightlifting.
