of Ukraine to the Rivne Region 30 July 2024 - 17:14 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak: We Must Expand the Influence of Ukrainian Cultural Products Worldwide 25 July 2024 - 19:41 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak held a video meeting with leaders of national cultural institutions, artistic directors, directors, actors, singers, musicians, and producers. The meeting was also attended by Deputy Head of the Office of the President Olena Kovalska. Andriy Yermak emphasized the significant contribution of Ukrainian cultural figures in the fight against the Russian aggressor. "It is very important that you do not stop working. People in Ukraine and around the world feel this. Culture during wartime is crucial because it is people's faith and the identification of Ukraine," said the Head of the Office of the President. Andriy Yermak noted that our country remains at the center of the world's attention, which opens up great opportunities for expanding the influence of Ukrainian cultural products. He proposed establishing a council under the Office of the President to address issues in the cultural sector. "For the President and our entire team, Ukrainian culture and its development are among the priorities. So let’s communicate and solve all the issues related to culture. Your position and your thoughts are very important to us," emphasized the Head of the Office of the President.
