15:34 The conference with the participation of all ambassadors of Ukraine, which will take place on December 21-22 in Huta, Ivano-Frankivsk region, will begin a large-scale process of geographical decentralization of Ukrainian diplomacy. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, who was speaking with media representatives after a trilateral meeting with the Presidents of Poland and Lithuania at the Syniohora residence. Trilateral meeting of presidents of Ukraine, Lithuania and Poland begins 20 December 2021 - 12:52 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, President of the Republic of Lithuania Gitanas Nausėda and President of the Republic of Poland Andrzej Duda are holding a trilateral meeting at the Syniohora State Residence in Ivano-Frankivsk region. The Polish and Lithuanian leaders arrived at the site in the morning, where they were met by the President of Ukraine. Volodymyr Zelenskyy: Ukrainians abroad are "soft" force that firmly defends Ukraine's interests 18 December 2021 - 20:53 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held an online meeting with members of leading organizations of Ukrainians living abroad. The event was attended by representatives of Ukrainian organizations from Australia, Argentina, Brazil, the United Kingdom, Estonia, Italy, Spain, Canada, Germany, Lithuania, South Africa, Poland, Portugal, Romania, the United States, Turkey and the Czech Republic. Roman Mashovets discusses ways to improve security situation in eastern Ukraine and near it with German Foreign Minister 17 December 2021 - 21:25 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Roman Mashovets discussed the security situation in eastern and around Ukraine, ways to improve it and some issues of bilateral security cooperation with Minister of State at the Federal Foreign Office of Germany Tobias Lindner. Situation with heating season is under control – meeting of energy coordination headquarters with participation of President 17 December 2021 - 20:57 The Government and the Ministry of Energy coordinated work with energy market operators, NPC Ukrenergo and the National Energy and Utilities Regulatory Commission of Ukraine (NEURC) to resolve the situation with coal shortages in the warehouses of some TPPs and CHPPs. Head of state meets with representatives of Ukrainian Council of Churches, signs law on military chaplains 17 December 2021 - 19:50 Representatives of the Council of Churches thanked the head of state for supporting this important law. For his part, Zelenskyy thanked the Ukrainian military, which is defending our state, for their help. Representatives of scout organizations hand over Light of Peace from Bethlehem to President and First Lady 17 December 2021 - 18:16 Congratulating the Head of State and the First Lady on the upcoming holidays, the representatives of the youth associations said that it was a great honor for them to pass the Light of Peace to them again. President discusses security situation concerning Ukraine with Prime Minister of the United Kingdom 17 December 2021 - 18:09 Zelenskyy and Johnson exchanged views on further steps of Ukraine's international partners to de-escalate the situation and reach a peaceful settlement in Donbas. Zelenskyy meets with G7, EU ambassadors to Ukraine 17 December 2021 - 16:27 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with the G7 and European Union ambassadors to Ukraine.
