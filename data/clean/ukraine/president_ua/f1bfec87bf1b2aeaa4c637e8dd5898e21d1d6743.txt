15:55 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak had a meeting with Dmitry Kozak in Minsk 1 March 2020 - 13:47 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a meeting with Deputy Head of the Presidential Administration of the Russian Federation Dmitry Kozak. Representatives of Ukrainian and Russian delegations also took part in the negotiations. During a meeting, the parties discussed the issue of mutual release of detainees in “all for all” format agreed by the leaders of the Normandy Four at the Paris Summit in December 2019. Andriy Yermak noted the intention to conduct the next exchange track in March. The parties stressed the need to continue negotiations to fully implement the agreements reached during the Normandy format Summit.
