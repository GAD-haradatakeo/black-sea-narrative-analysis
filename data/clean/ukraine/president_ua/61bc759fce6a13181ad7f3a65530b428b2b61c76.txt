Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Gitanas Nausėda hold meeting in Kyiv 28 June 2023 - 19:48 Zelenskyy thanked Nausėda for defense assistance to Ukraine, which reached about 1.5% of Lithuania's GDP. Separately, he noted today's decision to buy two NASAMS launchers by the Republic of Lithuania to transfer them to Ukraine. Presidents of Ukraine and Poland hold bilateral meeting in Kyiv 28 June 2023 - 19:32 Zelenskyy expressed gratitude for the defense assistance that Poland provides to our country. The President of Ukraine also highly appreciated Warsaw's leadership in creating a tank coalition and active participation in the coalition of fighters, in particular, the willingness to train Ukrainian pilots on F-16 fighter jets. Volodymyr Zelenskyy meets with presidents of Poland, Lithuania in Kyiv 28 June 2023 - 18:34 At the meeting, the leaders of the three countries discussed the entire range of current security issues. Zelenskyy briefed his colleagues on the active actions of the Ukrainian troops at the front, which make it possible to liberate our land step by step. The parties discussed preparations for the NATO summit in Vilnius.
