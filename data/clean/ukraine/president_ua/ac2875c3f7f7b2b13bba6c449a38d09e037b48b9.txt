President visited the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Museum and laid flowers at the memorial to the victims of the atomic bombing 21 May 2023 - 13:53 ﻿﻿During a working visit to Japan to participate in the G7 Summit, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Museum. In addition, the President of Ukraine and Prime Minister of Japan Fumio Kishida laid flowers at the memorial to the victims of the atomic bombing. President of Ukraine took part in the G7 Summit in Hiroshima 21 May 2023 - 11:04 In the Japanese city of Hiroshima, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in the G7 Summit. Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with Joseph Biden in Japan 21 May 2023 - 10:36 The leaders discussed further cooperation to bolster the defense capabilities of our country, as well as the implementation of the Ukrainian Peace Formula. President of Ukraine had a meeting with the President of the Republic of Korea 21 May 2023 - 09:06 The President of Ukraine expressed gratitude to the Republic of Korea for humanitarian and non-lethal assistance to our country, in particular for the provision of demining vehicles and protective equipment. President of Ukraine had a meeting with the President of Indonesia in Japan 21 May 2023 - 05:01 Volodymyr Zelenskyy called on the President of Indonesia to support Ukraine's efforts to return illegally deported children. President of Ukraine met with Prime Minister of Canada in Hiroshima 21 May 2023 - 04:31 The two leaders discussed topical issues on the bilateral agenda, paying special attention to further cooperation in the security and defense sector. President of Ukraine holds meeting with Federal Chancellor of Germany in Japan 20 May 2023 - 16:04 The Head of State and the Federal Chancellor of Germany coordinated their positions on the eve of the G7 Summit.
