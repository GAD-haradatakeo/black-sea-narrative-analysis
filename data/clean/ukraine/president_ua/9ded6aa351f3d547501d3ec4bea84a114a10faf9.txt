15:35 Go to all galleries Home News Current events President and his wife honored the Heavenly Hundred Heroes 21 November 2019 - 09:20 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and his wife Olena paid tribute to the Heavenly Hundred Heroes. The presidential couple put icon lamps to the Memorial Cross at the scene of their murder. The event was also attended by Prime Minister of Ukraine Oleksiy Honcharuk, First Deputy Chairman of the Verkhovna Rada Ruslan Stefanchuk, Chairman of the Security Service of Ukraine Ivan Bakanov, and other officials. The attendees honored the deceased with a moment of silence.
