posthumously 24 September 2022 - 08:10 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak had a telephone conversation with Jake Sullivan 26 September 2022 - 22:16 As part of the ongoing dialogue with Ukraine's partners and allies, Head of the Presidential Office Andriy Yermak had a telephone conversation with US President's National Security Advisor Jake Sullivan. Jake Sullivan expressed gratitude to President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, the head of his Office and the entire team for the successful operation to release foreign citizens, including two Americans, from Russian captivity. Andriy Yermak informed the American party of the operational situation at the front and the success of the Armed Forces of Ukraine. The parties discussed further security aid to the Ukrainian army. The Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine thanked the American party for the unwavering support of the Ukrainian people in the struggle for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of their country.
