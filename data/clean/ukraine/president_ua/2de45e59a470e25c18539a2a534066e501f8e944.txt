18:30 Preparations are underway for the fourth Conference on Reforms in Ukraine, which was postponed from 2020 to 2021 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. President Volodymyr Zelenskyy plans to visit the event in Vilnius. This was announced by Deputy Head of the Office of the President Ihor Zhovkva in an interview for the Lithuanian branch of the leading information agency of the Baltic region "Baltic News Service" (BNS). Ukrainian TV channels should advertise our country in the world - President 9 March 2021 - 14:24 Ukrainian TV channels should increase the amount of quality content that will help create a positive image of Ukraine in the world. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy in the framework of the All-Ukrainian Forum "Ukraine 30. Culture, Media, Tourism" in Kyiv. Implementation of the New Village project will help restore Ukrainian villages and life in them - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 9 March 2021 - 13:50 In order to revive the Ukrainian countryside, it is first necessary to take care of jobs and restore infrastructure in rural areas, and work has already begun in this direction. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy in an interview for Ukrainian TV channels in the framework of the All-Ukrainian Forum "Ukraine 30. Culture, Media, Tourism" in Kyiv. The main response to COVID-19 is vaccination, not lockdown - President 9 March 2021 - 13:50 The issue of vaccination against COVID-19 in Ukraine and the situation with countering the spread of coronavirus were discussed at a traditional conference call chaired by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. President: If there is no meeting of the leaders of the "Normandy Four", I will meet with each of them separately, postponing negotiations on Donbas will not be allowed 9 March 2021 - 13:06 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy says he is ready to meet with each leader of the Normandy format member states separately if the meeting of the "Normandy Four" is delayed. He stated this in an interview for the leading Ukrainian TV channels in the framework of the All-Ukrainian Forum “Ukraine 30. Culture. Media. Tourism” in Kyiv. State and society must realize all information security threats and find ways to counter them - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 9 March 2021 - 11:38 In the near future, the Center for Countering Disinformation will start operating in Ukraine, which in the future should become an international hub for combating disinformation and propaganda around the world. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy in his speech at the All-Ukrainian Forum "Ukraine 30. Culture, Media, Tourism".
