16:09 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Ihor Zhovkva had a phone call with the Diplomatic Advisor to the Prime Minister of Italy 1 February 2023 - 20:44 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva had a phone call with Diplomatic Advisor to the Prime Minister of the Republic of Italy Francesco Talò. The conversation was devoted to the discussion of topical issues in the context of preparations for the upcoming visit to Ukraine by Prime Minister of Italy Giorgia Meloni and its practical results. They also discussed cooperation in the framework of implementation of Ukraine's European integration course, including the next Ukraine-EU Summit and further contacts between the Ukrainian side and its European partners. The Deputy Head of the Presidential Office expressed gratitude to the Italian partners for providing significant practical support to strengthen the defense capabilities of our country and overcome the consequences of Russian aggression. Ihor Zhovkva and Francesco Talò also exchanged views on further diplomatic steps to implement President Volodymyr Zelenskyy's Peace Formula, including the organization of a "peace summit", and joint efforts to enhance international support for Ukraine.
