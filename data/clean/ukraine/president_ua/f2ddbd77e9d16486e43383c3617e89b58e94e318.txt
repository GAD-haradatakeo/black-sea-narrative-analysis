13:47 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy started his participation in the 3rd European Political Community Summit in Granada, Spain. Ukrainians are doing everything for this to be the last war in Europe and the world - Volodymyr Zelenskyy in an interview with Sky TG24 4 October 2023 - 20:28 The Head of State emphasized that the world cannot get tired of helping Ukraine, because our country is repelling the Russian aggressor. Volodymyr Zelenskyy: Ukraine will manage to maintain the support of the United States and Europe, any pauses today are helping the aggressor 4 October 2023 - 19:43 The Head of State emphasized that today the protection of human rights and life in Ukraine is also the protection of human rights and life in other countries, primarily in Europe and the United States. Because if the Russian aggression in Ukraine is not stopped, it will spread throughout the world. President met with the Chairman of the Board of Onur Group and the Ambassador of Türkiye to Ukraine 4 October 2023 - 18:41 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with Chairman of the Board of the Turkish company "Onur Group" Onur Çetinçeviz and Ambassador of the Republic of Türkiye to Ukraine Yağmur Ahmet Güldere. President held a coordination meeting on the preparation of Kharkiv region for the winter period 3 October 2023 - 19:04 It was noted that as of October 3, almost 90% of the region's inhabitants were provided with electricity (taking into account the actual availability of people). In June-August this year alone, electricity supply was restored to about 6,000 houses. President visited combat brigades in Kharkiv region and held a meeting on the security situation 3 October 2023 - 18:49 The President listened to the report by commander of "Khortytsia" operational-strategic grouping of troops Oleksandr Syrskyi on the defense of Kharkiv region and offensive actions in Bakhmut and other areas. The supply of weapons, ammunition and reserves to the troops was also addressed. President of Ukraine paid a visit to the brigades performing combat missions in the Kupyansk-Lyman area 3 October 2023 - 15:24 The Kupyansk-Lyman direction is one of the hottest on the frontline. The Russian occupiers are constantly trying to conduct offensive actions there. President of Ukraine received the Minister of Defense of Estonia 2 October 2023 - 20:42 The Head of State thanked Estonia for its leadership in providing comprehensive, efficient and timely assistance to Ukraine to counter Russian ongoing full-scale aggression. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with the President of the Government of Spain 2 October 2023 - 19:42 The parties discussed further defense cooperation between the two countries. Volodymyr Zelenskyy presented shoulder boards to Ukrainian military and law enforcement officers 2 October 2023 - 19:35 The Head of State thanked the officers for their service and once again congratulated them on the Day of Defenders of Ukraine, which the whole country marked on October 1.
