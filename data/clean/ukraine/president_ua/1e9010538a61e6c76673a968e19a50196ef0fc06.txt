News Presidential Office – At a meeting of advisers to the leaders of the "Normandy Four" held as a video conference the parties discussed the return to full ceasefire in Donbas 18 March 2021 - 22:11 On Thursday, Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak took part in a meeting of political advisers to the leaders of the "Normandy Four", which took place in the format of a video conference. Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva discussed the Transdniestrian Settlement with Special Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office Thomas Mayr-Harting 16 March 2021 - 19:59 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva met with Special Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office Thomas Mayr-Harting. The parties focused on the course of negotiations in the "5+2" format on a peaceful settlement of the Transnistrian conflict. The parties noted the slowdown in the negotiations in which Ukraine has been participating as a guarantor and mediator since 1995, and stressed the importance of making additional efforts to restore their dynamics.
