Forces 7 October 2024 - 15:57 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak Had a Phone Call with Anders Fogh Rasmussen 7 October 2024 - 18:34 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a phone call with former NATO Secretary General (2009–2014) Anders Fogh Rasmussen. The interlocutors coordinated their positions ahead of Anders Fogh Rasmussen’s upcoming visit to Washington and the 25th "Ramstein" meeting, which will take place on October 12 at the leadership level. As co-chairs of the International Task Force on Security and Euro-Atlantic Integration of Ukraine, the Head of the Office of the President and the former NATO Secretary General also discussed its future work plan Additionally, the parties reviewed the implementation of recommendations from the report titled "Ukraine’s Euro-Atlantic Future: Paving the path to peace & security," published in May of this year. The document outlines arguments and specific proposals regarding Ukraine's future NATO membership. "The key priorities for Ukraine remain a clear and understandable approach to the invitation to join NATO, with defined timeframes and conditions of accession, as well as the removal of all restrictions on the types of conventional weapons, particularly the use of long-range weapons for strikes deep into Russian territory," Andriy Yermak said.
