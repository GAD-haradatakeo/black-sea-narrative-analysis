13:54 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak discussed Ukrainian reforms with the World Bank Vice President 9 July 2020 - 09:52 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a telephone conversation with Anna Bjerde, Vice President of the World Bank for Europe and Central Asia. The parties discussed the provision of $ 350 million to Ukraine under the First Economic Recovery Development Policy Loan. The loan will support reforms that are critical to economic recovery and help Ukraine mitigate the effects of the COVID-19 pandemic. Andriy Yermak and Anna Bjerde discussed the progress of land reform in Ukraine, as well as corporate governance reform. Separately, the interlocutors spoke about the measures needed to ensure macroeconomic stability.
