together with a group of foreign ambassadors, visited the city of Izyum in the Kharkiv region de-occupied by the Armed Forces of Ukraine. Andriy Yermak had a conversation with Timothy Barrow who thanked for the release of the citizens of the United Kingdom and assured of unwavering support for Ukraine 23 September 2022 - 20:50 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a telephone conversation with the new National Security Adviser to the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, former British Ambassador to Ukraine Timothy Barrow. World leaders must firmly state that an attempted nuclear attack on Ukraine will have catastrophic consequences for the Russian Federation - Mykhailo Podolyak in an interview with The Guardian 23 September 2022 - 14:10 According to Mykhailo Podoliak, Russia's impunity for nuclear threats would erode the long-standing principle that nuclear weapons can only be used defensively when the survival of a country is at stake. The success of the operation to release people from Russian captivity was facilitated by the active involvement of international partners of Ukraine - Head of the Office of the President 23 September 2022 - 11:31 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak notes that many influential states and international partners are involved in the process of freeing Ukrainian and foreign citizens from Russian captivity. He stated this at a briefing dedicated to the exchange and return of defenders of Ukraine from captivity. Andriy Yermak held a video call with members of the US House of Representatives Armed Services Committee 22 September 2022 - 22:46 "We feel this support from the entire American people. And I want to thank you, as members of Congress, for making very important decisions that help us fight," he emphasized. Andriy Yermak and Members of the US House of Representatives Permanent Select Committee on Intelligence discussed further support for Ukraine in the war with the Russian Federation 20 September 2022 - 23:42 The Head of the President's Office emphasized that Ukraine not only repelled the onslaught of the aggressor, but also began to conduct successful counteroffensive operations, which made it possible to liberate almost 8.5 thousand square kilometers of Ukrainian territory in the east of the country. Andriy Yermak had a conversation with Jake Sullivan 20 September 2022 - 20:16 Andriy Yermak informed the American side of the situation at the front, as well as the measures taken to ensure security in the de-occupied territories and return normal life there. The parties also discussed continuing security assistance for the Ukrainian army. Andriy Yermak discusses support of Ukraine on way to OECD membership, creation of special tribunal on crime of aggression with heads of foreign diplomatic missions 16 September 2022 - 21:07 Head of the President's Office Andriy Yermak held a meeting with the heads of foreign diplomatic missions. The meeting was devoted to the issue of our country's accession to the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) and the creation of an ad hoc criminal tribunal to prosecute the crime of aggression against Ukraine.
