United States discussed further defense cooperation and bolstering the capabilities of the Ukrainian army 25 June 2023 - 22:20 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with US President Joseph Biden. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with Justin Trudeau 25 June 2023 - 21:47 Volodymyr Zelenskyy briefed Justin Trudeau on the current situation on the battlefield. He also shared Ukrainian assessments of the attempted coup in Russia and the impact of this situation on the course of hostilities. Head of State chairs meeting of National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine 23 June 2023 - 18:21 A meeting of the National Security and Defense Council of Ukraine (NSDC) was held under the chairmanship of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, during which the state of civil defense facilities, the acceleration of judicial reform and further steps towards gaining the European Union membership by Ukraine were discussed. Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in the events on the occasion of the graduation of officers of higher military educational institutions via video conference 23 June 2023 - 12:35 The Head of State emphasized that the last years of studying had become the most difficult and fateful for today's graduates. However, despite all the challenges associated with Russia's full-scale invasion, a worthy replenishment of officers is now joining the officer corps of the Armed Forces of Ukraine. President and the First Lady of Ukraine visit the XI International Book Arsenal Festival 22 June 2023 - 21:05 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and First Lady Olena Zelenska visited the book fair of the XI International Book Arsenal Festival, which will take place in Kyiv on June 22-25. Head of state accepts credentials from ambassadors of Norway, Switzerland, Azerbaijan, Pakistan and Iceland 21 June 2023 - 21:45 The ceremony of presenting the credentials traditionally took place on the territory of the Sophia Kyivska National Reserve. President had a phone call with the President of the World Bank 19 June 2023 - 18:17 The parties discussed further development of cooperation, in particular, mobilization of international support for Ukraine. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with Rishi Sunak 19 June 2023 - 12:28 The Head of State spoke in detail about the course of hostilities. The interlocutors discussed the defense needs of Ukraine and further cooperation in expanding the capabilities of the Ukrainian army on the battlefield, particularly through long-range weapons. No one can feel protected while Russia tries to make aggression a global norm – President of Ukraine after meeting with delegation of African countries in Kyiv 16 June 2023 - 21:17 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a meeting with President of the Union of the Comoros Azali Assoumani, President of the Republic of South Africa Cyril Ramaphosa, President of the Republic of Senegal Macky Sall, President of the Republic of Zambia Hakainde Hichilema and Prime Minister of the Arab Republic of Egypt Mostafa Madbouly, who arrived in our country with a visit. President addressed the graduates of the National Defense University: Our army is becoming stronger, our Ukrainian state is becoming stronger 16 June 2023 - 15:54 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in the graduation ceremony of the National Defense University of Ukraine.
