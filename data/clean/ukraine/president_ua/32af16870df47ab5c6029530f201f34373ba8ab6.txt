16:18 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak had a phone call with Jake Sullivan 15 September 2023 - 21:15 In the framework of the ongoing dialogue with allies, Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a phone call with National Security Advisor to the President of the United States Jake Sullivan. Andriy Yermak spoke about the current situation on the battlefield and the successes of Ukrainian defense forces in the de-occupation of Ukrainian territories. The interlocutors also discussed Ukraine's defense needs and capabilities for strengthening the Ukrainian army. The head of the President's Office expressed gratitude for the latest package of defense assistance from the United States. Andriy Yermak and Jake Sullivan also discussed the upcoming visit of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy to the United States. The head of the President's Office thanked US President Joseph Biden, Congress and the entire American people for their assistance and support to Ukraine in its struggle for independence, sovereignty, and territorial integrity.
