Presidents of Ukraine, Lithuania and Poland signed the Joint Declaration following the Second Summit of the Lublin Triangle in Lviv 11 January 2023 - 16:25 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy, President of the Republic of Lithuania Gitanas Nausėda and President of the Republic of Poland Andrzej Duda signed the Joint Declaration following the Second Summit of the Lublin Triangle held in Lviv. President held a meeting on security situation in northwestern regions of Ukraine in Lviv 11 January 2023 - 13:12 In particular, the parties discussed the operational situation on the border with the Republic of Belarus. In addition, information was provided on counter-subversive measures in the relevant territories. President had a meeting with Executive Vice-President of the European Commission for the European Green Deal 9 January 2023 - 17:21 The interlocutors discussed a range of topical issues of bilateral agenda, particularly in the context of preparation for the EU-Ukraine Summit scheduled for February 3 in Ukraine. Volodymyr Zelenskyy meets with US senators Jack Reed and Angus King 6 January 2023 - 18:05 The President of Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, met with Chairman of the United States Senate Committee on Armed Services Jack Reed, and a member of this Committee, Senator Angus King, who are visiting Kyiv. President of Ukraine had a video call with Prime Minister of Sweden 6 January 2023 - 14:17 Volodymyr Zelenskyy also told the head of the Swedish government about the Ukrainian Peace Formula and called for the active participation of the EU and Sweden, which holds the presidency of the Council of the European Union, in the implementation of this initiative aimed at bringing peace to the European region. President of Ukraine had a phone conversation with President of Türkiye 5 January 2023 - 17:12 The Head of State informed his colleague about the risks of escalation on the frontline and stated the need for further strengthening of the Ukrainian army. The leaders discussed the details of security cooperation between the two countries. A meeting of the Staff of the Supreme Commander-in-Chief was held under the chairmanship of Volodymyr Zelenskyy 5 January 2023 - 13:40 The state of provision of the Armed Forces of Ukraine with necessary weapons and ammunition, as well as strike and reconnaissance UAVs was analyzed. In this context, the participants reviewed the current results of the "Army of Drones" project. President held a meeting of the Staff of the Supreme Commander-in-Chief 3 January 2023 - 12:41 The report of Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Valerii Zaluzhny on the operational situation on the main frontline directions was heard. Chief of the Main Intelligence Directorate of the Ministry of Defense of Ukraine Kyrylo Budanov reported on probable actions of the enemy in the nearest period. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with the President of the European Commission 2 January 2023 - 16:55 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy had the first phone conversation of the year with President of the European Commission Ursula von der Leyen.
