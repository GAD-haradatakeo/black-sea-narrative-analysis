16:08 On the sidelines of the World Economic Forum in Davos (Switzerland), Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak met with National Security Advisor to the President of the United States Jake Sullivan. According to Andriy Yermak, since the beginning of Russia's large-scale invasion, Jake Sullivan has been in constant contact with Ukraine and is deeply informed about the current security situation. The Head of the President's Office expressed gratitude to the interlocutor for his professionalism, constant interaction and close coordination with American allies, which helped ensure the rapid supply of weapons and military equipment to Ukraine to repel Russian armed aggression. During the meeting, Andriy Yermak and Jake Sullivan discussed the situation at the front, Ukraine's defense needs and the course of implementation of the Ukrainian Peace Formula.
