with the President of Kazakhstan 2 February 2024 - 14:31 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with President of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev. Volodymyr Zelenskyy thanked the Kazakh people and business for the humanitarian aid provided to Ukraine. During the conversation, the parties emphasized the importance of adhering to the fundamental principles of international law with regard to the sovereignty and territorial integrity of states. The President of Ukraine outlined the results achieved by the national security advisors and foreign policy advisors in the implementation of the Ukrainian Peace Formula and measures to hold the Global Peace Summit. Volodymyr Zelenskyy also informed of the meeting of the International coalition for the return of forcibly displaced Ukrainian children that will be held in Kyiv today. The two leaders touched upon the intensification of bilateral trade, economic relations and political dialogue.
