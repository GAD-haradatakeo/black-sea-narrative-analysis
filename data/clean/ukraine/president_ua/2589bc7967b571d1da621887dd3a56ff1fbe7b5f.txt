President held a meeting with the Director General of UNESCO in the Chernihiv region 3 April 2023 - 19:36 The Ukrainian President noted the importance of UNESCO's solidarity with the people of Ukraine amid full-scale Russian aggression. President held a meeting with the Secretary General of the Council of Europe in Chernihiv 3 April 2023 - 18:16 During a working trip to the Chernihiv region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a meeting with Secretary General of the Council of Europe Marija Pejčinović Burić who is visiting our country for the second time since the beginning of the full-scale aggression. President of Ukraine held talks with Vice Chancellor, Federal Minister for Economic Affairs and Climate Action of Germany Robert Habeck in the Chernihiv region 3 April 2023 - 17:55 The Head of State expressed gratitude for the effective support and solidarity with Ukraine on the part of the government of Germany and Robert Habeck personally since the first days of full-scale Russian aggression. The successes of our people in the battles for the Chernihiv, Sumy and Kyiv regions revealed to the world the truth that Ukraine is capable of emerging victorious from this war - President on the occasion of the anniversary of the de-occupation of the Che 3 April 2023 - 17:05 During a working trip to the Chernihiv region, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy took part in the events marking the anniversary of the liberation of the region from the Russian invaders and presented state awards. Olena Zelenska and the Director General of UNESCO discussed the preservation of Ukrainian cultural heritage and the restoration of the educational sector 3 April 2023 - 16:27 The President's wife thanked Audrey Azoulay for supporting Ukraine in resisting Russian aggression, in particular for the positive decision of the World Heritage Committee on inscribing Odesa on the List of World Heritage in Danger (January 25, 2023). President paid a visit to the wounded warriors in the hospital of the Chernihiv region and presented them with awards 3 April 2023 - 15:58 During a working trip to the Chernihiv region, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy visited a military hospital and talked with defenders of Ukraine who are undergoing treatment after injuries they sustained during hostilities, in particular in the Donetsk region. President saw the consequences of crimes committed by the Russian occupiers in the village of Yahidne, Chernihiv region 3 April 2023 - 15:28 The Head of State, together with foreign guests, visited the school, in the basement of which the occupiers kept 367 civilians in unsanitary conditions, without water and food, from March 3 to March 30, 2022. Among the hostages were 50 children, including several babies - the youngest was one and a half months old. The second Ukrainian-language audio guide has already started working in Denmark 3 April 2023 - 14:12 First Lady of Ukraine Olena Zelenska talked about the launch of the 59th Ukrainian-language audio guide in the world. From now on, the Ukrainian language is available on hop-on hop-off bus and boat tours of Copenhagen. This is already the second Ukrainian-language audio guide in Denmark.
