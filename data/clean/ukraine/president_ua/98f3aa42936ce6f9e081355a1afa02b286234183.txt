Year 2024" 25 February 2024 - 20:07 Go to all galleries Home News Current events Confiscation of frozen Russian assets will weaken Putin – Volodymyr Zelenskyy 25 February 2024 - 22:02 Infliction of losses on the Russian army on the battlefield, political isolation in the world, increased sanctions pressure and the confiscation of frozen Russian assets will weaken President Vladimir Putin's power inside Russia. This was stated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a press conference at the forum "Ukraine. Year 2024". "For him, money is number one. If you listen to his messages, it is all about money. He ensures the protection of this money, even frozen assets abroad," the Head of State noted. The President emphasized that it is important for Vladimir Putin to preserve Russian business assets. "If all the frozen assets and money are transferred to Ukraine without any conditions, I think it will weaken him significantly... Without bloodshed, but it would be very effective. Then he would be calling everyone, not just me, looking for a way to end this war," Volodymyr Zelenskyy summarized. ph President's participation in the forum "Ukraine.
