10:37 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine had a video call with the Minister of Enterprises and Production of Italy 25 May 2023 - 11:05 Pursuant to the instruction of the President of Ukraine, Head of the Office of the President Andriy Yermak held a video call with Minister of Enterprises and Production of Italy Adolfo Urso. Andriy Yermak emphasized that Adolfo Urso is a good friend of Ukraine and expressed gratitude for the assistance in organizing the recent official visit of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy to Italy. The parties discussed in detail the plans to implement the agreements reached by the Head of State and Prime Minister of Italy Giorgia Meloni. The Head of the President's Office and the Italian Minister of Enterprises and Production considered ways to further coordinate efforts with the governments of the two countries to ensure the fulfillment of the priorities set by the leaders of Ukraine and Italy.
