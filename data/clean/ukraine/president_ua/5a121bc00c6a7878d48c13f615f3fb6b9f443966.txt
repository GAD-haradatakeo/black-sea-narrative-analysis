Presidential couple watched the exhibition "Lesya Ukrainka: 150 Names" 25 February 2021 - 20:37 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and the First Lady Olena Zelenska visited the exhibition "Lesya Ukrainka: 150 Names", which is part of an art project dedicated to the 150th anniversary of the prominent Ukrainian writer and public figure. President appointed Oksana Markarova to the post of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Ukraine to the United States 25 February 2021 - 18:25 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy signed Decree № 75/2021 on the appointment of Oksana Markarova to the post of Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Ukraine to the United States of America. We expect an increase in the presence of Emirati companies in Ukraine - Andriy Yermak at the meeting of the Ukrainian-Emirati Coordination Council 25 February 2021 - 18:06 In furtherance of the agreements reached during the official visit of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy to the United Arab Emirates, a meeting of the Ukrainian-Emirati Coordination Council was held in the format of a video conference. Head of the Office of the President Andriy Yermak is convinced that the work of the Council will contribute to the further development and deepening of bilateral relations between the states. Volodymyr Zelenskyy establishes the Congress of Local and Regional Authorities under the President, the first task of which is to develop the Municipal Code of Ukraine 25 February 2021 - 16:21 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy announced the creation of a Congress of Local and Regional Authorities, which should promote the establishment of qualitatively new relations between central and local authorities. President at the meeting of the Communities and Territories Development Council: We have to solve several important tasks to complete decentralization 25 February 2021 - 14:46 To complete decentralization, it is necessary to amend the Constitution of Ukraine on relations between central and local authorities, as well as to adopt legislation on local referendums and on self-government in urban communities. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during the meeting of the Communities and Territories Development Council within the All-Ukrainian Forum "Ukraine 30". Pleased to hear that NATO's door is open for Ukraine - Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a conversation with Jens Stoltenberg 25 February 2021 - 14:42 President Volodymyr Zelenskyy and NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg discussed topical issues related to the implementation of Ukraine's Euro-Atlantic course and the possibility of deepening practical cooperation with the Alliance. President honored the memory of Lesya Ukrainka 25 February 2021 - 10:09 On the 150th anniversary of the birth of a prominent Ukrainian writer and public figure Lesya Ukrainka, a basket of flowers was laid at the monument to the writer in Kyiv on behalf of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. Diplomats are working on the preparation of the visits of the President of Ukraine to the U.S. and the Asian region - Ihor Zhovkva 25 February 2021 - 09:40 Work is underway to prepare a major Asian tour of President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy.
