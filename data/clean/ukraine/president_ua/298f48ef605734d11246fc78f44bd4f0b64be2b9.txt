29 September 2022 - 17:34 The President noted that the first-ever visit of the head of the lower house of the French Parliament to Ukraine, which is taking place at a crucial time for our country, is clear evidence of solidarity on the part of the French Republic, support for the independence and territorial integrity of Ukraine. President of Ukraine had a phone call with the Prime Minister of Italy 29 September 2022 - 15:26 The main topic of the negotiations was the sham referenda organized by the Russian aggressor in the occupied territories. The interlocutors discussed in detail the appropriate reaction to sham referenda. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with Andrzej Duda 29 September 2022 - 14:10 The key attention was paid to the international reaction to the sham "referenda" held by Russia in the temporarily occupied territories of the Donetsk, Luhansk, Kherson and Zaporizhzhia regions. President honored the memory of the victims of the Babyn Yar tragedy 29 September 2022 - 12:07 On the 81st anniversary of the Babyn Yar tragedy, President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy honored the memory of all the victims of the mass executions of civilians by the Nazis in occupied Kyiv during World War II. The President also honored the memory of the Jews shot by the Nazis in Babyn Yar by putting an icon lamp next to the Menorah memorial sign. President had a phone call with the UN Secretary-General 28 September 2022 - 21:19 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with UN Secretary-General António Guterres to discuss the escalation of the Russian-Ukrainian conflict. President had a phone call with the Federal Chancellor of Germany 28 September 2022 - 21:00 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a substantive and constructive talk with Federal Chancellor of Germany Olaf Scholz. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a phone call with Liz Truss 28 September 2022 - 20:47 The Head of State thanked for the strong support of Ukraine consistently demonstrated by the government of the United Kingdom. The President noted the powerful speech of Liz Truss at the 77th session of the UN General Assembly in New York, which spoke about the importance of supporting our country until victory. President of Ukraine had a call with the Prime Minister of Canada 28 September 2022 - 18:05 The leaders constructively exchanged views on countering Russian aggression amid unprecedented pressure and blackmail by the Kremlin. Volodymyr Zelenskyy had a telephone conversation with Recep Tayyip Erdoğan 28 September 2022 - 16:19 The President of Ukraine thanked official Ankara for its principled position on non-recognition of sham referenda. Ukraine must return all its territories, this will be a victory of justice - Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a conversation with the Harvard university community 28 September 2022 - 09:55 Ukraine must return all its temporarily occupied territories, and only then can we talk about the end of the war.
