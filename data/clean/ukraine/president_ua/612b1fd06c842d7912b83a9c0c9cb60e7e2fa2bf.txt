09:17 On the instruction of the President, Head of the Presidential Office of Ukraine Andriy Yermak held a telephone conversation with National Security Adviser to the Prime Minister of the United Kingdom Timothy Barrow. The interlocutors discussed the outcomes of the third meeting of national security advisors and political advisors in Malta regarding the implementation of the points of the Ukrainian Peace Formula and progress in preparation for the Global Peace Summit. Andriy Yermak and Timothy Barrow also exchanged views on the security situation in the region, particularly the situation on the battlefield and the needs of the Ukrainian defense forces. They separately focused on the situation in the Middle East, emphasizing the necessity of ensuring humanitarian corridors and avoiding civilian casualties. The head of the Presidential Office expressed gratitude to the government of the United Kingdom and the entire British people for their unwavering support of the Ukrainian people in the struggle against Russian aggression.
