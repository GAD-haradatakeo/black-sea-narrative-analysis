23:02 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak Met with the Ambassador of China to Ukraine 25 December 2024 - 10:13 Head of the Office of the President Andriy Yermak met with the newly appointed Ambassador of China to Ukraine Ma Shengkun who had earlier presented his letters of credence to President Volodymyr Zelenskyy. During the meeting, they discussed prospects for bilateral cooperation between China and Ukraine. Andriy Yermak emphasized that China’s role is important in achieving a just and lasting peace for Ukraine. They also touched upon the possibility of a bilateral dialogue at the highest level.
