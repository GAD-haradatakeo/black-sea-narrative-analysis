13:16 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva held a meeting with Director of the Office of the President of Estonia Tiit Riisalo. The parties commended the dynamics of the bilateral political dialogue and discussed preparations for the high-level events to be held in the near future. Tiit Riisalo reaffirmed Estonia’s unwavering position in support of Ukraine’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as the provision of expert assistance in the implementation of important domestic reforms in our state. Ihor Zhovkva thanked for an invariable political support that the official Tallinn provides to Ukraine, in particular, given the non-permanent membership of the Republic of Estonia in the UN Security Council for the 2020-2021 period. Special attention was paid to the preparations and content of the Second Digital Forum of Ukraine and Estonia, which is to be held in Kyiv this year.
