phone call with the Minister of Enterprises and Made in Italy 20 February 2024 - 22:15 In continuation of the dialogue with partners, Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a phone call with Minister of Enterprises and Made in Italy Adolfo Urso. Andriy Yermak expressed gratitude to the government and people of Italy for their unwavering support of Ukraine and Ukrainians in their struggle for freedom, sovereignty and territorial integrity of our state. The interlocutors discussed issues related to strengthening and expediting work on a bilateral security agreement between Ukraine and Italy. Andriy Yermak informed Adolfo Urso about the Made in Ukraine economic platform initiated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy. The parties discussed how to increase the efficiency of this platform for interaction with businesses, taking into account, in particular, Italy's best practices. Additionally, the Head of the Office of the President and the Minister of Enterprises and Made in Italy touched upon current issues related to enhancing cooperation between Ukrainian and Italian businesses. Andriy Yermak and Adolfo Urso also discussed the directions for cooperation within the Italian Presidency of the G7 in 2024.
