11:43 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Olha Stefanishyna and Andriy Yermak met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Italy in Rome 5 October 2020 - 19:59 Deputy Prime Minister for European and Euro-Atlantic Integration of Ukraine Olha Stefanishyna met with Minister of Foreign Affairs of Italy Luigi Di Maio in Rome. The meeting was also attended by Head of the President's Office Andriy Yermak. Співрозмовники обговорили ситуацію на Донбасі та відновлення мирного процесу. The interlocutors discussed the situation in Donbas and the resumption of the peace process. Luigi Di Maio highlighted support for Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The Italian Foreign Minister commended the overall observance of the ceasefire regime in Donbas and expressed hope for progress in the negotiations on the implementation of the Minsk agreements. During the meeting, the parties also discussed the strengthening of bilateral relations between Ukraine and Italy, including economic and trade cooperation. The interlocutors touched upon the issue of preparation of the meeting of the Ukrainian-Italian Council for Economic, Industrial and Financial Cooperation.
