The First Peace Summit for Ukraine Should Become a Platform of Inspiration for the Future Peace Process 2 May 2024 - 11:00 The First Peace Summit for Ukraine will be held on June 15-16, following the January agreement between the President of Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, and the President of the Swiss Confederation, Viola Amherd. The parties have chosen Bürgenstock, located near the Swiss city of Lucerne, as the venue.
