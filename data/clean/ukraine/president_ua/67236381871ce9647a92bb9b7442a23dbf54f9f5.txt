Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak and Valerii Zaluzhny discussed support for Ukraine with Jacob Sullivan and Mark Milley 24 May 2022 - 16:55 Head of the President's Office Andriy Yermak and Commander-in-Chief of the Armed Forces of Ukraine Valerii Zaluzhny had a phone conversation with US President's National Security Advisor Jacob Sullivan and United States Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff General Mark Milley. The interlocutors discussed the security situation in Ukraine, measures to repel Russian aggression and the course of hostilities in the south and east of the country. The parties also considered the next steps to strengthen the defense capabilities of our country and the corresponding cooperation with partner countries. Andriy Yermak and Valerii Zaluzhny thanked the interlocutors for the fruitful dialogue and strong support for Ukraine.
