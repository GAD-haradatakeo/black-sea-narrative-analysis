After the war, Ukraine will become the center of building a new security system in Europe - Andriy Yermak 10 August 2022 - 21:11 "I definitely see Ukraine as a very powerful state. I see it as the center of Eastern Europe, the center of building a new security system, at least in Europe. I am convinced of this," said Andriy Yermak.
