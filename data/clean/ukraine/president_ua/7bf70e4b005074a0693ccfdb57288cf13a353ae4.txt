Peace Formula Implementation 10 August 2024 - 13:29 The Head of the Office of the President, Andriy Yermak, met with the Deputy National Security Advisor to the Prime Minister of India, Pavan Kapoor. Andriy Yermak described the situation on the frontline and the shelling of civilian infrastructure, in particular, the Russian attack on Kostyantynivka. The Head of the Presidential Office emphasized the importance of restoring just peace for Ukraine and India's participation in this process. He expressed the hope that the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi, would be able to join in this effort.
