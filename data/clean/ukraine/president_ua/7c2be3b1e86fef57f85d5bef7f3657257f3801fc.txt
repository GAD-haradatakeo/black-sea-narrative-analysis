Kyrylo Tymoshenko held a meeting with the Ambassador of Romania regarding participation in the implementation of the Fast Recovery Plan 27 September 2022 - 21:13 Kyrylo Tymoshenko spoke in detail about the recovery plan and the creation of a special reconstruction fund. He also informed about the initiative of President Volodymyr Zelenskyy, according to which partner states, individual cities and large businesses are offered to take under patronage the restoration of regions of Ukraine, individual objects or directions. President held a meeting with the Minister for Europe and Foreign Affairs of France 27 September 2022 - 19:07 The Head of State emphasized that Ukraine expects the toughest reaction from international partners to the sham referenda held by Russia in the occupied territories. Ahead of the event, Her Majesty The Queen of Sweden sent a letter in support of the event. Russia felt that it will lose and is now trying to delay this moment, to ensure some activity at the front - address by the President of Ukraine 26 September 2022 - 22:41 Despite the obvious senselessness of the war for Russia and the occupiers' loss of initiative, the command of the Russian military still drives them to their death. Andriy Yermak had a telephone conversation with Jake Sullivan 26 September 2022 - 22:16 Andriy Yermak informed the American party of the operational situation at the front and the success of the Armed Forces of Ukraine. The parties discussed further security aid to the Ukrainian army. President's Office presented the project of a special tribunal on the crime of aggression against Ukraine to the ambassadors of 30 countries 26 September 2022 - 22:07 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Smyrnov and Ambassador-at-Large of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Anton Korynevych held a meeting with the ambassadors of 30 countries, during which the project of creating a special tribunal on the crime of aggression against Ukraine was presented.
