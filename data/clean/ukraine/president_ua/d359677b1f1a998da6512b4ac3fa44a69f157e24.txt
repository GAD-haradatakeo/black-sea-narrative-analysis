Four Ukrainian Higher Education Institutions Join the Dual Degree Program with UK Universities 12 April 2024 - 15:20 The dual degree program is being implemented under the auspices of the President of Ukraine's Fund and as part of the Twinning initiative. It offers students with the opportunity to earn a Master's degree from both a Ukrainian and a British university, and allows the institutions to share their experiences. Approximately 200 students are already enrolled in the program.
