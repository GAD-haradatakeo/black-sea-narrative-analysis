in the ceremony honoring the Heroes of Ukraine 29 August 2022 - 20:10 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak met with businessman Palmer Luckey 18 August 2022 - 09:26 The Head of the Presidential Office of Ukraine Andriy Yermak held a meeting with the American inventor and entrepreneur Palmer Luckey. Mr. Luckey is the Founder of Anduril Industries, a defense technology company. The interlocutors discussed the possibilities of applying new defense technologies to counter Russian aggression. The head of the President's Office emphasized that Ukraine needs technologies that would speed up ousting the invading forces. At the end of the meeting, Andriy Yermak thanked Palmer Luckey for his support of Ukraine and invited him to cooperate with Ukrainian companies.
