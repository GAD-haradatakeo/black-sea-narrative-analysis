Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak had a phone call with National Security Advisor to the President of the United States Jake Sullivan as part of the ongoing dialogue with allies. Andriy Yermak briefed Jake Sullivan on the current situation at the front, particularly in the most difficult areas in Donetsk region. The Head of the Presidential Office emphasized the importance of providing the Ukrainian defense forces with the additional weapons and ammunition as soon as possible. Andriy Yermak expressed gratitude to the entire American people, the President of the United States and both houses of Congress for their continued strong support for Ukraine in the fight against the terrorist state, and unflagging support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of our country.
