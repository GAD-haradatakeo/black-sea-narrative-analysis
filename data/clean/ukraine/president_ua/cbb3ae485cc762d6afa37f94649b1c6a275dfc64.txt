presentation of state awards 23 December 2022 - 18:44 On the occasion of Power Engineers' Day, which is celebrated in Ukraine on December 22, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy met with energy industry workers and presented state awards. The solemn ceremony took place in the White Hall of the Heroes of Ukraine in the Mariinsky Palace in Kyiv. Addressing the energy industry workers, the Head of State said that they are all defenders of Ukraine because they are restoring the power supply after missile strikes by Russian terrorists. "Defenders of our lives, people, state, our society, economy and, in general, I'm sure, our future. It is an honor to see you here today, in the Mariinsky Palace, and to present state awards to you on the occasion of Power Engineers' Day. I congratulate all of you, all Ukrainian energy workers, on your professional holiday!" the President said. Zelenskyy said that Ukrainian workers in the energy industry are among those who, with their attention and conscientiousness, ensure the future victory of Ukraine, and all our people should know about this. "I want our country to always remember and honor those who died during this war, fighting and working for the good of everyone in Ukraine," the President added. Those present observed a moment of silence in memory of the workers of the energy sector, whose lives were taken by the war. Zelenskyy said that the next year will be decisive in the life of our state. "How it will pass, what results we will achieve, in many ways really depends on how you and I will pass this winter, how we will live it. This directly concerns our energy industry," he said. The head of state expressed his gratitude to all those who work to ensure the operation of Ukraine's power system, who restore stability to the Ukrainian energy industry after Russian missile and drone attacks. "Thank you all. Thank you to the masters, engineers, drivers, dispatchers, thank you to the loaders, drivers, locksmiths, repair crews, management of energy facilities and enterprises, and certainly to all those who train specialists for the energy industry," Zelenskyy said. The President congratulated those present and in their person – all the workers of the energy sector of Ukraine and their families with their professional day and the upcoming holidays, wishing Ukraine health and victory. The head of state also presented state awards and honorary titles of "Honored Energy Engineer of Ukraine" to the energy workers and family members of the fallen workers of the industry, awarded for a significant contribution to the development and maintenance of the energy industry in the conditions of martial law, personal courage and self-sacrifice, shown when responding to the accidents at facilities of energy infrastructure, conscientious performance of professional duty. ph Participation of the President of Ukraine in the presentation of state awards on the occasion of Power Engineers' Day 23 December 2022 - 17:33 ph Participation of the President of Ukraine in the presentation of state awards on the occasion of Power Engineers' Day 23 December 2022 - 17:33 Print version News Current events Speeches Congratulations Presidential Office President's wife All news Photos Social activities Conferences and meetings Working visits Foreign visits All galleries Videos President Biography About the President of Ukraine Office Office Management Press Office Upcoming events Newsletter subscription The Presidential Office of Ukraine.
