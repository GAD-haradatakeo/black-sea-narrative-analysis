For this to happen, Ukraine must win – Andriy Yermak speaking at a panel discussion at the World Economic Forum in Davos 17 January 2024 - 09:26 Andriy Yermak met with Saudi Foreign Minister in Davos 16 January 2024 - 21:55 Go to all news pg Photo gallery President's participation in the World Economic Forum in Davos 16 January 2024 - 12:15 Go to all galleries Home News Presidential Office Andriy Yermak met with Saudi Foreign Minister in Davos 16 January 2024 - 21:55 On the sidelines of the World Economic Forum in Davos (Switzerland), Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Prince Faisal bin Farhan Al Saud. As a follow-up to the dialogue between President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud, the parties discussed further steps to intensify bilateral relations in various areas. The Head of the President's Office and the Minister of Foreign Affairs paid special attention to humanitarian issues. Andriy Yermak noted that Prince Faisal bin Farhan Al Saud became the first Saudi Foreign Minister to pay an official visit to Ukraine. "I would like to thank the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine and for not being indifferent to the fate of our people. We highly appreciate everything that Saudi Arabia and our true friend Faisal bin Farhan Al Saud are doing for our people," the Head of the Presidential Office said. Following the meeting, Andriy Yermak and Prince Faisal bin Farhan Al Saud agreed to continue the work of the delegations of both countries tomorrow on the sidelines of the World Economic Forum in Davos.
