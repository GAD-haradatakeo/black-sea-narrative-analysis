Azovstal defenders and the First Deputy Mayor of Enerhodar released from Russian captivity 16 February 2023 - 23:54 Pursuant to the instruction of the President of Ukraine, Head of the Presidential Office Andriy Yermak spoke via video call with First Deputy Mayor of Enerhodar Ivan Samoydiuk, who was released from Russian captivity on Thursday, as well as with senior sailor of the Ukrainian Navy Vitaliy Ageyev and serviceman of the State Border Guard Service of Ukraine Vadym Dzhafarov, who defended Azovstal and Mariupol in the spring. Ihor Zhovkva: Ukraine is grateful to Lithuania for its unwavering and comprehensive support 16 February 2023 - 19:05 Deputy Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Ihor Zhovkva met with Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Lithuania Mantas Adomėnas. Andriy Yermak met with the famous British historian, Professor Timothy Ash 15 February 2023 - 16:03 Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak met with British historian, journalist and writer, Professor of European Studies in the University of Oxford Timothy Garton Ash, who is on a visit to our country. Andriy Yermak held a briefing for heads of diplomatic missions of the EU countries on the priorities of sanctions policy 14 February 2023 - 19:34 The main focus was on the need to further strengthen sanctions pressure on the aggressor country in the context of the adoption of the 10th EU sanctions package, which is to be approved on the eve of the anniversary of Russia's full-scale military invasion of Ukraine. Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine had a phone call with National Security Advisor to the President of the United States 14 February 2023 - 08:41 The parties discussed the strong support the United States will continue to provide Ukraine as the anniversary of the war approaches and the importance of our continued communication. Andriy Yermak held a meeting of the working group on the establishment of a special international tribunal for the crime of aggression against Ukraine 13 February 2023 - 20:43 Head of the Presidential Office Andriy Yermak held a meeting of the working group on the issue of establishing a special international tribunal for the crime of aggression against Ukraine. Office of Ukraine's President presents two acceptable ways of creating tribunal against Russian leaders 10 February 2023 - 19:15 At a meeting with French journalists, Deputy Head of the Office of the President Andriy Smyrnov spoke about the most desirable ways for Ukraine to create an international tribunal.
