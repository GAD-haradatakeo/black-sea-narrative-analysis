Those Who Survived Were Robbed of Decades after 1944 – President of Ukraine on the Remembrance Day of the Genocide of the Crimean Tatar People 18 May 2024 - 19:41 The President of Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, took part in the unveiling of a memorial stone, which will be replaced by a Memorial to the Victims of the Crimean Tatar Genocide. A Consolidation of Countries from All the Continents at the Global Peace Summit Will Show Who Really Wants to End the War – the President of Ukraine in an Interview for AFP 18 May 2024 - 14:51 Ukraine is ready to engage in dialogue with all the states that support its territorial integrity. That is why the President and his team are actively working to involve representatives of the Global South in the Summit. After all, nuclear security is very important for China, while food security is important for Africa and Asia, and prisoner exchange is important for the Arab world.
