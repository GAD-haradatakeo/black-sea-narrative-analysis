region 30 July 2023 - 15:11 Go to all galleries Home News Current events Volodymyr Zelenskyy: Brazil and President Lula da Silva could help implement one of the points of the Ukrainian Peace Formula 31 July 2023 - 17:46 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy notes that Brazil and President Lula da Silva could do a lot to end the war in Ukraine, in particular, help implement one of the points of the Ukrainian Peace Formula. The Head of State said this in an interview with a journalist from Globo. "I know that President Lula and the Brazilian society support Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity," he said. Volodymyr Zelenskyy is convinced that Brazil must be a party to the implementation of the Peace Formula proposed by Ukraine, in particular the implementation of the paragraph on global food security, because helping our country export agricultural products also means helping other countries that suffer from food shortages and may be on the verge of starvation. The President of Ukraine also believes that Brazil and its leader could help our country in the humanitarian sphere. For example, with special equipment for de-mining territories so that Ukrainian farmers would not be afraid to die in their fields while growing and harvesting crops. "I believe that today President Lula should use all his capabilities to bring peace closer. Every leader is responsible for whether there is a war in the world or not," Volodymyr Zelenskyy said. The Head of State said he would like to meet with his Brazilian counterpart. In his opinion, it is important for the President and the people of Brazil to hear him personally and receive truthful information about the events in our country. "Then the relations between our countries will only get better and stronger. I am sure our peoples are absolutely interested in this," Volodymyr Zelenskyy added. According to the Head of State, the position of Brazil and other Latin American countries is very important for Ukraine today.
