Today, Ukrainians Are Once Again Standing Up to Evil, Which Has Reemerged – Address by the President on the Day of Remembrance and Victory over Nazism 8 May 2024 - 09:06 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy recorded an address in the village of Yahidne, Chernihiv region, on the occasion of the Day of Remembrance and Victory over Nazism in World War II. Spain, Belgium, Latvia and Finland Confirmed Their Participation in the Global Peace Summit 7 May 2024 - 19:34 The President of Ukraine, Volodymyr Zelenskyy, held telephone conversations with the President of the Republic of Finland Alexander Stubb, the President of the Latvian Republic Edgars Rinkēvičs, the Prime Minister of Spain Pedro Sánchez and the Prime Minister of Belgium Alexander De Croo. All of them confirmed their participation in the Global Peace Summit that will be held in Switzerland this June.
