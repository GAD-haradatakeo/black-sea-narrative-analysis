The international community should put pressure on Russia so that the occupiers leave the territory of the ZNPP - President of Ukraine 23 August 2022 - 23:06 Ukraine is ready to provide a corridor for the arrival of the UN and IAEA mission to the Zaporizhzhia nuclear power plant, which is under the occupation of Russian troops. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy at a press conference following the Second Summit of the Crimea Platform in Kyiv. Ukraine will return Crimea in the way it decides - President 23 August 2022 - 22:56 President Volodymyr Zelenskyy emphasizes that Crimea is an integral part of Ukraine, and our state will return the peninsula occupied by Russia by any available means. The Head of State said this during a press conference following the Second Summit of the Crimea Platform. As of today, Ukraine is not ready to agree to a ceasefire, and there will be no new "Minsk" - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 23 August 2022 - 22:06 Currently, Ukraine is not ready for negotiations with Russia regarding a ceasefire, as it does not want to repeat the negative experience of the Minsk agreements, which led to the loss of control over our territories. President Volodymyr Zelenskyy said this at a press conference following the Second Summit of the Crimea Platform in Kyiv. On the initiative of the President of Ukraine, the Second Summit of the Crimea Platform was held 23 August 2022 - 21:23 The Second Summit of the Crimea Platform initiated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy took place. Representatives of about 60 foreign countries and international organizations took part in its work. In particular, the leaders of the G7, representatives of the African continent and Latin American countries joined the event. Joint statement of the International Crimea Platform Participants 23 August 2022 - 19:48 We, the Participants in the International Crimea Platform, reiterate our resolve to maintain pressure on Russia to end the temporary occupation of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the city of Sevastopol without delay to restore Ukraine’s control over its territory. In the event of an illegal show trial of the defenders of Mariupol, there will be no question of negotiations - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 23 August 2022 - 15:22 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy notes that the inhumane treatment of the captured defenders of Mariupol, in particular the servicemen of the "Azov" special purpose regiment, will make negotiations with the Russian side impossible. Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Andrzej Duda opened the Walk of the Brave in Kyiv, where true friends of our state will be honored 23 August 2022 - 14:43 The Walk was laid on the initiative of the Head of State on the occasion of the 31st anniversary of Ukraine's independence. The place was not chosen randomly, as modern independent Ukraine was born on Constitution Square in front of the Verkhovna Rada. For Ukraine today, support with weapons is a priority, and all our needs are known in detail to the President of Poland - Volodymyr Zelenskyy following the meeting with Andrzej Duda in Kyiv 23 August 2022 - 14:33 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy held a meeting with President of the Republic of Poland Andrzej Duda who arrived in our country to participate in the Second Summit of the Crimea Platform in Kyiv.
