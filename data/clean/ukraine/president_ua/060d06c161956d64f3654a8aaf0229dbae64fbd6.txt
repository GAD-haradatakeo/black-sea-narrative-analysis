Cooperation with Germany is of special importance for Ukraine's European integration - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 19 March 2021 - 11:41 Ukraine seeks to synchronize and integrate its economy into the economic system of the European Union, and cooperation with Germany is of particular importance in this regard. This was stated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy in the video address to the participants of the Fourth Ukrainian-German Business Forum. Ihor Zhovkva: The conclusion of the Declaration on the European Prospect of Ukraine is an important step towards the realization of the strategic goal of our state 19 March 2021 - 11:17 The official visit of President of the Republic of Lithuania Gitanas Nausėda to Ukraine was extremely productive. At a meeting of advisers to the leaders of the "Normandy Four" held as a video conference the parties discussed the return to full ceasefire in Donbas 18 March 2021 - 22:11 On Thursday, Head of the Office of the President of Ukraine Andriy Yermak took part in a meeting of political advisers to the leaders of the "Normandy Four", which took place in the format of a video conference. Volodymyr Zelenskyy: Small and medium business is the basis of Ukraine's economy, so it is an important priority for the state 18 March 2021 - 18:44 The state and business must work as a team, as allies who have a common interest - a successful and prosperous Ukraine. This was stated by President Volodymyr Zelenskyy during a speech at the All-Ukrainian Forum "Ukraine 30. Small and Medium Business and State", which the President attended together with President of Lithuania Gitanas Nausėda. Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Gitanas Nausėda held a meeting of the Council of Presidents: Ukraine and Lithuania are great friends 18 March 2021 - 16:53 A meeting of the Council of Presidents chaired by Volodymyr Zelenskyy and Gitanas Nausėda was held. There are no grounds to suspend the use of Covishield vaccine in Ukraine at the moment, there has been no fatal case - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 18 March 2021 - 16:12 President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy notes that currently there are no grounds for suspending the use of Covishield vaccine in the country, and at the same time work is underway to supply other vaccines to Ukraine. Ukraine and Lithuania agreed to unite actions to increase mutual trade - President 18 March 2021 - 16:04 Trade, economic and investment cooperation between Ukraine and Lithuania is an important issue of bilateral relations. This was stated by President of Ukraine Volodymyr Zelenskyy at a joint press conference with President of Lithuania Gitanas Nausėda following today's talks in Kyiv. Lithuania will support Ukraine on the path of European and Euro-Atlantic integration - Volodymyr Zelenskyy 18 March 2021 - 15:48 President Volodymyr Zelenskyy noted the importance of Lithuania's support for Ukraine's European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. He stated this at a joint press conference with Lithuanian President Gitanas Nausėda following the talks in Kyiv.
