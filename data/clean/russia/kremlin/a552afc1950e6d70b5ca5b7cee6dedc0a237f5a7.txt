Events Greetings on 100th anniversary of Moscow Academic Theatre of Satire The President sent greetings to the current and retired staff of the Moscow Academic Theatre of Satire on its 100 th anniversary. October 1, 2024 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Since its inception, the Moscow Academic Theatre of Satire has become a true centre of attraction for an entire constellation of gifted and dedicated stage directors, writers, actors and artists. Owing primarily to their inexhaustible inspiration, talent and professionalism, every premiere has invariably become an outstanding event in the cultural and social life of our capital and the entire country, evoking sincere and genuine interest among admirers of dramatic art. I am delighted to note that, drawing on the rich creative heritage of their predecessors and their dedication to educational traditions, the current team works with great passion, bringing bold and innovative ideas to life on the legendary stage of the Moscow Academic Theatre of Satire. They continue to delight audiences with a broad and diverse repertoire.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 1, 2024, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75242 Text version
