Events Meeting with Minister of Justice Konstantin Chuychenko September 20, 2022 18:50 The Kremlin, Moscow With Minister of Justice Konstantin Chuychenko. Vladimir Putin held a working meeting with Minister of Justice Konstantin Chuychenko. In particular, the Minister was informed that he had been awarded the Order for Services to the Fatherland (III degree).
