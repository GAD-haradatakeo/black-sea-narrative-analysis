Events Greetings to Rodion Shchedrin on his 90th birthday Vladimir Putin sent greetings to Rodion Shchedrin, composer, pianist, People’s Artist of the USSR, on his 90 th birthday. December 16, 2022 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Your remarkable jubilee is celebrated by numerous fans of your talent and everyone who loves music, both in Russia and around the world. They rightfully consider you a prominent composer of our time, a person of tremendous and remarkable talent, a true artist and creator.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 16, 2022, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70092 Text version
