Events Visit to Valaam Monastery Vladimir Putin and President of the Republic of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko visited the Valaam Transfiguration of the Saviour Monastery. July 26, 2024 15:30 Republic of Karelia Vladimir Putin and Alexander Lukashenko Lukashenko Alexander President of Belarus visited the Chapel of the Valaam Icon of the Mother of God on Svetly Island. They also had an informal conversation. A day earlier, the leaders of Russia and Belarus attended a service in the Church of the Smolensk Icon of the Mother of God in the Smolensk skete and also visited the Сhapel of Holy Royal Passion-bearers of the monastery’s metochion.
