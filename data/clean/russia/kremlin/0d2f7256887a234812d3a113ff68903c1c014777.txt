Events Vladimir Putin congratulated the Russian military on the liberation of Artemovsk May 21, 2023 01:00 The President congratulated the Wagner assault units as well as all service personnel of the Russian Federation Armed Forces who had rendered them the required support and protected the flanks, on the completion of the operation to liberate Artemovsk. All military personnel who distinguished themselves in the operation will be recommended for state decorations.
