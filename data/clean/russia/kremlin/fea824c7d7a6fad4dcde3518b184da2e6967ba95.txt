Events Greetings to participants in the event dedicated to the 600th anniversary of uncovering the relics of St Sergius of Radonezh Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants in the event dedicated to the 600 th anniversary of uncovering the relics of St Sergius of Radonezh. July 18, 2022 09:30 The message reads in part: ”Sergius of Radonezh was an outstanding historical figure, a man of great spiritual and moral strength, a great devotee and patriot. At a difficult time for our Fatherland, he made an appeal for unity, for gathering forces to defend his native land. His appeal strengthened faith, helped people to overcome a cruel enemy and to revive Russia. I am convinced that the spiritual feat of Sergius of Radonezh and his whole life will forever remain an example of selfless love for our Fatherland, of unbending will and greatness of spirit.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 18, 2022, 09:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69018 Text version
