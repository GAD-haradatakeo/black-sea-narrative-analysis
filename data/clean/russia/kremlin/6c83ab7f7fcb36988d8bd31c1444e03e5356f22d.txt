Events Condolences to President of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon Vladimir Putin sent a message of condolences to President of the Republic of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon over the tragic consequences of the avalanches in the Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Region. February 16, 2023 15:30 In his message, the President of Russia passed on his sincere condolences to the families and friends of the victims, and wished a speedy recovery to everyone injured in the natural disaster.
