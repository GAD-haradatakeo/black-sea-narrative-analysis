Events Greetings on the opening of the Thubden Shedrub Ling Buddhist Monastery Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants in the opening ceremony of the Thubden Shedrub Ling Buddhist Monastery in Kyzyl. April 28, 2023 08:00 The message reads, in part: “The implementation of this landmark and historically significant initiative became possible thanks to the constructive unity of Russia’s Buddhist community and its desire to preserve the enduring values of this ancient religion, which have served to strengthen friendship and mutual understanding among people for many centuries. I am confident that the new monastery will become one of the main Buddhist religious, cultural and educational centres in Russia, and that it will help believers, primarily young people, access the priceless spiritual and moral legacy handed down by their ancestors.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 28, 2023, 08:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71025 Text version
