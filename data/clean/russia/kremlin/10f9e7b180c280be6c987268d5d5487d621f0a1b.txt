Events Greeting on the opening the 25th All-Russian Shukshin Film Festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants and guests of the 25 th All-Russian Shukshin Film Festival. July 18, 2023 15:00 The message reads, in part: “In recent years your festival has become a large, significant event in the cultural life of the Altai Territory and the whole of Russia. The festival is famous for its distinct, rich and diverse programme. Guests are presented with a wide palette of documentary and feature films and can take part in creative discussions with famous directors and actors. I hope this festival will give an impetus to promising ideas and plans and discover new young talents. And of course, it will contribute to the preservation and popularisation of the legacy of our outstanding film director, actor and writer Vasily Shukshin.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 18, 2023, 15:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71695 Text version
