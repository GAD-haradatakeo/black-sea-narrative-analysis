Events Greetings to the 5th All-Russian Youth Forum Digoriya Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants in the 5 th All-Russian Youth Forum Digoriya, currently taking place in Nizhny Novgorod. July 10, 2023 11:15 The message reads, in part: “Your meetings have already become a tradition and have proved to be an authoritative platform for constructive and fruitful dialogue between young scientists, teachers and postgraduate students in the field of humanitarian knowledge: political scientists and historians, sociologists and psychologists, as well as representatives of other professions. Within the framework of open discussions, training sessions and consultations you will have to discuss, at the expert level, a wide range of problems that are at the forefront of public attention, offer your approaches to solving urgent problems related to the country's development. And of course, at the forum you will be able to exchange experience with like-minded people, and receive support for the implementation of meaningful, extraordinary projects and initiatives. I hope that this meeting will be held in a creative and constructive manner and that it will contribute to the professional and personal development of young men and women, as well as helping them to form an active stance in life and society.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 10, 2023, 11:15 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71622 Text version
