Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council Vladimir Putin held, via videoconference, a meeting on current issues with permanent members of the Security Council. September 14, 2023 11:15 Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region The participants discussed developing relations with the country's closest neighbours and allies and protecting Russia's interests in its relations with other foreign countries. Vladimir Putin elaborated on the results of his talks with the Chairman of State Affairs of the DPRK Kim Jong-un Kim Jong-un Chairman of State Affairs of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea that took place the day before.
