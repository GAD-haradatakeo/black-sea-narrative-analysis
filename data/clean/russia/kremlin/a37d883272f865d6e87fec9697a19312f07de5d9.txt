Events Greetings to Russian team of schoolchildren that took part in the 34th International Biology Olympiad in Al Ain, United Arab Emirates Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to Russia’s national team of schoolchildren that took part in the 34 th International Biology Olympiad in Al Ain, United Arab Emirates. July 14, 2023 21:00 The message reads, in part: “Congratulations on your excellent performance at the 34 th International Biology Olympiad. You fulfilled your tasks brilliantly and showed thorough knowledge, endurance, and solidarity. This team victory is a well-deserved reward for your talent, dedication, and hard work. And of course, special words of gratitude go to your teachers and coaches: everyone who helps you believe in yourself and supports you on your road to success.” Topics School Science and innovation Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 14, 2023, 21:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71684 Text version
