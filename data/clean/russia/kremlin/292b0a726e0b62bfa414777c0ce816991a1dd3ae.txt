Events Condolences to President of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev Vladimir Putin conveyed his condolences to President of the Republic of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev on large forest fires in eastern Kazakhstan. June 10, 2023 17:15 In his message, the President of Russia expressed confidence that the tragic aftermath of the natural disaster will be overcome, and the regions that have been affected will return to normal and prosperous life.
