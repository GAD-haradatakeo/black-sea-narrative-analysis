Events Greetings to the 5th Productivity 360 Federal Forum Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants and guests of the 5 th Productivity 360 Federal Forum, now underway in Kazan. July 20, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “You are focusing on the implementation of the Labour Productivity and Employment Support national project, the creation of modern and high-quality jobs, extensive use of digital technologies and advanced managerial methods in key sectors. All these strategic issues are of paramount importance for economic development, for strengthening technological sovereignty and boosting the efficiency and competitiveness of national businesses. In this context, it is necessary to facilitate close coordination between the authorities at all levels, the regions, the business community, academic circles and also to study the best experience and practices in great detail. I am confident that your forum will make an important contribution to strengthening this partnership, filled with real content, and that your initiatives and recommendations will, certainly, be in high demand.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 20, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71706 Text version
