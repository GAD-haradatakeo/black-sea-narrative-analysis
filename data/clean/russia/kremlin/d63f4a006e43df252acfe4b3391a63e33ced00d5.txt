Events Telephone conversation with President of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev. November 15, 2024 12:15 In the context of the agreements reached during Vladimir Putin’s state visit to Azerbaijan in August 2024, as well as recent contacts in Baku involving Russian Prime Minister Mikhail Mishustin, the presidents reviewed the implementation of several mutually beneficial projects in the sphere of industrial cooperation and transport. They reaffirmed their mutual interest in further comprehensively expanding the Russian-Azerbaijani strategic partnership and alliance. The leaders agreed to continue contacts at various levels.
