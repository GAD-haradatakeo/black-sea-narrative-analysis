Events Meeting with Emergencies Minister Alexander Kurenkov Vladimir Putin held a working meeting with Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief Alexander Kurenkov. December 27, 2024 14:00 The Kremlin, Moscow Alexander Kurenkov Kurenkov Alexander Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief of the Russian Federation reported to the President on the emergency response measures following the declaration of a federal-level state of emergency in the Krasnodar Territory as a result of a fuel spill in the Kerch Strait. The meeting took place on Rescue Worker’s Day and also covered current activities of the Ministry’s units.
