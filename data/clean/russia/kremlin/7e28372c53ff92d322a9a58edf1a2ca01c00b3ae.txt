Events Condolences to Emir of Kuwait Sheikh Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to Emir of Kuwait Sheikh Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah on the passing of his brother, Emir Nawaf Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah. December 16, 2023 14:25 The message reads, in part: “Emir Nawaf Al-Ahmad rightfully commanded great authority in the Middle East. His dedicated efforts contributed substantially to the socioeconomic development of Kuwait and the elevation of the country’s standing in the international arena. We appreciate his personal contribution to fostering strong and friendly Russian-Kuwaiti relations.” The President of Russia asked to convey his sincere sympathy and support to all members of the Al-Sabah family and the friendly people of Kuwait.
