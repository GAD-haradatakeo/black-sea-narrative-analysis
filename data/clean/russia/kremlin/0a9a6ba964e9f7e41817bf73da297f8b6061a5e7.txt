Events Condolences to President of India Droupadi Murmu and Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi Vladimir Putin sent a message of condolences to President of India Droupadi Murmu and Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi over the tragic consequences of landslides in the state of Kerala. July 31, 2024 18:10 The President of Russia asked to convey his words of sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends and wishes for a speedy recovery to all the injured.
