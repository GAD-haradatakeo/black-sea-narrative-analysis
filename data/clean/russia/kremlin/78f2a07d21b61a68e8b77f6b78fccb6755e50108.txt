Events Greetings on Bailiff Day Vladimir Putin sent greetings to civil servants, current and retired officials of the Federal Bailiff Service on their professional holiday, Bailiff Day. November 1, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the years, your service has considerably strengthened its potential, improved its performance and evolved into a modern and effective agency that uses advanced technologies and management approaches. The significance of objectives facing the Federal Bailiff Service today requires each of you to possess profound and thorough knowledge, to unfailingly fulfil your professional duties and to display impeccable personal qualities as your sphere of responsibility includes important and diverse issues linked with efforts to improve the country’s judicial and law enforcement system and to protect constitutional rights of citizens and the interests of the state and businesses.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 1, 2024, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75466 Text version
