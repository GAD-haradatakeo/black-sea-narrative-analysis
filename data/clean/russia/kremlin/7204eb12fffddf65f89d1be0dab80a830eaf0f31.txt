Events Greetings on Nuclear Industry Worker’s Day Vladimir Putin congratulated current and former nuclear industry workers on their professional holiday. September 28, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “You are rightfully proud of the glorious history of the industry, honour the names of legendary scientists, outstanding designers, engineers, and specialists whose achievements formed the basis of the national nuclear industry and its subsequent successes, contributed to the development of our national economy, the creation of a powerful industrial and advanced energy complex, the development of the Arctic Circle and the Northern Sea Route, and ensured nuclear parity and the state’s defence capability. It is gratifying that today you cherish this unique heritage, consistently implement strategic tasks to further strengthen the Russian nuclear power industry, develop new materials, introduce breakthrough solutions in the medical and space spheres, implement promising Arctic programs. You give special focus on building up the industry’s human resources, research and technological potential, and deepening mutually beneficial international cooperation. I am confident that you will continue to make a worthy contribution to Russia’s progress.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 28, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75199 Text version
