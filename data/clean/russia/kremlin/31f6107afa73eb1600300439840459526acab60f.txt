Events Congratulations to President of Abkhazia Aslan Bzhania Vladimir Putin congratulated President of the Republic of Abkhazia Aslan Bzhania on Victory and Independence Day. September 30, 2022 09:00 The message reads, in part: “The development of Russia-Abkhazia relations is based on the principles of alliance and strategic partnership. I am confident that by acting together we will be able to ensure the continued growth of the entire range of constructive bilateral ties to the benefit of our fraternal nations and in the interests of stronger regional stability and security.” Geography Abkhazia Topics Foreign policy Persons Bzhania Aslan Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 30, 2022, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69462 Text version
