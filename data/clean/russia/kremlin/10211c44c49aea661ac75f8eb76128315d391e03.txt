Events Birthday greetings to Yury Grigorovich Vladimir Putin extended birthday greetings to choreographer and People’s Artist of the USSR Yury Grigorovich. January 2, 2024 11:00 The message reads, in part: “You have dedicated your brilliant talent, creative energy, your entire life to selfless service to the arts, augmenting the great traditions of Russian ballet and educating young artists. Your multifarious creative and mentoring activities are a vivid example of the highest professionalism and dedication to the lifelong career you chose.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: January 2, 2024, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73208 Text version
