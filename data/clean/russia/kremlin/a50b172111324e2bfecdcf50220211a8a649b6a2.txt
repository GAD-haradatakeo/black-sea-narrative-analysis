Events Congratulations to Gennady Zyuganov Vladimir Putin sent congratulations to Chairman of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation Gennady Zyuganov on the occasion of his 80 th birthday. June 26, 2024 21:30 The message reads, in part: “You are known as an experienced politician and an honest and principled person devoted to the interests of the Fatherland. You remain immersed in the country’s public life striving to uphold the principles of social justice, making a weighty contribution to the legislative work and Russian parliamentarism, and addressing matters of national importance. In particular, I would like to recognise your efforts designed to improve people’s well-being and strengthen our country‘s sovereignty and positions internationally. Such multifaceted and much-needed activities deserve profound respect. I wish you good health, every success in implementing your plans, and all the best. Once again, please accept my heartfelt congratulations on being awarded the high title of Hero of Labour of the Russian Federation.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 26, 2024, 21:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74424 Text version
