Events Telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan, at the Armenian side’s initiative. March 13, 2023 12:35 The leaders discussed practical aspects of implementing the entire scope of agreements reached by the leaders of Russia, Armenia and Azerbaijan in 2020–2022, including steps to ensure stability and security on the Armenia-Azerbaijan border, restore economic and transport links in the region, and prepare a peace agreement between Armenia and Azerbaijan. Discussing the current situation around Nagorno-Karabakh, Vladimir Putin stressed the importance of resolving all emerging issues constructively, in close contact and through interaction between the parties and Russian peacekeepers.
