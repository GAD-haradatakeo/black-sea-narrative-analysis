Events Condolences to King of Morocco Mohammed VI Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to King of Morocco Mohammed VI over the tragic consequence of a devastating earthquake in the country’s central districts. September 9, 2023 10:30 The message reads, in part: “Russia shares the sadness and grief of the friendly people of Morocco. Please pass on my deepest condolences and words of support to the victims’ families and friends and wishes for speedy recovery to all those affected by this disaster.” Geography Morocco Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 9, 2023, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72234 Text version
