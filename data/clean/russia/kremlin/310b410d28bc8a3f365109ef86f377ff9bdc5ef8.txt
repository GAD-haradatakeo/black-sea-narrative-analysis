Events Greetings to current and retired editorial staff of Kultura newspaper Vladimir Putin congratulated the current and retired members of the editorial staff of the Kultura newspaper on the 95 th anniversary of its first issue. November 6, 2024 15:00 The message reads, in part: “You can be justly proud of the history of one of the oldest domestic periodicals which has always been in the midst of the country's social and cultural life, enjoyed the readers’ unwavering attention and introduced them to the most significant events in the world of theatre, cinema, art, original folk art and folklore. It is gratifying that today the newspaper continues to employ talented, enthusiastic people, dedicated professionals. Relying on the experience and traditions of your predecessors, you implement information, educational and publishing projects, striving to be in sync with the times.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 6, 2024, 15:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75511 Text version
