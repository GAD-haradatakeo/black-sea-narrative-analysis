Events Greetings on the opening of the 13th Russian National Summer Rural Games Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants and guests of the 13 th Russian National Summer Rural Games. July 27, 2022 16:00 The message reads, in part: “Today the Omsk Region is giving a hospitable reception to a large group of participants in this prestigious competition. It is a matter of honour for athletes from many regions of the country to take part in this major event, to prove their worth and to demonstrate their talents and skills. The games, which bring together our agricultural workers, people of many age groups and professions, enjoy well-deserved popularity, and are a significant and captivating event for our rural areas. It is notable that these competitions preserve their traditions, continue to build up their creative and constructive potential, are promoting the popularity of physical fitness values and are helping strengthen personal contacts among people.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 27, 2022, 16:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69068 Text version
