Events Greetings to Alexander Pankratov-Chyorny Vladimir Putin wished a happy 75 th birthday to actor, film director and People’s Artist of the Russian Federation Alexander Pankratov-Chyorny. June 28, 2024 14:30 The message reads in part: “You rightfully enjoy the sincere admiration from cinema enthusiasts who deeply appreciate your unique, vibrant talent, charm, artistic temperament and a wonderful sense of humour. I also commend your numerous educational and cultural projects, driven by your energy and commitment to uniting colleagues in the creative industry. I wish you good health and continued success in your acting, directing and literary pursuits.” Alexander Pankratov-Chyorny is one of the most popular Soviet and Russian actors, with a legacy encompassing over 100 films and television series, as well as poetic works.
