Events Condolences to President of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev Vladimir Putin expressed his condolences to President of the Republic of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev over the tragic consequences of a coal mine fire in the Karaganda Region. October 28, 2023 12:35 The message reads, in part: “Please convey my words of sympathy and support to the families of the victims and wishes for a speedy recovery to all those injured. We hope the miners that remain underground will be rescued.” Geography Kazakhstan Topics Foreign policy Persons Tokayev Kassym-Jomart Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 28, 2023, 12:35 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72617 Text version
