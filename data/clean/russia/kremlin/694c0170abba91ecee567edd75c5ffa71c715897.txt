Events Telephone conversation with Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Crown Prince and Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud, at the Saudi side’s initiative. June 27, 2023 12:50 In the wake of the June 24 events in Russia, Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud Al Saud Mohammed bin Salman Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia expressed his support of the measures taken by the Russian leadership to protect the constitutional order, lives and security of people. During the conversation, some issues of further development of mutually beneficial bilateral cooperation were also touched upon. Vladimir Putin asked the Crown Prince to convey his greetings on Eid al-Adha to the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Salman Al Saud Salman bin Abdulaziz King of Saudi Arabia and the entire Saudi nation. The parties agreed to maintain contacts.
