Events Greetings on the 50th anniversary of the Federal Service for Technical and Export Control of Russia Vladimir Putin congratulated the current staff and veterans of the Federal Service for Technical and Export Control (FSTEC) on its 50 th anniversary. December 18, 2023 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the past years, FSTEC has gained solid experience, significantly strengthened its potential, and made a huge contribution to ensuring the national security of Russia. The importance of the tasks facing the agency requires each of you to have deep, comprehensive knowledge and a responsible approach to the matter, while meticulously carrying out your professional duties. After all, your area of competence includes serious, multifaceted issues related to the implementation of export control and the protection of information containing state secrets. Let me emphasise that today, the importance of your Service is steadily increasing in the context of global challenges, the increasing level of society’s informatisation, and the development of digitalisation.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 18, 2023, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73016 Text version
