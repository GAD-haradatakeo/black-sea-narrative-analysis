Events Greetings to the participants of events dedicated to the start of the Russian Student Teams’ anniversary year Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants of events dedicated to the start of the Russian Student Teams’ anniversary year and congratulated them on the movement’s 65 th anniversary. February 17, 2024 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Today tens of thousands of people from various professions, ages and interests are marking this anniversary together with you. Over the past years, student team members have made a significant contribution to Russia’s socioeconomic development and have addressed key national tasks through their creative and selfless work. It is important for today’s generation of Russian students to uphold the wonderful traditions of the past and explore new horizons for their activities. You are needed where challenges are tough, where the enthusiasm and determination of youth, passion and excitement are essential. The energy and talents of student workers are valuable at industrial enterprises and in agriculture, housing and utilities services, construction and transportation sectors, social institutions, the hospitality industry, and in the implementation of other significant projects. I would like to highlight your efforts to take care of military graves and memorials, restore the infrastructure of new regions of Russia, and support the families of participants in the special military operation. I am confident that the forum will be held at a high organisational level, give a start to interesting ideas and initiatives, showcase the achievements of Russian student teams to delegates, and contribute to the resolution of our national task: to create ample opportunities for self-realisation, professional growth and success.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 17, 2024, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73482 Text version
