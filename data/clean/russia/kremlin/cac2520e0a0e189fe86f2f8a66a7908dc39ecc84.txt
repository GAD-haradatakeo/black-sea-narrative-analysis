Events Visit to the Chelyabinsk Forge-and-Press Plant During his working trip to the Urals Federal District, Vladimir Putin visited the Robotics Factory at the Chelyabinsk Forge-and-Press Plant. February 16, 2024 14:00 Chelyabinsk The President toured the plant that produces and assembles six-axis industrial robotic manipulators, and visited an exhibit of import-substitution parts for industrial robots, as well as samples of civilian products. Plant Director Andrei Gartung conducted the tour and told the President about the personnel training system for the plant and the industry. The Chelyabinsk Forge-and-Press Plant is one of the leading machine manufacturers. Founded in 1942, it produces parts for the auto industry, tractors, railway equipment and the oil-and-gas sector. The plant employs over 18,000 people and carries out an intensive training programme for manual workers and engineers in cooperation with secondary vocational and higher education institutions.
