Events Birthday greetings to Leonid Roshal Vladimir Putin wished a happy 90th birthday to Leonid Roshal, President of the Research Institute for Emergency Children's Surgery and Traumatology of the Moscow Health Department. April 27, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “A talented scientist, clinical surgeon and a prominent healthcare manager, you have come a long and eventful professional path. You have achieved success in your important, demanding, truly noble and compassionate work. For many years, you have honestly served medicine and society. More than that, I must specifically praise your significant public and educational activities, and your sincere concern for the preservation and continuation of the richest traditions of Russian medicine.” Topics Children Healthcare Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 27, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71000 Text version
