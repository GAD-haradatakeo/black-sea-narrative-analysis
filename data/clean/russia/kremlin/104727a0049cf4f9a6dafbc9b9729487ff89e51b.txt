Events Congratulations to Nicolas Maduro on his re-election as President of Venezuela Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to Nicolas Maduro on the occasion of his re-election as President of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela. July 29, 2024 12:00 The message reads, in part: “Russia-Venezuela relations are a strategic partnership. I am confident that your actions as the head of state will further contribute to the steady development of this partnership in all areas. This is fully aligned with the interests of our friendly nations and follows the goal of building a more just and democratic world order. I would like to reaffirm our commitment to continue our constructive cooperation on important bilateral and international matters. Please remember that you are always a welcome guest on Russian soil.” Geography Venezuela Topics Foreign policy Persons Maduro Nicolas Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 29, 2024, 12:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74660 Text version
