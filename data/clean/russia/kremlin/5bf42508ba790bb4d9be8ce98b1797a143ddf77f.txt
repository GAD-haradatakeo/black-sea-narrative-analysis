Events Greetings to the ProyeKTOriya national career guidance forum Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to participants in the ProyeKTOriya national career guidance forum, which opened in Yaroslavl. September 10, 2024 11:30 The message reads, in part: “Since its commencement in 2013, your forum has rightfully become one of the most successful educational projects focused on helping children and young people develop project management skills. Schoolchildren and college students use your platforms to become acquainted with modern professions, try their hand at modern in-demand jobs, and choose a profession where their personal and creative potential can be realised in the best possible way. Participation in your forum is a wonderful opportunity to show one’s worth and learn to work in a team. Of course, the bold and unusual ideas and initiatives presented at the forum will be supported for implementation by the leading Russian companies. It is gratifying that you, as part of the younger generation, are trying to contribute to the development of our country. All roads are open to you. You can opt for science and technology, create your own education and social platforms, or devote your life to creative endeavour, public or voluntary service. I would like to use this occasion to thank your teachers, mentors, parents and everyone ese who share your interests and helps you forge onwards and achieve strong results.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 10, 2024, 11:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75081 Text version
