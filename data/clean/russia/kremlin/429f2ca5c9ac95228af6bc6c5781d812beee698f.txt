Events Greetings on the opening of the Primordial Russia national nature festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants and guests of the Primordial Russia national nature festival. February 17, 2023 12:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the past years, your festival has vastly expanded its creative horizons and is by right regarded today as a major and eagerly awaited event in this country’s cultural life as well as a prestigious expert forum for discussing current environmental problems. The festival events invariably attract the interested attention of Moscow residents and visitors. They serve important educational goals and instil a caring and responsible attitude towards the environment and Russia’s invaluable natural riches. I am confident that this year’s anniversary festival will be a success, rally scientists, environmentalists, travellers and young naturalists around its noble goals, and present the best works by Russian photographers in a wide range of genres, trends and artistic devices at individual and group exhibitions.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 17, 2023, 12:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70531 Text version
