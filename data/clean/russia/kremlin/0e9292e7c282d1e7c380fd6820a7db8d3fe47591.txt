Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council Vladimir Putin held a briefing session with permanent members of the Security Council, via videoconference. January 20, 2023 14:10 Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region The meeting participants exchanged opinions on the progress of the special military operation. Defence Minister Sergei Shoigu Shoigu Sergei Defence Minister spoke about his visit to the special military operation headquarters earlier this week. The President talked about his recent international telephone conversations. Current issues on the socio-economic agenda were also discussed.
