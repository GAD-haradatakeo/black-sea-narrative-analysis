Events Greetings on Construction Worker’s Day Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to current and former builders on Construction Worker’s Day. August 13, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “Russia’s construction industry has always had highly qualified and dedicated professionals. Due to their effort and talent, cities and towns have been erected, as well as industrial and energy facilities, social and transport infrastructure. It is gratifying to see you carry on the great traditions of building and creating. And today, despite the current challenges, the construction industry continues to perform effectively. A definite national economy driver, construction largely ensures the stability and resilience of the labour market. Thanks to the widespread introduction of advanced technologies and engineering innovations, as well as competent management decisions, the volume of housing construction is growing, and large infrastructure projects are being successfully implemented in various regions of Russia. And I must certainly highlight Russian builders’ immense and selfless work to restore the Donetsk and Lugansk people’s republics, and the Kherson and Zaporozhye regions.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 13, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72026 Text version
