Events Visit to Tula Region Governor’s Situation Centre The President, accompanied by Tula Region Governor Alexei Dyumin, learned about the work of the Tula Region Situation Centre. December 23, 2022 22:25 Tula Director of the complex Alexei Gordeyev briefed Vladimir Putin on the centre, which is set to receive real-time data on the socioeconomic situation and security in the region. The full cycle of data analysis created here, ranging from planning to execution control, makes the centre a modern operating tool for continuous management of the constituent entity, makes it possible to build efficient cooperation between authorities at all levels, which reduces the response time to incidents in the region as well as to inquiries from its residents. The project of the centre has been implemented as per the instruction of the President of Russia.
