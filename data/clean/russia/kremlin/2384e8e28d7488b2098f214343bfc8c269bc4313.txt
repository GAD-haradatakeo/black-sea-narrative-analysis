Events Visit to Agro 07 horticultural farm Vladimir Putin visited the Agro 07 horticultural farm in the village of Germenchik in Kabardino-Balkaria. August 20, 2024 12:30 Germenchik village Established in 2007, Agro 07 specialises in the cultivation and processing of fruits and vegetables, primarily apples. The company’s gardens are located in two municipal districts of Kabardino-Balkaria spanning about 200 hectares. The farm also owns state-of-the-art fruit storage facilities. In 2020, it expanded its operations to include the cultivation of blueberries and also produces natural sugar-free fruit leather. During the visit, the President is being accompanied by Head of the Kabardino-Balkarian Republic Kazbek Kokov Kokov Kazbek Head of Kabardino-Balkarian Republic , Presidential Plenipotentiary Envoy to the North Caucasus Federal District Yury Chaika Chaika Yury Presidential Plenipotentiary Envoy to the North Caucasus Federal District , and Minister of Agriculture Oksana Lut Lut Oksana Minister of Agriculture of the Russian Federation . Agro 07 founder Rashid Tumov gave the visitors a tour of the farm.
