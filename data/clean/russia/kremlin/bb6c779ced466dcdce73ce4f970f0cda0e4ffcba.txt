Events Mikhail Kotyukov appointed Acting Governor of Krasnoyarsk Territory The President signed Executive Order On the Acting Governor of the Krasnoyarsk Territory . April 20, 2023 21:30 Following the request from Krasnoyarsk Territory Governor Alexander Uss to resign ahead of term, the President resolved to accept his voluntary resignation and appoint Mikhail Kotyukov Acting Governor of the Krasnoyarsk Territory until the elected Governor of the region takes office.
