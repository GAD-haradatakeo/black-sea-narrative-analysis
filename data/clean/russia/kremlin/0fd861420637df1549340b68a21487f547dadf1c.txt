Events Greetings to Patriarch Kirill of Moscow and All Russia Vladimir Putin sent birthday greetings to Patriarch Kirill of Moscow and All Russia. November 20, 2022 19:00 The message reads in part: ”As the Primate of the Russian Orthodox Church, you direct your efforts towards preserving the traditional spiritual and moral values in society, harmonising interfaith and interethnic relations, and improving cooperation between the church and the state. Your wise pastoral word serves to consolidate the nation and supports people at a time of trials.“ Persons Kirill Patriarch of Moscow and All Russia Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 20, 2022, 19:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69873 Text version
