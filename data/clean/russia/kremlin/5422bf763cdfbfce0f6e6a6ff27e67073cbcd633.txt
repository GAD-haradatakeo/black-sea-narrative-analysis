Events Greetings to Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva on winning presidential election in Brazil Vladimir Putin sent a message to congratulate Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva on winning the presidential election in Brazil. October 31, 2022 08:35 The message reads, in part: “The result of the elections has reaffirmed your high political prestige. I hope that by working together we will ensure the further development of constructive Russian-Brazilian cooperation in all spheres.” Geography Brazil Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 31, 2022, 08:35 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69719 Text version
