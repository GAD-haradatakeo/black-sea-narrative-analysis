Events Congratulations to Anastasia Demurchian on winning the 2023 IBA Women's World Boxing Championships Vladimir Putin congratulated Anastasia Demurchian on winning the light middleweight category at the 2023 IBA Women's World Boxing Championships in New Delhi. March 27, 2023 18:00 The message reads, in part: “You displayed outstanding leadership qualities, skills and determination by overcoming powerful and experienced rivals at the World Championships in New Delhi. You reaffirmed with your triumph the strong, solid positions of the Russian school of boxing.” Topics Sport Publication status Published in sections: News , Commissions and Councils , Council for the Development of Physical Culture and Sport Publication date: March 27, 2023, 18:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70782 Text version
