Events Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Alexandra Titarenko The President spoke by telephone with eight-year-old Alexandra Titarenko from the Zaporozhye Region, participant in the New Year Tree of Wishes national charity campaign. December 27, 2022 13:55 Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Alexandra Titarenko, participant in the New Year Tree of Wishes national charity campaign. Photo: Alexei Danichev, RIA Novosti On December 22, during his visit to the Youth Centre at the Manezh Central Exhibition Hall, Vladimir Putin took part in the New Year Tree of Wishes campaign for the fifth time – he chose three postcards shaped like New Year Tree baubles where children wrote about their dreams. The President fulfilled Alexandra’s wish to visit Father Frost. The girl, together with her parents, flew to Veliky Ustyug, toured the city, and met with the New Year’s magical gift bringer. Alexandra told the President about her impressions of the trip.
