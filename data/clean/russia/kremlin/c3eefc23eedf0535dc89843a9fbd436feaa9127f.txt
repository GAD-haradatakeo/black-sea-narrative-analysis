Events Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Taisia Leonova The President called 12-year-old Taisiya Leonova from the Moscow Region, a participant in the charity event the New Year Tree of Wishes. December 26, 2024 13:15 Taisia, a 7th-grade student, has been practicing folk dancing since she was six. She dreamed of attending a rehearsal of the Igor Moiseyev Folk Dance Ensemble, and Vladimir Putin made that dream come true. Accompanied by her mother and brother, Taisia visited the Igor Moiseyev Ensemble’s building, where they met with the ensemble’s artistic director, Yelena Shcherbakova, a student of Igor Moiseyev. The girl toured the theatre, including Igor Moiseyev’s office, and attended a master class with ballet dancers as well as a rehearsal of the ensemble. The President extended his New Year greetings to Taisia and her family. * * * On December 5, during the #WeAreTogether international awards ceremony, Vladimir Putin once again joined the New Year Tree of Wishes nationwide charity campaign and picked three ornaments from the tree with wishes from the children who found themselves in a challenging life situation.
