Events Telephone conversation with President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev. November 30, 2022 15:10 The presidents discussed topical issues of trade and economic cooperation, including the implementation of promising joint projects. All of them will be examined during the upcoming meeting of the Russia-Uzbekistan Joint Commission at the level of heads of government in Samarkand scheduled for December 1–2.
