Events Greetings to President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping Vladimir Putin sent greetings to President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping on the 73 rd anniversary of the People’s Republic of China. October 1, 2022 09:00 The message reads, in part: “China has achieved widely recognised success in the socioeconomic sphere, science and technology, as well as in many other fields. Your country enjoys well-deserved authority and prestige on the international scene and plays an important and constructive role in addressing topical issues of the regional and global agenda. Russian-Chinese relations are developing dynamically in the spirit of all-round partnership and strategic interaction. Despite the complicated international situation, we are cooperating successfully in the most diverse areas, pooling our efforts to build a more democratic and equitable world order and to counter modern threats and challenges. I would like to reaffirm our readiness to continue our dialogue and close joint work for the benefit of the friendly nations of the Russian Federation and the People’s Republic of China. I sincerely wish you good health, prosperity and, of course, every success in holding the upcoming 20 th Congress of the Communist Party of China, and happiness and prosperity to all Chinese citizens.” Geography People's Republic of China Topics Foreign policy Persons Xi Jinping Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 1, 2022, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69475 Text version
