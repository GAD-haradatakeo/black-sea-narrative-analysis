Events Visit to Church of Life-Giving Trinity in Pyongyang Following his talks with Kim Jong-un, Vladimir Putin visited the Church of the Life-Giving Trinity in Pyongyang. June 19, 2024 16:40 Pyongyang Rector of the church, Priest Vladimir (Jang Myung Il), told the President of Russia about the construction of the church and held a short prayer service. Vladimir Putin donated The Trinity icon to the church. The construction of the first Orthodox church in the capital of the DPRK was initiated by personal order of Kim Jong Il after his visit to the Church of St Innocent of Irkutsk in Khabarovsk in 2002. The church was erected in 2006 and was consecrated by Metropolitan Kirill of Smolensk and Kaliningrad, now the Patriarch of Moscow and All Russia. During his visit, Metropolitan Kirill consecrated priests who graduated from the Moscow Theological Seminary: deacons Feodor Kim and John Ra.
