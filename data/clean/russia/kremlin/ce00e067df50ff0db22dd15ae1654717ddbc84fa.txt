Events Greetings on 100th anniversary of the All-Russian Society for the Protection of Nature Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants, organisers and guests of celebrations marking the 100 th anniversary of the All-Russian Society for the protection of Nature. November 30, 2024 11:30 The message reads, in part: “Over the years, the All-Russian Society for the Protection of Nature has evolved into a prominent and respected public organisation. Today, it unites within its ranks and rallies around its noble goals representatives of various professions, ages, and generations, including prominent scientists, researchers, volunteers, school and university students, and entire teams. Your endeavours are directed at preserving our natural treasures through the implementation of extensive environmental, scientific, and outreach projects, as well as educational programmes for children and youth. I would like to particularly highlight the Society’s much-needed initiatives that contribute significantly to the preservation of our reserve areas, unique water resources, and biodiversity of ecosystems.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 30, 2024, 11:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75702 Text version
