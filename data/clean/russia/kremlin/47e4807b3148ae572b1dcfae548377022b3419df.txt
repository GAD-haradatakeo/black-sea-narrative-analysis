Events On June 29, Vladimir Putin will be in Ashgabat for sixth Caspian Summit Announcement, June 29, 2022 The presidents of Azerbaijan, Iran, Kazakhstan, Russia and Turkmenistan will address the current issues of cooperation in the Caspian Sea in various areas and discuss the implementation of the decisions taken at previous meetings of the heads of the Caspian Five states. Vladimir Putin will hold several bilateral meetings during the summit.
