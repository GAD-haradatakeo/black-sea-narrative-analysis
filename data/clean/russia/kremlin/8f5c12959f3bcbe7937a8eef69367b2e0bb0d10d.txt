Events Greetings to the Channel One Russia Worldwide Network team Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the Channel One Russia Worldwide Network team on the 25 th anniversary of its international broadcasting. September 27, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “The launch of Channel One broadcasting in Europe was an important and relevant initiative. Later, broadcasting in Russian was extended to other regions on the planet. This large-scale high-technology information project came into its own largely due to the professionalism of your staff, their creative attitude to work, and their adherence to the traditions of journalism. Today, Channel One Russia Worldwide Network is a powerful media resource with a vast audience all over the world, representing all nations, age groups, and interests. Additionally, I would like to highlight your team’s contribution to advancing Russian digital broadcasting, training TV personnel, and creating modern, high-quality TV shows for the rising generation. It is gratifying that you remain proactive in shaping the global information agenda and putting into practice promising ideas that resonate with partners and TV audiences.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 27, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75191 Text version
