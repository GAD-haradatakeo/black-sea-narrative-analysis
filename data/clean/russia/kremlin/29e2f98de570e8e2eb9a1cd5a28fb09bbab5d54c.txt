Events Birthday greetings to figure skating coach Tatyana Tarasova Vladimir Putin sent birthday greetings to figure skating coach Tatyana Tarasova. February 13, 2022 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the years of your successful coaching career, you have written unique and truly triumphant chapters into the history of Russian and world figure skating. In no small measure, it is thanks to your professionalism, talent, impeccable artistic taste and the victories of your brilliant students that this spectacular and dynamic sport has gained so much love and popularity in our country.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 13, 2022, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/67769 Text version
