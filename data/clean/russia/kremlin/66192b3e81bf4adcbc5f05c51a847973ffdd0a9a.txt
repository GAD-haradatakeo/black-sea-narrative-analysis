Events Greetings to the Third International Pacific Theatre Festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the guests, participants and organisers of the Third International Pacific Theatre Festival, which is taking place in Vladivostok from September 20 to October 6. September 20, 2024 12:00 The message reads, in part: “Your festival is rightfully considered a big and truly festive event in the cultural life of the Russian Far East and the entire country. By bringing together outstanding and talented representatives of creative professions from Russia and Argentina, Armenia and Belarus, Brazil and Germany, India and Canada, China and Japan on its stages, the festival creates a unique and inspiring atmosphere, allowing residents and guests of the Primorye capital to enjoy high art. In addition, the festival plays a major role in promoting international humanitarian cooperation, contributes to the successful implementation of joint projects, and strengthens trust and mutual understanding between people.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 20, 2024, 12:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75147 Text version
