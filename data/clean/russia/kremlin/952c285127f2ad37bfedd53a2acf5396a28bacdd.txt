Events Greetings to the IV Productivity 360 Federal Forum Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants of the IV Productivity 360 Federal Forum. October 14, 2022 14:00 The message reads, in part: “Your forum, which has brought together in Sochi CEOs of major companies, leading experts and government officials, is rightly considered a reputable platform for discussing a broad range of issues related to the implementation of the Labour Productivity national project. I would like to emphasise that higher labour productivity is a key factor of economic growth and sustainable development of businesses. Today, we will expedite the modernisation of all industry sectors, agriculture and the social sphere, resolve major import substitution problems, train skilled personnel and create new jobs with decent pay. I would like to note that we must address all these tasks at the advanced scientific, technological and educational level and with due account for the best domestic and global practices. This is why the consolidated efforts of all participants in the Labour Productivity national project are so important. I am referring to the state, businesses, research centres and all levels of vocational education.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 14, 2022, 14:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69608 Text version
