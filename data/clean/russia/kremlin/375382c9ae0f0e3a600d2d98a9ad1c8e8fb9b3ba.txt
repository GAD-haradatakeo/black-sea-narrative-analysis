Events Greetings to Vahagn Khachaturyan on being elected President of Armenia Vladimir Putin sent a message of congratulations to Vahagn Khachaturyan on being elected President of Armenia March 3, 2022 16:10 “I am certain that your activities in this high office will help further develop Russian-Armenian relations of alliance and promote mutually beneficial Eurasian integration processes for the prosperity of our fraternal peoples in the interest of strengthening regional security and stability,” the head of the Russian state noted in his message.
