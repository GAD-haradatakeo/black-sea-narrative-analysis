Events On April 19, Vladimir Putin will hold talks with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan On April 19, Vladimir Putin will hold talks with Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan, who will arrive in Russia on an official visit. Announcement, April 19, 2022 Add to Calendar Calendars iCal Google The discussion is to cover current issues pertaining to the further development of Russian-Armenian relations of strategic partnership and alliance. The two leaders will also consider the implementation of the agreements on Nagorno-Karabakh, reached by the leaders of Russia, Armenia and Azerbaijan on November 9, 2020 , and on January 11 and November 26, 2021, including measures to restore economic and transport ties in the region.
