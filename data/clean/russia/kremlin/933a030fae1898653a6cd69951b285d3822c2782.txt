Events Greetings on Knowledge Day with the Stars at the Kremlin gala concert Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants and guests of Knowledge Day with the Stars at the Kremlin gala concert to mark the start of the academic year. August 31, 2023 19:00 The message reads, in part: “It is gratifying that your project is progressing successfully, enriching itself with amazing ideas and initiatives, involving new participants, and for the past five years, deservedly recognised as one of the most striking cultural events dedicated to the beginning of the academic year. And of course, I would especially note the significant charitable initiatives of the festival organisers. Today, well-known pop stars and young talented singers from all over Russia, who have withstood worthy competition and won the MuzLeto national youth marathon, will again appear on the stage of the State Kremlin Palace. I know that this concert will be held, as always, on a high creative level, give everyone good, unforgettable feelings, and become a real holiday for all those present.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 31, 2023, 19:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72165 Text version
