Events Greetings to participants in the 22nd Likhachev International Scientific Readings Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants in the 22 nd Likhachev International Scientific Readings. April 12, 2024 10:30 The message reads, in part: “Your meetings in St Petersburg each year bring together renowned researchers, cultural figures, politicians and diplomats from various countries, offering an excellent platform for meaningful and constructive discussions. Furthermore, the agenda of the readings consistently encompasses a diverse array of topics related to the main trends in the development of contemporary society, spanning both national and international issues. The current readings focus on the position and significance of BRICS on the global stage. This theme holds particular relevance, given Russia’s chairmanship in this reputable and influential group of countries. As chair, Russia facilitates collaborative efforts among the participating states across various areas, based on the principles of equality, respect, and consideration for each other’s interests. I hope that you will engage in a comprehensive discussion on the future trajectory of BRICS at the highest expert level. Your ideas and initiatives will contribute to shaping innovative forms and areas of mutually beneficial multilateral cooperation that serve the interests of our countries and citizens, contributing to the establishment of a fairer, safer, and more prosperous international order.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 12, 2024, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73857 Text version
