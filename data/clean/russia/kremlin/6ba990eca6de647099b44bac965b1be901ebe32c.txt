Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council The President held a briefing session on current issues with permanent members of the Security Council, via videoconference. February 8, 2023 23:15 The Kremlin, Moscow The discussion focused, in part, on infrastructure development and measures to improve fire safety and reduce the risk of emergencies during the flood season.
