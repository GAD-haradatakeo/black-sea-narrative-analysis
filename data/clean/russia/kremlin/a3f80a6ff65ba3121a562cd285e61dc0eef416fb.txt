Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council The President held a briefing session, via videoconference, with permanent members of the Security Council. April 15, 2022 13:15 Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region In addition to current matters, the participants held a planned discussion of Russia’s humanitarian policy concept.
