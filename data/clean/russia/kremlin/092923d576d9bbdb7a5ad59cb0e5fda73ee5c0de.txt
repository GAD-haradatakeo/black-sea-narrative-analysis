Events Birthday greetings to Zurab Tsereteli, sculptor, People’s Artist of the USSR, and president of the Russian Academy of Arts Vladimir Putin extended his greetings to Zurab Tsereteli, sculptor, People’s Artist of the USSR, and president of the Russian Academy of Arts, on his 90 th birthday. January 4, 2024 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Please accept my best wishes on your 90th birthday. Throughout the years, your tireless creative endeavours have truly showcased your abundant, versatile and unique talents, earning you widespread recognition as a professional. I would like to highlight your significant public, educational and organisational initiatives, as well as your sincere commitment to serving for the benefit of our national culture.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: January 4, 2024, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73220 Text version
