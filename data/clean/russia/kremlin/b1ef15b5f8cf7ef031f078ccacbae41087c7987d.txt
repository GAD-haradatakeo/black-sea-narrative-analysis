Events Greetings on the opening of the tenth Trans-Siberian Art Festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the tenth anniversary Trans-Siberian Art Festival. March 8, 2023 13:00 The message reads, in part: “It is gratifying that, thanks to outstanding violinist Vadim Repin’s inspirational idea and creative energy, this wonderful festival brings out new brilliant ideas and concepts every year. This is the tenth time that residents and visitors of Russian cities have the incredible opportunity to hear from acclaimed masters and talented young musicians, composers and artists from different countries. It goes without saying that the festival faithfully carries out its noble mission, which is to help preserve and develop the great traditions of Russian culture, strengthening trust and mutual understanding between people.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: March 8, 2023, 13:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70654 Text version
