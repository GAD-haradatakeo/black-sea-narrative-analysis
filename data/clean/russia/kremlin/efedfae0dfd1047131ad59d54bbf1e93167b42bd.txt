Events Birthday greetings to journalist, TV and radio presenter Vladimir Solovyov Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to journalist and TV and radio presenter Vladimir Solovyov on his 60th birthday. October 20, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “You have achieved success in many creative career fields that require deep and versatile knowledge, energy and passion. Your accomplished and exceptional shows on radio and television, as well as other meaningful projects and undertakings, always arouse high, genuine interest.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 20, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72564 Text version
