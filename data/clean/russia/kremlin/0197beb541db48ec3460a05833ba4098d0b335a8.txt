Events Greetings to Za Rulyom magazine staff Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the staff of the Za Rulyom (Behind the Wheel) magazine on its 95th anniversary. March 15, 2023 22:00 The message reads, in part: “I wish you all the best on this wonderful occasion, the 95th anniversary of the legendary magazine, Za Rulyom . Founded in 1928, it has brought together motor vehicle enthusiasts across the country and has grown into one of the largest and most respected specialised media outlets. The vivid articles written by its talented team and friendly writers contributed to the development of the motor transport industry, the promotion of a driver code of behaviour and road safety. As in past years, the current generation of its readers appreciate the magazine for its respectful and caring attitude to traditions, for current news and analytics, interesting reports and interviews. And of course, it is important that you are effectively introducing modern technologies, engaging the widest audience, internet users and social media, with the automotive agenda.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: March 15, 2023, 22:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70687 Text version
