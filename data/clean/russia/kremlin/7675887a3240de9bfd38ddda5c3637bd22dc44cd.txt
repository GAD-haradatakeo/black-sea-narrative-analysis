Events Greetings to participants and organisers of the Russian Geographical Society's 10th international educational campaign Geographical Dictation Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants and organisers of the Russian Geographical Society's 10 th international educational campaign Geographical Dictation. November 16, 2024 00:00 The message reads, in part: ”I extend my welcome to you at the commencement of the Geographical Dictation, which the Russian Geographical Society is organising for the tenth time. Your unique important project, which unites over three and a half million people across numerous nations, comprising school and university students, professionals from diverse fields, and the older generation, entire families, is rightfully considered one of the most comprehensive educational and awareness-building initiatives in the field of geography. It is indeed heartening to observe the active participation of the youth. Geography, as a pivotal scientific discipline, unveils a vast expanse of knowledge about our planet, shapes the worldview and perspective of young people, and nurtures a profound affection for our nation and homeland. I express my sincerest gratitude to the organisers of this event – the Russian Geographical Society and the extensive, tightly-knit team of volunteers – whose efforts ensure that the dictation is conducted at a high creative level, fostering an atmosphere of camaraderie and innovation, and leaving a lasting impression on all participants. I wish you every success and all the best.“ * * * The Geographical Dictation is an international educational campaign that has been held annually since 2015. The primary goal of the campaign is not to evaluate geographical literacy but to promote geographical knowledge and enhance interest in science and national heritage. On November 16–17, 2024, the tenth Geographical Dictation international educational project will take place at 11,003 venues in Russia and over 110 foreign countries.
