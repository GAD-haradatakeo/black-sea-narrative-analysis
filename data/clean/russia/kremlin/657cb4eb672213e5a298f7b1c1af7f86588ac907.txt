Events Birthday greetings to Dmitry Kiselev Vladimir Putin sent greetings on the 70 th birthday to Russian journalist and TV host, Director General of the Rossiya Segodnya International Information Agency Dmitry Kiselev. April 26, 2024 14:30 A message of greetings reads, in part: “Your responsible civic position and ability to work creatively and with full dedication for many years are helping you to remain in demand in your profession, successfully run one of the largest news agencies in the country and the world, and implement meaningful news and educational projects. Such great, productive work is worthy of the deepest recognition.” Topics Media Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 26, 2024, 14:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73947 Text version
