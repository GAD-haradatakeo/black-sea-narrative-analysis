Events Birthday greetings to Sergei Skripka Vladimir Putin sent birthday greetings to artistic director and principal conductor of the Russian State Symphony Cinema Orchestra Sergei Skripka on his 75 th birthday. October 5, 2024 10:40 The message reads, in part: “You have dedicated your life to selflessly serving the art and made a tremendous contribution to the preservation and enhancement of the traditions of the Russian school of conducting and the overall national culture. I am pleased to see that you continue working today as head of the Russian State Symphony Cinema Orchestra, guiding young talents through their careers and doing a great deal to promote our abundant musical heritage.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 5, 2024, 10:40 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75273 Text version
