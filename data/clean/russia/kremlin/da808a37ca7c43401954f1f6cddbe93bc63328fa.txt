Events Greetings to the national schoolchildren team at the 35th International Biology Olympiad in Astana Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to Russia’s national team of schoolchildren that took part in the 35 th International Biology Olympiad in Astana, Republic of Kazakhstan: Vitaly Gagarochkin, Vsevolod Maslenikov, Boris Mikhailov, and Maria Yaryshkina. July 14, 2024 15:00 The message reads, in part: “All members of your close-knit and united team showed deep and fundamental knowledge, determined character and the will to win in the fair and intense competitions. I am confident that these notable qualities will help you achieve your goals both in studies and life in the future. Of course, special words of gratitude must be extended to your teachers, mentors, parents, and friends, who share the success you deserved.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 14, 2024, 15:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74565 Text version
