Events Vladimir Putin submitted documents to Central Election Commission December 18, 2023 16:45 Moscow Vladimir Putin visited the Central Election Commission, where he submitted documents to register as a self-nominated candidate for the Russian Federation presidential election. The presidential election will take place on March 15–17, 2024.
