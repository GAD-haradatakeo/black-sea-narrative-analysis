Events Birthday greetings to President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings on the 70 th birthday to President of the Republic of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko. August 30, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “While holding your high office, you have gained a reputation as a true patriot of your country. You persistently promote its interests on the international stage and are a wise politician capable of taking balanced and far-reaching decisions even in the most challenging situations. Your contribution to developing friendly ties between Russia and Belarus, building and strengthening the Union State’s institutions, as well as promoting mutually beneficial integration processes within the Eurasian space cannot be underestimated.
