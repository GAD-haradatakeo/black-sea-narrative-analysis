Events Greetings to Territory of Meanings National Education Youth Forum Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants in the Territory of Meanings National Education Youth Forum, which marks its 10 th anniversary this year. August 1, 2024 20:30 The message reads, in part: “Since its inception, the forum has firmly established itself as a prominent platform and distinguished itself by successfully undertaking youth policy projects. Time and again, it managed to come up with creative organisational solutions, as well as bold and innovative ways of tackling various topics on its agenda. Those who have gathered in Solnechnogorsk, Moscow Region, coming from across Russia and other countries to take part in this forum, will be able to enjoy in-depth conversations and roundtable discussions, exhibitions and lectures, and will also get to meet prominent public figures, media professionals, experts, and business executives. The forum’s rich programme has a lot to offer so that each and every one of you can find a way to demonstrate their talent and receive the support you need for making your ideas and plans come true. Importantly, 2024 is the Year of the Family, which means that the forum will pay special attention to strengthening traditional family, spiritual, and moral values, as well as educating the younger generation.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 1, 2024, 20:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74692 Text version
