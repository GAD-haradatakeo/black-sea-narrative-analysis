Events Visit to joint staff of military branches involved in special military operation December 17, 2022 05:00 The President spent all day on Friday working at the joint staff of military branches involved in the special military operation. The head of state was briefed about the work of the joint staff and on the progress made in the special military operation, held a general meeting and separate meetings with commanders.
