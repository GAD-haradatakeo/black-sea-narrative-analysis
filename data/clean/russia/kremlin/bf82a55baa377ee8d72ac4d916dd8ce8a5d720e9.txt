Events Greetings to President of Mongolia Ukhnaagiin Khurelsukh Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to President of Mongolia Ukhnaagiin Khurelsukh on the 85 th anniversary of victory over Japanese militarists on the Khalkhin Gol River. August 29, 2024 16:50 The message reads, in part: “Our nations revere the memory of the soldiers of the Red Army and the Mongolian People’s Revolutionary Army, who fought shoulder to shoulder against the invaders and defended the independence of Mongolia at the cost of tremendous sacrifices. The good traditions of friendship and mutual support, forged during that cruel war period, are a reliable foundation for the further development of relations of comprehensive strategic partnership between Moscow and Ulaanbaatar.” See also The President will make an official visit to Mongolia on September 3 August 29, 2024 Persons Khurelsukh Ukhnaagiin Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 29, 2024, 16:50 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74959 Text version
