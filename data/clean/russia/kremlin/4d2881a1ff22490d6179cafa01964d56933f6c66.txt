Events Second day of 16th BRICS Summit The 16 th BRICS Summit continued in Kazan. October 23, 2024 20:30 Kazan President of Russia Vladimir Putin, President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping Xi Jinping President of People's Republic of China , President of Egypt Abdel Fattah el-Sisi el-Sisi Abdel Fattah President of the Arab Republic of Egypt , Prime Minister of Ethiopia Abiy Ahmed Ahmed Abiy Prime Minister of Ethiopia , Prime Minister of India Narendra Modi Modi Narendra Prime Minister of India , President of Iran Masoud Pezeshkian Pezeshkian Masoud President of the Islamic Republic of Iran , President of South Africa Cyril Ramaphosa Ramaphosa Cyril President of the Republic of South Africa , President of the United Arab Emirates Mohammed bin Zayed Al Nahyan Al Nahyan Mohammed bin Zayed President of the United Arab Emirates and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Brazil Mauro Luiz Iecker Vieira attended a restricted-format meeting of the BRICS Summit. Before the meeting, the heads of delegations of the BRICS countries posed for an official photograph. The meeting continued in an expanded format, with President of Brazil Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva Lula da Silva Luiz Inacio President of the Federative Republic of Brazil joining it via videoconference. Kazan Declaration has been adopted following the 16 th BRICS Summit. Vladimir Putin held a series of bilateral meetings on the sidelines of the summit. In the evening, the President of Russia hosted a gala reception in honour of the BRICS Summit. The heads of BRICS states and leaders of the countries expressing interest in collaboration with BRICS have been invited to the reception.
