Events On November, 2 Vladimir Putin will hold talks with the President of Equatorial Guinea On November 2, Vladimir Putin will hold talks with the President of the Republic of Equatorial Guinea, Teodoro Obiang Nguema Mbasogo, who is arriving in Moscow on an official visit. Announcement, November 2, 2023 Add to Calendar Calendars iCal Google The presidents are expected to discuss the current state and prospects of bilateral cooperation in various fields, as well as the expansion of Russia’s ties with countries of the Central African region in view of Equatorial Guinea’s chairmanship in the Economic Community of Central African States (ECCAS).
