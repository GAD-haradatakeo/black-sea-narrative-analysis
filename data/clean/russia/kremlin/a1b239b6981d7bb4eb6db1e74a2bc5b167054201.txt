Events The President instructed authorities to provide assistance to victims of fires in the Sverdlovsk Region April 26, 2023 14:00 Vladimir Putin receives regular updates from Emergencies Minister Alexander Kurenkov Kurenkov Alexander Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief on the wildfire situation in the Sverdlovsk Region, the response and relief efforts and measures to preclude the further spread of fire. The President spoke by telephone with Sverdlovsk Region Governor Yevgeny Kuyvashev Kuyvashev Yevgeny Governor of Sverdlovsk Region . Instructions were given to provide social assistance and everything necessary to people who have lost their homes.
