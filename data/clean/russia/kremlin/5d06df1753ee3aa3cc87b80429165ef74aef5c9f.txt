Events Meeting with President of the National Medical Research Centre for Endocrinology Ivan Dedov Vladimir Putin met with Ivan Dedov, President of the National Medical Research Centre for Endocrinology of the Russian Ministry of Healthcare, Chief Visiting Endocrinologist of the Ministry of Healthcare and Academician of the Russian Academy of Sciences. June 23, 2023 17:50 The Kremlin, Moscow The President asked Ivan Dedov to tell him about the work of the centre. Academician Dedov spoke about the endocrinology congress held recently and said that the centre was the first to substantiate a fundamentally new theory of autoimmune disease development in the world literature. He also spoke about the genetic and epigenetic factors that cause diabetes and the progress that has been made in treating it. Vladimir Putin asked about the centre’s administrative development and said that, as agreed, additional funds had been allocated for the construction of a new building.
