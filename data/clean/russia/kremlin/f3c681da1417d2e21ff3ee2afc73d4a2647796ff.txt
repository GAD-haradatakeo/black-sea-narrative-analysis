Events Greetings to organisers, participants and laureates of the 20th Radiant Angel International Charity Film Festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to organisers, participants and prize-winners of the 20 th Radiant Angel International Charity Film Festival, timed to coincide with National Unity Day. November 7, 2023 17:00 The message reads, in part: “It is gratifying that this film festival has once again been held at a high organisational and creative level, convincingly demonstrating its relevance and reputation as a significant event in the social and cultural life of our country and other states. By preserving and developing the unique humanistic and artistic heritage of Russian cinema, it consistently promotes excellent films for the big screen, generously offering audiences a rich palette of vivid, original works that affirm eternal spiritual and moral ideas, the values of family, patriotism and social harmony.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 7, 2023, 17:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72684 Text version
