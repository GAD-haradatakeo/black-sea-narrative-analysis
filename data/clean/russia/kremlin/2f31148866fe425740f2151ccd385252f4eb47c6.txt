Events The President has been informed about natural disasters in various regions November 27, 2023 14:30 This morning, Vladimir Putin heard reports on natural disasters in various regions of Russia including Crimea, Sevastopol, the Krasnodar Territory, and the Far East. The President had a number of telephone conversations, including with Head of the Republic of Crimea Sergei Aksyonov Aksyonov Sergei Governor of the Republic of Crimea , and representatives of various services. Furthermore, the President instructed the Government to pay attention to the situation, decide what kind of assistance was needed in the affected regions, and take relief measures.
