Events Greetings to international conference on 90th anniversary of Chamber of Commerce and Industry International Commercial Arbitration Court at Russian Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants of an international conference marking the 90 th anniversary of the International Commercial Arbitration Court at the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of the Russian Federation. October 27, 2022 10:05 The message reads, in part: “I would like to note that over the past years the International Commercial Arbitration Court has accumulated great experience and is rightfully considered one of the world’s major arbitration centres that hears both international and domestic commercial disputes. Its excellent reputation and demand are largely the result of the fruitful work of Russian and foreign arbitrators, their professionalism, responsibility and dedication. And, of course, I would like to emphasise the significant contribution of the Russian Chamber of Commerce and Industry to the development of arbitration legislation and the creation of a favourable business and investment climate in Russia. I believe that in the future your expertise and firm commitment to the principles of legality and impartiality will help the ICAC to maintain its impeccable reputation.” Topics Court Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 27, 2022, 10:05 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69696 Text version
