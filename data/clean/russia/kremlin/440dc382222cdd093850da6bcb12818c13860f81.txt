Events Greetings to KinoBravo blockbuster film festival Vladimir Putin sent a message to the participants, organisers and guests of the 1 st KinoBravo blockbuster film festival in Sochi. September 28, 2024 13:30 The message reads, in part: “This new and exciting project is a big and impressive event in the cultural life of Sochi and our entire vast country since its participants and jury members include world-famous directors, actors, producers, and film-makers from many countries. Here, on the sidelines of the festival, they will be able to discuss pressing industry problems, exchange professional experience, demonstrate their creative works, communicate in a friendly and informal atmosphere. I am sure that the festival will delight us with a rich competition programme, introduce its guests to the best domestic and foreign films and, of course, will contribute to the development of international humanitarian cooperation and strengthening of people-to-people contacts.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 28, 2024, 13:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75200 Text version
