Events Congratulations to editorial team of Vechernyaya Moskva newspaper Vladimir Putin congratulated the editorial team of the Vechernyaya Moskva newspaper on the 100 th anniversary of its first edition. December 6, 2023 09:30 The message reads, in part: “You have every right to take pride in the outstanding and eventful past of Vechernyaya Moskva, a legendary newspaper, as well as the talented writers, poets, essayists, editors, and correspondents – those who stood at its roots and set its high standards by writing a remarkable chronicle of our capital’s social, cultural and sports affairs. I am glad that today’s team treasures this rich creative legacy inherited from its predecessors and maintains an ongoing dialogue with its devoted audience while catering to the interests and expectations of its readers and helping address the urgent challenges its home city faces. It is for this reason that your newspaper enjoys the recognition it deserves among Moscow residents.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 6, 2023, 09:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72920 Text version
