Events Vladimir Putin arrived in Chechnya The President of Russia, accompanied by Head of Chechnya Ramzan Kadyrov, visited the village of Akhmat-Yurt in the Kurchaloyevsky District, where he laid flowers at the grave of Akhmat Kadyrov, the first President of the Chechen Republic.
