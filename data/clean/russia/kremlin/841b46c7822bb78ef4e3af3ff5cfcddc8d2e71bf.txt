Events Congratulations to Russian school students who won the International Chemistry Olympiad in Tianjin Vladimir Putin congratulated Artem Gulyaev, Viktor Koshlan, Vladimir Mikhailenko and Nikita Perov, winners of the International Chemistry Olympiad held in Tianjin, China. July 18, 2022 19:30 The message reads in part: “You performed brilliantly at this prestigious competition, which brought together the strongest competitors from around the world. And you have rightfully won the long-cherished gold medals. I am convinced that your profound knowledge, dedication and perseverance will continue to be in demand and will help you achieve personal success for the benefit of your homeland.” Topics Science and innovation Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 18, 2022, 19:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69025 Text version
