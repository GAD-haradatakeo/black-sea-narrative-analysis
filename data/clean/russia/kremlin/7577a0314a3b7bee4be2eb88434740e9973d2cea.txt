Events Condolences on the passing of cosmonaut and two-times Hero of the Soviet Union Vladimir Aksyonov Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to the family of cosmonaut and two-times Hero of the Soviet Union Vladimir Aksyonov over his passing. April 9, 2024 20:00 The message reads, in part: “A two-times Hero of the Soviet Union, Vladimir Aksyonov was a man of great talent, courage and spirit. He made a meaningful contribution to developing our domestic space programme. He always served as a role model by his professionalism and the ability to effectively perform unconventional tasks during orbital missions and while testing and mastering new equipment and solutions.” * * * Soviet cosmonaut, two-times Hero of the Soviet Union Vladimir Aksyonov died on April 9, at the age of 89.
