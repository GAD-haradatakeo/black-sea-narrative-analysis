Events Greetings on Road Workers’ Day Vladimir Putin sent greetings to current and former road industry workers on Road Workers’ Day. October 16, 2022 09:00 The message reads, in part: “The road industry is a crucial and integral component of the country’s unified transport system. The unimpeded connectivity between the regions, the socioeconomic progress of the country and people’s quality of life largely depend on its effective operation. Therefore, the government gives priority to all aspects of improving road infrastructure. I want to note with satisfaction that road industry workers meet their professional holiday with good results: hundreds of kilometres of federal and regional roads are being built and upgraded, the basic network of motorways is being improved, new logistics technologies are being widely introduced and traffic safety is improving. Your next major challenge is the large-scale road construction plan for 2023–2027 and ambitious and much needed tasks on building and upgrading motorways, bridges and interchanges as well as orbital roads around large cities and towns. I am confident that you will continue to work with dedication and will persistently move forward to achieving your goals.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 16, 2022, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69619 Text version
