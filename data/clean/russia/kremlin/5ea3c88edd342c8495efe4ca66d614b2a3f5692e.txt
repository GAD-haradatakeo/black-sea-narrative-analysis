Events Vladimir Putin arrived in Yakutsk June 18, 2024 09:00 Yakutsk During the trip, the President will have an opportunity to explore the achievements and development prospects for the creative and IT industries of the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia). The President will pay special attention to the development of Russia’s Far East, particularly with respect to implementing the County Doctor, County Teacher, and Muravyov-Amursky 2030 programmes. A meeting with the participants of these programmes and cultural figures who chose to work in the Far East is also on the agenda. The President will also meet with students of the Vasily Bosikov Higher School of Music, a leading university that trains professionals in the field of music and culture in the Far East. Additionally, the President is expected to have a working meeting with Head of the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia) Aisen Nikolayev Nikolayev Aisen Head of the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia) .
