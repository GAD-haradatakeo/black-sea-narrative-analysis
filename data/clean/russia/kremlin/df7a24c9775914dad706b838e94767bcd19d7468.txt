Events Greetings on the opening of the National Youth Forum Yug Molodoy Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the National Youth Forum Yug Molodoy (Young South). November 1, 2024 14:00 The message reads, in part: ”I extend my greetings to you on the opening of the National Youth Forum Yug Molodoy, which has brought together talented and energetic young people from various regions of Russia in the city of Berdyansk in the Zaporozhye Region. You commence your work by addressing a matter of significant importance: the development of Donbass and Novorossiya – our historic lands, now forever united with the Fatherland. I am aware that participants in the forthcoming meetings have already distinguished themselves through tangible achievements in public and volunteer activities and are prepared to put forward fresh initiatives in sectors such as the economy, science and education, culture and sport, healthcare and family support. Undoubtedly, you will also engage in meaningful discussions and roundtables with prominent politicians and experts, representatives from ministries and agencies, and CEOs of large companies. Such a comprehensive and dynamic programme will afford each of you the opportunity to showcase your abilities, secure backing for the implementation of your ideas, and forge lasting friendships and partnerships with like-minded people. I am confident that the Yug Molodoy forum, which, starting this year, will be held at the new permanent educational centre Mayak, will earn its place in the diverse spectrum of national youth forums.“ Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 1, 2024, 14:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75469 Text version
