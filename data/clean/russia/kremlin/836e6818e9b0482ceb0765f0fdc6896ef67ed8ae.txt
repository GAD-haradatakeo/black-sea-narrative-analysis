Events Greetings on the opening of 16th Youth Delphic Games Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the 16 th Youth Delphic Games of the CIS member states. October 15, 2022 17:00 The message reads, in part: “The Delphic Games have long gained wide recognition in the CIS space. This large-scale cultural project helps young talents to uncover and tap their creative potential, promotes multifaceted humanitarian cooperation and strengthens mutual trust among our nations. Importantly, the young men and women who came to hospitable Dushanbe will not only showcase their achievements in painting, music, choreography and other arts but will also be able to share experience and communicate in a friendly and informal environment. We hope that encounters at the contest venues will start strong long-lasting friendships. I want to sincerely thank our Tajikistani partners who did so much to organise this creative Olympiad and to wish success, unforgettable impressions and all the best to the Delphic Games competitors.” Geography Tajikistan Topics Children Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 15, 2022, 17:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69617 Text version
