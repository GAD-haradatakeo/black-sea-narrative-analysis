Events On December 7, Vladimir Putin will hold talks with President of Iran Ebrahim Raisi On December 7, Vladimir Putin will hold talks in Moscow with President of the Islamic Republic of Iran Ebrahim Raisi who will arrive in Russia on a working visit. Announcement, December 7, 2023 Add to Calendar Calendars iCal Google The two presidents plan to review the current agenda of bilateral cooperation with an emphasis on the implementation of promising joint trade and economic projects, including transport and energy. They will also exchange views on the main international and regional issues.
