Events Congratulations to current staff and veterans of the Khrunichev State Research and Production Space Centre Vladimir Putin congratulated the current staff and veterans of the Khrunichev State Research and Production Space Centre on receiving the honorary plaque of the Russian Federation For Excellence in Labour. June 7, 2023 14:30 The message reads, in part: “This high decoration is the acknowledgement of the outstanding contribution of the current staff and veterans of the Khrunichev State Research and Production Space Centre to developing the national rocket, space and defence sectors and to strengthening the country’s national security, and attests to your exceptional professionalism, diligence and dedication. It is significant that this decision was made in the year of the 30 th anniversary of the State Research and Production Space Centre, whose history dates back to the beginning of the 20 th century. I am convinced that the unique scientific and production potential of the Khrunichev Centre and the wonderful traditions passed down from generation to generation will continue to contribute to achieving the goals set for the benefit of Russia.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 7, 2023, 14:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71313 Text version
