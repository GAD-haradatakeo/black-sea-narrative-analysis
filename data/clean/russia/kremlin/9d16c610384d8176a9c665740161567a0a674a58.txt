Events Executive Order on awarding Kirill Stremousov Order of Courage (posthumously) The President signed the Executive Order On Awarding Kirill Stremousov the Order of Courage . November 9, 2022 22:25 By Presidential Executive Order, Deputy Head of the Military and Civil Administration of the Kherson Region Kirill Stremousov is awarded the Order of Courage (posthumously) for the bravery and courage shown in the line of duty.
