Events Condolences to President of Indonesia Joko Widodo Vladimir Putin sent condolences to President of the Republic of Indonesia Joko Widodo in connection with numerous fatalities and large-scale destruction following an earthquake in West Java province. November 22, 2022 12:00 The President of Russia asked Joko Widodo Widodo Joko President of the Republic of Indonesia to convey words of sincere sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends and wishes for a speedy recovery to all those injured as a result of the disaster.
