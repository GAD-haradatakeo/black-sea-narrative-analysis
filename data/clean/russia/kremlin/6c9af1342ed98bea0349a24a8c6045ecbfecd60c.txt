Events Greetings on the 300th anniversary of the Russian State Archive of the Navy Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the staff of the Russian State Archive of the Navy on its 300 th anniversary. January 28, 2024 10:30 The message reads, in part: “Founded in 1724, your archive is considered one of Russia’s oldest. Dedicated specialists, loyal to their calling, truly like-minded people who made a tremendous contribution to studying and preserving the history of the Russian Navy, have always worked here. They have accomplished a lot in preserving the memory of the deeds of Russian sailors, great naval commanders, prominent seafarers, explorers, and shipbuilders. We are delighted to note that your staff continues the traditions of their predecessors in a worthy manner. You carry out interesting, substantial scientific, enlightening and educational projects, and you are involved in the development of the national archive sector.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: January 28, 2024, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73344 Text version
