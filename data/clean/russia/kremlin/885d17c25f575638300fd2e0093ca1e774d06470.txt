Events Greetings on the opening of the 7th Ildar Abdrazakov International Music Festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the 7 th Ildar Abdrazakov International Music Festival. November 6, 2024 16:00 The message reads, in part: “It is gratifying that your festival is strengthening its traditions every year and is rightfully respected for its rich programme, truly festive atmosphere, and relevant charity and educational initiatives aimed at supporting talented young people, such as Ildar Abdrazakov's Summer School, which has been held in St Petersburg for the first time [this year]. By bringing together world-renowned masters and talented young musicians from across the world, your festival provides people in Russian regions with the opportunity to enjoy high art and classical masterpieces.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 6, 2024, 16:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75509 Text version
