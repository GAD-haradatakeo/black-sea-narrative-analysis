Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council The President held, via videoconference, a briefing session with permanent members of the Security Council. May 5, 2023 17:10 The Kremlin, Moscow Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council, via videoconference. A number of issues related to preparations for Victory Day celebrations were discussed.
