Events Greetings on the opening of the All-Russian Patriotic Forum Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the All-Russian Patriotic Forum timed to coincide with Russia Day. June 12, 2023 12:00 The message reads, in part: “It is gratifying that your forum is becoming a tradition and once again bringing together, in person and online, leaders of patriotic centres, current and former participants in the special military operation, public activists, educators and volunteers from all over Russia. At the forum, you can share your experience and discuss a broad range of topical issues while attending large-scale displays and interactive events at Victory Park. I must emphasise that today, as we face grave challenges, your work is highly significant and very relevant. You are doing a lot to instil values of unwavering morality, ideals of patriotism and civic consciousness in our younger generation, focusing your efforts on preserving our historical memory, our rich cultural and spiritual heritage, strengthening your ranks by attracting energetic and passionate people who feel it is their personal duty to shape the future of their Fatherland”.
