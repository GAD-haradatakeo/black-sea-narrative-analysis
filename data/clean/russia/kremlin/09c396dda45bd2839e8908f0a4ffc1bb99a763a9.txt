Events Telephone conversation with Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud. June 7, 2023 22:20 The leaders discussed current aspects of multifaceted Russia-Saudi cooperation, in particular, ways to build up trade and economic ties and implement ambitious joint projects in investment, transport logistics and energy. They held an in-depth discussion of ensuring stability on the global energy market. Both sides expressed their appreciation of the high level of cooperation within the framework of OPEC Plus, which allows them to take prompt and effective measures to maintain the balance of oil demand and supply. They noted the importance of the agreements to this effect attained at the recent ministerial meeting in Riyadh. The sides also talked about various aspects of Russia-Saudi interaction at other multilateral organisations. They agreed to maintain contacts at different levels.
