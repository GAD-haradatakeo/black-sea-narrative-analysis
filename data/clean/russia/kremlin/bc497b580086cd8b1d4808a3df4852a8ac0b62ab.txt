Events Greetings to participants, organisers and guests of the 20th Volokolamsk Frontier International Festival of Military-Patriotic Films Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants, organisers and guests of the 20 th Volokolamsk Frontier International Festival of Military-Patriotic Films. November 13, 2024 18:00 The message reads, in part: “It is a pleasure to greet you in Volokolamsk, the City of Military Glory, at the opening ceremony of the 20 th Volokolamsk Frontier International Festival of Military-Patriotic Films. Holding such a prestigious forum pays deep tribute to the immortal feat of our nation during the Great Patriotic War, to the heroes who defended the freedom and independence of our Motherland and crushed Nazism. I am delighted to note that, over many years, this festival has enriched the cultural life of our country and the creative palette of Russian and world cinema. It makes a valuable contribution to fostering in the younger generation the enduring values of patriotism, civic duty and responsibility for the future of our Motherland. I am confident that this year’s festival, dedicated to the upcoming 80 th anniversary of Victory, will leave a bright and lasting impression, offering participants and guests an excellent opportunity to enjoy talented works from film directors and actors, and to meet with legendary, distinguished masters as well as promising young creators.” Topics Culture Great Patriotic War Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 13, 2024, 18:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75560 Text version
