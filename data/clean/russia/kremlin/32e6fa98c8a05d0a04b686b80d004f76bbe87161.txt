Events Telephone conversation with President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko. March 2, 2024 13:45 The Russian President once again congratulated Alexander Lukashenko Lukashenko Alexander President of Belarus on the successful parliamentary and municipal elections in Belarus, which were held on February 25, 2024. The leaders had an in-depth discussion on current issues related to furthering friendly Russian-Belarusian relations, based on the principles of allied and strategic partnership, as well as key aspects of the international situation.
