Events Greetings on the presentation of the Patriot 2023 National Prize Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants, organisers, and guests of the Patriot 2023 National Prize award ceremony. December 9, 2023 14:00 The message reads, in part: ”This prestigious award is traditionally given to representatives of the teaching community, volunteers and search organisations as a way to show appreciation for their creative and constructive work for the benefit of the Fatherland. You are making a significant contribution to the education of the younger generation, instilling in them unwavering moral values, the ideals of patriotism and good citizenship. Your efforts are focused on preserving the memory of our heroic history and our rich cultural and spiritual heritage. It is worth emphasising that your educational, enlightening, and selfless activities are highly valued and in great demand today. I believe that you will have the opportunity to discuss current issues that are of public concern, exchange experiences and best practices, and initiate new meaningful projects and initiatives at the forum.“ Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 9, 2023, 14:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72945 Text version
