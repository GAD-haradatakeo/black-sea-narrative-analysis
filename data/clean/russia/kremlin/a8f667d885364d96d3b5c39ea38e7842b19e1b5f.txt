Events Greetings to Sheikh Mishaal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah, Emir of the State of Kuwait Vladimir Putin sent greetings to Emir of the State of Kuwait Sheikh Mishaal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah on the occasion of his enthronement. December 20, 2023 12:50 The message reads, in part: “Relations between Russia and Kuwait are traditionally friendly. I am confident that these relations will continue to develop productively in various areas for the benefit of our peoples and in the interests of strengthening peace and security in the Middle East.” Geography Kuwait Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 20, 2023, 12:50 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73073 Text version
