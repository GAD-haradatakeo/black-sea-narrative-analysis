Events Condolences to President of Libyan Presidential Council Mohamed al-Menfi Vladimir Putin expressed his condolences to President of the Libyan Presidential Council Mohamed al-Menfi over the loss of life and massive destruction caused by a storm and floods in the northeast of the country. September 12, 2023 17:15 The message reads, in part: “We share the pain and grief of the friendly people of Libya. And, of course, we are ready to provide all the assistance needed.” The President of Russia asked to convey his words of sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends.
