Events Greetings on the opening of the 8th Chersonese International Opera and Ballet Festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests on the opening of the 8 th Chersonese International Opera and Ballet Festival. July 29, 2024 19:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the past years, your festival gained broad popularity and became a real holiday for residents and guests of Sevastopol and Crimea. Its creative space brings together renowned virtuosos and talented young people and is rightfully known for its established traditions, rich and diverse programme, and colourful and impressive premieres, as well as, of course, a special and inspiring atmosphere reigning on the stage. I am confident that the current festival will be a success and the audience will enjoy the pleasure of encountering with high art.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 29, 2024, 19:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74669 Text version
