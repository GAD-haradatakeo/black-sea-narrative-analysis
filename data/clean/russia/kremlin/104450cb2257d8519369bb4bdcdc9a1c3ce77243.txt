Events Greetings on the 15th anniversary of High Precision Systems Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the current staff and veterans of High Precision Systems on the occasion of the company’s 15 th anniversary. April 9, 2024 18:30 The message reads, in part: “You have every reason to be proud of the rich history of the enterprises that are part of the holding company, as well as of many generations of talented scientists, technical officers, designers, engineers, testers, and workers, whose selfless labour made a worthy contribution to strengthening Russia’s industrial and defence capability. During the Great Patriotic War, the factory workers quickly launched production of a broad range of devices for military equipment, as well as of the legendary T-34 tanks (at the Stalingrad Tractor Plant), which rolled out from the shop floors and went straight to the front, bringing Victory closer. It is important that the holding’s current team carefully preserves and promotes wonderful traditions of its predecessors and actively introduces the latest technology and advanced solutions. Today, the sophisticated powerful missile systems, infantry fighting vehicles and airborne assault vehicles that you created help our heroes fulfil their assigned missions in the course of the special military operation with dignity.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 9, 2024, 18:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73840 Text version
