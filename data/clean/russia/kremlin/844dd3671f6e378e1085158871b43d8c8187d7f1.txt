Events Birthday greetings to President of Kyrgyzstan Sadyr Japarov Vladimir Putin extended his heartfelt greetings to President of the Kyrgyz Republic Sadyr Japarov on his birthday. December 6, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Russia highly values your personal contribution to strengthening the bonds of allied relations and strategic partnership between our states. We will certainly further pursue the ongoing joint efforts to expand and improve bilateral ties across the board, as well as to promote integration in Eurasia and ensure regional security. I wish you the best of health, prosperity and success in your state activities for the benefit of the friendly people of the Kyrgyz Republic.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 6, 2024, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75776 Text version
