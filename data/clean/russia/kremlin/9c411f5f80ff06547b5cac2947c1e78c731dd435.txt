Events Greetings to Head of the Donetsk People’s Republic Denis Pushilin Vladimir Putin sent greetings to Head of the Donetsk People’s Republic Denis Pushilin on the Day of the Donetsk People’s Republic. May 11, 2022 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Eight years ago, the people of the Donetsk People’s Republic made a choice to become an independent state. Today, we are fighting together for the people in the republic to be able to enjoy their legitimate right to a free and peaceful life in keeping with their centuries-old cultural and spiritual traditions, and in friendship with brotherly Russia. I am certain that together, we will overcome all the challenges and win.” Persons Pushilin Denis Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: May 11, 2022, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68391 Text version
