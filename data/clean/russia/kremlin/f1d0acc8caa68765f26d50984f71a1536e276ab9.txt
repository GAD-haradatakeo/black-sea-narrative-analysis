Events Greetings on the opening of the Russian Military Historical Society Congress Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the Russian Military Historical Society Congress, marking the 10 th anniversary of the society. June 30, 2022 11:30 The message reads, in part: “By continuing the lofty mission of the Imperial Russian Military Historical Society, the Russian Military Historical Society unites, with its noble and popular tasks, scientists, members of search teams and military history clubs, representatives of law enforcement agencies and business circles, as well as those who sincerely love their Motherland, are proud of the victories and accomplishments of their ancestors, and who are ready to dedicate themselves to the preservation of the military chronicle of Russia. This responsible patriotic position of the Society members is especially evident today, during the special military operation. It is important that you are actively carrying out research and implementing substantive educational, publishing and exhibition projects dedicated to significant dates and key events of our history. And, of course, I would like to especially note your truly outstanding contribution to educating the youth based on the values of patriotism, civic consciousness and commitment to unshakable spiritual and moral ideals.” Topics Armed Forces Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 30, 2022, 11:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68786 Text version
