Events Greetings to 12th Congress of A Just Russia – Patriots – For the Truth political party Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the delegates and guests of the 12th Congress of A Just Russia – Patriots – For the Truth political party. May 20, 2023 11:05 The message reads, in part: “Your party is rightly considered to be one of Russia’s leading political movements. Over the past years, it has gained vast experience in participating in election campaigns at various levels, and has always received support from a large number of citizens. With basic principles of social justice and patriotism as the foundation, party members work in the Federal Assembly and regional and municipal authorities, focusing on protecting people’s rights and interests, increasing the quality of their lives, educating young people, and preserving our historical memory. Of course, I should also note your party’s aspiration for effective interaction with other political movements, public organisations and professional associations in order to successfully achieve the key goals of our country’s development. Today, when we are facing serious challenges, such a broad consolidation is essential for supporting participants in the special military operation and providing assistance to their families and residents of Russia’s new regions.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: May 20, 2023, 11:05 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71170 Text version
