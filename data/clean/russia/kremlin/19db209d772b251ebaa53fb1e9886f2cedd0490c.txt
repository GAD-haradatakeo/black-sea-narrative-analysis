Events On August 18–19, Vladimir Putin will make a state visit to Azerbaijan Announcement, August 18 − 19, 2024 During the talks, the parties will discuss matters pertaining to the further advancement of the Russian-Azerbaijani strategic partnership and alliance, as well as current international and regional issues. It is planned to approve a Joint Statement of the heads of state and sign intergovernmental agreements and other documents.
