Events Greetings to participants in opening ceremony for super final of the fifth Leaders of Russia national management competition Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants in the opening ceremony for the super final of the fifth Leaders of Russia national management competition. February 23, 2024 10:30 The message reads, in part: “Over the past years, this meaningful and highly relevant project has provided tens of thousands of young people from many regions of Russia with an excellent opportunity to fully showcase their talents, creativity and organisational skills, leading to significant achievements in their chosen fields. I would like to emphasise that the contestants represent a wide array of professions, spanning business, public administration, science, education, healthcare and the social sphere. Despite their varied backgrounds, they share a common trait: determination and responsibility, coupled with readiness to work for the benefit of their country. I am confident that you, the current finalists, will navigate the final challenges with aplomb. Your interaction with mentors, including esteemed politicians, scientists, governors and heads of major corporations, will undoubtedly broaden your professional and creative horizons, helping you in realising your aspirations.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 23, 2024, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73536 Text version
