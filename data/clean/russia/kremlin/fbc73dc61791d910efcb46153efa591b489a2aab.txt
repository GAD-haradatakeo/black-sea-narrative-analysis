Events Instructions pertaining to emergency situation on Crimean Bridge October 8, 2022 09:15 In light of the emergency situation on the Crimean Bridge, Vladimir Putin heard reports from Prime Minister Mikhail Mishustin Mishustin Mikhail Prime Minister of the Russian Federation , Deputy Prime Minister Marat Khusnullin Khusnullin Marat Deputy Prime Minister , Emergencies Minister Alexander Kurenkov Kurenkov Alexander Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief and Transport Minister Vitaly Savelyev Savelyev Vitaly Minister of Transport of the Russian Federation , as well as heads of security services. The President instructed the Prime Minister to set up a government commission to investigate the incident and eliminate the aftermath as soon as possible. The commission will include heads of the Krasnodar Territory and the Republic of Crimea as well as representatives of the National Guard, the Federal Security Service and the Interior Ministry. A per the President's instruction, Transport Minister Vitaly Savelyev and Emergencies Minister Alexander Kurenkov have left for the site of the incident.
