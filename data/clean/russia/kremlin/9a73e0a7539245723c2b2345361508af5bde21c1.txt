Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council The President held a briefing session, via videoconference, with permanent members of the Security Council. April 7, 2022 16:55 Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region The meeting participants discussed the progress of the ongoing special military operation in Ukraine, as well as the negotiations between the Russian and Ukrainian delegations, emphasising the need to strongly counteract any information attacks or other sabotage by the Ukrainian side, such as the recent provocation in Bucha.
