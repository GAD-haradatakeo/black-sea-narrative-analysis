Events Greetings on the opening of the Congress of the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the delegates and participants in the Congress of the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs (RSPP). June 29, 2022 11:00 “The Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs is rightly considered one of the leading business associations representing the interests of businesspeople and employers across all Russian regions. For many years, it has been a reliable partner of the state in addressing such key issues as increasing the effectiveness and competitiveness of Russian companies, improving labour and tax legislation and company law. Affirming its strong expert and intellectual potential, the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs actively contributes to drafting core documents, developing the economy and social sphere, and improving the investment climate. Notably, in the difficult situation of today, RSPP members are striving to respond with dignity to global challenges, maintain production and retain staff, enter new markets, form prospective cooperation chains and promptly deal with import substitution tasks. In this context, I want to note that many specific and constructive proposals from the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs have been included in Russia’s current economic agenda.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 29, 2022, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68777 Text version
