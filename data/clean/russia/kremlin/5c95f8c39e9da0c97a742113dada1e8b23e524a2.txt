Events Greetings to President of Pakistan Arif Alvi Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to President of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan Arif Alvi on the country’s national holiday, Independence Day. August 14, 2023 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Our two countries share friendly relations and have been generating positive momentum in our bilateral political dialogue and promoting fruitful cooperation in various spheres. Moscow and Islamabad have been proactive in working together on the counterterrorism agenda and Afghanistan policy, as well as in addressing other urgent international issues. I am certain that by working together we will succeed in further expanding the multifaceted ties between Russia and Pakistan for the benefit of our nations and for stronger regional stability and security.” Geography Pakistan Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 14, 2023, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72035 Text version
