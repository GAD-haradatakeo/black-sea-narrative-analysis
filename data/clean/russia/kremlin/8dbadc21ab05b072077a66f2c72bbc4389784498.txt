Events Congratulations to winner of the 2023 World Rapid Chess Championship Anastasia Bodnaruk Vladimir Putin congratulated Anastasia Bodnaruk on her victory at the 2023 World Rapid Chess Championship in Samarkand. December 28, 2023 20:00 The message reads, in part: “You have fittingly continued the remarkable traditions of the domestic school of women’s chess, demonstrated your skill, endurance and true sporting character in taking on strong, renowned rivals, and you have deservedly become the winner of the most prestigious international competitions. And, of course, special words of gratitude go to your mentors, to all those who have supported you on the road to success.” Topics Sport Publication status Published in sections: News , Commissions and Councils , Council for the Development of Physical Culture and Sport Publication date: December 28, 2023, 20:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73195 Text version
