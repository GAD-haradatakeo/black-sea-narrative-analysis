Events Greetings to Gazprom-Media Holding The President sent a message of greetings to Gazprom-Media Holding on its 25 th anniversary. January 21, 2023 10:10 The message reads, in part: “Your holding has traversed a long and eventful path in the past 25 years and brought together under its wing popular TV channels, radio stations, print media and internet platforms. The holding is rightly considered to be one of the biggest and most successful companies in the Russian media industry. It is great that you are not resting on your laurels: you keep working creatively and enthusiastically, introducing cutting-edge technology and focusing on the needs of the most discerning audiences. This competent and resonant approach helps Gazprom-Media to confidently progress and carry out news, analytical, educational and entertainment projects. I must certainly underscore in particular your much-needed volunteer initiatives and socially important charity work.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: January 21, 2023, 10:10 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70379 Text version
