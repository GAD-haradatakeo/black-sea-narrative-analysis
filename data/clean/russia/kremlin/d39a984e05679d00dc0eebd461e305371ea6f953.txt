Events Birthday greetings to fashion designer Vyacheslav Zaitsev Vladimir Putin extended his greetings to Vyacheslav Zaitsev, fashion designer, member of the Russian Academy of Arts and People’s Artist of the Russian Federation, on his 85 th birthday. March 2, 2023 10:30 The message reads, in part: “As a genuine artist and a true Master, you have never walked well-trodden roads. Your inexhaustible creative and constructive energy and your dedication to your chosen work have helped you score impressive professional success and become an influential specialist and bold innovator in the field of fashion design.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: March 2, 2023, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70625 Text version
