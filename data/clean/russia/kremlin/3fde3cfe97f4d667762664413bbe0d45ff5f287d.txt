Events Laying wreath at Liberation Monument Vladimir Putin and Chairman of State Affairs of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea Kim Jong-un laid a wreath at the Liberation Monument in Pyongyang. June 19, 2024 13:30 Pyongyang The monument was built in 1946 to honour Soviet soldiers who perished while liberating Korea from Japanese invaders. The ceremony concluded with a moment of silence and a ceremonial march by the guard of honour.
