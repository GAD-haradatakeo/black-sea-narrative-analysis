Events Mikhail Mishustin appointed Prime Minister of the Russian Federation The President signed Executive Order On the Prime Minister of the Russian Federation . May 10, 2024 18:10 Pursuant to the Presidential Executive Order and in accordance with Article 83 paragraph (a) of the Constitution of the Russian Federation, Mikhail Mishustin has been appointed Prime Minister of the Russian Federation.
