Events Congratulations to President of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev Vladimir Putin congratulated Kassym-Jomart Tokayev on his re-election as President of the Republic of Kazakhstan. November 21, 2022 11:05 The message reads in part: “You have received a convincing mandate of trust from your fellow citizens, which opens up new opportunities for the implementation of the state development course you are promoting. The relations of strategic partnership and alliance between our countries, which are based on the good traditions of friendship, neighbourliness and mutual respect, are developing very successfully. We will continue to work together to expand them further.” Geography Kazakhstan Topics Foreign policy Persons Tokayev Kassym-Jomart Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 21, 2022, 11:05 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69874 Text version
