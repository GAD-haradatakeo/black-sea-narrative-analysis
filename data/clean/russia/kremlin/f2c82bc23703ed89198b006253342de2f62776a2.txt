Events Condolences to President of the Republic of Chile Gabriel Boric Font Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to President of the Republic of Chile Gabriel Boric Font over the tragic aftermath of wildfires that have hit the country’s central regions. February 6, 2024 15:20 The President of Russia asked to convey words of sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends and wishes for a speedy recovery to all those affected by the disaster.
