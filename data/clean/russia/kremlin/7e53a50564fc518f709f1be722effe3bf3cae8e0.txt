Events Telephone conversation with President of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev During the telephone conversation, Vladimir Putin warmly congratulated President of the Republic of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev on his recent birthday. May 18, 2024 15:10 The presidents discussed certain issues of bilateral cooperation and upcoming contacts in various formats. They reaffirmed their mutual commitment to further strengthening the allied relations and strategic partnership between Russia and Kazakhstan. Vladimir Putin also shared his impressions from his recent state visit to the People’s Republic of China, which concluded yesterday.
