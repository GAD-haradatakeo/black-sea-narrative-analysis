Events Congratulations to the command and personnel of the 25th Guards Detached Special Operations Regiment Vladimir Putin congratulated the command and personnel of the 25 th Guards Detached Special Operations Regiment on being awarded the honorary name Guards. March 27, 2023 16:35 The message reads, in part: “This is a high honour and recognition of your special merits, mass heroism and valour, fortitude and courage shown in defending the Fatherland and protecting the state interests of Russia. The efficient and resolute actions of the regiment's personnel during the special military operation in Ukraine are an example of fulfilling military duty, courage, dedication and high professionalism. I am convinced that you, guardsmen, will continue to be true to your oath, serve the Fatherland with honour, and reliably ensure the safety and peaceful life of our citizens.” The President also wished the command and personnel of the 25 th Guards Detached Special Operations Regiment good health and success.
