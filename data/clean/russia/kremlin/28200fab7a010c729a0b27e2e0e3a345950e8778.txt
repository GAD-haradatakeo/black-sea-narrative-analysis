Events Telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan February 23, 2023 21:00 Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan Pashinyan Nikol Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia at the initiative of the Armenian side. During the conversation, the Armenian leader informed the President of Russia about his meeting with President of the Republic of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev in Munich on February 18, as well as his conversations with other foreign leaders. The two leaders discussed practical implementation of the package of trilateral agreements between the leaders of Russia, Armenia and Azerbaijan in order to ensure stability and security in the region. The current situation on the Armenia-Azerbaijan border was also touched on. Contacts on this matter will be continued at various levels.
