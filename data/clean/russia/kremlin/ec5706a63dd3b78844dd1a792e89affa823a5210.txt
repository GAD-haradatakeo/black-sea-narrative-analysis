Events Telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Israel Naftali Bennett Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Israel Naftali Bennett, at the initiative of the Israeli side March 8, 2022 19:00 The parties discussed various aspects of the situation with Russia’s special military operation to protect Donbass. Vladimir Putin shared his assessment of the third round of talks between the Russian delegation and representatives from Ukraine held on March 7 in Belarus. The President of Russia and the Prime Minister of Israel agreed to continue their dialogue on the matter.
