Events Greetings on opening of 15th Congress of Russian Concert Organisations’ Union Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, guests and organisers of the 15 th Russian Concert Organisations’ Union Congress. July 2, 2022 11:00 The message reads, in part: “For many years, the Russian Concert Organisations’ Union has been bringing together the country’s leading philharmonic orchestras, promoting efforts to preserve and enhance the best traditions in concert presentation in the academic musical arts, and making an important contribution to the mutual enrichment of ethnic music cultures, which Russia is rightfully famous for. Of course, support for creative groups and individual performers, and the methodological and practical assistance you provide is an important and necessary area of your activities. I am confident that the congress will be held in a constructive manner, and will allow you to share best practices and develop uniform approaches to resolving pressing professional issues.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 2, 2022, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68807 Text version
