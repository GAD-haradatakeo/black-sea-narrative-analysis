Events Congratulations to Russia’s team that competed in the International Olympiad in Informatics Vladimir Putin congratulated Alexander Babin, Danil Klishch, Vsevolod Nagibin and Fedor Romashov, members of the Russian team that competed at the International Olympiad in Informatics in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. August 16, 2022 09:30 The message reads in part: ”You have represented our country at this prestigious competition with dignity, proving in deed that Russian education is justly respected and the schools where you study can be proud of their talented students. Well done! I am certain that your knowledge, capacity for work and dedication will help you move forward with confidence and achieve success in life and in your future profession.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 16, 2022, 09:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69170 Text version
