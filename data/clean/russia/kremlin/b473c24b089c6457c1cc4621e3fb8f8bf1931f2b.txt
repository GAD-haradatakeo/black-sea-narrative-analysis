Events Telephone conversation with President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko. February 12, 2022 18:45 The leaders discussed issues pertaining to the further development of bilateral cooperation as well as the situation around the response by the USA and NATO to the Russian proposals on long-term international legal guarantees of Russia’s security. They agreed on further personal contacts.
