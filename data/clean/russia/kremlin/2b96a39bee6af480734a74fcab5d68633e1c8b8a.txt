Events Greetings to 12th Congress of Oncologists of Russia Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants and organisers of the 12 th Congress of Oncologists of Russia. May 19, 2023 12:00 The message reads, in part: “Your traditional forums are always big, important events in the life of Russia’s medical community. They make it possible to discuss current professional problems, exchange experience and best practices, and propose new approaches to the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of dangerous diseases at a high expert level. And of course, these meetings provide a good opportunity to review the preliminary results of the implementation of the Fight Against Cancer federal project. It is important that the Congress has a full, diverse programme. In addition to open discussions and round tables with famous scientists and healthcare organisers, special events for nurses and patients, and unique master classes by leading Russian surgeons will be streamed live.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: May 19, 2023, 12:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71166 Text version
