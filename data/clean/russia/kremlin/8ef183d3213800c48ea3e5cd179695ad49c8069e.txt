Events Telephone conversation with President of Turkiye Recep Tayyip Erdogan October 7, 2024 16:50 During the telephone conversation, President of the Republic of Turkiye Recep Tayyip Erdogan Erdogan Recep Tayyip President of Türkiye warmly wished a happy birthday to President of Russia Vladimir Putin. The leaders expressed satisfaction with the constructive nature of Russia-Turkiye relations and voiced their readiness to collaborate closely in the future. They discussed several topical bilateral and international issues, including dangerous developments in the Middle East. They confirmed their readiness to hold a separate meeting between Vladimir Putin and Recep Tayyip Erdogan during the events of the BRICS Background information BRICS summit in Kazan.
