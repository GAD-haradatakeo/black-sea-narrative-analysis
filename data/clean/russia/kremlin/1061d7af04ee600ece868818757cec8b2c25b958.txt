Events Greetings to participants, organisers and guests of the awards ceremony for the winners of the national Warm Heart social and public initiative with international involvement Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the 10 th awards ceremony for the winners of Warm Heart, a Russian national social and public initiative with international involvement. May 17, 2023 17:00 The message reads, in part: “The patriotic, spiritual and moral education of the young generation deserves to be among our important national priorities. It is good that these enormous and much-needed efforts are facilitated by this type of ambitious and in-demand social and public projects, one of which is, of course, your initiative. For ten years, it has been fulfilling an important and noble mission, which is to support courageous, thoughtful and compassionate young people who are coping with difficult situations in life with perseverance, providing unselfish assistance to veterans and everyone who needs care and attention. Of course, my special thanks go to the parents, teachers and mentors of the young winners, to everyone who helps them achieve the set objectives and teaches them to have respect for such perpetual values as friendship, integrity, mutual help and an honest, conscientious attitude to what they are doing.” Topics Children Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: May 17, 2023, 17:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71154 Text version
