Events Wreath-laying ceremony at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier On Defender of the Fatherland Day, Vladimir Putin laid a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier by the Kremlin Wall. February 23, 2024 12:10 The President honoured the memory of the fallen soldiers with a minute of silence. The ceremony was also attended by Defence Minister Sergei Shoigu Shoigu Sergei Defence Minister , army veterans, officers and cadets from military schools. The ceremony concluded with a ceremonial march by the guard of honour and a military band.
