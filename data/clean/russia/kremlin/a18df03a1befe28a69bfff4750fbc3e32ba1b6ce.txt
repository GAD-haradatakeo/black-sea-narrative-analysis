Events On March 20, the President will address the International Parliamentary Conference Russia-Africa in a Multipolar World Announcement, March 20, 2023 Add to Calendar Calendars iCal Google On March 20, Vladimir Putin will speak at the international parliamentary conference, Russia–Africa in a Multipolar World, to be held in Moscow under the sponsorship of the State Duma. The conference, which is one of the important events in the run-up to the second Russia-Africa Summit, will be attended by more than 40 delegations from the African continent.
