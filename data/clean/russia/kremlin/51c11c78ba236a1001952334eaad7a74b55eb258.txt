Events Birthday greetings to Boris Messerer Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to People’s Artist of Russia Boris Messerer on his 90th birthday. March 15, 2023 23:00 The message reads, in part: “You enjoy professional recognition as a talented artist, stage designer and a positively charged creative person. Your bright and inimitable paintings, drawings and theatre projects are always impressive, substantive and noted for irreproachable taste and a distinctive artistic signature style.” Boris Messerer is a Soviet/Russian theatre artist, stage designer, teacher, holder of two State Prizes of the Russian Federation, stage designer for over 150 opera, ballet and drama performances in the Bolshoi Theatre, the Sovremennik Theatre, the Moscow Art Theatre, the Mossovet State Academic Theatre and the Moscow Academic Theatre of Satire.
