Events Greetings to participants, guests and organisers of the 12th charity basketball game Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, guests and organisers of the 12 th charity basketball game. September 27, 2024 19:20 The message reads, in part: “The 12 th charity basketball game is about to begin in Moscow, and I would like to take this opportunity to welcome you and to offer you my greetings on this occasion. Organised by A Step Together charity foundation, this popular and important project fully reflects the growing awareness and attention that people have been paying to children with disabilities. Every year, this event brings together major cultural figures, athletes, government officials and business leaders, as well as media professionals. These people have a keen sense of the pain others feel and are steadfast in their commitment to do everything to make sure that these youngsters can live a life of joy, communicate, and feel our unwavering support. Of course, young sports fans who came to Moscow from Donbass and Novorossiya will be watching this exhibition game from the stands. I am certain that great sportsmanship will be on display during today’s meet, while a packed and fulfilling cultural, educational and sightseeing programme will leave great and positive impressions on these boys and girls.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 27, 2024, 19:20 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75203 Text version
