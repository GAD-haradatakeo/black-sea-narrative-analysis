Events Greetings to the 14th Congress of the Socialist Political Party A Just Russia – Patriots – For Truth Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the delegates and guests of the 14 th Congress of the Socialist Political Party A Just Russia – Patriots – For Truth. October 26, 2024 11:15 The message reads, in part: “Since the first days of its inception, your party has voiced a staunch patriotic position and consistently upheld the basic values of social justice. Your extensive experience in participating in multi-level election campaigns convincingly shows the high level of trust voters have in the party’s deputies. Its representatives make a constructive contribution to the work of the Federal Assembly, regional parliaments and municipal self-government agencies, prioritise such important issues as upholding the rights and interests of citizens, strengthening the family institution, educating the rising generation and improving people’s standards of living. I would also like to acknowledge your sincere commitment to collaborating fruitfully with other political parties and public organisations to successfully address the large-scale nationwide objectives facing the country.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 26, 2024, 11:15 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75420 Text version
