Events Greetings on Fisherman’s Day Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the current and former workers of Russia’s fishing industry on their professional holiday, Fisherman’s Day. July 14, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Fishery is one of the key industries of the Russian economy. It makes a significant contribution to strengthening food security and promotes the development of an entire range of regions, creating new jobs. Today, the Russian fishing industry is successfully adapting to the demands of the domestic market and difficult external economic conditions while undergoing a large-scale modernisation. Domestic shipyards are building new fishing vessels, processing plants and logistics infrastructure are being created, and competitiveness and technological independence of this strategically important sector are increasing. Particular attention is paid to industry-specific science, training of qualified personnel, and creating decent working conditions. It is gratifying that, when addressing the challenges facing the fishery, you rely on the experience and wonderful traditions of your predecessors. It is noteworthy that your professional holiday also embraces many amateur fishermen and athletes. The popularity of fishing grows each year and helps promote domestic tourism. It is necessary to make this hobby accessible for everyone who wants to try it, and those who love it should treat Russia’s natural resources sustainably and with care.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 14, 2024, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74555 Text version
