Events Telephone conversation with President of Iran Ebrahim Raisi Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Islamic Republic of Iran Ebrahim Raisi. January 19, 2023 16:10 The leaders discussed current issues of bilateral relations, including cooperation in the energy and transport sectors. The presidents also touched upon the situation in Syria, noting the importance of maintaining close coordination within the framework of the Astana process as a key mechanism to facilitate a Syrian settlement. It was agreed to maintain contacts at various levels.
