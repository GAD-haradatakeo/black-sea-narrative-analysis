Events Greetings to fourth Strong Ideas for a New Time forum Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the fourth forum, Strong Ideas for a New Time. February 19, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the years, your large-scale project has considerably strengthened its reputation. It has received constructive support from government agencies, large Russian corporations, and public organisations, and helped many talented individuals and business teams accomplish their goals and implement plans. Today’s forum has brought together people of different ages and professions – those who venture beyond conventional paths in search of unique, promising research, engineering, and technological solutions. They undertake ambitious objectives, earnestly striving for success and contributing to the betterment of the Motherland. I want to thank all forum participants, inventors, experts, and mentors for your major, sought-after work, talent, enthusiasm, and devotion, for your constructive contribution to the development of the country.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 19, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73488 Text version
