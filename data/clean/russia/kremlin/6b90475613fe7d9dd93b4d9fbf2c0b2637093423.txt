Events Greetings to President of South Ossetia Alan Gagloyev Vladimir Putin sent greetings to President of the Republic of South Ossetia Alan Gagloyev in connection with Republic Day. September 20, 2022 09:00 The message reads, in part: “This glorious holiday has come to symbolise the bravery and heroism of the people of South Ossetia who defended their legitimate right to freedom and independence in a persistent struggle. Relations between our countries are based on the principles of alliance and integration. We intend to continue providing all-round assistance to the fraternal people of South Ossetia in addressing their socioeconomic objectives and in ensuring their national security.” Geography South Ossetia Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 20, 2022, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69380 Text version
