Events Visit to exhibition at National Defence Control Centre After the expanded meeting of the Defence Ministry Board and the ceremony to present Gold Star medals to Heroes of Russia participating in the special military operation, Vladimir Putin visited an exhibition in the atrium of the National Defence Control Centre. December 19, 2023 18:00 Moscow The President was accompanied by Defence Minister Sergei Shoigu Shoigu Sergei Defence Minister and Chief of Staff of the Russian Armed Forces – First Deputy Defence Minister Valery Gerasimov Gerasimov Valery Chief of the General Staff of Russia’s Armed Forces – First Deputy Minister of Defence . Vladimir Putin examined stands exhibiting modern military hardware, drones, firearms, gear and other equipment. The President also examined models of various types of weapons, including the Sarmat missile complex.
