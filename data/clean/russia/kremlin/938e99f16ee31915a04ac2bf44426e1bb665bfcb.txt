Events Visit to Ustyansky timber industry complex During his working trip to the Arkhangelsk Region Vladimir Putin visited the Ustyansky timber industry complex. February 10, 2023 14:20 Oktyabrsky, Arkhangelsk Region Presidential Plenipotentiary Envoy to the Northwestern Federal District Alexander Gutsan Gutsan Alexander Presidential Plenipotentiary Envoy to the Northwestern Federal District and Governor of the Arkhangelsk Region Alexander Tsybulsky Tsybulsky Alexander Governor of Arkhangelsk Region accompanied the President, while Director General of ULK Group Vladimir Butorin led the tour. The President inspected the sawmill department and the forest selection/plant-growing centre. He also examined the ULK Group’s information stands. Built in 2011, the Ustyansky timber processing plant now covers 40 hectares. The Ustyansky seed-growing complex is one of six national forest reproduction centres. Its specialists conduct the full cycle of planting material preparation, from drying pinecones to hardening off young trees.
