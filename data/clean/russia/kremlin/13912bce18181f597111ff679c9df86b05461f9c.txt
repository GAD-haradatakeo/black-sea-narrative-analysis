Events Executive Order on awarding the title of Hero of Labour of the Russian Federation to Valentina Matviyenko Vladimir Putin signed Executive Order On Awarding the Title of Hero of Labour of the Russian Federation to Valentina Matviyenko . April 7, 2024 10:00 Speaker of the Federation Council of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation Valentina Matviyenko was awarded the title of Hero of Labour of the Russian Federation for her special services in labour to the state and the people of Russia.
