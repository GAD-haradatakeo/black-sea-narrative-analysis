Events Telephone conversation with President of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev. February 14, 2023 21:20 The presidents had an in-depth discussion of opportunities to expand trade and economic cooperation, including joint projects in energy and transport. The leaders reviewed the current situation in the South Caucasus and stressed the importance of consistent steps to ensure stability and security at the Azerbaijan-Armenia border. In this context, they reaffirmed their commitment to the unconditional implementation of the entire range of agreements reached by Russia, Azerbaijan and Armenia at the top level. It was agreed to maintain contact in various formats.
