Events Vladimir Putin took part in commemorative events dedicated to 80th anniversary of breaking siege of Leningrad The head of state visited the Nevsky Pyatachok Memorial Military-Historical Complex in the Leningrad Region, where he laid flowers at the Landmark Stone monument. Later the President took part in a wreath-laying ceremony at the Motherland monument at the Piskaryovskoye Memorial Cemetery. January 18, 2023 11:20 Leningrad Region Nazy Germany’s forces besieged Leningrad from September 8, 1941, until January 27, 1944. The siege was broken on January 18, 1943.
