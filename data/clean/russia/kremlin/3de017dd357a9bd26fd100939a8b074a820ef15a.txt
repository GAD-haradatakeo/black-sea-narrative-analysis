Events The President discussed the flood situation with Emergencies Minister and Orenburg Region Governor April 7, 2024 13:30 Orenburg Region Governor Denis Pasler Pasler Denis Governor of Orenburg Region and Emergencies Minister Alexander Kurenkov Kurenkov Alexander Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief updated Vladimir Putin on the current flood situation in the Orenburg Region around the city of Orsk. The Emergencies Minister informed the President mostly about the number of people evacuated and moved to temporary accommodation centres. In his discussion with Alexander Kurenkov, the President emphasised the importance of carrying out timely analysis, making forecasts and taking appropriate measures with regard to the potential flooding threat in the Kurgan and Tyumen regions. Vladimir Putin plans to hold telephone conversations with the heads of these regions on Sunday.
