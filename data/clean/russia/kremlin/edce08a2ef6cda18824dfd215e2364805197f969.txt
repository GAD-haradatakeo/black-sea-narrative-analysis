Events Congratulations to Emir Kusturica on his birthday Vladimir Putin sent a message of congratulations to Emir Kusturica on the occasion of the film director’s 70 th birthday. November 24, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “A person of bold and multifaceted talent, a true artist, you have rightfully earned the reputation as one of the most renowned film directors of our time. You have also made your mark as a sought-after actor, musician, and writer, and as a creator of extraordinary creative projects. Your brilliant work has written a vibrant chapter in the history of world art. I would particularly like to highlight your active civic stance and your tremendous contribution to significant humanitarian initiatives aimed at fostering trust and mutual understanding among nations.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 24, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75662 Text version
