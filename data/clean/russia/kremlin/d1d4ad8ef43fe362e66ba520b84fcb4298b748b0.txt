Events Congratulations to participants in a ceremony marking the 80th anniversary of liberation of Novorossiysk from Nazi occupiers Vladimir Putin sent congratulations to Novorossiysk residents on the city’s 185 th anniversary, the 80 th anniversary of its liberation from the Nazis and the 50 th anniversary of receiving the honorary title Hero City. September 12, 2023 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Since being founded, Novorossiysk has played an important role in promoting Russian statehood, defending Russia’s Black Sea coast, making its contribution to the economic and industrial development of the country. You are rightfully proud of your native city’s legendary history, covered with military and labour glory, as well as its wonderful patriotic, cultural and spiritual traditions. The courage and fortitude of the Soviet soldiers and officers that took part in the battles for Novorossiysk, the landings and the heroic defence of the bridgehead on Malaya Zemlya, will forever remain in the memory of the people. The city’s defenders pinned down Nazi forces, thus largely ensuring victory for the Red Army in the Battle of the Caucasus and preventing the enemy from transferring reinforcements to other key front sectors. We will always honour the heroic deeds of the generation of victors in the Great Patriotic War and remain faithful to their covenants of love and devotion to the Motherland.” Topics Great Patriotic War Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 12, 2023, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72292 Text version
