Events Meeting with President of Turkmenistan Serdar Berdimuhamedov June 29, 2022 14:00 Ashgabat Vladimir Putin held a meeting with President of Turkmenistan Serdar Berdimuhamedov Berdimuhamedov Serdar President of Turkmenistan on the sidelines of the 6 th Caspian Summit. * * * Earlier, the President of Russia also had a conversation with Speaker of the People’s Council of Turkmenistan Gurbanguly Berdimuhamedov Berdimuhamedov Gurbanguly .
