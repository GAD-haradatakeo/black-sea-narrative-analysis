Events Greetings to finalists and winners of National Open Championship of Creative Competencies ArtMasters 2024 Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to the finalists of the National Open Championship of Creative Competencies ArtMasters 2024, as well as its guests. October 1, 2024 19:50 The message reads, in part: “I am pleased to note that your project is gaining authority with each passing year, helping young men and women make their mark, realise their creative potential, and find their passion. Thanks to ArtMasters, many contestants have successfully begun their professional careers, achieved victories and remarkable accomplishments. This season, more than 40,000 talented young people from all Russian regions and foreign countries took part in this project. This demonstrates the growing interest in the contest, Russian culture, and our training system for creative professionals. I am confident that participation in the championship, substantive discussions with renowned scientists, cultural figures, artists, and journalists will help you improve your professional skills, exchange fresh, extraordinary ideas and plans with your peers, and gain unique teamwork experience. After all, ArtMasters is a large, friendly family that values collaboration and mutual support.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 1, 2024, 19:50 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75239 Text version
