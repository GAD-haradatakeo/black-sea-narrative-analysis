Events Condolences to President of China Xi Jinping September 7, 2022 13:00 Vladimir Putin sent a message of condolences to President of the People's Republic of China Xi Jinping Xi Jinping President of People's Republic of China in connection with the tragic consequences of an earthquake in Sichuan province. The President of Russia expressed sincere sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends and wished a speedy recovery to all those who have suffered as a result of this natural disaster.
