Events Condolences over the death of Alexander Maslyakov Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to the family and friends of famous television host Alexander Maslyakov over his death. September 9, 2024 12:00 The message reads, in part: “Alexander Maslyakov has passed away. He had great, multifaceted talent, and was one of those passionate professionals dedicated to their work and calling who personify our television and define its image. Of course, his name is inextricably linked with the Club of the Funny and Inventive People (KVN). In many ways, it is thanks to Alexander Maslyakov that KVN became a unique phenomenon in Russian culture, united gifted youth, and earned nationwide popularity.” Alexander Maslyakov passed away at the age of 82.
