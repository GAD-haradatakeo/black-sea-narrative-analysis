Events Greetings to the festival Ballads of the Special Military Operation: Time Has Chosen Us Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants, organisers and guests of the festival, Ballads of the Special Military Operation: Time Chose Us, dedicated to Heroes of the Fatherland Day. December 6, 2024 18:55 The message reads, in part: “We take justifiable pride in the vibrant and diverse array of musical compositions that pay tribute to the valiant chapters of our Fatherland’s history and the heroic deeds of our fathers, grandfathers, and great-grandfathers. This unique cultural and spiritual legacy fortifies the bonds between eras and generations, serving as a cornerstone of patriotic education for the youth. Therefore, I regard your project as a significant and necessary initiative, one that continues the esteemed traditions of the war song, which in our nation is deeply cherished and leaves no one indifferent. Today, you will experience works of exceptional significance, as their creators and performers are participants and veterans of the special military operation, as well as the wives, mothers, and relatives of the heroes. These talented and heartfelt songs convey honestly and without embellishment what is precious to everyone – loyal friends and comrades, courage and virtue, and love for the Motherland. I am confident that this event will be executed with high creative and organisational standards, making it a remarkable and memorable occasion for both performers and attendees.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 6, 2024, 18:55 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75791 Text version
