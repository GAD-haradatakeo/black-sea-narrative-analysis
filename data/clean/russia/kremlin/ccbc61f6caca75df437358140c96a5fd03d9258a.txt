Events Greetings to editorial staff of Kommersant daily Vladimir Putin congratulated the editorial staff of Kommersant on the newspaper’s 35 th anniversary. December 9, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “The publication of the first issue of Kommersant was an important event in this country’s public life. The newspaper’s close-knit team of talented journalists addressed information and creative tasks resonant with the times, providing a platform for prominent political writers, correspondents, leading politicians, economists and experts. The newspaper offered them an opportunity to express their viewpoints, engage in debate, and share forecasts and analyses. This enabled Kommersant to develop an original and readily recognisable style and retain its leadership in the Russian media market. I am confident that the key for this enduring and intransient success lies in your team of like-minded associates. You strive to uphold the finest traditions of journalism by providing your readers with timely and relevant news, as well as implementing diverse and substantial projects.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 9, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75804 Text version
