Events Vladimir Putin sent a message to President of Slovakia Zuzana Caputova The President expressed support and wished early and full recovery to Prime Minister of Slovakia Robert Fico who was injured in the assassination attempt. May 15, 2024 18:50 The message reads, in part: “This atrocious crime cannot be justified. I know Robert Fico as a courageous and strong-willed person. I truly hope these personal qualities will help him overcome this harsh situation.” Geography Slovakia Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: May 15, 2024, 18:50 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74047 Text version
