Events Meeting with permanent members of the Security Council The President held a briefing session, via videoconference, with permanent members of the Security Council. March 18, 2022 13:50 Novo-Ogaryovo, Moscow Region The discussion focused on current international developments and the ongoing special operation of the Russian Armed Forces in Ukraine. The President informed permanent members of the Security Council on the details of his numerous telephone conversations with international colleagues.
