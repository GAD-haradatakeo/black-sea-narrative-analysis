Events Gala event on 100th anniversary of Adygea, Kabardino-Balkaria and Karachayevo-Circassia Vladimir Putin spoke before the participants of a ceremony marking the 100 th anniversary of the Republic of Adygea, the Republic of Kabardino-Balkaria and the Republic of Karachayevo-Circassia September 20, 2022 19:35 The Kremlin, Moscow President of Russia Vladimir Putin: Friends, Residents of the Republic of Adygea, the Republic of Kabardino-Balkaria, and the Republic of Karachayevo-Circassia, Please accept my sincere and heartfelt congratulations on the 100 th anniversary of your republics. This is a big and significant date for you and for our country, for all of Russia. The creation of autonomous entities in 1922 was an important event in the history and life of the peoples of the three regions and a significant step forward in the history of our state as a common home for people of different ethnicities who are united by common values and aspirations and a common patriotic responsibility for the future of our great and vast Motherland. The decades that followed convincingly proved that this decision was correct and far-sighted, as was another decision that had been made long before that; I am referring to the truly fateful choice of the peoples of modern Adygea, Kabardino-Balkaria and Karachayevo-Cirkassia on rapprochement with the Russian state. This new status contributed to the rise of the economy and the social sphere in the autonomous regions, the preservation and promotion of the distinctive traditions and the rich cultural and spiritual heritage of all the peoples who live here, and opened up broad opportunities to receive an education and to fulfill their professional potential. To be continued.
