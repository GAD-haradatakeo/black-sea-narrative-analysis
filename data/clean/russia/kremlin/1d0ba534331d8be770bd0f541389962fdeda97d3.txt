Events Executive order on awarding Order of Courage to Boris Maksudov (posthumously) Vladimir Putin signed Executive Order On Awarding the Order of Courage to Boris Maksudov. November 25, 2023 08:00 Under the Executive Order, Boris Maksudov, a correspondent with the Rossiya 24 television channel of the National State Television and Radio Broadcasting Company, was awarded the Order of Courage posthumously for his bravery and dedication in the course of performing his professional duties.
