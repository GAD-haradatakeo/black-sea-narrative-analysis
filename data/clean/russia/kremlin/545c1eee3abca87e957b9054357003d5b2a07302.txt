Events Laying flowers at the Monument to Soviet Red Army Soldiers in Harbin May 17, 2024 07:40 Harbin On the second day of his state visit to the People’s Republic of China, President of Russia Vladimir Putin laid flowers at the Monument to Soviet Red Army Soldiers who perished while liberating Northeast China. The monument was erected in Hongbo Square in Harbin on November 7, 1945.
