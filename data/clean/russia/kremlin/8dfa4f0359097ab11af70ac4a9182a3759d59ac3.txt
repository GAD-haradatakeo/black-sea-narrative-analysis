Events Greetings to participants in the BRICS Chief Justices Forum Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to the participants in the BRICS Chief Justices Forum. June 19, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “It is gratifying to see that your meetings in this format are becoming traditional, with busy agendas and deep and substantive discussions. This time, you are also going to discuss a wide range of problems related to ensuring people’s social and other rights, judicial protection of entrepreneurs and investors, and the introduction of information technologies into judicial activities. Importantly, the forum serves to promote trust and cooperation between BRICS members, develop our legal systems, and helps achieve mutual understanding on such issues as improving judicial proceedings and ensuring uniformity in delivering justice. This dialogue fully aligns with our priorities in the field of building up integration ties between the BRICS countries based on equality and respect for each other’s interests. I believe that the forum will be held in a businesslike and constructive manner, and its results will be widely sought after.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 19, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74340 Text version
