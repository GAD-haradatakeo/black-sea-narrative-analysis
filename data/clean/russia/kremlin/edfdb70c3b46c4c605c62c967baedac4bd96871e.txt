Events Greetings to the 30th Window to Europe Russian film festival Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants, organisers and guests of the 30 th Window to Europe Russian film festival. August 5, 2022 19:30 The message reads, in part: ”For many years, your festival has been welcoming a large, close-knit group of participants and film lovers to Vyborg. It exposes the public to great films and the latest achievements in Russian cinematography, provides excellent opportunities for a deep and engaged discussion of current professional issues and long-term plans, and serves as a unique networking platform for strengthening business and personal contacts. It is gratifying that over the years the forum has maintained its established traditions and is well-known for its special, spiritual atmosphere. I am sure that this anniversary festival, which is taking place in the 100 th birth anniversary year of Stanislav Rostotsky and the 90 th birth anniversary year of Andrei Tarkovsky, will become an unforgettable celebration of creativity and friendly communication for all those present. And of course, it will help promote and popularise the best Russian feature, documentary and animated films.” Geography Leningrad Region Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 5, 2022, 19:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69122 Text version
