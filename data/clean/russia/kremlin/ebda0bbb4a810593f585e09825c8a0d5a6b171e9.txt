Events Greetings to residents of Ufa and the Republic of Bashkortostan Vladimir Putin congratulated residents of Ufa and the Republic of Bashkortostan on the 450 th anniversary of the republican capital. August 30, 2024 14:50 The message reads, in part: “This impressive anniversary is a good occasion to reflect on the city’s rich history and remember the names of your prominent fellow-countrymen who have brought fame to Ufa through their military victories and labour achievements, as well as their scientific, creative and sports triumphs. It is important to note that while preserving its unique identity, the city is also looking towards the future. Its economy and social sphere are developing dynamically, with the launch of modern hi-tech plants and new schools, theatres, cultural centres, and stadiums. Ufa has all the necessary infrastructure to host large-scale international forums and prestigious sporting competitions. These achievements are built on the foundations of respect and mutual understanding between people belonging to different ethnic groups, faiths, and cultures. They are also a testament to the initiative and hard work of Ufa residents, who set ambitious goals for themselves and achieve the desired results. I am confident that the love for your native city and your sincere desire to make a contribution of your own to its advancement will continue to help you carry out the plans that have been mapped out for the benefit of Bashkortostan and the whole of Russia.” Topics Regions Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 30, 2024, 14:50 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74967 Text version
