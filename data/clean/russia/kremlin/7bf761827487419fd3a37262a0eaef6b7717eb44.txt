Events Congratulations to the Russian national team of school students that took part in the 56th International Chemistry Olympiad in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia Vladimir Putin congratulated the Russian national team of school students that took part in the 56 th International Chemistry Olympiad in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia: Vadim Kharisov, Timur Akhmedov, Alexei Sharpilo and Matvei Vasiyarov. July 30, 2024 19:00 The message reads, in part: “I congratulate you on the triumphant performance at the 56 th International Chemistry Olympiad in Riyadh. In a serious and tense struggle, you showed team unity and a truly winning spirit and convincingly confirmed the strong position of the Russian scientific school of chemistry. And of course, you share this impressive and well-deserved success with your teachers and coaches, parents and friends.” * * * Vadim Kharisov, Timur Akhmedov, Alexei Sharpilo and Matvei Vasiyarov have won gold medals at the Olympiad.
