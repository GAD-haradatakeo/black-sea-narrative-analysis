Events Telephone conversation with President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev March 23, 2024 13:40 Shavkat Mirziyoyev Mirziyoyev Shavkat President of Uzbekistan strongly condemned the heinous terrorist attack at Crocus City Hall, expressed his sincere condolences in connection with the tragedy, and asked to convey words of support to the victims’ families and wishes for a speedy recovery to the injured. Both sides reaffirmed their intention to continue close cooperation to counter terrorism.
