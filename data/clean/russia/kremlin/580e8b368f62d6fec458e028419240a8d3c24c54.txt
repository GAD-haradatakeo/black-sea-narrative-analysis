Events Birthday greetings to three-time Olympic champion, President of the Cross-Country Skiing Federation of Russia Yelena Vyalbe Vladimir Putin wished a happy birthday to three-time Olympic champion, Merited Master of Sport and President of the Cross-Country Skiing Federation of Russia Yelena Vyalbe. April 20, 2023 11:00 The message reads, in part: “You have written outstanding and unforgettable pages into the history of elite sport and won awards at the most prestigious international competitions, becoming a three-time Olympic champion. Your remarkable success, fruitful coaching, mentoring and public activities, as well as your work in the responsible position of President of the Cross-Country Skiing Federation of Russia, serve as a worthy example of diligence, determination and loyalty to your chosen cause.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 20, 2023, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70961 Text version
