Events Congratulations to Anna Shcherbakova on winning women’s figure skating event at 2022 Winter Olympics Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to Anna Shcherbakova, winner of the women’s figure skating singles competition at the XXIV Olympic Winter Games in Beijing. February 17, 2022 18:00 The message reads, in part: “Your brilliant performance on Beijing ice demonstrated your excellent skills and enormous creative potential. The title of 2022 Olympic champion is a well-deserved reward for your hard work, perseverance and commitment. I also send my best regards to your coaches, mentors and, of course, your teammates.” Topics Sport Publication status Published in sections: News , Commissions and Councils , Council for the Development of Physical Culture and Sport Publication date: February 17, 2022, 18:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/67801 Text version
