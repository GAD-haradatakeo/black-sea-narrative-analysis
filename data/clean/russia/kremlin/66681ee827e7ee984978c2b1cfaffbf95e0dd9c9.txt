Events Birthday greetings to Karen Shakhnazarov Vladimir Putin sent greetings to Karen Shakhnazarov, film director, screenwriter, producer, and General Director of Mosfilm Cinema Concern, on his 70th birthday. July 8, 2022 15:00 The message reads, in part: “A talented film director, organiser, as well as a dedicated and enthusiastic professional, you have achieved impressive success and created films that are rightfully considered among the classics of our national cinema. And of course, I must mention your years-long prolific work as head of the legendary Mosfilm, Russia’s largest film production company.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 8, 2022, 15:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68846 Text version
