Events Greetings to the 9th Congress of National Medical Chamber Medical Community Union Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants in the 9th Congress of the National Medical Chamber Medical Community Union. October 20, 2023 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the past years, the National Medical Chamber has established itself as one of the major and most authoritative public associations in the field of healthcare. The issues that are raised at your forums are of national significance, without exaggeration. First of all, this applies to the preservation and strengthening of public health, demographic development, support for families, motherhood and childhood, and ensuring environmental well-being. You also pay special attention to medical science, promoting the values ​​of active living and physical fitness, training qualified specialists, and improving legislation in this field. I am convinced that the rich agenda and outstanding participants will allow you to discuss these and other equally important industry issues at the highest level and exchange experience, as well as to come up with initiatives and proposals during the congress that will undoubtedly enjoy great demand.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 20, 2023, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72570 Text version
