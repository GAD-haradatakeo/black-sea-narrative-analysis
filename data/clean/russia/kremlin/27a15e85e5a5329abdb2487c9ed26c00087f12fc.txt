Events Vladimir Putin spoke by telephone with Agata Bylkova Vladimir Putin spoke by telephone with 8 year-old Agata Bylkova from the Kurgan Region, a participant in the New Year Tree of Wishes campaign January 3, 2023 16:30 Agata told the President about realising her dream: she and her family visited the Gornoknyazevsk Natural and Ethnographic Centre (Yamal-Nenets Autonomous Area), where she saw the northern lights and rode in a reindeer sleigh. Earlier, Vladimir Putin fulfilled Alexandra Titarenko’s New Year wish: she visited Father Frost's home. * * * On December 22, 2022, during his visit to the Manege, the President took part in the New Year Tree of Wishes nationwide charity campaign for the fifth time: he chose three Christmas balls-postcards with children's New Year dreams.
