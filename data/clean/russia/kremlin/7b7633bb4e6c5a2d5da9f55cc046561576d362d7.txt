Events Telephone conversation with President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko. July 11, 2022 12:25 During their telephone conversation, the leaders reviewed topical matters of bilateral trade and economic cooperation. The presidents focused on the situation regarding the illegal restrictions Lithuania imposed on freight transit to the Kaliningrad Region. In this context, they discussed certain possible joint measures. Vladimir Putin and Alexander Lukashenko Lukashenko Alexander President of Belarus reaffirmed their mutual commitment to consistently strengthening the Russian-Belarusian partnership and alliance. The leaders agreed to remain in close contact.
