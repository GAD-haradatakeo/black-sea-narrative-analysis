Events Greetings to Investigative Committee staff and veterans Vladimir Putin congratulated the staff and veterans of the Investigative Committee of the Russian Federation on the 12th anniversary of the Committee's establishment as an independent federal state agency. January 13, 2023 14:00 The message reads, in part: ”Over the past years, the Investigative Committee has become a significant link in the national law enforcement system. You steadfastly defend the interests of the state, the rights and freedoms of Russian citizens, making a massive contribution to the fight against crime and corruption. Today your excellent professional and personal qualities, integrity, courage and loyalty to duty are in high demand to support the special military operation and to establish law and order in the new regions of the Federation. Under the most difficult conditions, the staff of the investigative agencies are accomplishing their important tasks and, together with other law enforcement agencies, are participating in the investigation of neo-Nazi crimes against civilians and serving their homeland with honesty and diligence.“ Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: January 13, 2023, 14:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70352 Text version
