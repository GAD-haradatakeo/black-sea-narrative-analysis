Events Condolences to Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan. August 15, 2022 12:20 The message reads, in part: “Please accept my deepest condolences over the death of people as a result of a fire in a shopping centre in Yerevan. Please convey my words of sincere sympathy and support to the families of those killed and wishes for a speedy recovery to all the injured.” Geography Armenia Persons Pashinyan Nikol Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 15, 2022, 12:20 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69162 Text version
