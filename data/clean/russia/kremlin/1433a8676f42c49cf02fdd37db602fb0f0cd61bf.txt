Events Greetings to the second National Forum of the Russian Red Cross Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the participants in the second National Forum of the Russian Red Cross. November 27, 2023 10:25 The message reads, in part: “Since its foundation, the Russian Red Cross has always united sensitive, caring, and selfless individuals who responded to the misfortune of others with compassion and came to the aid of those in need of care and attention. It is gratifying that you continue to uphold the wonderful traditions of mercy and self-sacrifice, participating in the implementation of large-scale social, medical, and charitable programmes, and promoting the donation movement. Today, as we face serious challenges, your activities are highly important and in demand. I am pleased to note the courage and dedication of the Russian Red Cross volunteers in providing comprehensive assistance to forcibly displaced persons from the war zone, as well as your invaluable contribution to supporting veterans and families of participants in the special military operation. Such great and responsible work deserves the utmost respect and recognition.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 27, 2023, 10:25 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72827 Text version
