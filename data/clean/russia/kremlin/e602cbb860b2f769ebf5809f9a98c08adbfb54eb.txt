Events Greetings on opening of 11th Congress of Russian public organisation Young Guard of United Russia Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants in the 11 th Congress of the Russian public organisation Young Guard of United Russia. February 20, 2024 11:00 The message reads, in part: “In recent years, the Young Guard has gained significant credibility, proving itself as an effective force capable of addressing crucial issues for the people and contributing to key aspects of the country’s development. Your patriotic, volunteer, social, and charitable initiatives consistently receive widespread public support and produce tangible, concrete results. In the current challenging environment, there is a great need for your responsible civil stance and devoted assistance to the participants in the special military operation and residents of Donbass and Novorossiya. I know that many Young Guard members are valiantly fighting side by side with their comrades-in-arms against neo-Nazis, defending Russia. Please, convey my best wishes to them.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 20, 2024, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73504 Text version
