Events Telephone conversation with President of Kazakhstan Kassym‑Jomart Tokayev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan Kassym-Jomart Tokayev July 12, 2022 12:20 The leaders discussed current issues of bilateral cooperation within the framework of the agreements reached following the Russia-Kazakhstan summit talks in St Petersburg on June 17. They reaffirmed their mutual resolve to further strengthen the strategic partnership and alliance between the two countries, and to consistently implement concrete trade, economic and energy projects.
