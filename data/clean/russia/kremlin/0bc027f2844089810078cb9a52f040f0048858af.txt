Events Congratulations to the winner of the World Rapid and Blitz Chess Championships 2023 Valentina Gunina Vladimir Putin congratulated Valentina Gunina, the winner of the World Rapid and Blitz Chess Championships 2023 in Samarkand. January 10, 2024 19:30 The message reads, in part: “You confidently passed the tournament distance, you were stronger than your famous rivals and rightfully became a two-time world blitz champion. Your success is a bright, significant page in the victorious chronicle of the Russian school of chess.” Publication status Published in sections: News , Commissions and Councils , Council for the Development of Physical Culture and Sport Publication date: January 10, 2024, 19:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73248 Text version
