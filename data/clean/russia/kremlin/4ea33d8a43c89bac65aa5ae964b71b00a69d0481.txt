Events Greetings on Automobile and Municipal Passenger Transport Day Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the current and retired employees of the automobile and municipal passenger transport sector on their professional holiday, Automobile and Municipal Passenger Transport Day. October 27, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Automobile and municipal passenger transport is an important and integral part of our country’s transport network. It plays a significant role in strengthening interregional and international ties, expanding industrial production, agriculture and business operations, and largely determines people’s living standards. The state devotes special attention to modernising the sector and improving its infrastructure. We continue to carry out a large-scale upgrade of the public transport network under national projects and programmes, introducing modern technologies and engineering solutions that meet advanced safety and environmental standards. We work to improve the level of services provided and make them more affordable and create favourable conditions for employing young and skilled specialists in the automobile transport sector. I am confident that competence, a responsible attitude towards work, reliance on labour traditions and the experience of predecessors and the industry’s veterans will allow you to continue addressing your objectives in the future.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 27, 2024, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75419 Text version
