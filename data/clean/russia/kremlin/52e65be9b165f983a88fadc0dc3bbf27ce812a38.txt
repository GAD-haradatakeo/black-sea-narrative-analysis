Events Condolences on the death of Pele Vladimir Putin sent condolences to President of the Federative Republic of Brazil Jair Messias Bolsonaro in connection with the death of the great Brazilian football player Pele. December 30, 2022 13:10 The message reads, in part: “Edson Arantes do Nascimento was an outstanding son of the Brazilian nation. Thanks to his talent, unique skill, and his beautiful and mesmerising game, football has become a favourite sport for millions of people around the world, including Russia. I was fortunate to personally meet that wonderful man, and I will forever cherish the memory of our meeting. Please convey my sincere sympathy to the family and friends of the legendary Pele, as well as to all citizens of your country.” Geography Brazil Persons Bolsonaro Jair Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 30, 2022, 13:10 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70306 Text version
