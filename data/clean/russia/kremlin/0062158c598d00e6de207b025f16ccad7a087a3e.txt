Events Greetings on Tiger Day Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the organisers, participants and the guests of the Tiger Day celebrations in Vladivostok. September 29, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “The population of the Amur tiger in Russia’s Far East is reliably protected and is increasing every year thanks to the efforts by biologists, local history specialists, conservationists and gamekeepers. I would like to note that such significant results have been achieved with active contribution from enthusiasts, volunteers of educational and humanitarian projects, and employees of the Amur Tiger Centre. Your initiative and concern, as well as your truly caring attitude to Russia’s natural heritage deserve respect and recognition. It is important to continue this work based on the best practices and advanced scientific, organisational and methodological approaches, to actively contribute to the implementation of government programmes aimed at environmental protection and raising the level of environmental culture in society.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 29, 2024, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75201 Text version
