Events Telephone conversations with President of Kyrgyzstan and President of Tajikistan September 18, 2022 13:45 During his telephone conversations with President of the Kyrgyz Republic Sadyr Japarov Japarov Sadyr President of the Kyrgyz Republic and President of the Republic of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon Rahmon Emomali President of Tajikistan , Vladimir Putin discussed the situation on the Kyrgyz-Tajik border. Vladimir Putin called on his counterparts to prevent further escalation and to take steps for a quick settlement of the situation through exclusively peaceful political and diplomatic means, and confirmed Russia’s readiness to provide the necessary assistance to ensure stability in the Kyrgyz-Tajik border areas. The leaders agreed to maintain contact.
