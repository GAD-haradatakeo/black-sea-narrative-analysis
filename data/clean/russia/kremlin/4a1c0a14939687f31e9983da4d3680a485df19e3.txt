Events Condolences on the passing of Nikolai Ryzhkov Vladimir Putin extended his condolences to the family and friends of Nikolai Ryzhkov following his passing. February 29, 2024 08:30 The message reads, in part: “Nikolai Ryzhkov’s distinguished professional career and great contribution to addressing the nation’s most pressing challenges deserve the highest respect. From the early stages of his career, to key governmental roles and prominent public roles, he remained steadfast in his ideals and principles, always placing the interests of the Fatherland and its people above all else.” * * * Nikolai Ryzhkov, a prominent Soviet and Russian political figure, passed away on February 28 at the age of 94. Nikolai Ryzhkov served as Chairman of the Council of Ministers of the USSR from 1985 to 1991. He was also a deputy of the State Duma of the 2 nd and 3 rd convocations. Additionally, from September 2003 to September 2023, he served as a member of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation as a senator. Nikolai Ryzhkov was awarded many state decorations of the USSR and the Russian Federation, including the title of Hero of Labour, orders For Services to the Fatherland, 1 st and 4 th degree, and the orders of Honour.
