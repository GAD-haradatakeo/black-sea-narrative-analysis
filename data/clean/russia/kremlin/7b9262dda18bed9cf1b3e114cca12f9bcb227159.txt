Events Congratulations to President of Kyrgyzstan Sadyr Japarov Vladimir Putin sent a message of congratulations to President of the Kyrgyz Republic Sadyr Japarov on the country’s national holiday, Independence Day. August 31, 2023 09:00 The message reads, in part: “Relations between our states hinge on the principles of strategic partnership and alliance. Bilateral cooperation continues to develop successfully in various spheres. We collaborate constructively within the Eurasian Economic Union, the Collective Security Treaty Organisation, the Commonwealth of Independent States, the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation and other multilateral associations. I am confident that strengthening diverse Russian-Kyrgyzstani ties fully meets the interests of our friendly nations and helps enhance security and stability in the Eurasian region.” Geography Kyrgyzstan Topics Foreign policy Persons Japarov Sadyr Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: August 31, 2023, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72156 Text version
