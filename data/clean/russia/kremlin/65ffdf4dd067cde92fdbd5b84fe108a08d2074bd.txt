Events Birthday greetings to Member of Russian Academy of Sciences, Moscow State University Rector Viktor Sadovnichy Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to Member of the Russian Academy of Sciences, Lomonosov Moscow State University Rector Viktor Sadovnichy on his 84 th birthday. April 3, 2023 09:15 The message reads, in part: “You rightfully enjoy great authority and prestige as an outstanding scientist and organiser and as the rector of a leading Russian university. Colleagues, Moscow State University students and alumni, all those who have had the opportunity to become acquainted with you invariably note your exceptional professional qualities, tremendous creative potential and your attentive and kind attitude towards people.” Topics Universities Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 3, 2023, 09:15 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70855 Text version
