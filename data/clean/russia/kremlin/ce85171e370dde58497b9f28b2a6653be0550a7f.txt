Events Greetings on 35th anniversary of Interfax news agency Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the staff of the Interfax news agency on the agency’s 35 th anniversary. September 5, 2024 13:00 The message reads, in part: “Founded in 1989 by talented professionals and passionate enthusiasts, Interfax was the first independent news agency in the country, leaving a lasting mark on the history of modern Russian media. Today, Interfax stands as a recognised leader in both the domestic and global media markets, supported by a large and cohesive team of correspondents, editors, translators, and information technology specialists. It is encouraging to see how well you know and love your work, uphold the traditions established by your senior colleagues, and remain open to new ideas. Equally important is your commitment to mentoring young journalists, fostering their creative thinking and instilling a sense of responsibility in their work. This dedication helps Interfax maintain high professional standards and successfully compete with top international media organisations.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 5, 2024, 13:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75070 Text version
