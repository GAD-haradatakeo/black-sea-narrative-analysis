Events Condolences to President of Nepal Ramchandra Paudel and Prime Minister of Nepal Pushpa Kamal Dahal Vladimir Putin sent a message of condolences to President of Nepal Ramchandra Paudel and Prime Minister of Nepal Pushpa Kamal Dahal over the tragic consequences of a devastating earthquake in the country’s western districts. November 4, 2023 10:40 The message reads, in part: “Please convey words of sincere sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends and wishes for a speedy recovery to all those injured in this disaster.” Geography Nepal Topics Foreign policy Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 4, 2023, 10:40 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72677 Text version
