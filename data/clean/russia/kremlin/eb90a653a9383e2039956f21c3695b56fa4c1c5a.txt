Events Greetings on the opening of the Terra Scientia National Educational Youth Forum Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the participants in the Terra Scientia National Educational Youth Forum held in Solnechnogorsk, Moscow Region. July 5, 2022 19:00 The message reads, in part: “This year, instead of a traditional forum, the participants will attend themed sessions: The City, Education, Business, Society, Administration, Security and Politics. I am confident that everyone will choose a topic they like and are dedicated to. At the forum, young scientists, teachers, officials, law-enforcement officers, NGO representatives and students will be able to demonstrate their professional and creative potential, offer brand new, extraordinary ideas, and acquire knowledge and new skills. You have a busy programme ahead of you, full of discussions, seminars and meetings with like-minded people, potential partners and employers. And, of course, you will definitely find time to relax and socialise.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 5, 2022, 19:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68823 Text version
