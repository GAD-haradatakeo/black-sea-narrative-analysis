Events Greetings to the leadership and members of Russian Popular Front Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the leadership and members of the Russian Popular Front public movement on the 10 th anniversary of its inaugural congress. June 11, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “These past years have seen the formation and active development of this broad social movement and the strengthening of its consolidating and creative potential. Largely due to your energetic efforts, awareness and dedication, branches of the Popular Front have now been created in all regions of our country, each of them bringing together a large, united team of like-minded people. It is important that you work in the interests of our citizens, view their needs and aspirations as your utmost priority, and provide assistance in solving pressing issues – particularly, in such relevant areas as improving education and healthcare, urban and rural development, housing and utilities, and promoting volunteer work. I would particularly like to mention your painstaking and much-needed efforts to handle appeals from millions of citizens who call during the Direct Line. Today, as we face serious challenges, the Popular Front views the support of the fighters taking part in the special military operation, as well as residents of Russia’s new regions, as its core task. Major humanitarian and charity initiatives such as #WeAreTogether, Everything for Victory and other projects, serve this goal as well. I know that you have many important, urgent tasks to implement. I wish you success in achieving your plans and all the best.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 11, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71337 Text version
