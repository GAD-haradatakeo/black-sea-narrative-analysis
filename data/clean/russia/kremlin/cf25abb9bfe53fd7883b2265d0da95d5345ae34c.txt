Events Telephone conversation with President of Venezuela Nicolas Maduro Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela Nicolas Maduro at the Venezuelan side’s initiative. May 4, 2023 21:00 Nicolas Maduro Maduro Nicolas President of the Bolivarian Republic of Venezuela resolutely condemned the Kiev regime’s attempt to attack the Kremlin with drones, and expressed solidarity and support for Russia on behalf of the people of Venezuela. During the telephone conversation, the presidents also discussed important issues of further promoting bilateral strategic partnership, including the implementation of joint trade, economic, energy and humanitarian projects. The presidents agreed to continue contacts at different levels.
