Events Telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan at the initiative of the Armenian side. July 6, 2022 19:25 There was further discussion of practical aspects of the implementation of trilateral agreements between the leaders of Russia, Armenia and Azerbaijan of November 9, 2020 , January 11 and November 26, 2021. In particular, views were exchanged on ways to ensure security along the border between Armenia and Azerbaijan and restore economic, transport, and logistic ties in the South Caucasus. Nikol Pashinyan informed Vladimir Putin about the recent contacts aimed at normalising relations between Armenia and Turkey.
