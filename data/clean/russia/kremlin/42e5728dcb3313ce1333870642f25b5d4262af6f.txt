Events Condolences to Chairman of State Affairs of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea Kim Jong-un Vladimir Putin sent condolences to Chairman of State Affairs of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea Kim Jong-un over the tragic consequences of floods in the north-western provinces of the country. August 3, 2024 14:45 The message reads, in part: “We share the pain and sorrow of the friendly people of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea. We are confident that the situation in the affected areas will be promptly brought back to normal. You can always count on our help and support.” President of Russia asked to convey his words of sympathy and support to everybody who lost their loved ones as a result of the disaster.
