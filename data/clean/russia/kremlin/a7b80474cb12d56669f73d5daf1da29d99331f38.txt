Events Greetings to the 20th Russian Congress of Children’s Rights Commissioners in the Russian Regions Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants in the 20 th Russian Congress of Children’s Rights Commissioners in the Russian Regions held in Kazan. April 26, 2023 10:45 The message reads, in part: “These congresses traditionally bring together leading experts on motherhood and childhood protection, government officials, representatives of healthcare and education facilities, and public associations. I would like to note the invariably constructive attitude of delegates who are committed to discussing topical issues of importance for the state and citizens that concern the protection of children’s rights and children’s balanced and comprehensive development. This year’s congress focuses on essential topics, such as medical help and prevention of diseases in children deprived of parental care, and improving the system of medical assistance in schools and children’s homes. I expect that your extensive agenda, including visits to the competent organisations in the Republic of Tatarstan and review of their best practices and experience, will contribute to the productive work of congress participants and implementation of sought-after initiatives and proposals.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 26, 2023, 10:45 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70987 Text version
