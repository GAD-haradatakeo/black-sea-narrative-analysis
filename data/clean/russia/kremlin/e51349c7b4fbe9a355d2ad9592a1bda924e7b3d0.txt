Events Telephone conversation with President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev. August 16, 2022 15:45 The two presidents discussed preparations for the upcoming summit of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation Background information Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) in Samarkand this September. In this context, the SCO’s high international standing and its growing contribution to strengthening regional stability and security were noted. Further development of bilateral strategic partnership and alliance was also touched upon.
