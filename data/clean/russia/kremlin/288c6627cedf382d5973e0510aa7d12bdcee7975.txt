Events Telephone conversation with President of Turkmenistan Serdar Berdimuhamedov During their telephone conversation, Vladimir Putin and President of Turkmenistan Serdar Berdimuhamedov exchanged greetings on the 30 th anniversary of diplomatic ties between the two countries. April 8, 2022 13:10 The two leaders discussed topical issues on the bilateral agenda and prospects for cooperation in regional formats, including within the Caspian Five. They reaffirmed their mutual resolve to further promote the strategic partnership between Russia and Turkmenistan. Vladimir Putin invited Serdar Berdimuhamedov to visit Russia. The invitation was accepted with gratitude.
