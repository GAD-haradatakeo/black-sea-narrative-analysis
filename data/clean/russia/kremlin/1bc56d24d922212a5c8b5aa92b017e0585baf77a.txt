Events Executive Order awarding title of Hero of Russian Federation to Vladimir Zhoga Vladimir Putin signed the Executive Order On Awarding the Title of Hero of the Russian Federation to Vladimir Zhoga . March 6, 2022 21:00 The title of Hero of the Russian Federation has been awarded to Vladimir Zhoga (posthumously) for acts of heroism and courage while fulfilling his civic duty.
