Events On the Day of Memory and Sorrow, the President laid a wreath to the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier On the 82 nd anniversary of the beginning of the Great Patriotic War, Vladimir Putin laid a wreath to the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier by the Kremlin wall to pay tribute to the memory of those who fell while fighting the Nazi invaders. June 22, 2023 12:15 Alexander Garden, Moscow Great Patriotic War veterans and Armed Forces veterans, as well as military personnel from the Moscow Garrison and military university graduates, took part in the ceremony. The event concluded with the ceremonial march of the honour guard squad and the military band in front of the national military glory memorial. The President went on to lay flowers at the Hero City obelisks and the monument in honour of the Cities of Military Glory.
