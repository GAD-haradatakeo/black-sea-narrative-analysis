Events Telephone conversation with President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev. January 4, 2023 13:10 The presidents reaffirmed mutual commitment to strengthening Russian-Uzbekistani relations of alliance and strategic partnership, boosting trade, and expanding mutually beneficial cooperation in various fields, including in energy. Shavkat Mirziyoyev Mirziyoyev Shavkat President of Uzbekistan expressed his gratitude for the hospitality extended during the informal summit of CIS heads of state in St Petersburg on December 26–27, 2022.
