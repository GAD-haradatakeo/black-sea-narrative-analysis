Events On November 22, the President of Russia will hold talks with the President of Cuba On November 22, Vladimir Putin will hold talks with President of the Republic of Cuba Miguel Diaz-Canel Bermudez, who is in Russia on an official visit. Announcement, November 22, 2022 Add to Calendar Calendars iCal Google The leaders are expected to discuss the current state and development prospects of the Russian-Cuban strategic partnership in the political, trade, economic, cultural and humanitarian spheres, as well as to exchange views on key topics on the international agenda. Vladimir Putin and Miguel Diaz-Canel Bermudez Diaz-Canel Bermudez Miguel President of the Republic of Cuba will also take part in the unveiling of a monument to Fidel Castro in Moscow.
