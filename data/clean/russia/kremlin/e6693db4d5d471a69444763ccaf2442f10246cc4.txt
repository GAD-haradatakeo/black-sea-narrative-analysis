Events Telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan. December 12, 2022 15:55 Following their December 9 conversation at the Supreme Eurasian Economic Council meeting in Bishkek, Vladimir Putin and Nikol Pashinyan Pashinyan Nikol Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia continued discussing issues of implementing the fundamental agreements between the leaders of Russia, Armenia and Azerbaijan. They focused on efforts to ensure security on the Armenian–Azerbaijani border and projects to reinstate transport and logistics links and trade and economic ties in the South Caucasus.
