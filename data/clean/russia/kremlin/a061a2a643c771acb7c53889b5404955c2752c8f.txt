Events Greetings to current and retired employees of the Kirov subsidiary of Apatit Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the current and retired employees of the Kirov subsidiary of Apatit shareholding company on the 95 th anniversary of the enterprise, a recognised leader in the domestic and global mining industry. November 14, 2024 09:30 The message reads, in part: “You are rightfully proud of your enterprise’s history and the numerous generations of scientists, miners, chemists, production managers and specialists whose selfless work made a substantial contribution to strengthening the country’s industrial and agricultural potential and facilitating its food security. I am pleased to note that the current staff members carry on the wonderful traditions of the ore mining and processing works’ veterans and recognise the great importance of their work. You continue to expand ore enrichment capacities without slackening your pace, develop the ore and raw materials potential, introduce modern environmental standards and take part in training skilled personnel. Furthermore, the company has an impeccable business reputation and sets an example of high social responsibility. I am confident that you will continue to work conscientiously and successfully accomplish your objectives.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 14, 2024, 09:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75566 Text version
