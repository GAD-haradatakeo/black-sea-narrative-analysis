Events Greetings to participants in the closing festival event of the Day After Tomorrow youth project Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants in the closing festival event of the Day After Tomorrow youth project. November 25, 2023 16:30 The message reads in part: “Your outstanding and original project brings together talented and determined young people from the Donetsk and Lugansk people’s republics and the Zaporozhye and Kherson regions who strive to make a personal contribution to the development of their cities and towns and to help other people. I would like to note that the organisers prepared a meaningful and diverse programme for you. You were able to take part in roundtable discussions and workshops, fascinating guided tours, to compete in various sports. And, of course, during your visit to the Russia international exhibition-forum, you had the opportunity to discover our great, strong, and wonderful country. I am confident that the festival held at a very high level, filled you with creative energy and opened up new creative horizons. Various ideas and initiatives offered by each of you will certainly evolve into real actions and projects.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: November 25, 2023, 16:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72823 Text version
