Events Vladimir Putin has arrived in Baku The President of Russia is in Azerbaijan on a two-day state visit. August 18, 2024 19:45 Baku Talks to discuss further development of the strategic partnership and allied relations between Russia and Azerbaijan, as well as current international and regional issues will be on the agenda. Following the visit, a joint statement by presidents of the two countries will be adopted and a number of agreements, intergovernmental and otherwise, will be signed.
