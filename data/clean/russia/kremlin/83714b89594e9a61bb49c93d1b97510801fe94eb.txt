Events Birthday greetings to Alexander Prokhanov Vladimir Putin wished a happy 85 th birthday to writer, journalist and public activist Alexander Prokhanov. February 26, 2023 21:00 The message reads, in part: “People in Russia and abroad know you as a talented writer and journalist, an outstanding public activist and debater. You have always firmly defended your convictions and civic position at historical turning points and during difficult ordeals experienced by our country. Today, your books and articles also serve to consolidate society around the values of patriotism and responsibility for the destiny of the Fatherland.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 26, 2023, 21:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70594 Text version
