Events Vladimir Putin talked to Nikita Miroshnichenko by telephone The President talked, by telephone, with 11-year-old Nikita Miroshnichenko from Kostroma who took part in the New Year Tree of Wishes charity campaign. December 27, 2023 16:40 The Kremlin, Moscow Vladimir Putin made the fifth-grader’s dream of visiting the Hermitage Museum come true. Nikita toured the Winter Palace’s exhibits and ceremonial halls with his parents and older sister. In addition, the Miroshnichenko family was offered a tour of St Petersburg’s landmarks. The boy shared his impressions with the President during their telephone conversation. * * * On December 4, Vladimir Putin once again joined the New Year Tree of Wishes charity campaign during the #WeAreTogether international awards ceremony by picking three decoration-postcards with New Year wishes from disadvantaged children. The President made 13-year-old Ksenia Mazneva’s dream come true when she performed as a news presenter.
