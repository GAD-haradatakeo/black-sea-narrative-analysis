Events Greetings on opening of 19th Krasnoyarsk Economic Forum Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to the participants in the 19 th Krasnoyarsk Economic Forum. March 2, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “Government officials, business leaders, and experts from Russia, as well as from several other countries, converged in Krasnoyarsk to discuss topical matters on promoting Siberia’s comprehensive development, and other essential socioeconomic issues. The Krasnoyarsk Economic Forum has a packed agenda, and its sessions are streamed online for everyone to see so that the participants can get to know the rich industrial, technological, cultural and tourist potential Siberian regions have, and the truly unique opportunities they offer to investors. Let me note that Russian and foreign business leaders have always been very interested in taking part in the past forums. The agreements they signed are now being successfully implemented. I strongly believe that this forum will take place in a creative atmosphere, helping improve the business climate and expand inter-regional and international cooperation.” Geography Krasnoyarsk Territory Topics Economy and finance Regions Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: March 2, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70624 Text version
