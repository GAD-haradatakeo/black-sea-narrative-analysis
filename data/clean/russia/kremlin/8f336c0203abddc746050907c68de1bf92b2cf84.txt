Events Passover greetings Vladimir Putin extended greetings to Russian Jews on Passover. April 22, 2024 09:00 The message reads, in part: “This ancient holiday, particularly revered by followers of Judaism, serves as a reminder of significant milestones in the history of the Jewish people – liberation from centuries of slavery and the long-awaited attainment of freedom. It symbolises the triumph of the ideals of goodness and justice. I am pleased to acknowledge that the Jewish community in Russia plays an important role in fostering interethnic and interreligious dialogue, actively engages in nurturing the younger generation, educational activities, philanthropy, and charity, and prioritises the preservation of sacred spiritual, moral and family values. Such remarkable and essential work is worthy of the deepest recognition.” Topics Religion Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: April 22, 2024, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73900 Text version
