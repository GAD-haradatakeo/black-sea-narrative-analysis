Events Message to delegates and guests of the 13th convention of the Socialist Political Party A Just Russia – For the Truth Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the delegates and guests of the 13th convention of the Socialist Political Party A Just Russia – For the Truth. December 23, 2023 11:00 The message reads, in part: “Your party is one of the country’s leading political forces as it unites its supporters around the values of social justice and patriotism. It has traversed a long way in the past years and gained people’s trust through its genuine efforts and initiatives. The people appreciate your legislative initiatives aimed at protecting their rights and interests, raising their living standards, educating the younger generation, and promoting traditional spiritual and moral values. I would like to underscore your commitment to promoting social cohesion and bringing together all patriotic forces to successfully resolve the key tasks in Russia’s development. This is particularly important today, in light of the serious challenges. You have provided valuable assistance to the special military operation participants and their families, and have been relentless in your efforts to restore peaceful life in the Donetsk and Lugansk people’s republics and the Zaporozhye and Kherson regions.” Topics Parties Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 23, 2023, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73103 Text version
