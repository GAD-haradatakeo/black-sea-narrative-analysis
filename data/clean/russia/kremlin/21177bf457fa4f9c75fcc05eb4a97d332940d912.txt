Events Congratulations to the command and personnel of the 8th Guards Artillery Regiment Vladimir Putin congratulated the command and personnel of the 8 th Guards Artillery Regiment on being awarded the honourary title of “guards.” June 30, 2023 14:30 The message reads, in part: “This is a great honour and recognition of your special achievements, mass heroism and valour, fortitude and courage during your defence of the Fatherland and of Russia’s national interests. The regiment’s skillful and resolute actions in the special military operation set a standard for fulfilling combat duty, and displaying courage, dedication and high professionalism. I am convinced that you – guards warriors – will continue being loyal to your Oath, serving the Fatherland with honour and reliably ensuring the security and peaceful life of our people.” See also 8th Artillery Regiment awarded honorary Guards designation June 30, 2023 Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 30, 2023, 14:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71565 Text version
