This is an extremely important matter for the Russian Federation, considering that this country has 190 ethnic groups. Today we will also address these issues in relation to ensuring Russia’s security, in this case domestic political security, considering the efforts our ill-wishers are still taking and stepping up in order to escalate the situation inside Russia. We must do everything we can to prevent this from happening at any cost.
