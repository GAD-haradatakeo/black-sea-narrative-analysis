Events Congratulations to Russia’s national team that participated in the 53rd International Physics Olympiad 2023 in Tokyo, Japan Vladimir Putin sent congratulations to Vyacheslav Bobkov, Roman Burtsev, Vsevolod Dolya, Alexander Yershov, Yegor Potapov – the Russian team that took part in the 53 rd International Physics Olympiad in Tokyo, Japan. July 17, 2023 19:00 The message reads, in part: “You prepared well for this prestigious competition, you were stronger than your rivals from more than 80 countries and you rightfully won five gold medals. With your triumph you have convincingly confirmed the traditionally high authority of the Russian school of physics. And of course, your teachers and mentors, coaches and relatives deserve the most heartfelt words of gratitude, everyone who accompanied you as you headed towards this challenging but so significant victory in your life.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 17, 2023, 19:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71698 Text version
