Events Condolences to President of China Xi Jinping Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to President of the People’s Republic of China Xi Jinping over the tragic aftermath of an earthquake in the Gansu and Qinghai provinces. December 19, 2023 10:15 The message reads, in part: “Russia shares the grief of those who have lost their family members and friends as a result of this disaster and wishes a speedy recovery to everyone injured.” Geography People's Republic of China Topics Foreign policy Persons Xi Jinping Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 19, 2023, 10:15 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73031 Text version
