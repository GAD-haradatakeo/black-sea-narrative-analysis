Events Greetings to participants in the 6th Meeting of Heads of BRICS Prosecution Services Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to the participants in the 6 th Meeting of Heads of BRICS Prosecution Services. June 19, 2024 10:00 The message reads, in part: “Your joint work bears special significance these days. Among other things, it makes it possible for us to successfully solve common tasks facing our law enforcement agencies, it contributes to raising the authority and weight of our association and involvement of new members in its multifaceted activity. The core subject of today’s meeting, Modern Digital Solutions to Protect the Law, is extremely relevant and important. The wide use of latest technologies raises the quality of the prosecutorial work, which means that it more efficiently protects the state and society interests, as well as people’s rights and freedoms. It helps to fight such modern threats as international terrorism and religious extremism, transborder crime and illegal migration. I am sure that your dialogue will be fruitful and its results will serve to increase the human rights potential of the agencies you head and deepen cooperation for the benefit of our countries and peoples.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 19, 2024, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/74341 Text version
