Events Greetings to the 5th Congress of the Russian Political Science Association Vladimir Putin sent greetings to participants, organisers and guests of the 5 th Congress of theRussianPolitical Science Association. February 9, 2024 16:05 The message reads, in part: ”Over the years, your forum has enhanced its authority and prestige, and today it brings together leading analysts, scientists, university professors and political strategists from all over Russia and many foreign states. You will review a wide range of issues during open and constructive discussions, and you will suggest your own approaches in addressing the current objectives of developing civil society and the public-ruling institutions in this country. Of course, you will raise such important issues as streamlining education and enlightenment in the humanities. I would like to note that Russian political analysts and the representatives of socio-political sciences are actively involved in drafting and evaluating state decisions and in law-making activities. Your position is also needed as we assess key issues on the domestic and international agendas.“ Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: February 9, 2024, 16:05 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73421 Text version
