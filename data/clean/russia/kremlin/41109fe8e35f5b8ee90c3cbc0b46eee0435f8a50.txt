Events Greetings to Russian State Symphony Cinema Orchestra The President sent is greetings to the Russian State Symphony Cinema Orchestra on the occasion of its 100th anniversary. October 5, 2024 10:30 The message reads, in part: “Your renowned orchestra’s bright and events-filled history is inseparably intertwined with the development of cinema art in our country and the names of our outstanding musicians, film directors, composers and conductors. It is gratifying that you deeply respect the wonderful traditions of the many generations of your predecessors, providing music for our movies, giving many concerts, performing at major public events, and bringing joy to the audiences with your brilliant execution and extensive repertoire. I am confident that the Russian State Symphony Cinema Orchestra will keep up the high professional standard as one of the oldest performing groups in the country.” Topics Culture Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 5, 2024, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75272 Text version
