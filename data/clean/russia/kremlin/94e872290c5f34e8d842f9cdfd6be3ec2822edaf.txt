Events Greetings on the 90th anniversary of Keldysh Research Centre Vladimir Putin congratulated current staff and veterans of the State Scientific Centre of the Russian Federation, the Keldysh Research Centre, on its 90 th anniversary. October 31, 2023 11:00 The message reads, in part: “The Centre, as the successor to the legendary Jet Research Institute, has made a truly outstanding contribution to strengthening the country’s defence capability and to ensuring its leadership in space exploration. Today, celebrating its anniversary, we pay tribute of profound gratitude to many generations of your predecessors: talented scientists, designers, engineers and specialists. It is gratifying that the current staff carries on these labour traditions with dignity, boldly introducing unique technologies and innovative ideas, implementing promising projects, and manufacturing high-quality, competitive products that are in demand both in the military and civilian spheres. I am confident that your experience, competence and responsible approach to business will enable you to successfully fulfil your tasks for the benefit of the Fatherland.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 31, 2023, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72620 Text version
