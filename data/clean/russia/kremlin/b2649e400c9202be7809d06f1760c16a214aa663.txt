Events Telephone conversation with President of Turkiye Recep Tayyip Erdogan Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Turkiye Recep Tayyip Erdogan. November 24, 2024 15:50 As a follow-up to their meeting in Kazan on October 23, the presidents discussed extensively topical issues on the bilateral agenda, with a focus on making trade and economic cooperation more effective. With this in view, contacts between relevant agencies will follow. Vladimir Putin and Recep Tayyip Erdogan also touched on several international issues.
