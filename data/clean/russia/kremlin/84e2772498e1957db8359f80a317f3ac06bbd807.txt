Events Greetings to the staff of the NTV television company Vladimir Putin congratulated the staff of the NTV television company on the 30 th anniversary of its establishment. October 10, 2023 10:15 The message reads, in part: “Over the years, you have brought many creative ideas to life and filled the air with a bright palette of informative, analytical and educational projects and programmes. Today, NTV is rightly considered one of the recognised leaders of Russian television with remarkable, time-tested traditions. Your talented team’s work is invariably prompt and accurate, providing unbiased coverage of the most important events taking place in the country and the world, and demonstrating an adherence to the principles of journalistic ethics. And of course, it is gratifying that you always respect your huge audience and endeavour to take its needs and interests into account.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 10, 2023, 10:15 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/72472 Text version
