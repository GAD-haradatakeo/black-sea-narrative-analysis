Events Birthday greetings to Alexander Mitta Vladimir Putin wished a happy 90 th birthday to film director, screenwriter, actor and National Artist of Russia Alexander Mitta. March 28, 2023 09:30 The message reads, in part: “You are rightfully regarded as an outstanding film director, a talented screenwriter and actor and a person endowed with inexhaustible creative capabilities. You always work with great dedication and pass on your energy to your colleagues, making them believe in their own abilities. This makes your films so unique, interesting and rich in content.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: March 28, 2023, 09:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/70794 Text version
