Events Telephone conversation with President of Abkhazia Aslan Bzhania Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Abkhazia Aslan Bzhania at the latter’s initiative. July 1, 2023 12:00 Aslan Bzhania Bzhania Aslan President of Abkhazia expressed his full support for the actions taken by the Russian leadership to defend the rule of law and constitutional order and to ensure the security of citizens and stability in the country. The discussion also touched upon certain current issues on the bilateral agenda, including the efforts to implement plans for the socioeconomic development of Abkhazia.
