Events Congratulations to the staff of the Lebedev Physical Institute of the Russian Academy of Sciences (LPI RAS) Vladimir Putin extended his congratulations to the staff of the Lebedev Physical Institute on the 90 th anniversary of one of Russia’s oldest research centres. December 6, 2024 15:00 The message reads, in part: “This anniversary offers a wonderful opportunity to reflect on the key milestones in the development of the legendary LPI RAS, to honour its founders, generations of distinguished scholars, and its remarkable group of Nobel laureates. Their hard work, exceptional talent, enthusiasm, and dedication shaped the institute’s remarkable history. They achieved groundbreaking discoveries that significantly broadened the boundaries of knowledge. It is gratifying to see that the institute's staff holds deep respect for the rich creative legacy of their predecessors and faithfully upholds the traditions of selfless dedication to their life’s work. By actively engaging in fundamental and applied research, you are discovering new professional horizons. There is no doubt that the institute plays a vital role in training highly skilled specialists who are committed to their field and to advancing scientific inquiry.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 6, 2024, 15:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75784 Text version
