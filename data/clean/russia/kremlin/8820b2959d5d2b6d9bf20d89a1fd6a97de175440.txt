Events Telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan President Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Prime Minister of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan at the Armenian side’s initiative. April 7, 2023 12:45 The parties continued their discussion on various aspects of the current situation in Nagorno-Karabakh. In this context, they reaffirmed the importance of the consistent implementation of the fundamental agreements reached by the leaders of Russia, Armenia and Azerbaijan in 2020–2022, including those related to ensuring security and stability on the Armenian-Azerbaijani border, restoring economic and transport ties in the South Caucasus, and laying the groundwork for a peace treaty between Armenia and Azerbaijan. Vladimir Putin and Nikol Pashinyan Pashinyan Nikol Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia also touched upon several key topics of bilateral relations.
