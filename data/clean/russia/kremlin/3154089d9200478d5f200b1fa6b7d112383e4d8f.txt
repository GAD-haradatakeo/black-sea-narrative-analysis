Events Meeting with Stavropol Territory Governor Vladimir Vladimirov At the end of his working trip, Vladimir Putin held a working meeting with Stavropol Territory Governor Vladimir Vladimirov. March 5, 2024 21:50 Solnechnodolsk, Stavropol Territory Vladimir Vladimirov Vladimirov Vladimir Governor of Stavropol Territory informed the President on the region’s preparations for spring planting, the progress on import substitution of agricultural machinery for Russian or Belarusian equipment as well as the substitution of imported seeds. They also discussed the implementation of the national projects Safe and High-Quality Roads, and Education, the growth of exports, which approached 2 billion rubles, per capita GRP growth by 150 percent, and a reduction in the region’s debt burden. The governor also spoke about the regional policies to support the special military operation, including support for the fighters’ families and for the Donbass regions under Stavropol patronage, in particular, the restoration of the cultural centre in Stakhanov.
