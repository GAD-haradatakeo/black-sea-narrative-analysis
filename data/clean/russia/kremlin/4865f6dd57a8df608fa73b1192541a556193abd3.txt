Events Greetings to participants, organisers, and guests of the Patriot-2024 National Prize award ceremony Vladimir Putin sent his greetings to participants, organisers, and guests of the Patriot-2024 National Prize award ceremony, which is traditionally held on Heroes of the Fatherland Day. December 9, 2024 18:30 The message reads, in part: “Among this year’s laureates of this honorary award are leaders of patriotic centres, educators, representatives of public, volunteer and search movement organisations, participants in the special military operation – active, talented, and passionate people with a strong sense of civic duty and a responsible approach to life. You are all united by an earnest desire to serve the Motherland and contribute to the preservation of historical memory and the patriotic education of youth. I would like to stress that in today’s challenging times, when Russia faces deliberate efforts to distort the truth about our ancestors’ military victories and labour achievements, to undermine the timeless spiritual and moral ideals they passed down to us, and to outlaw the Russian language and the great Russian culture, your work takes on particular importance.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 9, 2024, 18:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75814 Text version
