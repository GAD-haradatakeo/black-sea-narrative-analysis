Events The President has instructed the Government to continue its work until a new Cabinet is formed Vladimir Putin signed Executive Order On Resignation of the Government of the Russian Federation . May 7, 2024 13:30 Following the resignation of the Government of the Russian Federation in accordance with Article 116 of the Russian Federation Constitution, the President, being guided by part 5 of Article 117 of the Constitution, has issued an Executive Order instructing the Government of the Russian Federation to continue its work until a new Government is formed.
