Events Greetings on opening of 10th International Youth Industrial Forum Engineers of the Future 2022 Vladimir Putin has sent a greeting to the participants, organisers and guests of the 10th International Youth Industrial Forum Engineers of the Future 2022 , which is being held in Tula Region with the participation of young scientists, employees of major companies and machine-building enterprises, students and postgraduates from universities in Russia and other countries. June 27, 2022 11:00 The message reads, in part: ”Our country is rightly famous for its talented engineers, designers, researchers and innovators in production. Their brilliant discoveries and developments have become an important contribution to the development of scientific and technological thought, to reinforcing the significant potential of the key branches of the national economy and the military-industrial complex. It is a pleasure that the present generation of engineering specialists is continuing the time-tested traditions of the machine-builders. Here at the forum site, you have gathered talented and energetic young people, generous with their breakthrough creative ideas. A rich business, educational and cultural programme, discussions on a wide range of professional issues, meetings with representatives of leading corporations, respected teachers and mentors and well-known public figures are in store for you. I am convinced that your forum, taking place in the first year of the Decade of Science and Technology that has been declared in Russia, will become an important and anticipated event in the industry calendar and will provide you with an excellent opportunity to improve your professional competences, and to implement constructive initiatives and effective engineering solutions.“ Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: June 27, 2022, 11:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68708 Text version
