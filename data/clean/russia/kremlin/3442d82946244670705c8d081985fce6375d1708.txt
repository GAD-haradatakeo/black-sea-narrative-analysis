Events Condolences to President of Cuba Miguel Diaz-Canel Bermudez May 7, 2022 11:20 Vladimir Putin sent a message of condolences to President of Cuba Miguel Diaz-Canel Bermudez in connection with the tragic consequences of the explosion in Hotel Saratoga in Havana. In his message, President of Russia asked to convey words of sympathy and support to the victims’ families and friends and wishes for a speedy recovery to the injured.
