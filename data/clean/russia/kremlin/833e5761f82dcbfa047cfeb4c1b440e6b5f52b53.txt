Events On June 27 Vladimir Putin will hold talks with President of the Republic of the Congo Denis Sassou Nguesso Announcement, June 27, 2024 On June 27, President of Russia Vladimir Putin will hold talks with President of the Republic of the Congo Denis Sassou Nguesso, who is currently in Russia on an official visit. The discussion will focus on prospects for the development of Russian-Congolese relations in various areas, as well as regional and international matters.
