Events Greetings on 25th anniversary of Vedomosti newspaper Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the staff of the Vedomosti newspaper on its 25 th anniversary. September 7, 2024 14:30 The message reads, in part: “Over the years, your publication has made significant strides, bringing together a team of like-minded professionals to tackle important challenges. With timely and reliable information, in-depth analysis, and a broad spectrum of viewpoints featured in both the newspaper and its website, Vedomosti has earned a respected position in the domestic business press. It has rightfully gained recognition from the country’s entrepreneurial and expert communities, along with a wide readership. It is encouraging to see that you do not rest on past achievements but stay attuned to current events, launching interesting and impactful media projects that cover not only politics, economics, and finance but also sports, environmental issues, and other relevant topics. Your commitment to upholding the best traditions of journalism is commendable.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: September 7, 2024, 14:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75068 Text version
