Events Visit to Altair Children's Talent Development Centre Vladimir Putin visited the Altair Children’s Talent Development Centre, a multi-disciplinary children's club which opened in Beslan in 2022. August 20, 2024 15:00 Beslan The centre offers extracurricular education opportunities for children and teenagers aged from five to 18. The project includes centres for young technicians and young naturalists, fully-equipped laboratories, a lecture hall, recreational halls, a medical centre, and canteens.
