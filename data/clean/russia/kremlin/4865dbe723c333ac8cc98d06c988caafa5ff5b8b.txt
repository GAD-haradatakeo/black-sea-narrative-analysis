Events The President was briefed on the situation in Moscow and Kherson regions caused by weather deterioration January 8, 2024 23:20 Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Head of the Kherson Region Vladimir Saldo Saldo Vladimir Governor of Kherson Region to hear the latter’s report on a huge temperature drop, which caused power supply disruptions and damage to power lines. The President also heard a report by Moscow Region Governor Andrei Vorobyov Vorobyov Andrei Governor of Moscow Region , who reported on the situation in districts affected by disruptions in power and heat supply. Following these reports, the President had a separate conversation with Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief Alexander Kurenkov Kurenkov Alexander Minister for Civil Defence, Emergencies and Natural Disaster Relief , instructing him and Rosseti to continue to take all the necessary measures to ensure power and heat supply.
