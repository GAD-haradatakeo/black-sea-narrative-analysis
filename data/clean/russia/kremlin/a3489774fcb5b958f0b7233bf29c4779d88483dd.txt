Events Laying a wreath at the Eternal Flame monument in Baku August 19, 2024 11:45 Baku The President of Russia participated in a wreath-laying ceremony at the Eternal Flame monument in Baku. The monument is situated in the Alley of Martyrs, a memorial complex dedicated to those who gave their lives for Azerbaijan’s independence.
