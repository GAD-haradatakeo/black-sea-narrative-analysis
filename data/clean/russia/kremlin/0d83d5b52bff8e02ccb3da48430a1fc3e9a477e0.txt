Events The President is being informed on the developments at the Crocus City Hall and measures being taken in this regard March 22, 2024 22:45 Vladimir Putin was informed about the shooting at the Crocus City Hall in the first minutes of the attack. The President receives regular updates on the situation and measures being taken by all relevant agencies.
