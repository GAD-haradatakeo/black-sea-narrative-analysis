Events Greetings on 100th anniversary of Vologda Museum Reserve Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the staff of the Vologda State Historical, Architectural and Art Museum Reserve in connection with its 100 th anniversary. March 13, 2023 10:30 The message reads, in part: “Your wonderful northern territory is rightfully renowned for its ancient Christian holy sites, masterpieces of architecture and art, and unique folk crafts. I am happy to note that, thanks to the enthusiasm of many generations of your museum’s employees and their creative energy, these unique landmarks of the Vologda Region’s spiritual and material culture are carefully preserved, and they serve the noble goals of education. They instil love for the country’s history and respect for its national traditions in people, especially the younger generation. It is important that the current staff continue the effort of their predecessors in a worthy manner, working with inspiration and wholeheartedly, finding new ways of interacting with visitors of different ages and professions, and prioritising advanced global museology trends in their work.” * * * The Vologda State Museum Reserve is a research, development and educational institution that features artistic, historical, architectural and other objects of material and spiritual culture, as well as natural sites. The museum display has over 40 architectural monuments with a total area exceeding 9,000 sq m. About 200,000 people visit the museum each year.
