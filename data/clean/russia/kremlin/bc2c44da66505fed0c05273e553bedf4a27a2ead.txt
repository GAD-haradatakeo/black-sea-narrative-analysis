Events Vladimir Putin arrived in Astana The President of Russia arrived in the Republic of Kazakhstan on a two-day state visit. November 27, 2024 15:10 Astana During the discussions, Vladimir Putin and Kassym-Jomart Tokayev will deliberate on the advancement of the strategic partnership and alliance between the Russian Federation and Kazakhstan across diverse domains. This dialogue will take into account Kazakhstan's presidency this year in the Collective Security Treaty Organisation and Russia's chairmanship in the Commonwealth of Independent States and BRICS. On November 28, the President of Russia will take part in the scheduled session of the CSTO Collective Security Council.
