Events Working meeting with Head of North Ossetia – Alania Sergei Menyailo Vladimir Putin had a working meeting with Head of the Republic of North Ossetia – Alania Sergei Menyailo. August 20, 2024 17:10 Vladikavkaz The discussion focused, in particular, on the results of the meeting with representatives of the committee of the North Ossetian regional public organisation, the Mothers of Beslan Association of the Victims of Terrorist Attacks, held earlier. The President instructed Sergei Menyailo to convey to the prosecutor’s office and the investigators the concerns voiced by the meeting participants regarding the investigation into the terrorist attack at school No 1 in Beslan, which occurred in 2004. The implementation in the region of a programme for the overhaul of preschool facilities was discussed separately. According to Sergei Menyailo, 51 kindergartens have already been completed.
