Events Vladimir Putin will meet with President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko on October 9 Announcement, October 9, 2024 Add to Calendar Calendars Yandex iCal Google On October 9, Vladimir Putin is expected to meet with President of the Republic of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko Lukashenko Alexander President of Belarus , who will be in Moscow on a working visit. The leaders plan to discuss topical issues of the further development of bilateral relations of strategic partnership and alliance with an emphasis on integrational cooperation within the Union State. A ceremony will be held to award the President of Belarus with the highest state order of the Russian Federation, Order of Saint Andrew the Apostle the First-Called.
