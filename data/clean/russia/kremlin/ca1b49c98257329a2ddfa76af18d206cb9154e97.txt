Events The President was briefed on the situation along the line of contact Late Saturday night, Vladimir Putin heard reports from commanders performing combat tasks in the areas bordering on Ukraine. August 25, 2024 14:20 Commander of the 810 th Naval Infantry Brigade of the Black Sea Fleet, commander of the 115 th Naval Infantry Brigade of the Pacific Fleet, commander of the 11 th Detached Airborne Assault Brigade, commander of the 56 th Airborne Assault Regiment, and commander of the 51 st Airborne Assault Regiment updated the Supreme Commander-in-Chief on the situation along the line of contact and went on to present their proposals regarding further developments as approved by the command headquarters of the corresponding military groups within their respective areas of responsibility.
