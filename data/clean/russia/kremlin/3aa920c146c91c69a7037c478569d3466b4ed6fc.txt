Events On April 11–12, Vladimir Putin will hold talks with President of Belarus Alexander Lukashenko who will be on a working visit in Russia Announcement, April 11 − 12, 2024 In continuation of the regular dialogue at the highest level, the leaders plan to discuss key issues of bilateral cooperation, including implementation of decisions adopted at the meeting of the Supreme State Council of the Union State, held in St Petersburg on January 29, 2024.
