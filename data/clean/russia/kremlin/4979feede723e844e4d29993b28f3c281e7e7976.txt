Events Congratulations to staff of Central Clinical Hospital of St Alexis Vladimir Putin congratulated the staff of the Central Clinical Hospital of St Alexis, the main medical institution of the Russian Orthodox Church, on its 120 th anniversary. December 30, 2023 10:30 The message reads, in part: “You can take great pride in the hospital’s rich history and its founders – pastors, renowned philanthropists, scientists and doctors. These compassionate, spiritually generous people established the admirable traditions of self-sacrifice and charity, mercy and genuine care for people, which serve today as your tower of strength. Over the years the Central Clinical Hospital of St Alexis has grown into a large, well-established multi-discipline medical centre with branches in various Russian regions. It conducts vital research and implements advanced treatment and rehabilitation methodologies and procedures. In addition, it is important to note that you provide qualified medical assistance to participants in the special military operation and actively support residents of Donbass and Novorossiya.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: December 30, 2023, 10:30 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/73198 Text version
