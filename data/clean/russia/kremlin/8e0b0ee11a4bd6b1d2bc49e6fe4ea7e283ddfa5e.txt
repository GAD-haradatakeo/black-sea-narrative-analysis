Events Greetings on the 100th anniversary of Russia’s State Mine Rescue Service Vladimir Putin sent greetings to current and former workers of Russia’s State Mine Rescue Service on its 100 th anniversary. July 8, 2022 15:10 The message reads, in part: “Over the years, the service has made a long and heroic journey. Many generations of your predecessors performed their professional duties with dignity and selflessly saved human lives. In the most difficult situations, they showed the utmost professionalism, dedication, composure, fellowship, true camaraderie, and solidarity. You are justifiably proud of belonging to this strong, united team, as well as of the immense contribution that service workers make to prevent emergencies at Russia’s mining enterprises. And of course, you show your deepest respect to the labour traditions of veterans. I am confident that the Mine Rescue Service of the Russian Emergencies Ministry will further boost its potential and promptly achieve all the tasks that it sets.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 8, 2022, 15:10 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/68847 Text version
