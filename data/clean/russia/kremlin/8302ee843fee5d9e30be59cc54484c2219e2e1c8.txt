Events Greetings on the 10th anniversary of Public Television of Russia Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the staff of the Public Television of Russia on the occasion of the 10 th anniversary of the start of its broadcasting. May 19, 2023 10:00 The message reads, in part: “Over the past years, your TV channel has covered an eventful, creative path and made a significant contribution to the development of Russian television. It is important that you have achieved much success and won affection of large audiences with your interesting and informative journalistic, educational, and entertainment programmes, documentaries, and news projects. I am confident that the channel’s invariable popularity is the result of the competent and well-organised work done by your talented team, a team of true professionals, experts, and enthusiasts.” Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: May 19, 2023, 10:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71163 Text version
