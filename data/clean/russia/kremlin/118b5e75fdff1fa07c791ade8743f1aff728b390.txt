Events Executive Order on awarding the Order of Courage to Vladimir Zhdanov The President signed an Executive Order awarding the Order of Courage to Vladimir Zhdanov, the Head of the Administration of the Shebekino Municipal District in the Belgorod Region, for displaying bravery and dedication while performing his civic duty.
