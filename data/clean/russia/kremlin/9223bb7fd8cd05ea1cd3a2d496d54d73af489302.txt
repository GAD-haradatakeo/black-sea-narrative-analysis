Events Telephone conversation with President of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon. October 5, 2024 11:55 During the conversation, Vladimir Putin wished a happy birthday to President of the Republic of Tajikistan Emomali Rahmon Rahmon Emomali President of Tajikistan . The two leaders discussed a number of practical issues regarding the upcoming meeting of the CIS Background information Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS) Heads of State Council in Moscow on October 8. Vladimir Putin also sent a message of greetings to the President of Tajikistan. It says, in part: “You enjoy well-deserved authority among your fellow citizens and on the international stage. Under your guidance, Tajikistan is confidently moving forward along the path of socioeconomic development and is playing an important constructive role in regional and international affairs. We appreciate your consistent efforts aimed at strengthening relations of strategic partnership and alliance between our countries, as well as at promoting mutually beneficial ties within the framework of the CSTO, the CIS and the SCO. Mr Rahmon, before we meet in Moscow in a few days, I would like to wish you health, well-being and new achievements in your activities as the head of state.” Geography Tajikistan Topics Foreign policy Persons Rahmon Emomali Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 5, 2024, 11:55 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/75271 Text version
