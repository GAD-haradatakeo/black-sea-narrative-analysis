Events Telephone conversation with President of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with President of the Republic of Uzbekistan Shavkat Mirziyoyev. April 26, 2023 13:15 Vladimir Putin and Shavkat Mirziyoyev Mirziyoyev Shavkat President of Uzbekistan discussed matters on the bilateral agenda, in particular, steps to further expand mutually beneficial trade and economic cooperation. In this connection, they noted the successful 2023 INNOPROM Central Asia International Industrial Trade Fair, held on April 24–26 in Tashkent with Russia’s active involvement. See also Greetings on the opening of INNOPROM.
