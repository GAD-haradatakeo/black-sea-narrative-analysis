Events Condolences to President of Ethiopia Sahle-Work Zewde and Prime Minister of Ethiopia Abiy Ahmed Vladimir Putin sent his condolences to President of Ethiopia Sahle-Work Zewde and Prime Minister of Ethiopia Abiy Ahmed on the tragic consequences of landslides in the south of the country. July 25, 2024 15:00 The President asked the Ethiopian leaders to convey his sincere sympathy and support to the families of the victims, and wished a speedy recovery to all those injured.
