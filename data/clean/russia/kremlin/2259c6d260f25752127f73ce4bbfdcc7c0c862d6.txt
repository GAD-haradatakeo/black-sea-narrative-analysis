Events Greetings to people of Adygea Vladimir Putin sent greetings to the people of the Republic of Adygea on the 100 th anniversary of the republic’s foundation. October 5, 2022 09:00 The message reads, in part: “The establishment of the Adyghe Autonomous Region in 1922 largely determined its further development, helping to promote the region’s economic recovery and preserve its rich historical, cultural, and spiritual heritage. During this period, the republic has built industrial facilities and farms, as well as energy, transport, and tourist infrastructure facilities. It has opened new schools, research and educational institutions, and healthcare centres. It is important that you cherish the behests of your fathers and grandfathers, their original customs and traditions, and that you work productively for the benefit of your homeland and the whole of Russia.” Geography Republic of Adygeya Topics Regions Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: October 5, 2022, 09:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/69500 Text version
