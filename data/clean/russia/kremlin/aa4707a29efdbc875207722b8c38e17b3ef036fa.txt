Events Greetings to Russian team of schoolchildren that took part in the 64th International Mathematical Olympiad in Japan Vladimir Putin sent a message of greetings to the team of Russian schoolchildren that took part in the 64 th International Mathematical Olympiad in Japan. July 13, 2023 20:00 The message reads, in part: “Congratulations on your brilliant performance at the 64 th International Mathematical Olympiad in Japan. You have successfully represented Russia at this prestigious competition, where you showed deep knowledge, the ability to think creatively, and make the right decisions. You proved in practice that you are worthy of the highest marks, and your mentors can be proud of such talented students. And of course, sincere words of gratitude to your teachers, coaches, and families, whose contribution to your achievements is truly priceless.” Topics School Science and innovation Publication status Published in section: News Publication date: July 13, 2023, 20:00 Direct link: en.kremlin.ru/d/71685 Text version
