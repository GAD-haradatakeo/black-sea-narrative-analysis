Events Telephone conversation with Crown Prince of Saudi Arabia Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud Vladimir Putin had a telephone conversation with Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud, the Prime Minister of Saudi Arabia. July 17, 2024 23:10 The leaders highly appraised the current level of friendly relations between Russia and Saudi Arabia based on the principles of mutual respect and a balance of each other’s interests. Vladimir Putin and Mohammed bin Salman Al Saud discussed a number of bilateral political, trade, economic and energy cooperation issues and pointed out the importance of continued close coordination within OPEC Plus for maintaining stability on the energy market. The leaders agreed to maintain contacts at various levels.
