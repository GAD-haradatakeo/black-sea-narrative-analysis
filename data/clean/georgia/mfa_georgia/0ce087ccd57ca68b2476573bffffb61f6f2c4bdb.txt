22 October 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs ofGeorgia strongly condemns the attackcarried out by terrorists againstthe Egyptian Law EnforcementForcesonOctober 21,2017 in El Wahat,that resultedinthe death ofdozensof policemenandleft manyothersinjured. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism in all its forms and expresses support to the efforts of the Government of Egypt aimed onbuilding a stable and peaceful state. We expresshope that perpetrators of this brutal act will be brought to justice. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the Government of Egypt and the families of those who were killed by the terrorist attackand wishes speedy recovery to those who have been injured.
