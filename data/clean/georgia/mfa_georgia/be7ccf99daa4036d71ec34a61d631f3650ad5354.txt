10 April 2025 On 10 April 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the Ambassador of Canada to Georgia (residing in Ankara, Republic of Türkiye), Kevin Hamilton. During the meeting, the parties discussed the agenda of bilateral relations between Canada and Georgia, as well as recent developments in the region. Ambassador Hamilton reiterated Canada’s steadfast support for Georgia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity, and the country’s aspirations for European and Euro-Atlantic integration.
