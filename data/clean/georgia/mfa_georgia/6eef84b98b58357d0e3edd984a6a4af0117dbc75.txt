Panel Discussion on Democratic Development and International Legal Mechanisms for Human Rights Held at the Ambassadors’ Conference 29 December 2024 A panel discussion on democratic development and international legal mechanisms for human rights was held as part of the Ambassadors' Conference, moderated by Georgia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili.
