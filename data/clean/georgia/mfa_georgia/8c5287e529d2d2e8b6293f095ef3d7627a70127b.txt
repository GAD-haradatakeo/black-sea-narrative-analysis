27 May 2023 On 27 May 2023, within the framework of the forum "Strong Diaspora for Georgia's Unity, Peace and European Future" dedicated to the Day of the Georgian Diaspora, the Minister of Foreign Affairs Ilia Darchiashvili hosted the participants of the Diaspora Forum at an official reception. The head of the Foreign Ministry thanked the representatives of the Georgian diaspora for their active involvement in various projects and close cooperation with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. According to Ilia Darchiashvili, the close connection between the state and the diaspora contributes to further strengthening the Georgian diaspora abroad and ensuring their greater involvement in the process of the country's development. The Minister of Foreign Affairs once again confirmed that the Georgian diaspora is one of the most important priorities for the State, and the government will continue to actively take care of Georgian compatriots abroad.
