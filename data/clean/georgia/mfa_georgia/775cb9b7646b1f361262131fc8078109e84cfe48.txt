23 September 2025 On 23 September 2025, within the framework of the United Nations General Assembly, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of the Republic of Croatia, Gordan Grlić Radman. The ministers underscored the close and friendly relations between Georgia and Croatia, which significantly contribute to the consolidation of fruitful bilateral cooperation. During the meeting, the parties reviewed current issues on the bilateral and multilateral cooperation agenda and discussed prospects for further collaboration across a variety of sectors. The importance of sustaining an active high-level political dialogue and the value of recent bilateral high-level visits were particularly emphasized. Attention was also devoted to the need for deepening economic and trade relations between the two countries. The ministers exchanged views on existing security challenges in the region. Discussions also touched upon Georgia’s process of integration into the European Union, with particular emphasis placed on the importance of dialogue in advancing this process. Minister Botchorishvili expressed her gratitude to Croatia for its continued support of Georgia’s European integration aspirations.
