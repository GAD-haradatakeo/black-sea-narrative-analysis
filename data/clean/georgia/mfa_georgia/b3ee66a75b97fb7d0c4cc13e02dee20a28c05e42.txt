4 April 2025 On 4 April 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Republic of Sierra Leone to Georgia, Mohamed Yongawo. During the meeting, the parties discussed issues of mutual interest within bilateral and multilateral formats, as well as prospects for further enhancing cooperation. Special attention was devoted to the potential for sharing best practices and experiences related to successful reforms in the field of public administration.
