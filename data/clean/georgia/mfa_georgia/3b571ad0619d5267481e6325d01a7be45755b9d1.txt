27 April 2018 27.04.2018, Tbilisi – the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the accident that took place on 26 April 2018, in Nahal Tzafit, the southern Dead Sea area of Israel, leaving ten people dead. Georgia conveys its condolences to the Israeli people, as well as to the families of the students who died in the accident, wishing speedy recovery to those who have been injured.
