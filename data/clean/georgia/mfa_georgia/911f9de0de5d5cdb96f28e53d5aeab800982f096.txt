27 September 2018 27.09.2018, New York – During the meeting withthe Foreign Minister of Honduras held within the framework of the 73rd session of the UN General Assembly, David Zalkaliani and his Honduran counterpart Marïa DoloresAgüeroLara positively appraised the ongoing co-operation between Georgia and Honduras, including within the framework of the UN. The Honduran Foreign Minister briefed her Georgian counterpart on the Honduran agenda of reforms reaffirming her readiness to learn from Georgia’s experience in this regard. The two ministers spoke about issues relating to regional co-operation. The Georgian delegation spoke about Georgia’s business and investment environment, the good practice and opportunities of free trade regime. The two ministers reaffirmed their readiness to further promote communication between the two countries. ​ Documents Similar News
