29 January 2018 29.01.2017, Tbilisi – Deputy Foreign Minister, head of the Georgian delegation to the Geneva International Discussions, David Dondua a meetng with European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia Toivo Klaar. The sides discussed issues on the agenda of the next round of the Geneva International Discussions. David Dondua highlighted the need to achieve progress on such issues as Russia’s non-use of force pledge, the creation of international security mechanisms on the ground and the return of refugees and Internally Displaced Persons. He drew special attention to the role of the EU as the co-chair of the Geneva International Discussions in the peace process, and especially in ensuring that Russia complies with its committemnts under the 12 August 2008 Ceasefire Agreement. The Deputy Minister briefed the Special Representative on the illegal processes in Georgia’s occupied territories, as well as on the difficult security, humanitarian and human rights situation on the ground.
