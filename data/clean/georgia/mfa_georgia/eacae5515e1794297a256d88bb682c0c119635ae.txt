5 January 2021 As a result of the agreement reached between the Embassy of Georgia in the Republic of Serbia and the organizers of the Belgrade "International Ethnology Festival", Georgia is to represent the Guest of Honor of the festival in 2021. The relevant statement regarding the abovementioned event has been made by Tamara Vučić, Serbian First Lady. The festival has been held through her honorary patronage since 2017. The show of Georgian national clothing, sales exhibition of traditional handmade pieces, as well as the presentation of Georgian folklore, are to be launched within the scope of the 3-day event. The festival is aimed at the protection and preservation of the intangible cultural heritage, as well as raising awareness about it.
