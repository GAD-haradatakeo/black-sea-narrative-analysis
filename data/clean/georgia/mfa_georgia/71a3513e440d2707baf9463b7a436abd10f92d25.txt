17 January 2021 From 17 to 20 January 2020 the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, will hold a working visit in the Kingdom of Spain. Within the framework of the visit the head of the Georgian Foreign Ministry will participate in the 113th session of the executive council of the UN World Tourism Organisation (UNWTO), where a candidate will be selected for the position of the Secretary General of said organisation. It is noteworthy that Georgia proposed the candidacy of the acting chair, Zurab Popolikashvili, for a second term. The event will be attended by representatives of the 35 member states of the organisation. Within the framework of the visit the head of the Georgian Foreign Ministry will hold meeting with his counterpart, Arancha Gonzalez Laya, the Spanish Interior Minister, Fernando Grange-Marlaska, as well as José Luis Escrivá. Within the framework of the visit an official reception will also be held in the name of the King of Spain, Felipe VI.
