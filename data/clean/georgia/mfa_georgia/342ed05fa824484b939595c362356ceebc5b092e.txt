26 September 2019 26.09.2019, New York – On the sidelines of the 74th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Ukrainian colleague, Vadym Prystaiko. Reaffirming the strategic partnership between Georgia and Ukraine based on historical friendship, the sides paid special attention to the occupation of the Georgian and Ukrainian territories and the security climate. The sides reaffirmed mutual support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of each other’s countries and expressed their commitment to work towards consolidation of international support to address the challenges facing the two countries. The two ministers positively appraised Georgia’s and Ukraine’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration processes. They also welcomed the close contacts between the executive and legislative authorites of the two countries.
