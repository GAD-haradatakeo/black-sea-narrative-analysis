19 November 2018 19.11.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Assistant Secretary-General of the United Nations, and the UN representative to the Geneva International Discussions, Ayse Cihan Sultanoğlu. Discussions focused on the issues related to the peaceful resolution of the conflict. Georgian side discussed the illegal steps by the Russian Federation that further deteriorate already difficult security and human rights situation in Georgia’s Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. Georgian Foreign Minister expressed concern over Russia’s and its occupation regimes’ destructive steps that prevent the conduction of meetings within the framework of the Incident Prevention and Response Mechanisms (IPRMs). Special attention was paid to the need to effectively use the Geneva International Discussions and the importance of reaching progress on the key issues on the agenda.
