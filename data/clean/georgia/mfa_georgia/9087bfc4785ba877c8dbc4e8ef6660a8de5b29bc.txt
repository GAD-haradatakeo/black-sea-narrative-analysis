8 August 2017 8.08.2017, Tbilisi –Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze visited the Photo Exhibition “War Through Our Lens” that has been mounted at the Parliament of Georgia. Parliament Speaker Irakli Kobakhidze welcomed the exhibition visitors and spoke about the tragic developments nine years ago and the heroism of Georgian soldiers. On display of the exhibition are photographs by five photojournalists Giorgi Abdaladze, Zurab Kurtsikidze, Gleb Garanich, Vano Shlamov and Nodar Tskhvirashvili. Documents Similar News
