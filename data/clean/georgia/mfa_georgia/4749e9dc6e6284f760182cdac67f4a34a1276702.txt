27 January 2019 27.01.2019, Madrid – Within the framework of his visit to Spain, the Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with representatives of the Georgian Diaspora in Spain the meeting was held in the building of the Georgian Embassy in the Kingdom of Spain. David Zalkaliani presented the Georgian Judoist Nika Sherazadishvili with the credential of honorary tourism ambassador. The Minister highlighted the Georgian sportsman’s exceptional role in raising international awareness about Georgia. The meeting was also attended by the heads of the Georgian Sunday Schools in Spain. The Minister handed to them Sunday School Books developed through the efforts of the Foreign Ministry. At the meeting David Zalkaliani highlighted the important role of the successful Georgian Diaspora in promoting Georgia internationally.
