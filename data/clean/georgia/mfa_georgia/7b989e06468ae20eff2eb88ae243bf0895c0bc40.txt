25 August 2023 On 25 August 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, signed the condolence book opened at the UN House in connection with the death of Nick Beresford, the Permanent Representative of the United Nations Development Program (UNDP) in Georgia. The minister expressed his deep sorrow over the death of Nick Beresford and extended his condolences to his family, relatives and colleagues. According to Ilia Darchiashvili, Georgia-UNDP cooperation deepened under the leadership of Nick Beresford and his efforts and dedication will always be remembered. Documents Similar News
