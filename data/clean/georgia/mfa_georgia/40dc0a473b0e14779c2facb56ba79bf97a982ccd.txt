20 January 2023 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United Kingdom to Georgia, Mark Clayton. At the meeting, the sides discussed the current agenda of bilateral relations and emphasized the importance of further deepening of the strategic partnership between the two countries.
