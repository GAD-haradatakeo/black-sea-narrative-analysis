12 July 2022 On 12 July 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili signed the condolence book opened at the residence of the Japanese Ambassador in connection with the tragic death of the former Prime Minister of Japan, Shinzo Abe. Expressing his deep sorrow, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia offered his condolences to the Japanese government and people” : “On behalf of the Government of Georgia and on my own, please accept our heartfelt condolences on the tragic death of the former Prime Minister of Japan, HE Mr. ABE Shinzo. We remember Mr. ABE as an outstanding Japanese political leader whose distinguished years of service were defined by his deep commitment to the best interests of his country. We extend our sympathy and support to the family and friends of Mr. ABE, to the Government and the people of Japan”. Documents Similar News
