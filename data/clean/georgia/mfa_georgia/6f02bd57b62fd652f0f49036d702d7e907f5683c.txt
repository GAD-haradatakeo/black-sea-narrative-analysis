1 February 2018 1.02.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Deputy Chief of Mission at the U.S. Embassy Elizabeth Rood. The sides discussed issues relating to Georgia-U.S. co-operation both in bilateral and multilateral formats, as well as the activities carried out in the course of 2017. Discussions also focused on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. The sides highlighted the importance of the support of the United States as a strategic partner of Georgia in terms of keeping the humanitarian and security situation in Abkhazia and the Tskhinvali region high on the international agenda. David Zalkaliani and Elizabeth Rood underlined the need to continue co-ordinated co-operation between the two strategic partners, including in all the four areas under the Charter.
