15 December 2022 Within the framework of his visit to Japan, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia met with the Minister of Digital Transformation of Japan, Taro Kano. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked Taro Kano for his support of Georgia and for his visit to Georgia as the Minister of Foreign Affairs. The ministers exchanged information on the possibilities of cooperation in the field of digital technologies, in particular, towards the development of electronic services, especially taking into account the sharing of good practice and experience of Georgia in this field. The meeting focused on the reform of the governance system, which ensures compliance with the UN Sustainable Development Goals. The sides, in particular, mentioned the steps taken by Georgia towards the direction of digital governance development were. The sides reviewed the favorable investment and business environment of Georgia, especially the growing interest in the development of various technological start-ups and the government's policies to support them.
