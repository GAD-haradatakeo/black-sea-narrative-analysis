17 November 2025 As part of her official visit to the State of Israel, Georgia’s Minister for Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, met with Israel’s Minister of Agriculture and Food Security, Avi Dichter. During the meeting, the two ministers underscored the long-standing partnership between Georgia and Israel in the field of agriculture. The parties reviewed key aspects of cooperation in agriculture, trade, and the broader economic sphere.
