2 September 2020 On September 1, Ambassador Revaz Lominadze presented his credentials to H.E. Simonetta Sommaruga, the President of the Swiss Confederation. At the meeting held after the ceremony in Federal Palace, such important issues were highlighted as the importance, growing dynamics and opportunities of bilateral and multilateral cooperation between two countries, mediation role of Switzerland in Russian-Georgian conflict, the gravity of human rights and severe humanitarian situation in the occupied territories of Georgia, intensification of high level mutual visits and other important issues.
