20 March 2019 20.03.2019, Geneva – Georgia’s Permanent Representative to Geneva Victor Dolidze met with the Guaido interim government representative Maria-Alejandra Aristeguieta Alvarez. The meeting was held on the sidelines of the event named Human Rights Abuses and Violations in Venezuela. Georgia’s Representative Victor Dolidze stated Georgia did not recognize the results of the 2018 Venezuela parliamentary elections adding that the country supports the Venezuela National Assembly. Victor Dolidze expressed the hope that Venezuela will soon return to the scope of international law and then the relations between Georgia and Venezuela will be based on the principle of respect for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of countries.
