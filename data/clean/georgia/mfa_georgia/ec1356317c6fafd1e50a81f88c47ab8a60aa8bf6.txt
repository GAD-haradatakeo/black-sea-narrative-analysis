21 June 2022 Within the framework of the visit of the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Garibashvili to the State of Qatar, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the State Minister for Foreign Affairs of the State of Qatar, Soltan bin Saad Al-Muraikhi. At the meeting, the sides discussed the positive dynamics of bilateral relations between the two countries and the prospects for their further development, especially in the fields of economics, investments, bilateral trade and tourism. Discussions also focused on the situation in the region and the current security challenges. The sides emphasized the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries.
