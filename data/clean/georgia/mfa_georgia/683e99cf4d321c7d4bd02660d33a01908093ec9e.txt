26 March 2021 On 26 March 2021, the Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani, held a farewell meeting with the Permanent Representative of UNDP, Luisa Winton, who is to complete her mission to Georgia late in March. Within the framework of the meeting dimension of fruitful bilateral cooperation between Georgia and the UNDP were discussed, including economic development, democratic governance, crisis prevention and subsequent rehabilitation, as well as environmental protection and other issues. A particular emphasis was made on the localisation and modification of sustainable development goals to fit the Georgian national agenda, implementing them with the support of the efforts exerted by the UNDP.
