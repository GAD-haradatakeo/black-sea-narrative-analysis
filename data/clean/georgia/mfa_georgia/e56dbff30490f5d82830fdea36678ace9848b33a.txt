27 January 2018 27.01.2017, Tbilisi – the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns the terrorist act committed on 27 January in the centre of Kabul, the capital of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan, leaving tens of people dead and over 100 - wounded. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the governments of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan, as well as to families of terror victims, wishing speedy recovery to those who have been injured in the terrorist attack. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism in all its forms and fully supports the government of Afghanistan in the fight against terror. Documents Similar News
