25 August 2017 25.08.2017, Wiesbaden, Germany – As part of his visit to Germany, Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with the President of Hessen State Parliament Norbert Kartmann, where the parties discussed bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Germany. Introducing Georgia’s successful reforms to the President of Hessen State Parliament Norbert Kartmann, Janelidze spoke of the country’s business and investment opportunities. He said, the liberal economic policy in the country, free trade agreements with the European Union (EU), China and other leading countries makes Georgia a bridge between Europe and Asia. The sides highlighted co-operation between the two countries in the areas of energy, security, infrastructure, education and culture. Particular attention was paid to the need to raise awareness of Georgia in Germany. Special note, in this context, was taken of the importance of direct flights between Georgia and Germany that will further contribute to the deepening of people-to-people contacts. Accoridng to Mikheil Janelidze, the annual number of German tourists to Georgia keeps rising. High appraisal was given to the activities ongoing as part of the year of friendship of Germany and Georgia. Accoridng to Mikheil Janelidze, Georgia’s status as the Guest of Honour at the 2018 Frankfurt Book Fair is yet another clear proof of close partnership between Georgia and Germany.
