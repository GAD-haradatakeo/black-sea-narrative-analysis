12 September 2022 On 12 September 2022, a delegation of the Japan International Cooperation Agency (JICA) headed bythe Deputy Director General of the East, Central Asiaand the Caucasus Department of the Agency, Tanabe Hideki visited Georgia. The sides discussed the current issues on the agenda of JICA, including the high level of partnership between Georgia and Japan and successful cooperation in various directions, as well as the important role of the Agency in the implementation of agriculture, energy, infrastructure projects in Georgia. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed his gratitude for the JICA projects both ongoing and planned to be carried out in Georgia, as well as for the financial assistance provided to Georgia over the years. Highlighting the importance of further intensifying Georgia-Japan cooperation, Hideki Tanabe provided the Georgian side with updated information on the projects implemented in Georgia within the framework of the Country Assistance Policy developed by JICA.
