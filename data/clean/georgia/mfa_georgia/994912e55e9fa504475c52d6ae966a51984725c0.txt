25 September 2018 “At all bilateral meetings we received high appraisal with regard to the ongoing economic and political reforms, as well as with regard to our peace initiatives” - the Georgian Foreign Minister said when commenting on the bilateral meetings. During the meetings with the President of the European Commission,Jean-Claude Juncker and the Austrian Chancellor, Sebastian Kurz, discussions focused on Georgia’s integration with the EU. According to David Zalkaliani, special attention was paid to the issues relating to Georgia’s integration with the EU. “Georgia’s initiative to take more steps beyond the Association Agreement was highly appraised. Reference here is made to the Road Map envisaging greater sectoral and physical integration with the EU” – the Georgian Foreign Minister said.
