6 March 2025 On 6 March 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Ambassador of the Republic of Guatemala to Georgia, Eduardo Enrique Hernández Recinos, who is based in Ankara. Minister Botchorishvili congratulated Ambassador Hernández Recinos on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. During the meeting, both parties emphasized that Georgia views Guatemala as a key partner in Central America. The discussion covered key aspects of bilateral relations, highlighting the longstanding fruitful cooperation between Georgia and Guatemala in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Particular attention was given to opportunities for deepening economic and tourism ties. The meeting also addressed the grave situation in Georgia’s occupied territories.
