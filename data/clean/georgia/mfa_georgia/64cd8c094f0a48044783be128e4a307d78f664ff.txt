23 October 2023 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, participated In the 104th Rose-Roth high-level seminar organized by the NATO Parliamentary Assembly in Stockholm. The First Deputy Minister made a speech at the session "Georgia's Euro-Atlantic Road". In his speech, Lasha Darsalia spoke about the importance of Georgia’s Euro-Atlantic integration and the agenda of ambitious reforms in this regard. Lasha Darsalia emphasized the need to continue close NATO-Georgia cooperation and the need to strengthen the security architecture in the Black Sea region against the background of the ongoing geopolitical processes in the world. The NATO seminar was attended by the Ambassador of Georgia to Sweden, Irakli Khutsurauli, as well as by members of the Swedish government and parliament, members of the permanent parliamentary delegation to the NATO Parliamentary Assembly, and high-ranking delegates from various countries.
