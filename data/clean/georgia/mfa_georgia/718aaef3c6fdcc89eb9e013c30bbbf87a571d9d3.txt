12 April 2022 On 12 April, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted inter-agency consultations led by the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia, to complete the special EU membership questionnaire handed to Georgia by the European Commission on 11 April 2022. The purpose of the meeting was to share the content of the questionnaire with various agencies, to define action plans and to discuss the working format. The participants of the meeting agreed to continue the meetings at working and high level.
