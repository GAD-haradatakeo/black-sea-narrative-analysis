10 February 2024 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, participated in the event to mark the 45th anniversary of the Islamic Revolution of Iran. The deputy minister congratulated the Iranian people on their national day and wished them peace and prosperity. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili emphasized the friendly relations between the two countries and thanked the Iranian government for its steadfast support of Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
