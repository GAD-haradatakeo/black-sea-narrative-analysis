24 October 2022 On 24 October 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Finland,Nina Vaskunlahti. The sides discussed the issues of deepening the bilateral economic relations between Georgia and Finland, as well as Georgia’s investment and transit opportunities. Discussions also focused on Georgia's integration into the European Union and the progress achieved on this path. The sides also exchanged views on the geopolitical situation in the region against the backdrop of Russia's aggression against Ukraine. Within the framework of his visit to Georgia, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Finland visited the village of Odze located near the Georgian occupation line, and familiarized himself with the current situation.
