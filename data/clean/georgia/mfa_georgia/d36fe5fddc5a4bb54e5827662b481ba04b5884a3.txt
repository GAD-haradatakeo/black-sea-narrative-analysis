17 November 2025 Maka Botchorishvili and Patriarch Theophilos III discussed the deep historical and spiritual ties between the Churches of Georgia and Jerusalem. Special attention was paid to the unique role of the Orthodox Church in maintaining global and regional peace. The Foreign Minister congratulated Patriarch Theophilos III on the 20th anniversary of his enthronement. The Patriarch offered his blessings to the Minister and the members of the Georgian delegation, wishing them success in their work. Together with the delegation, Maka Botchorishvili visited the Church of the Holy Sepulchre in Jerusalem, paying her respects at the Tomb of Christ and venerating the sacred sites of Golgotha.
