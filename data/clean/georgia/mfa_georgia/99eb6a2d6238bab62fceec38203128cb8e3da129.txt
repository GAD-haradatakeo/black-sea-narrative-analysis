5 August 2021 The Russian president’s approval of a draft agreement on “simplified procedures for receiving dual citizenship and Russian citizenship” offered to the Tskhinvali occupation regime represents a continuation of Moscow's policy of illegal occupation and de facto annexation of Georgia's Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. Russia began illegal forced passportisation of the population of the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions in the early 2000s and in 2008 attempted to use this illegitimate process to justify the military aggression against Georgia. The international community is united in condemning the process of illegal passportisation of the occupied territories of Georgia by Russia and considers it a gross violation of the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, as well as of the principles of international law.
