4 December 2024 On 4 December 2024, in Malta, within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgia's Foreign Minister Maka Botchorishvili met with Armenia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ararat Mirzoyan. The Armenian Foreign Minister congratulated his Georgian counterpart on her appointment and wished her success in her new role. During the meeting, the parties reviewed the friendly relations and avenues for bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Armenia. Particular attention was devoted to the areas of economic and trade cooperation, alongside the region’s transit potential. The discussion also highlighted and positively appraised the cooperative efforts in culture, education, and people-to-people exchanges Special attention was given to the importance of the Declaration on Strategic Partnership between Georgia and Armenia, signed earlier in 2024, as a significant step in further strengthening bilateral relations. The positive dynamics of high-level visits between the two countries were also highlighted. The discussion underscored the importance of promoting peace and stability in the region. Maka Botchorishvili expressed gratitude to her Armenian counterpart for Armenia's steadfast support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The parties also touched upon cooperation within the framework of international organizations.
