15 December 2017 15.12.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a bilateral meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia Tadaharu Uehara. The sides discussed the issues relating to bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Japan. Special attention was paid to the increasing level of trade-economic relations and the Japanese companies’ participation in Georgia’s investment projects. The sides welcomed the progress in negotiations over the bilateral investment agreement, which is due to make an important contribution to the development of bilateral economic co-operation. The sides noted with satisfaction the activitis carried out in Tbilisi and Tokyo to mark 25th anniversary of establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries underlining that Georgia-Japan bilateral relations have become remarkable in many areas. The sides reconfirmed their commitment to continue to broaden the areas of GUAM-Japan cooperation.
