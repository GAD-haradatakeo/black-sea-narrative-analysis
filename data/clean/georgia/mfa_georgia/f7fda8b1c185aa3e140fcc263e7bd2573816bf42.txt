26 July 2021 At the decision of the World Heritage Committee, the ColchicRainforestsandWetlands will be inscribed on theWorld Heritage List. The nomination “ColchicRainforestsandWetlands” was prepared by the Ministry of Environment Protection and Agriculture, in cooperation with international experts. Three non-naturalGeorgian monumentsare inscribed on the list — the Gelati Monastery in the country’s west, the Historical Monuments of Mtskheta, outside the capital Tbilisi, and Upper Svaneti, a highland province in the country’s north.
