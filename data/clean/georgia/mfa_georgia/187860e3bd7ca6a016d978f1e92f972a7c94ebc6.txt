11 June 2024 "We are doing everything within our responsibility to advance Georgia towards European integration," stated Ilia Darchiashvili, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, to Georgian journalists in Berlin, where he is attending a conference on the restoration of Ukraine. Darchiashvili highlighted that the Georgian Parliament's adoption of all remaining legislative packages in the third reading reflects the government's commitment and sense of responsibility. "Georgia firmly stands on the path of European integration," added the Minister. He also discussed the current state of relations between Georgia and the United States, emphasizing that it is a strategic priority for the Georgian government to restore the Georgian-American bilateral relations to their previous status.
