28 July 2022 Within the framework of his visit to the Republic of Turkey, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the Speaker of the Grand National Assembly ofTurkey, MustafaŞentop. Speaking about the successful strategic partnership between the two countries, the sides paid special attention to high-level parliamentary cooperation, including at the level of parliamentary committees. The sides also emphasized the active inter-parliamentary relations in the trilateral Azerbaijan-Georgia-Turkey format. The Speaker of the Turkish Parliament once again confirmed Turkey's strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its foreign policy priorities in bilateral and multilateral formats.
