22 October 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia met with his Ukrainian counterpart, Mykola Tochytskyi. At the beginning of the meeting, Teimuraz Janjalia congratulated Mykola Tochytskyi on his appointment as Deputy Minister and wished him success in his future endeavors. The sides discussed issues of bilateral cooperation, underlined the existing strategic and friendly relations between the two countries, and discussed the further development of bilateral relations. Talking points included joint events planned and implemented by Ukraine and Georgia within the framework of European integration, a wide range of issues related to Euro-Atlantic integration. The sides also discussed European integration and the further deepening of relations within the Associated Trio, and stressed the importance of cooperation in the format of the Associated Trio. Teimuraz Janjalia put forward the initiative to make the cooperation within the "Trio" even more intensive and comprehensive, including at the inter-agency level, in all possible directions.
