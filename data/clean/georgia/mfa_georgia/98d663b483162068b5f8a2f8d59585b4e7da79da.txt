13 February 2018 13.02.2018, Kuwait - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidzemet with Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bosnia and Herzegovina Igor Crnadak, on the sidelines of the Global Coalition to Defeat ISIS Conference held in Kuwait. The two Ministers discussed the friendly and partner co-operation between the two countries highlighting the substantial progress that has been made on the Euroepan integration path. Special attention was paid to the importance of high-level meetings in the context of developing bilateral co-operation. Igor Crnadak reaffirmed his government’s firm support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia.
