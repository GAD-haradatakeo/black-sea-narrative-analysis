9 August 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Latvia to Georgia, Ingrida Levrence, who has completed her mission in Georgia. Ilia Darchiashvili highly appraised the close Georgia-Latvia relations, which have significantly expanded over the past few years, to which attests the exchange of high-level bilateral visits, as well as active interaction in various directions, including in trade-economic and cultural areas. The sides highlighted that greater potential still exists for further deepening of cooperation in the aforesaid areas. Special emphasis was placed on Georgia's foreign policy priorities and Latvia's firm support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration course.
