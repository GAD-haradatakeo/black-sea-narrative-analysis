10 February 2023 In connection with the earthquake occurring in the Republic of Türkiye, citizens of Georgia in need of assistance should call the Embassy of Georgia to Türkiye and the consulates-General in Istanbul and Trabzon using the hotlines: For the Embassy of Georgia in Ankara: +905336903040; +905392208280 For the Consulate-General in Istanbul: +905418184400 For the Consulate-General in Trabzon: +905413262226 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Georgia’s diplomatic representations are deeply saddened over the tragic loss of life in Türkiye and express their solidarity with the Turkish government and people.
