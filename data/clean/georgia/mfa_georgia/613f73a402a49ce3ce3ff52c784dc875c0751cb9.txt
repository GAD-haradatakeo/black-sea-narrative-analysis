31 March 2021 On 31 March 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Swedish Ambassador to Georgia, Ulrich Tidestrom. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of cooperation issues between Georgia and Sweden. Talking points included the aggravating human rights and humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. In this light, the sides took note of the need of the international community’s active engagement and the importance of further activating cooperation within the framework of international organizations.
