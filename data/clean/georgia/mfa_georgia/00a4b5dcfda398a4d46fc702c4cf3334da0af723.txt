3 November 2022 On 3 November 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, met the resident representative of UNDP in Georgia, Nick Beresford. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides discussed main directions of fruitful cooperation between Georgia and UNDP. It was also noted at the meeting that UNDP plays a major role in implementing the Sustainable Development Goals in Georgia and significantly contributes to this process. Discussions also focused on the situation in the regions of Georgia occupied by Russia. At the end of the meeting, the sides once again expressed their readiness to continue close partnership.
