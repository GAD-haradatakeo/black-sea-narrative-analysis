14 November 2023 On 14 November 2023, the closing event of the European Union’s innovative project was held at the Radisson Blu Iveria hotel, with the active participation of the Georgian diaspora. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Vladimer Konstantinidi participated in the event. He addressed the audience and emphasized the importance of the human capital of the Georgian diaspora, and the contribution our compatriots living abroad are making towards developing the country's economy and bringing it closer to international standards. Vladimir Konstantinidi reviewed the programmes and events implemented by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia with the support of the European Union and in close cooperation with international partners. He spoke about the initiatives and support opportunities that serve to attract the knowledge and experience of compatriots living abroad and ensure the involvement of the Georgian diaspora. It should be noted that the project was implemented with the involvement of four UN agencies, in close cooperation with business and state institutions.
