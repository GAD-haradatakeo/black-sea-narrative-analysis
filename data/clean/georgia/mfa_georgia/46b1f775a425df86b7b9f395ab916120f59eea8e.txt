6 February 2018 6.02.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze was presented with the State Medal on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of independence of the Republic of Kazakhstan, by the Charges d’Affaires ad interim of the Republic of Kazakhstan to Georgia Gulmira Sultanali . Mikheil Janelidze was awarded for his special contribution to the development of bilateral relations between Georgia and Kazakhstan by presidential decree of Nursultan Nazarbayev. Georgian Vice Prime Minister thanked the President of Kazakhstan for the award highlighting that 25-year close and friendly relations between the two countries provides a solid background for successful co-operation in the future.
