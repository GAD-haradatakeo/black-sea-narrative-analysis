14 June 2021 The Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the AmbassadorExtraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the State of Israel to Georgia, RanGidor. Discussing a broad spectrum of relations between the two countries, the sides highlighted the necessity of using the full potential of cooperation within the international organization. Special attention was paid to the ongoing processes in the Middle East and security challenges.
