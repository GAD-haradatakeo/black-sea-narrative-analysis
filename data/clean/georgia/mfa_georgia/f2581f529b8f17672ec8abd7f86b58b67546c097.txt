26 September 2023 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, delivered a speech at the event dedicated to the National Day of Saudi Arabia. On behalf of the Government of Georgia, he wished peace, success and prosperity to the Saudi people. According to the deputy minister, cooperation between Georgia and Saudi Arabia is successfully developing in various fields. In this context, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili emphasized the importance of Prime Minister Irakli Gharibashvili’s visit to Saudi Arabia last year. The deputy minister thanked Saudi Arabia for simplifying visa procedures for Georgian tourists by the decision made in August of this year. According to him, this is an important step for strengthening people-to-people contacts and intensifying relations between the countries. Alexander Khvtisiashil spoke about the importance of strengthening economic and business ties between the two countries.
