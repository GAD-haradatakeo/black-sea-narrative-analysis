18 March 2019 18.03.2019, Montevideo – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with the Chair of the Friendship Group of Georgia at the House of Representatives of Uruguay, Senator Monica Xavier. David Zalkaliani thanked the Senator for her efforts to further develop bilateral relations between Georgia and Uruguay. High appraisal, in this context, was given to Senator Xavier’s visit to Georgia 2018. The sides commended the tradition of effective co-operation between the legislative authorities of the two countries. The Minister highlighted the ongoing reforms in Georgia. The senator expressed her close interest in the healthcare and educations system reforms, as well as in the Prime Minister’s recent initiative to finance 6% of the GDP. The sides exchanged views over the ways to further develop co-operation between Georgia and Uruguay, with due regard for Georgia’s transit functions. The Minister updated the Senator on Georgia’s EU integration process and on the economic prospects offered by Georgia’s free trade agreements with the EU and other leading economies of the world. Conversations included the increasing number of human rights violations and cases of deprivation of life in the occupied regions.
