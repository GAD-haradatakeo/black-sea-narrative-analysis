14 February 2024 On 14 February 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with Viorel Moșanu, Special Representative of the OSCE Chairperson-in-Office for the South Caucasus, Co-Chairman of the Geneva International Discussions. The sides discussed the security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied regions of Georgia and the need to implement the EU mediated 12 August 2008 Ceasefire Agreement. Special attention was paid to the Geneva international discussions. The need to effectively use the format and achieve tangible results was also emphasized. Talking points included the efforts made by Georgia towards the implementation of the policy of peaceful resolution of the Russia-Georgia conflict, and the need for active involvement of European and international partners.
