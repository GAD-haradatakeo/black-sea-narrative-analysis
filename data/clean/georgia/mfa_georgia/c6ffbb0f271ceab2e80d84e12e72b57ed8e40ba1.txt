​“Georgia is Azerbaijan’s friend and strategic partner. Azerbaijan, along with Georgia, celebrates the election of new president” - Chairman of Azerbaijan’s Milli Majlis Ogtai Asadov 16 December 2018 16.12.2018, Telavi -“Georgia is Azerbaijan’s friend and strategic partner. Azerbaijan, along with Georgia, celebrates the election of new president and wishes happiness and prosperity to the Georgian people”, Chairman of Azerbaijan’s Milli Majlis Ogtai Asadov said when commenting on the inauguration of the 5th president of Georgia Salome Zurabishvili.
