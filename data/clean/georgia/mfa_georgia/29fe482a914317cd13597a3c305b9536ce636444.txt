26 November 2018 According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, the developments in the Azov Sea are alarming and call for the international community’s immediate and adequate action against the illegal activities of the Russian military forces and navy ships. “We are following with deep concern the developments near the Azov Sea, and specifically in the Kerch Strait. The facts that have been observed in the strait represent the blatant violation of international law and international maritime law. Such activities are absolutely unacceptable and are condemned by the international community. Immediate action is necessary to deescalate the situation”. The Georgian Foreign Minister reaffirmed Georgia’s support for Ukraine’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. According to him, this support will be further demonstrated by Georgia’s active involvement in all international initiatives with respect to Ukraine. “Georgia has acceded to all international initiatives condemning the ongoing processes in Ukraine, including the annexation of Crimea. Georgia has also joined the EU sanctions against Russian annexation of Crimea. We also support the UN Statements adopted within the framework of the General Assembly and Ukraine’s resolutions in this regard. It needs to be highlighted that Georgia not only joins but is also one of the co-sponsors of a great number of such resolutions. Georgia’s actions in this regard are consistent and we once again confirm our stalwart support for Ukraine’s sovereignty and territorial integrity” – David Zalkaliani said. Documents Similar News
