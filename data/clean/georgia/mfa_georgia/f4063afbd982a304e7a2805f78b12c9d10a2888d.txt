25 April 2024 On 23 April 2024, a consultation meeting took place between Giorgi Tabatadze, Director of the Consular Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and representatives from the Consular Directorate of the Federal Department of Foreign Affairs of the Swiss Confederation. The meeting covered various pertinent consular topics such as protection of citizens' rights and legal interests abroad, the integration of modern technologies in consular services, personal data protection, and consular crisis management. Notably, due to Swiss interest, Giorgi Kalandarishvili, Chairman of the Central Election Commission, participated in the meeting. During this session, Kalandarishvili briefed the Swiss delegation on recent developments and electronic advancements in electoral processes, sharing Georgia's experience in implementing electronic voting systems and the associated challenges.
