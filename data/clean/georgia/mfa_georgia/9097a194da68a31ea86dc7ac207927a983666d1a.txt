20 December 2021 The Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia Nikol Pashinyan visited Georgia on 20 December 2021. The official welcoming ceremony attended by the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani was held at Tbilisi International Airport. According to the Foreign Minister, the fact that the Prime Ministers will chair the Armenia-Georgia intergovernmental economic meeting scheduled to take place during the visit indicates that the bilateral relations between the two countries are highly dynamic. "The issues to be discussed today within the Economic Commission are relevant in terms of further deepening trade and economic relations between the two countries. "Of course, we will also talk about the important initiatives set forth by the Prime Minister of Georgia, especially in connection with the South Caucasus Peaceful Neighborhood Initiative," Zalkaliani said.
