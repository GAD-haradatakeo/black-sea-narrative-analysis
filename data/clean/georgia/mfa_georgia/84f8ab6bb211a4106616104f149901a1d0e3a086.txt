13 August 2025 On 13 August 2025, in Tbilisi, another round of consular consultations was held between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Uzbekistan. Within the framework of the consultations, the parties reviewed issues on the agenda of bilateral consular relations. Both sides underscored the close partnership and productive cooperation between the two countries in the consular sphere. Discussions focused on matters related to border crossing and labour migration. The parties also exchanged information on legislation regulating migration processes. The Georgian side was led by Giorgi Tabatadze, Director of the Department of International Law, Consular and Diaspora Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, while the delegation of the Republic of Uzbekistan was headed by Sherzod Batirov, Head of the Consular and Legal Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
