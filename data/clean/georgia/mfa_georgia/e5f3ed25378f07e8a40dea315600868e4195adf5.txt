Discussion on the 17th Anniversary of the 2008 Russia-Georgia War at the OSCE Permanent Council 19 September 2025 At the session, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, delivered a statement. He highlighted the severe situation in Georgia’s occupied territories with respect to security and human rights. He also drew attention to the peaceful resolution of the conflict and Georgia’s peace-oriented policies, which aim, on one hand, at the country’s de-occupation and, on the other, at restoring trust and promoting reconciliation among communities divided as a result of the war.
