30 June 2023 On 30 June 2023, the Secretary General of the Council of Europe, Marija Pejčinović Burić, visited the village of Odzisi near the occupation line. The Permanent Representative of Georgia to the Council of Europe, Tamar Taliashvili, and the representative of the State Security Service, informed the Secretary General of the Council of Europe about the grave humanitarian situation on the ground.
