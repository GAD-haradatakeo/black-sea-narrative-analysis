28 April 2021 The Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with the Lithuanian Foreign Minister, Gabrielius Landsbergis, who is holding an official visit to Georgia. A joint press-conference was held after the meeting, where David Zalkaliani expressed gratitude towards his Lithuanian colleague for visiting Georgia, which comes as another demonstration of staunch support on behalf of Lithuania, reconfirming our close friendship, underscoring the significance of the support provided by Lithuania to Georgia in terms of undertaking the first two phases of the fight against the COVID-19 pandemic. The heads of Foreign Ministries of their respective states also discussed close cooperation between Georgia and Lithuania, including in terms of the process of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration, underscoring Lithuania’s staunch support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, along with the aforementioned dimensions. Initiatives of the Georgian Government in terms of the European integration process also received positive assessment. David Zalkaliani expressed his gratitude towards the Government of Lithuania for its share provided in terms of the activities of the EUMM, along with objectively informing the EU regarding the illicit actives conducted by the Russian Federation on Georgia’s occupied territory.
