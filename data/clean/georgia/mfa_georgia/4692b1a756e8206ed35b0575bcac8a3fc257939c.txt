9 November 2023 On 9 November 2023, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia held his first meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Norway, Bergljot Hovland. The deputy minister congratulated the Norwegian diplomat on her appointment as ambassador to Georgia and wished her success in her future endeavours. The sides welcomed the excellent friendly relations and close cooperation between Georgia and Norway. The sides welcomed the mutual exchange of high-level visits and stressed the need to deepen the political dialogue, both at the bilateral level and within the framework of the Nordic region and the "Nordic-Baltic Eight". The sides emphasized the importance of fully utilizing the trade and economic potential between the two countries. They exchanged views on the development of business and people-to-people ties between the two countries, as well as educational and cultural relations. The Deputy Minister stated that Georgia highly appreciates the activities of Norway as a donor in our country – this has significantly contributed to the institutional modernization and socio-economic development of Georgia. Teimuraz Janjalia noted with satisfaction Norway's significant contribution to Georgia's Euro-Atlantic integration. Discussions also focused on the current situation in the region and Georgia's peace initiative to ensure sustainable peace, stability and prosperity in the Caucasus. Ambassador Bergljot Hovland, for her part, once again confirmed the Kingdom of Norway's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for our country's integration into the European Union and NATO.
