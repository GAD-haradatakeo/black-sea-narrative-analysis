16 February 2022 On 15 February 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the met with the European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia, the Co-Chair of the Geneva International Discussions, Toivo Klaar. The sides focused on the tense security situation in the region, and in this context, on Russia's illegal and provocative steps in the occupied territories of Georgia, which further aggravates the humanitarian crisis on the ground. The First Deputy Minister focused on Russia's illegal and provocative steps, including the intensified process of de facto annexation, increasing military build-up, the so-called "Boderization", illegal detentions and restrictions of free movement. The sides exchanged views over the ongoing Geneva International Discussions and highlighted the importance of making tangible progress in the negotiation process. Discussions also focused on the Georgian Government’s efforts to resolve the Russian-Georgian conflict peacefully.
