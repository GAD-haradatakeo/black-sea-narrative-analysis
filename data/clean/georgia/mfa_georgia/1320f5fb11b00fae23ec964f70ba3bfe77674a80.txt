16 December 2022 Within the framework of his visit to Tokyo, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Chairman of the Friends of Georgia Group at the Japanese Parliament, and members of the Group. At the meeting, the sides highly appreciated the bilateral cooperation and long-term friendship between Georgia and Japan. The sides positively evaluated the visit of the Chairman of the Georgian Parliament and the parliamentary delegation to Tokyo. Special attention was paid to various directions of Georgia-Japan relations, in particular, to trade and economic cooperation, strengthening of people-to-people ties and the necessary steps to be taken by the sides to develop cooperation in the mentioned areas. Discussions also focused on the importance of strengthening parliamentary cooperation. The Georgian side informed the Japanese side about the reforms implemented in Georgia and the Georgian Parliament’s important role in the process of implementing democratic reforms.
