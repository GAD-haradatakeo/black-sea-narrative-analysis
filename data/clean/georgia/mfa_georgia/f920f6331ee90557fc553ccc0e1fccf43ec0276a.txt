22 January 2021 The Armenian Foreign Minister, Ara Ayvazyan paid an informal visit to Georgia. The head of the Armenian Foreign Ministry was received by the Georgian Vice-PM and Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, who is to host his colleague in Tsinandali for an introductory meeting. During the informal meeting the Foreign Ministers of Georgia and Armenia will discuss the friendly, neighbourly bilateral relations between the two states, along with the current cooperation agenda and the prospects of future development of aforementioned relations.
