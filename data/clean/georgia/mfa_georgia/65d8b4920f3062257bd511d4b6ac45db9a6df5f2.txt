26 February 2019 26.02.2019, Geneva – At the meeting between the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani and the Director General of the World Health Organization, TedrosAdhanom Ghebreyesus, the sides highlighted Georgia’s successful co-operation with the WHO. Giving high appraisal to the reforms and projects implemented in Georgia in team with the Organization, the sides singled out for special mention C Hepatitis Elimination Project that is a real success story. Discussions focused on the possible employment of Georgia candidates within the World Health Organization.
