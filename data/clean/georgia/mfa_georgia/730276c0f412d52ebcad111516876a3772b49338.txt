29 January 2026 Within the framework of his visit to the Kingdom of Spain, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, George Zurabashvili held meetings with the Chair of the Joint Committee on the European Union of the Congress of Deputies and the Senate, Alberto Fabra Part, the Acting Chair of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Spanish Senate, Beltrán Villalba and members of the Committees. The meetings focused on the role of parliamentary diplomacy and opportunities to deepen cooperation between the legislatures of the two countries. Discussions addressed the dynamics of bilateral and multilateral cooperation, as well as prospects for its further development. The parties discussed Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration process, exchanged views on regional security issues and highlighted the grave situation in Georgia’s occupied regions. The Spanish side once again reaffirmed its unwavering support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
