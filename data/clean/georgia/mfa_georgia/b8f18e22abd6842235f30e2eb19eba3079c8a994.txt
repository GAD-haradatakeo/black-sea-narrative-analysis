5 October 2018 04.10.2018, Brussels - the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili participated in the meeting of high-ranking officials from Eastern Partnership countries. The meeting was also attended by representatives of EU Member States. The meeting was held in preparation for Eastern Partnership Summit in Luxembourg scheduled for 15 October 2018. Discussions focused on the results of the Eastern Partnership Summit in Brussels on 24 November and the process of implementing "20 deliverables for 2020". It was highlighted that 2019 marks the 10th anniversary of eastern partnership. The Georgian delegation reaffirmed that "20 deliverables for 2020" is fully in line with Georgia’s priorities.
