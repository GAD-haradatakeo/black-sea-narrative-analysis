18 February 2022 Within the framework of the Munich Conference, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Vice Prime Minister/ Minister of Foreign and Expatriate Affairs, Ayman Hussein Abdullah Al-Safadi. Discussing the Georgian-Jordanian bilateral relations the Ministers underlined the partnership and close cooperation established between the two countries. David Zalkaliani thanked the Jordanian side, personally, King Abdullah II of Jordan, for the important decision to donate to Georgia a plot of land near the site of baptism on the Jordan River. The sides reaffirmed their readiness to set up a Georgian cultural center on the mentioned land. The Ministers stressed the importance of exchanging high-level visits. The talks focused on Georgia's close and good cooperation with the Middle East countries, on the growing potential for trade and economic cooperation, as well as on successful tourism cooperation and people-to-people contacts.
