24 January 2025 On 24 January 2025, Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Turkmenistan to Georgia, Dovletmyrat Seyitmammedov. During the meeting, the parties emphasized the high-level cooperation between Georgia and Turkmenistan in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Special attention was paid to the existing trade and economic relations and their growing potential. The significance of the Intergovernmental Economic Cooperation Commission and its successful operations was highlighted. Positive evaluations were also given to the parliamentary cooperation between the two countries.
