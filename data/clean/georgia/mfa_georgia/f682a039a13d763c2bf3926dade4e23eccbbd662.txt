23 December 2019 Within the framework of the trilateral foreign ministerial meeting of Azerbaijan, Turkey and Georgia, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with his Azeri counterpart, ElmarMammadyarov. The parties highlighted the results of the Georgian Prime Minister, Giorgi Gakharia’s recent visit to the Republic of Azerbaijan. The two ministers also discussed a broad spectrum of political, economic, cultural and humanitarian cooperation between Georgia and Azerbaijan. Conversations also included the need to further convene the joint delimitation commission to discuss the issues relating to delimitation and demarcation of the border. The parties also spoke about the need to retain the existing practice for movement throughout the Davit Gareji territory. The Parties also highlighted the importance of conducting another regular meeting of the bilateral inter-governmental commission.
