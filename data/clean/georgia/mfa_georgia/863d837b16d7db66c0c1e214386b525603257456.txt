16 June 2022 On 16 June 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held an introductory meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Imamura Akira. The sides noted with satisfaction the close and friendly relations between two the countries. The Minister thanked the Ambassador for Japan’s outstanding support for Georgia. Discussions focused on the positive dynamics of bilateral cooperation between the two countries and on the high potential for further deepening of both political and economic relations. The importance of exchanging high-level visits was also stressed. During the meeting, the sides discussed the security challenges facing the region and noted the importance of the international community’s active efforts to ensure peace and stability. At the end of the meeting, the sides reaffirmed their readiness to actively cooperate on issues on both bilateral and multilateral agenda, which will contribute to the development of relations between the two countries in all areas of mutual interest.
