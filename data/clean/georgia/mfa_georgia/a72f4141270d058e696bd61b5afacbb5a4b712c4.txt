10 November 2025 The Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Indonesia, Tornike Nozadze, presented his Letters of Credence to the President of the Republic of Indonesia, Prabowo Subianto. The credential presentation ceremony took place at the Presidential Palace in Jakarta. During the meeting held as part of the ceremony, the parties discussed the friendly relations and close cooperation between Georgia and Indonesia and expressed their readiness to further strengthen collaboration across various areas of mutual interest.
