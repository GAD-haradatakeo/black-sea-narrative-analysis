26 June 2021 On 26 June 2021, the British destroyer HMS Defender docked at the Georgian Black Sea port of Batumi on Saturday. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia attended the ceremony. HMS DEFENDER’s arrival at the Batumi port points to the importance of Black Sea security and is yet another proof of the UK’s engagement in the region.
