23 March 2022 On 23 March 2022, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Abdullah Almutairi, who has completed his diplomatic mission in Georgia. During the meeting, the sides reviewed a wide range of bilateral agendas and highlighted the growing dynamics of relations between the two countries. Particular attention was paid to the importance of exchanging high-level bilateral visits.
