16 October 2023 On 16 October, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia, and the Director General of the General Directorate of the European Union, Archil Karaulashvili, met with with Jolanta Taczynska, the Head of Facility for the implementation of the EU-Georgia Association Agreement-II and Adam Lazowski, an expert of the same project. Discussions focused on the project activities aimed at supporting Georgia's integration into the European Union; Special attention was paid to strengthening the planning and electronic monitoring system for the implementation of the Association Agreement and adding a component of legislative convergence with the European Union and multi-year planning. Also, the sides talked about the challenges of EU integration, discussed the experience and examples of different countries in this process. The project to support the implementation of the Association Agreement between the European Union and Georgia has been operating in Georgia since 2015 with the financial support of the European Union. Its purpose is to help Georgian public agencies in the implementation of the EU Integration and Association Agreement. The second phase of the project will be completed by the end of 2023.
