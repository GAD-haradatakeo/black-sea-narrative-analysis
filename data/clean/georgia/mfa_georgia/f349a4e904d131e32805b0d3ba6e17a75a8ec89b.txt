2 August 2019 02.08.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili attended the reception evening to mark the National Day of the Swiss Confederation. He, together with Ambassador of the Swiss Confederation to Georgia, Patric Franzen delivered opening remarks before the attending audience. Congratulating the Swiss people on their national holiday and wishing them peace, stability and prosperity, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili noted with satisfaction the increasingly higher level of close relations between the two countries. “Georgians often compare Georgia to Switzerland, which is the proof of how much our citizens love Swirtzerlad. However, we want to be comparable to Switzerland not only in terms of landscapes, but as a strong and modern state with rich historical past and the outlook for the future that can be seen as a synthesis of traditions and innovations, stabile and sustainable economy and effective education system.” – Vakhtang Makharoblishvili said. He thanked the Swiss side for its support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its role as a mediator in Georgia’s relations with the Russian Federation. The First Deputy Minister also expressed his gratitude for Switzerland’s strong support for Georgia’s European integration. According to Vakhtang Makharoblishvili, the Swiss Natinal Council President’s upcoming visit to Georgia is yet another proof of the Swiss Government’s support for and solidarity with Georgia and can further contribute to the development of bilateral relations between the two countries.
