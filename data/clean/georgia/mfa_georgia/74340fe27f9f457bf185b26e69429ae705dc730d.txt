14 May 2019 14.05.2019 – “My deputy together with representatives of other authorities meets today with the Azerbaijani side. It is the first meeting between the co-chairs, not the meeting of the commission and I hope it will proceed in a constructive atmosphere, quite in line with our strategic partnership” – David Zalkaliani said on the meeting taking place between the Georgian and Azerbaijani sides. According to him, the Georgian and Azerbaijani sides have been in active communication and coordination since the issue was raised. The Minister once again called on the public to refrain from emotional actions around the issue. According to him, it is important to ensure that certain parties do not capitalize on the existing situation. “Anyone should avoid scoring political points. This will damage Georgia, Azerbaijan and our strategic partnership as well. And the only force that will benefit will be the one wishing to exacerbate the relations. With our territories still occupied we should in no case allow that our relations with our strategic partner are deteriorated” – the Minister said. Documents Similar News
