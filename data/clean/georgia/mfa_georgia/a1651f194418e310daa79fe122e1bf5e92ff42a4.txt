26 September 2018 26.09.2018, New York – Within the framework of the 73rd session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting withhis Uruguayan counterpart Rodolfo Nin Novoa. The two ministers discussed a broad spectrum of issues relating to bilateral co-operation, including in the areas of media freedom, security and democracy. The potential of further developing trade and agricultural sectors was also highlighted. The Uruguayan side expressed its close interest in Georgia’s transit and investment potential that can provide a good platform for Uruguayan businessmen to launch business not only in Georgia but also in the region. The sides highly appraised cooperation between the two countries within the framework of international organizations. David Zalkaliani updated his colleague on the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions and Georgia’s peace policy with respect to the conflict resolution.
