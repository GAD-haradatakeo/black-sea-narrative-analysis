10 February 2023 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the earthquake that hit Türkiye on 6 February, killing thousands of people, causing massive destruction and devastating cities and villages. Georgia extends its sincere sympathy to Türkiye over the heavy loss of life and extensive damage and expresses its solidarity with all those affected by this tragic disaster. Georgia has already sent a rescue crew and necessary equipment to Türkiye to help the rescue efforts in quake-stricken areas. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs remains in constant contact with the competent authorities of Türkiye and will continue active cooperation in this regard.
