8 May 2018 The Embassy of Georgia to the U.S. along with the Alexander Kartveli Association hosted the Opening Reception of the Alexander Kartveli STEM Speaker Series. At the Opening Ceremony, Ambassador David Bakradze welcomed the guests and emphasized the fact that NASA voiced the first Georgian song Chakrulo in the space. Richard Rubin- the President of the Alexander Kartveli Association- also addressed the guests and shared the life story of Alexander Kartveli. The evening's special guest John Cassani extensively discussed the NASA missions Voyager 1 and Voyager 2 along with his other projects. He also played Chakrulo for the audience. Documents Similar News
