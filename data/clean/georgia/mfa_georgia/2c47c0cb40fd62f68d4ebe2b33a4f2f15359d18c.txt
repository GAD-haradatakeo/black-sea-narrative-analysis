16 June 2025 On 16 June 2025, a courtesy meeting was held between the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Korea to Georgia, Hyondoo Kim. During the meeting, the parties discussed current issues and future prospects of bilateral relations. Particular emphasis was placed on the fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Korea within the framework of international organizations. The Deputy Foreign Minister of Georgia expressed gratitude to the Korean side for supporting the United Nations resolution on the "Status of internally displaced persons and refugees from Abkhazia, Georgia and the Tskhinvali region/South Ossetia, Georgia." Deputy Minister Darsalia also briefed the Korean Ambassador on Georgia’s key foreign policy priorities and the situation in the occupied territories. Lasha Darsalia expressed his satisfaction with the opening of a resident Embassy of the Republic of Korea in Tbilisi and wished the Ambassador success in his future diplomatic endeavors.
