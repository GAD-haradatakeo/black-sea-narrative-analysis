5 April 2024 Georgia's Foreign Affairs Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with Panama's Foreign Affairs Minister, Janaina Tewaney Mencomo, as part of his tour of Latin American nations. This marked the first such meeting in diplomatic history between the two countries. Panama's Foreign Ministry head warmly welcomed Georgia's minister and discussed various aspects of their foreign policies, national development, and economic conditions. The agenda included discussions on enhancing bilateral relations and exploring opportunities for cooperation through political consultations. Panama was particularly interested in Georgia's expertise in tourism management. Both ministers also explored ways to leverage their friendly ties to boost investment and trade. The conversation also centred on global security concerns, with Panama reaffirming its support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia.
