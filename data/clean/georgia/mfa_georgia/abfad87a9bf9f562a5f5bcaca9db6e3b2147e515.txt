16 June 2021 On 16 June 2021 the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the co-chair of a negotiating group on the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict, Andrew Schofer and the US Ambassador to Georgia, Kelly Degnan. The main topic of discussion concerned the recent dynamics of the Russo-Georgian conflict. The parties discussed the ongoing processes in the region. Within this context the parties discussed the increased frequency of provocations in the occupied regions, including in terms of actions directed towards annexation of Abkhazia and South Ossetia such as borderisation, illicit arrest of Georgian citizens and kidnappings. David Zalkaliani underscored the necessity to unconditionally and immediately release from captivity of Zaza Gakheladze, Irakli Bebua as well as all other illegally arrested Georgian nationals. The US representative reconfirmed unwavering support towards Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognised borders. Particular attention was paid to high level international events and meetings, to include the issue of Russo-Georgian conflict on the agenda of the international community. The Minister thanked the US party for keeping the Georgian issue relevant on the high political arena. The US partners thanked the Georgian party for facilitating the most significant dialogue between Armenia and Azerbaijan, which was an important humanitarian action performed with the involvement of the US.
