28 July 2025 Zurab Mchedlishvili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Colombia (with residence in Brasília), presented his Letters of Credence to the President of Colombia, Gustavo Petro. During the meeting held as part of the official ceremony, the parties discussed the current state of bilateral relations between the two countries and explored prospects for future cooperation, emphasizing the potential for deepening ties and fostering closer engagement. Ambassador Mchedlishvili expressed his readiness to actively contribute to the advancement of political dialogue and the organization of high-level bilateral visits, as well as to the further development of economic and cultural relations between Georgia and Colombia. He also conveyed his gratitude to the Colombian side for its steadfast support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
