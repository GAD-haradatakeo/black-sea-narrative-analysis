5 December 2024 Within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council in Malta, Maka Botchorishvili held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Finland, Elina Valtonen. During the meeting, the ministers reviewed issues of cooperation within international frameworks, including Finland's upcoming OSCE Chairmanship and its priorities. Regarding the developments in Georgia, the Georgian Foreign Minister reaffirmed the Government’s strong commitment to making active efforts to achieve further success in the country’s European integration process. The discussion also focused on the parliamentary elections held in Georgia.
