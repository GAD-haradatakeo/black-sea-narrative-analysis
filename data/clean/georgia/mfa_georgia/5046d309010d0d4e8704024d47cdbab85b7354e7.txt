17 February 2018 17.02.2018, Munich – Germany supports the Eastern Partnership, Georgia's European integration and Georgia's visa-free travel with the EU/Schengen zone, which is a good way to further strengthen ties, - Bundestag Vice President Claudia Roth said at the meeting with Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze. As it was noted at the meeting, Georgia enjoys significant support at the German Bundestag and Germany is interested in the further development of the cooperation. The Georgian Foreign Minister for his part stressed the importance of close friendship with Germany, which has deep historical roots. Janelidze thanked the Vice-President of the Bundestag for Germany’s great contribution to Georgia's democratic and economic development. He also underlined the successful inter-parliamentary cooperation. In addition, the sides praised Georgia's participation in the Frankfurt International Book Fair as an honorary guest, which is a unique opportunity for the promotion of Georgian culture not only in Germany but at the international level as well.
