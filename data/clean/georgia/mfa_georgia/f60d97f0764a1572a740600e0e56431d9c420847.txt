18 December 2019 18.12.2019, Tbilisi – Meeting on “Georgia and Neighbouring Countries“ was held within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference -2019. Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia spoke before the panel. Speakers included Director of the Department of Neighbouring Countries Malkhaz Mikeladze, Ambassador of Georgia to the Republic of Azerbaijan, Zurab Pataradze, Ambassador of Georgia to the Republic of Armenia Giorgi Saganelidze and Ambassador of Georgia to the Republic of Turkey, Giorgi Janjgava. Discussions focused on important aspects of the domestic and foreign policy of Georgia’s neighbouring countries, their relations with international organizations, including the EU, the European countries and the Russian Federation. The Ambassadors paid special attention to the formats of Georgia’s relations with the neighbouring countries, as well as their experience of implementing reforms and security challenges.
