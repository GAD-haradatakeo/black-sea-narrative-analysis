15 December 2022 Within the framework of his visit to Tokyo, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Executive Senior Vice President of the Japan International Cooperation Agency, Junichi Yamada. The Minister thanked the Japan International Cooperation Agency for many years of fruitful cooperation with Georgia and for its important projects that help the country respond to various needs. The Georgian side expressed its gratitude for the Agency's involvement in the implementation of such an important project as the East-West highway, etc. At the meeting, the minister drew attention to the priority areas that provide the opportunity for cooperation with the agency in the future, including in the areas of infrastructure, renewable energy, and transport. Mr Yamada expressed the Agency’s interest in exploring the current needs and future projects. As the vice-president of the agency noted, Georgia's economic growth rate and stable ratings provide a good opportunity to attract international investments.
