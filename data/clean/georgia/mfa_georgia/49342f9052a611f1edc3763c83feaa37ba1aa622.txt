15 September 2025 On 15 September 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Avi Dichter, Minister of Agriculture and Food Security of the State of Israel. During the meeting, the parties emphasized the historical friendship between the Georgian and Israeli peoples, the strong partnership between Georgia and Israel, and the close relations currently in place. The ministers reaffirmed their readiness to deepen cooperation, particularly in the areas of trade, economy, tourism, and agriculture. In this context, attention was drawn to food security as one of the most pressing challenges of the contemporary world, and to the promotion of mutually beneficial collaboration in this regard. The discussion also addressed ongoing developments in the Middle East. Particular focus was placed on the security environment in the South Caucasus region and the situation in the occupied regions of Georgia. The importance of ensuring peace and maintaining strong state institutions for sustainable development was underlined.
