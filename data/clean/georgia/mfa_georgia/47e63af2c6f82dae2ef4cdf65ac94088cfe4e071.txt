The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia continues to work on the main priority of the country - strengthening sovereignty and restoring territorial integrity in a peaceful way - Ilia Darchiashvili 7 February 2024 "The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia continues to work for the main priority of the country - strengthening the sovereignty of the country and restoring territorial integrity in a peaceful way"- Ilia Darchiashvili, the candidate for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, said at the joint session of the Foreign Relations, EU Integration, Diaspora and Caucasus Committees of the Parliament of Georgia.
