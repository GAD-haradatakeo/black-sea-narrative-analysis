23 September 2020 On 23 September 2020, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Republic of Azerbaijan in Georgia, Fayig Guliyev, presented his credentials to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani. After the ceremony, the parties held a brief introductory meeting, where the Minister congratulated the Ambassador upon his appointment, wishing him success in his future endeavours, expressing hope that the high level strategic and friendly relations between the two states will further deepen during his tenure in Georgia. During the meeting the parties also discussed the relevant issues on the agenda of the bilateral relations of the two states, including perspectives and dimensions of future cooperation.
