9 April 2025 On 9 and 10 April 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will pay a visit to the Hellenic Republic to participate in the international conference Delphi Economic Forum. This forum serves as a prominent platform where global leaders, policymakers, and experts convene to discuss key issues in global economics, politics and security. The Delphi Economic Forum will be opened by the President of the Hellenic Republic, Konstantinos Tassoulas. Among the high-level attendees will be the President of the Republic of Bulgaria. As part of the forum, Maka Botchorishvili will deliver remarks during a working session dedicated to the increasing regional and global importance of the Black Sea. She will also take part in a panel discussion focusing on Georgia’s pivotal role in the European Union’s enlargement and connectivity agenda.
