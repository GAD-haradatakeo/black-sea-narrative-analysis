22 January 2025 On 22 January 2025, Georgia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Hungary's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Georgia, Anna Maria Sikó. The discussion focused on the dynamic exchange of high-level visits between the two countries and upcoming meetings. The parties reviewed various aspects of Georgia-Hungary cooperation, with a particular focus on trade and economic relations. Particular emphasis was placed on collaboration within the framework of the “Agreement between the Government of Georgia and the Government of Hungary on the Promotion and Reciprocal Protection of Investments.” The meeting also addressed ongoing developments in Georgia and its relations with the European Union, highlighting the significance of Hungary's supportive stance on Georgia's path to European integration. Both sides underscored the strong, friendly relations and robust strategic partnership between Georgia and Hungary.
