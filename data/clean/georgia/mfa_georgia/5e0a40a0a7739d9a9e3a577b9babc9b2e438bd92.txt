19 July 2018 19.07.2018 - Netherlands has made a decision during the NATO Brussels summit to allocate €1mln to support self-defence capabilities of Georgia, Ukraine, Jordan, Tunisia and Iraq, the Foreign Ministry of Netherlands has announced. “With this contribution, the Netherlands is investing in stability in the countries surrounding Europe and in our own security.
