18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of New Zealand to Georgia, Paul Ballantyne (resident in Warsaw). The discussion focused on opportunities to deepen cooperation between Georgia and New Zealand across various sectors. The parties also addressed the holding of political consultations and mutual support within international organizations. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed gratitude to New Zealand for its steadfast support of Georgia’s territorial integrity and sovereignty. Within the framework of the visit, the Ambassador of New Zealand presented copies of his Letters of Credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, after which an introductory meeting was held. The parties discussed the friendly relations existing between the two countries.
