13 June 2022 On 13 June 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, took part in an event dedicated to the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and the Republic of Armenia. Speaking before the audience, the First Deputy Minister highlighted the close and friendly relations between Georgia and Armenia. "I am convinced that our past provides a solid basis for the further development and strengthening of relations. In this rapidly changing geopolitical environment, it is essential to strengthen good neighborly relations between our countries so that together we could tackle new challenges and exploit new opportunities." - Darsalia noted. Lasha Darsalia highlighted that cooperation between the two countries is actively developing in the fields of trade, transport, energy, culture, tourism and other areas. "I am convinced that Georgia-Armenia friendship is not only the groundwork upon which to base our bilateral ties, but also an important precondition for the establishment of peace and stability in our region," Lasha Darsalia said in his speech.
