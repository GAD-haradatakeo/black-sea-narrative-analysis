21 September 2017 21.09.2017, NewYork – On the sidelines of the 72nd session of the UN General Assembly, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Mikheil Janelidze and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Thailand Don Pramudwinai signed the Memorandum of Mutual Understanding. The two ministers spoke about co-operation in bilateral and multilateral formats expressing their interest in developing relations in the areas of mutual interest, including touris, people-to-people contacts, innovations, etc. Special note was taken of the successful co-operation between the two countries, including within the UN.
