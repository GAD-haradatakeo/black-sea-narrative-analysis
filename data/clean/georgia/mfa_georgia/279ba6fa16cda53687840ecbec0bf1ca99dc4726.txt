10 October 2022 On 10 October 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the MinisterofStateforExternal Affairs ofIndia, Meenakshi Lekhi. The sides discussed the relations between Georgia and India. Special attention was paid to the importance of developing bilateral economic, cultural, educational and humanitarian cooperation in Speaking about the existing cooperation between the two countries in the areas of culture and education, the sides noted with satisfaction that Georgia has created all conditions for Indian students to receive higher education. Discussions focused on the activities of Indian businessmen in Georgia, as well as on the issues of cooperation within the international organizations. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia thanked the Indian side for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia.
