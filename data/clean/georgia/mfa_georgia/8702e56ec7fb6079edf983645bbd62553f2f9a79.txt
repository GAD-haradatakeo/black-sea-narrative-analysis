19 September 2018 18.09.2018, Washington – According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, the President of the Overseas Private Investment Corporation (OPIC), Ray W. Washburne gave a high appraisal to Georgia’s economic and investment environment and outlined prospects for future co-operation. David Zalkaliani said that OPIC is interested in making investments in the areas of infrastructure, logistics and tourism in Georgia. “They maintain that the economic reforms and the course the Georgian government is implementing are in full line with the U.S. interests and their vision of how Georgia can become a role-model of economic development in the region” – David Zalkaliani said.
