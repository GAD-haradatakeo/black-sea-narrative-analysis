2 February 2018 02.02.2018, Tbilisi -​Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held his first meeting with the newly-appointed Head of Georgia Country Office of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC), Max Santner. The sides noted with satisfaction that after the lapse of several years, the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies opened its office in Georgia again. The sides spoke about their plans for the future and agreed to further continue close co-operation. Documents Similar News
