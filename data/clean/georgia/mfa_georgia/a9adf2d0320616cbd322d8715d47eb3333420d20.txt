30 November 2018 20.11.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani received Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia Johannes Douma. Discussions focused on the agenda of bilateral relations between Georgia and the Netherlands. Special attention in this context was paid to the Georgian Foreign Minister’s upcoming meeting with his Dutch counterpart due to be held within the framework of the 25th OSCE Ministerial Council.
