24 February 2020 24.02.2020, Geneva – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with UN Secretary General Antonio Guterres within the framework of the 43rd session of the UN High Level Segment of the Human Rights Council. Discussions focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions and the UN’s role in peaceful resolution of the conflict. Special attention was paid to the grave humanitarian situation in the occupied territories and the occupation regime’s destructive actions that further aggravate the already difficult humanitarian situation on the ground, including discrimination of ethnic Georgians, the ban on native language education, closure of checkpoints, installation of artificial barriers across the occupation line, illegal detentions and kidnappings. The sides emphasized the need to keep the Russia-Georgia conflict high on the UN agenda. Conversations also included the UN’s role in the Geneva International Discussions. Positive appraisal was given to the practice of adopting annual resolutions with respect to Georgia and the UN Secretary General’s reports on the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions.
