10 July 2018 10.07.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with UNICEF's Representative to Georgia, Laila Gad. The sides discussed various aspects of co-operation. David Zalkaliani thanked Laila Gad for her active involvement and UNICEF’s activities in Georgia. The sides highlighted the importance of further deepening co-operation between Georgia and UNICEF.
