1 December 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia met with his Swedish counterpart, Tobias Billström in Łódź, within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Meeting. Discussing the Georgia-Sweden bilateral cooperation, the sides highly appraised the close friendly relations between the two countries. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked Sweden for its support to Georgia and emphasized the importance of close cooperation with Sweden during the Swedish Presidency of the Council of the European Union. The ministers exchanged information on Georgia’s European integration agenda and the process of implementing the European Commission’s recommendations. The sides emphasized the importance of recognizing Georgia’s European perspective by the Commission and expressed the hope that the country will receive the candidate status as well. The minister welcomed Sweden’s bid for NATO membership and expressed the hope that Sweden will join the Alliance in the near future.
