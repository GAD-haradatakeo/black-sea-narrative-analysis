18 October 2017 18.10.2017, Brussels – According to the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze, Georgia has many friends within the European Parliament manifesting itself in the European Parliament’s firm and unconditional support for Georgia on its European Integration path. Mikheil Janelidze said that the European Parliament’s support translated into concrete results when the Foreign Affairs Committee of the European Parliament passed its Resolution on recommendations for the Eastern Partnership. “The Resolution reflects the visions regarding Georgia’s European future, concrete instruments to be employed and practical steps to be taken across the European integration path” – the Minister said. He expressed the hope that recommendations will be adopted at the European Parliament’s upcoming session in November since Georgia enjoys the support of all political groups. “Georgia is a country nurturing great ambitions for political, as well as sectoral and economic integration. We are successful on this path and will achieve even more with the tremendous political support from the European parliament. As for Georgia’s European future – the Association Agreement unequivocally reaffirms Georgia’s European choice and aspirations. We are a European country with great potential to take further steps towards its ultimate goal – membership of the European Union. The Government of Georgia does it utmost to make this success a reality” – The Minister said. The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze paid a visit to Brussels on 18 and 19 October where he holds meetings with representatives of the political groups of the European Parliament and will appear at the Foreign Affairs Committee of the European Parliament.
