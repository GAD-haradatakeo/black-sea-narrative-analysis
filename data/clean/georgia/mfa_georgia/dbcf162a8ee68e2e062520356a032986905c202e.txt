18 October 2023 On 17 October 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with the head of delegation of the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) in Georgia, Anne Montavon, and the ICRC Envoy on missing persons in Caucasus, Agnes Coutou. The minister thanked the representatives of the International Committee of the Red Cross for its humanitarian efforts in Georgia, especially for the support of the population living in the occupied Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions and of the people affected by the Russia-Georgia conflict. Discussions focused on Effective cooperation between Georgia and the International Committee of the Red Cross in the search for and identification of missing persons. The sides highlighted the important role of the ICRC in this regard. At the end of the meeting, the sides confirmed their commitment to continue close cooperation. Documents Similar News
