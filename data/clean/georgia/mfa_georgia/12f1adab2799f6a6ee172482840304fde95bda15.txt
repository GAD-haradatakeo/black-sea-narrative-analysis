20 July 2023 Ilia Darchiashvili met the Chairman of the European Affairs Committee of the National Assembly of the Republic of Portugal, Luís Capoulas Santos. At the meeting, the sides emphasized the friendly relations and partnership between the two countries. They talked about current issues of cooperation, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. Special note was taken of the cooperation between the legislative bodies of the two countries and the successful activities of the groups of friends. The minister reviewed Georgia's foreign policy priorities, and in this regard, special emphasis was placed on the country's European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Ilia Darchiashvili informed the chairman of the committee about the implementation of the recommendations of the European Commission and the reforms implemented in the country.
