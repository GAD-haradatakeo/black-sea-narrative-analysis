24 October 2023 On 24 October 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia, took part in the event dedicated to the National Day of Austria. In his speech, Teimuraz Janjalia congratulated the government and people of Austria on the National Day and wished them peace and prosperity. As he noted, Georgia and Austria have close friendly relations based on mutual respect, common interests and values. Teimuraz Janjalia emphasized the successful cooperation between the two countries in the fields of politics, economy, trade, education and agriculture. The Deputy Minister focused on the exchange of high-level visits between Georgia and Austria, singling out for special mention the Austrian Federal Chancellor’s first official visit to Georgia this year. Teimuraz Janjalia emphasized the importance of Austria's support for Georgia's European integration and thanked the Austrian government for its unwavering support for the country's European aspirations, as well as for Georgia's territorial integrity and sovereignty.
