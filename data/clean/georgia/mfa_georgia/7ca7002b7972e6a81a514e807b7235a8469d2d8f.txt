15 June 2022 Visa-free travel regime between Georgia and the United Arab Emirates came into force today. Under the agreement, ordinary passport holders of either country will be able to enter to, transit through and stay in the territory of the state of the other party without visa for not more than ninety (90) days from the date of entry. The amendments made have expanded the scope of the 2017 Memorandum of Understanding between the Government of Georgia and the Government of the United Arab Emirates on Visa-Free Travel for Holders of Diplomatic and Special/Service Passports and are designed to facilitate the movement of citizens of Georgia and the United Arab Emirates.
