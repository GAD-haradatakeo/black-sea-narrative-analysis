19 July 2017 19.07.2017, Tbilisi –Participants of Ambassadorial 2017 held a working meeting with representatives of civil society. The meeting was moderated by Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze. Mikheil Janelidze spoke about the importance of close co-operation between the civil and public sectors in formulating and effectively implementing the foreign policy of the country. Zviad Devdariani, Director of the Civil Development Agency (CiDA) highlighted the ways to fight against anti-western propaganda and the importance of raising people’s awareness about the concrete benefits of Euro-Atlantic integration. Vasil Sikharulidze Chairman of the Atlantic Council of Georgia paid special attention to the need to develop close co-operation with academic communities and think tanks of foreign countries in order to facilitate the promotion of Georgia internationally. Aleksi Aleksishvili, Chairman and CEO at Policy and Management Consulting Group (PMCG) underlined the need to create innovatve export product, to get close to the EU market, to diversify markets and find ways to encourage trade relations. Kakha Gogolashvili, Director of EU Studies at the Georgian Foundation for Strategic and International Studies spoke about Georgia’s European integration and the civil society’s role in this process.
