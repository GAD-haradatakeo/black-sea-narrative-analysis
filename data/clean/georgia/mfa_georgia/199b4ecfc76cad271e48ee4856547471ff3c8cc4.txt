22 August 2025 On 22 August 2025, the newly appointed Ambassador of the State of Israel to Georgia, Walid Abu Haya, presented copies of his credentials to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. During the meeting, the parties discussed the existing cooperation between Georgia and the State of Israel and emphasized the importance of its further deepening. The discussion also highlighted the broad potential for strengthening bilateral relations across a range of fields, including education, the economy, tourism, and beyond. The Georgian side wished Ambassador Walid Abu Haya success in his forthcoming diplomatic activities and reaffirmed its readiness to contribute to the further consolidation of bilateral relations.
