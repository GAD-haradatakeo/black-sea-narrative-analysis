29 December 2024 As part of the annual Ambassadors’ Conference, a working meeting was held with key representatives of the Georgian government. Participants included Prime Minister Irakli Kobakhidze, President Mikheil Kavelashvili, Chairman of Parliament Shalva Papuashvili, and Majority Leader Mamuka Mdinaradze. This meeting marked one of President Kavelashvili's first engagements with Georgian diplomats following his inauguration. Ambassadors extended their congratulations on his presidency and wished him success in his new role. The discussions focused on current developments within the country and the government’s strategic plans for national progress. Participants deliberated on foreign policy priorities and the government’s initiatives for achieving set objectives. The interactive session provided an opportunity for senior government officials to address questions from the ambassadors and share insights on foreign affairs and domestic political matters of interest.
