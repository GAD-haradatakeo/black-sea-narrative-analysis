30 October 2023 On 30-31 October, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia is hosting NATO assessment mission, the purpose of which is to assess the progress in the implementation of the 2023 Annual National Program (ANP) of Georgia. During the ongoing meetings within the framework of the visit, the representatives of the state agencies provide the assessment mission with comprehensive information about the reforms and activities that have been implemented within the framework of the 2023 document. In the ANP of 2023, special attention was focused on the reforms carried out by the executive, legislative and judicial authorities of Georgia in line with 12 recommendations of the European Union. The annual national program is one of the main mechanisms of Georgia's integration into NATO. The document outlines the country's foreign and security policy priorities and democratic reforms planned for the year. The document includes such important areas as protection of human rights, strengthening of legal supervision, reforms implemented in electoral, justice and judicial spheres, security and economic issues.
