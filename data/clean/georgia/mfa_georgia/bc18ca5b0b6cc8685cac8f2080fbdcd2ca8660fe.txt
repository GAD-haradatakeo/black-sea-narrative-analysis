19 April 2021 On 19 April 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the UNICEF Regional Director for Europe and Central Asia, Afshan Khan. The sides discussed issues relating to UNICEF activities in Georgia, especially amid COVID-19 pandemic. The Deputy Foreign Minister thanked the UNICEF Regional Director for the projects implemented and UNICEF’s support for Georgia in fighting the pandemic. Talking points also included the 2021-2025 Country Programme Document (CPD) for Georgia. Afshan Khan thanked the Georgian side for fruitful cooperation in the process of developing and adopting the document. Lasha Darsalia briefed Afshan Khan on the difficult security, human rights and humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied regions, which has been even further complicated by the COVID-19 pandemic. Speaking about the dire situation in terms of protection of children’s rights, the sides underlined the importance of international organizations’ continuous engagement in finding a solution to the problems.
