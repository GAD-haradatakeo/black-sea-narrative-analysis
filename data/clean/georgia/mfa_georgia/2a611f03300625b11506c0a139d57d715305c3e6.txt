5 February 2019 05.02.2019, U.S. - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Assistant Secretary of State for European and Eurasian Affairs, Wess Mitchell. The sides spoke about Georgia-U.S. co-operation in various fields. Highly appraising Georgia-U.S. strategic partnership and the role of Georgia as the key ally and partner of the U.S. in the region, the sides highlighted prospects for further enhancing Georgian-US ties for defence and security, as well as in the fields of economy and trade. The sides discussed the security situation in the region. The Georgian side thanked the United States for its firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, including through the U.S. Appropriations act. The Georgian Foreign Minister updated the Assistant Secretary of State for European and Eurasian Affairs on the ongoing reforms in Georgia and the process of European and Euro-Atlantic integration.
