27 September 2024 Within the framework of the 79th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Albanian Minister for Europe and Foreign Affairs, Igli Hasani. During the meeting, the ministers reviewed the current agenda of relations between Georgia and Albania, as well as issues of cooperation within international formats. The importance of developing political dialogue and exchanging high-level visits was emphasized. The ministers welcomed the political consultations held between the foreign ministries of the two countries in Tbilisi last year and expressed their readiness to continue cooperation in this format. Attention was also drawn to Georgia's European integration. Ilia Darchiashvili highlighted that joining the European Union is one of Georgia’s main foreign policy priorities, and the country is consistently implementing reforms on this path. The Georgian Foreign Minister also briefed his Albanian counterpart on the situation in Georgia's occupied territories. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked his colleague for Albania’s support for Georgia.
