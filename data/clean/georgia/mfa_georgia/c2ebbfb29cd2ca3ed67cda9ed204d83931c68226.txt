26 August 2021 On 26 August 2021, the Vice Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Indonesia to Georgia, Yuddy Crisnandi. David Zalkaliani thanked the Indonesian Ambassador for his personal contribution to the development of relations between the two countries. The Minister noted with satisfaction the positive dynamics of relations between Georgia and Indonesia in both bilateral and multilateral formats. He positively assessed the economic cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Indonesia and stressed the importance of its further development. Yuddy Crisnandi, for his part, noted that the four-year-term as Ambassador has been very important for him personally and he will remain a close friend and supporter of Georgia in the future.
