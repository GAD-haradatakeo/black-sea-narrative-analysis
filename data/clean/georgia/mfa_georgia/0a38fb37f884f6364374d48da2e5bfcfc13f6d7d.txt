24 January 2018 19.12.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the delegation of the Open Government Partnership led by its Deputy Director Joe Powell. The sides discussed the priorities of the Georgian chairmanship of the Open Government Partnership. Special attention was paid to the fact that as OGP Co-Chair, Georgia will host the 5th OPG Gobal Summit in Tbilisi on 17-19 July. Documents Similar News
