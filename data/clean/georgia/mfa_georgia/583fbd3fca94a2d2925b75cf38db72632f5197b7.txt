20 May 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Federal Minister for Foreign Affairs of Germany Annalena Baerbock within the framework of the Council of Europe Ministerial Meeting. The sides highlighted the long-standing Georgia-Germany partnership and Germany’s important role in Georgia's democratic and economic development and European and Euro-Atlantic integration. At the meeting, the German Minister once again expressed her support for Georgia's European aspirations. The Ministers discussed the application made by Georgia and two other countries of the Associated Trio for EU membership and its follow-up procedures, stressing the need for continued support from the EU and Partner countries at this crucial time, when the Associated Trio, including Georgia is confronted with the challenges, including the difficult security environment in the region and the occupation of Georgian territories by Russia. Minister Annalena Baerbock reaffirmed Germany's support for the country's sovereignty and territorial integrity. During the talks on bilateral relations, the sides noted the excellent cooperation between Georgia and Germany and expressed their readiness for close coordination in the future.
