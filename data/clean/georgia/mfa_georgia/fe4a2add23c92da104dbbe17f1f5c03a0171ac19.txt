12 September 2024 On 12 September 2024, Georgia's First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, attended an event marking Turkmenistan’s Independence Day. In his speech, Darsalia congratulated the people of Turkmenistan on this important occasion and emphasized the strong and friendly relations between Georgia and Turkmenistan, as well as the promising opportunities for deeper cooperation. “Georgia and Turkmenistan have long shared a relationship built on friendship and partnership, founded on the principles of equality, mutual respect, and cooperation. Beyond our exemplary political relations, the warm and enduring ties between our peoples, developed over decades, deserve special recognition,” he stated. Darsalia also underscored the importance of continuing high-level visits between the two nations and highlighted the fruitful collaboration in areas such as trade, economic development, transport, transit, and cultural exchange. On behalf of the Georgian government and its people, Darsalia once again extended his heartfelt congratulations to Turkmenistan, wishing its government and citizens continued success, prosperity, and a joyous national day.
