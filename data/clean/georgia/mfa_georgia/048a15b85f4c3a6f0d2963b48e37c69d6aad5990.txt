27 October 2023 On 27 October 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of Japan to Georgia, Ishizuka Hideki. At the meeting, the sides discussed various topical issues of bilateral relations. Special attention was paid to the possibilities of using Georgia's stable investment potential. Georgia-Japan partnership in terms of connectivity and transit was also emphasized. The sides put special emphasis on the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries, which would add a positive momentum to the existing bilateral relations. For the strengthening of people-to-people contacts, the sides emphasized the importance of establishing direct flights between Georgia and Japan and of introducing a visa-free regime for Georgian citizens to travel to Japan.
