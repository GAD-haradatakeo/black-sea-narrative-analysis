16 January 2025 On 16 January 2025, Maka Botchorishvili, in her capacity as Minister of Foreign Affairs, will pay her first official visit to the Republic of Azerbaijan. As part of the visit, Maka Botchorishvili will hold meetings with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Jeyhun Bayramov, for discussions in both bilateral and extended formats. Following their meeting, the two foreign ministers will hold a joint press conference.
