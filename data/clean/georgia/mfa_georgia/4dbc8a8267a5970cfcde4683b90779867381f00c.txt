8 February 2019 08.02.2019, U.S. - “This is yet another clear message for those who still continue to support Russia’s illegal actions that the international community will never recognize or accept the recognition of Abkhazia and the Tskhinvali region as independent states” the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani said when commenting on the U.S. Congressman Francis Rooney-initiated Resolution condemning the decision by the Syrian Arab Republic to recognize the territories of Abkhazia and South Ossetia as independent of Georgia. The Minister attached paramount importance to the fact that the sovereignty of Georgia is supported not only by the U.S. but also by the entire civilized part of the world. According to David Zalkaliani, U.S.-Georgia partnership manifested not only in resolutions and many other documents but also in concrete actions will bring about important results in the economic, defence and security areas.
