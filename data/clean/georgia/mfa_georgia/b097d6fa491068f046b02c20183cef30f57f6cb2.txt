19 October 2023 On 19 October 2023, a meeting was held between the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, and the U.S. Ambassador to Georgia, Robin Dunnigan. The sides discussed a wide range of strategic partnership between the two countries and prospects for further development of cooperation in respective areas. Within the framework of the meeting, special attention was also focused on regional policy and security issues. Robin Dunnigan once again confirmed the United States’ unwavering support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the country’s foreign policy priorities.
