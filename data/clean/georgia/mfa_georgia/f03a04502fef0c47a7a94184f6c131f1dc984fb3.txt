30 January 2018 29.01.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meetng with European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia Toivo Klaar. Mikheil Janelidze spoke about Russia’s illegal and provocative steps taken towards occupation and actual annexation of Georgia’s regions. The sides discussed the ways to peacefully resolve the conflict as well as the process of the Geneva International Discussions. The Georgian Foreign Minister thanked the European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia for the EU’s support in terms of the peaceful resolution of the Russia-Georgia conflict.
