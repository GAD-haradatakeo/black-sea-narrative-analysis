16 December 2022 On 16 December 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with the members of the special committee created at the National Assembly of the Republic of Korea to support the city of Busan at the "World Expo 2030. The sides discussed current issues of bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Korea. Attention was focused on the increasing dynamics of high-level visits, as well as on the need to deepen trade and economic ties between the two countries. The deputy minister thanked the Korean side both for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, as well as for the assistance provided at different times.
