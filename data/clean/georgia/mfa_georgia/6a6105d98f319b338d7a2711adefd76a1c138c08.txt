16 November 2022 On 16 November 2022, the Deputy Foreign Minister Teimuraz Janjalia participated in the official opening ceremony of the Regional Institute for Security Studies (RISS). Speaking before the audience, the Deputy Minister expressed the hope that the Institute will contribute to the studies of regional and global security issues. The deputy minister spoke about the challenges facing the region and the European continent as a whole, the ongoing war in Ukraine and the Black Sea security issues. Teimuraz Janjalia also highlighted the threats Georgia is confronted with, paying special attention to the occupation of the Georgian territories by Russia and noting the importance of the international partners’ support and cooperation. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia and the Director of the Regional Institute for Security Studies, Natia Seskuria, signed a memorandum of cooperation between the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Regional Institute for Security Studies (RISS).
