10 January 2024 On 10 January 2024, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the United Arab Emirates to Georgia, Ahmed Taher Al Nuaimi. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides noted with satisfaction that relations between the countries are intensively developing in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Special accent was put on the exchange of high-level visits planned during the current year. The importance of further deepening the trade, economic and tourism relations was also emphasized.
