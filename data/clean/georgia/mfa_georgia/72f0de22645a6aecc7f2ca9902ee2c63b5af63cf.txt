31 August 2022 On 31 August 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Defense of Ireland, SimonCoveney, within the framework of the meeting of foreign ministers of the EU member states in Prague. The sides discussed various aspects of cooperation between Georgia and Ireland. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked his Irish colleague for supporting the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Georgia, as well as the country's European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Ireland's significant contribution to the EU Monitoring Mission was also highlighted. Discussions focused on the Associated Trio’s European aspirations and Ireland's support in this regard. The Ministers emphasized the fruitful Georgia-Ireland cooperation in bilateral and multilateral formats, the successful and close relations at the legislative level and the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries.
