15 March 2019 15.03.2019, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with representatives of the Iranian Parliament’s Group of Friends with Georgia led by the chair of the group Alireza Beygi. The sides discussed the issues on the agenda of Georgia-Iran bilateral relations and plans for the development of co-operation.
