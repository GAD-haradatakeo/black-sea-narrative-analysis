28 September 2022 On 28 September 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Armenia to Georgia, Ashot Smbatyan. The Minister wished success to the Armenian diplomat in his future work and emphasized the importance of good-neighborly relations between Georgia and Armenia. The sides discussed the growing dynamics of relations between the two countries and expressed the hope that despite the difficulties in the region, Georgia and Armenia will continue close cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Discussions focused on the current agenda of bilateral trade, economic, transit and transport cooperation between the two countries.
