31 August 2022 Within the framework of the informal so-called Gymnich meeting of the ministers of foreign affairs of the European Union, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with his Estonian colleague, Urmas Reinsalu. The sides discussed the traditionally close and friendly relations between Georgia and Estonia. Special attention was paid to Estonia's strong support for Georgia, both for the country's European integration, as well as for its sovereignty and territorial integrity. Ilia Darchiashvili spoke about the steps taken by the Georgian government towards fulfilling the European Commission’s recommendations and the association agenda. Discussions also focused on a wide range of cooperation between Georgia and Estonia. The minister congratulated Urma Reinsalu on his appointment to the position of minister and expressed the hope that the traditionally fruitful cooperation between the two countries will further deepen.
