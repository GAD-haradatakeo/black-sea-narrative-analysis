7 June 2018 7.06.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze hosted the Slovak delegation at the Information Centre on NATO and EU. The delegation was comprised of outstanding representatives of various sectors who arrived in Georgia within the framework of the project “Let's get to know each other better”. The visit was organized by Georgia’s honorary consult to the Slovak republic, Nodar Giorgadze. Welcoming the guests, the Georgian Foreign Minister thanked the honorary consul for this important undertaking. According to him, the programme envisages a number of important activities designed to highlight Georgia’s history, traditions, cultural heritage and tourism potential. Paying special attention to the importance of Slovakia’s firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path, Mikheil Janelidze said that the Slovak President’s visit to Georgia within the framework of celebrating the centenary of the first democratic republic of Georgia was yet another proof of the Slovak government’s firm political support. “I hope the visit of the Slovak delegation will give a fresh impetus to the development of a series of new projects in Georgia” – Mikheil Janelidze said.
