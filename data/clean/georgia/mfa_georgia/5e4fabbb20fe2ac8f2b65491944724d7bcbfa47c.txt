21 November 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a telephone conversation with the Director General for European Neighborhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations, Gert Jan Koopman. The sides once again positively assessed the European Commission's recommendation on granting Georgia a candidate status, noted its historical importance and expressed the hope that this process will culminate in the European Council’s respective decision at the end of the year. Discussions focused on the European Commission's enlargement report and the Georgian government’s ambitious reform agenda. Positive appraisal was given to the Georgian government’s close coordination with the European Commission, as well as to the high-level meetings held in Tbilisi and Brussels in 2023.
