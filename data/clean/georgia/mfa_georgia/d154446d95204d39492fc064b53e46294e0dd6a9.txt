24 May 2017 24.05.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili received Maltese Ambassador Natasha Meli Daudey (residing in Warsaw), who arrived in Georgia to present her credentials to the President of Georgia. During the meeting the sides discussed bilateral and multilateral issues on the agenda of co-operation and the possibilities to develop these relations even further. The Maltese Ambassador reaffirmed her support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, also for Georgia’s European aspirations. For his part, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili thanked the Ambassador for the support of Georgia during the Maltese Presidency of the EU.
