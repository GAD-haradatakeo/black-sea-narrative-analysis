21 October 2022 On 21 October 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held his first meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to Georgia, Salman Bin Abdulrahman Al Al-Sheikh. The Minister congratulated the Ambassador on the launch of his diplomatic mission in Georgia and wished him success in his future activities. The sides discussed the ways to deepen cooperation between the two countries, emphasizing the importance of exchanging high-level visits.
