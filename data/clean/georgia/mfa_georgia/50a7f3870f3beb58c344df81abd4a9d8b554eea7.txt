21 September 2022 On 20 September 2022, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimer Konstatinidi, participated in an online meeting with representatives of the Georgian diaspora: "Economy and business - Georgian diaspora for the development of the country". The deputy minister welcomed the participants of the event and talked about the role and importance of the diaspora in the development of the country's economy. The Director of the Department of International Economic Relations of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Noshrevan Lomtatidze spoke about macroeconomic data, investment climate, ratings of international organizations, as well as business opportunities of the sectors of energy, transport and logistics. Ketevan Chukhua, the head of the international department of Enterprise Georgia LEPL under the Ministry of Economy and Sustainable Development of Georgia made a presentation about the Agency's programmes on the promotion of export and entrepreneurship.
