14 May 2021 On May 16-17, 2021, the Vice Prime-Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia David Zalkaliani, is to pay an official visit to Kyiv, Ukraine, where the bilateral meetings with the representatives of the legislative, as well as the executive government will be held. Within the scope of the visit, the parties will discuss the Georgian-Ukrainian strategic partnership, various areas of cooperation and prospects for further deepening of relations. During his visit to Kyiv, the Head of the Foreign Office of Georgia is to participate in a joint meeting of the Foreign Ministers of three associated countries - Georgia, Moldova and Ukraine. The Ministers will discuss the issues, related to the further cooperation between the three countries in order to strengthen political dialogue, economic and sectoral integration with the EU, preparations for the forthcoming Eastern Partnership Summit, as well as the security environment and challenges in the region.
