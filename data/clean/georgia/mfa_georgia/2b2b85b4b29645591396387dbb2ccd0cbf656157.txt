6 October 2023 On 6 October 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with Manfred Profazi, Regional Director for the IOM Regional Office for South -Eastern Europe, Eastern Europe and Central Asia. During the meeting, the sides discussed the current cooperation between Georgia and the IOM. The First Deputy Minister thanked the IOM representative for fruitful cooperation. Special note was taken of the ongoing projects in Georgia that are implemented through the involvement of the IOM. The participants of the meeting discussed the issues related to the future plans and perspectives of the IOM's activities in Georgia.
