3 August 2018 03.08.2018, Tbilisi – At the Japanese side’s initiative, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia Tadaharu Uehara held a meeting with the newly-appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Davit Zalkaliani. The Japanese ambassador congratulated the Foreign Minister on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavours. David Zalkaliani, for his part, reaffirmed his readiness to further contribute to the development of co-operation with Japan. The sides spoke about the increasing dynamics of Georgian-Japan relations, in particular, in the trade and economic areas. According to them, the bilateral investment agreement to be signed between Georgia and Japan will further encourage co-operation in the aforesaid sectors. Discussions focused on the Georgian Government’s priorities, particularly in education and social affairs. The Minister thanked the Japanese side for its contribution to the stabile development of Georgia. Special mention was taken of the importance of Japan’s participation in Georgia’s infrastructural projects.
