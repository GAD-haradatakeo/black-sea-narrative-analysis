18 February 2025 On 18 February 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Ambassador of the Republic of Turkey to Georgia, Ali Kaan Orbay. During the meeting, both parties expressed satisfaction with the fruitful cooperation between Georgia and Turkey in various fields and reviewed prospects for further deepening their strategic partnership. The positive dynamics of high-level visits and meetings were underscored. The parties also welcomed the upcoming visit of Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs to Ankara, highlighting it as an important opportunity to discuss both the future prospects of bilateral cooperation and ongoing developments in the region.
