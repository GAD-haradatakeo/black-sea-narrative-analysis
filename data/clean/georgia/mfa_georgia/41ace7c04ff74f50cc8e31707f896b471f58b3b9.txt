7 August 2020 Today’s conference addresses the consequences of the aggression perpetrated on behalf of the Russian Federation against Georgia in 2008 – an act of aggression, which has become a challenge for peace and security of the entire Euro-Atlantic space” – This was the opening statement of the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, in his online address, as the Georgian Foreign Ministry hosted the Eastern Front Initiative of the Middle East Institute of the US. According to the statement of Zalkaliani, this event represents a significant step forward in terms of formulating a strategic outlook on the issue of security in the Black Sea region. In his address the Minister discussed extensively the reality that came about as a result of the 2008 war, underscoring that Georgia has, at this point, already been in the state of war for 12 years. As per the address of the Minister, the most apparent manifestation of the aforementioned statement is the creation of a new type of the Berlin Wall along the occupation line, with numerous instances of illicit activity perpetrated on behalf of the occupying forces, terrorising the local population of daily basis.
