25 April 2018 25.04.2018, New York – The Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Qatari State Minister for Foreign Affairs, Soltan bin Saad Al-Muraikhi, on 24 April. The sides discussed main issues relating to bilateral relations and prospects for economic co-operation. The Qatari side expressed its interest in important infrastructural projects. The importance of strengthening business contacts between the two countries was also emphasized. The sides noted that direct flights contribute to increasing the level of tourist flows and raising awareness about both countries.
