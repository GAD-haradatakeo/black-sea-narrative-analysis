15 April 2020 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia is deeply saddened by the reports of loss of life and widespread destruction caused by the severe tropical Cyclone Harold in the South Pacific, in early April. The powerful tropical cyclone is blamed not only for extensive material losses but also for the death toll in the Solomon Islands, the Republic of Vanuatu and the Republic of Fiji. It also wreaked heavy infrastructural damage on the kingdom of Tonga. Extending its sincere sympathy and condolences to the families of the disaster victims, the Ministry expresses its solidarity with the governments and nations of the aforesaid States.
