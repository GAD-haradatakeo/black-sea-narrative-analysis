18 January 2019 18.01.2019, Tbilisi – The President of Georgia, Salome Zourabichvili and the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held their first working meeting. They discussed important issues relating to Georgia’s foreign policy. Highlighting the president’s essential role in implementing the foreign policy of the country, the sides expressed the readiness for close co-operation in this regard. Talking points included the Georgian President’s upcoming international visits. Special attention was paid to the visit scheduled to take place in Brussels from 21 to 23 January.
