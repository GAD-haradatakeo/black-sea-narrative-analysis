18 September 2024 On 17 September 2024, the 64th session of the Council of National Coordinators of the Organization for Democracy and Economic Development (GUAM) member states was held via video conference. Georgia's National Coordinator for GUAM, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Teimuraz Janjalia, took part in the session, along with Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan, Yalchin Rafiyev, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of Moldova, Sergiu Mikhov, and Ukraine's Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yevhen Perebyinis. During the session, the participants discussed key issues on the organization's agenda, with a primary focus on the region's security challenges. Teimuraz Janjalia provided an in-depth briefing on the situation in Georgia's occupied territories, highlighting the severe humanitarian conditions and the blatant human rights violations by Russian occupying forces. He also addressed Russia’s ongoing aggression in Ukraine and its broader impact on regional and European security. In light of these challenges, the session emphasized the need to enhance cooperation among GUAM member states and advance joint regional projects.
