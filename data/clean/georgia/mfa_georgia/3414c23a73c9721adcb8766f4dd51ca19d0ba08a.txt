20 November 2017 20.11.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Ministry hosted a round table between the EU, UN and OSCE Co-chairs and representatives of civil society. The aim of the Round Table was to keep the civil society updated on the process of the Geneva International Discussions in order to ensure their active involvement in the peace processes. The Round Table was moderated by the Deputy Foreign Minister, head of the Georgian delegation to the Geneva International Discussions, David Dondua led the meeting from the Georgian side.
