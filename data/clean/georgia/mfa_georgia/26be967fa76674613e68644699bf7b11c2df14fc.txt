26 January 2018 26.01.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with Ambassador of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Nothern Ireland, Justin McKenzie Smith. The sides spoke about the important issues on the agenda of NATO-Georgia co-operation, as well as about Georgia’s peace policy and the ongoing processes in Georgia’s occupied regions. The Deputy Foreign Minister paid special attention to steps taken by Georgia towards the peaceful resolution of the conflict and the the Geneval International Discusions.
