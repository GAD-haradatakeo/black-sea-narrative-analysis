22 April 2020 The Government of Georgia continues the gradual repatriation of Georgian citizens from abroad, via flights organized by the Coordination Council. The following flights will be added by decision of the Coordination Council: 5.05 Athens-Tbilisi 7.05 Amsterdam-Tbilisi 9.05 Berlin-Tbilisi Flights bound to Georgia have already been performed from various cities of Europe, including from Warsaw, Berlin, Vienna, London, Athens, U.S., Larkana, Amsterdam, Beijing, Rome, Paris, Tel Aviv, etc, bringing home Georgian citizens sating in these cities, as well as in other places across the world. Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 18,857 citizens of Georgia in the host countries.
