3 October 2023 On 3 October the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the Minister of State at the Federal Foreign Office, Anna Lührmann who is visiting Georgia. The sides noted with satisfaction the high dynamics of bilateral relations and emphasized the close cooperation in many fields. Special attention was paid to the process of Georgia&#39;s accession to the European Union. The Deputy Minister provided comprehensive information to his German colleague about the implementation of the 12 recommendations set by the European Commission and focused on the progress that Georgia has achieved on the path of integration with the European Union. Teimuraz Janjalia emphasized the need to grant Georgia a candidate status in this important period for the country and emphasized the importance of further support from EU member states, including Germany. The deputy minister expressed the hope that Georgia&#39;s efforts will be properly evaluated. The German side once again confirmed its support for Georgia&#39;s European perspective and for the country&#39;s efforts to join the European Union. The sides emphasized the importance of the Black Sea connectivity and of the involvement of European countries, including Germany, in strategic projects. Discussions focused on the security situation in the occupied territories and on the challenges facing the region.
