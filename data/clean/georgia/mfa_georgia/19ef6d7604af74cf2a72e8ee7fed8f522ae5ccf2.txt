12 April 2022 On 12 April, 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia met with the representatives of the UN Organizations accredited to Georgia – UN Resident Coordinator, Sabine Machl, UNHCR Representative Kemlin Furley, UNICEF Representative Ghassan Khalil, UN Women Representative, Kaori Ishikawa, Head of WHO Country Office Georgia, Silviu Domente and UN FAO Programme Coordinator Javier Sanz Alvarez. The severe security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied regions of Georgia was discussed at the meeting, including Russia's illegal and provocative actions, which create a heavy burden for the conflict-affected people. Lasha Darsalia expressed gratitude to the UN and its Agencies for their work, which contributes to improvement of the dire conditions of the conflict-affected people in the occupied territories.
