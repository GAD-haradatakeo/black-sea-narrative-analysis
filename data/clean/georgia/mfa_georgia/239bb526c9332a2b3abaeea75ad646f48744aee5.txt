5 December 2025 On the sidelines of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, Georgia’s Minister for Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with her Serbian counterpart, Marko Đurić. The Foreign Ministers positively assessed the dynamics of bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Serbia. Attention was also drawn to the prospects for deepening economic and trade relations between the two countries. The importance of conducting high-level visits was underscored. The Ministers reaffirmed their mutual respect for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and Serbia. The meeting also included an exchange of views on current issues on the international agenda, the challenges encountered on the path toward European integration, and possible ways to address them.
