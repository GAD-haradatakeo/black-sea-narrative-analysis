2 June 2021 From 1 to 4 June, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with theco-rapporteurs of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe for the monitoring of Georgia, Titus Corlatean and Claude Kern. The goal of the visit is to assess the implementation of the obligations taken by the Georgian side in terms of the existing political climate, undergoing democratic reforms and other issues associated with the responsibilities taken by Georgia when joining the organisation. During the meeting the discussion touched upon the issues on the agenda of the bilateral relations between the aforementioned organisation and Georgia, underscoring the significance of cooperation within the framework of the European Assembly.
