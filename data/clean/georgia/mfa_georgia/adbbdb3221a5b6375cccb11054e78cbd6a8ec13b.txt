30 April 2022 The sides discussed the centuries-old friendly relations and cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Armenia. Reaffirming the readiness to deepen relations between the two countries in various fields, the sides highlighted the paramount importance of cooperation between the Parliament of Georgia and the National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia. Special attention was paid to the importance of the visit of the Chairman of the Parliament of Georgia, Shalva Papuashvili to Yerevan. The partnership and friendship between the Heads of States of the two countries was emphasized. Speaking about the security challenges facing the world and the region, the sides stressed the importance of peace in the South Caucasus. The President of the National Assembly reaffirmed Armenia’s unwavering support for the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Georgia.
