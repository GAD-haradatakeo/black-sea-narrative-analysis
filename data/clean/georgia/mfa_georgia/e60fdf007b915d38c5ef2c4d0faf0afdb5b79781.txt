24 May 2017 24.05.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili received Portuguese Ambassador Paula Leal da Silva (residing in Ankara), who arrived in Georgia to present her credentials to the President of Georgia. During the meeting the sides discussed bilateral and multilateral issues on the agenda of co-operation and the possibilities to develop these relations even further. The Portuguese Ambassador reaffirmed her unflagging support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, also for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic Integration. For his part, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili emphasized the importance of bilateral political consultations held with Portugal in April, this year. The sides also discussed upcoming high-level visits. The deputy minister thanked the Portuguese side for condemning the so-called presidential election and referendum conducted on 9April 2017 in the occupied Tskhinvali region.
