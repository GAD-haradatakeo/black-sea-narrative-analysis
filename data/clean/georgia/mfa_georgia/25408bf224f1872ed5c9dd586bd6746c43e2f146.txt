10 May 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of State at the German Federal Foreign Office, Tobias Lindner. The sides discussed important issues relating to cooperation between the two countries in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Special attention was paid to Georgia's European integration. Discussions focused on the progress made by the country in this regard. Germany's support for Georgia's European integration was reaffirmed.
