24 September 2019 On the sidelines of the 74th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Estonian counterpart, MinisterforForeign Affairs UrmasReinsalu. Zalkaliani thanked the Estonian Minister for his Government’s firm support to Georgia, including for the Estonian President’s recent statements condemning the occupation of Georgian territories and declaring support for Georgia’s European integration. The Ministers discussed the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, including the ongoing illegal borderization, closure of checkpoints, increasing number of detentions of civilians and other destructive activities of the occupation regimes. According to them, the situatin calls for active involvement of all parties concerned and the effective use of all available international formats to de-escalate the conflict and achieve security on the ground.
