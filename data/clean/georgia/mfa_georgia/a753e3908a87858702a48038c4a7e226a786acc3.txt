7 July 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal Republic of Germany to Georgia, Hubert Knirsch. The aim of the meeting was to discuss details of the vaccination process in Georgia and Germany, recognition of vaccination certificates and the ways to assist students wishing to benefit from student employment programmes, in line with COVID regulations applying in Germany.
