29 December 2024 The annual meeting of Georgian diplomatic mission heads, the Ambassadors’ Conference, continues in a working format. Accredited diplomats to various countries and international organizations received a detailed briefing on the regional and global security environment and challenges from Levan Nasrashvili, Head of the Analytical Directorate of Georgia's Intelligence Service. Discussions also addressed administrative and budgetary matters critical for the smooth operation of Georgian embassies. The Ambassadors’ Conference, opened by Minister of Foreign Affairs Maka Botchorishvili, is now in its second day. The event brings together heads of Georgia’s diplomatic missions abroad and leaders of the Ministry’s central apparatus to review past activities, outline future plans, and discuss strategic priorities alongside representatives of the executive and legislative branches of government.
