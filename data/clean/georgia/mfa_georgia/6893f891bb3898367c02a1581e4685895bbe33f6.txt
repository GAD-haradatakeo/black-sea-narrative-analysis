2 December 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey, Hakan Fidan, congratulated Maka Botchorishvili on her appointment as the head of Georgia’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs. "I am confident that close relations between our countries will further deepen during your tenure. As strategic partners, I trust that we will continue to promote peace, stability, and prosperity, as well as economic cooperation in our region," wrote Hakan Fidan in his letter.
