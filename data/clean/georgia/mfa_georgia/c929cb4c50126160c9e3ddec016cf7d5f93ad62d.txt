28 May 2022 Thematic meetings - "Georgian Language Conference" and "Georgian Diaspora for the Development of the Country" were held within the framework of the forum dedicated to the Diaspora Day - "Georgian Diaspora for the European Future of Georgia". At the panel dedicated to the Georgian language, which was held in a hybrid mode, special attention was paid to the issues related to the teaching of the Georgian language in Sunday schools abroad. At the meeting, Sunday School teachers had the opportunity to express their views and recommendations so that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia could plan and implement future activities necessary for Sunday schools and tailored to their needs. At the thematic panel "Georgian Diaspora for the Development of the Country", the speakers discussed Georgia’s business and investment environment, national and international business support programs. The panel also focused on the discussion of the Deep and Comprehensive Free Trade Agreement (DCFTA) with the European Union, in particular, on the benefits to be derived by the citizens of Georgia.
