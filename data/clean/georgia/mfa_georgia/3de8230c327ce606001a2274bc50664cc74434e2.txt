19 April 2023 On 19 April 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a farewell meeting with the representative of UNICEF in Georgia, Ghassan Khalil. The sides focused on cooperation between Georgia and the United Nations Children's Fund were discussed. The minister thanked Ghassan Khalil for the activities carried out by UNICEF in Georgia in various directions, including education, health and social protection. For his part, the UNICEF representative thanked the minister for the fruitful cooperation and outlined the possibilities of its further development.
