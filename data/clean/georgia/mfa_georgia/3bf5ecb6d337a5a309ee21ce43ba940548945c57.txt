9 October 2023 On 9 October 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held his first meeting with the Ambassador of the Czech Republic to Georgia, Petr Kubernát. The sides highly appraised the Georgian-Czech relations, which have significantly deepened across the broad spectrum of cooperation over the recent years. Special attention was focused on high-level visits exchanged between the two countries at the executive and legislative levels. The sides highlighted Georgia's foreign policy priorities and the Czech Republic's firm support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Discussions also focused on the implementation of the European Commission's 12 recommendations by Georgia. The Minister provided the Ambassador with comprehensive information about the steps taken by the country and the results achieved in this regard. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed the hope that Georgia’s efforts will be duly evaluated and the country will be granted a candidate status.
