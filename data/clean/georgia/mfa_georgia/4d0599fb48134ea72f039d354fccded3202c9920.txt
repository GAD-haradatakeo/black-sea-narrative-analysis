11 June 2023 On 11 June 2023, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Education and Sports of Liechtenstein, Dominique Hasler who is visiting Georgia, arrived at the village of Odzisi near the occupation line. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, and the representative of the State Security Service informed the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Liechtenstein about the difficult humanitarian situation on the ground. Lasha Darsalia focused on the importance of the international community’s support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Education and Sports of Liechtenstein, Dominique Hasler , is visiting Georgia from 11to 13 June.
