20 June 2019 19.06.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani participated in the Midsummer Day – the National Holiday of Sweden marked by the Swedish Embassy in Georgia. Congratulating the Swedish people and the government on this holiday, David Zalkaliani highlighted the partner relations between Georgia and Sweden. He singled out for special mention the role of Sweden as one of the founders of Eastern Partnership Initiative in the European integration process. In his address David Zalkaliani also referred to his telephone conversation with his Swedish counterpart where the two ministers reaffirmed the close partner relations between the two countries and their intention to further strengthen these relations. Zalkaliani expressed his gratitude for Sweden’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for active participation of Swedish monitors in the EUMM.
