4 April 2023 On 4 April 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Head of the Council of Europe Office in Georgia, Natalia Voutova. During the meeting, the sides once again emphasized the close cooperation between Georgia and the Council of Europe and discussed issues on the current agenda, as well as future plans. Special attention was paid to the projects that were successfully implemented within the framework of the action plan with the Council of Europe. Discussions focused on the current situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. In this context, the sides particularly noted the importance of the consolidated reports of the Secretary General of the Council of Europe.
