15 November 2021 “The invitation of Georgia, along with Ukraine, to Riga Summit is another recognition of tangible progress of Georgia and Ukraine on the path of NATO integration” – the Georgian Vice Prime Minister/Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani told journalists. Before that, according to the Minister, within the framework of the 2018 NATO Brussels Summit, Georgia participated for the first time in the Meeting of the North Atlantic Council (NAC) with Georgia and Ukraine at the level of Heads of State and Government. Afterwards, Georgia and Ukraine were invited to other ministerial meetings as well. “As for the invitation to the NAC meeting in December, it is a very positive and serious message from our NATO Partners” – Zalkaliani said. He expressed the hope that the Allies will once again support Georgia’s initiatives, including the renewed Substantial Package, NATO-Georgia cooperation in the light of Black Sea security and other practical instruments.
