24 September 2024 Starting from 30 September 2024, Georgian citizens will be able to travel to the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of China without a visa. According to the decision by the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, Georgian citizens can visit Hong Kong without a visa and stay for up to 30 consecutive days. This agreement on visa-free travel between Georgia and Hong Kong was achieved through active diplomatic efforts. It is worth noting that visa-free travel for Georgian citizens to the People's Republic of China has been in effect since 28 May 2024.
