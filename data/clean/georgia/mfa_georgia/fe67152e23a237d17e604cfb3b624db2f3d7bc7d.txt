26 May 2018 26.05.2018, Tbilisi, - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the Vice Prime Minister for Romania's Strategic Partnerships' Implementation Ana Birchall, who participates in the celebrations of the 100th anniversary of the first democratic republic of Georgia. The sides exchanged their views over the increasing level of bilateral relations between Georgia and Romania manifested in exchange of high-level visits, sectoral co-operation and successful interaction within the framework of international organizations. Mikheil Janelidze thanked his Romanian colleague for the Romanian government’s strong support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. The Romanian Vice Prime Minister, for his part, underlined the importance of the issues relating to Georgia and the Eastern Partnership during Romania’s EU Presidency from January 2019. In parallel to political relations, the sides highlighted the importance of sectoral co-operation, as well as the possibilities of carrying out joint transport, infrastructure and energy projects.
