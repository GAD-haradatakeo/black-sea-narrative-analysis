16 February 2019 16.02.2019, Munich - Georgia and the Republic of North Macedonia signed a protocol on the establishment of diplomatic and consular relations. The Protocol was signed by the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani and the Minister of the Republic of North Macedonia Nikola Dimitrov, within the framework of the Munich Security Conference. We are sure that through the joint efforts we will develop our relations and provide benefits for each other. Availing myself of this opportunity I would like to congratulate my Macedonian colleague on this historic decision regarding the new name of the country and on the signature of the accession of the Republic of North Macedonia to NATO that is yet another proof that NATO’s Open Door Policy is in action and the door of the Alliance is open to the countries that share NATO’s values and standards.
