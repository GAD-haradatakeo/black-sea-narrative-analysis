30 January 2019 30.01.2019, Tbilisi - The Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Acting U.S. Ambassador to Georgia, Ross Wilson. The sides discussed issues relating to bilateral co-operation and prospects for deepening strategic partnership. Discussions focused on U.S.-Georgia co-operation in the areas of defence and security, as well as trade and economy. Talking points included issues relating to regional security, including in the Black Sea context. The role of Georgia as the United States strategic partner and ally in the region. The sides expressed their satisfaction at the high-level of communication between the countries and expressed the hope that events scheduled for 2019 will contribute to the further expansion of strategic partnership.
