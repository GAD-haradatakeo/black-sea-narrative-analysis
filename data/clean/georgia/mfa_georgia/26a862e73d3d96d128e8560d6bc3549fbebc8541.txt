Statement of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in reaction to the earthquake that hit the Islamic Republic of Iran and the Republic of Iraq 13 November 2017 13.11.2017 - The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the devastation caused by the powerful earthquake that struck the Islamic Republic of Iran and the Republic of Iraq on November 13, 2017 killing more than 200 and injuring thousands of people.
