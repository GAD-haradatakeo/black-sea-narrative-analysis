21 July 2025 On 22 July 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Jeyhun Bayramov, will pay an official visit to Georgia. Within the framework of the visit, Minister Bayramov will meet with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili. The Azerbaijani Foreign Minister is also scheduled to hold meetings with the President of Georgia, Mikheil Kavelashvili; Prime Minister, Irakli Kobakhidze; and the Chairman of the Parliament, Shalva Papuashvili. During the visit, Minister Bayramov will lay a wreath at the memorial dedicated to the heroes who died for the unity of Georgia and will also pay tribute to the monument of the national leader of Azerbaijan, Heydar Aliyev. On 21 July 2025, a new round of political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Azerbaijan is being held in Tbilisi at the level of deputy ministers.
