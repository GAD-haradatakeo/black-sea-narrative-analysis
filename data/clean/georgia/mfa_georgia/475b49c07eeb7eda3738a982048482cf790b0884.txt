13 September 2018 13.09.2010 Tbilisi - Ambassador of the United Arab Emirates to Georgia, Isa Abdullah Abdulrahman Alnoyimi presented copies of his credentials to the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Davit Zalkaliani. At the meeting following the credentials ceremony, the Minister of Foreign Affairs positively assessed the decision on the opening of the Embassy of the United Arab Emirates in Georgia. It is noteworthy that Abdullah Abdulrahman Alnoyimi is the first resident ambassador of the United Arab Emirates in Georgia. The sides discussed a full range of issues on the agenda of relations between Georgia and the Emirates underlining the importance of strengthening links in the political, trade-economic, tourism and other fields. The sides agreed to further develop cooperation. It was noted that the opening of the Embassy of the United Arab Emirates in Georgia will significantly facilitate the strengthening of relations between the two countries and will activate the inter-community contacts.
