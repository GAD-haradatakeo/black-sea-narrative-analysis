16 October 2025 On 16 October 2025, Irakli Kurashvili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the French Republic and Permanent Representative of Georgia to UNESCO, presented his credentials to the Director-General of UNESCO, Audrey Azoulay. Following the credential ceremony, a meeting was held to discuss the ongoing cooperation between Georgia and UNESCO, as well as prospects for its further strengthening. Special attention was devoted to the areas encompassed by the Organization’s mandate - education, science, culture, sports, and communication. During the meeting, the Director-General expressed appreciation to Georgia for its active engagement in UNESCO’s work and for its steadfast commitment to the Organization’s goals and values.
