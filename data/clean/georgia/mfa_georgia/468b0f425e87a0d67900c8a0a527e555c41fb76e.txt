1 July 2018 01.07.2018, Berlin – The Minister highlighted the importance of the visit to Germany and Germany’s role in the effective implementation of the foreign policy goals of Georgia. David Zalkaliani spoke about the NATO Summit in Brussels and Georgia’s expectations in this regard. The Minister brought to the attention of the embassy officials the EU integration roadmap developed by Georgian Government and envisaging Georgia’s greater institutional, sectoral and physical rapprochement with the EU standards. Discussions also focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and Georgia’s peace policy, including the initiative “A step toward a better future”. David Zalkaliani also spoke about the Georgian Prime Minister’s new programme prioritizing Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration, rapid modernization and economic development, including through the use of digital technologies, development of education system and a small government concept.
