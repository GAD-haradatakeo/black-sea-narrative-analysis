21 May 2020 20.05.2020 – David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with his Moldovan counterpart, the Minister of Foreign Affairs andEuropean Integration,Oleg Țulea. The Minister ofForeign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with his Moldovan counterpart, the Minister of Foreign Affairs andEuropean Integration,Oleg Țulea. The two Ministers discussed issues relating to Georgia’s and Moldova’s EU integration and the need to further deepen cooperation with the EU. The parties paid special attention to Eastern Partnership as the strategic and ambitious initiative, exchanging their views over its priorities and expectations. The Ministers also spoke about the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, the occupation force’s illegal and destructive activities and the need of the international community’s active involvement. Discussions also focused on the need to fight against the pandemic, steps taken by the two governments.
