1 December 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, has taken on her duties. Today, the Minister held a working meeting with the ministry's senior officials to discuss priorities of future activities. As Maka Botchorishvili emphasized, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will continue to pursue constitutionally defined objectives and protect national interests through unified efforts. The Minister highlighted the Ministry's crucial role in addressing the challenges facing the state. She also focused on the ongoing campaign of pressure against diplomats, describing it as unacceptable and intolerable. The Minister expressed her gratitude to all employees who understand the responsibilities of a civil servant and stand firm in protecting national interests.
