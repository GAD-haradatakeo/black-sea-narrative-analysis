5 April 2021 At a briefing held on 5 April, the Deputy Foreign Minister Vladimer Konstantinidi announced the launch of the grant programme “Support for Diaspora Initiatives”. The aim of the programme is to popularize Georgia internationally, to carry out measures aimed at supporting the country’s European integration and upholding its national interests and to strengthen the sense of national identity and cultural uniqueness, including by raising awareness of Georgia among the young generation and deepening cultural and economic relations with the host countries. The grant programme will allow Georgian citizens living abroad to put into practice their ideas and to establish stronger ties with their fellow compatriots both in Georgia and beyond its borders.
