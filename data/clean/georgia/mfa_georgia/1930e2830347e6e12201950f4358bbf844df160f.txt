12 September 2018 12.09.2010 Tbilisi - Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Davit Zalkaliani held a meeting with US Assistant Secretary of State for European and Eurasian Affairs Wess Mitchell who paid a visit to Georgia. The sides discussed main directions of ongoing cooperation within the framework of strategic partnership between the two countries. Discussions also touched upon the planned visit details of the Prime Minister of Georgia to the United States. The US side confirmed its strong support for Georgia's territorial integrity and sovereignty, as well as for the country's European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. The sides also reviewed the ongoing processes in the region, including security challenges. Discussions focused on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the US involvement in conflict resolution issues.
