13 September 2018 13.09.2018 Tbilisi - Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Davit Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Secretary General of the OSCE Thomas Greminger, on his official visit to Georgia. The sides discussed issues relating to deepening cooperation between OSCE and Georgia and talked about future prospects. Special attention was paid to peaceful settlement of the Russia-Georgia conflict and to increasing the OSCE engagement and role in this respect. The sides expressed their support for the Geneva International Discussions and the format of incident prevention and response mechanism meetings. During the meeting the sides emphasized the active and fruitful cooperation of Georgia with OSCE independent institutions. Special note was taken of the involvement of the OSCE Democratic Institutions and Human Rights Office in the process of democratization of the country. The importance of participation of ODIHR'sobservationmission in the presidential elections scheduled for October was emphasized as well.
