1 October 2021 Within the framework of his visit to the Republic of Korea, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with the Chairman of the Korea-Georgia Parliamentary Friendship Group at the National Assembly of Korea, Lee Won-wook. The sides welcomed the establishment of friendship groups at the parliaments of the two countries, which play an important role in strengthening and further developing ties between Georgia and Korea. The meeting focused on the opportunities to increase inter-parliamentary cooperation. High appraisal was given to the bilateral meeting between the chairman of the Parliament of Georgia and the Speaker of the National Assembly of the Republic of Korea within the 5th World Conference of Speakers of Parliament in Vienna, from 7 to 9 September 2021.
