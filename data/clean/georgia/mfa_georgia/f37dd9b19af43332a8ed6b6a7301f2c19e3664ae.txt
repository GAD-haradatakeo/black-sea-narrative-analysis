7 November 2024 On 7 November 2024, within the framework of the European Political Community Summit, Georgian Foreign Minister Ilia Darchiashvili, together with his Hungarian counterpart Péter Szijjártó, signed "Action Plan on Knowledge Sharing in the Process of EU Membership between the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia." According to the document, the Hungarian Diplomatic Academy will offer a seminar to Georgian public officials, providing Georgia’s representatives with comprehensive information on Hungary's experience in the process of EU membership.
