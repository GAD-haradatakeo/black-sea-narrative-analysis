11 May 2021 On 11 May 2021, the 4th round of online political consultations was held between the Department of International Relations and Cooperation of South Africa (DIRCO) and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia at the level of Directors of Departments (the previous round was held in Pretoria in September 2018, before the outbreak of the pandemic). The Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili delivered opening remarks before the participants of the online consultations. The sides discussed various issues relating to bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and South Africa and the ways to further deepen this relationship amid the pandemic. The delegations of the two countries exchanged their views on the increasing dynamic of positive cooperation in the areas of economy, education, sport and culture. They also assessed the existing contractual framework on trade, culture, sport and consular issues. Special attention was paid to the need to raise South African businessmen’s awareness of the investment and business climate in Georgia.
