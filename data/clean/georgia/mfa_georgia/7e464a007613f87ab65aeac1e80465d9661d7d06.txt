23 October 2021 On 22 October, within the framework of his visit to the Kingdom of Spain, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimer Konstantinidi together with representatives of the International Agency for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD) met with the Director of the Valencia Regional Technical Agency, Antonio Rodi. Discussions focused on the projects implemented and planned by the Agency in the field of digitalization, which aim to incorporate modern technologies into innovations and regional development. Within the framework of the visit, the Georgian delegation also held a meeting with Professor Juan Lopez, the Head of International Relations and Projects at theUniversity of Alicante. The sides discussed issues of deepening cooperation between the University and the Levan Mikeladze Diplomatic Institute at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The University of Alicante admits students from different countries every year, including from Georgia. The Georgian language corner has been functioning there since 2017. Within the framework of the working visit to Spain, the Georgian delegation also took part in the presentation of Georgian wine to Spanish entrepreneurs, who were introduced to the tradition of Georgian winemaking and the ways to increase export of Georgian wine products to the international market.
