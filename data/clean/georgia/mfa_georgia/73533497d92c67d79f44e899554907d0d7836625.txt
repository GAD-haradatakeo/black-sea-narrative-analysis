17 May 2018 17.05.2018, Astana - Within the framework of his official visit to the Republic of Kazakhstan, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participated in the Astana economic forum - Global challenges summit 2018. The economic forum opened by President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev is dedicated to the issues relating tp global development, digital economy, modern and future technologies and the challenges facing the international community. The forum brought together speakers from different countries, including former UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon, Former President of France François Hollande, Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak, futurist Michio Kaku, Jim O'Neill, the British financier and the former Chief Economist of Goldman Sachs, others are expected to participate in it.
