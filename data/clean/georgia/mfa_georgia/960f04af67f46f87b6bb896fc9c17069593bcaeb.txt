27 October 2017 27.10.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participated in the reception dedicated to Austria’s Independence Day where he together with Austrian Ambassador Arad Benkö spoke before the attending audience. The Minister congratulated the Austrian people on the 25th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Austria highlighting the importance of opening up the Embassy of the Republic of Austria in Georgia. The Minister thanked the Austrian Government for its support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. He underlined Austria’s efforts for peaceful resolution of the conflict. Mikheil Janelidze also spoke about the visit of the Austrian Federal Minister for European and International Affairs Sebastian Kurz to Georgia that can be seen as a clear demonstration of support to and solidarity with Georgia.
