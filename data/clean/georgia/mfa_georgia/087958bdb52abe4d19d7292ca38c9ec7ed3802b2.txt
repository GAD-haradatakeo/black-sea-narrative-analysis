2 March 2026 On 2 March 2026, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Hideki Ishizuka. During the meeting, the parties focused on the Japanese side’s interest in the Middle Corridor and Georgia’s significant role in facilitating the transit of cargoes along this route. Particular attention was devoted to the positive dynamics of bilateral and multilateral relations between Georgia and Japan with the necessity of exchanging high-level visits underscored. The meeting also addressed Georgia’s participation in the high-level international event scheduled to take place in Yokohama in 2027- the International Horticultural EXPO 2027. At the conclusion of the meeting, the parties expressed their hope that the results-oriented cooperation between Georgia and Japan will continue to advance successfully in the future.
