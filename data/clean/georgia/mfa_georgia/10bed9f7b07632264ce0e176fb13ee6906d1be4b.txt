8 May 2025 On 8 May 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Ishizuka Hideki. During the meeting, the parties discussed a broad range of issues related to bilateral relations and multilateral cooperation. Both sides expressed satisfaction with the positive dynamics of Georgian-Japanese cooperation. Special emphasis was placed on identifying avenues for future cooperation in the areas of political dialogue, trade and economic relations, tourism, cultural exchange, and people-to-people contacts. The discussion also addressed ongoing developments and challenges in the region. Ambassador Ishizuka reaffirmed Japan’s unwavering support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders.
