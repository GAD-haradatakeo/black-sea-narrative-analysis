21 April 2023 On 21 April 2023, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia held a meeting with the Deputy Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Sejm of the Republic of Poland, Pawel Kowal, representatives of the Warsaw Security Forum and of the Polish Kazimir Pulawski Foundation. During the meeting, the Deputy Minister reviewed Georgia's foreign policy priorities and informed the Polish delegation about the work carried out towards EU integration, including important progress on the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations. Discussions also focused on Georgia's integration into NATO and the priorities of our country in this regard. Emphasis was placed on the security environment in the region, which has become even more difficult in the light of Russia's aggression in Ukraine. Teimuraz Janjalia spoke about the difficult security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and emphasized the special importance of the consolidated support of partners.
