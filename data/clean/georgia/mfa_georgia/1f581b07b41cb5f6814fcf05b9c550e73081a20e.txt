9 February 2019 07.02.2019, U.S. - Within the framework of his visit to the United States, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the U.S. Special Representative for Afghanistan Reconciliation, Zalmay Khalilzad. Discussions focused on Georgia’s active efforts aimed at ensuring stability in Afghanistan. The Special Representative thanked Georgia for its important contribution to NATO’s peace operations and for its political support for the peaceful development of Afghanistan. David Zalkaliani reaffirmed that Georgia as a member of the civilized world condemns terrorism in any form and supports the international community’s efforts on Afghanistan, as well as the ongoing peacemaking process. The sides underlined the importance of co-operation with Afghanistan in terms of developing international transport corridors and welcomed the implementation of Lapis Lazuli Corridor connecting Europe with Asia and contributing to the economic development of the countries involved in this projects, including Georgia and Afghanistan.
