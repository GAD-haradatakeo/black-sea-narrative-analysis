28 May 2021 On 27 May, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili participated in the conference of Euroactiv (EU media network). The conference titled“Georgia’s Path to the EU – Next Steps”was held on the Day of Independence and was dedicated to Georgia’s European integration. Along with Vakhtang Makharoblishvili, the conference was attended by Lawrence Meredith, Director for Neighbourhood East & Institution Building at the European Commission, Marina Kaljurand, a Member of the European Parliament (MEP) and the Chair of theEU-​GeorgiaParliamentaryAssociationCommittee,LucDevigne, DeputyManaging Directorfor Europe andCentral Asia of theEuropeanExternal ActionService and Michael Emerson, Associate Senior Research Fellow at the Centre for European Policy Studies (CEPS).
