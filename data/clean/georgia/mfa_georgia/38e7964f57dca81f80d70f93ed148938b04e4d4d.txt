6 October 2025 On 6 October 2025, the 76th Session of the UNHCR Executive Committee commenced in Geneva and will continue until 10 October. The Georgian delegation is participating in the event under the leadership of the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia. During the general debate held on 6 October, the Deputy Minister addressed the Committee, focusing on the growing global challenge of forced displacement and the need for the international community to consolidate efforts to effectively address it. He also emphasized the close cooperation between the Government of Georgia, UNHCR, and other international organizations, which has enabled the country to respond to the humanitarian needs of people affected by conflict over the past years. Special attention was drawn to the situation of internally displaced persons (IDPs) from the occupied territories of Georgia and to the Government’s continued efforts on the international stage aimed at ensuring their safe and dignified return to their homes. In the traditional segment dedicated to reactions to national statements, the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees, Filippo Grandi, commended the steps taken by the Government of Georgia to protect the rights of refugees and internally displaced persons. He also expressed deep appreciation for Georgia’s hospitality and assistance to Ukrainians displaced by the war. At the same time, the High Commissioner voiced concern over the stagnation of the return process of displaced persons to Abkhazia and the Tskhinvali region, while reaffirming UNHCR’s readiness to continue its engagement and efforts in this regard.
