4 December 2017 4.12.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze will participate in the international parliamentary conference: EU and Partners – Embracing Diversity for Stronger Unity on 4 and 5 December. The conference aims to discuss integration with the European Union as a framework for promoting internal reforms, pointing out the benefits and challenges associated with the EU-inspired transformation. Georgian Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili and Parliamentary Chairperson Irakli Kobakhidze, Vice President of the European Parliament Ryszard Czarnecki, EU Ambassador to Georgia Janos Herman UNDP Resident Representative to Georgia Niels Scott will appear before the conference with opening remarks. The conference will bring together representatives of the parliaments of the European Union Member States, Candidate States and Eastern Partnership countries, members of the European Parliament, Georgian Government, civil society and international organizations.
