26 November 2017 26.11.2017, Tbilisi – Prime Minister of Ukraine Volodymyr Groysman paid an official visit to Georgia. The official delegation of Ukraine was met by Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze at the Tbilisi Shota Rustaveli International Airport. Mikheil Janelidze thanked Volodymyr Groysman for his visit to Georgia and participation in the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum. The Ukrainian Prime Minister will be hosted by his Georgian counterpart, Giorgi kvirikashvili. A face-to-face meeting between the two prime ministers will be followed by a meeting in an extended format. From 26 to 28 November Volodymyr Groysman will hold meetings with the President of Georgia, Speaker of the Parliament and the Tbilisi Mayor. The Prime Minister of Ukraine will participate the Silk Road Forum in Tbilisi. He will visit the rehabilitation center of the Ministry of Defense in Tserovani and will open a monument to Lesya Ukrainka in Kakheti. He will also visit the Aleksandre Chavchavadze Museum in Tsinandali. Within the framework of his visit, Volodymyr Groysman will lay a wreath upon the memorial of the fallen heroes.
