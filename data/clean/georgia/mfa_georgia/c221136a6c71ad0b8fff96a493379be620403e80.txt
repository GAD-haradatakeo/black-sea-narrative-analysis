25 October 2018 On 25 October 2018, representatives of the Georgian Foreign Ministry held a meeting with members of the delegation of Denmark’s European Movement who arrived for their first visit in Georgia. Representatives of the Georgian Foreign Ministry updated the Danish guests on Georgia’s reforms on the European and Euro-Atlantic integration path and the human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. Within the framework of the visit the Danish delegation will visit the occupation line and will hold meetings within the Parliament of Georgia and other government agencies. Documents Similar News
