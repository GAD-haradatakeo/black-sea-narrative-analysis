25 September 2025 On 23 September, at the Sándor Palace, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to Hungary, Nikoloz Laliashvili, presented his Letters of Credence to the President of Hungary, Tamás Sulyok. Within the framework of the official ceremony, a meeting was held between the Ambassador of Georgia and the President of Hungary, during which the parties emphasized the friendly relations and strategic partnership established between the two countries and discussed prospects for further deepening bilateral cooperation. President Tamás Sulyok reaffirmed Hungary’s steadfast support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for Georgia’s European integration. The Georgian Ambassador expressed his gratitude to the President of Hungary for the warm reception. President Sulyok extended his best wishes for a successful diplomatic mission to Ambassador Laliashvili. Following the meeting, Ambassador Nikoloz Laliashvili made an entry in the guestbook of the Presidential Palace.
