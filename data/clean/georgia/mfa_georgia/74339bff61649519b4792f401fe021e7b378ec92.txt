20 March 2020 Online consultations on coronavirus will be launched for Georgian citizens living abroad. Consultations will be jointly organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministryof IDPs from theOccupied Territories, Labour, Health and Social Affairs of Georgia and the National Public Health Centre, by decision of the Prime Minister-led Inter-Agency Coordination Council. Infectiousdiseasephysicians will use Facebook live to answer questions of Georgian citizens in the countries where their concentration is the highest. The first such online consultations will be conducted today, on 20 March, for Georgian citizens in Italy, at 17:00 Italian time, via the embassy FB page: https://www.facebook.com/ambasciatadigeorgia/ Georgian citizen living in Italy should sent their questions to: [email protected] .
