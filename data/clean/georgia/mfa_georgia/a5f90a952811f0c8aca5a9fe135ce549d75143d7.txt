3 December 2022 Today, within the framework of the 29th OSCE Ministerial Council, the OSCE Group of Friends of Georgia delivered a statement reaffirming their full support for the country's sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognised borders. The Group condemned Russia’s aggression against Georgia in 2008 and expressed their deep concern over Russia’s ongoing military occupation of the country's Abkhazia and Tskhinvali (South Ossetia) regions.
