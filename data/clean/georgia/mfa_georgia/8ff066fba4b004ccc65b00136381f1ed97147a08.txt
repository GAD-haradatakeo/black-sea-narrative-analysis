19 February 2018 19.02.2018, Tbilisi – “We must strengthen our efforts to ensure that international organizations and partners increase their pressure on Russia and bring it to comply with its commitments” - the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze said at the joint meeting of the three parliamentary committees. Mikheil Janelidze spoke in detail about the steps taken by the Foreign Ministry towards the restoration of Georgia’s territorial integrity and de-occupation, as well as about unprecedented results achieved in this direction. According to the Minister, Georgia continues constructive participation in the Geneva International Discussions and pursues the non-recognition policy trying at the same time to prevent Russia from using its private contacts to achieve illegal recognition of occupation regimes”.
