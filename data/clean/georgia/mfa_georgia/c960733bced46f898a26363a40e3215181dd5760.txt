Georgia-U.S. relationship is today at an all-time high – Zalkaliani 17 October 2019 17.10.2019, Tbilisi – According to David Zalkaliani, Georgia-U.S. relationship is today at an all-time high and Georgia enjoys the bipartisan support of the U.S. executive and legislative authorities. He highlighted the importance of the Joint Declaration marking the 10th anniversary of the U.S. –Georgia Strategic Partnership Charter, which defines all priority areas of cooperation between the two countries.
