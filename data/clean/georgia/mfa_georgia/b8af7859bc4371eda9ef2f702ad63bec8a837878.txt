26 October 2018 26.10.2018, Tbilisi - the Georgian Foreign Minister, Davit Zalkaliani held a meeting with the United States National Security AdvisorJohn Bolton. The sides discussed priority issues of bilateral co-operation, including prospects for the deepening ofstrategic partnership. Special attention was paid to the aspects of trade-economic, defence and security co-operation. Discussions focused on the security challenges in Georgia and in the region. David Zalkaliani brought to the attention of John Bolton the difficult humanitarian and human rights situation in the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. John Bolton reaffirmed the United States and Trump’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity and the Euro-Atlantic integration. Special accents were placed on the peace and stability in the South Caucasus Region, as well as on the region’s importance in the geo-political context. Speaking about the regional security climate, the sides emphasized the increasing role of the United States as an outstanding partner and an ally in the region. The role of Georgia in the context of ensuring the Black Sea region’s security was also highlighted. Special attention was paid to Georgia’s contribution towards strengthening global security.
