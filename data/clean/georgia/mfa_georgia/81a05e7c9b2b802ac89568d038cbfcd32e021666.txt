16 February 2022 During the meeting, the sides exchanged information on the security environment in the region, developments around Ukraine and the ways to strengthen peace and stability in the region. They reiterated support for Ukraine. David Zalkaliani thanked the U.S. Government for its active communication with Georgia in the light of the ongoing processes. The U.S. side reaffirmed that Washington continues to coordinate closely with Tbilisi within the scope of the security dialogue with Russia, remains committed to supporting Georgia's sovereignty, territorial integrity, and European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations.
