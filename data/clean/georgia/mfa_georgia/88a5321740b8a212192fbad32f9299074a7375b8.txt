6 March 2025 On 5 March 2025, non-resident ambassadors accredited to Georgia visited the village of Odzisi, located near the occupation line. During the visit, the diplomats assessed the current situation on the ground and received detailed information regarding the conditions in the occupied territories, as well as the severe humanitarian challenges faced by the local population. The delegation included ambassadors from the Kingdom of Belgium, the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, the Republic of Cyprus, the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Republic of Tajikistan, the Republic of Guatemala, the Republic of the Philippines, the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the Federal Republic of Somalia, the Republic of Burundi, and the Republic of Mali. Prior to their visit to the occupation line, the ambassadors presented copies of their Letters of Credence to the Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs, Giorgi Zurabashvili and Khatuna Totladze.
