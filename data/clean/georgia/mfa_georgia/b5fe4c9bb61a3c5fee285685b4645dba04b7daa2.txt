1 February 2021 The First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvilipresented introduced a report for 2020 on fulfillment of the Association Agreement commitments by the Ministry. Speaking before the EU Integration Committee of the Parliament of Georgia, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili highlighted the steps taken during 2020 by the Ministry as the agency leading and coordinating the European integration process. He also spoke the progress in implementing the commitments under the EU-Georgia Association Agreement and Association Agenda.
