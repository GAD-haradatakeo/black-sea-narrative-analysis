Lasha Darsalia has met with the Senior Advisor to the US Secretary of State 19 May 2022 On 19 May, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia met with the Senior Advisor to the U.S. Secretary of State, Derek Scholes, who arrived in Georgia on 18 May together with representatives from various U.S. agencies, including USAID, Departments of State and Defence. The sides discussed issues relating to U.S.-Georgia strategic partnership and the difficult security environment in the region in the wake of Russian aggression in Ukraine. See All
