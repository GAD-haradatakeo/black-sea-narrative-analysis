30 June 2022 Within the framework of the NATO Summit in Madrid, Ilia Darchiashvili has met with the Deputy Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, Wopke Hoekstra. The conversation focused on the successful cooperation between the two countries in both bilateral and multilateral formats and the potential for further development of partner relations. Special attention was paid to Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. The sides welcomed the Council’s decision to grant Georgia a European perspective. The steps taken by Georgia towards EU membership and the positive dynamics of reforms were also highlighted. The ministers highly appraised the decisions made at the Madrid summit, including the support expressed by the Alliance to Georgia, which will contribute to Georgia's progress on the NATO integration path. It was noted that Georgia is a strong partner for NATO, which is well demonstrated by Georgia's active participation in international peacekeeping missions and in ensuring international security, in general. The meeting focused on the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia.
