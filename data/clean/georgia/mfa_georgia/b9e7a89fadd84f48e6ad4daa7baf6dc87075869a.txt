7 October 2021 On 7 October 2021, the Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia held his first meeting with the South Caucasus Regional Director of HALO Trust, Fiona Kilpatrick Cooper. The sides discussed Halo Trust’s activities in Georgia, including ongoing projects in the occupied Abkhazia region. The security situation in the South Caucasus region was also discussed. the representative of the Halo Trust spoke about the organization’s activities across the region.
