3 May 2023 On 3 May 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Speaker of the Israeli Knesset, Amir Ohana. The sides discussed the historically close friendship between Georgia and Israel and the prospects for the further development of partnership. They also talked about the ways to deepen parliamentary relations and cooperation in various fields. Special mention was made of the role and importance of parliamentary diplomacy in terms of development of multidisciplinary cooperation between the two countries.
