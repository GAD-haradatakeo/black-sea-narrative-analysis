23 June 2021 Within the framework of his visit to Brussels, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with Didier Reynders, the European Commissioner for Justice. The Minister presented the Commissioner with detailed information regarding the gross violations of human rights in Georgia’s occupied territories, including the illegal arrest and incarceration of Zaza Gakheladze, underscoring the significance of the engagement of the EU, as well as other international partners, in terms of the release of Georgian citizens. “I discussed in detail this issue during my meeting today, asking our EU partners to elevate this issue on their bilateral agenda with the Russian Federation. Tomorrow, during the meeting with Josep Borrell I will also bring up this issue, along with other meetings including those with MEPs, making the best of this visit in order to bring to the forefront the issue of Zaza Gakheladze” – stated David Zalkaliani.
