19 May 2017 19.05.2017, Nicosia - Within the framework of the 127th Session of the CoE Committee of Ministers, Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with his Latvian counterpart, Foreign Minister Edgars Rinkēvičs. The two ministers spoke about important issues relating to bilateral co-operation paying special attention to Georgia’s European integration agenda and Georgia’s priorities in this regard. The sides highlighted Georgia’s reforms, democratic development and steps taken towards EU integration. Discussions also focused on Georgia’s expectations for the Eastern Partnership summit scheduled to take place in Brussels in November.
