17 October 2018 15.10.2018, Luxembourg - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Ambassador of the European Union to Georgia, CarlHartzell. Highly appraising the increasing level of Georgia-EU co-operation, the sides paid special attention to upcoming events due to be conducted within the margins of bilateral co-operation. The Minister positively assessed the Eastern Partnership Ministerial meeting held in Luxembourg on 15 October 2018 and highlighted the need to identify goals of Eastern Partnership Policy. Discussions also focused on the upcomingEU–Georgia Strategic Security Dialogue meeting scheduled for 23 October and the high-level dialogue with the European Union to be chaired by the President of the European Commission and the Prime Minister of Georgia that will be conducted for the first time in Brussels, on 21 November.
