25 February 2020 25.02.2020, Geneva – The Chair of the Committeee of Ministers of the Council of Europe, Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with the CoE Secretary General, Marija Pejčinović Burić. Describing in detail priorities of the Georgian Presidency, the Georgian Foreign Minister highlighted the importance of upcoming events to be conducted under the aegis of the Georgian Presidency. Special attention was focused on the upcoming conference “Environmental Protection and Human Rights“ – Georgia’s innovative priority that met with great approval by the organization, as well as its members. According to participants of the meeting, Georgia’s priorities held resolve the challenges facing the organization.
