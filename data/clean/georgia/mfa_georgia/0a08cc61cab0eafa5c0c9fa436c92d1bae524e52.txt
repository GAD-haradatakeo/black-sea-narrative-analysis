29 June 2024 The Diaspora Forum - "Strong Diaspora in the Service of the Motherland" continues. On June 28, 2024, at the event, children from the diaspora who won the competition announced as part of Mother Language Day were awarded. The competition, which included three components: an essay, a calligraphic work, and a drawing, saw 13 winners selected. An exhibition showcasing the participants' works was also held within the framework of the Diaspora Forum. The forum dedicated to the Day of the Georgian Diaspora is held annually with the aim of deepening ties and cooperation between the state and the diaspora, discussing existing challenges and opportunities, and enhancing the role of diaspora representatives in the development of the country.
