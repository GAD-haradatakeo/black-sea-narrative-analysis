25 December 2025 On 25 December 2025, within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference, a working session entitled “Key Issues in the Implementation of Foreign Policy Priorities” was held. The panel discussion focused on the key directions and objectives of Georgia’s foreign policy. The session was opened by the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili. The discussion focused on the complex geopolitical environment at the international and regional levels, global security challenges and their implications for Georgia’s foreign policy agenda. Particular emphasis was placed on the importance of a consistent and pragmatic foreign policy aimed at upholding national interests, maintaining peace and stability, deepening cooperation with partner countries, and strengthening Georgia’s international standing. The working session on foreign policy priority areas, held within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference, was moderated by the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Gia Zurabashvili.
