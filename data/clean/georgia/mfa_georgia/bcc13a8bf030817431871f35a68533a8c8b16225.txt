14 December 2025 Maka Botchorishvili participated in an event held to mark the inscription of “Georgian Wheat Culture: Traditions and Rituals” on UNESCO’s Representative List of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity. She addressed the audience and congratulated the Georgian people on UNESCO’s recognition of Georgian wheat culture as part of the Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity. According to the Minister, UNESCO’s recognition serves as a clear evidence that the international community values and acknowledges Georgia’s contribution to the development of humanity. Maka Botchorishvili extended her appreciation to all those who contributed to the preparation of the nomination and to the recognition process that resulted in this significant achievement for Georgia. It is noteworthy that the event was attended by the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze, President Mikheil Kavelashvili, Mayor of Tbilisi Kakha Kaladze, members of the Cabinet of Ministers and Parliament, as well as representatives of the Patriarchate.
