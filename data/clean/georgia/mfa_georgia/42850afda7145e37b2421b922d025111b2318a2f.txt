25 July 2022 The grant competitions announced under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia’s programs—“Be Your Country’s Young Ambassador,” “Support for Diaspora Initiatives,” and “Support for Georgian Dance and Song Ensembles Abroad”—have concluded. During the selection of successful candidates, the competition commission, composed of representatives from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, the Administration of the President of Georgia, the Parliament’s Committee on Diaspora and Caucasus Issues, the Ministry of Education and Science, the Government of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia, the Youth Agency (a legal entity under public law), and the International Centre for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD), placed particular emphasis on the relevance and scope of the projects and action plans submitted, the candidates’ motivation, their experience in diaspora-related activities, and their knowledge of the programs, as well as of Georgia and the host country. To this end, the winners of the programs will carry out a diverse range of cultural, creative, sports, educational, and other activities throughout the year in their countries of residence.
