Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili has met with Members of the House of Representatives of Japan 23 December 2025 On 23 December 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with members of the House of Representatives of Japan, Karen Makishima, and Japan’s Parliamentary Vice-Minister for Digital Transformation, Hideto Kawazaki, who were on an official visit to Tbilisi.
