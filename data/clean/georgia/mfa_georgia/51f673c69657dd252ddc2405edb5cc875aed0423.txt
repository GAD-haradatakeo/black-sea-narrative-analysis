18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the newly appointed Ambassador of the Kyrgyz Republic to Georgia, Maksat Mamytkanov (resident in Baku). During the meeting, the parties discussed a broad spectrum of issues pertaining to bilateral relations, including cooperation in the fields of trade and economy, transport, and tourism. It was noted that Kyrgyzstan is one of Georgia’s significant partners in terms of exports. The sides also exchanged views on the development of joint projects across various sectors and on the sharing of expertise and experience. With a view to further deepening bilateral relations, the importance of exchanging high-level visits was underscored. Ambassador Mamitkanov conveyed to Minister Botchorishvili an invitation from the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kyrgyz Republic, Jeenbek Kulubayev.
