18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Zurabashvili, met with the Ambassador of Montenegro to Georgia, Borjanka Simečević. The parties discussed bilateral relations, shared priorities in the field of European integration, the reform agenda, and the need to further strengthen cooperation. They expressed satisfaction with the friendly ties between Georgia and Montenegro and underlined the significant potential for developing cooperation in specific areas, particularly in trade and economic relations. The First Deputy Minister thanked the Ambassador for Montenegro’s firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the country’s Euro-Atlantic aspirations. Giorgi Zurabashvili wished Ambassador Simečević success in her future endeavors. Prior to the meeting with the First Deputy Minister, the newly appointed Ambassador of Montenegro presented copies of her credentials to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Directorate of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia.
