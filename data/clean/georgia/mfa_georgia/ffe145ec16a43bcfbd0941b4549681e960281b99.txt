9 October 2021 On October 8, the 4th thematic group (Information society; audiovisual and media services; science and technologies; education, training and youth; culture, sport and physical activity) of the Georgia-EU Association Subcommittee on Economic and Sectoral Cooperation convened its 6th Session - chaired from the Georgian side by Davit Bujiashvili, Director of the EU Assistance Coordination and Sectoral Integration Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and from the EU side - by Emma Modoran, Head of the Georgia-Moldova Relations Group at the European External Action Service. The sides discussed Georgia's efforts towards EU integration in the field of education, science, culture, sports, youth, audiovisual policy, information society and technologies.
