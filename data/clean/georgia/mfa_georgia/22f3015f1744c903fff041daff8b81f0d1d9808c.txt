25 June 2018 25.06.2018 – On 25 June, at the Armenian side’s initiative, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with his Armenian counterpart, Foreign Minister Zohrab Mnatsakanian. Congratulating his Georgian colleague on the appointment as Foreign Minister, Zohrab Mnatsakanian wished David Zalkaliani success in his future activities. Talking points also included prospects for future co-operation. The two Ministers reaffirmed their willingness to further contribute to the development of good-neighbourly relations between the two countries. Special note, in this context, was taken of the recent official visit of Prime Minister Nikol Pashinyan to Georgia, which gave an added impetus to the improvement of relations between Georgia and Armenia.
