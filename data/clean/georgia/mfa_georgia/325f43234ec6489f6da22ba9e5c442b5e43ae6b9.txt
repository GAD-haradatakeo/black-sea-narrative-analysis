19 January 2021 Georgia’s candidate Zurab Pololikashvili was elected to the position of Secretary General of the World Tourism Organization (UNWTO) for the second term (2022-2025). The 113th session of the Executive Council of the Organization was held on 19 January, in Madrid. The newly-elected Secretary General will have to be ratified in the second half of 2021, by the General Assembly. The 113th session of the UNWTO Executive Council was attended by the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, Chair of the Sector Economy and Economic Policy Committee of the Georgian Parliament, David Songulashvili and other members of the Georgian delegation.
