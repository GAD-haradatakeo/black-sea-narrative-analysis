25 December 2020 On 25 December 2020, the Georgian Foreign Ministry and the Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University have signed a memorandum on mutual cooperation, with the dignitaries being David Zalkaliani and Giorgi Sharvashidze respectively, along with the director of the Scientific Research Institute of the International Law department of the Faculty of Law, Irine Kurdadze. The memorandum entails further strengthening of the existing cooperation between the Ministry and the Institute, increasing the involvement of the latter in the process of legal assessment and insurance of Georgia’s foreign policy and international relations.
