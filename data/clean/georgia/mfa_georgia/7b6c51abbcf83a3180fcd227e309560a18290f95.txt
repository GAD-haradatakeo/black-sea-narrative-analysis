19 February 2018 13.02.2018, Kuwait - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze has announced that his body will have a new structure to further enhance Georgia-EU ties. The minister stated that the name of the structure will be the General Directorate for European Integration. Janelidze explained that the new structure will unite some departments of the foreign ministry and former departments of the Ministry for European and Euro-Atlantic Integration, which was merged the Foreign Ministry last year. Janelidze stated that the structure will bring together professionals to further enhance Georgia-EU positive relations.
