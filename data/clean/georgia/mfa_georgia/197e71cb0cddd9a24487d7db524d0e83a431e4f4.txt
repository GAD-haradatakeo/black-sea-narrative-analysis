3 November 2023 During his working visit to Riga, on 3 November 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili met with his Latvian colleague, Krišjānis Kariņš. At the meeting, the sides expressed their satisfaction with the visit of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, which is being held for the first time after a few years&#39; pause, and also coincides with a remarkable date - the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Latvia. The ministers reviewed current issues of cooperation between the countries and emphasized the importance of strengthening political dialogue and sectoral cooperation between Latvia and Georgia. Special attention was paid to the agenda of Georgia&#39;s integration into the European Union. The Georgian Foreign Minister informed his Latvian colleague about the progress achieved in the implementation of the European Commission’s 12 recommendations and emphasized the importance of the member states’ support for granting the candidate status to Georgia. Latvia once again expressed its support for Georgia&#39;s European future. Special note was taken of strong friendship and reliable partnership between Latvia and Georgia, which was repeatedly expressed by Latvia in support of the country&#39;s territorial integrity and sovereignty, as well as for its European integration. The sides reviewed the security environment in the region and the existing challenges. The sides highlighted the importance of high-level visits, activation of political consultations and close coordination between the two countries.
