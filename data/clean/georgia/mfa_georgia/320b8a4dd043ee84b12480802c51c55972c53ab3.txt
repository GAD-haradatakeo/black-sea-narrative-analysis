31 October 2025 Within the framework of the UNESCO General Conference, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with her Azerbaijani counterpart, Jeyhun Bayramov. The ministers discussed the strategic partnership between Georgia and Azerbaijan and explored opportunities for further strengthening their close relations. The positive dynamics of high-level exchanges between the two countries were noted with satisfaction. Particular attention was devoted to the progress achieved in trade and economic cooperation between Georgia and Azerbaijan. In discussing regional developments, both sides emphasized the importance of establishing lasting peace and stability in the South Caucasus, noting that stability and security in the region are essential preconditions for economic development and prosperity. The parties also reviewed cooperation between the two countries in the context of developing the Middle Corridor. The Ministers of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Azerbaijan reaffirmed their mutual respect for each other’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and expressed readiness to further deepen political dialogue and cooperation.
