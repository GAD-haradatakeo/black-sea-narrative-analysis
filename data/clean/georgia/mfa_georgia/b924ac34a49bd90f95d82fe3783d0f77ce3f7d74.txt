12 December 2022 During his visit to Brussels, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia met with the High Representative of the European Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy, Josep Borrell. The sides positively evaluated the EU-Eastern Partnership ministerial meeting as a good opportunity to discuss the challenges and opportunities of cooperation within the mentioned format. At the meeting with Joseph Borel, special attention was paid to Georgia’s efforts on the path of European integration. The Minister provided the High Representative with detailed information about the progress achieved in terms of implementing the European Commission’s recommendations. The sides highly appraised the political decision to give Georgia a European perspective and the High Representative’s special role in the positive dynamics of the relationship between Georgia and the European Union. The sides discussed the priority areas of cooperation with the European Union, including the importance of economic and sectoral integration with the European Union, especially in the transport, energy and digital fields. Discussions also focused on the security challenges and the EUMM’s positive role in the process of maintaining peace and stability on the ground.
