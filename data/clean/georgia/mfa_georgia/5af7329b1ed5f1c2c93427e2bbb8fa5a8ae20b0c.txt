3 December 2021 Within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani held his first meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Canada, Mélanie Joly. The ministers underlined the traditional friendship and partnership between the two countries. David Zalkaliani thanked his Canadian counterpart for her support of Georgia, including for her position regarding our country's sovereignty and territorial integrity within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council.
