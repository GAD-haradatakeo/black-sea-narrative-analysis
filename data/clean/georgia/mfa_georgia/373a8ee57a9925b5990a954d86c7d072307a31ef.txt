3 February 2021 On 2 February 2021, the Georgian First Deputy Foreign MinisterVakhtang Makharoblishvili held a meeting withthe U.S. Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary toGeorgia, Kelly Degnan. The parties discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral relations between Georgia and the United States. Talking points also included meetings to be held within the framework of the U.S.-Georgia Charter on Strategic Partnership, bilateral meetings to be scheduled with the new U.S. administration and the communication agenda. The parties reaffirmed the new U.S. administration’s commitment to work towards fulfilment of strategic partnership goals. The Parties focused their attention on the Georgian Vice-Prime Minister and Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani’s letter to the Secretary of State Antony Blinken where David Zalkaliani sets forth his visions of the further development of Georgia’s strategic partnership with the U.S. Talking points also included the security situation and ongoing processes in the region.
