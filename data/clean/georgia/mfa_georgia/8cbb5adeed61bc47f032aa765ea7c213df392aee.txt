17 February 2022 On 17 February, in Brussels, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia met with the Director-General of the Directorate-General for European Neighbourhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations, Maciej Popowski. The sides discussed the implementation of the Georgia-EU Association Agreement and Georgia's progress in harmonizing the Georgian legislation with that of the EU in all sectors. Discussions also focused on the EU Economy and Investment Plan and infrastructure projects to be implemented in Georgia. Under the plan, the European Commission plans to mobilize up to 4 billion euros for the implementation of projects. Particular attention was paid to physical connectivity with the EU, including to transport and energy projects, which should bring Georgia even closer to the EU. The Georgian side reiterated its commitment to fulfill its obligations under the Association Agreement, while the European Commission pledged financial and technical support in the reform process.
