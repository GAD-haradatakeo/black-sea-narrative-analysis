23 September 2022 Ilia Darchiashvili and Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu discussed the current agenda of strategic partnership between Georgia and Türkiye, the high-level visits between the two countries and their results. The Ministers also reviewed the cooperation between Georgia and Türkiye in international formats and the issue of mutual support. Discussions focused on the successful regional formats of cooperation and the need to intensify efforts in this regard. Ilia Darchiashvili and Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu exchanged views on the current security environment and emphasized the importance of peace and stability for the sustainable development of the region. Turkey's strong support for Georgia was also highlighted. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked his colleague for Turkey's support during the fire extinguishing works in Borjomi valley.
