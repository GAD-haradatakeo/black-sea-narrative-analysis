2 May 2023 On 2 May 2023, within the framework of his official visit to the State of Israel, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the President of Israel, Isaac Herzog. The meeting focused on important issues of Georgia-Israel bilateral and multilateral cooperation. The sides took special note of the traditionally friendly relations between the countries. At the end of the meeting, the sides once again confirmed their readiness to continue close cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats.
