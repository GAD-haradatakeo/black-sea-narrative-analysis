28 June 2021 Within the framework of the Global Coalition Ministerial held in Rome the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held an introductory meeting with his Finnish counterpart, Pekka Haavisto. During the meeting the two Ministers expressed satisfaction over the existing friendly relations between the two states, expressing readiness to actively cooperate in terms of deepening ties and development. David Zalkaliani presented his colleague with information regarding the existing situation on the ground in the occupied regions of Georgia, noting the significance of active engagement of the international community in order to mitigate the dire outcomes of the Russo-Georgian conflict and peacefully resolve it.
