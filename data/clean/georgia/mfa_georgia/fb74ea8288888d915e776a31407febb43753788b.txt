22 October 2025 On 22 October 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with Péter Sztáray, State Secretary at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary. The Deputy Minister welcomed the participation of the Hungarian delegation in the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum, taking place in the Georgian capital, and highlighted the close cooperation between Georgia and Hungary. He expressed gratitude for Hungary’s steadfast support for Georgia, including both political and expert-level assistance in the country’s European integration process. For his part, the State Secretary reaffirmed Hungary’s continued support for Georgia’s priorities and commitment to further strengthening bilateral relations. The discussion emphasized the ongoing high-level dialogue between the two countries, as well as recent and upcoming reciprocal visits. The Deputy Minister provided an overview of the difficult security and humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied regions and underlined the crucial importance of the support extended by international partners.
