21 March 2020 Georgian citizens living in Spain will be provided with online medical consultations 21.03.2020 - Online consultations on coronavirus are underway for Georgian citizens living abroad. Consultations were jointly organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs,the Ministryof IDPs from theOccupied Territories,Labour, Health and Social Affairs of Georgia and the National Public Health Centre,by decision of the Prime Minister-led Inter-Agency Coordination Council. Today, online medical consultations will be provided for Georgian citizens living in Spain, at 17:00, Madrid time, via the embassy FB page: https://www.facebook.com/embajadageo/ Interested citizens should post their questions at: [email protected] will be answered by specialists of the National Disease Control Centre.
