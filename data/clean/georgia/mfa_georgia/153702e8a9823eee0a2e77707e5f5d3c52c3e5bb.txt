9 June 2022 On 9 June 2022, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Azerbaijan to Georgia, Faig Guliyev. Discussing current issues on the agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Azerbaijan the sides stressed the importance of exchanging high-level visits. They expressed their readiness to take appropriate measures to mark the 30th anniversary of diplomatic relations between the two countries.
