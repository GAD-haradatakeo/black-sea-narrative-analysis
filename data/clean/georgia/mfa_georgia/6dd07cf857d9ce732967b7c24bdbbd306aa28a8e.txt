26 September 2023 On 26 September, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vladimir Konstantinidi met with the Chairman of the State Committee on Religious Associations of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Mubariz Gurbanli, who arrived for an official visit inTbilisi. At the meeting, the sides emphasized the good-neighbourly relations and strategic partnership between the two countries and the possibilities for further deepening the regional cooperation. The sides expressed their satisfaction with the high-level visits and the growing dynamics of ties between the countries. The sides also welcomed the close people-to-people contacts between Azerbaijan and Georgia, successful cooperation on diaspora issues and culture. The Azerbaijani side also noted the successful cooperation with the State Agency for Religious Affairs of Georgia. Discussions focused on the importance of peace and stability in the region.
