"We hope that the issues related to Open Door Policy will be adequately reflected in the new strategic Concept, which will clearly formulate the Alliance’s specific strategy for the Black Sea region" - David Zalkaliani 19 November 2021 "The Georgian side welcomes the decision made by the Alliance at the Brussels Summit to update the NATO Strategic Concept, which will be approved at the 2022 Alliance Summit. "We hope that the issues related to Open Door Policy will be adequately reflected in the new strategic concept which will clearly formulate the Alliance’s specific strategy for the Black Sea region," Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani said at the Parliament’s plenary session “Minister's Hour” on 19 November.
