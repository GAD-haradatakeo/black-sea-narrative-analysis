18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Chile, Mr. Eliseo Rodrigo Ruiz Ortiz (with residence in Warsaw), presented copies of his credentials to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Directorate of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. Following the credential presentation ceremony, a meeting was held with Kakhaber Sikharulidze, Director of the Global Issues Directorate of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The parties discussed matters of cooperation between Georgia and Chile in both bilateral and multilateral formats, underlining the existing friendly relations. Particular emphasis was placed on the successful cooperation between the two countries within international organizations and the importance of continuing mutual support.
