27 September 2019 27.09.2019, New York – On the sidelines of the 74th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Paraguayan Foreign Minister AntonioRivas Palacios. The Ministers signed Agreement on Visa-Free Travel for Holders of Ordinary Passports of Georgia and Paraguay. According to the Ministers, the Agreement will promote the development of people-to-people contacts, tourism and business between the two countries. The sides also expressed close interest in further developing trade-conomic contacts and agricultural cooperation.
