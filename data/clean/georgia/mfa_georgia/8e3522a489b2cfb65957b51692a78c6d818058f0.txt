15 November 2022 Another round of political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Armenia was held in Tbilisi on 14 November 2022. The political consultations were co-chaired by the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia and the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia, Mnatsakan Safarian. At the meeting, the sides discussed the positive agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Armenia, current opportunities to deepen trade, economic, transit and transport, cultural and people-to-people ties. Special attention was drawn to the security environment in the region and the sides emphasized the importance of stability for sustainable and peaceful development.
