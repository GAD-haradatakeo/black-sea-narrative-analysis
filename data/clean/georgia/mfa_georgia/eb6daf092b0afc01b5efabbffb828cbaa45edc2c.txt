7 May 2025 On 7 May 2025, Archil Dzuliashvili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia (with residence in Amman), presented his Letters of Credence to the President of the Republic of Lebanon, Joseph Aoun. During the meeting, the two parties reviewed the agenda of bilateral relations between Georgia and the Republic of Lebanon, as well as avenues for cooperation within international frameworks. The discussion also touched upon prospects for enhancing relations across various fields. President Aoun wished the Ambassador success in his future endeavors.
