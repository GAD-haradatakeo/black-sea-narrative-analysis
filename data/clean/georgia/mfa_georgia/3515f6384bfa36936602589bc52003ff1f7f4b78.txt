9 October 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, received the Ambassador of the State of Israel to Georgia, Hadas Meitzad. The sides discussed the ongoing developments in Israel. Ilia Darchiashvili confirmed to the Israeli diplomat Georgia's strong support for the State of Israel, once again condemned the terrorist attack against Israel and conveyed his condolences.
