23 September 2019 23.09.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Alexandre Khvtisiashvili met with the Secretary General of the Council of Europe Congress of Local and Regional Authorities AndreasKiefer. The sides discussed important issues relating to cooperation between Georgia and the Congress highlighting the fact that 2019 marks the 70th aaniversary of foundation of the Council of Europe, 25th anniversary of the Congress and 20th anniversary of Georgia’s membersip in the CoE. Discussions also focused on Georgia’s forthcoming chairmanship of the CoE Committee of Ministers, as well as on cooperation with the Congress within the framework of this chairmanship. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister updated the CoE representatives on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and Russia’s illegal activities there. Khvtisiashvili underscored the need to grant international monitoring organizations an access to the occupied regions and to keep the issue of Georgia high on the international agenda. The Deputy Minister thanked the CoE Congress delegation for their statements regarding the illegal activities in Georgia’s occupied regions. The sides agreed to continue active cooperation.
