25 November 2022 The 60thMeetingof theGUAM Council of National Coordinators(CNC) was held on 25 November 2022, via videoconference. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, GUAM National coordinator, Teimuraz Janjalia participated in the meeting. The parties spoke about the need to strengthen regional cooperation, which, against the background of the ongoing war in Ukraine, is gaining an increasingly greater importance. The participants of the session highlighted the significance of coordinated interaction between the GUAM member states within the framework of international organizations. The parties summarized the activities carried out within the framework of Moldova's chairmanship and noted with satisfaction that, despite the existing challenges, several important agreements were signed within the ministerial meeting held in New York in September 2022. Special attention was paid to the implementation of regional projects in areas of common interest, including transport and connectivity. At the end of the meeting, Teimuraz Janjalia thanked Moldova for its fruitful chairmanship and wished success to Ukraine, which will take over the GUAM chairmanship in 2023. Documents Similar News
