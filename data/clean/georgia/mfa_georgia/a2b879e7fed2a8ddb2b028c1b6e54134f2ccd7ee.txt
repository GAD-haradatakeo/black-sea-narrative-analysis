23 January 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Slovak Republic to Georgia, Matúš Bušovský. The parties discussed the close cooperation between the two countries and the current issues on the bilateral agenda. The meeting highlighted the dynamics of Georgian-Slovak relations, including the importance of high-level visits and political dialogue. The conversation also touched upon sectoral cooperation and the achievements in trade and economic relations. Emphasis was placed on Georgia’s European integration.
