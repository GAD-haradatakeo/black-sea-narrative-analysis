19 January 2021 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, participated in a reception held in the name of the King of Spain dedicated to the heads of delegations of the member-states of the executive council of the UN World Tourism Organisation, during which he held a meeting with the King, Felipe VI. The King of Spain expressed interest in the region and Georgia in particular, with the Minister presenting his majesty with information regarding the existing geo-political circumstances and the general security environment. The particular importance of Georgia’s European aspirations was noted, with the Spanish King being notified of the progress that the country has achieved along the path of Euro-integration, aspiring to become a full-fledged member of the European family. David Zalkaliani expressed gratitude towards Felipe VI for the unconditional and staunch support that Georgia receives from Spain.
