17 December 2022 On 16 December 2022, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted an event dedicated to promoting the export of agricultural products, as part of economic diplomacy. The event was held in cooperation with the Georgian Rural Development Agency. Participants of the meeting discussed the possibilities of increasing sales of Georgian products on foreign markets and the ways for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs to support Georgian exports. Representatives of export promoting associations operating in Georgia, the Rural Development Agency and about 30 exporting companies benefiting from the USAID agriculture program participated in the meeting. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, the First Deputy Minister of Environment Protection and Agriculture, Giorgi Khanishvili, and the Head of the USAID Agriculture Program, Luiza Namicheishvili, gave a welcome speech to the producers of agricultural export products.
