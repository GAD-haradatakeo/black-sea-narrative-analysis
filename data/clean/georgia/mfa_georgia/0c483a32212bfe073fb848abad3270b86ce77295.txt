3 May 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Ilia Darchiashvili received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United Kingdom to Georgia, Mark Clayton. The British diplomat congratulated Ilia Darchiashvili on his appointment and wished him success in his work. Highlighting the strategic partnership established between the two countries and the busy agenda of relations in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The sides welcomed the 30th anniversary of the re-establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and the United Kingdom. The Minister discussed with the British diplomat the main areas of bilateral relations, including the importance of exchanging high-level visits and holding the Wardrop Strategic Dialogue. The sides also stressed the need for the further deepening of sectoral cooperation. As the UK Ambassador noted, despite withdrawing from the European Union, the United Kingdom remains a firm supporter of Georgia's European aspirations. The British diplomat reaffirmed his unconditional support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as the UK’s role in bringing Georgia closer to NATO standards and in facilitating the process of integration into the Alliance.
