15 December 2025 On 15 December 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Khatuna Totladze held a meeting with the Ambassador-designate of the Republic of Panama to Georgia, Karina Arias Fonseca de Gianotti (resident in Ankara). The Deputy Minister congratulated the Ambassador on her appointment and wished her success in her new role. The parties discussed prospects for the further development of cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Panama. Khatuna Totladze emphasized the importance for Georgia to deepen relations both with Panama and with the Latin American region, as well as to further advance cooperation with regional organizations of the Caribbean countries. The discussion also addressed the issue of holding the next round of political consultations. The parties reaffirmed their readiness to continue and intensify cooperation within multilateral formats.
