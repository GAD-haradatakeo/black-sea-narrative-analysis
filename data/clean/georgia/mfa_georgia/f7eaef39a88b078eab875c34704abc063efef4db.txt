22 April 2020 I have no illusion that the opposition will ever recognize the success we may achieve internationally, however great this success may be. Speaking on the air of Imedi TV, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani said that the opposition considers the government’s successful fight against coronavirus as its own failure. According to him, despite such approach of the opposition, people can very well see the difference between what is going on now and what used to be before. “Whatever the opposition may say, we must do our business and keep looking ahead. We have our goal, on which must set our sights and pursue our path consistently towards this goal” – Zalkaliani said. Documents Similar News
