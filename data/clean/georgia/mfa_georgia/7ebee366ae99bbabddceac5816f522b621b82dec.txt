2 May 2025 On 2 May 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Kazakhstan to Georgia, Malik Murzalin. During the meeting, the parties discussed key issues related to bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Kazakhstan, as well as prospects for the further development of relations across a range of areas. The conversation also focused on opportunities to increase trade and investment flows between the two countries, the positive trends observed in tourism, and avenues for deepening cooperation in the fields of transport and logistics, education, and culture.
