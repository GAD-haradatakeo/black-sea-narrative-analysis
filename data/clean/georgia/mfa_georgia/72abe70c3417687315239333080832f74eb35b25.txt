22 April 2025 The Secretary General of the Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE), Feridun Sinirlioğlu, is paying an official visit to Georgia. On April 22, a meeting will be held with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, after which Maka Botchorishvili and Feridun Sinirlioğlu will make joint statements to the press. The OSCE Secretary General is also scheduled to hold meetings with senior officials from both the executive and legislative branches of the Georgian government.
