2 June 2022 On 2 June 2022, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Imamura Akira. At the meeting, the sides discussed current issues on the Georgia-Japan bilateral agenda and prospects for the further development of relations. Discussions also focused on cooperation within the international organizations. The sides put emphasis on the need to further deepen ongoing cooperation in various fields. In this regard, the importance of exchanging high-level visits was especially emphasized. The sides paid special attention to the emerging security threats in different regions of the world and the importance of the international community’s consolidated efforts to ensure an adequate response to them.
