23 January 2024 Within the framework of the visit, the Georgian Foreign Minister will meet with his colleague, Prince Faisal bin Farhan Al Saud. After the meeting of the foreign ministers of the two countries, an agreement will be signed between Georgia and Saudi Arabia on the creation of an intergovernmental coordination council. The signing of this document will significantly contribute to the deepening of economic and political cooperation between the countries and will bring the existing relations between the two countries to a qualitatively new stage. The creation of the joint intergovernmental commission between the two countries was decided during the meeting between the Prime Minister, Irakli Gharibashvili and His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz, Crown Prince of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, within the framework of the visit of the Prime Minister of Georgia to Saudi Arabia in 2022.
