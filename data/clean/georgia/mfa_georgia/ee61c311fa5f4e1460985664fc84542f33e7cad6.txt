14 May 2021 The Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Hungary to Georgia, Victoria Horvat. Discussions focused on the ways to deepen cooperation in combating COVID-19. The Deputy Minister thanked the Hungarian Ambassador for assistance in this regard. Victoria Horvat, for her part, stressed that Hungary will continue to support Georgia actively in this and other areas. Talking points also included development of bilateral relations in various areas and the conduction of a meeting of the bilateral inter-governmental economic commission due to be held in the second half of May, 2021.
