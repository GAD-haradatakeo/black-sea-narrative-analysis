23 October 2019 23.10.2019, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Minister described as unprecedented the bipartisan Georgia Support Act unanimously passed by the U.S. House of Representatives on 22 October 2019. “We have never had such an unprecedentedly high level legislative initiative in the history of Georgian-American strategic partnership. This is a really comprehensive act and it equals the existing strategic partnership between Georgia and the United States. We wish to have this kind of partnership with our strategic partner and this document defines all the main directions of our strategic partnership. The adoption of the bipartisan Georgia support act by the House of Representatives once again confirms that we have the firm bipartisan support from the American side and the issue of Georgia is highest on the political agenda of the United States| - Zalkaliani said. According to him, the Georgia Support Act is yet another step towards strengthening Georgia’s special relations with the U.S. and points to how much important Georgia’s strategic partnership is to the U.S. Documents Similar News
