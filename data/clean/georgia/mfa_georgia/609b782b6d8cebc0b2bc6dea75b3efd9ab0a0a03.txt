17 September 2021 On 17 September 2021, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Imamura Akira. Discussing issues of bilateral/multilateral cooperation and the Georgia-Japan agenda, the sides paid particular attention to the so-called connectivity cooperation. Talking points also included prospects for economic cooperation in both energy and transit/infrastructure, Georgia’s role in ensuring shipment of cargoes from Asia to Europe and back. The sides highlighted Japan’s initiative to deepen relations and achieve closer cooperation with GUAM member states within the regional organization.
