8 June 2017 8.06.2017, Washington – Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze has held meetings at the White House, within his visit to Washington. In Particular, he held a meeting with National Security Advisor General McMaster. Dicussions focused on U.S.-Georgia bilateral relations and Georgia’s progress in Euro-Atlantic integration process. General McMaster highlighted Georgia’s significant contribution towards the strengthening of Euro-Atlantic security. Mikheil Janelidze also held a meeting with Deputy National Security Adviser Ricky Waddell. The sides discussed issues relating to defence, Euro-Atlantic security and territorial integrity.
