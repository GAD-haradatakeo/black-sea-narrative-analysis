24 October 2017 24.10.2017, London - The Lord Mayor of London, Andrew Parmley and the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze delivered opening remarks for representatives of the British business community attending the round table at the Mansion House. The meeting was devoted to Georgia’s economic development and investment opportunities. The meeting in the format of round table was held for the first time at the Mansion House. According to the Lord Mayor of London, one of his priorities is to promote co-operation with the rapidly developing economies. He emphasized that Georgia is the most business-friendly country in the region Deputy Minister of Economy and Sustainable Development Genadi Arveladze provided the presentation on Georgia for representatives of the British business, investment, energy and financial communities attending the Round Table.
