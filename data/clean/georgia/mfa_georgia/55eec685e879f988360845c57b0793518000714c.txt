21 April 2023 On 21 April 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia met with the German ambassadors to Georgia, Azerbaijan and Armenia, who are holding meetings in Tbilisi within the framework of the regional conference of German ambassadors. Discussions focused on the current situation in the region, security challenges and prospects for cooperation. The importance of peace and stability in the region was emphasized, as well as the growing role of the "Middle Corridor", which increases the attractiveness of the region and contributes to the connectivity between the West and the East. Special attention was focused on the importance of strategic regional projects. The sides highlighted Georgia's role in the region and its contribution to ensuring peace and stability. Special mention was taken of the importance of the Georgian Prime Minister’s implementation of the "Peaceful Neighborhood Initiative".
