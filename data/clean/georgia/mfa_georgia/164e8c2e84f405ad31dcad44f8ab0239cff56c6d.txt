2 June 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the UK to Georgia, Mark Clayton. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral relations, the situation in the region and the security challenges. Special attention was focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, the progress in the Geneva International Discussions and the Georgian Government’s reconciliation policy.
