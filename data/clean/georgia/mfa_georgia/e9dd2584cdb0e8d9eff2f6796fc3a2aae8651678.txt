25 November 2019 25.11.2019, Berlin – At a meeting with the President of the German Bundestag, David Zalkaliani and Wolfgang Schäuble highly appraised Georgia-Germany relations. The President of the German Bundestag reaffirmed Germany’s political and practical support for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration, as well as for its sovereignty and territorial integrity. The parties highlighted the close cooperation between the parliaments of the two countries as evidenced by the exchange of high-level visits and German Parliamentarians’ statements made at the international level in support for Georgia. Special note was taken of the particular progress Georgia has achieved in terms of European integration, including within the format of Eastern Partnership. Discussing the global security challenges, the parties highlighted the importance of the country’s stabile development in terms of achieving both domestic and foreign policy goals. Zalkaliani briefed the Bundestag President on the Georgian Government’s peace policy and its steps towards de-escalation of the situation. Discussions focused on the situation in the occupied regions, including, on the illegal detention of the medical doctor, Vazha Gaprindashvili – that is yet another flagrant violation of human rights. The parties highlighted the necessity of international reaction to such incidents While on the theme of bilateral relations, the parties paid special attention to high-level communication and visits between the two countries, including the German Federal Chancellor’s and President’s recent visits to Georgia. Zalkaliani thanked Wolfgang Schäuble for the practical assistance Germany has been rendering to Georgia since regaining its independence. According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, this assistance makes a tremendous contribution to the development of Georgia, as well as to its economic progress and the implementation of its foreign policy goals.
