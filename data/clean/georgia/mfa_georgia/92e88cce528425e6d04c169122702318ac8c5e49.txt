29 November 2022 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Khatuna Totladze met with her Czech counterpart, Martin Smolek, on 29 November 2022, in Prague. At the meeting, the sides spoke about the importance of close cooperation and friendly relations between the two countries; discussions focused on consular, educational, cultural and other issues. The Deputy Ministers also emphasized the importance of facilitating the process of issuing student visas to Georgian students. The sides emphasized the need to deepen cooperation in the area of legal migration. The Georgian side thanked the Czech government for increasing the number of employment quotas for Georgian citizens.
