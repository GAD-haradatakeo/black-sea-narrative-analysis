The Prime Minister highlighted Georgia’s progress, as well as the challenges facing the country as he spoke before the PACE 10 April 2019 10.04.2019, Strasbourg – According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, the Prime Minister’s visit to Strasbourg, as well as events scheduled to take place within the framework of the visit to mark the 20th anniversary of Georgia’s membership in the Council of Europe are of paramount importance, with due regard for the fact that Georgia becomes the chair of the Council of Europe Parliamentary Assembly.
