26 June 2017 26.06.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to Georgia Lukas Beglinger and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to Russia who arrived in Georgia within the framework of a working visit. The sides discussed important aspects of bilateral relations, issues relating to the Swiss Confederation’s role as a mediator between Georgia and Russia. Mikheil Janelidze thanked the Swiss side for the Support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and for its mediation efforts between Georgia and Russia. The Minister highlighted Switzerland’s positive role in establishing contacts between Georgia and Russia since the two countries broke their diplomatic relations, expressing his gratitude for the Swiss diplomats’ assistance to Georgia’s interests section in Moscow in ensuring the solution of specific issues, including the smooth functioning of the consular service.
