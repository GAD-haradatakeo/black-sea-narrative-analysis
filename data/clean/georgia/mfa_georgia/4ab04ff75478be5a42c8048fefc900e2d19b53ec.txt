18 February 2024 On 18 February 2024, the Georgian Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Chief Executive Officer of the U.S. International Development Finance Corporation, Scott Nathan, within the framework of the Munich Security Conference. The sides explored the key areas of strategic partnership between the two countries and the possibilities for future collaboration, particularly in bolstering economic ties and attracting investments. They highlighted the considerable capabilities of the U.S. International Development Finance Corporation in enhancing connections between trade and financial sectors.
