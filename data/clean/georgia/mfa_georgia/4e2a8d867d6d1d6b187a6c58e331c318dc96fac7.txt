4 March 2021 The Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with the Turkish Vice President, Fuat Oktay. The Meeting was also attended by the Turkish Foreign Minister, Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu. The Vice President congratulated David Zalkaliani upon his reappointment; wishing both him as well as the new Government of Georgia success, inviting the Prime Minister to visit Turkey. The parties discussed all dimensions of the Georgian-Turkish strategic partnership, expressing readiness to actively cooperate in order to further develop bilateral relations, along with effectively implement significant, strategic projects. The Georgian Foreign Minister expressed gratitude towards Tukey for the support shown to Georgia during the global pandemic.
