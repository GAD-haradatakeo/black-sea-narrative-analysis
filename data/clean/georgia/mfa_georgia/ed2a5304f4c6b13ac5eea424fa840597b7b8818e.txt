2 June 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the armed attack in the Resorts World Manila casino in the Philippine capital, leaving tens of innocent people dead. Georgia strongly condemns any attempt targeting civilians, for which there is no justification whatsoever. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its solidarity with the government and people of the Republic of the Philippines conveying its condolences to the families of the terror victims and wishing speedy recovery to those who have been killed in the incident.
