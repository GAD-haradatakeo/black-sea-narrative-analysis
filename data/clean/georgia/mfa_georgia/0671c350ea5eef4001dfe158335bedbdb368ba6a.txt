29 January 2021 Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Khatuna Totladze held a meeting with the ambassador-designate of Japan to Georgia, Imamura Akira. The Parties discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral and multilateral relations. The Japanese Ambassador expressed his gratitude to the Georgian Foreign Ministry for its role in developing Georgian-Japanese relations and noted with satisfaction the increasing dynamics of successful cooperation between the two countries. For her part, Khatuna Totladze expressed the hope that the fruitful cooperation between the two countries will further continue and be upgraded to a higher level.
