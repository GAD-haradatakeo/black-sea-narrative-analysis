According to Maka Botchorishvili, developing strategic partnerships with various countries and exploring new formats is a priority and of particular importance for the Government of Georgia 26 November 2024 Given Georgia's status as an EU membership candidate, it will be of particular importance and priority to strengthen bilateral relations with European countries. While maximizing the use of existing strategic partnership formats is crucial, we will also continue to establish new platforms - and promote regular consultations and political dialogue with our European partners - the candidate for Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili said during her speech at the joint session of the parliamentary committees.
