6 December 2018 6.12.2018, Milan - GUAM-Canada meeting – the first in such a format - was held on the sidelines of the 25th OSCE Ministerial Council. The meeting was attended by the Foreign Ministers of Azerbaijan, Georgia, Moldova and Ukraine - Elmar Mammadyarov, David Zalkaliani Tudor Ulianovschi and Pavlo Klimkin; Canadian Foreign Minister ChrystiaFreeland and the Secretary General of the Organization. Welcoming co-operation with Canada in this new format, the sides highlighted the important potential of further developing this co-operation in various areas. According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, Canada is a strong supporter of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as its European and Euro-Atlantic path.
