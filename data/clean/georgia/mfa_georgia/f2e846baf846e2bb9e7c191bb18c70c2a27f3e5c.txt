25 April 2018 25.04.2018, New York – Within the framework of his visit to New York, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister, Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of Moldova, Tudor Ulianovschi. The two ministers discussed issues relating to bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Moldova, as well as the European integration agenda of the two countries. Discussions also focused on the current co-operation within the framework of international organizations. Mikheil Janelidze informed his Moldovan colleague on the latest developments in Georgia’s occupied regions, as well as on the Georgian Government’s peace proposal. The Ministers reaffirmed their commitment to support each other’s efforts towards European integration. The sides highlighted the importance of exchanging high-level visits.
