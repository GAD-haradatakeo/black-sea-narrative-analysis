8 November 2022 On 7 November 2022, Georgian delegation met with the Co-Chairs of the Geneva International Discussions (GID) from the UN, EU and OSCE at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. From the Georgian side, the meeting was led by the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and the Head of the Georgian delegation to the GID, Lasha Darsalia. At the meeting, special emphasis was made on the necessity of achieving progress on core agenda items of the Geneva International Discussions related to the need of full implementation of the EU-mediated 12 August 2008 Ceasefire Agreement by Russia as well as the safe and dignified return of internally displaced persons (IDPs) and refugees to their homes. In this context, crucial importance of the effective functioning of the GID was stressed. At the meeting, special attention was paid to the need to restore the Gali Incident Prevention and Response Mechanism (IPRM) without any preconditions as well as to the conduct of the Ergneti IPRM on the basis of founding principles and ground rules. Georgian delegation expressed concern over Russia’s provocative and illegal actions in Georgia’s occupied regions and in the vicinity of the occupation line, further aggravating security, humanitarian and human rights situation on the ground.
