20 January 2022 On 20 January 2022, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia received representatives of the UN agencies operating in Georgia, the International Committee of the Red Cross and international non-governmental organizations working on various projects in the occupied regions of Georgia. The meeting focused on the dire humanitarian situation in Georgia’s Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions and the Russian Federation’s provocative actions, which weigh heavily on the conflict-affected population. The sides also put emphasis on the needs of internally displaced persons and refugees and on the importance of protecting their rights. The First Deputy Foreign Minister thanked the partners for their work that helps improve the humanitarian situation of the people living in the occupied territories, and expressed the commitment of the Georgian side to continue to support them in this important matter.
