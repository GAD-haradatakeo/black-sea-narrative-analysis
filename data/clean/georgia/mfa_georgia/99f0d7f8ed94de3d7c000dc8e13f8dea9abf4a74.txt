18 December 2024 On 18 December 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili held an introductory meeting with the Ambassador of the United Arab Emirates to Georgia, Ahmed Alnuaimi. The Ambassador congratulated the Minister on her appointment and wished her success in her future endeavors. During the meeting, both parties expressed satisfaction with the growing intensity of relations between their countries. They discussed the exchange of high-level and top-level visits and underscored the importance of further deepening cooperation across various sectors. The parties also exchanged information regarding developments in the Middle East region.
