16 February 2026 On 16 February 2026, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Kingdom of Spain and Permanent Representative of Georgia to the United Nations World Tourism Organization, Alexander Chkuaseli, presented his letters of credence to the UNWTO Secretary-General, Shaikha Nasser Al Nowais. Following the ceremony for the presentation of credentials, a meeting was held between the Secretary-General and the Ambassador. The parties discussed the further deepening of fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the UNWTO. Attention was focused on Georgia’s tourism potential and the prospects for organizing joint activities. The Secretary-General extended his best wishes for success to the Georgian Ambassador.
