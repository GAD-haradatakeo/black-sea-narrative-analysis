23 May 2022 Negotiations and the relevant EU procedure have been completed for the text of the EU-Georgia Association Agenda 2021-2027, which sets out short- and medium-term priorities across a broad spectrum of EU-Georgia cooperation. The agreed text of the Association Agenda will be finally approved in accordance with the Written Procedure of the EU-Georgia Association Council.
