16 October 2025 On 16 October 2025, a preparatory meeting for the 65th round of the Geneva International Discussions was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia with the participation of the Co-Chairs from the European Union, the United Nations and the OSCE. The meeting was chaired on the Georgian side by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and Head of the Georgian Delegation to the Geneva International Discussions, Lasha Darsalia. The participants devoted particular attention to the need to ensure the effectiveness of the Geneva Discussions format. In this context, emphasis was placed on the core issues on the agenda, including the implementation of the international obligations undertaken by the Russian Federation under the EU-mediated Ceasefire Agreement of 12 August 2008, and the necessity of achieving tangible progress toward the safe and dignified return of internally displaced persons and refugees. The meeting underscored the importance of restoring, without any preconditions, the Incident Prevention and Response Mechanism (IPRM) in Gali, as well as holding regular meetings of this mechanism in Gali and Ergneti in full compliance with its founding principles. The Georgian side raised with particular concern the deteriorating security, humanitarian, and human rights situation in the occupied regions and adjacent areas along the occupation line. Attention was also drawn to the attempts by the occupation regime in the Abkhazia region to restrict or block confidence-building projects aimed at fostering trust and reconciliation.
