5 December 2025 On the margins of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili met with the Minister for Foreign Affairs and Tourism of the Republic of Malta, Ian Borg. During the meeting, the parties discussed relations between Georgia and Malta in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The discussion addressed ongoing developments in Georgia and Georgia–European Union relations. Emphasis was placed on the importance of objective assessments and constructive dialogue.
