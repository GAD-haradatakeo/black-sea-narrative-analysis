28 March 2019 28.03.2019, Brussels - Within the framework of his visit to Brussels, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with representatives of the Georgian Diaspora. According to David Zalkaliani it is necessary to ensure that Georgian missions abroad are in active communication with Georgian Diaspora. The Minister highlighted the importance of Diaspora web-portal, which is based on the single window concept and gives Georgian citizens abroad an access to various government services, programmes and projects. Zalkaliani also about Sunday School Books created through the support of the Georgian government that are distributed free of charge among schools. The meeting was attended by Father Superior of the King Tamar Church in Belgium. David Zalkaliani emphasized the significant role Georgian Churches play in implementing Georgia’s foreign political goals as evidenced by the visit of the Georgian chief priests to Brussels and their meetings with NATO and EU representatives.
