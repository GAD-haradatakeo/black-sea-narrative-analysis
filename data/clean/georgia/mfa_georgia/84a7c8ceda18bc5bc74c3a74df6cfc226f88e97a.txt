16 January 2023 On 16 January 2023, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Republic of Kazakhstan to Georgia, Malik Murzalin, handed over copies of his credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili. At the meeting held after the official ceremony, the deputy minister congratulated the ambassador on his appointment, expressed his readiness for future cooperation and wished him success in his future activities. The sides noted with satisfaction the friendly relations between the two countries; They discussed a wide range of issues on the agenda of bilateral and multilateral relations between Georgia and Kazakhstan; They drew attention to the importance of further deepening cooperation in the political, economic and humanitarian spheres, as well as the perspectives of cooperation within international organizations; The sides emphasized the importance of high-level bilateral visits. In this context, the official visit of the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili to the Republic of Kazakhstan in 2022 was especially noted. The deputy minister provided the ambassador with information on the initiatives put forward by Georgia last year, on the ongoing reforms in the country and opportunities for cooperation. He also spoke about the security and human rights situation in the occupied regions of Georgia.
