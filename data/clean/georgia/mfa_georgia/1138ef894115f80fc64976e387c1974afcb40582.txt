31 August 2018 31.08.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Estonia to Georgia Kai Kaarelson The sides discussed the friendly relations and successful co-operation between the two countries. Discussions focused on the results of the NATO summit and important implications for the region. David Zalkaliani brought to the attention of the Ambassador the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, as well as the Georgian Government’s vision of the peace policy highlighting the importance of the consolidated support of the partner countries.
