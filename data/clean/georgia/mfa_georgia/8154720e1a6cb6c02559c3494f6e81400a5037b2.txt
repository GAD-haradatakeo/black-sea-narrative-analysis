1 November 2022 On 30 October, the singer Lasha Ghlonti's charity concert called Georgian Music was held in Dublin, the capital of Ireland. The money raised from the concert will be donated to the Solidarity Fund and will be used for the treatment of children suffering from oncological diseases. The folk band "Caucasion" also took part in the charity concert. The Georgian Ambassador to Ireland, Giorgi Zurabashvili, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vladimer Konstantinidi, the Director of the Solidarity Fund, Ekaterine Festvenidze, the Deputy Mayor of Dublin, Joana Tufi and the Head of Georgian Diaspora in Ireland Lela Shurupova addressed the audience at the event.
