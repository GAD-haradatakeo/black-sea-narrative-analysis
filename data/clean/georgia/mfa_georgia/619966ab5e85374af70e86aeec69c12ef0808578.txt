6 November 2018 6.11.2018, Tbilisi – Ambassador Designate of Spain to Georgia Juan González-Barba (residing in Ankara) presented copies of his credentials to the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili. Discussions during the meeting following the presentation ceremony focused on issues on the agenda of bilateral and multilateral co-operation, as well as on the need to exchange visits and intensify sectoral co-operation. Briefing the Ambassador on Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration agenda, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili thanked him for Spain’s principled position with respect to Georgia and for its support for successful co-operation in multilateral format. For his part, Juan González-Barba reaffirmed Spain’s unequivocal support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration.
