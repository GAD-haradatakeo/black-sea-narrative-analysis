27 August 2025 On 27 August 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the French Republic to Georgia, Sheraz Gasri. The discussion addressed the current agenda of cooperation between Georgia and the French Republic, as well as ongoing developments in the wider region. It was emphasized that, in the face of shared threats and challenges, active dialogue and the exchange of views remain of particular importance. Among the issues considered was Georgia’s process of integration into the European Union. The meeting underlined the significance of further cooperation in this regard, while expressing the hope that EU-Georgia relations will soon return to their normal course.
