26 May 2020 Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani spoke over the phone with his Estonian counterpart, UrmasReinsalu, who congratulated the Georgian people on the Independence Day and reaffirmed Estonia’s firm support for Georgia. The two ministers paid special attention to Georgia’s EU integration process expressing their support for the Eastern Partnership as the European Union’s strategic and ambitious initiative. David Zalkaliani shared with his colleague Georgia’s priorities and expectations with respect to the future goals of Eastern Partnership. The parties discussed the pandemic consequences in the two countries and across the world. Special note was taken of Georgia’s impressive progress in the fight against the pandemic and Georgia’s readiness to host tourists from Estonia. Zalkaliani thanked his colleague for Estonia’s firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration process.
