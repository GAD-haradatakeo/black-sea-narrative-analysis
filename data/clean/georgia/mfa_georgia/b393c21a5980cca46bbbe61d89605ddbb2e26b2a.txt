9 March 2019 09.03.2019, Tbilisi - Tbilisi hosted a marathon #SilkRoadCup-2019 for the representatives of the diplomatic corpsaimed to promote a healthy lifestyle. The event organised by Georgia’s Corporate Sports Federation in the frames of#BeActiveinGeorgia campaign, was supported by Georgian Foreign Ministry and a number of embassies based in Georgia. The event was attended by the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani. According to him, health and healthy lifestyle are very important for our citizensand the foreign ministry will always be in the avant-garde of this initiative. The marathon was held in the Lisi Lake territory and involved members of the diplomatic corps accredited in Georgia. Marathon winners were awarded symbolic medals, cups and other prizes.
