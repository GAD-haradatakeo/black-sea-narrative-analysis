2 May 2018 2.05.2018, Kutaisi – “I’d like to congratulate Kutaisi on this remarkable day. Kutaisi is an ancient European city, which is part of European culture. The central and local authorities are considering many new projects that are sure to give a fresh impetus to the city” - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze said during the celebrations of the Gviriloba festival. President of Georgia Giorgi Margvelashvili, Speaker of the Parliament of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze and Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze attended the Gviriloba festival. The festival derives its origin from the 1920s and its history goes back over 100 years. Documents Similar News
