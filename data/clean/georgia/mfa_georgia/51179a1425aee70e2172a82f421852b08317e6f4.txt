3 February 2025 On 3 February 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to Georgia, Salman bin Abdulrahman Al Al-Sheikh. The discussion focused on the friendly relations between the two countries and their close cooperation across various fields. Particular emphasis was placed on the positive dynamics of high-level visits. Both sides expressed hope that the Georgia-Saudi Arabia Coordination Council will soon commence its work, further strengthening and elevating bilateral partnership to a new qualitative level. The importance of continuing bilateral political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Saudi Arabia was also underscored. Additionally, the meeting addressed opportunities to enhance business ties between the two countries. The parties agreed to continue their active efforts to deepen cooperation in various sectors.
