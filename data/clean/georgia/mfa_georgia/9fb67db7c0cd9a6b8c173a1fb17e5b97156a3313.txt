10 May 2024 During his working visit to the Kingdom of Spain, Teimuraz Janjalia, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, conducted meetings with Fernando Sampedro, the Spanish Secretary of State for European Union Affairs, and Diego Martínez Belio, the Secretary of State for Foreign and Global Affairs, at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Spain. The discussions encompassed Georgia's foreign policy priorities on both bilateral and multilateral fronts, particularly focusing on European integration and relations with Spain. Both parties highlighted the robust bilateral cooperation and expressed readiness to enhance collaboration across political, economic, and various other spheres. Deputy Minister Janjalia briefed his Spanish counterparts on the progress of implementing EU recommendations and explored avenues for achieving more effective outcomes through joint efforts. He also highlighted the challenging humanitarian situation in Georgia's occupied territories. The Spanish officials reiterated their steadfast support for Georgia's sovereignty, territorial integrity, and European integration aspirations.
