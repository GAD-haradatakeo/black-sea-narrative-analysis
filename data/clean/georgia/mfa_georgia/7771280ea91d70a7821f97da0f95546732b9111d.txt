15 September 2022 On September 15, 2022, in Tbilisi, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia, met with the Anna Capello, Head of Confidence Building Measures Division of the Council of Europe’s Directorate of Political Affairs, the political advisor of the same department, Albina Ovchiarenko, and the deputy head of the Council of Europe Office in Georgia, Stefan Sirbu. Discussions focused on the fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the Council of Europe. The Deputy Minister shared with the delegation of the Council of Europe detailed information on the grave humanitarian situation and human rights violations in the occupied regions of Georgia. During the meeting, Anna Capello spoke about the CoE’s confidence-building projects , which aim to restore trust between communities divided by the Russian occupation of the Georgian territories. Representatives of civil society and various professional groups are involved in the projects. The sides agreed to continue working for further deepening of cooperation. Documents Similar News
