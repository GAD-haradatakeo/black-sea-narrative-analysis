8 August 2017 8.08.2017, Tbilisi –Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze paid homage to the memory of the heroes fallen in the Russian-Georgian war in 2008. The members of the Cabinet of Ministers led by Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili laid a wreath upon the memorial of the fallen heroes. “Georgia came through the most difficult period in August of 2008. Our heroes laid down their lives for the territorial integrity of Georgia, in an unequal war, falling victim to the large-scale aggression. The Government of Georgia, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the diplomatic corps do their utmost to achive the peaceful resolution of the conflict through the maximum involvement and mobilization of the international community. We received unprecedented support in all international formats over the past year. This provides yet another proof that Georgia keeps on the right path and will achieve restoration of justice through our peaceful policy and active diplomatic efforts” – Mikheil Janelidze said.
