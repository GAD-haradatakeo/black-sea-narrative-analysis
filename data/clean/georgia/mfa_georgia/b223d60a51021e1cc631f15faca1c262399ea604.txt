Within the framework of the UN General Assembly, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia continues meetings with his colleagues 20 September 2022 Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Singapore, Vivian Balakrishnan. The Georgian Foreign Minister emphasized Georgia's interest in developing relations with Singapore.
