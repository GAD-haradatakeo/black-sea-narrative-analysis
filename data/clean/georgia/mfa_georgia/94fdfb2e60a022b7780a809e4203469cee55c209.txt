3 March 2020 3.03.2020, Tbilisi – Intensive bilateral visits between the neighbours further points to the importance of Georgia to Armenia and vice versa” – David Zalkaliani said following the Armenian Prime Minister’s welcome ceremony at the Tbilisi International Airport. “We will discuss all main areas of cooperation, including trade and economy, transport and energy, culture and people-to-people contacts. Discussions will also focus on the challenges facing regional security. As far as you know Georgia and Armenia are EaP countries and since EaP summit is scheduled to take place in 2020, it is highly important that we coordinate our positions regarding various issues. It is also important to coordinate our efforts to prevent the spread of coronavirus” – Zalkaliani said. Documents Similar News
