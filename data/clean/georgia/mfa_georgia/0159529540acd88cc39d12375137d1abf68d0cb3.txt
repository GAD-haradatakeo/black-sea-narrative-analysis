18 December 2025 On 18 December 2025, within the framework of an official visit to the Republic of Serbia, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with her Serbian counterpart, Marko Đurić. During the meeting, the Ministers discussed key issues on the agenda of bilateral relations between Georgia and the Republic of Serbia, as well as ways to further deepen the bilateral dialogue. The discussion addressed the prospects for concluding a free trade agreement, as well as the implementation of investment projects in both countries and the exchange of experience in this field. The Ministers reaffirmed their firm mutual support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of both countries within their internationally recognized borders. Challenges on the path toward European integration were also noted, along with the need for continued efforts in this direction.
