24 December 2018 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the hundreds of casualties caused by the disastrous tsunami on the island of Java, the Republic of Indonesia on 22ndof December 2018. The Ministry conveys its condolences to the families of the victims and wishes soonest recovery to those who were injured. In this moment of grief, the Ministry wishes strength and unity to the Government and People of Indonesia. Tbilisi, 24 December 2018 Documents Similar News
