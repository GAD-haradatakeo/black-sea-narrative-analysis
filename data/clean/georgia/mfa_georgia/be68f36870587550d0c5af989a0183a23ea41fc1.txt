7 February 2024 "The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia continues to work for the main priority of the country - strengthening the sovereignty of the country and restoring territorial integrity in a peaceful way"- Ilia Darchiashvili, the candidate for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, said at the joint session of the Foreign Relations, EU Integration, Diaspora and Caucasus Committees of the Parliament of Georgia. According to him, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs is making efforts to implement the policy of peaceful resolution of the Russian-Georgian conflict, both in terms of de- occupation of the Georgian territories by Russia, and in terms of reconciliation and engagement between societies divided by the occupation line. In this context, Ilia Darchiashvili focused on the importance of achieving tangible results in the Geneva International Discussions. &quot;We will continue to work closely with the co-chairing organizations and the US as a participant in the negotiations, as well as with the EU member states to find ways to make progress on the negotiations and to provide effective international support,&quot; said the ministerial candidate.
