18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili met with the Ambassador of the Sultanate of Oman to Georgia, Saif Rashid Saif Al-Jahwari (resident in Ankara). The parties discussed a wide spectrum of relations between the two countries. Particular attention was devoted to the importance of developing cooperation in the fields of economy, trade, investment, tourism, and education. Both sides expressed their readiness to promote the strengthening of political dialogue and the organization of high-level bilateral visits, as well as to deepen existing ties in the areas of economy and culture. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed gratitude to Oman for its steadfast support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. The Ambassador of the Sultanate of Oman presented copies of his Letters of Credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. The parties also exchanged views on current issues of bilateral relations.
