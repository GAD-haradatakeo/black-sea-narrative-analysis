15 May 2017 On the occasion of the 25th anniversary of establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Mongolia, the Foreign Ministers of the two countries exchanged congratulatory letters. According to the letter by Mikheil Janelidze Georgia attaches great importance to the development of bilateral relations with Mongolia and stands ready to further advance this co-operation in all spheres. In his letter, the Mongolian Foreign Minister reaffirms the traditionally fruitful partnership between Georgia and Mongolia expressing the hope to further enhance this cooperation in the fields of trade, tourism and agriculture.
