19 September 2018 18.09.2018, Washington –According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, during the Georgian Prime Minister’s visit to the United States of America, American partners made important messages regarding Georgia’s democratic development that point to the fact that Georgia is an important partner to the U.S. in the region. “We heard very important messages from the U.S. high-ranking officials, and especially from the National Security Advisor of the United States, John Bolton saying that Georgia is the leader of the region and the reforms being carried out in the country provide an example to be modelled on and we are becoming a pillar for our strategic partner in the region and beyond” – David Zalkaliani said. According to him, meetings were held with representatives of both: the executive and legislative authorities where bipartisan support for Georgia was confirmed. The Minister paid particular attention to the meeting with Senator Lindsey Gram known as a firm supporter of Georgia and an author of a series of important initiatives. “Senator Lindsey Gram gave high appraisal to the ongoing democratic reforms, as well as to the European and Euro-Atlantic integration processes in Georgia” – David Zalkaliani said.
