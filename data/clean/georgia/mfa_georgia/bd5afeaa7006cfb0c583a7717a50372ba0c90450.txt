13 August 2025 Shota Getsadze, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Swiss Confederation and the Principality of Liechtenstein, presented his credentials to His Serene Highness Hereditary Prince Alois of Liechtenstein. Following the credential presentation ceremony, the Ambassador expressed gratitude to the Prince for his steadfast support of Georgia’s territorial integrity and sovereignty. The discussion touched upon Georgia’s strategic location and geopolitical significance, with particular emphasis on major economic projects implemented in the country, as well as other important issues. It is noteworthy that prior to the credential presentation ceremony, an introductory meeting was held at the Government Palace between the Ambassador and Sabine Monauni, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Environment, and Culture of Liechtenstein, and Martin Frick, Director of the Office for Foreign Affairs. The parties discussed existing cooperation between the two countries in both bilateral and multilateral formats.
