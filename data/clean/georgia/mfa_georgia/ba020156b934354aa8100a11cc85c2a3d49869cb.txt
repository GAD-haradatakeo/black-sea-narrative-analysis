1 August 2018 High ranking guests from Poland, Lithuania, Latvia and Ukraine will visit Georgia on 6-7 August 10 years after theRussia-Georgia 2008 war. Foreign Ministers from Poland, Lithuania and Latvia and Vice Prime Minister from Ukraine will participate in a round table organised by Georgia’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the main topic of which will be ‘Peace and Security – 10 Years Since the Russia-Georgia War’. Challenges facing peace and security in Europe will be discussed within the round table format. Special attention will be paid to peaceful resolution of conflicts in the region. Talking points will also include the Russia-Georgia conflict, the difficult humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the Georgian government’s peace policy. The guests will meet the President of Georgia, the Prime Minister of Georgia and the Parliament Speaker. While in Georgia the guests will visit the occupation line and honour the memory of those who died for Georgia’s territorial integrity.
