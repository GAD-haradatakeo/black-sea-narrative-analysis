19 July 2019 19.07.2019, Washington – At the meeting held within the framework of “Ministerial to Advance Religious Freedom” dedicated to a freedom of religion, Davit Zalkaliani, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Mick Mulvaney, Chief-of-Staff of the White House, expressed their satisfaction about the high level relations between Georgia and the acting administration of the USA, about the firm support to Georgia reflected in the most important visits, enhanced cooperation in defense and security and steps taken to strengthen economic connections between two countries. Strategic partnership between Georgia and the USA, being at the highest level under the current administration of the USA, was underlined. The Chief-of-Staff thanked the Georgian party for participation in the ministerial dedicated to a freedom of religion, which was held under the patronage of the US Secretary of State Mike Pompeo for the second time.
