5 November 2022 Speaking before the audience, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili congratulated the diplomatic staff on their professional holiday and thanked them for their contribution to the country's protection of state interests. "We have a special attitude towards our diplomats, who protect Georgia's interests internationally during hard times. Diplomats have a special role in representing our country on the international arena and in upholding the country's interests abroad" - the Minister of Foreign Affairs said. Ilia Darchiashvili presented Sulkhan-Saba Orbeliani award to: Merab Antadze, a famous Georgian diplomat, a researcher of the Levan Mikeladze Diplomatic Training and Research Institute of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, for his fruitful work in various positions at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia since the restoration of independence of Georgia and for his contribution to the process of reconciliation and peaceful conflict resolution; Mikheil Ukleba, an experienced Georgian diplomat, currently a researcher at the Levan Mikeladze Diplomatic Training and Research Institute of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the State of Georgia, for his long and fruitful work in the diplomatic service of Georgia, including for his significant contribution to the country's integration into the European Union. Diplomat's Day is celebrated every year since 2004 and is linked to the greatest Georgian writer, public figure and diplomat, Sulkhan-Saba Orbeliani.
