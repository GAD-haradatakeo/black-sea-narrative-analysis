8 September 2021 The Prime Minister of Armenia, Nikol Pashinyan arrived in Georgia on 8 September. The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani participated in the official welcome ceremony held at Tbilisi International Airport. During his visit to Georgia, the Armenian Prime Minister will hold meetings with his Georgian counterpart, Prime Minister Irakli Gharibashvili and the President of Georgia, Salome Zourabichvili.
