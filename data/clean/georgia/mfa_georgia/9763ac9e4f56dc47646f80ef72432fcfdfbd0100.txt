29 November 2018 29.11.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani attended the reception to mark Romania’s National Day. Speaking before the audience, Zalkaliani highlighted the intensity of political relations between Georgia and Romania and Romania’s firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. According to David Zalkaliani, Georgia and Romania are historically connected by the Black Sea and of paramount importance is the full use of transport and energy potential.
