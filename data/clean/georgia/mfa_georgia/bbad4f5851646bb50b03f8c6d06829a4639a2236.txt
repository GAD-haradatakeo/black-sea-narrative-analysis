26 November 2019 “One important point I’d like to highlight is that creeping annexation and occupation is an ongoing process in Georgia. Therefore, we have to assess the situation in the strongest possible terms”. As we speak now illegal borderization process is taking place in Georgia. A few weeks ago, the famous Georgian doctor was detained and kidnapped in the occupied territory when he was on his way to his patient. Now he remains remanded in custody. We call on the international community to increase pressure on those forces of the Russian Federation that are in control of those territories. Ethnic Georgians who have returned to the paces of their origin are now under threat. Many families are divided up. They have no access to their agricultural lands. They are denied the right to native language education. We should not turn the blind eye to such illegal acts as it will send a wrong signal to Georgian society, to our people” – David Zalkaliani said at the Foreign Policy Forum in Berlin.
