3 September 2025 On 3 September 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Kazakhstan to Georgia, Malik Murzalin. The discussion focused on relations between Georgia and Kazakhstan and on prospects for the further development of bilateral cooperation, including in the areas of trade and economy, transport and logistics, education, tourism, and sports. The importance of high-level exchanges of visits was underscored.
