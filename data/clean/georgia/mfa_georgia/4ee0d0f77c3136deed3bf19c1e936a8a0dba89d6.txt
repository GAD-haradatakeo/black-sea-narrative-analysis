11 July 2025 On 11–12 July 2025, Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs Maka Botchorishvili is paying a working visit to the Republic of Croatia, where she is participating in the 2025 Dubrovnik Forum at the invitation of Croatian Foreign Minister Gordan Grlić Radman. The Dubrovnik Forum serves as a key international platform for dialogue, bringing together participants to discuss regional, European, and global challenges, explore solutions, assess geopolitical shifts, and identify new opportunities for cooperation. The conference brings together political leaders, foreign ministers, experts, academics, and other high-ranking officials from various countries.
