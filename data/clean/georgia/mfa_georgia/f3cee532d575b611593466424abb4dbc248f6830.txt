21 July 2020 On 21 July 2020, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, held a farewell meeting with the ambassador of the United Kingdom, Justin McKenzie Smith. Within the framework of the meeting a positive assessment was provided to the existing dynamics of the Georgian-British strategic partnership, which is unravelling with utmost success, both in terms of bilateral as well as multilateral formats. The Minister expressed his gratitude towards the Ambassador for the efforts exerted on his behalf in terms of the process of deepening of the relations between Georgia and the UK.
