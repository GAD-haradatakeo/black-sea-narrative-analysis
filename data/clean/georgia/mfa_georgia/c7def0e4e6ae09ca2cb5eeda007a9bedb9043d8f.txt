20 December 2019 20.12.2019 – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani commented on the illegal imprisonment of Doctor Vazha Gaprindashvili at the press-conference following the Ambassadorial Meeting in Tbilisi. “We condemn the decision of the Tskhinvali occupation regime to leave doctor Vazha Gaprindashvili in prison.We have activatedall the levers available and got all the international structures involved for the release of the doctor fromillegal detention. The decision to send the doctor to prison once again confirms that his detention was a deliberate provocation. I will have an immediate communication with co-chairs of Geneva International Discussions, as well as with the heads of all international organizations, including the EU, OSCE and the UN. We will take immediate actions to ensure the release of the doctor” – Zalkaliani said.
