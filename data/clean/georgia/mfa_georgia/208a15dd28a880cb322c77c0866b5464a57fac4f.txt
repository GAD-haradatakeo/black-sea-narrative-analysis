28 June 2021 Invited by the US State Secretary, Antony Blinken, and the Italian Foreign Minister, Luigi Di Maio, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani participates in a meeting of the global coalition. The Minister addressed the attendees and discussed the following points: Reconfirming the significant contribution of Georgia in terms of ensuring global security; Expressing readiness to continue joint cooperation in the future in order to overcome the existing challenges; Underscoring the priceless contribution of Georgian military personnel to international missions.
