29 November 2017 28.11.2017, Tbilisi – “Georgia is the leader among the Eastern Partnership countries in terms of the full and effective implementation of the Association Agenda. The EU considers that Georgia is best-prepared for integration with the EU” - Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Slovenia, Karl Erjavec said during a joint press-conference with his Georgian counterpart, Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze. Karl Erjavec highlighted Georgia’s important contribution towards the strengthening of Euro-Atlantic security and reaffirmed Slovenia’s strong support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The Slovenian Deputy Prime Minister pledged to assist Georgia in achieving a greater level of legal, institutional and sectoral compatibility with the EU. Karl Erjavec invited Mikheil Janelidze to Slovenia to mark the 25th anniversary of establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries, expressing his readiness to arrange a business forum that will contribute to the strengthening of trade-economic links between the two countries and implementation of investments.
