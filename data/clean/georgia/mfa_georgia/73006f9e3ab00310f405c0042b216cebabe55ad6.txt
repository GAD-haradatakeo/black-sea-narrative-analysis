19 May 2017 19.05.2017, Nicosia - Within the framework of the 127th Session of the CoE Committee of Ministers, Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Council of Europe Secretary General Thorbjørn Jagland. The Minister thanked the Secretary General for continuing the publication of consolidated reports on the conflict in Georgia, which contain important information on the human rights situation in the occupied territories. Mikheil Janelidze also emphasized the importance of the decisions made by the Committee of Ministers' Deputies of the Council of Europe.
