7 August 2021 On 7 August 2021, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Lasha Darsalia visited the Tserovani IDP settlement. Lasha Darsalia met with IDPs as a result of the August 2008 war to hear from them about their problems and needs, as well as about those of residents of villages near the occupation line. The First Deputy Minister spoke on the Georgian government's efforts and peace policy, as well as on security, human rights and humanitarian issues at the Geneva International Talks. On 4-7 August, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs visited Kutaisi, Khoni, Zugdidi, Enguri Bridge, Gugutiantkari, Gori and Tserovani as part of the governmental commission for elaboration and implementation of Strategy for De-occupation and Peaceful Conflict Resolution.
