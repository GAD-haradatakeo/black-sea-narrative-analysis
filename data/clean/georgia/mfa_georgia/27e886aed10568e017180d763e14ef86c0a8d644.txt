10 September 2019 Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Azerbaijan to Georgia Dursun Hasanov. As a tribue for the Georgian Foreign Minister’s contribution towards the strengthening of Georgia-Azerbaijan relations, the Ambassador presented Minister Zalkaliani with Medal issued on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of diplomatic service. The sides discussed important issues of cooperation between the two countries “The history of friendship and strategic partnership between Georgia and Azerbaijan goes back many years and will continue for many years to come” – Dursun Hasanov said.
