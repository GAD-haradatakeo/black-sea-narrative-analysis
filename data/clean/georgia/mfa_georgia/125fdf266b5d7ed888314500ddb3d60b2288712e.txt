3 October 2023 On 3 October 2023, within the framework of his visit to Strasbourg, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the European Commissioner for Crisis Management, Janez Lenarčič to discuss the reforms carried out by Georgia on the road to European integration and the progress achieved in this regard. Ilia Darchiashvili highlighted the achievements Georgia has made after being granted the European perspective, and expressed the hope that Georgia's efforts will be properly assessed and reflected in the decision to grant the country a candidate status. Discussions focused on the important steps taken on the way to Georgia's rapprochement with Europe, both at the legislative and institutional levels. The Minister once again confirmed Georgia's intention to join the EU Civil Protection Mechanism (UCPM) and in this regard, emphasized the progress in terms of modernizing the relevant institutions of Georgia and bringing them into compliance with European standards.
