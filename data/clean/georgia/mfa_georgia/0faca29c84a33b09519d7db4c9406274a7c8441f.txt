25 April 2020 In coordination with the Government of Georgia, Belavia will perform Minsk-Tbilisi charter flights on 3, 10 and 17 May. The decision was taken due to the increasing number of Georgian citizens using Minsk-Yerevan transit flights. Air tickets are available at Belavia web page: https://belavia.by/ A total of 7,786 citizens of Georgia have returned home by air, sea and road as of 25 April 2020. A total of 22 flights have been performed to date.
