12 December 2017 12.12.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participated in the celebration of Hanukkah at the Synagogue in Tbilisi. Georgia’s Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili congratulated the Jewish people on their holiday. Wishing the Georgian and Jewish nations peace and prosperity he spoke about the close relations between Georgia and Israel. Prime Minister of Israel Benjamin Netanyahu addressed the audience in the Tbilisi Sinagogue via vide conference. Accoridng to the Israeli Ambassador to Georgia, Hanukkah is a festival symbolizing the victory of the good over the evil.
