25 September 2020 On 25 September 2020, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with the representative of the International Federation of the Red Cross and the Red Crescent (IFRC) in South Caucasus, Olga Jumaeva and the President of the Georgian Red Cross Society, Natia Loladze. During the meeting the parties discussed issues associated with the activity of the International Federation of the Red Cross and the Red Crescent and the Georgian Red Cross Society.
