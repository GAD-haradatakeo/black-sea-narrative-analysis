2 September 2022 On 2 September 2022, the new EU Ambassador, Pawel Herczynski presented copies of his credentials to the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili. At the meeting following the presentation ceremony, discussions focused on a wide range of EU-Georgia cooperation, including on the issue of granting a European perspective to Georgia by the European Union in June of this year. The Minister informed the Ambassador about the progress in the implementation of the 12 priorities of the European Council. The sides also discussed issues related to the agenda of the Georgia-EU Association Council scheduled for 6 September, in Brussels. The new EU Ambassador welcomed the participation of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia in the informal meeting of the EU foreign ministers of affairs of the member states last week. Discussions also focused on the current situation in the occupied regions and the role of the European Union in ensuring the stability of the region.
