24 January 2025 On 24 January 2025, the Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Armenia to Georgia, Ashot Smbatyan. The meeting focused on the traditional friendly relations between Georgia and Armenia, as well as current issues on the bilateral cooperation agenda. The parties emphasized the close strategic partnership between the two countries and highlighted the positive dynamics in terms of the exchange of high-level visits. In this context, discussions included the upcoming session of the Georgia-Armenia Intergovernmental Economic Commission scheduled for next week, which presents an excellent opportunity to identify new areas of collaboration. The planned visit of the Georgian Foreign Minister to Armenia at the end of January was also positively noted.
