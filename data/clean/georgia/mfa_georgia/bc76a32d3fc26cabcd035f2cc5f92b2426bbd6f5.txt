24 June 2021 Within their joint visit to Brussels, the three Foreign Ministers of the Associated Trio – Georgia, Ukraine and Moldova, participated in the discussion organised by the Konrad-Adenauer-Stiftung. Within the framework of the discussion the Ministers touched upon: The goals and future activity of the Association Trio; The existing dynamics in terms of their relations with the EU and future possibilities in this regard; Visions for developing the Eastern Partnership framework; The Minister also discussed the challenges faced by the three nations of the Association Trio, as well as the existing regional security environment.
