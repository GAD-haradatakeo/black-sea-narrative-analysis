14 June 2019 On 13 June 2019, the Government of the Republic of Slovenia added Georgia to the list of safe countries of origin. Thus, Slovenia became the 14th country in the EU/Schengen zone to recognize Georgia as a safe country. Only 20countries in the EU/Schengen zone have the official safe country list. Slovenia’s decision provides yet another proof of the country’s political stability, economic development and positive environment for human rights protection and safety. Following this decision,Georgian asylum-seekers’ applications will be examined in an accelerated mode.The other countries that listed Georgia as a 'safe country'areBulgaria, France, Lichtenstein, Austria, Belgium, Netherlands,Iceland, Luxembourg, Ireland, Estonia, Denmark,the Czech Republic and Cyprus.
