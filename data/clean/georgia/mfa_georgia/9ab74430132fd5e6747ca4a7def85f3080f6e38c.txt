30 July 2025 On 30 July 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the State of Israel to Georgia, Hadas Meitzad. During the meeting, the parties reaffirmed the historic and close friendship between Georgia and Israel, founded on mutual respect and multidimensional cooperation. They reviewed key issues on the current agenda of bilateral partnership and noted with satisfaction the dynamic development of Georgian-Israeli relations, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. Minister Botchorishvili expressed her gratitude to Ambassador Meitzad for her significant contribution to strengthening the relations between Georgia and Israel and wished her success in her future endeavors.
