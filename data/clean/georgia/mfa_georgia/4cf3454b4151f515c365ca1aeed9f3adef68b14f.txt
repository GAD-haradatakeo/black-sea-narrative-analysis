16 July 2025 On 16 July 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili held a meeting with the Chargé d’Affaires of the Republic of India to Georgia, Subbu Ramesh. The parties underscored the friendly and partnership-based relations between Georgia and the Republic of India. The meeting focused on key issues on the bilateral agenda, including planned high-level visits, as well as cooperation in trade, economy, transport, logistics, and other sectors.
