18 July 2017 18.07.2017, Tbilisi –Participants of Ambassadorial 2017 held a working meeting with President of Georgia Giorgi Margvelashvili. Discussions focused on Georgia’s foreign policy priorities and plans for the future. Talking points also included the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, important progress Georgia has achieved on its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path, preparations for the Eastern Partnership Summit scheduled to take place in Brussels, on 24 November, Georgia’s relations with partners. The President shared with Ambassadors his views about Georgia’s role and potential in the global and regional context. The meeting proceeded in a Q&A format. Documents Similar News
