3 December 2021 The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Serbia, Nikola Selaković, within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council. The meeting focused on Serbian-Georgian relations. The ministers highlighted the traditionally friendly ties between the two nations, exchanged information on the economic situation and the potential for deepening trade ties. The sides positively assessed the political consultations between Serbia and Georgia held in Tbilisi this month.
