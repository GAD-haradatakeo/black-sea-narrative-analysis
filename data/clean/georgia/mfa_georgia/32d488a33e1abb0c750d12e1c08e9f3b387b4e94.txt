23 October 2021 On 21 October, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimer Konstantinid together with representatives of the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD) met with members of the Georgian diaspora living in Alicante. Discussions focused on Georgia’s state policy regarding the diaspora. The Deputy Minister informed Diaspora representatives about the current and planned projects the Ministry is implementing for Georgian compatriots living abroad. The meeting focused on the challenges facing the Georgian Diaspora and the ways to solve the existing problems. At the meeting, a presentation of Guide on Spanish Migration Legislation prepared by the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD), through cooperation with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, within the framework of ENIGMA2 programme, for our compatriots living in the Kingdom of Spain. The guides were also developed for the Georgian diaspora in Italy and Greece. During the visit, the Deputy Minister and other members of the delegation attended a festive event - "Iveria to Iberia under the Ministry's program "Support to Diaspora Initiatives”.
