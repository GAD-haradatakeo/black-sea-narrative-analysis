28 February 2024 On 28 February 2024, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the non-resident ambassador of the Republic of Uganda to Georgia, Moses Kawaaluuko Kizige. At the meeting, the sides emphasized the importance of deepening relations between Georgia and Uganda and the necessity of continuing cooperation in various fields. It was noted that the foreign ministries of the two countries will maintain active efforts towards facilitating mutual high-level visits and enhancing the bilateral legal and contractual framework.
