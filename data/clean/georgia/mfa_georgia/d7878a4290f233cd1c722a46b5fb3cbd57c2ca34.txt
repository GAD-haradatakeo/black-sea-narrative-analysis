7 December 2017 7.12.2017, Vienna - Within the framework of the 24th OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with OSCE High Commissioner on National Minorities Lamberto Zannier. The sides discussed the topic of national minorities in addition to the occupation of Georgian territories. Special attention was paid to the facts of ethnic cleansing and discrimination against ethnic Georgians living in the occupied territories.
