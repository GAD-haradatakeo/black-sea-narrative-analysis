7 October 2021 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a webinar of the Be the Young Ambassador of Your Country Project winners of the project, on 7 October 2021. The Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani delivered a welcome speech. The webinar was attended by representatives of the Georgian Parliamentary Committee on Diaspora and Caucasus Affairs, the Presidential Administration, the LEPL Youth Agency, the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia, various departments of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD). The Programme Be the Young Ambassador of your country" is implemented by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia with the support of the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD) within the framework of the EU-funded project ENIGMMA 2. The program aims to establish institutional relations with Georgian youths living abroad, to conduct cultural, educational and sports events for young people, to form a network of young foreign friends, to popularize Georgia in the host countries.
