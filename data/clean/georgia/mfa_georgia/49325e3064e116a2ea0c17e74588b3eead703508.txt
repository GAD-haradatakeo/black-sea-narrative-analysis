23 October 2019 Vakhtang Makharoblishvili addressed the audience and on the behalf of the Government of Georgia congratulated the Spanish people on the National Day. While delivering a speech, First Deputy Minister highlighted the shared values of Georgia and Spain with regards to democracy, rule of law, human rights, solidarity among people and nations, as well as rules-based international order, and expressed hope of enhancing ties is numerous dimensions. Vakhtang Makharoblishvili strongly accentuated importance of the support of Spain towards Georgia in terms of the country’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. “The matter of utmost importance I would like to mention is your consistent support for our sovereignty and territorial integrity, irreversible path toward the European Union. Throughout the decades, since the independence, each achievement has been a tough battle on the way to restoringour statehood, democratic institutions, public confidence but now we stand ready to embrace the European family and the act of supporting this vital issue by the friends is so highly appreciated,” – noted Makharblishvili. First Deputy Minister also focused on the significance of the launch of the fully-fledged embassy of the Kingdom of Spain in Tbilisi, expressing hope of partnership between the two countries to become closer and more fruitful. Vakhtang Makharoblishvili spoke about the growing interest of the Spanish companies towards Georgia’s business and investment potential, as well as the increased number of Spanish tourists travelling to Georgia.
