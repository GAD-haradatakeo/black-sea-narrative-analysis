6 December 2019 05.12.2019, Bratislava – David Zalkaliani and Aureliu Ciocoimet on the sidelines of the 26th Ministerial Council inBratislava. Discussing the issues of European integration, the parties welcomed the signing of the joint declaration between Georgia, Moldova and Ukraine, which the Ministers presented to the EU High Representative, Josep Borrell. The Ministers highlighted the need to duly consider the individual aspirations and the progress achieved within the Eastern Partnership format. According to them, it is important to deliver much more tangible benefits to the population. David Zalkaliani acquainted his Moldovan counterpart with Georgia’s initiatives and visions of Eastern Partnership. The Ministers also discussed the issues relating to bilateral relations.
