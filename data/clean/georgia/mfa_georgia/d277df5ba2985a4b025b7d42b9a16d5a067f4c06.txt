Roundtable 10 years since the Russia-Georgia War held at Permanent Representation of Georgia to the United Nations 8 August 2018 On 6 August, 2018 Permanent Representation of Georgia to the United Nations organized a “Roundtable for Lasting Peace and Security – 10 years since 2008 Russia-Georgia War”. The Roundtable intended to facilitate an exchange of views on the prospects and challenges for lasting peace and security with particular focus on the peaceful settlement of unresolved conflicts, through showcasing national examples and experiences. The Roundtable prompted a discussion on possible ways of addressing the pattern of aggressive tactics and hybrid warfare of the Russian Federation in numerous different cases. See All
