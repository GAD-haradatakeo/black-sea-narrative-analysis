24 February 2020 24.02.2020, Geneva – Speaking at the 43rd session of the UN High Level Segment of the Human Rights Council, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani reiterated the importance of member states’ support to the "Otkhozoria-Tatunashvili List" as a preventive step to avoid further aggravation of the situation through imposing the restrictive measures on the individuals responsible for human rights abuses in the occupied regions. Let me remind the Council that the cases of deprivation of lives of Georgian citizens in occupied regions has become a dangerous tendency. On 10 March 2019, the Georgian citizen Irakli Kvaratskhelia was illegally detained by the Russian FSB forces at the occupation line and later died, what is even more alarming – in the Russian military base, illegally deployed in the occupied Abkhazia. David Basharuli, Giga Otkhozoria and Archil Tatunashvili are the names of those Georgians, who had been deprived of their lives in previous years. The most alarming is that perpetrators of these hideous crimes remain unpunished. Such kind of approach fuels the sense of impunity, and furthermore, encourages violence against ethnic Georgian population” – the Minister said. Documents Similar News
