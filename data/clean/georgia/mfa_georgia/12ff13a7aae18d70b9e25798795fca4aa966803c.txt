26 July 2022 On 26 July 2022, within the framework of the visit of the Georgian Prime Minister, Irakli Gharibashvili to the Republic of Kazakhstan, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the Deputy Prime Minister of Kazakhstan, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mukhtar Tileuberdi. The sides discussed the issues relating to cooperation between Georgia and Kazakhstan in bilateral and multilateral formats. They emphasized the traditionally friendly relations between the two countries and the positive dynamics of cooperation, including in the fields of trade and economics, transport, tourism and culture. Special attention was paid to the need of increasing the efficiency and capacity of the Trans-Caspian Corridor. The ministers noted with satisfaction cooperation within the framework of international organizations, including in terms of supporting their candidacies in international structures. The sides also talked about the development of cooperation between the foreign ministries of the two countries. In this context, they emphasized the effectiveness of the mechanism of political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of the two countries. The Ministers agreed that the next round of political consultations will be held by the end of 2022, in Georgia. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked the Government of Kazakhstan for its firm support of Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
