30 November 2021 At the invitation of the North Atlantic Alliance, the Vice Prime Minister /Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani, together with his delegation, will take part in the Meeting of NATO Ministers of Foreign Affairs to be held in Riga, on 30 November and 1 December. Within the framework of the Ministerial Meeting, the Georgian Foreign Minister together with his Ukrainian counterpart will take part in the session of the North Atlantic Council, where David Zalkaliani will deliver a speech. On the sidelines of the NATO Ministerial, David Zalkaliani will also hold bilateral meetings with the foreign ministers of the NATO Member States. The security environment in the Black Sea region and the recent development of events will be key topics on the agenda of the Meeting of NATO Ministers of Foreign Affairs in Riga. The allies will hear the visions of the aspirant countries’ foreign ministers regarding regional security and will take stock of their contribution to ensuring regional and global security. Within the framework of the Ministerial Meeting, the participants will also discuss the steps taken by Georgia and Ukraine on their path to NATO membership. Within the framework of the Ministerial Meeting, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia, will take part in the ''NATO’s outlook towards 2030 and beyond'' conference.
