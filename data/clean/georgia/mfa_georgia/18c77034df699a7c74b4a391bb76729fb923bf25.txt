1 March 2019 1.03.2019 – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani participated in the event held on the occasion of the National Day of the Republic of Bulgaria. Speaking before the attending audience, Zalkaliani said that Georgia and Bulgaria share long history of traditionally close and friendly relations. Over the years, these bilateral relations have been steadily growing and leading to what is today a strong bond of friendship and vibrant cooperation in many areas of common interest and active dynamics of high-level visits. Zalkaliani put special accent on the importance of the Black Sea region. Georgia and Bulgaria are connected geographically – the Black Sea builds a natural bridge between us, offering unique opportunities and bearing huge potential, that we are committed to exploit, especially in the fields of transport and energy. The Black Sea is increasingly becoming the interest of the EU. In this regard, I’d like to highlight the role of the Black Sea as the sea of prosperity and development” – Zalkaliani said. The Minister expressed his appreciation for Bulgaria’s strong support towards Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and input in the process of European and Euro-Atlantic integration of Georgia. He congratulated the Bulgarian Embassy for successfully combining the function of NATO contact point Embassy during 2016-2018.
