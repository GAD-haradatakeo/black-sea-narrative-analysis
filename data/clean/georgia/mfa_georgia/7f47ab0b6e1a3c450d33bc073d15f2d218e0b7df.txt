The EU-Georgia Association subcommittee on economic and sectoral cooperation has met in its 7th session 4 December 2023 On 4 December, the 7th session of the first thematic group of the EU-Georgia Association subcommittee on economic and sectoral cooperation - ‘"economic dialogue, public finance management and financial control; statistics; financial services; accounting and auditing; fighting and controlling fraud was held in Tbilisi. From the Georgian side, the meeting was headed by Davit Bujiashvili, director of the Department of EU Assistance Coordination and Sectoral Integration of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and from the EU side – by Bettina Croman, the head of Regional Eastern Partnership, Regional Cooperation and OSCE of the European External Action Service, on the EU side.
