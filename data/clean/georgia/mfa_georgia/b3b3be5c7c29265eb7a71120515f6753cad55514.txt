12 September 2022 On 12 September 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Khatuna Totladze held a meeting with the Secretary of State for Asylum and Migration of the Kingdom of Belgium, Nicole de Moor. Discussing the issues related to visa-free travel of Georgian citizens to the EU and Schengen area, the sides emphasized the importance of free movement in the process of Georgia's European integration and the need for close cooperation between the two countries in the fight against illegal migration. Khatuna Totladze provided the participants of the meeting with detailed information about the measures taken by the Government of Georgia, including by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, to promote legal migration, as well as to raise the awareness of Georgian citizens regarding visa-free travel. The Belgian Secretary of State for Asylum and Migration positively assessed the activities of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in this direction.
