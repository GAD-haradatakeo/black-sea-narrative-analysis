8 October 2017 8.10.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with his Ukrainian counterpart, Elmar Mammadyarov Pavlo Klimkin, within the framework of the special meeting of the GUAM Council of Foreign Ministers. The Ministers welcomed the successful co-operation within the organization confirming the importance of this format in terms of outlining plans for the future. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral relations highlighting the importance of exchanging high-level visits.
