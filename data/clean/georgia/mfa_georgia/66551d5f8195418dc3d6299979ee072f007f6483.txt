8 September 2022 On 8 September 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a telephone conversation with his Czech counterpart, Jan Lipavský. The sides focused on the importance of supporting Georgia's European integration process and the Czech presidency. The Ministers also talked about the European Commission’s decision to grant Georgia a European perspective and the importance of fulfilling the recommendations of the European Commission. Ilia Darchiashvili informed his Czech counterpart about the steps taken by Georgia in this direction. The importance of the European Union’s unified approach to the associated countries was highlighted once again. The sides discussed the results of the EU-Georgia Association Council held in Brussels, on 6 September. Ilia Darchiashvili and Jan Lipavský also discussed the agenda of Georgian-Czech cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats.
