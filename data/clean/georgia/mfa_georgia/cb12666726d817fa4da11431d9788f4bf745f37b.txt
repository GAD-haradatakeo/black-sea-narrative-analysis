28 May 2018 28.05.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Minister of Education and Science Lia Gigauri and a representative of the Ministry, Maia Melikidze highlighted the reforms ongoing in the education system during the meeting with representatives of the thematic working group of the Diaspora Forum. Special attention was paid to the issues relating to teaching the Georgian language. The representatives of the Ministry of Education held a presentation titled “Georgian Language Integrated Programme and Textbooks for Sunday Schools”. According to Maia Melikidze, “It is important that foreign-educated Georgian students return to Georgia and that Georgia becomes for them a centre of science and education”. Special note was taken of the importance of strengthening Georgian Sunday Schools abroad. Along with the Education Ministry, presentations were also offered by the National Center for Teacher Professional Development, the Georgian Cultural Centre and the Madrid Sunday School.
