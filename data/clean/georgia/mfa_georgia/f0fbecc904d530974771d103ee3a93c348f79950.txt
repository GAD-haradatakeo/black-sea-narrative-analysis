4 June 2018 04.06.2018, Tbilisi – On 4 June 2018, the Italian Foreign Minister attended the reception on the occasion of the the National Day of Italy. The event was organized by the Embassy of Italy in Georgia. Highlighting the close bilateral relations between Georgia and Italy Mikheil Janelidze congratulated the Italian people on their national holiday “Italy is one of the leading member states’ of the European Union, a model and key partner for Georgia. Georgia has much to thank Italy for! Italy took a strong stand in support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and our non-recognition policy towards occupied regions” – Mikheil Janelidze said. Highlighting Georgia’s aspiration to Euro-Atlantic structures, the Georgian Foreign Minister said that “Today Georgia with its very strong ambition towards European and Euro-Atlantic integration tries to find its well-deserved place in the European family. The Minister also spoke about the importance of the high-level visits between the two countries, the activated economic cooperation between Georgia and Italy and already existing concrete tangible results. He also paid special attention to the significance of the events held in Italy and Georgia within the framework of the centenary of the First Democratic Republic of Georgia The reception dedicated to the National Day of Italy was attended by representatives of the executive and legislative branches of the government and members of the diplomatic corps accredited to Georgia and other invited guests.
