3 September 2021 On 3 September 2021, the Ambassadors of the Philippines, Uzbekistan and Jordan, Maria Elena Algabre, Bakhrom Ashrafkhanov and Sami Asem Ghosheh presented copies of their credentials to the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili. At a meeting held following the presentation ceremony, Alexander Khvtisiashvili congratulated the ambassadors on their appointment and wished them success in their activities. At the meetings, the sides discussed topical issues of bilateral and multilateral relations. The focus was on the exchange of high-level visits and the need for bilateral political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs. The need to intensify trade and economic ties, as well as cooperation in the field of education and culture was also stressed. The Deputy Foreign Minister thanked the Ambassadors for their support of Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
