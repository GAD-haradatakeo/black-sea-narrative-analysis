14 February 2022 The Vice Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani spoke over the phone to his Azerbaijani counterpart, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Jeyhun Bayramov, on 14 February 2022. The Ministers discussed the ongoing processes in the region, exchanged information on the situation in Georgia and Azerbaijan. The conversation also focused on various issues of cooperation between the two countries. Jeyhun Bayramov inquired about the consequences of the earthquake that hit Georgia on 13 February and expressed his readiness for assistance, if necessary.
