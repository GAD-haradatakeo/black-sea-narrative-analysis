13 November 2023 On 10 November 2023, in Hanoi, the second round of political consultations was held between the ministries of foreign affairs of Georgia and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. From the side of Georgia, the consultations were led by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, and from the side of Vietnam, by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Le Thi Thu Hang. Within the framework of consultations, the sides reviewed the wide range of existing relations between the two countries and prospects for the development of cooperation in various fields. Special attention was paid to the exchange of high-level visits, deepening of parliamentary ties and development of the legal framework; The importance of deepening relations in trade-economic, investment, cultural, tourism, agriculture, transport and logistics areas was also emphasized. Discussions also focused on the importance of cooperation in multilateral formats, including on opportunities for mutual support within international organizations. During the consultations, the sides discussed the current developments in the South Caucasus and South-East Asia. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili thanked the Vietnamese side for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia within its internationally recognized borders and shared information about the current situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. As part of the visit to Hanoi, the delegation had an meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, Bui Tan Son.
