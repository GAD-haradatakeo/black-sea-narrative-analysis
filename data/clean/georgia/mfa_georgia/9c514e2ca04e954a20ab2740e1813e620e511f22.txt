27 May 2019 27.05.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held an official reception within the framework of the annual forum “Strong Diaspora for United Georgia” dedicated to the Diaspora Day. The forum was attended by representatives of the Georgian government, parliament and other government agencies. Present were also 120 Diaspora representatives from 25 countries. David Zalkaliani spoke before the attending audience. He thanked Diaspora representatives for their efforts and said that meetings with Georgian communities are one of the key priorities during his visits abroad. According to the Minister, the unity of Diaspora representatives is essentially important for overcoming the challenges faced by Georgia. “Together we’ll overcome the problems and challenges coming in our way. Our fight will continue until our territories are occupied and actual annexation is ongoing. Unity has never been as important as it is now. And equally as important is the consolidation of the nation, and our involvement and support” - Zalkaliani said. He also spoke extensively about the process of Georgia’s European integration and the tangible results achieved on this path.
