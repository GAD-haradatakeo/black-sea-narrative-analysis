11 December 2023 On 11 December 2023, another round of consular consultations was held between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Türkiye. The Georgian delegation was headed by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Khatuna Totladze, and the Turkish delegation - by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Yasin Ekrem. All current issues on the agenda of consular relations were discussed within the framework of consular consultations. The sides emphasized the existing friendship, fruitful cooperation and close partnership between the two countries. They also expressed the hope that the current dynamics will be maintained in the future and successful steps will be taken jointly towards the strengthening of human rights protection, promotion of legal migration, and deepening of people-to-people contacts.
