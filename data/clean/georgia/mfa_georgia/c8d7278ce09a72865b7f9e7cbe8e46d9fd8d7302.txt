31 May 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns the terrorist act committed on 31 May 2017 in Kabul, the capital of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan leaving over 80 people dead and over 350 - wounded. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism and violence in all their forms and attaches particular importance of the international community’s joint efforts against violent extremism. Georgia supports the Afghan Government’s efforts aimed at building a stable, peaceful and democratic state. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the government of Afghanistan and the families of terror victims, wishing speedy recovery to those who have been injured in the attack.
