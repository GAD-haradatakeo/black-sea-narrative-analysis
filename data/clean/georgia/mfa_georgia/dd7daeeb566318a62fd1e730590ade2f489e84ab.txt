29 September 2021 Within the framework of his official visit to the Republic of Korea, on 29 September, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, met with the accompanying delegation, the Deputy Minister of Trade, Industry and Energy of the Republic of Korea. The sides discussed the possibility of opening negotiations on a free trade agreement between the two countries. The deputy ministers stressed the successful completion of the feasibility study by the relevant agencies of Georgia and Korea in September. Alexander Khvtisiashvili thanked the Korean side for the progress made in this direction and expressed the hope that the two countries will move to the next stage of negotiations in the near future.
