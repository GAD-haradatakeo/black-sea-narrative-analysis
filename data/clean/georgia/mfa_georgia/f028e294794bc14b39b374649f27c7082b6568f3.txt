8 July 2020 On 8 July 2020, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a farewell meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Lithuania to Georgia, Giedrius Puodžiūnas. The Minister thanked the Ambassador for his contribution to strengthening the relations between Georgia and Lithuania. Conversations also included many high-level visits and events conducted both in Georgia and Lithuania. The Minister noted with satisfaction Lithuania’s and the Ambassador’s support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and the practical assistance rendered by Lithuania in Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. The sides discussed the COVID-19 situation in Georgia and Lithuania. The Minister welcomed the recent EU decision to lift travel restrictions from Georgia, along with 14 other countries. According to him, this fact is a further proof of the EU’s firm support for Georgia. At the end of the meeting, the Minister wished the Ambassador success in his future Documents Similar News
