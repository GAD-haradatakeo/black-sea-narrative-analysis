1 November 2022 Speaking at the local economic development forum held under the name "Sustainable Economic Recovery", Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vladimir Konstantinidi paid special attention to the importance of involving Georgian citizens living abroad in the process of the country's economic development. According to the Deputy Minister, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, together with partner international organizations, pays special attention to active communication with representatives of the Georgian diaspora, ensuring a close connection of compatriots living abroad with their homeland. "Every year, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosts business forums and economic events to familiarize diaspora representatives with the investment environment of Georgia and to provide updated information on Doing Business in Georgia" - Vladimir Konstantinidi is quoted a saying.
