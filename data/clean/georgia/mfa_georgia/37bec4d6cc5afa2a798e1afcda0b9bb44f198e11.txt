19 March 2020 19.03.2020 - Speaking at a press briefing following the meeting of the Prime Minister-led Inter-Agency Coordination Council, Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani provided the media with information on the Georgian citizen, who recently died from coronavirus in Spain. David Zalkaliani expressed his deep sorrow over this fact conveying his condolences to the family of the deceased. “The deceased was elderly, with underlying health conditions. I wish to convey my sincere condolences to her family” – Zalkaliani said. According to the Minister, the dead body cannot be transported to Georgia under previously established rules and the transportation in this case will be subject to WHO Protocol on Dead Body Management. He added that the Georgian consulate in Spain remains in contact with the family members of the deceased. The Georgian citizen - elderly woman died at 73 from coronavirus in Spain.
