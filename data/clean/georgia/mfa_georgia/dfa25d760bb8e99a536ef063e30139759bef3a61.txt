6 August 2021 Within the framework of the Government Commission for elaboration and implementation of Strategy for de-occupation and sustainable peaceful conflict resolution, the First Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia visited the Samegrelo Region. Lasha Darsalia held meetings with representatives of civil society organizations working on conflict issues, local NGOs and experts, as well as local IDPs.
