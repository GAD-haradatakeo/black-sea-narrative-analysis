16 February 2018 17.02.2017, Munich – The Georgian delegation led by Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili is due to take part in the in the 54th Munich Security Conference from 16 to 18 February 2018. Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze is among the members of the delegation. Mikheil Janelidze will hold bilateral meetings with his Japense, Italian, Norwegian, Moldovan, Liechtensteinian ad Czech counterparts. He will also meet with the Vice President of the Bundestag, Assistant Secretary of State for Energy, Federal Chancellor's foreign and security policy adviser, Chair of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the National People's Congress of China, representatives of the international think tanks, others. While in Munich, the Vice Prime Minister will take part in the security conference event "Georgian politics and literature” dedicated to Georgia.
