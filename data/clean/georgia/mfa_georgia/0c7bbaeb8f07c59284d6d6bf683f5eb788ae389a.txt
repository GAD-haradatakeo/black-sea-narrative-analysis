5 November 2019 5.11.2019, Sweden – A trilateral meeting was held between the Georgian, Ukrainian and Moldovan Foreign Ministers within the framework of the trilateral meeting to mark the 10th anniversary of Eastern Partnership. Discussions focused on cooperation between the three EaP partners, in the light of the upcoming summit. Reaffirming the EU aspirations of the three countries, the Ministers underscored the need to set new ambitious goals within Eastern Partnership, including by opening up new vistas for greater economic and sectoral integration with the EU that will contribute to Georgia’s integration into the EU single market.
