21 October 2025 Within the framework of the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum, the Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia, Nikol Pashinyan, arrived in Georgia. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, welcomed the Prime Minister of Armenia at Tbilisi International Airport. Nikol Pashinyan was accompanied by the Armenian delegation. During the visit, a meeting is scheduled between the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze, and the Prime Minister of the Republic of Armenia, Nikol Pashinyan.
