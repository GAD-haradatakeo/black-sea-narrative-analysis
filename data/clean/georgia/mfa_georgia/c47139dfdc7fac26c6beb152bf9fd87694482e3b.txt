28 November 2017 28.11.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze made a comment on the Russian government’s decision to incorporate the so-called troop formations of the occupied region. According to the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, “the incorporation of the armed forces of Georgia’s breakaway regions into the Russian military’s command structure is the continuation of the annexation policy by the Kremlin. "This is yet another illegal step by the Russian Federation, which contradicts international law, including the international commitments undertaken by Russia, and it is yet another step towards the factual annexation of Georgia’s occupied regions, aiming to subordinate and control them”. Janelidze urged the international community to react to this action by Russia. He also stressed that the Government of Georgia is doing its best to settle the conflict in a peaceful manner and is using all international levers at its disposal. "It is important that Russian troops leave the territory of the sovereign country – the occupied regions of Georgia – in order to create an opportunity for a peaceful resolution of the conflict”, Janelidze said.
