25 July 2023 On 25 July the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, started a visit to the Republic of Bulgaria. On the first day of the visit, the Deputy Minister held a meeting with Foreign Policy Secretary of the President of the Republic of Bulgaria, Roussi Ivanov. The sides talked about current issues on the bilateral agenda and noted the importance of exchanging high-level visits which has a positive impact on the further deepening of the political dialogue. Special attention was paid to Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. The Bulgarian side expressed its support for the implementation of the foreign-policy priorities.
