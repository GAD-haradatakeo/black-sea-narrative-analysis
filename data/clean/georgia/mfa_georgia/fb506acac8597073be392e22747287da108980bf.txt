24 February 2026 During her visit to Geneva, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kyrgyz Republic, Zheenbek Kulubaev. At the meeting, the Ministers discussed current issues in bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Kyrgyzstan. Emphasis was placed on the importance of exchanging high-level visits, the prospects for developing trade and economic partnership, the significance of the Middle Corridor for both countries and opportunities for cooperation across various sectors.
