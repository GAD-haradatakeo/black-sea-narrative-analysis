21 December 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili participated in the 40th session of the GUAM Council of Ministers of Foreign Affairs, which was held on 20 December 2022, via video conference. The session was chaired by the Vice Prime Minister of the Republic of Moldova, Minister of Foreign Affairs and European Integration Niku Popescu. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine Dmytro Kuleba, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan Elnur Mammadov and the GUAM Secretary General of Altai Efendiyev also took part in it. The Ministers hailed Moldova's successful chairmanship in 2022, despite challenges. Amid the ongoing war in Ukraine, the outstanding contribution of the GUAM Secretariat to the continuous functioning of the organization and the smooth operation of the GUAM working groups was highlighted. gainst the background of the existing challenges, the meeting focused on the importance of strengthening regional cooperation and coordinated interaction of the GUAM member states within the framework of international platforms. The sides highlighted the priority of the transport, connectivity and energy fields and the importance of developing the GUAM transport corridor. Within the framework of the meeting, coordinators of the GUAM working groups for the years 2023-2024 were approved.
