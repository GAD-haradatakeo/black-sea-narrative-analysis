18 March 2024 NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg visited Georgia on 18 March 2024. The official welcome ceremony for the NATO Secretary General took part at the Tbilisi Shota Rustaveli International Airport. The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili took part in the ceremony. As noted at the welcome ceremony, the visit of the NATO Secretary General to Georgia once again confirms the special relations and high level of cooperation between NATO and Georgia and emphasizes the strong support of the Alliance to Georgia.
