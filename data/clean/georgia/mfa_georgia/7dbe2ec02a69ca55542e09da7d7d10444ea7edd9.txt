25 January 2020 25.01.2020, U.S. - Following the meeting of the U.S.-Georgia Strategic Partnership Commission Working Group on People-to-People and Cultural Exchanges, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili and the Georgian Ambassador to the U.S., David Bakradze met with the Assistant Secretaryof State for Educational and Cultural Affairs, Marie Royce. The parties discussed the issues relating to close partnership between the two countries including in such areas as education, culture, civil integration and the need to protect the rights of the people living in the occupied territories.
