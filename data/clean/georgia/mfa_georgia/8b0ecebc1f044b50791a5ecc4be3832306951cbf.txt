10 April 2025 Maka Botchorishvili will join Prime Minister Irakli Kobakhidze on an official visit to the Republic of Türkiye, where they will participate in the Antalya Diplomacy Forum. The event is hosted by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Türkiye and will be officially opened by the President of Türkiye, Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. The forum will be attended by leaders from various countries. The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, is scheduled to deliver a speech during the session titled “Challenges and Opportunities for Regional Cooperation in the South Caucasus.” Within the framework of the forum, Botchorishvili will also hold bilateral meetings with fellow foreign ministers and representatives of international organizations.
