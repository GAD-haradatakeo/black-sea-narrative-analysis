10 December 2019 10.12.2019 Tbilisi – Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia David Zalkaliani participated in the event held on the occasion of the National Day of Qatar. Minister delivered a speech for the audience, strongly accentuating close partnership established between the two countries. The Foreign Minister highlighted that Qatar was the first of the Gulf countries to launch the Embassy in Georgia, whereas the Embassy of Georgia in Qatar was established a year earlier in 2012. David Zalkaliani also spoke about the augmenting dynamics of cooperation between Georgia and Qatar. “I would like to emphasize the fact that relations between our two states are outstanding in terms of growing dynamics in case of the Foreign Ministries, as well as a number of different agencies. The core directions of our bilateral agenda include enhancement of cooperation with Qatar in the fields of politics, economy, culture and interpersonal ties.” – noted David Zalkaliani. The Minister focused on the active cooperation between the two countries with regards to the field of tourism and expressed hope that the close partnership will become even more consolidated in future.
