21 March 2020 Georgia makes every effort to assist those Georgian citizens, whose ability to move freely or return to their country is hindered by the spread of coronavirus across the world, according to the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani. According to him, Georgian Airways will soon provide a commercial flight to Berlin, and other directions will be further agreed. “We are well aware of the fact that many of our citizens are faced with problems. It is also true, however, that through the efforts of the Government of Georgia and with the assistance of our Foreign Ministry, an increasing number of Georgian citizens were able to return to their country. We managed to arrange for charter flights from Italy, China, Iran, Poland, Bulgaria, and many of our compatriots returned to Georgia on the government-organized or supported flights. Georgian embassies and consulates kept consulting and helping our compatriots in finding alternate ways to return to the country. The situation is really difficult and extraordinary, the like of which has never been seen before. Given this, we are doing our utmost to make movement easier for our citizens. As far as you know we have already arranged several flights from Dubai, Minsk and a number of European cities, and another flight will be performed from Berlin soon. We keep coordinating and communicating with the Georgian Airlines to determine other flights in the coming days” – Zalkaliani said. Documents Similar News
