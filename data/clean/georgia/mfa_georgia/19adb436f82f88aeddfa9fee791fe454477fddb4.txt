7 May 2018 7.05.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participated in the event dedicated to the Days of Europe at the European School. Highlighting the priorities of Georgia’s EU integration and the importance of education in this process, the Minister paid special attention to European values that are based on freedom the rule of law, democracy and human rights. “Quality education is the cornerstone and an essential prerequisite for sustainable development. We want the school education, as well as higher and vocational education to be based on the best international practices. I am happy that the European school represents one of the role models of European development. The Director of the European School, Sopio Bazadze also spoke before the participants of the event stating that the aim of the school is to help students become globally thinking international citizens able to benefit from the culture and traditions of various countries. The Days of Europe are held for the 8th time at the European School, which is currently attended by students of 23 various nationalities.
