8 September 2017 8.09.2017, Tallinn – As part of his visit to Tallinn, the Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with his Estonian counterpart, Foreign Minister Sven Mikser. Mikheil Janelidze thanked the Estonian side for the invitation of the foreign ministers of the Partnership countries to participate in the informal meeting of the EU Member States, thus giving them the opportunity to discuss issues relating to the preparation of Eastern Partnership Summit scheduled to take place in Brussels, in November of 2017. Mikheil Janelidze thanked his colleague for Estonia’s support of Georgia’s European integration, to which further attests the upcoming visit of the President of Estonia to Georgia scheduled for November 2017. The Ministers spoke about co-operation between the two countries in various areas including in e-government, information technologies, tax system reforms.
