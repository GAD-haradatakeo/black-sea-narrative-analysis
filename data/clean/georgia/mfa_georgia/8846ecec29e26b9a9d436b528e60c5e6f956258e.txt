17 September 2019 17.09.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili held a meeting with Titus Corlatean and Claude Kern, co-rapporteurs of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe (PACE) for the monitoring of obligations and commitments by Georgia. Vakhtang Makharoblishvili provided the co-rapporteurs with information on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. Paying special attention to the recent development of events on the ground and Russia’s illegal activities, he highlighted the need of providing international monitoring organizations with unhindered access to the occupied territories. The First Deputy Foreign Minister thanked the PACE co-rapporteurs for their statements condemning Russia’s illegal actions in Georgia’s occupied territories. The importance of keeping the Russian-Georgian conflict on the CoE agenda was also emphasized during the meeting.
