Information regarding the meetings held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Spain 10 May 2024 During his working visit to the Kingdom of Spain, Teimuraz Janjalia, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, conducted meetings with Fernando Sampedro, the Spanish Secretary of State for European Union Affairs, and Diego Martínez Belio, the Secretary of State for Foreign and Global Affairs, at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Spain.
