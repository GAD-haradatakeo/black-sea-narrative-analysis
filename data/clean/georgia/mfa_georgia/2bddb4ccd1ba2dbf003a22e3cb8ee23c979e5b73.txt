7 December 2017 7.12.2017, Vienna - Within the framework of the 24th OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with OSCE Secretary General Thomas Greminger. It was Mikheil Janelidze’s first meeting with the new OSCE Secretary General. Cooperation between Georgia and the Organisation for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE), the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and the role played by OSCE in the peaceful resolution of conflict were the main topics of discussions at the meeting between Mikheil Janelidze and Thomas Greminger.
