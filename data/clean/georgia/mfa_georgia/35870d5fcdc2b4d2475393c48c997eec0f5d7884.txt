19 July 2017 19.07.2017, Tbilisi –Participants of Ambassadorial 2017 discussed the challenges related to Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and steps taken by the Government to overcome these challenges. State Minister of Georgia for Reconciliation and Civic Equality Ketevan Tskikhelashvili, Deputy Head of the State Security Service Alexandre Tabatadze, Chair of the Committee for Foreign Relations Sopio Katsarava and Chair of the Interim Commission on Territorial Integrity Gedevan Popkhadze spoke before the attending audience on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the Government’s efforts towards deterring Russia’s aggression and measures of confidence-building between the communities torn apart by the war.
