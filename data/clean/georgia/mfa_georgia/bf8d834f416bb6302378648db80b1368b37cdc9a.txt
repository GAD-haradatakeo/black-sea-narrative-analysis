13 April 2022 MFA of Georgia welcomes the decision of Yemeni President Abed Rabbu Mansour Hadi to transfer his powers to a new Presidential Leadership Council which is important step forward towards establishing peace and stability in the Yemeni Republic. We encourage all parties to abide by the UN-negotiated truce and advance inclusive intra Yemeni talks (dialogue) aimed at achieving durable political settlement to the conflict for the benefit of Yemeni people.
