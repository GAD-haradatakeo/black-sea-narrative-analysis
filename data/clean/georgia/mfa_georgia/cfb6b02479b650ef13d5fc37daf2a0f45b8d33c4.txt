7 October 2021 The UNECE Committee on Urban Development, Housing and Land Management held its 82nd session on “Affordable, adequate, and resilient housing in liveable cities, including cities, which face extreme weather conditions” in Geneva, on 6-8 October. The Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili took part in the Ministerial Meeting. In his statement, he focused on the Georgian Government’s efforts and the ongoing process to improve the legislative framework, in line with the Sustainable Development Goals, to improve housing infrastructure. Alexander Khvtisiashvili spoke in detail about the government programme "House in the Village", which aims to provide housing for IDPs. The head of the Georgian delegation focused on the ongoing processes in the country aimed at rehabilitation of roads, water supply systems and tourism infrastructure. Within the framework of the Ministerial meeting, the Deputy Foreign Minister met with the Executive Secretary of the UN Economic Commission for Europe, Olga Algaierova, to discuss current and future issues of cooperation between Georgia and UNECE, including joint projects on sustainable energy, urban development, resilient housing and road safety.
