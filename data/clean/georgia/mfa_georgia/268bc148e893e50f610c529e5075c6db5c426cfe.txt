30 November 2023 Within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Principality of Andorra, Imma Tor Faus. At the meeting, the sides discussed the prospects for deepening the relations between the two countries. In this regard, they emphasized the possibilities of strengthening tourism cooperation and people-to-people contacts. The ministers focused on the important challenges in the international arena and the importance of maintaining an active political dialogue with the European partners.
