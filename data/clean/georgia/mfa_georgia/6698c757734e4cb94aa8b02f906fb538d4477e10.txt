25 May 2018 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, Pavlo Klimkin congratulates the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze, on the occasion of the 27th anniversary of the restoration of independence and the 100th anniversary since the foundation of the first democratic republic of Georgia. According to Pavlo Klimkin, in view of the historical ties and parallels, these events are especially significant for my country. “Unfortunately, our nations continue to struggle against the aggressor who occupied some parts of our countries, but will not stop our joint efforts until we restore the territorial integrity of Ukraine and Georgia” – reads the letter.
