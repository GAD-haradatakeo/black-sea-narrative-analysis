17 February 2019 17.02.2019, Munich – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister ofForeign Affairsof theCzechRepublic Tomáš Petříček. The two ministers highlighted the excellent bilateral relations between Georgia and the Czech Republic both in bilateral and multilateral formats. David Zalkaliani thanked the Czech side for the support of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration path that has not only political but also practical dimension in the form of Czech assistance programme. The sides highlighted the need to mark the 10th anniversary of the Eastern Partnership. David Zalkaliani invited his Czech counterpart to the Batumi International Conference. Documents Similar News
