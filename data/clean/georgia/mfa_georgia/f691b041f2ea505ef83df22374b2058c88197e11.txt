14 April 2022 The High Representative of the EU for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy, Josep Borrell, the Council of Europe Secretary General, Marija Pejčinović Burić and the GUAM Secretary General, Altai Efendiev congratulate Ilia Darchiashvili on his appointment as Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. “I look forward to working closely together to advance Georgia’s reform agenda. The EU stands with Georgia, also in facing regional security challenges in the context of Russia’s aggression against Ukraine" Borrel twitted. "We are confident that your extensive professional experience and deep knowledge will best serve the national interests of Georgia in a time full of these challenges," GUAM Secretary General Altai Efendiev wrote in a letter to the newly appointed Minister. “I look forward to working with you on the challenges that Europe and the Council of Europe face today and wish you every success with your new mandate” – reads the letter of the Council of Europe Secretary General, Marija Pejčinović Burić addressed to Ilia Darchiashvili.
