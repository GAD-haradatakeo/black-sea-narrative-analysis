9 February 2019 07.02.2019, U.S. - Within the framework of his visit to the United States, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the U.S. special envoy forVenezuela Elliott Abrams. The U.S. Representative thanked the Georgian side for its support of the Venezuelan people. For his part, David Zalkaliani reaffirmed that Georgia’s position with respect to Venezuela will continue to be in line with the position of the U.S. -Georgia’s key strategic ally –and the civilized world. According to him, Georgia is closely following the developments in Venezuela and expresses the hope that the country will soon return to the scope of international law, after which Georgia and Venezuela will restore full-scale relations based on the principle of respect for sovereignty and territorial integrity of states.
