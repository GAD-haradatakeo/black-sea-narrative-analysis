18 February 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Secretary of State at the Federal Foreign Office of Germany, Susanne Baumann, within the framework of the Munich Security Conference. At the meeting, special attention was paid to the European integration process of Georgia. The sides noted the European Council’s historic decision of 14 December 2023 to grant Georgia candidate status. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed gratitude to Germany for its steadfast support of Georgia's European aspirations and underscored the importance of ongoing support from allies and partners, particularly Germany. The discussion also highlighted the robust and mutually beneficial partnership between Georgia and Germany, spanning various sectors. The multidimensional cooperation between the two nations across various domains was assessed positively. At the same time, attention was drawn to the importance of exchanging high-level visits.
