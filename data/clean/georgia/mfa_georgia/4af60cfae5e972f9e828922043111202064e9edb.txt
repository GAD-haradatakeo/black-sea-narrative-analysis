5 March 2025 On 5 March 2025, ambassadors-designate from the Republic of the Philippines, the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, the Federal Republic of Somalia, the Republic of Burundi, and the Republic of Mali, accredited to Georgia, presented copies of their Letters of Credence to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Khatuna Totladze. Following the ceremony of presenting the copies of their Letters of Credence, discussions were held regarding the issues on the cooperation agenda between the countries and the potential for developing bilateral relations in various fields. The parties confirmed their readiness to contribute to the deepening of partnerships for the benefit of mutual interests. The Deputy Minister wished the ambassadors success in their future endeavors.
