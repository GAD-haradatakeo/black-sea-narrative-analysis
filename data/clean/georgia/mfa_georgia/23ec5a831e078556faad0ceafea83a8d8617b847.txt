6 October 2018 06.10.2018, Moldova – Within the framework of the Georgian Prime Minister Mamuka Bakhtadze’s visit to the Republic of Moldova, Georgiaand Moldovasignedanagreement on exchangeand protection of classified information. The document was signed by the Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and the Minister of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of the Republic of Moldova, Tudor Ulianovschi. The Georgian Prime Minister Mamuka Bakhtadze paid a visit to Moldova on 5 October. Within the framework of the official visit he participated in the meeting of the Heads of State of the GUAM Member States. Mamuka Bakhtadze held a meeting with the Prime Minister of Moldova Pavel Filip. The Georgian delegation led by the Prime Minister included the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani and the Minister of Economy and Sustainable Development, Giorgi Kobulia.
