David Zalkaliani and David Sergeenko have presented Georgia’s candidate to Regional Director of the World Health Organization (WHO) 29 March 2019 29.03.2019, Tbilisi - At a meetingwiththe diplomatic corps accredited to Georgia, Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and Minister of Labour, Health and Social AffairsDavid Sergeenko presented Nata Menabde as Georgia’s candidate to Regional Director of the World Health Organization (WHO).
