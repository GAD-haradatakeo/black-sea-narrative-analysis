9 December 2025 On 9 December 2025, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with Sargis Khandanyan, Chair of the Foreign Relations Committee of the National Assembly of Armenia, who is visiting Georgia, along with the accompanying delegation. The parties noted the positive development of the multidimensional strategic partnership between Georgia and Armenia and highlighted the prospects for further advancing bilateral cooperation. Attention was focused on the important role of parliamentary diplomacy. The parties also noted the active dynamics of high-level visits, which attest to the positive trends shaping the bilateral agenda. Emphasis was placed on the importance of establishing lasting peace and stability in the region, as well as on Georgia’s readiness to contribute to strengthening security, cooperation and development across the region.
