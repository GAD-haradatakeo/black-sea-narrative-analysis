16 June 2021 On 16 June the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with the German Ambassador to Georgia, Hubert Knirsch. During the meeting the parties emphasised the bilateral agenda, discussing the strengthening of cooperation within frameworks of international organisations. The parties also discussed the existing situation in the region, including the issues of facilitation between Azerbaijan and Armenia, resulting in a significant humanitarian act. This was a step taken towards peace and stability in the region.
