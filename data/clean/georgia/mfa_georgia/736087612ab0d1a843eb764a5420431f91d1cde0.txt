26 April 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili will pay an official visit to the Republic of Azerbaijan on 26 and 27April. During the visit, the Foreign Minister will hold meetings with the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Ilham Aliyev, Prime Minister Ali Asadov, Speaker of the Milli Mejlis Sahiba Gaparova and the Minister of Foreign Affairs Jeyhun Bayramov.
