2 July 2021 On 2 July 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia held a meeting with the HeadofEUDelegation Carl Hartzell. The sides discussed the dynamics of Georgia-EU bilateral dynamics. Highlighting the importance of further deepening Georgia-EU cooperation, the sides paid special attention to the need to further strengthen connectivity and fully realize the Black Sea infrastructural and economic potential, for which the EU strives to further intensify cooperation, including by identifying and implementing concrete strategic projects. The Sides also spoke about cooperation within the framework of Eastern Partnership. Special note, in terms of further strengthening the partnership, was taken of the importance of the summit scheduled for December 2021. The sides exchanged views over the pandemic situation in Georgia. The Georgian side expressed its interest in joining the EU digital Covid certification system. The Deputy Minister also highlighted the possibility of adding Georgia to the EU list of third countries whose citizens are allowed to travel without restrictions to the EU/Schengen area.
