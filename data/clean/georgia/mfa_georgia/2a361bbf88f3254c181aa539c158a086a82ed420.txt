13 June 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its concern over the missile attack carried out on the Abha International Airport of Saudi Arabia leaving 26 people injured. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism and extremism in all their forms and maintains that this fact may cause further escalation of the conflict that will negatively affect the difficult situation in the region. Wishing speedy recovery to those injured in the attack, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs expresses the hope that such incidents will be avoided in the future through the joint efforts of the international community.
