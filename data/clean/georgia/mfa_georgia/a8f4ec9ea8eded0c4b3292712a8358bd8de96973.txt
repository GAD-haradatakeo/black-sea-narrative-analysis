16 April 2021 On 16 April 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of India to Georgia K. D. Dewal (residing in Yerevan). The sides highlighted the increasing dynamic of bilateral relations between the two countries, including India’s commitment to cooperation amid the pandemic. Talking points also included the meetings held by the Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili during his visit to the Republic of India in March. Alexander Khvtisiashvili briefed the Indian Ambassador on the Georgian Government’s measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 and the implications of these measures for the entry to and stay in Georgia of foreign nationals, including Indian nationals.
