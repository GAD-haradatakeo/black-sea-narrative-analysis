14 November 2025 On 14 November 2025, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Zurabashvili, held a meeting with the Boglárka Illés, the State Secretary for Bilateral Relations at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary. The First Deputy Minister welcomed the participation of the Hungarian delegation in the ministerial meeting dedicated to the Agreement on Strategic Partnership in the Field of Green Energy Development and Transmission, which is being held in Georgia. The close partnership and active dynamics of cooperation between the two countries were highly appreciated. In this context, the discussion touched upon recent high-level bilateral visits, as well as prospects for the further deepening of sectoral cooperation. The meeting also addressed the current state of relations between Georgia and the European Union. Gratitude was expressed for Hungary’s position regarding Georgia’s key priorities, including its support for Georgia’s European integration process at both the political and expert levels.
