27 August 2025 On 27 August 2025, Ambassador Khatia Tsilosani, Permanent Representative of Georgia, presented her credentials to the Secretary General of the Council of Europe, Alain Berset. Following the credential presentation ceremony, Ambassador Tsilosani expressed her gratitude to the Secretary General for his firm and unwavering support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. She noted that this support is clearly reflected in the Secretary General’s consolidated reports and in the decisions of the Committee of Ministers concerning the conflict in Georgia. “As a genuine member of the European family, Georgia stands fully prepared to continue an active and constructive dialogue with the Council of Europe and to contribute to the strengthening of human rights, democracy, and the rule of law, while safeguarding the country’s best interests,” stated Ambassador Tsilosani. The Secretary General reaffirmed both the Council of Europe’s and his personal commitment to further deepening and strengthening the successful cooperation with Georgia.
