10 September 2025 On 10 September 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Kvtisiashvili, held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Thailand to Georgia (residing in Ankara), Apeerat Sugondabiro. During the meeting, the parties reviewed current issues in Georgia-Thailand relations and explored opportunities for future cooperation. The significance of exchanging high-level visits, maintaining an active pace of meetings, and continuing regular dialogue was emphasized. Attention was drawn to the potential for further deepening economic and sectoral cooperation between the two countries, as well as the importance of establishing direct flights. The Ambassador expressed gratitude to the Deputy Minister for the fruitful cooperation and underscored the friendly relations existing between the two nations. At the conclusion of the meeting, Deputy Minister Khvtisiashvili expressed appreciation for the Ambassador’s contributions to strengthening Georgia-Thailand relations during his diplomatic tenure and wished him success in his future endeavors.
