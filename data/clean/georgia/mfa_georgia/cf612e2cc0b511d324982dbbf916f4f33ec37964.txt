10 February 2026 On 10 February 2026, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, held a meeting with Chea Vandeth, Minister of Post and Telecommunications of the Kingdom of Cambodia. During the meeting, the parties discussed opportunities to deepen bilateral dialogue, as well as the prospects for enhanced cooperation in trade, economic development and tourism. Emphasis was placed on the importance of exchanging high-level visits. Alexander Khvtisiashvili expressed gratitude to the Government of Cambodia for its support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders. It is noteworthy that, during its visit to Tbilisi, the Cambodian delegation will also hold meetings at the Ministry of Economy and Sustainable Development, the Ministry of Justice and the Revenue Service of Georgia.
