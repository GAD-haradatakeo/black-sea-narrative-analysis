LEPL Digital Governance Agency conducted training sessions for the electronic system on the "Communication Strategy of the Government of Georgia on Georgia's Membership in the EU and NATO for 2021-2025" 15 December 2023 On 13-14 December, with the organization of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the assistance of the EU funded project "Facility for the Implementation of the EU-Georgia Association Agreement - II", LEPL Digital Governance Agency conducted training sessions for the electronic system on the "Communication Strategy of the Government of Georgia on Georgia's Membership in the EU and NATO for 2021-2025".
