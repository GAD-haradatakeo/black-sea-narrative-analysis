17 December 2025 On 17 December 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, delivered a speech at the reception dedicated to the National Day of the State of Qatar. On behalf of the Government and the people of Georgia, Khvtisiashvili congratulated the Qatari people on their National Day and wished them peace, prosperity and continued progress. He noted that relations between Georgia and the State of Qatar are dynamic and steadily growing. He emphasized the importance of high-level political dialogue between the two countries, the active engagement of the embassies in Tbilisi and Doha, bilateral visits and political consultations, all of which play a significant role in deepening Georgia-Qatar relations. The Deputy Minister also highlighted people-to-people ties, including the growth of tourism and visitor flows, facilitated by the direct air connection between the two countries. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed his gratitude to the Ambassador of Qatar and the entire embassy team for their efforts in strengthening the friendly ties between the two nations and conveyed hope that the partnership between Georgia and Qatar will continue to grow even stronger in the future.
