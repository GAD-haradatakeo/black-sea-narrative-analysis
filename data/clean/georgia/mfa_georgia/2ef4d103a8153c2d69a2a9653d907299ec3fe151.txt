29 April 2025 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with the Ambassador of Israel to Georgia, Hadas Meitzad. The meeting covered a broad range of topics related to the bilateral cooperation agenda. Discussions focused on the priority areas of relations between the two countries and prospects for their further development. The parties also exchanged information regarding ongoing developments and challenges in the Middle East region. At the conclusion of the meeting, both sides expressed their readiness to continue cooperation in implementing key issues on the bilateral agenda, including the organization of high-level visits.
