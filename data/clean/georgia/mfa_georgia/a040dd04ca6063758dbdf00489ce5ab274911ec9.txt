4 December 2025 Within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary, Péter Szijjártó. The parties reviewed the issues on the Georgia-Hungary bilateral relations agenda and prospects for further deepening the dialogue. Emphasis was placed on recent developments. The discussion also focused on Georgia-EU relations, with appreciation expressed for Hungary’s steadfast support of Georgia’s priorities. The importance of Hungary’s continued political and expert support in the process of European integration was emphasized.
