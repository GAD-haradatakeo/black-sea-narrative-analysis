24 September 2021 On 23 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the Republic of Rwanda, Vincent Biruta. The meeting focused on current issues of cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Rwanda, as well as on prospects for further development of relations, including in economic and cultural areas. David Zalkaliani thanked Vincent Biruta for his strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity and provided him with information on the security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the Russian-occupied Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions.
