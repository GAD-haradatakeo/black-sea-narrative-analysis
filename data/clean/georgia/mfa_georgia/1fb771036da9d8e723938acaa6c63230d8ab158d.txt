10 April 2024 On 10 April 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the newly appointed head of the EU Monitoring Mission in Georgia, Bettina Boughani. During their discussion, they highlighted the EU Monitoring Mission's crucial role in overseeing the implementation of the Ceasefire Agreement brokered by the EU on 12 August 2008. They also acknowledged the Mission's efforts in preventing further escalation of tensions on the ground. Both sides emphasized the importance of granting the Mission access to the occupied territories in line with its mandate. The conversation also centred on the security, human rights, and humanitarian situation in Georgia's occupied regions. There was particular emphasis on the necessity of Russia fulfilling the ceasefire agreement signed in 2008, mediated by the EU, to establish security on the ground and achieve a peaceful resolution to the Russia-Georgia conflict.
