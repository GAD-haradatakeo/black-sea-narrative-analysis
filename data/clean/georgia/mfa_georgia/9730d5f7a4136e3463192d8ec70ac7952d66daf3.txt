10 October 2017 10.10.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Undersecretary of State in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Poalnd Joanna Wronecka. The sides discussed the existing relations between Georgia and the Republic of Poland emphasizing the high-level political dialogue between the two countries. The sides reaffirmed their readiness to contribute to the further development of economic co-operation. Mikheil Janelidze thanked Joanna Wronecka for Poland’s strong support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration, Joanna Wronecka as a responsible person for the Development Programme at the Polish Fooreign Ministry summarized the meetings she held with various representatives of the Georgian Government reaffirming Poland’s readiness to implement new projects in the priority areas to Georgia. Speaking about co-operation within the framework of multilateral diplomacy, the sides also discussed the Poland-Georgia agenda of co-operation. The Georgian Vice Premier extensively spoke about the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories.
