12 April 2025 Within the framework of the Antalya Diplomacy Forum, Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Maldives, Abdulla Khaleel. The meeting focused on bilateral relations between Georgia and the Republic of Maldives, as well as prospects for future cooperation. The two sides emphasized the potential for strengthening sectoral cooperation in various areas, including tourism, education, and investment. The ministers also discussed the successful collaboration between the two countries within international organizations.
