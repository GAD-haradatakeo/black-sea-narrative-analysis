25 September 2021 In the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ghana, Shirley Ayorkor Botchwey. During the meeting the sides discussed prospects of bilateral cooperation between the two countries, including in the fields of economy, tourism and agriculture. Talking points included investments, business contacts and cooperation in various formats.
