9 February 2023 The Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of Croatia, Gordan Grlić-Radman will pay a visit to Georgia from 8 to 11 February 2023. This is the first visit of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Croatia to Georgia, which is being carried out as part of the 30th anniversary of diplomatic relations between the two countries. The Croatian Foreign Minister will hold meetings with the senior officials of the executive and legislative authorities of Georgia, including with the Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili. Within the framework of the visit, the sides will sign an agreement on economic cooperation and a memorandum of understanding between the foreign ministries on diplomatic training. Gordan Grlić-Radman will visit the occupation line and get acquainted with the current situation on the spot. Also, the Croatian minister will lay a wreath at the memorial of heroes who died for the territorial integrity of Georgia. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Croatia will deliver a public lecture at the Tbilisi State University.
