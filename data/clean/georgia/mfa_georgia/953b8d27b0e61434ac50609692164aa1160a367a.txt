David Zalkaliani has shared new initiatives with Diaspora representatives 21 November 2018 20.11.2018, Brussels – Within the framework of his visit to Brussels, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with representatives of the Georgian Diaspora abroad. Discussions focused on the platform of communications with Georgian citizens abroad and on the recently-created Sunday School Books. According to the Minister, Diaspora-related issues are of priority importance and the Ministry puts forward an increasing number of new initiatives in order to strengthen the Georgian Diaspora abroad and ensure their strong connection with their homeland.
