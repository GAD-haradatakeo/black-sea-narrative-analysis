Khatuna Totladze has Presented Georgia’s 2025 National Report on the State of Human Rights Protection at the Parliamentary Committee on Foreign Relations 7 November 2025 Today, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Khatuna Totladze, presented the draft of Georgia’s 2025 National Report on the State of Human Rights Protection within the framework of the fourth cycle of the United Nations Universal Periodic Review (UPR) at a session of the Parliamentary Committee on Foreign Relations.
