7 July 2025 Levan Beridze, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Hellenic Republic and the Republic of Serbia, presented his Letters of Credence to the President of Serbia, Aleksandar Vučić. Following the credential presentation ceremony held at the Palace of Serbia, a meeting took place between the President and the Ambassador. The discussion focused on the friendly relations between Georgia and Serbia, recent steps taken to deepen bilateral cooperation, and the 30th anniversary of diplomatic relations between the two countries.
