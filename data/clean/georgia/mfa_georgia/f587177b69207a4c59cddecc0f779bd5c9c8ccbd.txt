15 May 2017 15.05.2017, Tbilisi - The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the DPRK’s systematic violation of a number of international obligations and UN SC resolutions and condemns yet another test-firing of ballistic missiles by Pyongyang, on 14 May 2017. As a member state of the Nonproliferation Treaty and Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty,Georgia distances itself from any activity posing a threat to interntional security, as well as to maintaining peace and stability in the region.
