18 December 2019 18.12.2019, Tbilisi – Meeting on “Issues related to Georgia within International Organizations” was held on the sidelines of the Ambassadors’ Conference -2019, where participants of the meeting discussed the situation within the international organizations and the related challenges. Special emphasis was put on Georgia’s active cooperation within the international system and Georgia’s contribution towards strengthening the global security. Speaking before the participants of the meeting, the Permanent Representative of Georgia to the United Nations, Kaha Imnadze, Permanent Representative of Georgia to the OSCE, and the international organization in Vienna, David Dondua, and Georgia’s Permanent Representative to the UN Office and other international organisations in Geneva Victor Dolidze highlighted Georgia’s plans for further cooperation within the international organizations.
