19 February 2022 The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the Holy See Secretary for Relations with States, Paul Richard Gallagher. Discussing the issues of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and the Vatican, the sides praised the traditionally friendly and partner relations and the recent high-level historical visits. The ministers also spoke about the security environment in the region and the world, and stressed the importance of global peace and the need to use every opportunity achieve it. The Minister briefed his colleague on the dire security and humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied regions due to the occupation regimes’ provocative activities on the ground.
