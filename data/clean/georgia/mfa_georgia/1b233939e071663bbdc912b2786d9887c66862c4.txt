16 December 2018 ​6.12.2018, Telavi -“The swearing-in ceremony of Ms President is an exceedingly important event in the history of Georgia” - Deputy Chairman of the Council of the Republic of the National Assembly of the Republic of Belarus Marianna Shchetkina said congratulating Salome Zurabishvili on her election as President of Georgia. Speaking about the close relations between the two countries, the representative of the Belarusian legislative body highlighted the importance of close co-operation between the two countries based on mutual respect and partner relations. The inauguration ceremony of Salome Zurabishvili has been attended by 150 high level guests from 55 countries in Telavi, Kakheti region. Documents Similar News
