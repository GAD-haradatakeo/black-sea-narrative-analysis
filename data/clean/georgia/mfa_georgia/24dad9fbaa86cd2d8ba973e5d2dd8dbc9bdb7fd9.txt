20 March 2019 20.03.2019, Argentina – At a meeting between the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani and the President of the Chamber ofDeputies,Emilio Monzó,the sides discussed the successful co-operation and the ways to further deepen relations between Georgia and Argentina. The Minister expressed his gratitude for Argentina’s historical support, namely, for being the first to recognize Georgia’s aspiration to Freedom in 1919. Highlighting the importance of the centenary of Georgian parliamentarism, David Zalkalaini invited Emilio Monzó to Georgia. The President of the Argentine Chamber ofDeputies,Emilio Monzóexpressed his desire to visit Georgia. The sides highly apprised the political and sectoral co-operation between the two countries, as well as the parliamentary dimension of co-operation and the activities of the Group of Friends of Georgia at the Argentine Parliament that was set up on 26 September 2018. Conversations also included the potential of developing trade-economic, cultural and people-to-people contacts between the two countries. Zalkaliani informed the President of the Chamber ofDeputies on Georgia’s European integration agenda.
