24 November 2023 On 24 November 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held his first meeting with the Ambassador of Slovakia, Matus Bushovski, who recently started his diplomatic activities in Georgia. The first deputy minister and the ambassador welcomed the traditional close cooperation between Georgia and Slovakia, which is developing successfully and is characterized by growing dynamics. Matus Bushovski spoke about his future plans in Georgia and expressed his readiness to actively contribute to the further deepening of relations. He also confirmed Slovakia's strong support for Georgia's foreign policy priorities. During the meeting, Lasha Darsalia highlighted Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Special note was taken of the European Commission's recommendation to grant Georgia the EU candidate status, which is a recognition of our country's efforts and progress. The Georgian side expressed the hope that, based on the European Commission’s recommendation, the European Council will grant Georgia a candidate status in December. Discussions also focused on the ongoing processes in the region and the existing security environment. Special attention was paid to the difficult security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied territories of Georgia.
