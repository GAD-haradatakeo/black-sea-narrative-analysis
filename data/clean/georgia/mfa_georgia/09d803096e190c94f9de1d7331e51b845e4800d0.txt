10 May 2021 On 10 May,Georgian and North Macedonian presidents signed the Agreement "On Exemption from the Obligation to Obtain Visas" between the two countries, during the first official visit of Georgian President Salome Zurabishvili to North Macedonia. The agreement provides for persons with a biometric passport to be allowed to enter, stop or transit through the country without a visa. As part of the visit, The Minister of Culture, Sports and Youth Affairs of Georgia Tea Tsulukiani and the Minister of Culture of Northern Macedonia Irena Stefoska signed a memorandum on cooperation in the field of culture. The Memorandum of Understanding between the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Youth Affairs of Georgia and the Ministry of Culture of the Republic of Northern Macedonia aims to deepen relations between the agencies of the two countries, as well as to develop cultural ties. Encourage activities, exchange information, participate in seminars, conferences or other cultural events. The memorandum also provides for the promotion of direct contacts in various areas of the cultural field, in particular: literature and publishing, library activities, visual arts, music, cinema, theater and cultural heritage protection.
