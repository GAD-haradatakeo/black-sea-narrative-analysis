22 June 2021 On 22 June a meeting was held between the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani and Secretary General of the intergovernmental coalition – “Community of Democracies”, Thomas Garrett. During the meeting the parties discussed activity conducted within the framework of the coalition associated with Georgia. David Zalkaliani presented the Secretary General of the coalition with information regarding Russia’s occupation of Georgia’s territories and the dire humanitarian situation on the ground. Particular attention was paid to the restriction on the freedom of movement for the local population, closure of the occupation line, the so called borderisation process, illicit arrests and kidnappings, as well as gross violations of human rights.
