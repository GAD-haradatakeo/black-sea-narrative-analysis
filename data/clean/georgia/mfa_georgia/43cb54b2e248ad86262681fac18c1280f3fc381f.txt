15 August 2023 Within the framework of the visit to the Republic of Paraguay, the Georgian delegation, led by the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the newly appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Paraguay, Rubén Ramirez Lezcano. Discussions focused on a wide range of cooperation between Paraguay and Georgia, both in a bilateral format and within the international organizations. The sides noted the positive dynamics of bilateral relations, including in terms of the political dialogue and people-to-people ties. The potential of strengthening trade and economic cooperation and the readiness to implement joint measures were highlighted. The First Deputy Minister provided the Paraguayan Minister with information about the current situation in Georgia, opportunities and challenges facing the region.
