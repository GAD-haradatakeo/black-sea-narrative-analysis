11 June 2018 ​11.06.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Ministry hosted the first meeting of the working group on the elaboration of the concept of the EU integration roadmap -ROADMAP2EU. The meeting was chaired by the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili. The meeting was attended by representatives of various ministries at the level of deputy ministers and heads of departments. Vakhtang Makharoblishvili provided the participants of the meeting with information on the structure of the roadmap, timelimits for its development and the works performed within each component. The Deputy Foreign Minister highlighted the importance of the roadmap as a contributing factor to the consolidation of reforms needed to move to the further stages of integration with the EU.
