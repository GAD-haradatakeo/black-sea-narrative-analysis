29 July 2025 Sopio Katsarava, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia, presented her Letters of Credence to the President of Ireland, Michael D. Higgins, at the Presidential Palace. Following the official ceremony, a meeting was held between the President and the Ambassador. The discussion focused on the friendly relations between the two countries and explored avenues for strengthening future cooperation.
