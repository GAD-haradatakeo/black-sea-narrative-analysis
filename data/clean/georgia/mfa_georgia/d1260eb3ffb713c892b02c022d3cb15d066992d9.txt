14 July 2022 On 14 July 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs Ilia Darchiashvili held his first meeting with the Ambassador of the State of Qatar to Georgia, Hassan Ahmed Ali Mohammed Al Mutawa. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides discussed the busy agenda of the bilateral relations between Georgia and Qatar and positively appraised, in this regard, the successful visit of the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili, to Doha, In June. They expressed the hope that the results of the said visit will further deepen the ties between the two countries, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. In addition, the importance of further deepening trade, economy and tourism cooperation was emphasized. The sides expressed their readiness to actively work in this direction. Documents Similar News
