13 November 2025 On 13 November 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan, will pay an official visit to Georgia. Within the framework of the visit, a meeting will be held between the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan. The parties will discuss issues related to strategic partnership and regional cooperation. Following the meeting, the two Ministers will make statements to the press. During his visit, Minister Mirzoyan will also hold meetings with the President of Georgia, Mikheil Kavelashvili, the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze, and the Chairperson of the Parliament of Georgia, Shalva Papuashvili.
