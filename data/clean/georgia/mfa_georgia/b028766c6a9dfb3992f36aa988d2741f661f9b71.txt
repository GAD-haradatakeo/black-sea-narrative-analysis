21 June 2017 21.06.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze signed the condolence book, which has been opened at the Embassy of the Federal Republic of Germany to Georgia. He expressed his condolences to the German people in connection with the passing away of Former German Chancellor Helmut Cohl, the architect of Germany's reunification.
