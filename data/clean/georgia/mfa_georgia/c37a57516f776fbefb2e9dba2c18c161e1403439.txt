23 August 2024 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with Jesper Moller, the Representative of the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) in Georgia. During the meeting, they discussed various aspects of cooperation between Georgia and UNICEF, including matters related to Georgia's occupied territories.
