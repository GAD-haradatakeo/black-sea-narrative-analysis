2 September 2022 On 1 September 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador of the State of Israel, Hadas Ester Meitzad. The minister congratulated the Israeli ambassador on his appointment and wished him success in his future work. Discussions focused on a wide range of relations between the two countries. Special attention was paid to the need of deepening trade, economic and tourism cooperation between Israel and Georgia. In this regard, the sides highlighted the importance of the Georgian Prime Minister Irakli Gharibashvili’s visit to the State of Israel, on 30 May.
