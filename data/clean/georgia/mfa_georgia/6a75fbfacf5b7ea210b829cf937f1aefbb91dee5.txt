24 September 2021 Within the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with his Danish counterpart, Minister of Foreign Affairs Jeppe Kofod. During the meeting the sides discussed a wide range of cooperation in bilateral and multilateral formats, positively assessed the rapidly growing agenda of cooperation between the two countries and noted that 2022 marks the 30th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Georgia and the Kingdom of Denmark. The diplomats also spoke about Georgia's European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. David Zalkaliani briefed his Danish counterpart on the difficult situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and focused on the Georgian government's peace policy in this regard. He thanked Jeep Kofod for Denmark's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the country's European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations.
