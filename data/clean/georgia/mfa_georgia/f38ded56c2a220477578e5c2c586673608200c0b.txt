13 September 2021 The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hellenic Republic to Georgia, Dimitrios Karabalis, who is completing his office in Georgia. David Zalkaliani thanked the Greek Ambassador for his significant contribution to the agenda of positive cooperation between the two countries. The sides focused on Georgia's European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations and highlighted the progress made by Georgia on this path. At the same time, the sides put the emphasis on the need to continue the reforms and expressed the hope that Georgia will be prepared to apply for EU membership in 2024. Dimitrios Carabalis reaffirmed Greece's strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the country's European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The sides also spoke about the difficulties caused by the pandemic and expressed the hope that the pandemic will not hinder the development of fruitful cooperation between Georgia and Greece.
