6 April 2018 5.04.2018, Washington – Within the framework of his working visit to Washington, Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the U.S. Deputy Trade Representative for Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and Industrial Competitiveness, Jeffrey Gerrish. The sides discussed the potential of deepening bilateral trade and investments relations between Georgia and the U.S. highlighting the importance of the high-level trade and investment dialogue between the two countries (HLTID) and the preparations for the successful conduction of its next round. Mikheil Janelidze brought to the attention of Jeffrey Gerrish the recent economic and institutional reforms in Georgia. He also paid special attention to the process of transforming Georgia into a regional hub. Special mention was made of the successful completion of the negotiations over the free trade agreement. The sides focused on the growing level of trade and investments co-operation between the U.S. and Georgia that is facilitated by the good business and investment environment in Georgia.
