5 December 2025 On the sidelines of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Yermek Kosherbayev. Maka Botchorishvili congratulated her Kazakh counterpart on his appointment to office. The Ministers discussed the bilateral relations agenda between the two countries. The conversation also covered cooperation in the trade, economic and transit-logistics sectors.
