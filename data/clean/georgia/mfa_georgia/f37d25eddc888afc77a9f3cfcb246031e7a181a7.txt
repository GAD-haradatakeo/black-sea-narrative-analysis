17 November 2025 On 17-18 November 2025, Maka Botchorishvili is paying her first official visit to the State of Israel in her capacity as Minister of Foreign Affairs. During the visit, Minister Botchorishvili will hold meetings with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State of Israel, Gideon Sa’ar; the Speaker of the Knesset, Amir Ohana; the Minister of National Security, Itamar Ben-Gvir; and the Minister of Agriculture and Food Security, Avi Dichter. As part of the programme, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia will also visit the Orthodox Patriarchate of Jerusalem and meet with His Beatitude Patriarch Theophilos III.
