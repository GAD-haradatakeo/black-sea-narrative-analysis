6 July 2021 On 6 July 2021, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with the Azerbaijani Minister of Culture, Anar Karimov, during his official visit to Georgia. During the meeting the sides highlighted the significance of the existing strategic relations between Georgia and Azerbaijan, including main areas of cultural cooperation, as well as future prospects and initiatives, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. The sides emphasized that planning and implementation of various joint projects, including in the area of culture are important for ensuring peace and stability in the region.
