24 March 2022 On 24 March 2022, a farewell meeting was held between the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani and the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China to Georgia, Li Yan, who completed her mission in Georgia. The sides discussed a wide range of current issues in the relations between the two countries and highlighted the positive dynamics of bilateral and multilateral cooperation in various fields. During the meeting, it was noted that Georgia and China are celebrating the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations this year. In this context, both sides expressed their readiness to further develop the partnership established over the years. Li Yan reaffirmed China's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. She also noted China's respect for Georgia's foreign policy goals and aspirations. For his part, the Georgian Foreign Minister reiterated Georgia's support for the "One China" policy. He thanked the Chinese Ambassador for her work in Georgia and stressed her personal contribution to intensive cooperation with the Chinese side during the pandemic.
