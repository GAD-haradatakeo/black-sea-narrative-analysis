2 April 2025 On 2 April 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with a Chinese business delegation on a special visit to Georgia. The delegation comprised executives from approximately ten leading companies representing various sectors. The discussions focused on enhancing economic cooperation between Georgia and China, expanding trade relations, strengthening business ties, and exploring opportunities for joint projects and investments across different sectors in Georgia. Particular attention was paid to the development of direct flights between cities of the two countries, as well as cooperation in logistics and tourism. Specific business proposals were reviewed, along with their potential implementation in Georgia.
