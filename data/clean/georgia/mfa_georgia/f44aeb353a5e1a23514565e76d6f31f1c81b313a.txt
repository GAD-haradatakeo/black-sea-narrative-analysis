5 May 2020 Acting at the instruction of the Coordination Council and in coordination with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad continue to assist Georgian citizens stranded in foreign countries due to the pandemic. Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 18,857 citizens of Georgia in the host countries. The Government has organized the repatriation 9,630.citizens to Georgia, as of 5 May The Georgian MFA website is updated every day to provide the latest information regarding support measures undertaken by the Georgian government with respect to Georgian citizens abroad. Upcoming flights are as follows: 5 May – Athens-Tbilisi 7 May – Amsterdam-Tbilisi 9 May – Berlin-Tbilisi On 6 May, Georgian citizens will return from Jordan and Saudi Arabia Documents Similar News
