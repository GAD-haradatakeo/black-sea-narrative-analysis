17 January 2024 On 17 January 2024, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Finland, Kirsti Narinen. The sides welcomed the fruitful relations established between the two countries, both at the bilateral level and within the framework of international organizations. Discussions focused on issues related to mutual exchange of high-level visits and to the deepening of bilateral political dialogue. Talking points included the Russia-Georgia conflict and the security situation in the region. The First Deputy Minister emphasized Finland's participation in the EU monitoring mission in Georgia and the importance of its continuation. Ambassador Kirsti Narinen once again confirmed Finland’s unequivocal support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, as well as for our country's accession to the European Union and NATO. The Ambassador of Finland highly appreciated Georgia's peacekeeping role in maintaining peace and stability in the region.
