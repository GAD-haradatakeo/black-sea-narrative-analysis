14 February 2026 On 14 February 2026, on the sidelines of the Munich Security Conference, Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia Maka Botchorishvili held a meeting with OSCE Secretary General Feridun Sinirlioğlu. The meeting addressed key issues related to cooperation between Georgia and the OSCE. Particular attention was devoted to the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions. The Minister underscored the importance of the Geneva International Discussions format and highlighted the OSCE’s engagement in the peaceful resolution of the conflict. The parties also reviewed the situation in the region, including prevailing security challenges and possible avenues for addressing them.
