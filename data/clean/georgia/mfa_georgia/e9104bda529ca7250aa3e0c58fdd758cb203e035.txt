13 February 2018 13.02.2018, Tbilisi -​Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with U.S. Co-Chair of the OSCE Minsk Group for Nagorno-Karabakh Andrew Schofer. The sides discussed the security environment in Georgia and in the region as a whole. David Zalkaliani briefed his colleague on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, as well as on the government’s vision to achieve peaceful resolution of the conflicts, through engagement of the international community. U.S.–Georgia partnership was also highlighted at the meeting. Andrew Schofer highly appraised the Georgian government’s engagement policy with respect to the occupied territories.
