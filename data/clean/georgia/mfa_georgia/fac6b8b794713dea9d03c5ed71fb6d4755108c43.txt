6 May 2022 On 6 May 2022, a telephone conversation took place between the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the State of Israel, Yair Lapid. Yair Lapid congratulated his Georgian counterpart on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. During the telephone conversation, the Ministers underlined the historically close friendship between Georgia and Israel and discussed the prospects for the further development of the partnership between the two countries. The ministers noted the great potential for economic cooperation between the two countries. During the telephone conversation, Ilia Darchiashvili and Yar Lapid also discussed regional security issues.
