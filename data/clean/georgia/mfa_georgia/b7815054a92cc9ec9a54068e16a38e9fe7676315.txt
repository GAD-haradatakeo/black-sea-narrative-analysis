3 May 2018 3.05.2018, Tbilisi - The newly-appointed ambassador of Latvia to Georgia Ingrida Levrence presented copies of her credentials to the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze. Mikheil Janelidze congratulated Ingrida Levrence on her accession to new office wishing her success in her future endeavours. Highlighting the close political relations, as well as the cultural and educational co-operation and people-to-people contacts between the two countries, Mikheil Janelidze stated that Latvia is one of the strong supporters of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Special accent was put on the importance of deepening economic co-operation and business contacts. The newly-appointed ambassador of Latvia reaffirmed Latvia’s support for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration path.
