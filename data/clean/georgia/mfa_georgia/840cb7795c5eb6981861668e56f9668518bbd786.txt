22 October 2025 On 22 October, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, attended the farewell ceremony for the Prime Minister of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Ali Asadov, held at Tbilisi International Airport. The Prime Minister of the Republic of Azerbaijan visited Georgia within the framework of the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum. During his visit, he took part in the opening ceremony of the anniversary forum and held a meeting with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze. The Tbilisi Silk Road Forum is being held on 22-23 October, bringing together more than 2,000 participants from over 60 countries, including prime ministers and deputy prime ministers of regional states, high-level political figures, business leaders, and representatives of international organizations.
