11 February 2022 The Vice Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the U.S. Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Georgia, Kelly Degnan. Discussing the security situation in the region and developments around Ukraine, the sides reaffirmed their strong support for Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity. They focused on the U.S.-Russia security dialogue and the United States’ close cooperation with partners in this process. David Zalkaliani thanked the U.S. Government for its close coordination and support for Georgia within the scope of its dialogue with Russia. The sides reaffirmed the strong strategic partnership between Georgia and the United States, which is essential for the common interests of both countries, as well as for the regional and Euro-Atlantic security. It was highlighted that despite the Russian occupation, Georgia, with the support of the United States, has taken important steps on the path of European and Euro-Atlantic integration, as well as towards strengthening democratic institutions and economic development.
