15 February 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, is on an official visit to the Republic of Turkey. Before the start of the official meetings, the Georgian Foreign Minister visited the Mustafa Kemal Atatürk Mausoleum and laid a wreath on his grave. Ilia Darchiashvili signed the memory book. It should be noted that within the framework of the visit, the Minister of Foreign Affairs holds meetings with his Turkish counterpart, Hakan Fidan, and the Chairman of the Parliament of the Republic of Türkiye, Numan Kurtulmus.
