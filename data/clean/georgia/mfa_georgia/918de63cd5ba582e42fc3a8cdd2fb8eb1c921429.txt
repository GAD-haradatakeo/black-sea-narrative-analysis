9 February 2026 On 9-10 February 2026, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will pay an official visit to Hungary, where she will participate in the Budapest Global Dialogue. As part of the visit, Minister Botchorishvili will hold a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary, Péter Szijjártó, followed by joint press statements by the two ministers.
