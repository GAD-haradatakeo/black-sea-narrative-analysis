6 December 2018 6.12.2018, Milan – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Dutch counterpart, Foreign Minister Stef Blok. The sides discussed the ways to further develop bilateral co-operation and the importance of exchanging high-level visits. The Dutch Minister reaffirmed his support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity both in bilateral and multilateral formats. The sides highlighted the importance of the Netherlands’ support for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Special note, in this context, was taken of the Dutch Government’s practical assistance in Georgia’s institutional development process, including within the framework of the Matra Programme aimed at promoting the development of democratic institutions in the country. Talking points also included the presidential elections held in Georgia. The Ministers noted with satisfaction the enhanced people-to-people contacts and the increasing number of Dutch visitors to Georgia. The sides also exchanged their views over the great potential of developing co-operation in various areas, especially in the trade-economic sector.
