24 May 2021 On 24 May 2021, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with the AmbassadorExtraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Indonesia to Georgia, Yuddy Crisnandi (residing in Kyev). Discussions focused on an increasing dynamics of bilateral relations between the two countries. The sides exchanged views on a broad spectrum of cooperation including in the economic sector. The sides also spoke about anti-pandemic measures taken by the governments of the two countries.
