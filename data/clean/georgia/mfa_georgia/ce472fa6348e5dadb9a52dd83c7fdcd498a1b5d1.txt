18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador of South Africa to Georgia, Shoayb Casoo (resident in Kyiv). The Deputy Minister congratulated the Ambassador on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. During the meeting, the friendly relations between Georgia and South Africa were underscored, and important issues of cooperation were discussed in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Ambassador Shoayb Casoo reaffirmed South Africa’s unwavering support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders. Within the framework of the visit, the newly appointed Ambassador presented a copy of his Letters of Credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia.
