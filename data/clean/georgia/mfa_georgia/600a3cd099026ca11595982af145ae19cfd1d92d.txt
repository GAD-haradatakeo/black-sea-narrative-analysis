21 February 2018 21.02.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meetng with Designated European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia Toivo Klaar. The sides discussed the difficult security and human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories reaffirming the need to achieve the progress in the Geneva International Discussions. Mikheil Janelidze highlighted the important role of the institute of European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia in terms of the peaceful resolution of the Russia-Georgia conflict.
