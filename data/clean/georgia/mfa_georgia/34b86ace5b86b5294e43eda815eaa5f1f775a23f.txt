20 May 2019 20.05.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani attended the reception dedicated to the Constitution Day and congratulated the Polish people on their national holiday. The event was held as a confirmation of the remarkable friendship and partnership between Georgia and Poland, and Poland’s firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations.
