28 September 2024 Within the framework of the 79th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the President of the 79th session of the General Assembly, Philemon Yang. During the meeting, the parties reviewed the successful cooperation between Georgia and the organization. Once again, Georgia's support for the implementation of the organization’s priorities was emphasized, which involves upholding the values and principles of the UN Charter, ensuring peace, prosperity, and sustainable development. The discussion also focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked the President of the General Assembly for supporting Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The importance of backing Georgia’s resolution on internally displaced persons at the General Assembly was highlighted. The parties also discussed the security environment in the region, noting the need for long-term peace and prosperity.
