21 October 2021 The Steering Committee of the Council of Europe Action Plan for Georgia 2020-2023 met by video conference on 21 October 2021. The meeting was attended by members of the executive and legislative branches of Georgia, as well as bt representatives the Council of Europe and other stakeholders. The meeting was opened by the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia, and the Director of the Office of the Directorate General of Programmes of the Council of Europe, Verena Taylor. The purpose of the meeting was to review the projects under the Council of Europe Action Plan for Georgia 2020-2023 and to summarize the achieved results. The projects under the Council of Europe Action Plan developed in close cooperation with the Georgian side aim to strengthen Council of Europe values ​​in the country: human rights, the rule of law and democracy, to promote democratic reforms in Georgia, and to bring Georgia’s legislation and state institutions closer to European standards.
