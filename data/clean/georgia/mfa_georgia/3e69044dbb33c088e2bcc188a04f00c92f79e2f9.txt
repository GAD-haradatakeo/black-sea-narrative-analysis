18 November 2025 On 18 November 2025, during her official visit to the State of Israel, Georgia’s Minister for Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Speaker of the Knesset, Amir Ohana. During the meeting, the parties highlighted the historical ties between the Georgian and Israeli peoples, as well as the friendly relations that exist between the two countries. The discussion focused on cooperation between the legislative bodies of Georgia and Israel, emphasizing the important role of the parliamentary dimension in further strengthening bilateral relations. In this context, the productive work of the Georgia-Israel Parliamentary Friendship Groups was underscored. Particular attention was devoted to the issue of deepening trade-economic cooperation and people-to-people contacts. The discussion also touched upon the existing security environment in the region.
