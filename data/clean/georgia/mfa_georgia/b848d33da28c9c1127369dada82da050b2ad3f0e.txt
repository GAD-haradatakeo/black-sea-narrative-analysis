22 November 2023 On 22 November 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held his first meeting with the Norwegian Ambassador to Georgia, Bergljot Hovland. The first deputy minister congratulated the Norwegian diplomat on his appointment as ambassador to Georgia and wished him success in his future work. The sides welcomed the friendly ties and fruitful cooperation between the two countries at both bilateral and multilateral levels. Discussions focused on issues related to the Russia-Georgia conflict, on the ongoing process of Georgia's integration into the European Union, as well as on the situation in the South Caucasus region. Ambassador Bergljot Hovland, for his part, once again confirmed the Kingdom of Norway's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its EU integration path. The Norwegian ambassador gave a high assessment to Georgia’s peaceful neighborhood policy and thanked the Georgian side for the country’s constructive role in promoting peace and stability in the South Caucasus region.
