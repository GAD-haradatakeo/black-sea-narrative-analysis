23 February 2021 The Ministers underscored the outstanding, friendly relations between the two states, with the Latvian party reconfirming support for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. During the discussion concerning the current processes unfolding in Georgia, the Ministers noted the importance of de-escalation and constructive dialogue, underscoring the significant progress undertaken by Georgia in terms of European and Euro-Atlantic integration, as well as in terms of democratic development. The Ministers expressed readiness to continue cooperation regarding said issues, along with issues of regional and international relevance.
