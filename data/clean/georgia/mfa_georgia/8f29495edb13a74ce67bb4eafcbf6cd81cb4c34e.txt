6 February 2020 06.02.2020, Washington – Within the framework of his visit to Washington, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkalianiheld a meeting with theU.S. Assistant Secretary of State, Philip Reeker. The sides discussed issues relating to strategic partnership between the two countries. Special attention was paid to the security situation in the region. The Georgian side updated Philip Reeker on the latest developments in the occupied territories. Zalkaliani thanked the United States for its firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its involvement in peaceful resolution of the conflict.
