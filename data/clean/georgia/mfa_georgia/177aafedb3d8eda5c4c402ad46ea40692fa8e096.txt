The Georgian Foreign Minister met with the Speaker of the Parliament of Switzerland 28 October 2019 28.10.2019, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, met with the president of the National Council of Switzerland, Marina Carobio-Guscetti, who is paying an official visit to Georgia.
