4 October 2021 On 4 October, the Director of the International Organizations Department, Shalva Tsiskarashvili met with the Head of the Council of Europe Office in Georgia, Natalia Voutova. The Director of the International Organizations Department expressed his gratitude to the International Election Observation Mission, including the observation mission of the Council of Europe Congress of Local and Regional Authorities, for the effective monitoring of the local self-government election process in Georgia. The sides commended the close cooperation between Georgia and the Council of Europe and discussed current issues on the agenda. Speaking about the Council of Europe Action Plan for Georgia 2020-2023, the Head of the Council of Europe Office highlighted that the Office is actively working to implement the existing projects under the plan. Shalva Tsiskarashvili stressed the need to monitor the difficult situation in the Russian-occupied territories of Georgia, highlighting, in this context, the importance of the CoE Secretary General’s consolidated reports and the access to international mechanisms /actors to the occupied territories.
