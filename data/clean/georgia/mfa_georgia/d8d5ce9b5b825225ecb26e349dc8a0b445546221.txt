18 May 2018 18.05.2018, Helsingør - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the Minister of State for Europe at the Federal Foreign Office, Michael Roth within the framework of the 128th Ministerial Meeting of the Council of Europe hosted by Denmark. The sides discussed the agenda of bilateral relations, including the Georgian government-initiated EU Integration Roadmap suppoted by German partners. Discussions during the meeting focused on the visa liberalization process. The German Minister of State thanked the Georgian side for measures taken to remove the problems arising in the visa-free travel process, which have already yielded their results. Mikheil Janelidze welcome the the German side’s initiative to grant Georgia the safe country status, which reduces the period for consideration of asylum applications by Georgian citizens and provides the proof that Germany recognizes the democratic and human rights reforms implemented in Georgia.
