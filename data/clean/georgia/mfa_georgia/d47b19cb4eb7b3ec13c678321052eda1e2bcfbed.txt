12 September 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia condemns the annexation of the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the City of Sevastopol and hence, does not recognize the so-called ‘local elections’ conducted on 10 September 2017, in the Autonomous Republic of Crimea and the City of Sevastopol, in gross violation of the norms of International Law and the legislation of Ukraine. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia reaffirms its firm support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Ukraine within its internationally recognized borders. Documents Similar News
