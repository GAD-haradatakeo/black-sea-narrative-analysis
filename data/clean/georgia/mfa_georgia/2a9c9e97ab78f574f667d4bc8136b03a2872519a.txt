5 September 2024 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, attended the 33rd Economic Forum of Karpachi in Poland, a prominent annual event in Central and Eastern Europe that draws distinguished guests from around the world. At the forum, Khvtisiashvili participated in a panel discussion that focused on Georgia’s strategic role as a bridge between Europe and Asia. The discussion highlighted Georgia's importance for Europe’s economic and energy security, as well as the country’s economic reforms and infrastructure developments. Topics such as EU enlargement and Georgia's European integration were also explored. The forum also featured an evening dedicated to Georgian culture and wine tasting, organized by the Caucasus Foundation with support from the Embassy of Georgia in Poland.
