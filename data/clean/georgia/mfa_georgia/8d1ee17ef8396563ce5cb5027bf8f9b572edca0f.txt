23 September 2022 At the invitation of the U.S. Secretary of State, Antony Blinken, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, participated in the Transatlantic Foreign Ministers’ meeting held in New York, within the framework of the UN General Assembly. The NATO Secretary General, Jens Stoltenberg and the EU High Representative for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy, Josep Borrell were also present at the meeting. The key items on the agenda of the meeting was Russia's military aggression in Ukraine and transatlantic security.
