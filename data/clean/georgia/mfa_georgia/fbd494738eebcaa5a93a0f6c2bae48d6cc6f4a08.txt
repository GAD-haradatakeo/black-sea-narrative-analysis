31 May 2022 On 31 May 2022, at the Embassy of Georgia in Kuala Lumpur, the Ambassador of Georgia, Nikoloz Abkhazava, and the Ambassador of the Lao People's Democratic Republic, Vyngsavan Tsipraseut, signed an agreement between the Government of Georgia and the Government of the Lao People's Democratic Republic on Visa Exemptions for Holders of Diplomatic, Service/Official Passports.
