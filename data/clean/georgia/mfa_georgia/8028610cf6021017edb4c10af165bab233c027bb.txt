16 June 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Wyatt Toehlke, Senior Advisor at the United States Department of State. The discussion focused on U.S.-Georgia bilateral relations, prospects for future cooperation, and current developments in the region. It was emphasized that Georgia is a key partner of the United States. The Minister reiterated Georgia’s commitment to renewing the strategic partnership with the United States.
