19 April 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Azerbaijan to Georgia, Faig Guliyev. Discussions focused on issues on the bilateral agenda of Georgia-Azerbaijan cooperation, with special focus on the situation in the region. The sides highlighted the importance of regional peace and stability and underlined that given the new realities, the countries of the region should cooperate closely, in line with the international order. The sides reaffirmed the importance of further deepening strategic cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Azerbaijan. The Armenian Ambassador thanked the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister for productive cooperation and expressed the hope that the political and economic cooperation between the two countries, as well as people-to-people contacts will continue in the future as well.
