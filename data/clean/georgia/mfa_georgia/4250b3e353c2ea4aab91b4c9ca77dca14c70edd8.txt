14 April 2022 On 14 April 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Austria to Georgia, Thomas Mülmann. The First Deputy Minister and the Ambassador discussed the security situation in the region and Russia's large-scale war against Ukraine. It was noted that the ongoing war in Ukraine extends beyond the region and poses a threat to European security as a whole. Speaking about the security challenges facing Georgia, special attention was paid to the difficult security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied regions of Georgia and to Russia's illegal and provocative actions. Special note was taken of the 30th anniversary of restoration of diplomatic relations between the two countries and the events planned to celebrate the anniversary date.
