1 June 2017 1.06.2017, Japan – Georgian Foregn Minister Mikheil Janelidze met Georgian citizens living in Japan. The meeting was held at the Embassy of Georgia. The Georgian community in Japan is largely made up of students, researchers and businessmen. During the meeting the Minister spoke about Georgia-Japan relations, his ongong visit and meetings held within its framework. Discussions also focused on the National Diaspora Strategy and on the steps the Government plans to take towards strengthening the ties of Diaspora representatives with Georgia and ensure their involvement in the ongoing processes. The participants of the meeting highlighted the Diaspora’s role in promoting Georgian culture and raising awareness of Georgia in Japan. Diaspora representatives brough to the attention of the Minister various projects they carry out in Japan, including Georgian language training courses, translation of Georgian literary works, etc The Minister expressed his readiness to further meet Georgians living in Japan and assist them in realizing their knowledge in Georgia, as well as in promoting Georgia in Japan.
