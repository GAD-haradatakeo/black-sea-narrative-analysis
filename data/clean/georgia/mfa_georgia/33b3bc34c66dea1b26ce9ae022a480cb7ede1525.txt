23 August 2023 On 23 August 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, European Affairs and Foreign Trade and the Federal Cultural Institutions of the Kingdom of Belgium, Hadja Lahbib, arrived at the village of Odzisi near the occupation line. Georgia's Representative to the European Union, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili and the representative of the State Security Service, informed the Belgian Foreign Minister about the grave humanitarian situation on the ground.
