9 October 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the State of Israel to Georgia, Walid Abu Haya. Minister Botchorishvili congratulated Ambassador Abu Haya on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. The Ambassador, in turn, conveyed to the Minister an official invitation from the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Israel. During the meeting, the parties discussed the existing partnership between Georgia and Israel, the priorities for future cooperation, and opportunities for further strengthening bilateral relations across various fields. Both sides reaffirmed their commitment to maintaining close cooperation within international formats. Minister Botchorishvili welcomed the agreement reached between Israel and Hamas on the first phase of the Gaza peace plan, emphasizing the exceptional efforts and contribution of the United States, and of President Trump personally, to advancing peace in the region.
