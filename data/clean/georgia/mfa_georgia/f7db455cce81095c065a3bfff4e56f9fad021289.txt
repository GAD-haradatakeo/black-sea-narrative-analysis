23 April 2018 23.04.2018 – The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister, Mikheil Janelidze will pay a working visit to New York from 23 to 25 April 2018 where he will participate in „High level meeting on peacebuilding and sustaining peace“. The Foreign Minister will speak before the participants of the high-level segment of the UN General Assembly and will highlight the challenges facing Georgia, including the occupied territories, and the Georgian Government’s peace initiative “A step toward a better future”, which is aimed at improving the humanitarian and social-economic situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and encouraging contacts along the dividing lines and relations that are based on mutually beneficial interests. On the sidelines of the general debates, a side event entitled „Achieving sustainable peace based on the respect of human rights” will be co-organized by Georgia where the Georgian Prime Minister will provide the attending audience with information regarding the difficult human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. The event co-organized by Georgia will be attended by high-ranking delegates of the UN Member States taking part in the general debates.
