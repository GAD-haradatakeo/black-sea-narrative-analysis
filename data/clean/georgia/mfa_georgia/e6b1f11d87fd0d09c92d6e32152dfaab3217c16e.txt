26 October 2022 On 25 October 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vladimer Konstantinidi took part in the event dedicated to the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Finland. Speaking before the audience, he talked about the strong friendly relations established between Georgia and Finland over the past 30 years, based on mutual respect and common values. "Three decades of friendship and multidimensional relations in bilateral and multilateral formats have brought us many tangible results and laid a solid foundation for the further expansion of this mutually beneficial cooperation, especially in the fields of politics, economy, cyber security and education, as well as in terms of deepening people-to-people contacts" the Deputy Minister said. Vladimer Konstantinidi drew special attention to the importance of Finland's support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and thanked the Finnish side for its active involvement in the EU Monitoring Mission. The Deputy Minister expressed the hope that Finland will continue to support Georgia's European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. "I firmly believe that Georgia-Finland friendship and future cooperation can play an important role in overcoming our common challenges and ensure a more peaceful and brighter European and Euro-Atlantic future," Konstantinidi said.
