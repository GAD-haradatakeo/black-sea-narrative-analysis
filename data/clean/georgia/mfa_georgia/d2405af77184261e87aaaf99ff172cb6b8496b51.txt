25 January 2024 On 25 January 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a telephone conversation with the Director General of the General Directorate of Neighborhood and Enlargement Negotiations of the European Commission, Gert Jan Koopman. Discussions focused on the current agenda of EU-Georgia cooperation. Special attention was paid to the efforts made by Georgia in light of EU enlargement policy. The sides highlighted the progress on Georgia’s implementation of the European Commission’s 9 recommendations.
