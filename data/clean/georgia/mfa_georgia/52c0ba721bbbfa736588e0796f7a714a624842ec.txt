5 April 2019 05.04.2019, Washington –Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Deputy Prime Minister of Ukraine for European and Euro-Atlantic IntegrationIvanna Klympush-Tsintsadze. Highlighting the historic friendship between Georgia and Ukraine, the sides spoke about Georgia’s and Ukraine’s European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. Conversations included the occupation of the Georgian and Ukrainian territories by Russia and the need to consolidate the international community’s support against Russian aggression. The sides highly appraised the package of measures for improving security in the Black Sea region. Discussions also focused on EU integration agenda, the tenth anniversary of Eastern Partnership and Georgia’s and Ukraine’s visions regarding further steps on integration.
