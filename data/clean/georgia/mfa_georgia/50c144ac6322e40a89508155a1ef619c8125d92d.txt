16 June 2024 An event commemorating the 10th anniversary of the establishment of the Belgian and Dutch diocese of the Georgian Church was held in Brussels, with the support of the Embassy of Georgia. Members of the Holy Synod of the Georgian Church and Mukhran Gulagashvili, the director of the Diaspora Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, participated in the event. During the celebration, Ambassador of Georgia Vakhtang Makharoblishvili presented a certificate of honour and a special award from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia to Bishop Dositheos. This was part of the "Recognition of Special Merits of Representatives of the Diaspora 2024" program. The anniversary event featured a solemn liturgy at the Brussels Cathedral, followed by musical performances by the ensemble "Didgori" and members of the Georgian diaspora in Belgium. The event was attended by diplomats from Georgia's missions to the European Union and NATO, the Kingdom of Belgium, and the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, as well as representatives from the Georgian diaspora in Belgium. During the meeting, representatives of the Georgian Embassy and the diaspora exchanged views on current issues affecting the Georgian community in Belgium. As part of the events marking the 10th anniversary of the establishment of the Diocese of Belgium and Holland, the Embassy of Georgia hosted members of the Holy Synod.
