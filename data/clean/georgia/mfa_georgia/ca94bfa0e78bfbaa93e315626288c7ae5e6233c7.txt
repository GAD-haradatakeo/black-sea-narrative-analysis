25 June 2018 25.06.2018 – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Italian Ambassador to Georgia Antonio Enrico Bartoli, Dutch Ambassador to Georgia Johannes Douma, French Ambassadorto Georgia Pascal Meunierand German Ambassador to GeorgiaHeikePeitsch. Discussions focused on Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. The sides highlighted Georgia’s reforms on its path to Euro-Atlantic integration. Special attention was paid to the process of preparations for the NATO Summit in Brussels. The sides spoke about the importance of the support of NATO Member States in Georgia’s NATO integration process reaffirming their readiness to co-operate both in bilateral and multilateral format for the further strengthening of relations.
