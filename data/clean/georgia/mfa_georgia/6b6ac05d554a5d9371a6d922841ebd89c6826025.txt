18 June 2019 On 17 June 2019, the German Federal Foreign Office spokesperson,Maria Adebar, made a comment on the so-called elections conducted in the occupied Tskhinvali. According to him, Georgia does not recognize the so-called elections and firmly supports Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. “The results of the so-called elections are illegitimate. We fully share the position of the European Union in this regard. Germany reaffirms its support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity” - Maria Adebar said. Documents Similar News
