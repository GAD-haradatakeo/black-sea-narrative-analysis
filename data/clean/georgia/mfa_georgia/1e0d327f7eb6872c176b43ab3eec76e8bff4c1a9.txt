16 February 2020 16.02.2020, Munich – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with his Polish counterpart Jacek Czaputowicz. The Ministers welcomed the strategic partnership between Georgia and Poland. David Zalkaliani thanked his Polish counterpart for Poland’s strong support of Georgia. The Ministers highly appraised the existing formats of cooperation between the countries. They also discussed the recent developments in Georgia’s occupied regions The parties expressed their readiness to actively cooperate both for the strengthening of strategic partnership, as well as for the implementation of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic agenda and goals.
