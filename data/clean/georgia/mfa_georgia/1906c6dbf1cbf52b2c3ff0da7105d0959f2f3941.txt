Comment of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the 29th anniversary of the fall of Sokhumi 27 September 2022 27 September 2022 marks 29 years since the fall of Sokhumi. 27 September is the day of the common tragedy suffered by Georgian and Abkhaz people, which has led to the Russian occupation of Georgia’s Abkhazia region, and has resulted in the death of tens of thousands of people and displacement of hundreds of thousands fleeing their homes.
