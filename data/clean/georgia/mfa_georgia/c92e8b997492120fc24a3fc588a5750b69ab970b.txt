16 July 2018 16.07.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani participated in the official meeting held between the Georgian and Italian delegations at the President’s Palace. Following the official meeting, the sides signed several memorandums of cooperation in the field of defence, security, justice, education, science, culture, innovations and technology, in particular: 1. MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN THE CARABINIERI CORPS OF THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE OF THE ITALIAN REPUBLIC AND THE MILITARY POLICE DEPARTMENT OF THE MINISTRY OF DEFENCE OF GEORGIA. 2. MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN THE DEPARTMENT OF PUBLIC SECURITY OF THE MINISTRY OF THE INTERIOR OF THE ITALIAN REPUBLIC AND THE MINISTRY OF INTERNAL AFFAIRS OF GEORGIA ON STRENGTHENING POLICE COOPERATION. 3. MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN THE INTERNATIONAL EDUCATION CENTRE (IEC), REPUBLIC OF GEORGIA THE CONFERENCE OF ITALIAN UNIVERSITY RECTORS (CRUI), ITALY. 4. Agreement on Scientific Cooperation between LEPL - Shota Rustaveli National Science Foundation of Georgia and the National Research Council of Italy. 5. Cooperative Programme of the agreement on Scientific Cooperation between LEPL - Shota Rustaveli National Science Foundation of Georgia and the National Research Council of Italy. 6. MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING FOR EDUCATIONAL AND SCIENTIFIC COOPERATION BETWEEN THE NATIONAL CENTER FOR DISEASE CONTROL AND PUBLIC HEALTH OF GEORGIA AND THE ISTITUTO SUPERIORE DI SANITÀ OF THE ITALIAN REPUBLIC. 7. Agreement on Innovation and technological cooperation between COTEC (Foundation for Technological Innovation) and Georgian Innovation and Technology Agency (GITA). 8. MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING BETWEEN THE MINISTRY OF FOREIGN AFFAIRS AND INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION OF THE ITALIAN REPUBLIC AND THE MINISTRY OF EDUCATION, SCIENCE, CULTURE AND SPORT ON COOPERATION ON ARTISTIC RESIDENCIES.
