22 April 2018 22.04.2018 –​Within the framework of his working visit to South Africa the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Zviad Gonadze held a meeting with Georgians living in Nice. The Deputy Minister accompanied by the Georgian Ambassador to France visited representatives of Georgian Dance Studio "International" based in Nice. It needs to be mentioned that the head of the studio continues to promote Georgian folklore throughout France, and Georgian folk dance groups in Lion and Paris are also run by him. At the meeting with Georgians living in Nice, the Deputy Minister spoke about Georgia’s state programme on Diaspora and its priorities. The importance of the Georgian Diaspora’s active engagement in the state building process was also highlighted. According to Zviad Gonadze, the Government of Georgia attaches great priority to the establishment of close contacts with the Diaspora.
