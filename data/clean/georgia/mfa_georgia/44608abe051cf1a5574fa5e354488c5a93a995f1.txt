28 February 2021 The President of the European Council, Charles Michel, will pay a visit to Georgia. Within the framework of the meeting the President of the European Council will hold meetings with the Georgian President, Salome Zourabichvili, the Prime Minister, Irakli Gharibashvili and the Parliament Speaker, Archil Talakvadze. Charles Michele will also hold meetings with representative of political parties. Additionally, the President of the European Council will visit the occupation line, familiarising himself with the situation on the ground. It is noteworthy, that Charles Michel will also visit Moldova and Ukraine. According to the assessment of the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, said visit comes as another manifestation of the support that the EU provides to its significant partner, Georgia, in the region.
