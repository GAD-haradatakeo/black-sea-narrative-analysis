18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Zurabashvili, met with the Ambassador of the Portuguese Republic to Georgia, Maria Virgínia Mendes da Silva Pina. The parties discussed the main directions of cooperation between Georgia and Portugal. Particular attention was given to Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration and the support provided by Portugal in this process. The high level of cooperation within international organizations was positively assessed. Giorgi Zurabashvili expressed gratitude to the Portuguese diplomat for Portugal’s active support of Georgia in international formats. The discussion also covered the intensification of political consultations between the two countries, as well as cooperation in the fields of economy, culture, tourism, and education. Giorgi Zurabashvili thanked the Ambassador for Portugal’s support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and emphasized the critical importance of Portugal’s position in advancing the policy of non-recognition among Lusophone countries. Prior to the meeting with the First Deputy Minister, the newly appointed Ambassador of Portugal presented copies of her Letters of Credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia.
