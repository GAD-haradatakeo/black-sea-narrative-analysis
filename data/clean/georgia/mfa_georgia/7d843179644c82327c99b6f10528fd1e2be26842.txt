15 December 2025 On 15 December 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili held a meeting with the Ambassador-designate of Iceland to Georgia, Friðrik Jónsson (resident in Warsaw). The Deputy Minister welcomed Iceland’s decision to appoint its first non-resident ambassador to Georgia and wished the diplomat success in his future endeavours. The meeting underscored the friendly relations and close cooperation between the two countries, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed his appreciation to the Icelandic side for its support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
