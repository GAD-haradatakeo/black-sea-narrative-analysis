9 October 2021 On 8 October 2021, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the German Ambassador to Georgia, Hubert Knirsch. The sides discussed issues of cooperation between the two countries in bilateral and multilateral formats. Focusing on the difficult security and humanitarian situation in the occupied territories of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia spoke about the agenda of the next round of the Geneva International Discussions. The meeting focused on the security environment and challenges in the South Caucasus region. Lasha Darsalia noted Georgia's commitment to promote peace and cooperation in the region and stressed the need to strengthen the involvement of Western partners in the region. At the end of the meeting, the First Deputy Foreign Minister thanked Hubert Knirsch for Germany's strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
