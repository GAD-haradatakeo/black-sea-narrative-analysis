3 August 2018 03.08.2018, Tbilisi – Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Davit Zalkaliani held a farewell meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Slovak Republic to Georgia, Rudolf Michalka. David Zalkaliani thanked Rudolf Michalka for activation of links between the two countries and contribution towards the strengthening of friendly relations. Discussions focused on the friendly relations between the two countries. The sides highlighted the importance of Slovakia’s express political support for Georgia’s integration with the EU and NATO. According to Rudolf Michalka, Slovakia remains Georgia’s reliable partner and will continue to support its sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as its European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations.
