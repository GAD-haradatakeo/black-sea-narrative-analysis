2 June 2022 Within the framework of his working visit to Brussels, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili continues his meetings with the European Commissioners. The Minister of Foreign Affairs met today with the European Commissioner for Budget and Administration, Johannes Hahn, and the European Commissioner for Agriculture, Janusz Wojciechowski. At the meeting with the European Commissioners, the sides positively assessed the dynamic cooperation between Georgia and the European Union, highlighting the importance of Georgia's application for EU membership. Special attention was paid to the active efforts and activities of Johannes Hahn, as European Commissioner for European Neighborhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations in Georgia. In this light, the sides made a reference to the EaP European School launched in Tbilisi. The meeting also focused on the ongoing or implemented reforms in the country under the Association Agreement. The sides discussed Georgia's sectoral rapprochement with the European Union and the steps already taken and planned in this regard. At the meeting with the European Commissioner for Agriculture, special emphasis was placed on the implementation of the Deep and Comprehensive Free Trade Agreement, playing an important role in increasing trade turnover between Georgia and the EU and increasing the country's export potential.
