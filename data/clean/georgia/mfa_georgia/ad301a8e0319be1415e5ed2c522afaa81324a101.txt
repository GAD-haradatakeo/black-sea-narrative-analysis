1 July 2025 Within the framework of the United Nations High-Level Conference, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with Imma Tor Faus, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Principality of Andorra. The two ministers reviewed the dynamics of bilateral cooperation across various fields and discussed the potential for its further deepening. Particular attention was given to sectoral collaboration in the areas of tourism, environmental protection, innovation, culture, and education, with an emphasis on opportunities for sharing best practices and experiences.
