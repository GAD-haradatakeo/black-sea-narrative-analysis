19 February 2024 On 19 February 2024, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the Representative of the United Nations Children's Fund in Georgia, Jesper Moller. The meeting addressed issues concerning cooperation between Georgia and the United Nations Children's Fund. Discussions centered on the difficult situation in the occupied regions of Georgia.
