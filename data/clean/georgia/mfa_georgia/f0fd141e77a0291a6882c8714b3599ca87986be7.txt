24 March 2025 On 24 March 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with a business delegation from China’s Shaanxi Province. The discussions focused on cooperation in the field of investments, the development of trade between Georgia and China, and the strategic importance of the Middle Corridor. Particular emphasis was placed on Georgia’s strategic geographic location, its attractive investment climate, and the favorable business environment. The meeting also addressed the details of future collaboration with the Chinese delegation, including specific investment and business projects across various sectors. The parties agreed to maintain close communication to facilitate cooperation and to coordinate future visits of the delegation to Georgia.
