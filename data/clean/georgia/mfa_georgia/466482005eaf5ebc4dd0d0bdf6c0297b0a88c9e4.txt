2 November 2023 Within the framework of his visit to Berlin, the Minister of Foreign Affairs held a meeting with the French Secretary of State for European Affairs, Laurence Boone. The sides discussed the main aspects of partnership and friendly relations between the two countries. The importance of strategic dialogue between Georgia and France was emphasized. Discussions focused on the situation in the region and the security environment. The sides reviewed the European integration process of Georgia. Ilia Darchiashvili spoke at length about Georgia’s progress on the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations and the results achieved in this regard.
