24 September 2025 On 23 September 2025, within the framework of the United Nations General Assembly, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary, Péter Szijjártó. The ministers discussed current issues of bilateral relations and the close cooperation between Georgia and Hungary, which is characterized by the nature of a strategic partnership. The meeting also addressed relations with the European Union and matters related to Georgia’s European integration process. Maka Botchorishvili expressed gratitude to her Hungarian counterpart for supporting Georgia’s European aspirations at both the political and practical-expert levels. It was emphasized that the two sides will continue their joint efforts in this direction.
