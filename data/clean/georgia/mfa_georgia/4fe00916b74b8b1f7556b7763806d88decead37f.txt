23 September 2019 On the sidelines of the 74th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Iranian counterpart, Ministerof Foreign Affairs Mohammad JavadZarif. Discussing the existing relations between Georgia and Iran, the two ministers welcomed the increasing level of people-to-people contacts and tourism cooperation between the two countries. The Georgian Foreign Minister briefed his Iranian colleague on the difficult security situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. The sides emphasized the importance of peace and security for stabile development and prosperity. Zalkaliani thanked Iran for the support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
