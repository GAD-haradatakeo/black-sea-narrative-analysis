25 April 2018 25.04.2018, New York – Within the framework of his visit to New York, Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Côte d'Ivoire Marcel Amon-Tanoh. The two ministers spoke about the co-operation between Georgia and Côte d'Ivoire within the framework of international organizations paying special attention to various aspects of co-operation under the UN Sustainable Development Goals. Mikheil Janelidze brought to the attention of his Ivorian colleague Georgia’s priorities during the chairmanship of Open Government Initiative.
