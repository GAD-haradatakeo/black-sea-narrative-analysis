19 February 2021 On 19 February 2021, Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili held an online meeting with Thomas Buchsbaum, Special Envoy on Eastern Partnership of the Austrian Federal Ministry for Europe, Integration and Foreign Affairs. Discussions focused on main topics on the agenda of the forthcoming summit scheduled for the second half of 2021. The Austrian Ambassador reaffirmed the strategic importance of Eastern Partnership, as well as the need for more effective use of differentiation principle within EaP. He also stressed that significance of showing respect for the associated partners’ European choice and aspirations and assisting them on their path to EU integration.
