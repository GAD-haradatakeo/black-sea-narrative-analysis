14 December 2017 14.12.2017, Tbilisi – Under the aegis of the Tbilisi Office of the Republic of Korea to Georgia, Georgia and Korea celebrated the 25th anniversary of establishing diplomatic ties. Representatives of diplomatic corps and other high-level guests attended the special reception on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Korea today. Addressing the guests attending the reception evening, Georgia’s Deputy Foreign Minister George Sharvashidze praised cooperation between Georgia and Korea. Minister Sharvashidze said the trade-economic relations between the two countries have been of the greatest importance in recent years. He also spoke about the people-to-people contacts and cultural links noting with satisfaction the translation and publication of Rustaveli’s “The Knight In the Panter’s Skin” in the Korean language.
