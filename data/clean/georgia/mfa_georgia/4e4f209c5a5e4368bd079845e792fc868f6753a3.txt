28 June 2018 28.06.2018, Tbilisi – on 28 June 2018, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Armenian Ambassador to Georgia Ruben Sadoyan. Ruben Sadoyan handed the Armenian Foreign Minister’s congratulation letter to David Zalkalian on the occasion of the latter’s appointment as Foreign Minister. The sides discussed issues relating to bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Armenia. It was emphasized that the new government of Georgia will continue good-neighbourly relations with Armenia.
