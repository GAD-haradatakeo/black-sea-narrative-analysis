28 February 2023 Discussions focused on Georgia's successful cooperation with the UN High Commissioner's Office and the important projects that the organization implements in Georgia to improve the condition of internally displaced persons. High appraisal was given to the Georgian Government’s policy to improve the social and humanitarian situation of IDPs. The High Commissioner positively evaluated the Georgian government’s policy to receive Ukrainian refugees, which is very important considering the severe humanitarian situation that has been created in the world amid the war in Ukraine.
