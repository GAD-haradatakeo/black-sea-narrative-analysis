6 February 2019 06.02.2019, Washington – The bipartisan support of the U.S. Congress for Georgia was highlighted at the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani’s meeting with Senator Bill Flores(R-Texas). The Minister thanked the Senator for the unprecedented support of the U.S. Congress to which attests an increasing number of its acts and resolutions in support for Georgia. The sides welcomed the increased amount of U.S. financial assistance to Georgia as evidenced by the U.S. Appropriations act 2019. Bill Flores expressed his wish to become a member of the House Georgia Caucus and further assist Georgia in dealing with the challenges facing it. The U.S. Senator reaffirmed his strong support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. While on the theme of the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, David Zalkaliani updated Bill Flores on the Georgian government’s peaceful initiative with regard to conflict resolution.
