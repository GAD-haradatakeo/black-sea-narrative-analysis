1 November 2017 1.11.2017 Tbilisi -The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the terroristact that hit New York City on October 31, 2017 leaving several people dead and injured. We are shocked by this tragedy and our hearts and minds are with the families and loved ones of the victims of this appalling attack. In this hour of grief and sorrow, our utmost sympathy and solidarity goes to the people of the United States and the U.S. government. Documents Similar News
