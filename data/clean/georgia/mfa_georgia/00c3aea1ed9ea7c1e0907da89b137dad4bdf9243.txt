11 April 2023 On 11 April 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia held a meeting with the U.S. Assistant Secretary of Commerce for Export Administration, Thea Rozman Kendler. The sides talked about the important issues of the strategic partnership between Georgia and the U.S. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and the U.S. Assistant Secretary of Commerce also discussed the current processes and existing challenges facing the region.
