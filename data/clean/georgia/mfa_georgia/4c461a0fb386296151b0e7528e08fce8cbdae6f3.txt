15 June 2020 Within the framework of the Georgian Prime Minister Giorgi Gakharia’s meeting with the diplomatic corps, the Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani thanked diplomats and the international community for their cooperation and support of Georgia during the COVID-19 pandemic. “I was looking forward to this day as personal relations are critically important for us, diplomats. I have had many telephone conversations with my colleagues over the past months, today I spoke over the phone with the German Foreign Minister and we expressed the concern that remote relations create some kind of barriers in diplomacy and we are pleased that we can now return to normal gradually and restore what is called personal relations in diplomacy” -Zalkaliani said. He thanked diplomats, international organizations and various countries for their assistance to Georgia in the fight against the pandemic. The Minister underlined that even amid the pandemic, Russia continued illegal activities and the borderization process in Georgia’s occupied territories, and even conducted a disinformation campaign against such successful institutions as the Lugar laboratory. “Our friends and partners demonstrated to Georgia an unprecedented level of support. The UN, OSCE, EU and various countries made statement in support for Georgia that is a very important fact” – Zalkaliani said.
