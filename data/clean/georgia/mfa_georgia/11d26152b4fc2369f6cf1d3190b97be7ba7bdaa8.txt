24 July 2017 24.07.2017 Tbilisi -The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns aggressive and violent actions in the eastern Ukraine. 11 people were killed and 15 people wounded in the period between 18 and 20 of July as a result of an assault on the Ukrainian armed forces, which emphasizes an unprecedented aggravation of the situation. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its condolences to the families of the perished and wishes speedy recovery to the wounded. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia condemns once again the act of aggression committed by the Russian Federation against the Ukrainian state and calls on the international community for a forceful response.
