27 April 2021 From 27 to 29 April 2021, the Lithuanian Foreign Minister, Gabrielius Landsbergis, will hold an official visit to Georgia. Within the framework of the meeting the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, will hold a meeting with his colleague, after which the delegations of the two states will hold a meeting in a wider format. The Lithuanian Foregin Minister will also hold meetings with President, Salome Zourabichvili, the PM, Irakli Gharibashvili and Chairman of Parliament of Georgia Kakha Kuchava. During his stay in Tbilisi, the Lithuanian Foreign Minister will visit the occupation line in the Odzisi village, where he will acquaint himself with the situation on the ground along with the EUMM, honouring those who gave their lives for the unity of the Georgian nation, laying wreath to the heroes’ memorial.
