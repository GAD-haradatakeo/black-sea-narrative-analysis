16 December 2025 The Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will participate in the diplomatic conference in The Hague held under the auspices of the Council of Europe to launch the International Claims Commission for Ukraine. Georgia will sign the Convention establishing the International Claims Commission for Ukraine. The Commission will serve as a body mandated to examine claims for compensation for damage, loss or injury caused by the Russian Federation’s internationally wrongful acts in or against Ukraine on or after 24 February 2022.
