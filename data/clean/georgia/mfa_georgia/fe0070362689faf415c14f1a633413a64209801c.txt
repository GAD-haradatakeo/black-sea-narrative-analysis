29 March 2024 Within the framework of his visit to Argentina, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia met with representatives of the Georgian diaspora. The gathering took place at the Georgian Embassy in Buenos Aires and was attended by Georgians with longstanding ties to Argentina, including those who arrived during Soviet repressions, World War II, and a smaller group who migrated in the 1990s following Georgia's independence. During the meeting, the minister expressed gratitude to the diaspora for their efforts in upholding the national identity.
