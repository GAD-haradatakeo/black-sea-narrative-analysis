25 May 2017 25.05.2017, Kvareli – Diplomatic Tour - 2017 organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs for foreign diplomats accredited to Georgia came to a close. Within the framework of the tour, both resident and non-resident ambassadors travelled to Kvareli, Kakheti Region. Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze delivered a welcoming speech for the foreign diplomats thanking them for participation in the tour and welcoming the fact that the level of participation increases from year to year. According to the Minister, the diplomatic tour offers the diplomats the opportunity to get familiarized with the economic and tourist potential of various regions of Georgia. The Kakheti Development Agency conducted a presentation on culture and traditions of the Kakheti region, whereas a representative of the Georgian Tourism Administration highlighted the tourism potential of Georgia.
