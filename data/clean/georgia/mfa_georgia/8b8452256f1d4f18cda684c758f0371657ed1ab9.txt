31 October 2018 31.10.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a farewell meeting with a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Socialist Republic of Viet Nam, Doan Thi Xuan Hien. Thanking the Ambassador for his contribution to the development of friendly relations between the two countries, the Minister wished him success in his future activities. The sides discussed the bilateral relations, including the increasing level of co-operation between Georgia and Viet Nam expressing the hope that this co-operation will further deepen in the future.
