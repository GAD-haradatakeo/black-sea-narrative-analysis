2 November 2017 2.11.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Abdullah ibn Muhammad Al ash-Sheikh, the Chairman of the Consultative Assembly of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, also known as Majlis Ash-Shura. Discussions focused on issues on the agenda of bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Saudi Arabia. Special attention was paid to the increasing level of relations in the areas of economy, tourism and investments that will further contribute to the strengthening of people-to-people contacts. Talking points also included the situation in the region and security challenges. The sides expressed the readiness to further deepen fruitful co-operation. Documents Similar News
