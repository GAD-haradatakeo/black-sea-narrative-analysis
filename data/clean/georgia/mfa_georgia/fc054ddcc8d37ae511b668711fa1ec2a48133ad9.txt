3 September 2021 On 3 September 2021, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimir Konstantinidi, met with the Ambassador of Bosnia and Herzegovina to Georgia, Adis Alagić. The diplomat handed copies of his credentials to the Deputy Foreign Minister. At the meeting, the sides discussed the dynamics of relations between the two countries and stressed the importance of deepening fruitful cooperation.
