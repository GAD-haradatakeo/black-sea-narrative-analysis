24 October 2022 "Georgia and Azerbaijan have a close strategic partnership, good neighbourly relations. Within the framework of the working visit, the prime minister and the president will have the opportunity to discuss all the important issues that we have in our bilateral relations”, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili said when commenting on the visit of the President of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Ilham Aliyev to Georgia. “There is a very wide range of topics in relations between the two states, with Aliyev’s visit being an opportunity to discuss them. Another important topic to be discussed on the backdrop of regional challenges is how the two states should ensure peace and stability in our region and all the issues that exist in multilateral formats. Georgia’s position is that peace and stability in the region is essentially important. Georgia has always been committed to making its own contribution to maintaining peace and stability in the region”, Ilia Darchiashvili is quoted as saying. As the Georgian Foreign Minister noted, Georgia and Azerbaijan have a special role in ensuring the region’s main transit function. The President of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Ilham Aliyev visited Georgia today for a working visit.
