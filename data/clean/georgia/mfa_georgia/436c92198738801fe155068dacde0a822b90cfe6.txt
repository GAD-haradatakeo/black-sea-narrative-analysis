31 July 2018 31.07.2018 –Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Turkey to Georgia, Fatma Ceren Yazgan. The Ambassador congratulated David Zalkaliani on his appointment as Foreign Minister and wished him success in his future endeavours. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral relations highlighting the importance of partnership between the two countries.
