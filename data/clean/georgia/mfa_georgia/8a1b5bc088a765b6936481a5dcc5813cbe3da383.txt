14 January 2021 On 14 January 2021 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, held an online working meeting with his Ukrainian colleague, Dmytro Kuleba, and the interim head of the Moldovan government, Moldova’s Foreign and European Integration Minister, Aureliu Ciokoi. The parties discussed the ways of strengthening cooperation and the prospects for prospects for dimenstions of mutual interest. The main emphasis was made on the relations with the EU and the joint efforts of the three associated states in terms of the process of speeding up European integration. The parties discussed the Eastern Partnership Initiative and the importance of its upcoming summit to be held in 2021 in terms of future strengthening of the trilateral partnership.
