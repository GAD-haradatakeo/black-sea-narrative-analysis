23 September 2024 On 23 September 2024, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, attended the event celebrating Saudi Arabia's National Day, where he extended wishes of peace, success, and prosperity to the Saudi people on behalf of the Government and people of Georgia. In his address, the Deputy Minister highlighted the recent efforts by the Georgian government that have further strengthened the friendly relations between Georgia and Saudi Arabia. He underscored the significance of the Prime Minister of Georgia's visit to Saudi Arabia two years ago, following the historic visit of Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs to the Kingdom in January, marking the first such visit in the history of independent Georgia. Khvtisiashvili expressed satisfaction with the deepening and expanding relations between the two countries in various sectors. He remarked, "We have dynamic relations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs and various sectoral agencies. I am also pleased to note the fruitful cooperation between the legislative bodies of both countries. Parliamentary friendship groups have been formed, and high-level parliamentary visits have taken place. It is encouraging to see the growth of cultural relations and people-to-people connections between our nations." The Deputy Minister also stressed the critical importance of diversifying and expanding economic cooperation, especially given the complex challenges facing the region and the world. The event was attended by representatives of accredited diplomatic missions in Georgia, as well as officials from the legislative and executive branches, along with business and cultural leaders.
