22 March 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the death of over 90 people in a ferry sinking in the Tigris River near the city of Mosul of the Republic of Iraq on March 21, 2019. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the Iraqi Government as well as to the families of the disaster victims and wishes speedy recovery to the injured.
