31 October 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a working meeting with the Ambassador of the United States of America to Georgia, Kelly Degnan. Discussions focused on a wide range of strategic partnership between the U.S. and Georgia. The U.S. Ambassador once again reaffirmed the U.S. government’s strong support for Georgia and the readiness to further deepen strategic partnership in all main areas, including defence and security, as well as trade, economy and investment. The sides also reviewed the security environment in the region; Once again, the U.S. support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia was confirmed.
