17 September 2021 On 17 September 2021, with the support of the Administration of the President of Georgia, an award ceremony was held within the framework of the programs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia - "Be the Young Ambassador of Your Country", "Support to Diaspora Initiatives" and "Support to Georgian Dance and Song Ensembles Abroad". The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani delivered a speech. "We fully realize the Georgian Diaspora’s important role in the development of our country. The development of our country would be hardly successful without the diaspora. "Ensuring the diaspora’s involvement in building our country is our government’s priority, which is reflected in the government program for 2021-2024 "Building a European state ", as well as in the foreign policy strategy," said David Zalkaliani. According to the Minister, the 2021 program incorporated a new component, which envisages the empowerment of public diplomacy in the process of Georgia's European integration. "Georgia has a very ambitious plan - to become a full member of the European Union, which calls for the consolidation of the entire nation and the active involvement and support of the diaspora," - said the Minister. "The diaspora can act as a bridge between the country of origin and the host country, it has the great role to play in building our country’s positive image abroad and in spreading our culture, values ​​and values," said David Zalkaliani. Georgia recognizes young people’s important role in the development our country and creates a system of support for young people abroad, which ensures the full realization of their potential, as well as their active participation in the political and public life of Georgia.
