10 July 2017 Georgia strongly condemns the terrorist attacks on an army checkpoint in Rafah, Sinai Peninsula of the Arab Republic of Egypt, on 7 July 2017 that resulted in the death of at least 23 Egyptian soldiers and left tens of others – injured. Georgia condemns terrorism in all its forms and supports the efforts of the government of the Arab Republic of Egypt aimed at strengthening stability and eradicating violent extremism in the country. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs conveys its condolences to the families of terrorism victims, and wishes speedy recovery to the injured expressing the hope that perpetrators of this brutal crime will be brought to justice.
