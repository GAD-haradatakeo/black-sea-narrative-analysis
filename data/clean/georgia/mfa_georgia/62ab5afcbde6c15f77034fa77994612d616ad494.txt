9 September 2021 On 9 September 2021, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia met with the Representative of the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in Georgia, Camlin Ferrell. The sides discussed issues related to the activities of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in Georgia. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia briefed the UNHCR Representative on the humanitarian situation in the Russian-occupied regions of Georgia. Talking points also included restrictions on free movement, the violation of property rights and the threat of complete isolation of the Russian-occupied regions of Georgia. The sides highlighted the importance of international organizations’ continuous involvement in ensuring the due protection of human rights in the Russian-occupied regions of Georgia.
