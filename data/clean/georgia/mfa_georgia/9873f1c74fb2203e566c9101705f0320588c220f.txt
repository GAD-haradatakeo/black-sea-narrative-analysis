15 December 2023 On 13-14 December, with the organization of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the assistance of the EU funded project "Facility for the Implementation of the EU-Georgia Association Agreement - II", LEPL Digital Governance Agency conducted training sessions for the electronic system on the "Communication Strategy of the Government of Georgia on Georgia's Membership in the EU and NATO for 2021-2025". Within the framework of the training, the members of the inter-agency working group had the opportunity to get acquainted in detail with the electronic system, which will serve for the development, coordination and implementation of the action plan of the communication strategy regarding Georgia's accession to the EU and NATO.
