19 February 2022 Within the framework of the Munich Security Conference, the Vice Prime Minister/ Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the OSCE Secretary General, Helga Schmid to discuss the difficult security environment and the challenges facing OSCE area. Particular attention was paid to the recent development of events around Ukraine and the ongoing security dialogue between the West and Russia. The sides emphasized the need to protect the rights of a sovereign state and to avoid further escalation of the armed conflict, which calls for maximum international efforts. Thanking the Georgian side for the work of Georgian monitors in the OSCE Special Mission in Ukraine, the OSCE Secretary General highlighted that everything is done to ensure their security. The sides also paid special attention to the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions and to the occupation regimes’ frequent provocations threatening the daily lives of local residents and their long-term security.
