23 September 2025 On 22 September 2025, within the framework of the 80th session of the United Nations General Assembly, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the United Arab Emirates, Sheikh Mohamed Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan. During the meeting, the ministers expressed satisfaction with the dynamic development of friendly and partnership-based relations between the two countries, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. The exchange of high-level visits was assessed positively, with particular emphasis placed on the visit of the President of the United Arab Emirates, Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan, to Georgia. Within the framework of this visit, the UAE side conveyed its readiness to implement large-scale investment projects in Georgia and to expand cooperation across various fields. The ministers discussed cooperation in the sphere of investments, the deepening of trade and economic ties, as well as the importance of the Middle Corridor. In this context, special attention was drawn to Georgia’s strategic location, attractive investment climate, and business-friendly environment.
