29 March 2019 29.03.2019, Tbilisi - Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili met with the Executive Director of theInternational Visegrad FundAndor Dávid. Speaking about the Fund’s main activities Andor Dávid highlighted the active participation of Georgian NGOs in the Fund-financed projects both ongoing and already implemented. According to him, special attention is paid to co-operation with Eastern Partnership countries. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili thanked Andor Dávid for theInternational Visegrad Fund’s support of Georgia’s non-governmental sector that makes its positive impact on the democratic development processes in Georgia. According to Andor Dávid, theInternational Visegrad Fund will continue to support Georgia in all important areas by sharing best practices and financing needs-adapted projects.
