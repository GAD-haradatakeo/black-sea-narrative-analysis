11 April 2018 11.04.2018 - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting held a meeting with students of the College of Europe. The Foreign Minister provided the students with insights in Georgia’s foreign policy priorities, integration with the EU and NATO, economic progress of the country, as well as the situation in Georgia’s occupied occupied territories and on the government’s efforts to overcome the challenges.
