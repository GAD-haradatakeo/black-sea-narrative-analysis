26 August 2022 On 26 August 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the special representative of the President of the Republic of Korea. Discussing a wide range of bilateral relations, the sides highlighted the need to strengthen economic ties between the two countries. The importance of agreements on the promotion and protection of free trade and investments was emphasized in this context. Discussions also focused on the main issues of cooperation within the international organizations. The Georgian Deputy Minister thanked the Korean side for supporting Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
