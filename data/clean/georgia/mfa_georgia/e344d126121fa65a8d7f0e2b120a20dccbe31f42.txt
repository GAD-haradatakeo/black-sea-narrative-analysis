3 September 2025 Davit Mekvabishvili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Holy See (Vatican), presented copies of his Letters of Credence to His Excellency Giampaolo Cantini, Grand Chancellor for Foreign Affairs of the Sovereign Military Order of St. John of Malta. Following the presentation ceremony, a meeting was held between the Ambassador of Georgia and the Grand Chancellor of the Order of Malta. During the meeting, the parties discussed the existing friendly relations between Georgia and the Order of Malta, as well as prospects for their further deepening. Particular attention was devoted to the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and to the policy of the Government of Georgia aimed at the peaceful restoration of the country’s territorial integrity.
