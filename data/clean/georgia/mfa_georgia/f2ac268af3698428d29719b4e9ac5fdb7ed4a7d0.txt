20 March 2019 20.03.2019, Argentina –Within the framework of the United Nations High-level Conference on South-South Cooperation, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with his Mozambican counterpart, JoséCondungua Pacheco. David Zalkaliani conveyed to his colleague his condolences over the death and injury of tens of people by the Tsunami and expressed his readiness to contribute, along with the international community, to the elimination of disaster consequences. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of issues relating to co-operation between the two countries. The Georgian delegation expressed its readiness to host the Mozambican delegation to share with them Georgia’s successful reforms in the field of public services, healthcare, good governance, education and agriculture, as well as to conduct the next round of political consultations between the Foreign Ministries of Georgia and Mozambique.
