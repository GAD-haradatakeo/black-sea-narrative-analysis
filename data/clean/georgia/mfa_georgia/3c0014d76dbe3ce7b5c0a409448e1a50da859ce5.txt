12 September 2017 As part of the regional tour of South Asia, the Georgian delegation led by the Deputy Foreign Minister David Jalagania paid a working visit to the People’s Republic of Bangladesh. David Jalagania held a meeting with the Foreign Minister of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh Abul Hassan Mahmood Ali. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral and multilateral cooperation in the areas of tourism, education, light industry and pharmacy. The need of strengthening inter-parliamentary relations and further improvement of the bilateral legislative framework was also highlighted during the meeting. The sides expressed their readiness to continue co-operation within the framework of international organizations. The Foreign Minister of Bangladesh reaffirmed his government’s firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. The same day, the Georgian delegation held meetings with the vice president of the Bangladesh Garment Manufacturers and Exporters Association (BGMEA) and the governing director of the BITOPI Group Ltd.
