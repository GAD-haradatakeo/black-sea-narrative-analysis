10 December 2023 On 11-12 December 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili will pay a working visit to Brussels. The Minister will take part in the Eastern Partnership Ministerial Meeting to discuss the existing cooperation within the Eastern Partnership, focusing on the achievements in 2022-2023 and future prospects.
