20 April 2022 On 20 April 2022, a telephone conversation took place between the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the People's Republic of China, Wang Yi. The Chinese Foreign Minister congratulated his Georgian counterpart on his appointment as Foreign Minister and wished him success in his future endeavours. Discussing a wide range of relations between Georgia and China, the Ministers highlighted the positive dynamics of trade and economic cooperation. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked the Chinese government for its humanitarian support to Georgia during the global pandemic. Speaking to Ilia Darchiashvili, Wang Yi reaffirmed China's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The Ministers welcomed the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and China. The sides reaffirmed their commitment to continue fruitful cooperation between the two countries. The Chinese Foreign Minister invited his Georgian counterpart to Beijing for a visit.
