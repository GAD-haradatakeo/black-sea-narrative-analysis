22 March 2024 "As an ordinary citizen and Minister of Foreign Affairs, I would like to express special respect and warmth to our citizens who temporarily live outside our country. The government's vision is that the representatives of the diaspora are one of the most valuable segments of society." - said the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, during his speech in the parliament. Furthermore, Darchiashvili indicated that he would return to the legislative body within the next two months during the minister's hours to provide a detailed overview of the Georgian government's efforts. These efforts are aimed at safeguarding the rights of citizens abroad, enhancing their experience, and creating opportunities that could potentially encourage their return to Georgia. He assured the diaspora that the government is committed to building a strong state and positioning Georgia as a robust member of the European family, outlining this as their primary vision. Also, the minister once again called on the citizens of Georgia outside the country to register at the consular register. "I appeal to the diaspora that every Georgian diplomat is ready to protect the rights of the Georgian population, the consular registration is also aimed at that, so that we can help you and implement the mechanisms you need when you are outside the borders of the country," the minister said. The minister reiterated his call for citizens of Georgia residing outside the country to register at the consular register. “I appeal directly to the diaspora that every Georgian diplomat is prepared to defend the rights of the Georgian population. The purpose of consular registration is to facilitate assistance and implement necessary mechanisms for Georgian citizens when they are outside the country's borders”, the minister said.
