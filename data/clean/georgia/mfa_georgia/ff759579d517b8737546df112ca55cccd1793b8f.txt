24 August 2023 On 24 August, 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a farewell meeting with Kelly Degnan, the extraordinary and plenipotentiary ambassador of the United States of America to Georgia. During the meeting, the sides emphasized the importance of the existing strategic partnership between Georgia and the United States of America and reviewed the current agenda of bilateral relations and future plans for cooperation in priority areas. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked Kelly Degnan for the fruitful cooperation, for her contribution to deepening the ties between the two countries and wished her success in her future activities.
