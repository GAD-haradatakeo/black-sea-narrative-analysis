14 May 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a working meeting with the Ambassador of the United States of America to Georgia, Kelly Degnan. The sides discussed a wide range of strategic partnership between the United States and Georgia. The Foreign Minister expressed his gratitude for the United States’ strong support for Georgia and its Euro-Atlantic integration course, as well as for its significant contribution to strengthening Georgia’s defence capabilities and democratic institutions, and to boosting the country’s economic growth. Special mention was made of the United States' strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders. Discussions also focused on the further deepening of the U.S.-Georgia strategic partnership in all key areas, including defence and security, trade, economic and investment fields.
