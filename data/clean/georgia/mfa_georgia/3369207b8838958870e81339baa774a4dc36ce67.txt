12 May 2020 The Georgian government continues the orderly and gradual repatriation process. Today the charter flight will bring home up to 120 Georgians from London, including students, elderly people, individuals with special needs and transit passengers. Not all tickets for the flight were sold, which points to the decreasing number of Georgians wishing to repatriate. Under the Coordination Council’s schedule, flights have been performed from various cities of the world, including from Berlin, Vienna, London, Athens, U.S., Larnaca, Amsterdam, Beijing, Rome, Paris, Tel-Aviv, Barcelona, Warsaw, Madrid, Prague, etc. Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 19,965 citizens of Georgia in the host countries. The Government has organized the repatriation 10,698 citizens to Georgia, as of 12 May. The Georgian MFA website is updated every day to provide the latest information regarding support measures undertaken by the Georgian government with respect to Georgian citizens abroad.
