18 December 2019 18.12.2019, Tbilisi – Meeting on “U.S and Georgia; Developments in South America” was held within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference -2019. First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili, Director of the Department of Americas, Gvaram Khandamishvili, Ambassador of Georgia to the U.S. David Bakradze, Ambasador of Georgia to the Argentine Republic, Irakli Kurashvili and Ambassador of Georgia to the Federative Republic of Brazil, David Solomonia participated in the panel discussions. Discussions focused on Georgia-U.S. strategic partnership and the ways to further strengthen close relations between the two countries. The Ambassadors also paid special attention to the problems facing the region, the situation within the international organizations, political and economic processes taking place in the host countries and their cooperation with Georgia.
