29 May 2018 29.05.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Secretary General of the Inter-Parliamentary Union (IPU), Martin Chungong. The sides highly appraised the co-operation between Georgia and the IPU emphasizing the importance of further deepening the relations. Special attention was paid to the grave security and human rights situation in Georgia’s in Georgia’s occupied territories. Special note was taken of the need of the international community’s due involvement and response. Discussons focused on the recognition by Syria of the so-called independence of Georgia’s occupied territories. It was emphasized that with this step, Asad’s regime demonstrated its support for the Russian aggression in Georgia, the illegal occupation of the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions and the ongoingethnic cleansing process.
