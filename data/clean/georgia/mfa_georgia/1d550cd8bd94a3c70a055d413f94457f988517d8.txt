31 October 2023 On 31 October 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Czech Republic to Georgia, Petr Kubernat, who recently started his diplomatic mission in Georgia. The first deputy minister congratulated the ambassador on his appointment to Georgia and wished him success. The sides welcomed the traditional close relations between Georgia and the Czech Republic and fruitful cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The First Deputy Minister gave a detailed review of Georgia's foreign policy priorities. Special note was taken of the progress achieved by Georgia in the process of European integration and the importance of receiving the candidate country status at the end of this year. Special attention was paid to the security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied territories of Georgia; The sides emphasized the great importance of partners’ strong support; the sides also discussed the process of Georgia's integration into NATO. Discussions focused on the security environment in the region and Georgia’s role in ensuring peace in the region.
