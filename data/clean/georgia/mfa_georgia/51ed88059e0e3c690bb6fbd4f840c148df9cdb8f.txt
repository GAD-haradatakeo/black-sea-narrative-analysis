20 June 2022 On 20 June 2022, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Democratic Republic of Algeria, Sofiane Mimouni, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Burundi, Gerard Ntahorwaroye Bikebako, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia, Adem Mohammed Mahmud, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Ghana, Francisca Ashitey-Odunton and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Chad, Dangaï Nokour Guet Adoum presented copies of their credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili. At the meetings held following the presentation ceremony, the sides discussed current issues of bilateral relations and reaffirmed their readiness for further cooperation and intensification of relations in both bilateral and multilateral formats.
