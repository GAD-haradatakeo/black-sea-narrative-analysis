2 December 2017 Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze will visit Brussels, Belgium on 4 and 5 December where he will participate in the NATO Foreign Ministers’ meeting. Janelidze will meet with high officials including NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg and United States (US) Secretary of State Rex Tillerson, as well as foreign ministers of other NATO member states. Minister Janelidze will take part in Georgia-NATO commission meeting that will take place in Brussels.
