6 March 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Ambassador-designate of the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg to Georgia, Alain de Muyser (residing in Luxembourg). During the meeting, both parties expressed satisfaction with the friendly relations established between their countries and underscored the importance of expanding cooperation in the future. Particular attention was given to Georgia-EU relations. The discussion also covered ongoing developments in Georgia and the broader regional security environment.
