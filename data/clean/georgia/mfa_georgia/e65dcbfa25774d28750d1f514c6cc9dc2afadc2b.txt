29 June 2018 28.06.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani participated in the meeting dedicated to Independence Day of Belarus. Congratulating the Belarusian people on their national holiday, the Minister highlighted the particularly friendly relations between Georgia and Belarus and an increasing number of mutual visits. David Zalkaliani recalled his first diplomatic assignment to Belarus in the capacity of Ambassador as an additional factor for enjoying the celebration of the day. The event dedicated to the Independence Day of Belarus was attended by representatives of Georgia’s executive and legislative authorities, members of the diplomatic corps accredited to Georgia and other invited guests.
