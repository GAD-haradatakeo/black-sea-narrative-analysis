20 October 2025 On 20 October 2025, hosted by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Lasha Darsalia, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia held a presentation of Georgia’s candidate for the World Health Organization (WHO) Executive Board, Irakli Sasania, First Deputy Minister of Internally Displaced Persons from the Occupied Territories, Labor, Health, and Social Affairs of Georgia, to representatives of embassies of countries in the WHO European Regional Committee. During the meeting, Irakli Sasania outlined his priorities in the event of his election to the Executive Board and emphasized Georgia’s steadfast commitment to the values of the World Health Organization. The First Deputy Minister highlighted that, if elected, he would contribute to advancing global health priorities, including strengthening health systems and promoting the digital health agenda, with a focus on data ethics and the integration of artificial intelligence. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia continues to actively implement its campaign in support of Georgia’s candidacy.
