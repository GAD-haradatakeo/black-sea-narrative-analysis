12 September 2022 On 12 September 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Minister of State for Economy and Trade of the United Arab Emirates Ahmed Ali Al Sayegh, who is on an official visit to Georgia. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides discussed the issues on the agenda between the two countries and prospects for future cooperation. The Georgian side thanked the Arab side for their steadfast support of Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. It was highlighted that the partnership with the United Arab Emirates has significantly increased in many directions in the last period. The sides focused on the increasingly higher level of economic cooperation between the two countries, including in the fields of investments and tourism, which contributes to the further activation of people-to-people ties.
