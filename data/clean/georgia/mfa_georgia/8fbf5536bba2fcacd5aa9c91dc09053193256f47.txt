26 February 2021 On 25 February, the Georgian Institute of Politics (GIP) organized an online discussion on: Georgia’s EU aspirations: Expectations and Challenges. The discussion was opened by the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili who spoke about Georgia’s EU integration process aimed at achieving EU membership as a final goal. According to Makharoblishvili, the eligibility of Georgia to apply for EU membership in 2024 will be determined based on the progress Georgia has achieved in the implementation of the EU-Georgia Association Agreement. According to the First Deputy Foreign Minister, the EU should continue to support Georgia’s European integration, despite Russia’s aggressive actions against EU associated partners, aimed at re-establishing its undemocratic influence in the EaP area.
