20 July 2017 19.07.2017, Tbilisi –Deputy Foreign Minister David Jalagania and participants of Ambassadorial 2017 discussed issues relating to co-operation with Asian and Pacific countries. Discussions, in particular, focused on relations with China, India, South Corea, Australia and other countries of the region within bilateral and multilateral formats. Talking points also included free trade regime with China, Georgia’s active participation in One belt One road Initiative, strategic partnership with Japan resulting in the simplification of immigration regime for Georgian citizens and the launch of investment agreement negotiations.
