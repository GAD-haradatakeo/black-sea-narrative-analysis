Alexander Khvtisiashvili has met with non-resident ambassadors to Georgia 3 September 2021 On 3 September 2021, the Ambassadors of the Philippines, Uzbekistan and Jordan, Maria Elena Algabre, Bakhrom Ashrafkhanov and Sami Asem Ghosheh presented copies of their credentials to the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili. At a meeting held following the presentation ceremony, Alexander Khvtisiashvili congratulated the ambassadors on their appointment and wished them success in their activities. See All
