26 January 2018 26.01.2018, Davos - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the Speaker of the National Assembly of the Republic of Serbia Maja Gojkovic. The sides reaffirmed their mutual respect for the principle of sovereignty and territorial integrity. They welcomed the increasing level of co-operation between the two countires both in bilateral and multilateral formats. The discussions focused on the upcoming visits and bilateral co-operation agreements to be signed between the sides. Special note was taken of the progress Georgia has made on the European integration path and the importance of sharing Serbia’s successful experience. The importance of deepening inter-parliamentary co-operation was also highlighted during the meeting. Mikheil Janelidze brought to the attention of Maja Gojkovic Georgia’s investment climate and the achievements in the tourism sector with a focus on the great potential of tourism co-operation between Georgia and Serbia.
