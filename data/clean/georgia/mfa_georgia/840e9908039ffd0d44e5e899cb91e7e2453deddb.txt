24 September 2022 An online meeting “Diaspora as Key Partner Across the Humanitarian-DevelopmentPeace-Nexus” was held during theHigh-level week of the 77th Session of the United Nations General Assembly.Antonio Vittorino,Director Generalof theInternational OrganizationforMigrationaddressed the participants with a welcome speech. During the meeting, the speakers presented their best experiences of sustainable and effective engagement of the diaspora. Emphasized the resources and capabilities of diaspora representatives through which they can provide invaluable humanitarian aid, and contribute to peace and development. Following the main panel, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimir Konstantinidi gave a speech as the first speaker. Emphasizing the close cooperation between Georgia and the Georgian diaspora, he talked about the effective steps taken jointly with Georgian compatriots living abroad during the crisis (especially during the global pandemic). Vladimir Konstantinidi acquainted the public with the programmes implemented and planned in cooperation with the International Organization of Migration (IOM), which promote the involvement of the diaspora in the economic development of the country.
