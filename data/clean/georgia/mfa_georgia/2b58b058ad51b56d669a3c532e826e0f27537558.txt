4 December 2025 On 4 December 2025, within the framework of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Deputy Prime Minister, Minister for Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade of the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, Xavier Bettel. The discussion focused on the dynamics of bilateral and multilateral cooperation, as well as on prospects for its further development. Particular attention was devoted to issues related to the Russia-Georgia conflict, ongoing developments in the region, and security challenges. The discussion also touched upon ongoing developments in Georgia, the challenges in Georgia–EU relations, and engagement with the institutions of the European Union.
