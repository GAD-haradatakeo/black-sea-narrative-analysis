Ilia Darchiashvili has met with his German counterpart 13 September 2022 Within the framework of his visit to Berlin, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with his German counterpart, the Federal Minister for Foreign Affairs, Annalena Baerbock. The Ministers reviewed the upcoming visit of the Georgian Prime Minister to Germany and his planned meetings with both the German Chancellor and the Federal President, and expressed the hope that the visit will take the partnership between the two countries to an even higher level.
