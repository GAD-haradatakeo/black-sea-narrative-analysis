16 July 2018 16.07.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Deputy Director General for European Neighbourhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations (DG NEAR) Katarina Mathernova. The sides discussed Georgia’s achievements in terms of EU integration, the process of ongoing reforms within the Association Agreement and the European Union’s financial assistance to Georgia. The sides discussed a meeting scheduled within the framework of the high-level sectoral dialogue. According to David Zalkaliani, European Integration is one of Georgia’s key priorities that is reflected in the Georgian Government’s programme. He also spoke about the Georgian Government’s new initiative – Road Map of EU Integration designed to speed up Georgia’a rapprochement with European standards. The delegation of the European Commission’s Directorate General reaffirmed once again that Georgia is the region’s leader in terms reforms that is attested by progress the country has achieved in all areas of the Association Agreement.
