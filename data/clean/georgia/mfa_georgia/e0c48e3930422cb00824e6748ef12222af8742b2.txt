3 September 2021 On 3 September 2021, the First Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador of Ireland to Georgia, Martina Feeney. The non-resident ambassador presented copies of her credentials to the First Deputy Minister. Discussing the existing cooperation between Georgia and Ireland, the sides and talked about the prospects of deepening relations in bilateral and multilateral formats.
