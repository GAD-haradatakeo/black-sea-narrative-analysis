6 December 2018 7.12.2018, Milan - On the sidelines of the 25th OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Foreign MinisterofBosnia and HerzegovinaIgorCrnadak. The Ministers discussed the issues relating to bilateral co-operation and to the exchange of high-level visits. Special attention was paid to Georgia’s success on the NATO integration path. Zalkaliani thanked his colleague for Bosnia and Herzegovina’s firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and for co-operation within the international format. The Ministers expressed their readiness to further facilitate co-operation in the trade-economic areas, as well as in terms of people-to-people contacts.
