​Georgia shares its success of the SDG implementation with the international community 31 October 2017 31.10.2017, Tbilisi - Representatives of twenty countries gather in Tbilisi, Georgia, for an international Forum “From Words to Action: SDG 16+ Implementing SDG 16+ at the local and national level.” From October 31 to November 2, 2017, the Forum will discuss best policy and practice in the achievement of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), with the focus on SDG16 – Peaceful, Just and Inclusive Societies.
