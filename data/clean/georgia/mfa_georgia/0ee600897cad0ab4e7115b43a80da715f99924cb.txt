31 August 2024 On 31 August 2024, the foreign ministers of the Organization of Eastern Caribbean States (OECS) visited the village of Khurvaleti, situated along the occupation line, during their official visit to Georgia. While there, they observed the severe humanitarian challenges resulting from the occupation. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, accompanied the ministers at the occupation line, providing them with detailed information about the current situation in the occupied territories and highlighting the significance of international community support in this matter. The OECS foreign ministers also paid tribute by laying a wreath at the memorial dedicated to the heroes who lost their lives fighting for Georgia's unity. During the visit, the ministers met with Georgia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili. Representatives of both the executive and legislative branches hosted the delegation. At the invitation of Ilia Darchiashvili, the foreign ministers of the OECS member states, including Grenada, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines, Saint Kitts and Nevis, Saint Lucia, and Antigua and Barbuda, are in Tbilisi for their first official visit.
