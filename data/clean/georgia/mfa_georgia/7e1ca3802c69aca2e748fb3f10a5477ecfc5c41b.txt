24 October 2022 On 24 October 2022, the ambassador-designate of the Republic of North Macedonia, Krum Efremov (residence in Kyiv) presented copies of his credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili. The Deputy Minister welcomed the first ambassador of the Republic of North Macedonia to Georgia and wished him success in his future work. The sides positively evaluated the dynamic development of relations between the two countries. They expressed the willingness to promote the deepening of bilateral cooperation in trade-economic, tourism, education and other areas of mutual interest. The sides focused special attention on the importance of sharing experience on the path of EU integration. While on the theme of NATO integration, the need to learn from the best practices of North Macedonia as the newest member was highlighted. Talking points also included prospects for the further deepening of existing positive relations within the framework of international formats.
