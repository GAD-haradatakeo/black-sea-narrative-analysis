12 October 2021 On 11 October 2021, during his visit to Geneva, the Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Assistant State Secretary at the Federal Department of Foreign Affairs of the Swiss Confederation, Anna Ifkovits Horner. The sides discussed issues of cooperation between Georgia and the Swiss Confederation in bilateral and multilateral formats. Discussions also focused on the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and the ongoing Geneva International Discussions. The Georgian side thanked the Swiss representatives for their support of Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity and for their contribution to the peaceful settlement of the conflict. Lasha Darsalia noted Switzerland's significant contribution to supporting the reconciliation and confidence-building process between the communities divided by the occupation lines. Significant attention was paid to Switzerland’s efforts of mediation between Russia and Georgia amid the severed diplomatic relations between Georgia and Russia. Talking points also included the security environment and current challenges facing the South Caucasus region. Lasha Darsalia stressed the need to ensure peace and stability in the region and the importance of the international community’s effective and active involvement in this process.
