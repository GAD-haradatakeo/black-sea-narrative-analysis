27 September 2022 On 27 September 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia received the Executive Director of the International Visegrad Fund (IVF), Petr Mareš, who is paying a visit to Georgia. The Executive Director reviewed the main areas of IVF activities, projects implemented or ongoing, as well as opportunities that the Visegrad Fund offers to Georgia. The Georgian side expressed its close interest in further deepening the interaction. In this context, Teimuraz Janjalia introduced to Petr Mareš the priorities of Georgia, the implementation of which calls for donors’ support including in terms of raising public awareness about the European and Euro-Atlantic integration of Georgia, promoting Georgian exports to the EU market, and supporting IDPs from the occupied regions. Petr Mareš confirmed that the International Visegrad Fund will continue regular communication with Georgia and support projects tailored to the priorities and needs of our country.
