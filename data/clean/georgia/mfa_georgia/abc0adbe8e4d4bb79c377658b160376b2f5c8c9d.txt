16 November 2023 On 16 November 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the head of the Secretariat of the Venice Commission of the Council of Europe, Simona Granata-Menghini. Discussions focused on the long-term and fruitful cooperation with the Venice Commission, which serves to promote the implementation of democratic reforms in Georgia and bring the legislative framework closer to European standards. The Georgian side thanked the delegation of the Venice Commission for conducting the visit and sharing expertise. Talking points included the preparation of the Venice Commission’s opinions on certain legislative changes.
