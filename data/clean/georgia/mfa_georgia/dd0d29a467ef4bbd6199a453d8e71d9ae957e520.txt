3 October 2023 Within the framework of his visit to Strasbourg, Ilia Darchiashvili met the Vice President of the European Commission, the European Commissioner for Demography and Democracy, Dubravka Šuica. At the meeting, discussions focused on Georgia&#39;s implementation of the 12 recommendations set by the European Commission. The Minister informed the Vice President of the European Commission about the steps taken by Georgia in this regard. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed the hope that another historic decision will be made by European partners and Georgia will be granted a candidate status. The sides focused on the need to carry out reforms and to strengthen the democratic institutions. The minister once again confirmed Georgia’s readiness to strengthen future cooperation.
