26 March 2018 26.03.2018 Tbilisi -​ Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the Head of the Georgian Intelligence Service Davit Sujashvili. The sides spoke about the security situation in the region highlighting the importance of coordination between the foreign ministry and the intelligence service in addressing the challenges facing the region.
