16 March 2025 On 17 March 2025, the 65th meeting of the Council of National Coordinators of the Organization for Democracy and Economic Development – GUAM was held via video conference. The meeting was attended by Georgia’s National Coordinator, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Giorgi Zurabashvili, as well as State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of the Republic of Moldova, Sergiu Mihov, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, Oleksandr Mishchenko, and representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan. During the meeting, participants discussed key issues on the organization’s agenda, with a particular focus on strengthening cooperation among GUAM member states and the implementation of joint regional projects. Giorgi Zurabashvili presented Georgia’s foreign policy priorities regarding the European Union. Emphasis was placed on the significance of connectivity and trade-economic cooperation both within the GUAM framework and in the broader regional context. He also addressed the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the security challenges facing the region.
