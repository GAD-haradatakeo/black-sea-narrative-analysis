27 October 2018 26.10.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani took part in the reception dedicated to the National Day of Austria. Speaking before the attending audience, David Zalkaliani highly appraised the close co-operation between Georgia and Austria. He highlighted Austria’s strong support for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. The Georgian Foreign Minister thanked Austria for strong support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and for its important contribution to the EUMM. “It is the only international monitoring organization capable to provide objective information to the international community on the situation on the ground.” – Zalkaliani said.
