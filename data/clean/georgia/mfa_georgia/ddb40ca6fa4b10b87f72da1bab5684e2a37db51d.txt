26 May 2018 26.05.2018, Tbilisi - European cultural sites and governmental buildings including the central bridge in Vilnius, Lithuania, Sheraton Ankara Hotel in Turkey, the Municipality of Soeul, Korea and the City Hall of Lisbon, Portugal , and the Embassy of Georgia to Estonia will also be illuminated in the Georgian flag colours. The flag itself will be raised at public offices in Estonia, Republic of Ireland and Canada, among others. In Brussels, the trademark monument of Manneken-Pis will be dressed in Georgian national costume chokha.
