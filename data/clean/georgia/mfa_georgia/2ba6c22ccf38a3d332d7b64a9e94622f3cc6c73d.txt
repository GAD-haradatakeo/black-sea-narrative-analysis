22 November 2018 22.11.2018 - Georgia-EU relations took another step forward in Brussels yesterday – Sajjad Karim, Chair of the EU-Georgia Parliamentary Association Committee twitted commenting on the first high-level meeting between the Georgian Government and the European Commission held in Brussels, on 21 November. The meeting was chaired by the Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani from the Georgian side and Commissioner for European Neighbourhood Policy and Enlargement Negotiations, Johannes Hahn – from the EU side, and was attended by 6 Ministers of the Georgian Government and 13 European Commissioners.
