19 January 2022 The Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani will pay an official visit to the Republic of Lithuania from 19 to 21 January 2022. During the visit, the Georgian Foreign Minister will hold meetings with the President of the Republic of Lithuania, Gitanas Nausėda, the Speaker of the Seimas, Viktorija Čmilytė-Nielsen and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Lithuania, Gabrielius Landsbergis. During his visit to Lithuania, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, together with members of the delegation, will pay tribute to and lay a wreath at the memorial to those killed in the fight for independence and at the the grave of Datiko Machavariani, a Georgian officer who emigrated to Lithuania.
