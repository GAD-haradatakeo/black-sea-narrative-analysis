9 July 2021 On 9 July 2021, the55th Meetingof theGUAMCouncil ofNational Coordinatorswas held under the Chairmanship of the Georgian Side in the Secretariat of the Organization for Democracy and Economic Development –GUAM, Kyiv. The meeting was attended by the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia, the Azerbaijani Deputy Foreign Minister, Mahmud Mamad-Guliev, the Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister, Vasyl Bodnar, the Secretary ofStateof the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of the Republic of Moldova, Daniela Cujba and the GUAM Secretary General, Altai Efendiev. The GUAM National Coordinator, Teimuraz Janjalia presented the programme setting forth the priorities of Georgian Chairmanship. According to the Deputy Foreign Minister, the Georgian Chairmanship will be focused on political interaction, inter-parliamentary and sectoral cooperation within the GUAM, as well as on possibilities of developing cooperation with partner states. The GUAM national coordinators also discussed various topical issues on the Organization’s agenda. Special attention was paid to the importance of strengthening cooperation in the fights against the pandemic. The need to get the tourism sector fully functioning was also highlighted.
