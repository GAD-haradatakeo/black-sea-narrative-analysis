11 March 2019 11.03.2019 – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the crash of the Ethiopian Airlines passenger jetBoeing-737 killing 157 passengers on board, on 10 March 2019. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the Government and people of the Federal Democratic Republic ofEthiopia, as well as to the families of disaster victims coming from various countries.
