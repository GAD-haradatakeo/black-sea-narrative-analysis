11 December 2024 On 10 December 2024, during her visit to Hungary, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with Hungary’s Minister for European Union Affairs, János Bóka. During the meeting, the parties welcomed the friendly relations and close cooperation between the two countries, both bilaterally and multilaterally. The discussion focused on the ongoing processes in Georgia and its relationship with the European Union. Maka Botchorishvili once again emphasized Georgia&#39;s readiness to fulfill its commitments under the Association Agreement and DCFTA in preparation for EU membership. She also spoke about the progress made in this regard, highlighting the importance of the country&#39;s economic development. The conversation also touched on the prospects for deepening cooperation between Georgia and Hungary.
