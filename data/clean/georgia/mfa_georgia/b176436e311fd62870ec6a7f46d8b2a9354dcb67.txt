3 October 2018 3.10.2018, Tbilisi - According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia keeps active communication with all international partners, including the co-chairs of the Geneva International Discussions in connection with the kidnapping of Maia Otinashvili, a citizen of Georgia from the territory adjacent to the village of Khurvaleti. The Minister said that the office of the State Minister keeps seized of the matter around-the-clock. “Such facts are categorically unacceptable. We continue to engage actively with all international partners. The co-chairs of the Geneva International Discussions, as well as the EU Monitoring Mission have already been informed about this incident, which will be discussed during the next round of the Geneva Discussions. We call on the international community and the force exercising effective control in the occupied territory to ensure that the abducted citizen of Georgia is released immediately” – the Minister said.
