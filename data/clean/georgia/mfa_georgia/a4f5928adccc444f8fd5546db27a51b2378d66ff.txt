6 September 2022 Within the framework of the Georgia-EU Association Council visit to Brussels, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Ilia Darchiashvili met with the European Parliamentarian Witold Jan Waszczykowski. The sides noted the close cooperation between Georgia and the European Parliament and welcomed the European Parliament's support for Georgia's European integration. Discussions also focused on the prospect of deepening Georgia's cooperation with the European Union. Special attention was paid to the European Council's decision to grant Georgia a European perspective, as recognition of Georgia’s progress on its path to joining the European Union. The sides expressed the hope that Georgia will receive the candidate status in the near future, and the European Parliament will also make its own positive contribution to achieving this goal. Speaking about the challenges facing the region, attention was paid to the humanitarian and security situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and to the European Union’s important role in the process of peaceful resolution of the conflict. The Georgian delegation, under the leadership of Prime Minister Irakli Gharibashvili, is in Brussels to participate in the 7th meeting of the Georgia-EU Association Council.
