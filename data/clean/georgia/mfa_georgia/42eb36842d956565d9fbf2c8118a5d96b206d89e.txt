12 November 2025 The Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, spoke by telephone with Hakan Fidan, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Türkiye. Minister Botchorishvili expressed her condolences to Minister Fidan, to the families of the victims, and to the people of Türkiye over the tragic loss of life resulting from the crash of a Turkish Air Force cargo plane in Georgia.
