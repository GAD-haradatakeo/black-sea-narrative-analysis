23 February 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the Italian Republic, Antonio Tajani, within the framework of his visit to the United Nations. The ministers discussed the opportunities for the development of bilateral relations in various fields. It was once again confirmed that the Italian Republic supports Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Ilia Darchiashvili provided his colleague with information on the European Commission’s recommendations and the progress achieved by Georgia on the road to European integration, in general. At the meeting, the ministers expressed their readiness to work actively to strengthen the partnership between Italy and Georgia and to activate both high-level visits and cooperation formats between the foreign ministries of the two countries. Special emphasis was placed on opportunities for cooperation in the economic and energy sectors. The important potential of Georgia in this direction was emphasized at the meeting.
