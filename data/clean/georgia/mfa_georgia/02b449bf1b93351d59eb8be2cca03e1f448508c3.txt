18 July 2017 18.07.2017, Tbilisi –Annual gathering of Heads of Georgian Diplomatic Missions abroad -"Result-Oriented Diplomacy in Turbulent Environment” was officially launched today. Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze welcomed the participants of the meeting and invited guests. President of Georgia Giorgi Margvelashvili, Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili, Speaker of the Parliament of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze also spoke before the attending audience. The opening ceremony was attended by members of the cabinet of ministers, chairs of the parliamentary committees, leaders of the majority and minority groups of the Parliament, Prime Minister’s Special Representative for Relations with Russia, Deputy Foreign Ministers, , Amassadors at large and Heads of Georgian Diplomatic Missions abroad and Foreign Diplomatic Missions in Georgia.
