8 October 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the State of Qatar to Georgia, Hassan Ahmed Ali Mohammed Al Mutawa. During the meeting, the parties discussed the positive dynamics of bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Qatar and underscored the importance of high-level visits in further strengthening bilateral relations. Particular attention was paid to cooperation in the fields of trade, economy, and connectivity. In this context, both sides emphasized the significance of concluding a Free Trade Agreement between the two countries. The parties also positively assessed the ongoing cooperation in the sphere of tourism. The discussion further touched upon the security environment in the region and beyond. The Minister expressed appreciation for Qatar’s continued support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
