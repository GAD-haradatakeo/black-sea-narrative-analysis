22 December 2019 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with the Council of Europe Commissioner for Human Rights, Dunja Mijatovic. The conversation focused on the illegal imprisonment of the doctor, Vazha Gaprindashvili by the occupation regime. Once again expressing his deep concern over the so-called court’s illegal decision, the Minister said that such actions further aggravate the already difficult security and human rights situation in the occupied regions. David Zalkaliani highlighted the importance of the international community’s, including the international organizations’ active involvement in addressing the humanitarian crisis. The Minister also informed Dunja Mijatovic on the steps taken by Georgia with regard to the issue of Vazha Gaprindashvili. The Parties agreed to exchange information on the ongoing situation.
