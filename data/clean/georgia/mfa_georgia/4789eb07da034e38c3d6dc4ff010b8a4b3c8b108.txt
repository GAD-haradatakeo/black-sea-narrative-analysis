25 March 2020 The Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani responded to the decision of the EU member states on opening ofaccession talkswithAlbaniaandNorth Macedonia. “My congratulations to Albania and North Macedonia on this awaited EU decision. This is a truly historic moment for a region & an outcome that fills with hope those of us, who aspire to join one day the largest Union of democracies” – he posted on his twitter.
