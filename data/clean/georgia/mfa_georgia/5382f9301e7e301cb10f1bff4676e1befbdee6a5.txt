9 May 2020 The Georgian government continues the orderly and gradual repatriation process. Today the charter flight will bring home 180 Georgians from Berlin, including students, elderly people, individuals suffering from oncological diseases, minors, and transit passengers. It is the fourth charter flight from Berlin since the imposition of COVID-19-related restrictions. Under the Coordination Council’s schedule, flights have been performed from various cities of the world, including from Berlin, Vienna, London, Athens, U.S., Larnaca, Amsterdam, Beijing, Rome, Paris, Tel-Aviv, Barcelona, Warsaw, Madrid, Prague, etc. Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 19,607 citizens of Georgia in the host countries. The Government has organized the repatriation 10,115 citizens to Georgia, as of 8 May. The Georgian MFA website is updated every day to provide the latest information regarding support measures undertaken by the Georgian government with respect to Georgian citizens abroad.
