17 November 2017 17.11.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a farewell meetng with the head of the OSCE/ODIHR observation mission for local elections in Georgia, Corien Jonker. Mikheil Janelidze thanked the OSCE/ODIHR for assigning observers to Georgia highlighting years of Georgia’s fruitful co-operation wth the organization. The sides spoke about the recent local elections in Georgia emphasizing that the election process was well-administered and the fundamental rights were largely protected during this process.
