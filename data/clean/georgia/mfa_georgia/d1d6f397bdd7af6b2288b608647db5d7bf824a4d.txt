18 October 2021 On 18 October 2021, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Secretary General of BSEC, Ambassador Lazăr Comănescu.The Minister congratulated Lazăr Comănescu on his appointment as Secretary General of tBSEC and wished him success in this important post. The sides discussed prospects for economic cooperation between BSEC Member States and the ways to enhance the effectiveness of the organization during the Georgian chairmanship of BSEC from 1 July to 31 December 2021. The Minister stressed that Georgia attaches special importance to the further development of the Black Sea region’s transit potential. Discussions also focused on the possibility of deepening the BSEC's cooperation with the European Union. The sides exchanged views over the security environment in the region. The Minister spoke about the situation in the occupied territories and stressed the importance of resolving regional conflicts peacefully, in accordance with the principles of sovereignty and territorial integrity. The Secretary General of the BSEC thanked the Georgian side for its warm hospitality and active cooperation within the Presidency. During his visit to Georgia, the BSEC Secretary General met with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili, and the Chairman of the Culture Committee of the Parliament of Georgia, Eliso Bolkvadze.
