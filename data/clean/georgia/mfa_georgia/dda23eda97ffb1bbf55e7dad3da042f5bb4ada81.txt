13 September 2023 On 13 September 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to Georgia, Anne Toft Sørensen. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides welcomed the active dynamics of bilateral relations and emphasized the importance of mutual exchange of high-level visits and regular political dialogue between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs. The sides highlighted Georgia's implementation of the 12 priorities defined by the European Commission and the progress in this regard. In this context, they emphasized the strategic importance of the decision to grant a candidate status to Georgia at the end of the year. At the meeting, special attention was paid to the long-term development cooperation between Denmark and Georgia. Teimuraz Janjalia thanked the ambassador for the assistance provided by Denmark to Georgia and welcomed the fact that Georgia is a priority country in the programs developed by the Danish side. Anne Toft Sørensen confirmed the strong position of the Danish government to promote the democratic transformation and sustainable development of the Eastern neighborhood, which is reflected in the country's strategic documents.
