2 July 2024 On 3 July 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan, will pay an official visit to Georgia. During this visit, Minister Mirzoyan will meet with Georgia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili. Their discussions will focus on the current and future areas of cooperation as outlined in the joint declaration on strategic partnership between Georgia and the Republic of Armenia. The Ministers will also address the ongoing regional and global developments. Following their meeting, press announcements will be made. Additionally, Ararat Mirzoyan will hold discussions with the Prime Minister of Georgia and the Chairman of the Georgian Parliament.
