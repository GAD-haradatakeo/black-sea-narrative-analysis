14 November 2019 14.11.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Minister for Foreign Affairs, Cooperationand Communities of the Democratic RepublicofSão ToméandPríncipe, Elsa MariaNeto d'Alva Teixeira de Barros Pinto, who arrived for an visit in Georgia. The sides discussed issues relating to bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and the Democratic RepublicofSão ToméandPríncipe. They welcomed the growing dynamics of bilateral relations between the two countries highlighting the importance of deepening cooperation in various fields. Zalkaliani paid special attention to the main challenges facing Georgia – the human rights and humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and provocative activities across the occupation line. The Minister for Foreign Affairs, Cooperationand Communities reaffirmed São ToméandPríncipe’s firm support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia based on the principles of international law.
