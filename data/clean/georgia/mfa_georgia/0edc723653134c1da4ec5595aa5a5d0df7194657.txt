27 October 2025 On 27 October 2025, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia, Nikoloz Abkhazava, presented his Letters of Credence to the President of the Arab Republic of Egypt, Abdel Fattah El-Sisi. Ambassador Abkhazava conveyed the warm greetings and best wishes of the President of Georgia, Mikheil Kavelashvili, to the President of Egypt. The parties expressed their readiness to further deepen the bilateral relations between Georgia and the Arab Republic of Egypt.
