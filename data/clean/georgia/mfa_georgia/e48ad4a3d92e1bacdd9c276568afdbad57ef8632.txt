24 September 2019 Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Austrian colleague AlexanderSchallenberg. Highlighting the close and successful relations between Georgia and Austria, Zalkaliani paid special attention to the role of the Austrian Development Agency that has been making a trenedous contribution towards the development of many sectors in Georgia since the restoration of the country’s independence. Discussions focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, including the ongoing illegal borderization, closure of checkpoints, and increasing number of detentions of civilians. According to the Austrian Minister, the international community should employ all available instruments to achieve de-escalation of the situation and peaceful resolution of the conflict. The Ministers highlighted that it is necessary to ensure that all existing formats are functioning effectively. The sides noted with satisfaction the positive dynamics of trade-economic relations between Austria and Georgia. Special note was taken of the Autrian companies’ increasing interest in Georgia.
