21 March 2019 20.03.2019, Buenos Aires – “We have to make bonds between Georgia and Argentina stronger and more substantive especially in economic and commercial terms. We are countries that have common traces. We are producers of wine and you have the 8000-year history of wine making so we should join the effort to at least drink together but we can also do something more creative to use this link and capacity in the area of world wine producing. We have also initiatives on joint agricultural projects“ - Argentine Foreign minister JorgeFauriesaid following his meeting with the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani. While on the theme of bilateral relations, JorgeFauriesaid that Argentina was one of the first states to recognize Georgia’s independence in 1019. According to him, Argentina highly appreciates the activities of the Georgian Embassy in Buenos Aires. The Argentine Foreign Minister paid special attention to the importance of strengthening people-to-people contacts. According to him, Georgia is famous for its rugby and football teams and therefore, it is advisable to develop relations in the area of sport. “The objective is to do more together. This is our message from Argentina” - JorgeFauriesaid. Documents Similar News
