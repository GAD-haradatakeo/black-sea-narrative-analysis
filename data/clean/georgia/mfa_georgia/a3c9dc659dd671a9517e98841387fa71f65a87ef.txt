26 February 2020 26.02.2020, Tbilisi - Strasbourg will host 130th meeting of the Council of Europe Committee of Ministers in May this year instead of Tbilisi, The decision was made by the Committee of Ministers’ Deputies, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani told journalists during the briefing. According to Zalkaliani, intensive consultations have been underway with the CoE General Secretariart and all partner countries to ensure that the ministerial under the Georgian presidency is held in line with high standards, with full respect for the CoE statutes and in full compliance with the Georgian laws, and that Georgia as the presidency ensures unhindered participation of all member states, including Russia.
