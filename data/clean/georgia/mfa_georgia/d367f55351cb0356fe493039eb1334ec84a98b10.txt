25 June 2021 The foreign ministers of Austria, Romania and Lithuania, Alexander Schallenberg, Bogdan Aurescu and Gabrielius Landsbergis, will pay a visit to Georgia. The three foreign ministers will arrive in Georgia as part of their regional tour to the South Caucasus, which includes visits to Azerbaijan, Armenia and Georgia, in coordination with High Representative Josep Borrell and the European External Action Service. Alexander Schallenberg, Bogdan Aurescu and Gabrielius Landsbergis will hold meetings with Prime Minister Irakli Garibashvili and Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani.
