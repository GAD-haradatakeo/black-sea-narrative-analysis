10 July 2020 On 10 July 2020, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a farewell meeting with the Head of the Council of Europe Office in Georgia,Christian Urse, who completed his 5-year term of mission in Georgia. The sides highlighted the importance of the policy instrument of strategic cooperation – Council of Europe Action Plan. The Minister thanked Christian Urse for his contribution to fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the Council of Europe. Zalkaliani singled out for special mention Urse’s involvement in conducting a political dialogue on constitutional amendments and achieving a broad political consensus. For his part, the Head of the Council of Europe Office in Georgia thanked the Minister for fruitful cooperation.
