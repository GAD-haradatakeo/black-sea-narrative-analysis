12 April 2025 Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of International Affairs of Botswana, Phenyo Butale. The discussion focused on cooperation between the two countries within the framework of international organizations and emphasized the importance of respecting international norms and principles.
