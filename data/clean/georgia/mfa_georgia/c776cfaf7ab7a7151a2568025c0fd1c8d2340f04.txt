Maka Botchorishvili: Both Georgia and Hungary clearly understand the risks associated with attacks on sovereignty and the need to firmly protect it 9 February 2026 “Both Georgia and Hungary clearly understand the risks associated with attacks on sovereignty and the need to firmly protect it” - This was stated by the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, at a joint press conference with her Hungarian counterpart, Péter Szijjártó, held in Budapest.
