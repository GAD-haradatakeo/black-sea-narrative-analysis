7 September 2022 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with the U.S. Deputy Assistant Secretary of State for Democracy, Human Rights and Labor, Kara McDonald. The sides discussed issues relating to the US-Georgia strategic partnership. The sides highlighted the importance of strong U.S.-Georgia strategic partnership and U.S. support for Georgia against the background of the challenges facing the region. The sides focused on Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration, as well as on the process of democratic reforms in the country. They noted the importance of further expanding the political dialogue and the dynamic U.S.-Georgia cooperation in all priority areas of strategic partnership.
