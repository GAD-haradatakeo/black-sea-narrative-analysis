Ilia Darchiashvili has met with the delegation from the Political and Security Committee (PSC) of the Council of the European Union 10 April 2024 On 10 April 2024, Georgia's Foreign Affairs Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with the delegation from the Political and Security Committee (PSC) of the Council of the European Union, visiting Georgia for two days on a work trip. The delegation includes representatives of all member states of the European Union and representatives of the European Commission, the European External Action Service (EEAS), and the Delegation of the European Union to Georgia.
