9 November 2017 9.11.2017, Tbilisi - A piece from the Berlin Wall was installed in Europe Square as a symbol of Georgian-German friendship. Georgian Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili, Georgian Ambassador to the Federal Republic of Germany Lado Chanturia and German Ambassador to Georgia HeikePeitsche spoke before the guests attending the unveiling ceremony. Lado Chanturia spoke about the close Georgian-German relations, and the symbolic importance of installing the Berlin Wall monument in Tbilisi. According to him, Prime MinisterKvirikashvili received a piece of the wall during his visit to Germany as a symbol of peace and freedom and as a proof that all walls of evil will break one day. Thanking the German people and the government for "this important gift” Kvirikashvili said that this occasion reminds everyone of breaking the wall that separated German people. “The wall erected by evil will also break down in Georgia and this will be done peacefully because any time when the civilized world stands together, such a result is feasible through straightforward, right steps and brave policy”, Kvirikashvili stated. German Ambassador to GeorgiaHeike Peitsch said that this piece of the wall proves that nothing is impossible in life and that one day Georgian territorial integrity will also be restored. The official part of the ceremony was followed by a concert. The evening was attended by the newly elected Mayor of Tbilisi Kakha Kaladze, as well as members of the Sakrebulo and other officials.
