16 October 2017 16.10.2017, Tbilisi – The Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Head of the European Council Office in Georgia, Christian Urse. The sides spoke about issues relating to co-operation between Georgia and the Council of Europe. Special attention was paid to the Georgia-CoE Action Plan for 2016-2019 and the importance of finalizing the ongoing projects within this Plan. The sides agreed to further continue co-operation in the future. Documents Similar News
