28 July 2025 Konstantine Kvachakidze, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia, presented his Letters of Credence to the President of the Slovak Republic, Peter Pellegrini. Following the official credential presentation ceremony, a bilateral meeting was held between the Georgian Ambassador and the Slovak President. During the meeting, the parties discussed key issues on the agenda of bilateral relations.
