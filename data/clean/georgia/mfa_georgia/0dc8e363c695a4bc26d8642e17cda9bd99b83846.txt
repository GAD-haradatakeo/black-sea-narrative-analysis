20 June 2023 On 20 June 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, addressed the representatives of international and donor organizations at the event organized by the National Competition Agency of Georgia. He noted that ensuring consumer protection is one of the important parts of the Association Agreement with the European Union, and Georgia has made significant progress on key reforms, both at the legislative and institutional levels. The Law of Georgia on Protection of Consumer Rights, which entered into force last year, was prepared in accordance with the requirements of the Association Agreement. Within the framework of the event, the participants discussed the possibilities of strengthening mutual cooperation and the initiatives prepared by the agency to improve the competition policy.
