23 May 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the Manchester Arena blast that occurred in the United Kingdom. Georgia is dismayed at the tragedy resulting from this barbaric act and expresses its support and solidarity with the British people and the government. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs strongly condemns this act of cruelty, which amounts to a crime against humanity and is directed against international peace. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs conveys its condolences to the families of the terror victims wishing speedy recovery to those who have been injured as a result of the incident. Documents Similar News
