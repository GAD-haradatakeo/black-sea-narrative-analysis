30 January 2026 On 30 January 2026, the fourth round of bilateral political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the State of Qatar was held in Tbilisi. The consultations were led on the Georgian side by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Alexander Khvtisiashvili, while the Qatari delegation was headed by the Secretary General of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the State of Qatar, Ahmad Hassen Al-Hammadi. During the meeting, the parties reviewed current issues on the agenda of Georgian-Qatari relations, including prospects for the further development of bilateral dialogue and the deepening of trade and economic cooperation. Discussions also addressed opportunities to intensify high-level visits. Within the framework of the consultations, the importance of continuing cooperation aimed at further strengthening the legal framework between the two countries was emphasized. The parties exchanged views on the security environment and challenges at both the regional and global levels.
