26 February 2018 26.02.2018, Tbilisi - Third wave of the visa free travel awareness campaign was launched at the Information Center on NATO and EU. Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Vakhtang Makharoblishvili, Deputy Minister of Justice Aleksandre Baramidze, Head of the International Relations Department of the Ministry of Internal Affairs Eka Machavariani and Director of the Information Center on NATO and EU Nino Bolkvadze provided the guests with detailed information on the main aspects of information campaign, ongoing process of visa-free movement, planned legislative amendments to prevent the growth of illegal asylum seekers in the EU/Shengen area and the practice of cooperation with EU / Shengen member states in the field of law enforcement.
