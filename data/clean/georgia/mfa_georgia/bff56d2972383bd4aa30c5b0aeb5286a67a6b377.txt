8 January 2026 Head of the Mission of Georgia to NATO, Irakli Beraia met with NATO Secretary General, Mark Rutte. The Georgian Representative conveyed his credentials to the Secretary General of the Alliance. During the meeting, the sides discussed NATO-Georgia cooperation. The Georgian Representative emphasized Georgia's important role in the regional security and thanked the Secretary General for the Alliance's firm support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
