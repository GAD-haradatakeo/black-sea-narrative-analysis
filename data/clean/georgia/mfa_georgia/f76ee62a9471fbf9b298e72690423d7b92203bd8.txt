7 April 2023 On 7 April 2023, the 61st Meeting of the GUAM Council of National Coordinators (CNC) was held via videoconference under the Chairmanship of Ukraine. Teimuraz Janjalia congratulated Ukraine on the chairmanship and expressed Georgia’s readiness to take an active part in the implementation of the priorities determined by the Ukrainian chairmanship. The deputy minister emphasized Georgia’s full solidarity and support towards Ukraine since the beginning of the war. The Deputy Minister spoke about the current situation in the occupied regions of Georgia, including the grave humanitarian situation and human rights violations. He noted that the ongoing illegal "borderization" and other provocative actions of Russia further aggravate the existing security environment. At the meeting, the parties discussed current issues on the GUAM agenda: cooperation with partners in political, inter-parliamentary, sectoral, GUAM+ formats and high-level events planned within the framework of the Ukrainian Chairmanship in 2023. The parties particularly noted the importance of strengthening regional cooperation in the light of the current war and threats in Ukraine, and also emphasized the need to strengthen regional cooperation in the areas of transport and connectivity and, for this purpose, steps planned for the development of the GUAM transport corridor.
