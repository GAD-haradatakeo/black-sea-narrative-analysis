27 November 2023 On 27 November 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Czech Republic to Georgia, Petr Kubernát. The sides highlighted the traditionally friendly and partner relations between Georgia and the Czech Republic. Special note was taken of high-level visits and the readiness to further deepen the bilateral dialogue between the two countries. The deputy minister and the ambassador focused on the ways of deepening the sectoral cooperation and noted the existing potential in the fields of trade, economy and tourism. The deputy minister paid special attention to Georgia’s European integration process and acquainted the ambassador with the steps taken in this direction. The sides stressed the importance of the European Commission’s positive recommendation to grant Georgia the EU candidate status, in recognition of the efforts and progress made by our country. The Georgian side expressed the hope that, based on the European Commission’s recommendation, the European Council will grant Georgia a candidate status in December. The ambassador expressed the Czech Republic's support for the foreign policy priorities of our country and noted that the Czech Government is committed to continue to support and assist Georgia in this process.
