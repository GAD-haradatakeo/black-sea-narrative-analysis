20 September 2022 Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Singapore, Vivian Balakrishnan. The Georgian Foreign Minister emphasized Georgia's interest in developing relations with Singapore. Special attention was paid to the common policy of the two countries in terms of economic development. The sides expressed their interest in sharing successful experience in this regard. The ministers reviewed the existing cooperation in international formats and noted the great potential of bilateral relations. Within the framework of the General Assembly, Ilia Darchiashvili also met with the Minister forForeign Affairsand ExternalTradeofSolomon Islands, JeremiahManele. Discussions focused on Georgia's successful cooperation with the countries of the Pacific Ocean Basin and Georgia's active involvement in the Pacific international organizations. Speaking about bilateral relations, the ministers emphasized the possibility of developing education cooperation and public services, and also noted the importance of intensifying relations between the two countries.
