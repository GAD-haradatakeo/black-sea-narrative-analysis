25 September 2021 Within the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Deputy Prime Minister of Georgia, Minister of Foreign Affairs Davit Zalkaliani continues to hold bilateral meetings with his foreign counterparts. On 24 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with his Singaporean counterpart, Foreign Minister Vivian Balakrishnan. The sides discussed issues on the agenda of cooperation between Singapore and Georgia. The Ministers noted the recent intensification of relations between the two countries, including in the fields of trade, economy and tourism. Special accent was put on the importance of strengthening inter-parliamentary cooperation and exchanging high-level visits. David Zalkaliani thanked the Singaporean Foreign Minister for his unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. According to that Singaporean Foreign Minister, Singapore is interested in deepening cooperation with Georgia and is ready to support Georgia's aspirations to develop relations with the South and East Asia countries.
