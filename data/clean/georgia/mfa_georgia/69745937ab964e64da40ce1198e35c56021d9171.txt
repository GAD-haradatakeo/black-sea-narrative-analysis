21 March 2018 21.03.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Hungary to Georgia Viktória Horváth. Mikheil Janelidze and Viktória Horváth noted with satisfaction the friendly relations and dynamically developing partnership between the two countries, both at the level of political co-operation and people-to-people contacts. The Georgian Foreign Minister thanked the Hungarian Ambassador for her government’s firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. Mikheil Janelidze paid special attention to the successful cooperation in the area of education and thanked the Hungarian side for the scholarships offered to up to 80 Georgian students. Viktória Horváth, for her part, highly appraised the Days of Hungarian Culture successfully conducted in Georgia in December 2017.
