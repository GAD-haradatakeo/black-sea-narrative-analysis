28 February 2018 27.02.2018, Geneva – Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Director-General of the United Nations Office at Geneva, Michael Møller. The sides spoke about the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions. The Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze paid particular attention to the recent death of Georgian citizen Archil Tatunashvili in the country’s occupied Tskhinvali region during his meeting with Michael Moller, Director-General of the United Nations Office at Geneva. The officials highlighted the importance of the international community’s adequate response to such facts. Janelidze and Moller also touched upon the enforcement process of the UN Sustainable Development Goals and Georgia’s achievements in this regard.
