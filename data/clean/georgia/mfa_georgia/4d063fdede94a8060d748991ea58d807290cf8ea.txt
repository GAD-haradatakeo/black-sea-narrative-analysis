27 September 2023 High-level delegations of the IAEA member states, international and non-governmental organizations take part in the general conference. The head of the Georgian delegation, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, addressed the participants of the general conference, and in his speech, he paid special attention to the importance of the Agency’s role in terms of preventing the further spread of nuclear weapons and maintaining international security. Lasha Darsalia reviewed recent steps taken by Georgia towards ensuring nuclear and radiation safety and emphasized the importance of active international cooperation in this regard. The Deputy Minister spoke about potential threats from the occupied territories of Georgia and put a special emphasis on the importance of international verification and monitoring on the ground; Also, he noted the dangers related to Ukraine&#39;s nuclear reactors against the backdrop of Russia&#39;s aggression against Ukraine. At the end of the address, the head of the Georgian delegation thanked the partner states and organizations for the implementation of the projects that contribute to the strengthening of the regulatory body of Georgia and the improvement of the national system for nuclear and radiation safety.
