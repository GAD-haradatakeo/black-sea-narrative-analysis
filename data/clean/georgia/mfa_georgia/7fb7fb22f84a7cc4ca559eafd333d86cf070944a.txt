5 September 2017 ​5.09.2017, Tbilisi - The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the DPRK’s large-scale nuclear test conducted on 3 September 2017, posing a serious threat to global security. Standing in solidarity with the international community, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns Pyongyang’s provocative actions and systematic violation of its international obligations and UN SC resolutions. As a member state of the Nonproliferation Treaty and the Comprehensive Nuclear-Test-Ban Treaty,Georgia opposes any action jeopardizing global security and posing a threat to peace and stability in the region.
