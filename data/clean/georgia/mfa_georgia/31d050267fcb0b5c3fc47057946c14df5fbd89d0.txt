28 October 2020 On 28 October, the ambassador of the Republic of Lithuania in Georgia awarded the Georgian minister of foreign affairs with the letters of credence. After awarding the letters, there was a meeting where both sides attempted to get to know each other. At the beginning of the meeting, the Georgian minister congratulated the Lithuanian ambassador on his new position and wished him all the luck in his future endeavours in Georgia. During the meeting, the conversation touched upon the topic of extremely amicable relations between the two countries. The minister thanked the Lithuanian side once again, for the active support of the Georgian sovereignty and territorial integrity, and the immense help that Lithuania provides Georgia with; in terms of European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The existing dynamics between the two countries have also been discussed. In this context, high standard political dialogue and active cooperation in various fields were emphasized.
