15 January 2022 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its concern over the hijacking by Hussites of the United Arab Emirates-flagged ship Rawabi off the coast of Yemen’s Hodeidah province, on 2 January. Georgia strongly condemns extremism inallits forms andmanifestations and believes that this fact can lead to further escalation of the conflict, which will negatively impact the already difficult situation in the region.
