10 November 2019 10.11.2019, Tbilisi - The Secretary General was met by the permanent representative of Georgia at the Council of Europe and the representatives of the Georgian Foreign Ministry at the Tbilisi International Airport. The visit of the Secretary General is linked to Georgia’s forthcoming chairmanship of the Committee of Ministers of the Council of Europe. Within the frames of the visit, she is to hold meetings with the President, Prime Minister and the Chairman of the Parliament of Georgia, as well as the head of the delegation of Georgia at the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe. “From 27 November 2019 to 15 May 2020, Georgia is set to become the chairman country of the Committee of Ministers of the Council of Europe. The visit of the Secretary General is aimed at her to familiarize herself with the priorities of Georgia with regards to chairmanship and set particular goals for the further cooperation. There are various high-level events planned to take place during the timeframe of chairmanship, which will extensively represent Georgia in Strasbourg” – stated Irakli Giviashvili, permanent representative of Georgia in the Council of Georgia.
