22 June 2018 22.06.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Acting U.S. Ambassador to Georgia Elizabeth Rood. The sides discussed issues relating to Georgia-U.S. co-operation both in bilateral and multilateral formats, as well as the preparations for the forthcoming NATO Summit in Brussels. David Zalkaliani thanked the U.S. side for expressing support for Georgia and condemning the occupation of the Georgian territories in the U.S. Fiscal Year 2018 Budget. Special note was taken of the irreversibility of Georgia-U.S. strategic partnership. The Acting Ambassador congratulated David Zalkaliani on his appointment as Minister of Foreign Affairs.
