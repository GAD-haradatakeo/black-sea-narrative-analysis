16 July 2022 The Foreign Ministers of Azerbaijan and Armenia paid a visit to Georgia. The delegation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan led by Minister Jeyhun Bayramov arrived in Georgia today. The delegation of Armenia, led by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan, also visited Georgia. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met the delegation of the two countries at the Shota Rustaveli Tbilisi International Airport. Within the framework of the visit, the Ministers of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan and Armenia met with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Garibashvili, as well as with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili.
