15 November 2021 The European Commission took a decision to recognize the validity of COVID-19 certificates issued by Georgia. The Commission has recently published the information on its website. Based on this decision, Georgian citizens holding full vaccination certificates will be accepted for travel to the EU. Georgia’s COVID-19 Certificate comprising proof of vaccination, negative PCR test result and recovery from COVID-19 is accepted by the EU as equivalent to the EU Digital COVID Certificate, which is intended to facilitate free movement within the Union.
