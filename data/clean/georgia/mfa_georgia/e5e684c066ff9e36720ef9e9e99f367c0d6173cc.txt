6 November 2018 6.11.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Aleksandre Gvtisiashvili hosted non-resident ambassadors of the six countries accredited to Georgia. Ambassador of the Republic of Ghana Salma Frances Mancell-Egala (residing in Ankara), Ambassador of the Republic of Zimbabwe Joseph Chilengi (residing in Ankara), Ambassador of the Lebanese Republic Maya Dagher (residing in Yerevan), Ambassador of the Kingdom of Morocco Faouz El-Achchabi (residing in Kiev), Ambassador of Tunisia Mohamed Fayçal ben Mustapha (residing in Ankara) and Ambassador of the Republic of Uzbekistan Sherzod Faiziev (residing in Baku) presented copies of their credentials to Aleksandre Gvtisiashvili. Discussions during the meetings held with each ambassador focused on issues on the agenda of bilateral, as well as multilateral co-operation.
