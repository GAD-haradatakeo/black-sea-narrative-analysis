12 May 2021 The Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held an official meeting with his Armenian counterpart, Ara Ayvazyan, in Yerevan, on 12 May 2021. The sides discussed a wide spectrum of the agenda of bilateral relations, including the prospects for deepening political, economic and humanitarian cooperation.
