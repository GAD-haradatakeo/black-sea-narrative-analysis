13 September 2023 From 13 to 15 September 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Ilia Darchiashvili will pay an official visit to Ireland. Within the framework of the visit, Ilia Darchiashvili will hold meetings with the chairman of the Irish Senate, Jerry Buttimer, the speaker of the Parliament, Sean O'Farrell, with Minister for Foreign Affairs and Minister for Defence, Deputy Prime Minister, Micheál Martin, Minister of State for European Affairs and Defense, Peter Burke, the Minister of State for International Development and Diaspora, Sean Fleming and the Lord Mayor of Dublin, Daithí de Róiste.
