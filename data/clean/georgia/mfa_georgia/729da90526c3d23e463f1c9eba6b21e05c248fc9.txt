21 May 2021 On 21 May 2021, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with the AmbassadorExtraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Kazakhstan to Georgia, Baurzhan Mukhamejanov. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of bilateral and multilateral cooperation, including within the international organizations, in the political, economic, humanitarian, and public health areas. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister briefed the Kazakh Ambassador on the grave humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and on Russia’s destructive activities and human rights violations. For his part, Baurzhan Mukhamejanov reaffirmed Kazakhstan’s unequivocal support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. The sides exchanged information on measures taken in dealing with the coronavirus pandemic.
