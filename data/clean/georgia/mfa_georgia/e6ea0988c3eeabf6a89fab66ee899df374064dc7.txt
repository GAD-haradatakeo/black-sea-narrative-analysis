1 August 2018 1.08.2018 –Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani participated in Noble Partner 2018, a Georgian Armed Forces and the US Army Europe led exercise. According to David Zalkaliani, Georgia’s participation in this project, as well as an increasing interest of the U.S. and other partner countries is a clear recognition of Georgia as a dignified partner of our key strategic partner – the U.S. and other members of NATO. Georgia makes a significant contribution towards strengthening international security and our participation in the exercise today aims to enhance participating nations’ readiness and interoperability, as well as our compliance with NATO standards” – Zalkaliani said. Noble Partner 2018, a Georgian Armed Forces and the US Army Europe led exercise officially started in Georgia today. Under the slogan Strength Through Partnership, the exercise is being hosted by the Vaziani Military Base in the outskirts of Tbilisi for the fourth time.
