31 January 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Italy to Georgia, Massimiliano D’Antuono. The discussion focused on the agenda for bilateral cooperation and future plans between the two countries. The parties also addressed ongoing regional and global developments, highlighting the challenges faced and underscoring the importance of collaboration within international frameworks. Additionally, the meeting touched upon current domestic developments in Georgia.
