4 December 2024 From 4 to 6 December 2024, Georgia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, will attend the 31st session of the OSCE Ministerial Council in Malta. The Foreign Minister will deliver a speech during the Ministerial Council session. Additionally, she will participate in and address a Ukraine support event organized by Malta's Deputy Prime Minister and Minister for Foreign and European Affairs, Ian Borg.
