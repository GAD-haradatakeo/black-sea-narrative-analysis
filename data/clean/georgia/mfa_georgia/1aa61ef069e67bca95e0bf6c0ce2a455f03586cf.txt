28 June 2022 On 28 June 2022, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia met with a delegation of the NATO Defence and Security Committee (DSC), the Sub-Committee on Future Security and Defence Capabilities (DSCFC). Discussions focused on priority issues of Georgia's cooperation with the European Union, the country's NATO integration process, the security environment in the region and the situation in the occupied territories.
