13 December 2021 On 13 December, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United Kingdom to Georgia, Mark Clayton. During the meeting, the sides discussed issues of bilateral strategic cooperation, as well as NATO integration and regional security challenges. Special attention was paid to the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and the ongoing international negotiations in Geneva. Given the changing architecture of regional security, Lasha Darsalia stressed the importance of the Western partners’ consolidated position and their involvement in regional processes.
