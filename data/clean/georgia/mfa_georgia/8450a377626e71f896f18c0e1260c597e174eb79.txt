As part of his first official visit, the Indian Minister of External Affairs will bring to Georgia a holy relic of St Queen Ketevan 9 July 2021 Indian Minister of External Affairs Subrahmanyam Jaishankar will pay his first official visit to Georgia, on 9 and 10 July, to donate to the Georgian people a holy relic of St Queen Ketevan, at the decision of Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi.
