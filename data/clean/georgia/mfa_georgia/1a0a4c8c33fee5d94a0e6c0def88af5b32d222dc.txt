16 June 2022 On 16 June 2022, a farewell meeting was held between the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the State of Israel, Ran Gidor, who is completing his mission in Georgia. During the meeting, the sides discussed current issues of the existing partnership between the two countries and priorities for future cooperation. Talks also focused on cooperation within international organizations. The sides noted with satisfaction that the relations between Georgia and Israel are developing dynamically in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The official visit of the Georgian Prime Minister to Israel and the importance of this visit was also underlined during the meeting. The Israeli Ambassador thanked the Minister for the fruitful cooperation and reaffirmed Israel's strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
