24 September 2019 23.09.2019, New York – On the sidelines of the 74th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Cuban counterpart, Ministerof Foreign Affairs EduardoRodríguez Parrilla. Discussing the agenda of bilateral and multilateral cooperation, the sides expressed their interest to further develop relations between the two countries, particularly in the areas of culture and education. The sides underlined the need to develop the bilateral legislative basis and the regular format of cooperation between Georgia and Cuba. Highlighting Georgia’s successful cooperation with the Latin Amrican and Caribbean countries, Zalkaliani thanked his Cuban colleague for his government’s firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and briefed him on the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions.
