4 September 2023 On 4 September 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with the Speaker of the Knesset, Amir Ohana, who arrived for an official visit in Georgia. The sides discussed current issues of partnership between the two countries and the priorities of future cooperation. The Minister of Foreign Affairs emphasized the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the countries and the measures already implemented in this direction. Discussions focused on cooperation within the international organizations.
