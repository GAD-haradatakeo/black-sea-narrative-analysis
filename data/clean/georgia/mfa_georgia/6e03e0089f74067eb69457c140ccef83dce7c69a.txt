23 September 2021 On 22 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of European and Foreign Affairs of Malta, Evarist Bartolo. During the meeting, the sides discussed current issues of relations between the two countries and opportunities for deepening cooperation in various fields. The importance of strengthening cooperation in multilateral formats was also stressed.
