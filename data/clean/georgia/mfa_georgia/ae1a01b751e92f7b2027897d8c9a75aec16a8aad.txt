24 November 2019 24-26.11.2019, Berlin - Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia David Zalkaliani is to pay a visit to the Federal Republic of Germany, where he will hold meetings with Heiko Mass, German Foreign Minister, Jan Hecker, the Foreign Policy and Security Advisor to German Chancellor, as well as Wolfgang Schäuble, President of the Bundestag. Within the scope of the visit, Minsiter Zalkaliani will participate in the 9thBerlin Foreign Policy Forum and hold bilateral meetings with the representatives of executive and legislative government. The high-rank politicians, representatives of the government, experts and journalists from Germany as well as other European countries, Middle East, Asia and the USA are to take part in the forum.
