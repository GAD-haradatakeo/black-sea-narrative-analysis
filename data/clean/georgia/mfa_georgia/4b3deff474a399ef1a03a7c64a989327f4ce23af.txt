16 April 2021 Within the framework of his visit to Vienna, on 15 and 16 April, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia held bilateral meetings with the OSCE Secretary General, Helga Schmid, the Director of the OSCE Office for Democratic Institutions and Human Rights, Matteo Mecacci and the OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media, Teresa Ribeiro. The sides discussed various issues on the agenda of Georgia-OSCE bilateral cooperation and the ways to deepen relations with OSCE Institutions.
