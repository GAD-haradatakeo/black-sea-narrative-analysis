16 September 2024 During his visit to the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, Minister of Foreign Affairs Ilia Darchiashvili met with His Royal Highness, Prince Ghazi bin Muhammad. The discussions focused on the strong cooperation between Georgia and Jordan across various sectors, highlighting the pivotal role of Prince Ghazi in fostering these relations. Special emphasis was placed on cooperation in economic, cultural, and people-to-people exchanges. Darchiashvili expressed gratitude to Prince Ghazi for the land grant near the site of the Savior's baptism at the Jordan River, where the Georgian Cultural Center is being constructed. He noted that this project will further strengthen the ties between the two nations and provided an update on the progress of the center, thanking Prince Ghazi for his support and involvement. The conversation also addressed regional challenges, with both sides reaffirming their commitment to enhancing relations between Georgia and Jordan.
