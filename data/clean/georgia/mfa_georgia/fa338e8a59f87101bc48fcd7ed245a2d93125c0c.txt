2 February 2024 A coordination meeting of consular officials of Georgia is being organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Within the framework of the coordination meeting, the participants will discuss all important issues related to consular activity, including: improving the quality and availability of consular services, the legal framework, modern technologies, effective assistance to citizens in crisis situations. Attention is also paid to the recent developments on the European integration path and legislative and systemic changes in the context of granting Georgia a candidate status. Prospects for increasing visa-free travel opportunities for Georgian citizens, personal data protection, circular labour migration, steps taken by the Ministry in the consular area and future events are being discussed.
