17 November 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a telephone conversation with the Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of the Republic of Slovenia. Ilia Darchiashvili and Tanja Fajon discussed Georgia's European integration, bilateral relations and current events in the region. The sides highlighted the important progress achieved by Georgia in the process of European integration, in particular, the importance of the European Commission’s recommendation to grant Georgia a candidate status. According to Ilia Darchiashvili, this recommendation will give an additional impetus to the successful continuation of reforms in Georgia and the transition to a new stage of relations with the European Union. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia noted that the position of the allies acquires special importance in this process and expressed the hope that Slovenia will be a strong supporter of Georgia on the way to join the European Union. For her part, Tanja Fajon emphasized the need to speed up the EU enlargement process. She confirmed her unwavering support for the priority areas of Georgia's foreign policy and noted that Slovenia, as before, will continue to stand by Georgia. The Ministers also talked about the possibilities of strengthening bilateral relations between Georgia and Slovenia. The willingness for further deepening the political dialogue was also highlighted.
