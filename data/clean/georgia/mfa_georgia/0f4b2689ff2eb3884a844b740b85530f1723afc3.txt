24 February 2022 The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani participated in the OSCE Permanent Council’s Reinforced Meeting on the situation in and around Ukraine. In his speech, the Foreign Minister expressed his concern over Russia's aggression against Ukraine and stressed the need for the international community’s unified and firm position in response to Russia's violation of the fundamental principles of international law. David Zalkaliani highlighted that Russia's actions undermine Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as security in Europe and across the world, contrary to the fundamental principles of the UN Charter and international law. He spoke about the Russian military intervention into Georgia in 2008 and the harsh effects of occupation of the Georgian territories and the severe humanitarian and security situation on the ground.
