27 November 2021 On 27 November, during their working visit to Italy, members of the Georgian delegation paid tribute to the memory the first Georgian diplomat and painter, Giorgi Abkhaz, by visiting his newly discovered tomb in Italy. The delegation included the Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimir Konstantinid, the Chairman of the Parliament’s Diaspora and Caucasus Committee, Beka Odisharia, the Chairman of the Government of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia, Ruslan Abashidze, representatives of the International Organization for Migration and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The tomb of Giorgi Abkhazi was renovated through the involvement of the Georgian Embassy to Italy. The grave was discovered through the efforts of Georgian historian Giorgi Kalandia and the winner of the 2018-2019 program "Be the Young Ambassador of Your Country" and the winner of the grant program "Supporting Diaspora Initiatives", Neno Gabelia.
