2 October 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Georgian diplomats mourn the death of the Georgian composer, Maestro Gia Kancheli. Gia Kancheli’s genius has earned Georgia and the Georgian culture international fame. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and diplomatic representations of Georgia abroad are credited with having organized many events dedicated to Mr Kancheli, including those where the composer performed his own works. Gia Kancheli has been and will remain Georgia’s greatest ambassador abroad. We are proud that so many people in the world admire him for his talent and believe that Gia Kancheli’s timeless music will continue to be heard in the music halls of many cities as an everlasting hymn to Georgian music.
