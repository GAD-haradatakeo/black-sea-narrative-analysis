26 March 2018 26.03.2018 Tbilisi -​ Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with students of the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE) who arrived in Georgia on 26 March. The visit was co-organized by the Georgian Embassy in London and the Grimshaw International Relations Club. The Foreign Minister brought to the attention of the LSE students Georgia’s achievements and foreign policy priorities, economic progress and international rankings, steps taken towards Georgia’s integration with the EU and NATO. Mikheil Janelidze also provided information on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and on the government’s efforts to overcome the challenges.
