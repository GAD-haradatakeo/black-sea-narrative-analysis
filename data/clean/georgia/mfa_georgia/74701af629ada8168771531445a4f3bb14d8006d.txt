21 July 2023 As part of his working visit to Lisbon, on 21 July 2023, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the State Secretary for European Affairs Tiago Antunes. The sides discussed current issues relating to relations between the two countries and prospects for future cooperation. Discussions also focused on Georgia's integration into the European Union. The Minister of Foreign Affairs spoke extensively about Georgia's progress in terms of fulfilling the European Commission’s 12 recommendations. The minister once again confirmed the country's commitment to European and Euro-Atlantic integration and emphasized the importance of granting Georgia a candidate status. Talking points included the current events in the region and the difficult challenges facing Europe as a result of Russia's war in Ukraine.
