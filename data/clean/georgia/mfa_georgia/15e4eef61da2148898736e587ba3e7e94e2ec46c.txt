25 February 2020 25.02.2020, Geneva – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with United Nations HighCommissioner forRefugees, Filippo Grandi. Discussing the successful cooperation between Georgia and the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees aimed at improving the living standards of refugees and IDPs, the parties paid special attention to the grave humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied regions. Participants of the meeting expressed their concern over the occupation regime’s destructive actions that further aggravate the already difficult humanitarian situation on the ground, including discrimination of ethnic Georgians, the ban on native language education, closure of checkpoints, installation of artificial barriers across the occupation line, illegal detentions and kidnappings.
