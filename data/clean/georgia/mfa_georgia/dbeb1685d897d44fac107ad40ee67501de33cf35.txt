31 January 2024 On 31 January 2024, a meeting took place between the Acting Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Turkmenistan, Dovletmirat Seitmamedov. During the meeting, both sides underscored the amicable relations and the high-level cooperation between Georgia and Turkmenistan, both bilateral and multilateral formats. A particular focus was put on the significance of high-level visits of parliamentary delegations, and political consultations between the two nations. The sides highlighted the importance of enhancing collaboration in the areas of trade, economics, transport, logistics, and culture, and the necessity of organizing bilateral business forums. The pivotal role of the Georgia-Turkmenistan Intergovernmental Economic Cooperation Commission was emphasized and its successful work – highlighted. The participants of the meeting reiterated Turkmenistan's steadfast support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
