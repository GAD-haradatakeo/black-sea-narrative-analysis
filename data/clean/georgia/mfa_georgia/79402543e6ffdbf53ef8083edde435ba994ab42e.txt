31 August 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with his Spanish counterpart, Jose Manuel Albares, within the framework of the informal ministerial meeting of the ministers of foreign affairs of the EU Member States. Discussions focused on the bilateral relations between Georgia and Spain, as well as on the agenda of Georgia’s European integration. Spain's support for the country's European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations was emphasized, Madrid's hosting of the NATO summit and decisions supporting Georgia were highly appreciated. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia provided his Spanish colleague with information on the current agenda of European integration, as well as on Georgia's progress in fulfilling its obligations under the Association Agenda and the recommendations of the European Commission. In this regard, the Georgia-EU Association Council, which will be held in the near future, was positively evaluated.
