8 November 2021 On 8 November, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Headof theOSCE/ODIHR Election Observation Mission, Albert Jonssonand his Deputy, Gilles Saphy. At the meeting, the First Deputy Foreign Minister thanked Albert Jonsson for the work of the election observation mission and his personal efforts over the past two months. Summarizing the pre-electoral environment, the OSCE/ODIHR preliminary findings for both rounds of elections, Albert Jonsson noted that the elections were well administered and the overall atmosphere remained calm. According to Lasha Darsalia, it is important for Georgia to ensure that elections are conducted democratically and with high standards.
