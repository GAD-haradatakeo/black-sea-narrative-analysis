13 September 2017 13.09.2017, Chengdu, China - The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participates in the official opening of the 22nd General Assembly of the UN World Tourism Organization (UNWTO) held in Chengdu, China from 12 to 15 September 2017. Today he will speak before the high-level segment highlighting the importance of tourism industry in the development many countries across the world and the success of Georgia’s tourism sector. Mikheil janelidze will also participate in the tree planting ceremony. The Assembly officially opened in Chengdu. The Vice Premier of the State Council of the People’s Republic of China Wang Yang and the Secretary-General of the United Nations' World Tourism Organization, Taleb Rifai appeared before the General Assembly. The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze attended the ceremony as an honorary guest. The 22nd General Assembly will approve candidate for the post of UNWTO Secretary-General for the period 2018-2021. The 105th session of the UNWTO Executive Council, meeting in Madrid, Spain, on 12 May 2017, recommended Zurab Pololikashvili, Ambassador of Georgia to Spain, as a nominee for the post of Secretary-General for the four-year-period starting January 2018. Candidate will be approved by the General Assembly tomorrow. The UNWTO General Assembly is attended by 1500 delegates from 150 countries across the world. Documents Similar News
