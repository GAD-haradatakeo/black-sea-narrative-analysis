30 December 2024 Protecting Georgia's interests means striving to ensure the security, stability, economic development, and genuine European future of our nation, stated Georgia's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, during her address at the closing ceremony of the Ambassadors' Conference. According to Maka Botchorishvili, alongside challenges, there are also abundant opportunities that must not be overlooked but fully realized for the benefit of the country. "Today marks the conclusion of the two-day work of the Ambassadors' Conference. First and foremost, I would like to extend my gratitude to the President for beginning his presidential activities by meeting with those who represent our nation's interests abroad. I also express my appreciation to the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze, and members of the Georgian government for the engaging meetings and discussions over the past two days. My thanks extend to the chairman of the Georgian Parliament, Shalva Papuashvili, and members of Parliament for their active participation in the Ambassadors' Conference. And, of course, my heartfelt gratitude goes to all of you, our representatives abroad, who serve our homeland with dedication and love. Today, Georgia faces numerous challenges, and in this complicated geopolitical environment, our unity and resilience hold paramount importance. Defending Georgia's interests today means striving for the security, stability, economic development, and authentic European future of our country. Alongside the challenges, there are also many opportunities that we must not miss but rather fully embrace for the benefit of our country," stated Maka Botchorishvili. Documents Similar News
