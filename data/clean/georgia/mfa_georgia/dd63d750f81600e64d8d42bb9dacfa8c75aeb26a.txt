20 June 2022 On 20 June 2022, the Ambassadors of the Commonwealth of Australia, New Zealand, the Kingdom of Thailand and the Kyrgyz Republic presented copies of their credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili. At the meetings held following the presentation ceremony, the sides focused on current issues relating to bilateral relations and expressed the desire to further deepen cooperation. The Deputy Foreign Minister congratulated the Ambassadors of the above-mentioned countries on their appointment to this very responsible position, and expressed the readiness for cooperation and wished them success in their future activities.
