7 September 2022 Within the framework of his visit to North Macedonia, on 7 September, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the Secretary of State for European Affairs of North Macedonia, Drita Abdiu Halili. The sides discussed the relations at different levels, including the strengthening of European integration in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Enhancing Georgia's involvement in the regional initiatives of the Western Balkans, especially in the field of connectivity was also an important topic of the consultations.
