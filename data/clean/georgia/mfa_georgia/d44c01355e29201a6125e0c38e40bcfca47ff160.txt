20 August 2021 Davit Zalkaliani congratulated his Armenian counterpart on his appointment to the post of Minister of Foreign Affairs and wished him success in his future endeavors. The Georgian Foreign Minister also congratulated his colleague on the democratic parliamentary elections in Armenia. During the telephone conversation, the sides discussed main issues on the agenda between the Foreign Ministries of the two countries. The importance of regional peace and stability was also highlighted. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan thanked his Georgian counterpart for Georgia’s significant contribution to the repatriation of Armenian prisoners. For his part, Davit Zalkaliani reaffirmed Georgia’s commitment to further fulfill its role as peacemaker, which will help the countries of the region, and the region as a whole, promote dialogue, peace and stability.
