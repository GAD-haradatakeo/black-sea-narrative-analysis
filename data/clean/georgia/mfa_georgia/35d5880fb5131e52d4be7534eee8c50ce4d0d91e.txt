21 September 2022 On 20 September 2022, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the United Arab Emirates, Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan. The Ministers noted with satisfaction the friendly and partnership ties between Georgia and the United Emirates, as well as successful cooperation in various directions, including in economy. The growing interest of the Arab business communities towards Georgia and the already implemented large investment projects were also emphasized. The Ministers expressed their readiness to work on the preparation of a relevant contractual basis for the activation of trade and economic ties. The sides noted the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries. The sides welcomed the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the United Arab Emirates and Georgia and noted that the anniversary date is a good opportunity to further deepen the ties between the two countries.
