2 November 2021 On 2 November 2021, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Austria to Georgia, Thomas Mülmann. The First Deputy Minister and the Ambassador reviewed the recent active dynamics in the relations between the two countries, especially the bilateral meeting between the Prime Ministers of Georgia and Austria within the framework of the 26th UN Climate Change Conference and the official visit of the President to Georgia to Austria last month. The meeting focused on the recent round of Geneva International Discussions between Georgia and Russia, Georgia 's important initiative in the context of developing regional cooperation and resolving the Armenia - Azerbaijan conflict.
