26 April 2022 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Akira Imamura, on 26 April 2022. The sides discussed in detail the Georgia-Japan cooperation agenda and the prospects for its further development in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Discussions also focused on issues of regional and global importance. The sides noted with satisfaction the increasing dynamics of relations between the two countries. In this regard, economic cooperation and the importance of its further deepening were underlined. Highlighting the importance of exchanging high-level visits, the sides considered the possibility of holding joint events in the field of culture and public diplomacy to mark the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Japan this year.
