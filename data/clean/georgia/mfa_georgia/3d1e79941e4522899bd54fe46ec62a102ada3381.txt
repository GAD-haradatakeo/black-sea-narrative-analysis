22 September 2023 At the meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Guatemala, Mario Adolfo Búcaro, the sides emphasized the fruitful cooperation between the two countries in both bilateral and multilateral formats. It was noted that Georgia attaches special importance to the further deepening of cooperation with the countries of the region. The ministers expressed their readiness to work for the further development of bilateral relations. During the meeting with the Foreign Minister of the Republic of Marshall Islands, Jack Ading, the successful cooperation of Georgia with the countries of the Pacific Ocean basin was discussed. The sides emphasized the great potential of bilateral cooperation.
