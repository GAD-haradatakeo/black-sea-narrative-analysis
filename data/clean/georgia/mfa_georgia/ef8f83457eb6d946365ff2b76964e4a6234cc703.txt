3 February 2023 On 2 February 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Sweden to Georgia, Ulrik Tideström. The sides discussed the current agenda of cooperation between the two countries, including the exchange of high-level visits and the development of bilateral relations between Georgia and the Kingdom of Sweden. Special attention was paid to Georgia’s European integration path and the reforms carried out in this regard. The special role of Sweden, as the EU presidency, was noted on the path of Georgia's accession to the European Union.
