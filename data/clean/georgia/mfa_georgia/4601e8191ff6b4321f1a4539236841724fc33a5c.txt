25 December 2025 Within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference, a working meeting was held with Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze. The session was chaired by the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili. The Prime Minister addressed Georgia’s foreign policy priorities, ongoing developments within the country and the existing challenges. The discussion also addressed the Government’s future plans, with particular emphasis on the necessity of maintaining peace and security in the region. Emphasis was placed on the important role of the diplomatic service in advancing and safeguarding Georgia’s national interests on the international stage.
