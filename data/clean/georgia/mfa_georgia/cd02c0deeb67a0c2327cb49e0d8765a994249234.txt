28 October 2019 28.10.2019, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Deputy Secretary-General of the United Nations on political issues, Rosemary Dicarlo. During the meeting David Zalkaliani expressed gratitude towards Rosemary Dicarlo for close cooperation with Georgia and active support of the UN in terms of finding ways of peaceful resolution of the Russian-Georgian conflict. The tenuous and difficult situation in the occupied regions of Georgia was also discussed within the framework of the meeting. A particular emphasis was made on the challenging reality in terms of human rights violations that take place on the ground, as well as other, latest developments along the occupation line. The major role of UN’s involvement in the Geneva International Discussions was also, once again, emphasized. The parties agreed to continue the close cooperation on all priority issues. The Deputy Secretary-General of the UN is paying a visit to Georgia during the period of 27-28th of October.
