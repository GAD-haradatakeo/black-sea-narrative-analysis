27 February 2024 On 27 February, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with the UN High Commissioner for Refugees, Filippo Grandi. The Minister congratulated Filippo Grandi on the effective organization of the &quot;Second Global Refugee Forum&quot; in late 2023 and expressed appreciation for the efforts of the Office of the High Commissioner in Georgia. Ilia Darchiashvili addressed the challenging humanitarian conditions in regions of Georgia occupied by Russia, highlighting the importance of upholding the rights of internally displaced individuals and refugees. The minister emphasized the significance of the Tbilisi office of the High Commissioner&#39;s efforts concerning the needs of the citizens living in the occupied regions. Discussions also focused on instances of human rights violations in those regions. Ilia Darchiashvili highlighted the importance of granting access to the international mechanisms to the occupied areas and stressed the significance of maintaining the matter prominently on the agenda of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees.
