29 October 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will participate in the 43rd session of the UNESCO General Conference, to be held in Samarkand. Within the framework of the visit, the Minister will deliver an address at the 43rd session and hold meetings with her counterpart ministers as well as senior UNESCO officials. As part of the UNESCO General Conference, a Georgian pavilion will be organized, dedicated to Georgia’s intangible cultural heritage - the traditional method of Qvevri wine-making and Georgian polyphonic singing. Additionally, a concert by the Georgian folk ensemble Basiani will be held on the stage of the Samarkand Music and Drama Theatre.
