10 March 2021 "The statement of the Director of the Foreign Intelligence Service, Sergey Naryshkin is yet another example of the hybrid war waged by the Russian Federation against Georgia and a provocative step aimed at provoking instability in Georgia. Pursuing its policy of illegal occupation and annexation of Georgian territories, the Russian Federation continues its attempts, through the active use of hybrid war elements and other methods, to interfere with Georgia’s sovereign choice for the democratic development of the country and its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. With such statements, the Russian Federation tries to spark nihilism, polarization and confrontation directed against establishing peace and stability not only in Georgia but also in the region in a time of turbulence it is facing. The Russian Federation’s efforts are aimed at discrediting the very close relationship between Georgia and its key strategic partner – the U.S. – the relationship that has been actively developing since the day Georgia regained its independence and has reached its highest level during the past few years. Relying on the support of its strategic partners and friends, Georgia will continue its relentless pursuit of democratic development and unswervingly follow the path towards fulfilment of the Georgian people’s historic choice.
