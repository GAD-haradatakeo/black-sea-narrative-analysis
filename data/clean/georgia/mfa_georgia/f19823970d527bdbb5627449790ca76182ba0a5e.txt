8 August 2025 On the occasion of the 17th anniversary of the 2008 Russia–Georgia war, and in honor of the memory of those who lost their lives, the national flags are flying at half-mast at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, as well as at Georgia’s diplomatic missions and consular offices abroad. It is noteworthy that, on the initiative of Georgia’s diplomatic representations, various commemorative events dedicated to the 17th anniversary of the war are being held worldwide.
