5 November 2019 5.11.2019, Sweden – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with representatives of the Georgian Diaspora in Sweden to highlight that the government attaches priority importance to deepening relations with Georgian diaspora. According to Zalkaliani, all embassies have been instructed by the foreign ministry to remain in active communication with Diaspora representatives. The Minister said that he personally follows all Diaspora-related issues and is always aware of Diaspora needs. He brought to the attention of the Georgia compatriots living abroad all projects and initiatives aimed at Diaspora support, including the creation of Sunday schools, Diaspora Support Fund and universal communication platform. Special mention was also taken of the ongoing projects in Georgia focused on the development of business, agriculture and attraction of investments. According to Zalkaliani, the Prime Minister attaches priority to the development of inclusive economy and solution of daily problems for the population.
