25 August 2022 On 25 August, 2022, the Ambassador-designate of the Republic of Israel to Georgia, Hadas Ester Meitsad, presented copies of her credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili. The sides discussed a wide range of issues on the agenda of bilateral relations. Special attention was focused on the importance of further deepening cooperation in political, economic, humanitarian and other fields. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs congratulated the Ambassador of Israel on her appointment to this highly responsible position and wished her success in her diplomatic activities.
