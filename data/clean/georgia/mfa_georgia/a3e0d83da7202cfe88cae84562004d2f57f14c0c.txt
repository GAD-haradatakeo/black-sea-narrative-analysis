16 October 2019 16.10.2019, Great Britain - Within the UK-Georgia Wardrop Strategic Dialogue’s Parliamentary Dimension, at the meeting between All-Party Parliamentary Group on Georgia and Georgian Parliamentary delegation held on 14 October 2019, Georgian and British MPs adopted the Joint Communiqué that reflects various aspects of bilateral strategic partnership, also defines priorities for future cooperation. Joint Communiqué underlines Russia’s occupation of Georgian territories, and at the same time highlights UK’s support to the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia as well as towards Georgia’s Euro-Atlantic integration and ongoing reforms.
