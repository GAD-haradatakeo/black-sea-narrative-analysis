23 October 2019 23.10.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Deputy Prime Minister of Armenia Tigran Avinyan on the sidelines of the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum. The sides discussed the increasing level of trade-economic relations and tourism cooperation between the two countries. Zalkaliani thanked the Armenian Deputy Prime Minister for high-level participation in the Silk Road Forum. Discussions also focused on the Georgian Prime Minister’s recent visit to the Republic of Armenia.
