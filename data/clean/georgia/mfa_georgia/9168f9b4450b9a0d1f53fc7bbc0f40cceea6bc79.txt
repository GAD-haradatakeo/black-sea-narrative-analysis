7 November 2023 On 7 November 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador of the Slovak Republic, Matus Busovski. Discussions focused on strong friendly ties and fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Slovakia. The minister emphasized the importance of Slovakia's support for Georgia's foreign policy priorities and expressed his gratitude for the firm support for the country's territorial integrity and sovereignty, as well as for its European aspirations. The sides highlighted Georgia’s European integration process and the progress achieved on this path.
