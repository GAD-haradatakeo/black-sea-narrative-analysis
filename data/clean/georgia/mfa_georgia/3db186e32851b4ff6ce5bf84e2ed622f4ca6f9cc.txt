29 March 2022 On 29 March 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Armenia Ararat Mirzoyan will pay a working visit to Georgia. During the visit, the Armenian Foreign Minister will hold meetings with the Prime Minister of Georgia Irakli Gharibashvili and the Vice Prime Minister /Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani. At the meetings, the sides will discuss a wide range of bilateral relations and prospects for their development.
