9 February 2022 A special mission of the European Commission pays a visit to Georgia from 7 to 11 February 2022, to hold consultations with the Georgian side on current and future priorities/projects under the European Union’s bilateral financial assistance. Within the framework of the visit, the Mission held consultations with current and potential beneficiaries of the EU TAIEX and Twinning programs in such areas as digitalization; "Green" transformation; Transport Connectivity and DCFTA & Economic Recovery (7 February). During the visit, a bilateral meeting was held with the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia, and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs organized consultations with the relevant agencies on the Eastern Partnership Economic and Investment Plan (EIP) flagship initiatives (February 8). By the end of the visit, bilateral meetings with individual sectoral agencies will be held on EU Assistance Priorities 2021 and 2022 (9-11 February).
