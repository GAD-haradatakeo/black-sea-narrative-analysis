26 February 2025 The Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili is on her first official visit to the Republic of Türkiye. Before the commencement of official meetings, the Foreign Minister visited the mausoleum of Mustafa Kemal Atatürk, where she laid a wreath at his tomb as a tribute. Maka Botchorishvili also signed the memorial book. As part of her visit, the Foreign Minister has already held a meeting with her Turkish counterpart, Hakan Fidan, and is scheduled to meet with the Speaker of the Grand National Assembly of Türkiye, Numan Kurtulmuş.
