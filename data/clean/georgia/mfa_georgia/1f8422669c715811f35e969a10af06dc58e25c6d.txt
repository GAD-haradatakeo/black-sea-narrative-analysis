9 March 2018 9.03.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of South Africa to Georgia Christiaan Albertus Basson. The sides discussed important issues on the agenda of bilateral and multilateral relations, as well as prospects for deepening trade-economic links and co-operation within international organizations. David Zalkaliani thanked the Ambassador for his contribution to the strengthening of bilateral relations between Georgia and South Africa and wished him success in his future endeavours.
