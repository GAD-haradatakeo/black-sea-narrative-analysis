31 January 2024 Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili has met with the Ambassador of the Republic of Kazakhstan On 31 January 2024, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, convened a meeting with the Ambassador of the Republic of Kazakhstan to Georgia, Malik Murzalin. During the session, particular emphasis was placed on the exchange of high-level visits of parliamentary delegations between the two nations. Both sides expressed satisfaction with the existing amicable relations and engaged in discussions encompassing a broad spectrum of topics on the bilateral and multilateral agenda between Georgia and Kazakhstan. The sides discussed prospects for enhancing and deepening cooperation in the areas of economy, transportation, and tourism in the immediate future, including matters pertaining to the forthcoming round of the Georgia-Kazakhstan Intergovernmental Economic Commission and participation in high-level international events in both countries.
