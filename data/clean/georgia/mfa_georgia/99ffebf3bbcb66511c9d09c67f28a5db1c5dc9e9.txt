28 April 2022 On 28 April 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of Bangladesh to Georgia (residing in Ankara), Mosud Mannan. The sides discussed the agenda of bilateral cooperation between the two countries and the prospects for its further development, as well as the formats of regional cooperation. The sides noted that Georgia and Bangladesh are interested in deepening sectoral cooperation, strengthening trade and economic ties, as well as in cooperation on tourism, textile industry, medicine and other fields. The sides highlighted Georgia’s attractive business climate, as well as the support mechanisms created by the Government of Georgia for investors. Special note was taken of Bangladeshi students’ increasing interest in Georgian higher education institutions. The sides exchanged views on the security environment in Georgia and across the region. They reaffirmed mutual support for the territorial integrity of each other’s countries.
