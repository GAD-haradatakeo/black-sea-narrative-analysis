23 February 2023 On the sidelines of the UN special session in New York, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili he met with the Vice Prime Minister of the Republic of Slovenia, Minister of Foreign and European Affairs, Tania Fajon. At the meeting, the ministers reviewed both the issues of bilateral cooperation and current topics of the international agenda. Ilia Darchiashvili informed his colleague about the progress in Georgia’s the European integration process, including in the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations by the government of Georgia. The great importance of granting Georgia a candidate status was highlighted at the meeting. The Slovenian Foreign Minister once again confirmed Slovenia's support to Georgia and expressed his readiness to share his practical experience with Georgia.
