16 February 2026 On 16 February 2026, a preparatory meeting for the 66th round of the Geneva International Discussions was held in Tbilisi with the participation of the Co-Chairs from the European Union, the United Nations, and the Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe (OSCE). The meeting was chaired on behalf of Georgia by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs and Head of the Georgian Delegation to the Geneva International Discussions, Lasha Darsalia. The discussions focused on the importance of effective functioning of the Geneva International Discussions. The necessity of making progress on the key agenda items of the Geneva International Discussions – Russia’s compliance with its international obligations under the EU-mediated 12 August 2008 Ceasefire Agreement, as well as the return of internally displaced persons and refugees in a safe and dignified manner was stressed. Furthermore, the meeting emphasized the necessity to restore the Incident Prevention and Response Mechanism (IPRM) in Gali without any preconditions and to hold regular meetings of the IPRM in Gali and Ergneti in full accordance with its founding principles. The Georgian side highlighted the security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied regions and in areas adjacent to the occupation line. Additionally, attention was drawn to actions that constitute blatant violations of fundamental principles of international law, aimed at the illegal annexation of the occupied territories. The participants of the meeting also discussed the intensified attempts of the Russian Federation and its occupation regime in Sokhumi aiming to restrict and block confidence-building projects.
