6 March 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Ambassador-designate of Belgium to Georgia, Julien de Fraipont (residing in Baku). The discussion focused on prospects for deepening the friendly relations between the two countries. Particular attention was given to Georgia-EU relations and opportunities for future development. Additionally, the parties exchanged views on the security environment in Europe and the broader region, as well as the associated challenges. The meeting also addressed ongoing developments within Georgia. Minister Botchorishvili wished the Ambassador success in his tenure and expressed readiness for further cooperation.
