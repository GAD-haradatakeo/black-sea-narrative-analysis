22 July 2022 From 18 to 20 July 2022, the delegation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, led by the Deputy Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia, paid a working visit to Montenegro and Bosnia-Herzegovina. During his visit to Bosnia-Herzegovina, the Deputy Minister met with his Bosnian counterpart, Josip Brkic, and in Montenegro, with the State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Montenegro, Lubomir Misurovic, and the Deputy Minister of European Affairs of Montenegro, Alessandar Mashkovic. The main topic of discussions was Georgia’s integration with the European Union and the exchange of relevant experience between the two countries. The sides highlighted the importance of further deepening cooperation between Georgia and the countries of the Western Balkans and coordination of joint efforts on the path of integration into the European Union. In addition, during the meeting, the conversation touched on the prospects for the development of bilateral and regional cooperation.
