29 December 2025 On 29 December 2025, a telephone conversation took place between Alexander Khvtisiashvili, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Zhou Qian, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the People's Republic of China to Georgia, (on his request). During the conversation, the parties discussed the cooperation between the two countries and plans for next year. The parties exchanged information on the current developments in the region and issues of international importance.
