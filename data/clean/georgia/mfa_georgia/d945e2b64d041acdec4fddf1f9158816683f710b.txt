25 May 2018 25.05.2018, Tbilisi - The President of the European Commission Jean-Claude Juncker arrived to Georgia today to participate in the celebrations of the centennial of the country’s first Democratic Republic on 26 May. Tomorrow Juncker will deliver a speech at the main spot for the celebrations, Freedom Square.
