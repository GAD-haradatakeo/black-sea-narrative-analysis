10 May 2018 10.05.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with students participating in the European Youth Project offering them insights into Georgia’s successes on the European integration path. According to Mikheil Janelidze, the ultimate goal of Georgia is to attain EU membership. The Minister provided the students with information on the Georgian government’s peace policy paying special attention to the mechanisms of peaceful resolution of the conflicts. Underlining the European Union’s important role in this process, Mikheil Janelidze said that Georgia is the European Union’s reliable security partner. Mikheil Janelidze spoke extensively about visa-free travel as a substantial achievement of on the path of European integration. He presented to the students a concept of the EU integration roadmap.
