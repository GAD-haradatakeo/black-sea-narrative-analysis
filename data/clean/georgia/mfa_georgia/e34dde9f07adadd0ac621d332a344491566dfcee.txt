30 December 2020 On 30 December, First Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili held an online meeting with civil society representatives working on European integration issues. The aim of the meeting was to hold consultations with non-governmental sector with regard to Georgia’s plans to apply for full European Union membership in 2024. The meeting was attended by representatives of civil society and expert community, as well as by MFA officials responsible for European integration. The participants of the meeting positively appraised the government’s decision to apply for full European Union membership in 2024.
