25 December 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, attended a Hanukkah celebration at Tbilisi’s Main Synagogue, alongside Prime Minister Irakli Kobakhidze and representatives of the executive, legislative, and local governments. In her remarks, Maka Bochorishvili congratulated the Jewish community on the Festival of Lights, symbolizing the triumph of light over darkness, and wished them happiness and prosperity.
