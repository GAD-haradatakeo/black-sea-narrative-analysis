5 August 2021 On 4 August 2021, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Chairman of the Government Commission for elaboration and implementation of Strategy for de-occupation and sustainable peaceful conflict resolution, Lasha Darsalia visited Kutaisi and Khoni. During the visit to the regions, Lasha Darsalia held meetings with representatives of civil society organizations working on conflict issues, local NGOs and experts, as well as local residents. The First Deputy Foreign Minister will also visit Zugdidi, Gori and Tserovani on 5-7 August The First Deputy Foreign Minister’s visit to and meeting in the regions of Georgia are held within the framework the Government Commission for elaboration and implementation of Strategy for de-occupation and sustainable peaceful conflict resolution. The purpose of the field meetings is to carry out the inclusive whole of the nation process to develop a unified state vision for de-occupation and peaceful resolution of the conflict, and to conduct a broadly-based public dialogue with representatives of different regions of Georgia and internally displaced persons.
