25 June 2018 25.06.2018 – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with his Turkish counterpart, Foreign Minister, Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu. David Zalkaliani congratulated his Turkish counterpart on the successful conduction of the elections. Expressing the hope for fruitful co-operation, Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu, for his part, congratulated the Georgian Foreign Minister on his recent appointment to a new position. The Turkish Foreign Minister expressed his firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. The sides noted with satisfaction the strategic partnership between the two countries. The sides expressed the hope that mutually beneficial co-operation will develop successfully both in bilateral and multilateral, regional and international formats.
