1 November 2017 01.11.2017, Tbilisi - State Minister of Georgia for Reconciliation and Civic Equality Ketevan Tskikhelashvili and Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a working dinner for representatives of the international forum “From Words to Action: SDG 16+ Implementing SDG 16+ at the local and national level”, as well as ambassadors to the UN and representatives of the foreign ministries of various countries.
