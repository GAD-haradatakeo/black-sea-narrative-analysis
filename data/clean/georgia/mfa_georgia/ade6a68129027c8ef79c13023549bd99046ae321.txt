5 July 2017 5.07.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze attended the reception to mark Independence Day of Belarus The event was opened by introductory remarks of Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze and Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Belarus to Georgia Mikhail Myatlikov. Mikheil Janelidze congratulated Belarus and Belarusian people on the Day of Independence wishing them peace, stability and welfare. According to the Minister, the opening of the Belarusian Embassy in Georgia have already brought about tabgible results, including the intensification of trade-economic and cultural relations and people-to-people contacts between the two countries. Mikheil Janelidze especially welcomed Georgian veterans of the Second World War paying tribute to the memory of those Georgian soldiers who laid down their lives for the liberation of Minsk, the capital of Belarus in 1944.
