31 March 2022 On 31 March 2022, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with the Vice Prime Minister of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Minister of Foreign Affairs, Mukhtar Tleuberdi. The Ministers reviewed the situation in the region, paying special attention to the long-term economic and political consequences of the ongoing war in Ukraine, both in the region and around the world. Talks focused on the agenda of Georgian-Kazakh bilateral cooperation and its development prospects, with particular emphasis on trade and economic cooperation, namely, on the further development of the Trans-Caspian international transport.
