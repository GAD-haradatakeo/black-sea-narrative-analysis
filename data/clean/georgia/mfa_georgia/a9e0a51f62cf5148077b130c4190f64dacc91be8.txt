6 December 2018 6.12.2018, Milan – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Director of the OSCE Office for Democratic Institutions and Human Rights Ingibjörg Sólrún Gísladóttir, on the sidelines of the 25th OSCE Ministerial Council. The sides discussed the presidential elections held in Georgia. It was highlighted that the elections were conducted in a free and competitive environment. Zalkaliani thanked the OSCE/ODIHR for monitoring these elections and expressed the Government’s readiness to follow up on its recommendations. According to the Foreign Minister, the government looks forward to the final report of the OSCE/ODIHR observation mission.
