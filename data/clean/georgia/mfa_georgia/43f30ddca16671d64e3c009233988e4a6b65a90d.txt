6 December 2018 6.12.2018, Milan - On the sidelines of the 25th OSCE Ministerial Council, the Georgian delegation held a meeting with the Co-chairs of the Geneva International Discussions Toivo Klaar, Cihan Sultanoğlu and Rudolf Michalka. Highlighting the importance of involvement of the EU, OSCE and UN in the Geneva International Discussions, the sides reaffirmed that more efforts need to be made to deliver results on the main issues of the agenda. The sides also highlighted the role of the IPRMs in de-escalating the situation and ensuring peace. The need of restarting the IPRMs in full compliance with the fundamental principles and rules on the ground was also underlined during the meeting.
