5 December 2019 05.12.2019, Bratislava – Within the framework of the 26th Ministerial Council inBratislava, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with his Lithuanian Linas Linkevičius. Zalkaliani thanked the Lithuanian Foreign Minister for his country’s support for Georgia within the scope of the OSCE Group of Friends. Discussions during the meeting focused on the difficult human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied regions that is cause of special concern to our partners. The Parties paid special attention to the illegal detention of Vazha Gaprindashvili and to the necessity of his immediate release.
