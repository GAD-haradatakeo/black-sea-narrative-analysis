5 August 2025 Tornike Nozadze has been appointed as the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Indonesia, as well as Georgia’s Representative to the Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN). Since 2024, Mr. Nozadze has served as the Chargé d’Affaires of the Embassy of Georgia in the Republic of Indonesia and as the Head of Georgia’s Mission to ASEAN. Tornike Nozadze brings extensive experience in the public sector. Notably, from 2019 to 2022, he served as Chargé d’Affaires of Georgia in the Republic of Finland.
