24 October 2017 24.10.2017 - Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Vincenzo Amendola, the Undersecretary of State for Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of Italy due to assume the chairmanship of OSCE in 2018. Discussions focused on issues relating to co-operation between Georgia and the OSCE. The Italian side was provided with the detailed information regarding the security and human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the policy of ethnic cleansing against ethnic Georgians in the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. Special attention was paid to OSCE’s role and to the need of the international community’s involvement in the conflict resolution process.
