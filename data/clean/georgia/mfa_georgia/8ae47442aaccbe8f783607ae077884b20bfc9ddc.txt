18 March 2021 The United Nations Economic Commission for Europe's 2021 Regional Forum on „Sustainable and resilient recovery from the COVID-19 pandemic and action and delivery on the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development in the UNECE Region“ was held on 17 March. The session was attended by Deputy Foreign Minister Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili. The session put a specific focus on achieving Sustainable Development Goals and on tackling the socioeconomic impacts of COVID-19.
