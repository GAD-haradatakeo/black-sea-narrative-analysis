11 July 2025 Within the framework of the 2025 Dubrovnik Forum, Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs Maka Botchorishvili held a meeting with Gordan Grlić Radman, Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of the Republic of Croatia. The ministers discussed key issues on the bilateral and multilateral cooperation agenda, emphasizing the importance of maintaining an active high-level political dialogue. The sides reviewed Georgia’s progress on its path toward European Union integration and highlighted Croatia’s firm support for Georgia’s European aspirations. In this context, they underlined the value of sharing Croatia’s experience in the EU accession process.
