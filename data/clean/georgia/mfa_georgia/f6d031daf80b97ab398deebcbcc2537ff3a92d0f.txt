17 November 2017 17.11.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meetng with Designated European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia Toivo Klaar. Mikheil Janelidze congratulated Toivo Klaar on his appointment to a new position wishing him success in his future endeavours. Mikheil Janelidze highlighted the important role of the institute of European Union Special Representative for the South Caucasus and the Crisis in Georgia in terms of the peaceful resolution of the Russia-Georgia conflict. The sides discussed the difficult security and human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories reaffirming the need to achieve the progress in the Geneva International Discussions.
