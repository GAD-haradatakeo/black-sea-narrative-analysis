19 March 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with a parliamentary delegation from Latvia, during which they discussed various aspects of bilateral cooperation. One key topic was the significance of maintaining regular political dialogue and the necessity of conducting high-level visits between the two countries. Additionally, they focused on Georgia's efforts and reforms aimed at joining the European Union, with the Latvian side reaffirming strong support for Georgia's EU accession. The conversation also touched on Georgia's Euro-Atlantic integration. Trade and economic relations between the two countries were also reviewed, highlighting the importance of maximizing the existing potential. The complex regional security challenges were also on the agenda.
