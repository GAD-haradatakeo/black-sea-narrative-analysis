20 July 2022 Within the framework of the visit of the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili to Turkmenistan, the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Turkmenistan signed the 2023-2024 Programme of Cooperation on 20 July 2022, in Ashgabat. From the Georgian side, the Programme was signed by the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, and from the Turkmen side, the Deputy Chairman of the Cabinet of Ministers of Turkmenistan, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Rashid Meredov. The Programme will contribute to the further strengthening of bilateral cooperation on matters of foreign policy, expansion of bilateral economic ties, improvement and development of the international legal framework.
