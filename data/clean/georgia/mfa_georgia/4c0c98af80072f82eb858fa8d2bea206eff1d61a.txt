28 April 2023 Within the framework of her visit to Georgia, the Minister of Europe and Foreign Affairs of France, Catherine Colonna, together with the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, laid a wreath at the memorial of the heroes who died for the unity of Georgia. It should be noted that within the framework of the visit, Catherine Colonna held meetings with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Garibashvili, the President of Georgia, Salome Zourabichvili, and the Georgian Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili.
