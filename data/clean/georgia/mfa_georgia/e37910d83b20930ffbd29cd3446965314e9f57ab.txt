16 November 2023 On 16 November 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the Acting UNDP Deputy Resident Representative in Georgia, Douglas Webb. During the meeting, the sides talked about the issues on the agenda of cooperation between Georgia and UNDP. Special accent was put on the UNDP’ efforts towards the implementation of sustainable development agenda, economic development, democratic governance and protection of human rights in Georgia.
