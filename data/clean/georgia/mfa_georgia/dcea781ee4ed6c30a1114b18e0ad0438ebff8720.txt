3 February 2022 On 3 February 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal Republic of Germany to Georgia, Hubert Knirsch. During the meeting, the sides discussed the agenda of bilateral relations and plans for future cooperation. Discussions focused on strategic issues between the foreign ministries of the three countries within the Georgian-German-French triangle. The sides highlighted the importance of high-level visits conducted throughout the year by representatives of both the legislative and executive branches of the government. They spoke about the celebration of the 30th anniversary of the resumption of diplomatic relations between the two countries. In addition to the agenda of bilateral relations, the sides discussed and exchanged views on the situation in the region and security challenges, Germany's involvement in the de-escalation of the Ukraine crisis, Russian aggression against Georgia and the situation in the occupied territories. The sides highlighted the importance of keeping Georgia-related issues high on the agenda of the German G7 presidency. Talks also focused on Georgia's foreign policy priorities, such as EU integration and NATO membership.
