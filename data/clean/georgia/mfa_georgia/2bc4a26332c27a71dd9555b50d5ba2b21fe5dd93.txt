28 June 2017 28.06.2017, Vienna - On the sidelines of the Annual Security Review Conference (ASRC) in Vienna, Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with representatives of the non-formal group of Georgia’s Friends to OSCE (U.S., Canada, Great Britain, Sweden, Poland, Romania, Norway). Discussions focused on issues relating to co-operation within OSCE. Special attention was paid to the Georgian-Russian conflict. It was underlined that OSCE represents an important platform for discussing the conflict-related isues.
