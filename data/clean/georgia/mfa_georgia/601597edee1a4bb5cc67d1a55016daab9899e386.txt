18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held an introductory meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador of the Arab Republic of Egypt to Georgia, Amal Afifi (resident in Yerevan). The parties welcomed the positive agenda of cooperation between Georgia and Egypt, both in bilateral and multilateral formats. They discussed the state of relations between the two countries in the fields of trade and economy, tourism, education, and culture, and emphasized the importance of fully realizing the existing potential in these areas. Both sides reaffirmed their readiness to deepen relations across all areas of mutual interest.
