Our sincere thanks for the support of Georgia during the challenging time of the pandemic” – David Zalkaliani to the diplomatic corps accredited in Georgia 15 June 2020 “I was looking forward to this day as personal relations are critically important for us, diplomats. I have had many telephone conversations with my colleagues over the past months, today I spoke over the phone with the German Foreign Minister and we expressed the concern that remote relations create some kind of barriers in diplomacy and we are pleased that we can now return to normal gradually and restore what is called personal relations in diplomacy” -Zalkaliani said.He thanked diplomats, international organizations and various countries for their assistance to Georgia in the fight against the pandemic.The Minister underlined that even amid the pandemic, Russia continued illegal activities and the borderization process in Georgia’s occupied territories, and even conducted a disinformation campaign against such successful institutions as the Lugar laboratory. “Our friends and partners demonstrated to Georgia an unprecedented level of support. The UN, OSCE, EU and various countries made statement in support for Georgia that is a very important fact” – Zalkaliani said.He also highlighted Georgia’s successful presidency of the CoE Committee of Ministers amid the COVID-19 pandemic.
