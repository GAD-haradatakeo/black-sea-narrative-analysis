20 November 2019 At the initiative of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, the deadline for submitting applications under the grant program “Be Your Country’s Young Ambassador” has been extended. Originally, applications could be submitted between 31 October and 20 November 2019. Taking into account requests from representatives of the diaspora, the submission deadline has been extended, with the new final date set for 25 November 2019. The “Young Ambassadors of Georgia” program of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs enables young Georgians living abroad to serve for a one-year term as “Young Ambassadors,” promoting Georgia in their countries of residence. The main objective of the Young Ambassadors is to raise awareness of Georgia’s economy, education, culture, sports, youth policy, and history among Georgian and foreign youth, while also expanding the network of Georgia’s young friends abroad. This is to be achieved through cultural, educational, and sports initiatives. The program is open to Georgian citizens aged 18–29 residing in: Austria; Azerbaijan; the United States of America; Belarus; Belgium; Bulgaria; Germany; Denmark; the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland; Spain; Estonia; Turkey; Japan; Ireland; Israel; Italy; Canada; Cyprus; Korea; Latvia; Lithuania; Malta; Moldova; the Netherlands; Norway; Poland; Greece; France; Ukraine; Hungary; Finland; Switzerland; Sweden; China; and the Czech Republic. As part of the project, selected Young Ambassadors will participate in the Young Ambassadors’ Assembly in Georgia, where they will present their initiatives and one-year action plans. They will also meet representatives of the cultural, political, economic, diplomatic, and other sectors.
