9 September 2025 On 9 September 2025, the third round of bilateral political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Kingdom of Thailand was held in Tbilisi. The Georgian delegation was led by Kakhaber Sikharulidze, Director of the Directorate of Global Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, while the Thai delegation was headed by Krongkanit Rakcharoen, Director-General of European Affairs of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Thailand. During the consultations, the parties welcomed the friendly relations and cooperation existing between Georgia and Thailand. A wide range of issues in bilateral relations was discussed, including cooperation in the fields of trade, economy, tourism, and people-to-people contacts, as well as prospects for their further development. The parties also addressed opportunities for launching joint projects across various sectors and for the exchange of experience. Particular emphasis was placed on the importance of reciprocal visits at both high and sectoral levels, including exchanges between government institutions and the private sector. The discussions also covered current issues on the regional and international agenda, including developments and challenges in the South Caucasus and Southeast Asia. The consultations underscored the importance of further deepening political dialogue and dynamic cooperation between Georgia and Thailand across all priority areas.
