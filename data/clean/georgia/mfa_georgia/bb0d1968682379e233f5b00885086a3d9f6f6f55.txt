14 September 2017 14.09.2017, Chengdu, China – “This is the great victory of our country, which highlights the important role Georgia plays in the development of world tourism. This is indeed a historical event” – Zurab Pololikashvili said after his candidacy to UNWTO Secretary General was approved by the General Assembly. Emphasizing the importance of the unity among member states of the UNWTO, Zurab Pololikashvili thanked the current Secretary-General of the Organization Taleb Rifai and the Member States for their support and trust in him. He also expressed his thanks to Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili and Vice Premier, Minister of Foreign Affairs Mikheil Janelidze: “I personally want to thank our Prime Minister, Giorgi Kvirikashvili. The credit for this great victory goes to him and the Minister of Foreign Affairs, as well as to many other people involved in this process. The achievement of this goal would be hardly conceivable without their support and unity” – the newly-elected Secretary General said. This is the first time ever that a Georgian candidate has been appointed to such a high-ranking post at the international organization. Zurab Pololikashvili was elected by the UNWTO General Assembly within its 22nd session taking place in Chengdu, China, from September 12 to September 15. Documents Similar News
