14 November 2022 Georgia is hosting the NATO assessment team, on 14 and 15 November 2022. The purpose of the visit is to assess the progress in the implementation of the 14th Annual National Programme. At the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, the NATO delegation meets the representatives of the state agencies involved in the implementation of the Annual National Programme. The Annual National Programme is one of the main mechanisms of Georgia's integration into NATO, through which the country presents the progress of its reforms to the Alliance. The document covers political and economic issues, including the protection of human rights, strengthening of oversight by the legislature, ongoing reforms in the field of justice and courts, and the country's foreign policy priorities. The Annual National Programme outlines issues related to enhancement of the country's defence capabilities and the existing security environment, as well as definite opportunities for strengthening cooperation with NATO.
