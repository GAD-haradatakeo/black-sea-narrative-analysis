8 October 2017 8.10.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze together with the Tbilisi Mayor Davit Narmania took the Foreign Ministers of Azerbaijan, Ukraine and Moldova Elmar Mammadyarov, Pavlo Klimkin and Andrei Galbur to renewed Agmashenebeli Avenue. The GUAM representatives took a sightseeing tour of the avenue attending the exhibitions and other activities organized as part of the Tbilisoba celebrations.
