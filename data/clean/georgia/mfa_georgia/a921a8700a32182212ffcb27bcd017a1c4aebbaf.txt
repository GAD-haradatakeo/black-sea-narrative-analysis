7 August 2022 On 6-7 August 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, and the Charge d'Affaires of Georgia to the Federative Republic of Brazil, Nikoloz Sakhvadze, participated in the events dedicated to the inauguration of the newly elected President of the Republic of Colombia, Gustavo Francisco Petro Urrego. On 6 August, they held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Colombia, Alvaro Leyva Duran. At the meeting, the sides discussed current issues of bilateral relations and future prospects of cooperation in many fields, including economy, trade, culture and education. It was highlighted that, despite the geographical distance, Georgia and Colombia have for many years enjoyed strong partnership based on common values. Lasha Darsalia thanked Alvaro Leyva Duran for his support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the UN Resolution "On the Status of Internally Displaced Persons and Refugees from Abkhazia, Georgia and Tskhinvali Region/South Ossetia, Georgia".
