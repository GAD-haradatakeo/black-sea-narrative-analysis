31 May 2022 On 27 May 2022, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Khatuna Totladze met with the Director of the Consular Affairs and Visa Policy Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, Dirk Jan Newenhus. The meeting was also attended by the Director of the Consular Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Tabatadze, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia Maaike van Koldam, Officer of the Consular Affairs and Visa Policy Department, Medellin Mullenkamp and the Head of the Embassy’s Operations Management and Consular Affairs Department. Carolyn Deikstra. The sides discussed issues of consular cooperation between Georgia and the Kingdom of the Netherlands. Particular emphasis was placed on fruitful cooperation between the two countries in terms of assisting their citizens during the pandemic period. The sides shared their experience and best practices in protecting the rights and interests of their own citizens and those holding dual citizenship. The sides exchanged information on measures taken to assist internally displaced Ukrainian citizens as a result of hostilities in Ukraine. At the end of the meeting, the sides agreed to continue close cooperation on consular issues. Documents Similar News
