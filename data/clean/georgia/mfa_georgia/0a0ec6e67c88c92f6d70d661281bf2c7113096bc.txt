1 May 2025 On 30 April 2025, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Kingdom of Spain, Aleksandre Chkuaseli, presented his Credence to His Majesty King Felipe VI of Spain. The official credential presentation ceremony was held at the Royal Palace of Spain. Following the ceremony, an audience took place between the Ambassador of Georgia and His Majesty the King, during which a comprehensive exchange of views occurred. Both sides expressed their mutual readiness to further strengthen the friendly relations and enhance cooperation across all areas between Georgia and Spain. His Majesty the King wished the Ambassador success in the fulfillment of his diplomatic mission.
