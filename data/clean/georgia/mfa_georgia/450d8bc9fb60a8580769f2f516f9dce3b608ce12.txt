6 October 2017 06.10.2017, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Georgian experts specializing in international issues and media representatives summarising the activities carried out by the ministry over the past three months. The journalists and experts received updates about the important steps taken during the reporting period towards deepening bilateral relations with the partner countries, as well as regarding the positioning of Georgia in international formats. Mikheil Janelidze thanked experts and media representatives for their deep interest in Georgia’s foreign policy course. “Our dialogue with media is regular and we openly answer any question that the civil society may have to us. This format of meetings is very valuable to us and we are determined to keep up this tradition” – Mikheil Janelidze said. Documents Similar News
