18 May 2018 18.05.2018, Helsingør - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with his Danish counterpart, Foreign Minister, Anders Samuelsen within the framework of the 128th Ministerial Meeting of the Council of Europe hosted by Denmark. The Ministers spoke about the successful bilateral co-operation between the two countries with special focus on the support Denmark renders to Georgia in the process of European and Euro-Atlantic integration, including financial assistance allocated by Denmark to support Georgia’s and Ukraine’s European integration, as well as democratic reforms and economic development. Talking points also included the upcoming summit of NATO. The sides expressed the hope that Georgia’s progress on the NATO integration path will be duly assessed. The Minister paid special attention to the potential of developing bilateral economic relations between Georgia and Denmark. Highlighting Georgia’s tourism opportunities, the Danish Foreign Minister congratulated Mikheil Janelidze on the fact that Georgia’s ski resorts of Bakuriani and Gudauri will host the 2023 Freestyle Ski and Snowboard World Championships in 2023.
