29 February 2024 On 29 February, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with Lesley Bearman Lahm, Country Director for Georgia of the Asian Development Bank and representatives of the Regional Cooperation and Integration Department of the Asian Development Bank. During the meeting, ongoing projects in Georgia's energy and transportation sectors were reviewed, with a focus on enhancing the country's transit capabilities. The importance of Georgia as a regional transport hub and a reliable transit ally was underscored. Lesley Lahm updated the deputy minister on the advancement of several projects funded by the bank and the participation of Georgian agencies in them. Additionally, Lahm highlighted the ADB's decision to proceed with a new economic initiative for both Georgia and Armenia.
