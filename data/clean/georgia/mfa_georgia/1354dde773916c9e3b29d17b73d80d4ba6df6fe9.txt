13 November 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held an online meeting with the heads of diplomatic missions of Georgia to EU member states. The main issue of the meeting was about the enlargement policy of the European Commission. Reference was made to the report of 8 November in which the European Commission recommends to the European Council to grant Georgia a candidate status. The minister congratulated the ambassadors on this important decision for the country and noted the special role of the Georgian diplomatic missions and the foreign ministry in this process.
