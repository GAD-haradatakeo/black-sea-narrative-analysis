12 April 2022 On 12 April 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the IOM Georgia Chief of Mission, Sanja Celebic Lukovac. The sides discussed issues related to IOM’s current activities in Georgia and the priority areas for future cooperation between the organization and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The First Deputy Minister thanked the IOM Georgia Chief of Mission for the Organization’s fruitful cooperation with the country.
