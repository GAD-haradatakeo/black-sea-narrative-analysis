7 September 2023 On 7 September 2023, the newly appointed ambassador of the Kingdom of Sweden to Georgia, Anna Lieberg, presented copies of credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia. At the bilateral meeting held after the ceremony, the sides welcomed the close friendly ties between the countries and fruitful cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Discussions focused on the importance of intensively continuing the political dialogue and, for this purpose, the necessity of mutual exchange of high-level visits. Teimuraz Janjalia thanked the Swedish side for its unwavering support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and for Sweden's active involvement in the EU monitoring mission. In addition, the deputy minister gratefully noted the long-term support of the Swedish side in the process of the European integration of Georgia and the successful implementation of the reforms on the European agenda. for her part, the Ambassador of Sweden confirmed the readiness of intensive cooperation with the Georgian side and emphasized the importance of deepening political relations and fully utilizing trade and economic potential. At the end of the meeting, the deputy minister once again congratulated the Swedish diplomat on her appointment to the position of ambassador and wished her success in her future work.
