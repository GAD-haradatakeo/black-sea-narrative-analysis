18 March 2019 18.03.2019 – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs expresses its deep sorrow over the death of tens of people and the injury of hundreds of others as a result of floods that hitIndonesia'seastern province ofPapua. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs conveys its condolences to the government of the Republic of Indonesia and the families of disaster victims, wishing speedy recovery to those who have been affected by the disaster.
