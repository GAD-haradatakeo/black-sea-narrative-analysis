21 May 2024 On 21 May 2024, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Vladimir Konstantinidi participated in the celebration of Israel's Independence Day. In his address, Konstantinidi spoke about the centuries-old friendship and cooperation between Georgia and Israel. He emphasized that the two countries share common values and interests, which form the foundation of their partnership. He also highlighted the critical importance of peace and stability in the Middle East and globally. The deputy minister reviewed the existing cooperation between Georgia and Israel across various fields, focusing on joint projects implemented in recent years. As part of the event commemorating Israel's 76th Independence Day, the "Israeli Olive Garden" was inaugurated in Tbilisi.
