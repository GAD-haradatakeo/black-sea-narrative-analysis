1 July 2020 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia held a meeting with the EU Special Representative forthe South CaucasusandtheCrisis in Georgia,Toivo Klaar. The sides spoke about the increasing number of provocations by Russia and the security and humanitarian situation in the occupied territories, the intensification of the so-called illegal borderization process, Russia’s increased use of hybrid war instruments and disinformation campaigns against Georgia. Discussions also focused on the importance of effectively using the Geneva International Discussions. Special mention was taken of the need to immediately resume the functioning of the Gali and Ergneti Incident Prevention and Response mechanisms. The sides also underlined the necessity of complying with the EU-brokered ceasefire agreement of 12 August 2008.
