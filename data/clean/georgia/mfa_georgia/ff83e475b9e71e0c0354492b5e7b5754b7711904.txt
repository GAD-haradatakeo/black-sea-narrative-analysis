31 December 2024 Dear Compatriots, On the occasion of the New Year and Christmas, we extend our warmest wishes for peace and prosperity to your families, as well as to our homeland! We deeply appreciate the efforts of every Georgian abroad who contributes to preserving our national and cultural identity and promoting Georgian culture and traditions. The Georgian diaspora’s dedication and unwavering spirit are invaluable, driving its commitment to harness its diverse potential—knowledge, expertise, and cultural and economic connections—for the well-being and progress of Georgia. The bonds between the diaspora and the state continue to strengthen, fostering closer and mutually beneficial cooperation. The Government of Georgia remains committed to policies that support diaspora organizations, address shared challenges, and encourage active diaspora engagement in the country’s development. In recent years, our ministry has significantly expanded successful programs and initiatives, which have garnered growing interest from our compatriots. Increased resources have positively impacted Georgian Sunday schools, folklore ensembles, and diaspora organizations abroad, now numbering over 300. Our efforts to deepen the diaspora's involvement in national development continue with renewed vigor. We are refining existing mechanisms, enhancing partnerships with various institutions and international organizations, and drawing on the best practices of leading countries to adapt successful models to our context. Georgia actively continues to promote its diaspora policies and experiences on international platforms, including high-level meetings and conferences in collaboration with international organizations focused on migration and development. By joining the "Dublin Declaration," Georgia has embraced global approaches to diaspora engagement, committing to creating for compatriots living abroad the conditions that align with international standards. With support from the European Union and in partnership with the International Centre for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD), a comprehensive package of amendments to Georgia’s Law on "Compatriots Living Abroad and Diaspora Organizations" has been developed. This initiative involves leading global experts specializing in migration and diaspora studies. Achieving EU candidate status holds immense significance for Georgia. The country remains unwavering in its commitment to European integration, with the government fulfilling its obligations. Together with the Georgian diaspora, Georgia will take every practical step toward EU membership, guided by principles of peace, dignity, and shared prosperity. The united efforts of the state and the diaspora will play a pivotal role in advancing our common national goals and interests. Once again, we extend our warmest wishes for a joyous Christmas and a prosperous New Year. May 2025 bring progress and success to you and your families! Documents Similar News
