26 May 2018 26.05.2018, Tbilisi- The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participated in the Crynica International Economic Forum in Tbilisi held on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the first democratic republic of Georgia. The Krynica Economic Forum in Tbilisi was opened by the Presidents of Georgia, Poland and Slovakia, together with the Prime Minister of Georgia. The forum brought together representatives of the Georgian business sector and up to 100 members of the Slovak and Polish business delegations. Generally held in Poland, the Krynica Economic Forum is one of the most famous economic forums in Eastern Europe.The Crynica International Economic Forum in Tbilisi is conducted at the initiative and under the aegis of the President of Georgia.
