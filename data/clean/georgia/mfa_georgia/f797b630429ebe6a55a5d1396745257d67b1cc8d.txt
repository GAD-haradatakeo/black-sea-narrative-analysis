4 April 2018 4.04.2018, Washington - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze is paying a four-day visit to Washington from 3 to 6 April. During the visit, Minister Janelidze will hold meetings at the US Department of State and the Office of the US Trade Representative. He will also exchange views with leading experts of scientific research centers working on Georgian issues. The Foreign Minister is due to meet with the Assistant Secretary of State for European and Eurasian Affairs, Wess Mitchell and the Deputy Trade Representative for Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and Industrial Competitiveness, Jeffrey Gerrish.
