18 May 2017 On 17 May 2017, Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister David Dondua met with representatives of the non-governmental organizations and expert communities dealing with conflict issues The Deputy Minister shared with representatives of the civil society information on the Geneva International Discussions and the Georgian Government’s steps towards resolution of the conflicts. For their part, experts brought to the attention of the representatives of the Ministry their visions of the ongoing developments in the regions, as well as of the peace processes. On closing, David Dondua highlighted the importance of consolidated efforts of the Georgian Government and civil society in peaceful resolution of the conflictand the need of regular meetings with representatives of the non-governmental organizations and expert communities dealing with conflict issues.
