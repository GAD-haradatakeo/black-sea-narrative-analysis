20 May 2019 20.05.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with foreign diplomats accredited in Georgia. Zalkaliani spoke about the important issues on the agenda of multilateral co-operation. Conversations included Georgia’s goals and initiatives within the framework of international organizations. The Georgian Foreign Minister drew the foreign diplomats’ attention to the humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied territories.
