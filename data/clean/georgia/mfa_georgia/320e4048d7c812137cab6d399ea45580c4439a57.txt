25 October 2018 19.10.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia attended the reception dedicated to Turkmenistan’s Independence Day. Speaking before the attending audience, Lasha Darsalia congratulated the Turkmen people on this anniversary date. He spoke about the possibilities of deepening relations between the two countries in the multilateral format, including for the development of the international transport corridor.
