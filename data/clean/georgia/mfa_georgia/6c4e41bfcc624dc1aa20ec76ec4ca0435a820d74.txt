30 March 2018 30.03.2018, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Ambassador of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland to Georgia, Justin McKenzie Smith. The Ambassador thanked the Foreign Minister for Georgia’s support of Great Britain and its steps in reaction to the use of chemical weapon. Being strategic partners, Great Britain and Georgia consider this fact as a challenge facing common security and highlighting the necessity for the international community to adopt a consolidated position. For his part, the Foreign Minister thanked the Ambassador for the UK’s support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its Euro-Atlantic integration.
