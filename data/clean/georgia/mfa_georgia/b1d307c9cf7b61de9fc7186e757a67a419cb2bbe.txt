12 February 2025 On 12 February 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with Kaori Ishikawa, the UN Women Country Representative in Georgia, and Karin Füg, Senior Adviser on Women, Peace, and Security in the South Caucasus. The discussion focused on Georgia’s successful cooperation with various United Nations institutions, particularly UN Women, and the key priorities of their partnership. Special attention was given to UN Women’s strategic areas of engagement, particularly in the fields of peace and security. The parties reviewed ongoing projects and the regional agenda, welcoming Georgia’s progress in fulfilling its commitments under the action plan. The meeting also included an exchange of information on the security, humanitarian, and human rights situation in Georgia’s Russian-occupied Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions . In this regard, the importance of continued and regular engagement from international organizations, including relevant UN institutions, was underscored. In conclusion, both sides reaffirmed their commitment to close cooperation, particularly in the context of activities marking the 25th anniversary of UN Security Council Resolution 1325.
