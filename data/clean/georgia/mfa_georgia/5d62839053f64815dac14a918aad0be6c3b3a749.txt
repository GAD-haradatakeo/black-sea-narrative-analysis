7 December 2021 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Alexander Khvtisiashvili participates in the Seoul UN Peacekeeping Ministerial taking place virtually, on 7 and 8 December. Hosted by the Republic of Korea, the Ministerial brings together representatives from over 80 countries. The objective is to improve the performance and impact of UN peacekeeping operations. During the event, the Georgian side welcomed the initiative of the Republic of Korea to highlight medical capacity building and technology in peacekeeping as important cross-cutting issues.
