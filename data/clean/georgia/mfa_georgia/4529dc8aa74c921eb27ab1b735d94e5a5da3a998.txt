25 March 2022 On 25 March, the EU-Georgia Association Subcommittee on Employment, Social Policy and Equal Opportunities held its 7th session via video conference, chaired by Davit Bujiashvili, Director of the EU Assistance Coordination and Sectoral Integration Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, and David Ringrose, Head of Division of Connectivity and Digital Transition of the European External Action Service. The EU side positively assessed the steps taken by Georgia in terms of legislative and institutional compatibility with the EU under the Association Agreement, and gave high appraisal to the progress made by Georgia in the field of labour and social affairs over the recent years and the concrete and tangible steps taken in terms of approximation to European standards, including the efforts to strengthen the mandate of the Labour Inspector's Office. The sides also positively assessed the activities of the Employment Support Agency in terms of employment of Georgian citizens both in Georgia and beyond its borders. Emphasis was placed on significant efforts and progress in the field of healthcare and disease control, especially during the Covid-19 pandemic. Georgia's cooperation with the European Center for Disease Prevention and Control (ECDC) was described as exemplary.
