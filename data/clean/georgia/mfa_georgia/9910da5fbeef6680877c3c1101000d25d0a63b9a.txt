17 November 2025 On 17 November 2025, as part of her first official visit to the State of Israel, Georgia’s Minister for Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Israeli Foreign Minister, Gideon Sa’ar. During the meeting, the ministers highlighted the historical and cultural ties between Georgia and Israel and discussed key issues of bilateral relations. Special attention was paid to prospects for deepening trade and economic cooperation between the two countries. The discussion also addressed the regional security environment. The Georgian Foreign Minister welcomed the agreement reached between Israel and Hamas on the first phase of the Gaza peace plan, emphasizing the significant efforts and contributions of the United States and, in particular, President Trump, in promoting peace in the region. She once again reaffirmed Georgia’s support for the peaceful resolution of the conflict.
