7 December 2017 7.12.2017, Vienna - Within the framework of the 24th OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with the Director of the OSCE Office for Democratic Institutions and Human Rights Ingibjörg Gísladóttir. Mikheil Janelidze thanked the ODIHR Director for assigning observers to monitor the nation-wide local elections in Georgia. The Vice Prime Minister reaffirmed Georgia’s support for the expansion of mandate of OSCE’s independent institutions. Documents Similar News
