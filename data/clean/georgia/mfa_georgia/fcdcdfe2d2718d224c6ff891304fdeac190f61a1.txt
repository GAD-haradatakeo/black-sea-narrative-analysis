9 November 2020 On 9 November of 2020, the Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, conducted a meeting with the international organization of migration (IOM) and the head of the mission in Georgia, Sanja Celebic Lukovac. During the meeting, the sides discussed the further agenda and the existing cooperative state between Georgia and IOM. In the above-mentioned context, Sanja Celebic Lukovac talked about problems regarding the Covid-19 in the migrational context, which require an international effort to be solved. The head of the IOM mission also emphasized the successful experience of Georgia in helping thousands of Georgians living abroad, during the pandemic. According to her, said fact gives Georgia a good opportunity to share its experience with other members of UN and be able to share with the countries involved in migration, from a higher standpoint. During the end of the meeting, the Minister thanked the head of the IOM mission, for the years of work done in Georgia and expressed readiness to continue their partnership in terms of all priorities.
