​Statement by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia regarding the sixth anniversary of annexation of Crimea 16 March 2020 The so-called referendum conducted in Crimea six years ago under the pressure of Russian armed forces led to the illegal occupation and annexation of the Crimean Peninsula and the City of Sevastopol by the Russian Federation. These acts were committed in gross violation of the fundamental norms and principles of international law, the UN Charter and the 1975 Helsinki Act, as well as in complete disregard for the Constitution and legislation of Ukraine.
