23 November 2023 On 23 November 2023, the European Parliament adopted an extraordinary resolution on the killing of Tamaz Ginturi and illegal abduction of Levan Dotiashvili by the Russian occupation forces. The European Parliament strongly condemns the killing of Tamaz Ginturi and the illegal detention of Levan Dotiashvili by the occupying forces in the village of Kirbal near the occupation line. The European Parliament strongly condemns Russia's illegal occupation of Georgia's Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions and draws special attention to the difficult security, human rights and humanitarian situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. The Resolution underlines the need of providing the international organizations and the European Union Monitoring Mission (EUMM) with access to the occupied territories. The European Parliament draws special attention to the "Otkhozoria-Tatunashvili List" adopted by the Government of Georgia in 2018 and puts forward the initiative to impose sanctions on the persons responsible for violating the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and for breaching human rights in the occupied regions. The European Parliament calls on the Russian Federation to fully implement the EU-brokered ceasefire agreement of 12 August 2008, and to withdraw the occupation forces from the territory of Georgia. without any precondition.
