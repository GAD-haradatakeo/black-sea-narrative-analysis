By opening the embassy, Denmark is sending another clear signal that our support for Georgia’s European integration is unwavering - Lars Løkke Rasmussen 15 May 2023 On 15 May 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, together with his Danish counterpart, participated in the official opening ceremony of the Danish Embassy to Georgia. Ilia Darchiashvili addressed the audience and talked about the importance of the Danish Foreign Minister’s visit of members of the parliament and business delegations to Georgia and the opening of the embassy.
