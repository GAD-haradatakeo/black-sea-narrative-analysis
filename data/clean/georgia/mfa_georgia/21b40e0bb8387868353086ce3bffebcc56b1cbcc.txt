29 January 2025 On 30 January 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will pay her first official visit to the Republic of Armenia. During the visit, Minister Botchorishvili will hold both bilateral and expanded-format meetings with her Armenian counterpart, Ararat Mirzoyan. Following the discussions, the two foreign ministers will hold a joint press conference. Maka Botchorishvili will take part in the session of the Georgia-Armenia Intergovernmental Commission on Economic Cooperation, co-chaired by the Prime Ministers of both countries.
