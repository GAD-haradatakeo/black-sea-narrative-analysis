3 March 2026 An expert meeting of the Intergovernmental Commission on Economic Cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Armenia was held on 3 March 2026, in Tbilisi, under the co-chairmanship of the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili and the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia, Vahan Kostanyan. The meeting was attended by representatives of the relevant agencies of the two countries. Within the framework of the meeting, the parties discussed and aligned their positions on issues related to sectoral cooperation, the outcomes of which were reflected in the final protocol of the Commission’s session. The fifteenth session of the Intergovernmental Commission on Economic Cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Armenia will be held on 4 March 2026, under the chairmanship of the Prime Ministers of the two countries.
