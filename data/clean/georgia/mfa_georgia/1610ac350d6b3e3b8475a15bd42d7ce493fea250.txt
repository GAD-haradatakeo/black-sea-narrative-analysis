17 February 2019 ​17.02.2019, Munich – Discussions during the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani’s meeting with the European Commissioner for Humanitarian Aid and Crisis Management,Christos Stylianides focused on the successful co-operation between Georgia and the EU in the areas of civil security and natural disasters. The sides positively appraised the agreement signed between the Office of the European Commission and the Georgian Emergency Management Service, which lays good foundations for the further development of co-operation in this field. The sides also discussed the ways to engage Georgia in respective EU Programmes. The sides welcomed the EU group of advisers’ upcoming visit to Georgia, as well as Georgia’s intended participation in the EU Programme “Caucasus 2019” envisaging conduction of joint trainings in the field of crisis management.
