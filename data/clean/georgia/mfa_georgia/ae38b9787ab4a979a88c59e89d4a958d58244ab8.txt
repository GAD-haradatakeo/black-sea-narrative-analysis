24 May 2017 24.05.2017, Tbilisi -​Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili signed the condolence book, which has been opened at the Embassy of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. Signing the condolence book, the Deputy Minister, on behalf of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, expressed his deep concern over the Manchester Arena terrorist act conveying his condolences to the families of the terror victims and to the UK government.
