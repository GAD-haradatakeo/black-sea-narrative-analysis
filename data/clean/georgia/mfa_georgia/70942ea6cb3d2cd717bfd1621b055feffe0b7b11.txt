18 April 2019 18.04.2019, Tbilisi – The newly-appointed Head of the World Health Organization (WHO) Georgia Office Silviu Domentepresented his credentials to the Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani. Following the presentation ceremony, the sides held a meeting where Silviu Domentespoke about the current activities and future plans of the Organization. Discussions focused on co-operation between the Government of Georgia and the WHO. Silviu Domenteexpressed his appreciation of the ongoing programmes in Georgia and the progress the Georgian government has made in the sector of healthcare. David Zalkaliani reaffirmed the Georgian Government’s support for the WHO.
