1 May 2023 From 1 to 3 May 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili will visit the State of Israel. He will hold meetings with the President of the State of Israel, as well as with the high-ranking officials of the country's executive and legislative authorities. Ilia Darchishvili will sign an agreement of cooperation in tourism with the Israeli Government during his visit to the country. A declaration on launching a technical and economic study for a free trade deal between the two countries will also be signed as part of the trip.
