18 August 2021 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Uruguay, Francisco Bustillo Bonaso will pay an official visit to Georgia as part of the regional tour, from 18 to 20 August, 2021. The Uruguayan Foreign Minister will meet with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Garibashvili, the Vice Prime Minister/Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Agriculture and Environment, Levan Davitashvili. During his stay in Tbilisi, Francisco Bustillo Bonaso will take a sightseeing tour of Tbilisi. He will also visit the Georgian National Museum. Documents Similar News
