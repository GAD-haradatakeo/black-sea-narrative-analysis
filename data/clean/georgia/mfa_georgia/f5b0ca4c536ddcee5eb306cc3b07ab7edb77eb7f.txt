12 February 2025 Through joint efforts between Georgian and Malaysian authorities, a streamlined passport control procedure has been introduced for Georgian citizens at Kuala Lumpur International Airport. This system allows Georgian travelers to enter Malaysia without passing through an immigration checkpoint. Prior to arrival in Malaysia, Georgian citizens are required to complete an online application within 72 hours via the following website: https://imigresen-online.imi.gov.my/mdac/main Georgian citizens are eligible for visa-free entry, exit, or transit through Malaysia, provided that their stay does not exceed 30 days.
