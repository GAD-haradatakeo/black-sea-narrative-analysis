19 September 2025 On 19 September 2025, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Zurabashvili, hosted a reception in honor of newly appointed ambassadors. The First Deputy Minister addressed the newly accredited ambassadors of the French Republic, Hungary, the State of Israel, the Portuguese Republic, Montenegro, the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, the Commonwealth of Australia, the Arab Republic of Egypt, Belarus, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, the Republic of South Africa, the Sultanate of Oman, New Zealand, the Kyrgyz Republic, and the Republic of Chile. He extended his best wishes for success in their forthcoming diplomatic missions. In his remarks, Giorgi Zurabashvili spoke about the priorities of Georgia’s foreign policy, as well as the challenges and opportunities facing the country.
