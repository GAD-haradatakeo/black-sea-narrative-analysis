25 April 2020 Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 14,585 citizens of Georgia on the ground. The Government has organized the repatriation 7,786.citizens to Georgia, as of 25 April. Acting at the instruction of the Inter-Agency Coordination Council, Georgia’s diplomatic representations and consular offices abroad continue to assist with the repatriation process of Georgian citizens to Georgia and, where necessary, provide them with accommodation, food and other basic needs. When taking decisions with respect to Georgian citizens intending to return to Georgia in the nearest future, Georgia’s diplomatic representations and consular offices abroad are guided by general criteria and key priorities determined by the Coordination Council. The Georgian MFA website is updated every day to provide the latest information regarding support measures undertaken by the Georgian government with respect to Georgian citizens abroad.
