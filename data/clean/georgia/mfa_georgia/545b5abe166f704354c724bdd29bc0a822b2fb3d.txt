11 October 2023 Within the framework of the visit of the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia to the Czech Republic, on 9 October, a meeting was held with the Director General of the Czech Ministry of Foreign Affairs for European Affairs, Jaroslav Kurfürst. Discussions focused on the progress achieved by Georgia in the European integration process, including on the implementation of 12 recommendations of the European Union.
