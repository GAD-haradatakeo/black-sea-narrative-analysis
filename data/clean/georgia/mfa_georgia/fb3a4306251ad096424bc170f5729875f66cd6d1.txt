22 June 2018 21.06.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani participated in the farewell evening held at the Ethnographic Museum in honour for the outgoing Ambassador of Sweden to Georgia, Martina Quick. David Zalkaliani spoke about the Georgian-Swedish relations whose history goes back 100 hundred years to the period when the first Georgian mission was founded in Stockholm. Paying special attention to the diplomatic efforts aimed at deepening the relations between the two countries David Zalkaliani thanked Sweden for its involvement in the EU Monitoring Mission. According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, Sweden represents the best example of modern democracy and one of the founders of Eastern Partnership – the initiative, which helped Georgia make important steps towards European integration. David Zalkaliani also highlighted the paramount role of SIDA in Georgia’s development.
