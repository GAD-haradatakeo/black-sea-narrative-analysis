18 December 2017 18.12.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze received the Lithuanian delegation led by Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Darius Skusevičius. Discussions focused on the issues relating to the deepening of co-operation with the EU, Georgia’s progress in this regard and the results of the fifth Eastern Partnership summit. The sides also spoke about plans to further develop relations between Georgia and the EU. Mikheil Janelidze thanked the Lithuanian side for special support and contribution to Georgia’s European integration process.
