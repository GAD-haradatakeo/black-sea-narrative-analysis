16 December 2018 16.12.2018, Telavi -“I am happy to participate in the swearing-in ceremony of the new president of Georgia. Georgia and Serbia enjoy very close and friendly relations and have mutual understanding on a number of important issues. Today is a special day for Georgia and I am very proud that a woman has been elected as the president in the history of Georgia, as well as in the history of the entire region. I congratulate the Georgian people on this important fact” - President of the National Assembly of Serbia Maja Gojković said who arrived in Georgia to attend the inauguration ceremony of the President of Georgia. The inauguration ceremony of Salome Zurabishvili has been attended by 150 high level guests from 55 countries in Telavi, Kakheti region. Documents Similar News
