7 April 2022 The Georgian Foreign Minister met with his Turkish counterpart Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu, within the framework of the meeting of the North Atlantic Council (NAC) at the level of Ministers of Foreign Affairs. The Ministers underlined the close friendship and strategic partnership between the two countries and reaffirmed their commitment to actively cooperate for the further development of Georgian-Turkish relations. The sides highlighted Turkey's strong support for Georgia's Euro-Atlantic integration and its sovereignty and territorial integrity. Discussions focused on the crisis in Ukraine and the difficult security environment in the region. The Ministers noted Turkey’s special role in the negotiation process and Ankara's further efforts in this direction. The Ministers expressed their readiness to work on the exchange of high-level visits between the countries and to organize a meeting of the Georgian-Turkish Strategic Council. Emphasis was also placed on the successful trade and economic ties between the two countries and the need to further deepen cooperation in this area.
