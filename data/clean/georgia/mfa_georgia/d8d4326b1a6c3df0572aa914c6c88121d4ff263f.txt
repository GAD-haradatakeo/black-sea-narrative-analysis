19 April 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Armenia to Georgia, Ruben Sadoyan. Discussions focused on issues relating to Georgia-Armenia cooperation, with special focus on the situation in the region. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister highlighted the importance of regional peace and stability. According to him, Georgia is a country keeping up the tradition of friendly and good-neighbourly relations for many centuries. The sides underlined that given the new reality the region is facing, the countries should move to the next stage of cooperation, which will be in line with the international order. The Armenian Ambassador thanked the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister for productive cooperation and expressed the hope that the political and economic cooperation between the two countries, as well as people-to-people contacts will continue for many years to come.
