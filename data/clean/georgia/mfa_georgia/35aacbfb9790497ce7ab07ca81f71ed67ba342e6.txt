18 January 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the DusitD2 Hotel Compound attack committed by the terrorist groupal-Shabab on 15 January 2019, in Nairobi, the capital of Kenya, leaving over 21 people dead and 28 - wounded. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism in all its forms and supports the Kenyan Government’s efforts aimed at strengthening stability and eradicating violent extremism. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the government and people of Kenya, as well as to the families of terror victims. Documents Similar News
