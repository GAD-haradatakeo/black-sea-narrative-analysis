15 September 2021 On 15 September 2021, the Vice Prime Minister/Foreign Minister of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the heads of diplomatic missions of the European Union and its member states accredited to Georgia. The meeting focused on the priority issues on the agenda of Georgia-EU cooperation. The Minister reaffirmed to the Ambassadors of the EU Member States the country’s readiness to continue the effective implementation of the Association Agreement and the course of reforms aimed at bringing the country as close as possible to the EU. The issues of cooperation within the Eastern Partnership and the expectations for the summit scheduled for December were also discussed. Talking points also included upcoming local elections. Zalkaliani welcomed the European Parliament's decision to send to Georgia an observer mission. Discussing the security situation in the region, the sides exchanged views over the issues of international importance.
