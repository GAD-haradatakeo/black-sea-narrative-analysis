23 March 2020 The Georgian diplomatic missions abroad are working around-the-clock to provide maximum possible assistance to our compatriots living abroad. Despite limited resources, our diplomatic missions still manage to deal with the increasing number of requests for assistance. “At the current stage, our embassies are entirely focused on assisting our citizens, including with basic products, we also help them solve shelter-related problems” – the Minister said expressing his gratitude to Georgian diplomats.
