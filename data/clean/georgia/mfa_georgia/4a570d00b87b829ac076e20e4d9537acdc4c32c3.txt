17 March 2020 Up to 80 citizens of Georgia prevented from returning to Georgia from Bulgaria via Turkey due to the outbreak of coronavirus, will be able to come back to their country thanks to the active involvement of the Prime Minister-led Inter-Agency Coordination Council and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The Georgian citizens stuck at Bulgaria’s border with Turkey will depart from Bulgaria in special buses.
