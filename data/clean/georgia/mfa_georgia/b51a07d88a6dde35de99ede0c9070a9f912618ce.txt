8 April 2021 On 8 April 2021, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili held a meetings with the ambassadors/ chargés d'affaires ad interim of Poland, Slovakia, Hungary and the Czech Republic in Georgia. At the meeting with the Visegrád Group representatives, the First Deputy Foreign Minister highlighted the issues of topical importance both in the EU-Georgia and EaP formats, including the ministerial meeting scheduled for 29 April, which will be attended by the Member States of the Visegrád Group and the Eastern Partnership. The Georgian side expressed its gratitude for the Visegrád Group’s firm support of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration processes.
