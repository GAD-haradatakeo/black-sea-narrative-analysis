28 April 2022 On 28 April 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia, Maaike van Koldam. The purpose of the meeting was to discuss the relations between the two countries in bilateral and multilateral formats. The Ambassador congratulated Ilia Darchiashvili on his appointment to the post of Minister and wished him success in his work. Welcoming the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and the Netherlands, the sides discussed the prospects for the development of bilateral cooperation in various fields. Special attention was paid to the importance of continuing high-level meetings, which will add a fresh impetus to the relations between the two countries. The Ambassador of the Netherlands welcomed Georgia's application for EU membership and noted the importance of supporting Georgia's EU integration process. The Ambassador reaffirmed the Netherlands' unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty, territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. She pledged that this support will continue in the future at all levels. Speaking about the difficult security environment in the region, the sides noted the impact of the ongoing war in Ukraine on European and global security.
