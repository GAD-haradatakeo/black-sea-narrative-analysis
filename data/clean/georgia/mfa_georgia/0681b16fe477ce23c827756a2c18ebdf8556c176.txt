16 April 2019 16.04.2019, Bulgaria – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Bulgarian counterpart Ekaterine Zakharieva. The Ministers discussed main areas of bilateral relations, including co-operation within the international organizations. High appraisal was given to co-operation in ensuring security in the Black Sea Region. The sides highlighted Georgia’s achievements on the European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. Special note was taken of the position of Georgia as a leader in the format of Eastern Partnership and the importance of duly marking the tenth anniversary of Eastern Partnership. Conversations included the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. David Zalkaliani thanked his Bulgarian counterpart for Bulgaria’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. The two ministers expressed their readiness to co-operate closely for the further deepening of bilateral co-operation.
