21 June 2023 On 20 June, the European Union and Georgia held the 16th round of their annual Human Rights Dialogue in Tbilisi The sides exchanged views on the current situation in Georgia in terms of human rights protection and the developments of the Georgia-EU dialogue on human rights protection after June 2022 in the direction of promotion and protection of human rights and democracy. The participants exchanged views on the human rights situation in Georgia and on the latest developments on the promotion and protection of democracy and human rights. The Georgian delegation was chaired by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia. The EU delegation was led by Dorota Dlouchy-Suliga, Head of Division in the European External Action Service. The next round of the EU-Georgia dialogue is planned to be held in Brussels, in 2024.
