16 June 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Turkey to Georgia, Fatma Jeren Yazgan. Highlighting the strategic partnership between the two countries, the sides underlined the growing dynamics of the existing relations successfully developing in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Discussions focused on topical issues on the agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Turkey and prospects for its further development. The sides also stressed the need for further deepening sectoral cooperation and the importance of exchanging high-level visits. Talking points included the situation in the region and stressed the importance of peace and stability for the further development of the region. The sides welcomed the 30th anniversary of the re-establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and the Republic of Turkey, which will be celebrated this year. At the end of the meeting, the Minister thanked the Turkish diplomat for her significant contribution to the development of relations between Georgia and the Republic of Turkey and wished her success in her future endeavors.
