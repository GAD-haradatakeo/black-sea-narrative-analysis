5 December 2025 On the sidelines of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Uzbekistan, Bakhtiyor Saidov. During the meeting, the parties discussed various aspects of bilateral and multilateral cooperation, including political, economic and cultural-humanitarian spheres. Attention was also focused on the enhancement of the bilateral legal framework and cooperation within international organizations. The dynamics of high-level visits were noted with appreciation. Particular attention was devoted to strengthening trade and economic ties between the two countries. The discussion also touched on cooperation issues related to the Central Asian region.
