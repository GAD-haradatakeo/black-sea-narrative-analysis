13 July 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of partner relations between the two countries. Special attention was focused on the imporrtnace of high-level visits and regular political dialogue bringing the bilateral relations to a qualitatively new level. The sides also stressed the need to further activate economic cooperation and to restore tourism cooperation. The Ambassador reaffirmed the Netherlands’ firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The sides condemned violent attacks on civil activists and journalists on 5 July. They agreed on the need to protect the highest standards of freedom of expression and assemply, as well as the right to peaceful protest.
