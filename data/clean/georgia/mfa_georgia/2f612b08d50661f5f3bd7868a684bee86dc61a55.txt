25 November 2022 On 25 November 2022, within the framework of his visit to Madrid, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs, European Union and Cooperation of the Kingdom of Spain, José Manuel Albares. The sides discussed the fruitful Georgia-Spain cooperation, which is based on common values ​​and interests. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia thanked his Spanish colleague for his unwavering support for the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Georgia. Considering Spain's upcoming presidency of the Council of the European Union, the sides paid special attention to the issue of Georgia's integration into the European and Euro-Atlantic structures. The conversation focused on Georgia’s advance on the road to European integration, the progress in the implementation of the 12-point plan and the importance of receiving the status of a candidate country. Ilia Darchiashvili emphasized the importance of Spain's firm support for Georgia's integration into the European Union. The ministers also discussed Georgia-NATO cooperation and Georgia's role in and contribution to strengthening international security. The sides exchanged views on cooperation and mutual support between the two countries within the framework of international organizations.
