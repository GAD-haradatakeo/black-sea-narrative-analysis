7 November 2023 At the meeting, the sides positively assessed the positive dynamics of the development of cooperation between the two countries and noted that over the past years, Georgia-Turkmenistan cooperation has strengthened both in bilateral and multilateral formats. Special attention was paid to the fact that the year 2022 marked the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries. Discussions focused on the prospects of strengthening cooperation in such areas as: trade, tourism and transport. In this context, the importance of conducting high-level visits was also noted.
