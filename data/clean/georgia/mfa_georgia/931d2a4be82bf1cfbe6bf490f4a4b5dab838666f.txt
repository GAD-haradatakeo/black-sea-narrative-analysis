24 January 2022 On January 24, 2022 the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Mr. Lasha Darsalia paid a working visit to Ankara, Republic of Turkey. In the scope of the visit L. Darsalia held meeting with the Deputy Foreign Minister of Turkey Mr. Sedat Onal. At the meeting sides discussed current issues on the agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Turkey and the prospects for further enhancing the strategic partnership. The parties underlined with satisfaction successful and fruitful cooperation that exists between the two countries in a number of areas, including political, trade, economic, transport, energy and other spheres. The parties discussed NATO-Georgia cooperation issues, as well as global and regional security challenges. Turkish side once again reiterated its unwavering support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its Euro-Atlantic aspirations.
