5 December 2018 5.12.2018, Brussels – “By participating in the Resolute Support Mission, Georgia makes an important contribution towards ensuring Trans-Atlantic security and it is duly appreciated by our partners” - Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani said when commenting on the meeting of the Resolute Support Operational Partners at the level of Foreign Ministers within the framework of the North Atlantic Council. “Georgia’s contribution to this mission is in fact proportionate to that of NATO Member States. With its troops in Afghanistan, Georgia is the largest non-NATO troop contributor. In the face of global challenges, our soldiers fight shoulder-to-shoulder with NATO partners and make a considerable contribution to the troops in Afghanistan” – the Minister said.
