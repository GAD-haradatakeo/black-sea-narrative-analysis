24 March 2025 On 24 March 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will pay an official visit to Hungary. During the visit, Maka Botchorishvili will hold a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary, Péter Szijjártó. At the invitation of Minister Szijjártó, she will also participate in the Hungarian Ambassadors’ Conference, where she will take part in a panel discussion alongside her Hungarian counterpart. As part of her visit, the Georgian Foreign Minister will deliver a public lecture at the Hungarian Diplomatic Academy, addressing young diplomats on Georgia’s foreign policy priorities.
