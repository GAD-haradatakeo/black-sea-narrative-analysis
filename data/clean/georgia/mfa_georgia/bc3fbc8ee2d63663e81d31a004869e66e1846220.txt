17 March 2020 Amid the rapidly spreading coronavirus across the world, many countries and transport companies impose restrictions on international travelers and nationals of foreign countries. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Georgia’s embassies abroad will regularly update schedules of international flights, including those to Georgia, on their websites and social media. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Georgia’s embassies abroad operate 24-hour hotlines to help their citizens choose most effective routes to return to Georgia.
