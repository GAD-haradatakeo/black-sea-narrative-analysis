24 November 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns the heinous terrorist attack on November 24, 2017 on the mosque inBir al-Abed in the North Sinai province of the Arab Republic of Egypt that resulted in the death of hundreds of people. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism in all its forms and expresses support to the efforts of the Government of Egypt aimed at strengthening security and stability in the country. Wehope that the joint efforts of the international community will help to eradicate violent extremism in the world. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the Egyptian people and the families of those who were killed by terrorists and wishes speedy recovery to those who have been injured in the attack.
