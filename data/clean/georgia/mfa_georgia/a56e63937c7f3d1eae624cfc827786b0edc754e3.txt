4 July 2018 04.07.2018, Berlin – “Germany recognizes Georgia’s important contribution and the progress we have made in terms of integration with NATO” - Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani said when commenting on the Georgian delegation’s visit to Germany. The Minister highly appraised Germany’s role in implementing the instruments and mechanisms that assist Georgia on its path to NATO membership. He paid special attention to the importance of the NATO-Georgia Substantial Package and the Defence Institution Building School where Germany is actively represented. He also highlighted Georgia’s close co-operation with Germany in the Rapid Response Mission in Afghanistan. “Germany’s support for Georgia is clear and we hope that this support will be clearly expressed at the Brussels Summit as well” – David Zalkaliani said.
