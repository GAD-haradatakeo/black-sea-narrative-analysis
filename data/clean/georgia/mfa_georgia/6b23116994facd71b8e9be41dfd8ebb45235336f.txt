14 February 2026 On 14 February 2026, within the framework of the Munich Security Conference, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Argentina, Pablo Quirno. During the meeting, the Ministers discussed current issues pertaining to bilateral and multilateral cooperation and underscored the need to further intensify trade and economic partnership. The cooperation within international organizations was positively assessed. The Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia expressed her gratitude to her Argentine counterpart for Argentina’s support of Georgia-sponsored resolutions within the framework of the United Nations. The parties also exchanged views on ongoing developments in the South Caucasus and Latin America.
