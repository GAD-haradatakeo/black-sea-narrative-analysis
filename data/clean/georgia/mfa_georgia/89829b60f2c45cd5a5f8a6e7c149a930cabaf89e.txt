27 November 2021 On 27 November, with the support of the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD), an updated guide to the Italian migration legislation has been presented to the representatives of the Georgian Diaspora living in Rome, Italy. The guide for the Georgian Diaspora in the Italian Republic was prepared by the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD) in cooperation with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, within the framework of the ENIGMA 2 programme. The similar guides have also been developed for the Georgian diaspora in Spain and Greece. The guide was presented to the audience by the winners of the 2018-2019 and 2020-2021 programmes - "Be the Young Ambassador of your Country": Neno Gabelia and Mariam Bochorishvili. The event was attended by Deputy Foreign Minister, Vladimir Konstantinidi, the Parliament’s Chairman of the Diaspora and Caucasus Committee, Beka Odisharia, Chairman of the Government of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia Ruslan Abashidze and representatives of the ICMPD.
