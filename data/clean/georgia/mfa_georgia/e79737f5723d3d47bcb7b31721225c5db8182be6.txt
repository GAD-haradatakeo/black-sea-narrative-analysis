1 September 2025 On 1 September 2025, Deputy Ministers of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili and Lasha Darsalia, held an introductory meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador of the State of Israel to Georgia, Walid Abu Haya. During the meeting, the sides underscored the historic and close friendship between Georgia and Israel and discussed prospects for further developing the partnership between the two countries. Special attention was devoted to the considerable potential for enhancing bilateral cooperation in the economic sphere. It was noted with satisfaction that relations between Georgia and Israel are developing dynamically in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The importance of further strengthening cooperation between the two countries within the framework of international organizations was also highlighted.
