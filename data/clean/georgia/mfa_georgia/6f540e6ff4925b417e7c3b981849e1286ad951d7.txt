28 June 2022 On 28 June 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, received the Human RightsAmbassador of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, Bahia Tahzib-Lie. The sides welcomed the friendly cooperation between the two countries, both in the bilateral format and within the framework of international organizations. Particular attention was paid to Georgia's European integration process and the Association Agenda. The country’s progress on institutional transformation and democratic standards was also noted. At the same time, the Deputy Minister stressed the Georgian government’s commitment to the irreversibility of the European integration process. The sides focused on the grave humanitarian situation and human rights violations in the occupied territories of Georgia. The sides highlighted the importance of the international community’s response and engagement, to improve the situation. The sides discussed the ongoing military aggression by Russia in Ukraine and the challenges facing the regional security.
