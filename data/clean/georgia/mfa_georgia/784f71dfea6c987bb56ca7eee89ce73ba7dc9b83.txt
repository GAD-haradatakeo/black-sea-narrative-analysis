2 March 2021 An official departure ceremony was held in the Tbilisi International Airport for the President of the European Council, Charles Michel. The President was accompanied by the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani. The head of the Administration of Georgia’s President, Natia Sulava, the Ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Kingdom of Belgium and the Grand Duchy of Luxemburg and head of the mission to the EU, Natali Sabanadze, as well as the EU ambassador to Georgia, Carl Hartzell. Within the framework of his visit to Georgia the Preisdent of the European Council held meetings with the Georgian President, Salome Zourabichvili, the Georgian Prime Minister, Irakli Gharibashvili, and the Speaker of Parliament, Archil Talakvadze.
