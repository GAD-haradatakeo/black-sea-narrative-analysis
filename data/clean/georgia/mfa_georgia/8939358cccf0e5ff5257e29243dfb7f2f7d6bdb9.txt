8 September 2019 8.09.2019, Tbilisi – “All formats, all forums of international organizations are used to the full to ensure that Georgia remains a priority issue. This process is irreversible and we are determined to take further steps in this direction” – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani told journalists after the parliament confirmed the new Prime Minister’s cabinet. According to him, Georgia has made a tangible progress on the foreign policy front and it appears paradoxical that opponents refer to this very field to lead the society into error and to give them the false impression that Georgia has allegedly problems with respect to the foreign policy course. Georgia uses all high-level formats to ensure that the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the illegal activities taking place there are duly assessed by the international community. Georgia, as never before, has made clear its policy aimed at European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Georgia is recongnized as the leader of EaP countries. Georgia is currently the associated member of the EU but it is moving ahead towards full membership. Georgia is also the most successful aspirant country of NATO which has all practical instruments for membership” – the Minister said. Documents Similar News
