26 April 2024 During his visit to the Kingdom of Norway, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with Ine Eriksen Søreide, Chairwoman of the Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs and Defense of the Norwegian Parliament. The discussions focused on the ongoing cooperation between Georgia and Norway, with both sides positively acknowledging the strengthening ties across various sectors. They explored avenues for further deepening these relations and emphasized the significance of enhancing dialogue between their respective legislative bodies. The meeting also highlighted global security challenges as a key topic of concern. Specifically, the parties discussed Georgia's European and Euro-Atlantic integration efforts, taking note of the progress made by the country in this direction.
