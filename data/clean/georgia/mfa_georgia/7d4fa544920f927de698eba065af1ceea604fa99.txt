20 October 2022 On 20 October 2022, within the framework of his working visit to the Republic of Latvia, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia met with the Advisor to the President of Latvia on Foreign Affairs, Solveiga Silkalna. The sides discussed current issues relating to Georgia-Latvia cooperation. Special note was taken of the need to deepen the political dialogue, to intensify high-level bilateral visits and emphasize the importance of Latvia's support for Georgia's foreign priorities. Discussions focused on the Euro-Atlantic integration agenda. The deputy minister spoke about Georgia's expectations regarding the upcoming NATO summit in Vilnius next year and emphasized the importance of Latvia's support in this regard. Special attention was paid to the integration of Georgia into the European Union. Teimuraz Janjalia discussed in detail the progress in Georgia's European integration process and in the implementation of twelve recommendations of the European Union. Reviewing the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia, the sides highlighted the importance of the international partners’ support for Georgia’s foreign policy priorities.
