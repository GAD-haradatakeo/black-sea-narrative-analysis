5 August 2019 05.08.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with Minister of Statefor Europe and the Americas at the Foreign and Commonwealth Office of the United Kingdom, Christopher Pincher. MinisterZalkalianicongratulated Christopher Pincher on his appointment to this important position and wished him success in his future activities. Discussing the successful cooperation between the two countries both in bilateral and multilateral formats, the sides highlighed the importance of the Wardrop Strategic Dialogue and expressed the hope that this platform of cooperation will become even more dynamic after Brexit. The newly appointed Minister of State reaffirmed that the UK will remain Georgia’s important ally and supporter.
