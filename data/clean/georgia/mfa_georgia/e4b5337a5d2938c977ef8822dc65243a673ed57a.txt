16 November 2023 On 16 November 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Denmark, Lars Løkke Rasmussen. The foreign ministers discussed Georgia’s European integration. They focused on the European Commission’s recommendation on granting Georgia the candidate status, in recognition of the work and efforts of the Georgian side. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed his expectation that, based on the European Commission’s recommendation, the European Council will make a decision on granting Georgia a candidate status in December. At the same time, the Minister of Foreign Affairs expressed the hope that Georgia, as always before, will continue to rely on the unwavering support of the Kingdom of Denmark during the next stages of the country&#39;s European integration. Discussions focused on all current issues of cooperation between the two countries. Ilia Darchiashvili emphasized the recent exchange of high-level visits between Georgia and the Kingdom of Denmark. The sides noted with satisfaction the visit of Lars Løkke Rasmussen to Tbilisi and the opening of the embassy of the Kingdom of Denmark, which is another proof of growing cooperation between the two countries and the increasing role of the Kingdom of Denmark in the region. The sides highlighted the importance of Denmark&#39;s support for Georgia&#39;s foreign policy priorities, on the one hand, and on the other, they confirmed that Georgia is a strong partner of the Kingdom of Denmark in the region. The sides noted the importance of regular political dialogue welcomed the signing of memorandum on enhanced political and development cooperation between the foreign ministries of the two countries.
