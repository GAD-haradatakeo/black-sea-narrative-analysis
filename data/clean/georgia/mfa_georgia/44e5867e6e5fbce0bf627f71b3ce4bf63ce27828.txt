17 February 2023 Within the framework of the Munich Security Conference, the Georgian Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with his Icelandic counterpart, Thórdís Kolbrún. The Ministers exchanged information regarding the developments that took place after the meeting between said parties held in New York, both in terms of the context of security, as well as bilateral cooperation.
