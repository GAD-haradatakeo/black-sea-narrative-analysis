20 December 2023 On 20 December 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the non-resident ambassador of India - Nilakshi Saha Sinha. The sides emphasized the traditionally friendly relations between Georgia and the Republic of India and close, fruitful cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Particular attention was paid to the necessity of organizing high level visits between the two countries, as well as the need to activate relations in various directions. The sides highlighted the great potential of trade and economic cooperation between the two countries and the importance of its full realization. The importance of bringing the business communities of the two countries closer to each other was also noted. Special accent was put on the fruitful Georgia-India cooperation in the field of education, as well as the intensive relations in the field of culture. The Minister of Foreign Affairs thanked the Ambassador for India's unwavering support for the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Georgia. The minister wished the ambassador further success during her diplomatic mission in Georgia.
