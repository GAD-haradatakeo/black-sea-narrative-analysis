11 December 2018 The10th annual assembly of the Eastern Partnership Civil Society Forum(EaP CSF)is held in Tbilisi from 10 December to 12 December 2018 that has brought together high-ranking representatives EaP partners, EU Member States and EU Institutions. Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili participated in the high-level panel, along with Armenian Foreign Minister ZohrabMnatsakanyan, Lithuanian Deputy Foreign Minister DariusSkusevičius, Director for Neighbourhood East in DG NEAR in the European CommissionLawrence Meredith, and a member of the Steering Committee of the Eastern Partnership Civil Society Forum, Lasha Tugushi. Speaking at the panel, Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili spoke extensively about the ongoing European integration processes and the important role of the civil society in these processes.
