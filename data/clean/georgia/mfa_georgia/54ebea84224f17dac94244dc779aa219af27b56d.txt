17 September 2018 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the death of tens of people and injury of hundreds of others, and heavy material damage amid the typhoon Mangkhut in the north region of Philippines. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the families of disaster victims, as well as to the Government of the Philippines and wishes speedy recovery to those who were injured by the landslide.
