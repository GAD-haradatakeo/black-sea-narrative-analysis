1 December 2025 On 1 December 2025, the newly appointed Ambassador of the State of Qatar to Georgia, Ali Yousef Abdulrahman Al Mulla, presented copies of his credentials to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. During the meeting, the sides discussed current issues in Qatar-Georgia relations and prospects for further deepening bilateral cooperation. The Georgian side wished Ali Yousef Abdulrahman Al Mulla success in his future work and expressed its readiness to contribute to the further strengthening of bilateral relations.
