5 December 2025 Within the framework of the 32nd OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, Georgia’s Minister for Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan. During the meeting, the parties noted with satisfaction the successful strategic partnership between Georgia and the Republic of Armenia and once again reaffirmed their readiness to further strengthen the existing close cooperation. The parties assessed positively the dynamics of high-level visits between the two countries. In discussing ongoing developments in the region, emphasis was placed on the importance of establishing lasting peace and stability in the South Caucasus, as well as on Georgia’s readiness to contribute to this process.
