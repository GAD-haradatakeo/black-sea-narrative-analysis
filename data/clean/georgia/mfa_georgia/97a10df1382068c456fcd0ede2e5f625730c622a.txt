12 October 2022 On 12 October 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the newly-appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Denmark to Georgia, Anne Toft Sørensen. The Minister congratulated the Danish diplomat on her appointment and wished her success in her work and welcomed the decision of the Kingdom of Denmark to open an embassy in Georgia. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides emphasized the importance of opening the Danish embassy in Georgia, which will contribute to the deepening of relations between the two countries and the full realization of the existing potential. The sides discussed the current agenda of bilateral relations and the exchange of high-level visits. Ilia Darchiashvili provided the Danish diplomat with information about Georgia’s European integration agenda and the progress in the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations. The irreversibility of the reforms implemented by Georgia was also highlighted. The sides exchanged views over the security challenges facing the region in the light of the ongoing war against Ukraine and paid special attention to the importance of increasing international partners’ engagement. Anne Toft Sørensen once again confirmed Denmark’s strong support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, as well as for its European integration.
