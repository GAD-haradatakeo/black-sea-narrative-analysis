9 April 2018 9.04.2018, Tbilisi – Today marks the 29th anniversary of the 9 April tragedy. Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze and other senior officials of the Ministry paid tribute to the memory of heroes who laid down their lives for the freedom and independence of Georgia 29 years ago. They laid a wreath of flowers at the memorial in remembrance of those who died during the demonstration. Mikheil Janelidze said that 9 April is very important in the history of Georgia as this date embodies both huge joy and huge pain, and will always remain as a symbol of fight for the freedom of Georgia. “9 April takes on a particularly meaningful importance this year as we celebrate the 100th anniversary since the foundation of the first democratic republic of Georgia” – the Foreign Minister said.
