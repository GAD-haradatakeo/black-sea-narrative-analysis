11 March 2025 On 10 March 2025, in New York, within the framework of the United Nations Commission on the Status of Women (CSW) session, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Education and Sports of the Principality of Liechtenstein, Dominique Hasler. The parties expressed satisfaction with the friendly relations between the two countries and their successful cooperation within the international organizations. Emphasis was placed on the high-level visits that have taken place recently. During the meeting, it was noted that the two countries have the potential to deepen their trade and economic ties, as well as a mutual interest in enhancing cooperation in the fields of education and science. The parties also discussed the current situation and security environment in the region.
