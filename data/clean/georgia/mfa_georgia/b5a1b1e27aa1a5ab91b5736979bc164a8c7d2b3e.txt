2 October 2022 European and Euro-Atlantic integration of Georgia, development of strategic partnership with friendly states, de-occupation and peace policy were the key issues at one of the working sessions of the Conference of Honorary Consuls, which dealt with foreign policy priorities. Special attention was paid to the importance of honorary consuls’ active involvement in the realization of foreign policy priorities. The participants of the event once again confirmed the country's strong support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. They highlighted the importance of granting European perspective to Georgia and the firm choice of the country to become a worthy member of the European family, as determined by the Constitution of Georgia.
