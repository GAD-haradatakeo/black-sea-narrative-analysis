30 August 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, together with his Ukrainian and Moldovan colleagues, will take part in the informal Gymnich format meeting of the European Union foreign ministers in Czech Republic capital city of Prague, on 30 and 31August, at the invitation of the Chairman of the Council of the European Union. Ilia Darchiashvili will attend the official opening ceremony and will participate in the meeting of the associated trio foreign ministers - Moldova, Georgia and Ukraine with the foreign ministers of the EU Member States.
