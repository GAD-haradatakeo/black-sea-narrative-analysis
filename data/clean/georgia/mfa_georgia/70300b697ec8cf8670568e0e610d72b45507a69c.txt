19 July 2024 Ilia Darchiashvili, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, will visit Hungary, which currently holds the presidency of the European Union. During the visit, Darchiashvili will have a meeting with Hungary's Minister of Foreign Affairs, Peter Szijjártó, both in a private and extended format. Following the meetings, the ministers will sign an agreement between the governments of Georgia and Hungary on the promotion and mutual protection of investments.
