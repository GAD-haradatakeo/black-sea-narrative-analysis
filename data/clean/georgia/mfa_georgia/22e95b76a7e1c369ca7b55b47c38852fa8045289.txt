2 December 2017 1.12.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze spoke before the audience gathered to mark the 10th anniversary of establishment of the German Business Association (DWV). Speaking about the bilateral relations between Georgia and Germany, Mikheil Janelidze took special note of Germany’s trmendous contribution to Georgia’s European integration. He expressed the hope that the process of Georgia’s political and economic integration with the EU will further increase Germany’s interest in Georgia’s investment potential. The Minister also highlighted the impressive character of people-to-people contacts which are further facilitated by the increasing flow of tourists, visa-free travel and direct flights. Underlining the importance of the Georgian-German year, Mikheil Janelidze paid special attention to participation of Georgia as an honorary guest in the international book fair in Frankfurt, in October of 2018.
