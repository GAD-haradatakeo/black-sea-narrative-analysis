22 November 2024 On 22 November of this year, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili and Beka Chkheidze, Director of the Ministry's Department of International Economic Relations, held a meeting with Ding Yuan, General Manager of Air China's Tbilisi office. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili emphasized the historic friendship and growing cooperation between Georgia and China. He welcomed the decision by the Chinese side to launch direct flights between the two countries, highlighting the importance of such flights for tourism development and the strengthening of people-to-people connections. The Air China representative noted that, after thorough research, the company decided to initiate flights on the Tbilisi-Ürümqi route, with plans to establish direct flights between the capitals starting next year.
