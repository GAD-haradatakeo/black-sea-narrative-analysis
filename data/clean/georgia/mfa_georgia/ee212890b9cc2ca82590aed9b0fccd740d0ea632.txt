21 September 2017 20.09.2017, NewYork – US –GUAM Meeting took place on the sidelines of the 72nd session of the UN General Assembly. The meeting was attended by Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze, Foreign Minister Elmar Mammadyarov, U.S. Deputy Assistant Secretary in the Bureau of European and Eurasian Affairs Bridget Brink. The sides welcomed many years of co-operation between the U.S. and Georgia and active support of the United States in this format. The importance of sectoral co-operation was also highlighted. The participants of the meeting took special note of the GUAM’s tremendous potential in terms of developing international transport and transit routes.
