18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held an introductory meeting with the newly appointed non-resident Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of the Congo to Georgia, Yvan Vangu Ngimbi. The Deputy Minister congratulated the Ambassador on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. During the meeting, the parties discussed the current state of relations between the two countries and explored possible avenues for future cooperation. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed his gratitude to the Democratic Republic of the Congo for its support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. Within the framework of the visit, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Democratic Republic of the Congo presented a copy of his Letters of Credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia.
