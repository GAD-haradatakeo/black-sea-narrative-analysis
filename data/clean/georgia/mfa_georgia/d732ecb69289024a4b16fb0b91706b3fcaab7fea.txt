24 October 2025 On 24 October 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Philippe Leclerc, Director of the Regional Bureau for Europe of the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR). The parties reviewed a wide range of issues related to the cooperation between Georgia and UNHCR. The discussion also addressed the activities of the UNHCR in Georgia, including matters pertaining to its engagement in the Russian-occupied region of Abkhazia, Georgia. Special attention was given to the human rights and humanitarian situation in the occupied territories. The Georgian side emphasized the importance of the role and engagement of the UNHCR in the peaceful resolution of the conflict, including in the implementation of Georgia’s reconciliation and engagement policy towards the occupied territories. The significance of the UNHCR’s participation within the framework of the Geneva International Discussions was also underscored. Philippe Leclerc welcomed the steps taken by the Government of Georgia towards the protection of the rights of refugees and internally displaced persons (IDPs). He also expressed particular appreciation for Georgia’s efforts in hosting, assisting, and supporting war-affected Ukrainians residing in the country.
