29 September 2023 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia is on a working visit to the Republic of Poland. On 28 September 2023, he held a meeting with the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Poland, Wojciech Gerwel. Discussions focused on the fulfillment of the European Commission’s 12 recommendations and granting Georgia the candidate status. The sides discussed Georgia-Poland bilateral cooperation in both political and economic areas.
