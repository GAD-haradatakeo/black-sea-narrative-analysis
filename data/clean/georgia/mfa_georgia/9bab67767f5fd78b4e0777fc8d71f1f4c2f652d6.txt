16 December 2018 16.12.2018, Telavi -“I am looking forward to cooperating with the new president of Georgia to further strengthen the bilateral relations between our countries” - President of Armenia Armen Sarkissian said congratulating Salome Zurabishvili on her new position and wishing her a successful presidential term. Highlighting the historically close relations between Georgia and Armenia, Armen Sarkissian emphasized the importance of such co-operation between the two countries. The inauguration ceremony of Salome Zurabishvili has been attended by 150 high level guests from 55 countries in Telavi, Kakheti region. Documents Similar News
