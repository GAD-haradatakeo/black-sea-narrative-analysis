16 November 2022 Within the framework of the two-day visit, the European Commissioner held a meeting with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili, after which they made joint statements for the media. Oliver Varhelyi also held high-level meetings were with the President of Georgia, Salome Zourabichvili and the Chairperson of the Parliament, Shalva Papuashvili.
