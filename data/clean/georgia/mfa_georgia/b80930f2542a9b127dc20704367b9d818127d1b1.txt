7 May 2019 7.05.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a farewell meeting with Ross Wilson,Chargé d'Affaires, ad interim of the United States in Georgia. The sides discussed important issues relating to strategic partnership between the two countries and priorities of future co-operation. The role of Georgia as a key U.S. partner and ally was also highlighted. Special attention was paid to the importance of the U.S. support in Georgia’s EU integration process. David Zalkaliani thanked Ambassador Ross Wilson for his dedicated efforts during his term of office in Georgia and wished him success in his future endeavours. Documents Similar News
