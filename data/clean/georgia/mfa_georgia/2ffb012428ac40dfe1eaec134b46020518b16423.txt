20 November 2018 20.11.2018 – Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the U.S. David Bakradzeparticipated in a conference: The USA – Georgia – Azerbaijan: Global and Regional Perspectives of Trilateral Cooperation at the Daniel Morgan Graduate School of National Security. Within the framework of the first panel - South Caucasus at the Crossroads: Challenges to Regional Stability - Ambassador Bakradze discussed the current challenges of the region. He emphasized Georgia's European and Transatlantic aspirations and spoke about the existing multilateral partnerships in the region and highlighted the prospects of expanding the partnerships in trade and security.
