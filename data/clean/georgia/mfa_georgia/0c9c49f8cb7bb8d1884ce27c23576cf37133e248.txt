​David Zalkaliani has addressed the Annual U.S. Georgia Strategic Partnership Conference 12 June 2019 12.06.2019, Washington – “10 years have passed since the launch of the U.S.-Georgia Charter on Strategic Partnership and the results we have achieved over this period are remarkable. Georgia is a reliable partner of the U.S, and this partnership is based on common values, freedom, democracy, and common vision of the challenges facing the global security” – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani said when speaking at the 3rd Annual U.S. Georgia Strategic Partnership Conference at the U.S. Institute of Peace.
