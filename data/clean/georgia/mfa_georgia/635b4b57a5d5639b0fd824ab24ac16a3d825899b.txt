6 December 2022 On 6 December 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a farewell meeting with the head of the EU Monitoring Mission in Georgia, Ambassador Marek Szczygieł, who is working in Georgia. At the meeting, special attention was paid to the importance and role of the EU Monitoring Mission, as the only international mission operating in Georgia, in preventing the escalation of the situation on the ground. The sides also discussed the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied regions from security, human rights and humanitarian points of view. The minister expressed his gratitude for the EU's support and involvement in the process of peaceful resolution of the Russia-Georgia conflict, thanked the EU Monitoring Mission and Ambassador Marek Szczygieł for his efforts to strengthen peace and security, and wished him success in his future work.
