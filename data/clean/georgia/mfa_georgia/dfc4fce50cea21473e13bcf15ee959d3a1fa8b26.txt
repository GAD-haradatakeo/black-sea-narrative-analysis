20 December 2018 20.12.2018, Tbilisi – Participants of the thematic panel on consular issues held within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference – 2018 discussed plans to launch new services for Georgian compatriots abroad. Special attention was paid to problems faced by Georgian citizens abroad. Future plans were outlined to better uphold the rights and legal interests of such citizens. According to Deputy Foreign Minister Khatuna Totladze, one of the main goals of the Foreign Ministry is to give Georgian citizens better access to the services the government provides abroad and to raise their awareness about such services, as well as about the rights and responsibilities of Georgian citizens living abroad.
