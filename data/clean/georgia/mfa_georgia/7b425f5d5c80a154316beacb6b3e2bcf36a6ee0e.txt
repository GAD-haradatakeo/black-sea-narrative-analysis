17 June 2020 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani spoke over the phone with the Chairman of theCouncil of Europe Committee of Ministers,AlternateMinisterfor Foreign Affairs of Greece,MiltiadisVarvitsiotis. The sides discussed the achievements of the Georgian Presidency during the period from 27.11.19 to 15.05.20 and the priorities of the Greek presidency Emphasizing Georgia’s successful presidency, MiltiadisVarvitsiotis said that Georgia effectively adapted its presidency to the new reality of the global crisis. The Georgian Presidency’s legacy of priorities and the need of their continuance was also highlighted. The Georgian Foreign Minister welcomed the priorities of the Greek Presidency as relevant to the existing challenges and expressed his appreciation to Greece for its plans to host a meeting of the Committee of Ministers on 4 November 2020. The Ministers exchanged information on the pandemic situation in both countries. Zalkaliani updated his colleague on the Georgian Government’s steps to contain the spread of the virus and to consequently lift the restrictions. Special attention was paid to the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories during the pandemic.
