8 July 2019 07.07.2019 – We strongly condemn the expressions used by Rustavi 2 TV Company that have nothing in common with the freedom of speech and represent a provocation pure and simple aimed at increasing tensions in already difficult Georgian-Russian relations. We call on the international community and all international press freedom advocacy organizations to give proper appraisal to the actions of the TV channel and the journalist in question, who grossly violated the international professional norms and standards. This is apparently a targeted action aimed at heightening the perception of instability in the country, against the background of the efforts of the Georgian Government doing its utmost to de-escalate the tensions, in line with the national interests of the country.
