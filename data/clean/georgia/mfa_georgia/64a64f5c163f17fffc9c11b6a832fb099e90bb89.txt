3 December 2025 On 3-5 December 2025, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will participate in the 32nd meeting of the OSCE Ministerial Council in Vienna. The Minister will deliver an address to the participants of the Ministerial Council meeting. Within the framework of the Ministerial Council meeting in Vienna, the Minister for Foreign Affairs will hold bilateral meetings with her counterparts, the OSCE Secretary General, the OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media, and the President of the OSCE Parliamentary Assembly.
