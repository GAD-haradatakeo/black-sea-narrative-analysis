17 August 2018 17.08.2018, Tbilisi – According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, the Government of Georgia has important peace initiatives that will contribute to a dialogue and will provide out citizens living on the opposite side of the occupation line with access to the same benefits that Georgia has received, including from European integration. David Zalkaliani said about this when commenting on the initiative of Sergey Shamba, MP of the so-called parliament of the occupation regime. "The dialogue has no alternative, but this dialogue should not be politicized. We have very important peace initiatives. Our response to barriers and artificial dividing lines is cooperation, including in terms of sharing the benefits of Georgia's European integration. The peace initiative "Step to a Better Future" presented by the Georgian government provides for sharing these peace initiatives with our fellow citizens on the other side of the occupied line - initiatives that are focused on the development of trade, access to education and free movement” – the Minister said.
