22 August 2019 22.08.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkalaini held his first meeting with the Ambassador Designate of the French Republic to Georgia, DiégoColas, who presented to the Minister copies of his credentials. Congratulating the French diplomat on his recent oppointment, David Zalkaliani wished him success in his future activities. The sides discussed the partner relations and fruitful cooperation between the two countries both in the bilateral format and within the framework of international organizations. Special accent was placed on the importance of the Dimitri Amilakhvari Georgian-French Dialogue and on the potential of further deepening economic and sectoral cooperation. Speaking about Georgia’s EU and NATO integration process and the difficult situation in the occupied territories, Zalkaliani expressed his gratitude for the French Republic’s firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
