9 July 2021 On 9 July 2021, the Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with the Ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Iran to Georgia, Akbar Ghasemi Ali Abadi. Discussions focused on topical issues on the agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Iran.
