27 August 2018 27.08. 2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani signed the condolence book, which has been opened at the Embassy of the United States of Amercia to Georgia in connection with the passing away of Amercian Senator John McCain. Expressing his deep sorrow over the death of Senator McCain, David Zalkaliani conveyed his condolences to the bereaved family and the American people. “We pay tribute to the memory of Senator McCain. Everybody knows about his great sense of solidarity and support towards Georgia. He always stood by Georgia in the most difficult times. And he continued to support Georgia in the aftermath of the 2008 war as well, constantly reaffirming his unequivocal support for Georgia’s Euro-Atlantic aspirations and democratic processes in all his public appearances and statements. He was one of the outstanding politicians and his memory will always remain in our hearts” – David Zalkaliani said. American Senator John McCain died on 25 August, at the age of 81.
