26 October 2023 On 25 October, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Vladimir Konstantinidi attended the event dedicated to the 30th anniversary of the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD). In his address, Vladimir Konstantinidi briefly reviewed the events supported by the International Center for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD) in Georgia, emphasized the participation and active involvement of the ICMPD's representative office in Georgia the development of the country's migration policy and relations with the diaspora. The Deputy Minister also spoke about the programs and initiatives that the Ministry of Foreign Affairs has been successfully implementing for many years in cooperation with the International Development Center. Vladimir Konstantinidi thanked the ICMPD officials for many years of fruitful and consistent cooperation. The event dedicated to the 30th anniversary of ICMPD was attended by representatives of the diplomatic corps and international organizations operating in Georgia and the International Center for Migration Policy Development.
