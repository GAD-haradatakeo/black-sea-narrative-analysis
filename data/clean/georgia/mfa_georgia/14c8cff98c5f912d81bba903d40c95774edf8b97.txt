27 April 2022 Within the framework of his visit to Azerbaijan, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Prime Minister of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Ali Asadov. The Minister expressed his satisfaction with the warm reception and meetings held during his visit to Azerbaijan. The sides expressed the hope that the visit will give additional impetus to cooperation between the two countries. During the meeting with the Prime Minister, special attention was paid to trade and economic ties between the two countries. He noted with satisfaction that Azerbaijan is among Georgia's key trade partners and expressed his commitment to further develop these ties. During the meeting with the Prime Minister of Azerbaijan, special attention was paid to the role of Georgia and Azerbaijan in the region in terms of ensuring energy independence and energy security, as well as in promoting the development of alternative transit routes.
