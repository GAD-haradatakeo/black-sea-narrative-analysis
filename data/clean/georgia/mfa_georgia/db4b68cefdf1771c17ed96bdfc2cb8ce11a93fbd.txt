9 August 2018 07.08.2018 Strasbourg -On 7 August, the Permanent Representation of Georgia to the Council of Europe organised anevent dedicated to the 10 years after Russian-Georgian 2008 war. The Permanent Representative of Georgia to the Council of Europe, Ambassador Irakli Giviashvili addressed the guests regarding 2008 August war, theGovernment of Georgia’s initiatives on peaceful resolution of the conflict and thesteps undertaken by the Government on this matter. A special attention was drawn to the international legal aspects of the August war. The speech by the Ambassador was followed by thediscussion. In the framework of the eventTako Robakidze's short video"Creeping Borders" was screened. The event was attended by therepresentatives of thediplomatic missions accredited to the Council of Europe.
