25 March 2022 On 24 March 2022, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani had a telephone conversation with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia, Ararat Mirzoyan. The two Ministers discussed the current agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Armenia. They stressed the importance of high-level visits, including close communication in terms of ensuring peace and sustainable development in the South Caucasus region. The Georgian side reaffirmed its readiness to contribute to ensuring regional peace, including in the framework of the Peaceful Neighborhood Initiative.
