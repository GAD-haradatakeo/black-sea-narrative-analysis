17 October 2021 On 17 and 18 October, Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic, Ivan Korčok will pay an official visit to Georgia. During the visit, Ivan Korčok will hold a meeting with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili, and the Chairman of the Parliament, Kakha Kuchava. The Slovak Minister of Foreign and European Affairs will also meet with his Georgian counterpart, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani. Ivan Korčok will visit the occupation line and get acquainted with the situation on the ground. The Slovak Minister will arrive in Georgia together with the Slovak business delegation. Within the framework of the visit, it is planned to hold a bilateral business forum with the participation of the Ministry of Economy and Sustainable Development and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry.
