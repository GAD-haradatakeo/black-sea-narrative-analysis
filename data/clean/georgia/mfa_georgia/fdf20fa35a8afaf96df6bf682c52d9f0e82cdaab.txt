1 November 2019 From 31 October to 20 November 2019, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia will accept applications from those wishing to participate in the “Young Ambassadors of Georgia” program. The Ministry’s program offers young Georgians living abroad the opportunity, with a one-year mandate, to serve as “Young Ambassadors” and contribute to the promotion of Georgia in their host countries. The main objective of the “Young Ambassadors” initiative is to raise awareness among Georgian and foreign youth about Georgia’s economy, education, culture, sports, youth policy, and history, as well as to expand the network of Georgia’s young foreign friends. This goal will be pursued through the organization of cultural, educational, and sports activities. The program is open to Georgian citizens aged 18–29 residing in: Austria, Azerbaijan, the United States, Belarus, Belgium, Bulgaria, Germany, Denmark, the United Kingdom, Spain, Estonia, Turkey, Japan, Ireland, Israel, Italy, Canada, Cyprus, Korea, Latvia, Lithuania, Malta, Moldova, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Greece, France, Ukraine, Hungary, Finland, Switzerland, Sweden, China, and the Czech Republic. As part of the program, a Young Ambassadors’ Assembly will be held in Georgia, during which the selected ambassadors will present their initiatives and one-year action plans. They will also have the opportunity to meet representatives from the fields of culture, politics, economics, diplomacy, and other sectors. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs’ grant project “Be Your Country’s Young Ambassador” is implemented with the support of the International Centre for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD) within the framework of the EU-funded ENIGMMA 2 project. 👉 For more details about participation and to access the application form, please visit: Young Ambassadors of Georgia Program Competition Application Form For additional inquiries, please contact: Tel.: (+995 32) 2945000 (ext. 3111).
