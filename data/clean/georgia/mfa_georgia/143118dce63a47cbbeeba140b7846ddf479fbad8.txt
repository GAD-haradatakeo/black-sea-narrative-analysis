29 November 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses deep sorrow over the death of theFormer Prime Ministerof Japan, Yasuhiro Nakasone(1982-1987). Yasuhiro Nakasonewas Japan’s outstanding leader and we believe that his contributions to his country will leave an indelible imprint on the memory of the Japanese people. We convey our sincere condolences to the family of Yasuhiro Nakasone, as well as to the government and people of our friend country, Japan. Documents Similar News
