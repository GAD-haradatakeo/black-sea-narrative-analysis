24 March 2023 On 24 March 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan to Georgia (with residence in Baku) Sami Asem Ghosheh. Reviewing the wide range of bilateral relations, the sides emphasized the close partnership and friendly cooperation between the two countries. The Deputy Minister thanked the Jordanian side for the most important decision, according to which ownership over a plot of the holy land on the banks of the Jordan River was transferred to Georgia. The sides emphasized the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries and Georgia’s readiness to host the visit of the King of Jordan to Georgia in 2023.
