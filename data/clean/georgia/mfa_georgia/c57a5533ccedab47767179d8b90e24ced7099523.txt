15 November 2021 A meeting of the Foreign Ministers of the Associated Trio was held in Brussels as part of the Eastern Partnership Foreign Ministerial. The Foreign Ministers of Georgia, Moldova and Ukraine, David Zalkaliani, Dmitro Kuleba and Niku Popescu, discussed the future plans of the Associated Trio and the ways of strengthening ties with the EU. The Ministers discussed the issues of activating various formats of cooperation of the Associated Trio, as well as closer rapprochement with the EU, strengthening political dialogue and closer coordination in the direction of economic and sectoral integration. Talks focused on concrete steps to promote full integration into the EU single market as well as further development of dialogue between the EU and the Associated Trio in priority areas such as transport, energy, digital transformation, green economy, strategic communications, others.
