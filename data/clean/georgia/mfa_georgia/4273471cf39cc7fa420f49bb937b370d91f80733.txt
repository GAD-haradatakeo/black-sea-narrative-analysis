23 October 2018 23.10.2018, Malaysia – Memorandum on Co-operation between the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Georgia and the National ChamberofCommerce and IndustryofMalaysiawas signed within the framework of the visit of the Deputy Foreign Minister, Giorgi Sharvashidze to Malaysia. The Georgian delegation was hosted by the President of the NCCIM,TanSri Datuk Ter Leong Yap. At the meeting preceding the official ceremony, the sides spoke about the possibilities of deepening co-operation between the chambers of commerce and industry, as well as prospects for exchanging visits and establishing contacts between the business communities of the two countries. The sides expressed the hope that the Memorandum will facilitate the further development of trade-economic relations between the two countries. Within the framework of the visit,the Deputy Foreign Minister made a detailed presentation on Georgia’s investment potential highlighting Georgia’s interest in expanding co-operation in the areas of energy, tourism and production, as well as in sharing experience in the area of economic reforms. The meeting, which was attended by representatives of the private sector and the media was followed by a joint press conference by Giorgi Sharvashidze and TanSri Datuk Ter Leong Yap.
