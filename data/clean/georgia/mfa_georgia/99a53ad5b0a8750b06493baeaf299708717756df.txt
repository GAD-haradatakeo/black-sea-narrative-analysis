16 September 2024 On 16 September, Georgia's Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with members of Qatar's Parliamentary Friendship Group visiting Georgia. Both parties expressed satisfaction with the steady development of relations between the two nations, highlighting progress in both bilateral and multilateral contexts. They emphasized the importance of recent high-level visits and expressed hope for even more robust cooperation moving forward. Strengthening ties in trade, economy, and tourism was also a key point of discussion.
