31 October 2025 On 31 October 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, inaugurated the Georgian Pavilion at the Silk Road Samarkand complex in the city of Samarkand, within the framework of the 43rd session of the UNESCO General Conference. The opening ceremony was attended by the Director-General of UNESCO, Audrey Azoulay. The exhibition is dedicated to Georgia’s intangible cultural heritage - specifically, the ancient tradition of qvevri wine-making and Georgian polyphonic singing - and has been organized in cooperation with the Ministry of Culture and the National Wine Agency of Georgia. The display features replicas of unique artifacts related to Georgian wine culture.
