5 March 2024 On 5 March 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held his first meeting with the Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the Italian Republic to Georgia, Massimiliano D'antuono. During the meeting, the friendly ties established between Georgia and Italy and fruitful cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats were emphasized. It was noted that great importance is attached to the high-level dialogue established in April 2023, which covers the main aspects of bilateral relations between Georgia and Italy. The sides expressed their satisfaction with the active dynamics of bilateral economic relations and emphasized the importance of using the existing potential. The Italian ambassador highly appreciated the progress made by Georgia on the road to European integration and welcomed the historic decision of the European Council to grant Georgia candidate status. Lasha Darsalia thanked the Italian diplomat for his country's support for Georgia's accession to the European and Euro-Atlantic structures. During the meeting, the current situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and the security challenges in the region were discussed. Massimiliano D'antuono confirmed Italy's firm and unconditional support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
