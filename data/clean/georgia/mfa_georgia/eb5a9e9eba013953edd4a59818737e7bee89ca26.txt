26 June 2018 26.06.2018, Tbilisi -​ The Head of Delegation of the International Committee of theRed Cross(ICRC)inGeorgia, BiljanaMilosevic, on her completion of office in Georgia, visited the Georgian Foreign Ministry to meet with Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani. The next Head of the ICRC Delegation in Georgia Severin Shappaz also attended the meeting. The sides highlighted the importance of the ICRC activities in Georgia’s occupied regions and the ongoing ICRC projects in the country. Special mention was taken of the effective operation of coordination mechanisms for identification of missing persons. David Zalkaliani thanked Biljana Milosevic for her activity in Georgia and reaffirmed Georgia’s readiness to comtinue co-operation with the organization. Documents Similar News
