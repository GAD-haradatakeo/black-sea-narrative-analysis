13 December 2018 Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Islamic Republic of Iran Seyed Javad Ghavamshahidi. The sides discussed the agenda of bilateral relations between Georgia and Iran, including consular issues and problems arising out of border-crossing. The sides exchanged information on the aforesaid issues outlining plans for future co-operation. The sides agreed to further contribute to the coordination of consular issues. Documents Similar News
