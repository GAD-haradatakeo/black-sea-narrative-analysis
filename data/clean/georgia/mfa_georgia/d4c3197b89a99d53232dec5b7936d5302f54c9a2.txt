3 December 2021 During the event co-sponsored by Georgia, Lasha Darsalia expressed Georgia's unwavering support forUkraine againstRussia's aggression. Georgia, as one of the founders of the Crimea Platform, together with the international community, condemns the persistent and systematic violations of human rights and fundamental freedoms in Crimea -the Ukrainian territory temporarily occupied by Russia. The First Deputy Minister spoke in detail about the ongoing and aggressive actions of Russia against Georgia and Ukraine and the need for Russia to fulfill its obligations. In his speech, Lasha Darsalia also emphasized the important role of the OSCE in reducing tensions on the ground through the OSCE Special Monitoring Mission. The First Deputy Minister expressed Georgia's full support for Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders, including territorial waters, and called on Russia, as an occupying State, to fulfill its international obligations and end its occupation of Crimea.
