26 February 2019 26.02.2019, Geneva – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with the United Nations High Commissioner for Human Rights, Michelle Bachelet. The sides gave a high appraisal to co-operation between Georgia and the Commissioner’s Office. Zalkaliani highlighted the important role of the Office of the UN High Commissioner for Human Rights in assessing the difficult human rights and humanitarian situation in Georgia’s occupied regions. Special note was taken of the High Commissioner’s reports on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, which fully reflect the gravity of human rights violations on the ground. Special attention was paid to the need to give the international human rights monitoring mechanisms an access to the occupied regions and to deliver justice on the murder cases of Archil Tatunashvili, Giga Otkhozoria and Davit Basharuli. The sides welcomed the participation of representatives of the Commissioner’s Office in the Geneva International Discussions. It was highlighted that the peaceful resolution of the conflicts is only possible through the active involvement of the international community.
