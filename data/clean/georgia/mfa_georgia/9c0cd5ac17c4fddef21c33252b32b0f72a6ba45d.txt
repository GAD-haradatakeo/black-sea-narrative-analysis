12 October 2023 The newly appointed ambassador of the United States of America to Georgia, Robin Dunnigan, presented copies of her credentials to the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili. Following the presentation ceremony, the sides held a meeting to discuss current issues of cooperation between Georgia and the U.S. in bilateral and multilateral formats. Special accent was put on the need to deepen cooperation in all priority areas, including in the four directions under the Charter on Strategic Partnership. Robin Dunnigen once again confirmed the U.S. strong and unwavering support for the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Georgia, as well as for Georgia's foreign policy priorities - European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The American ambassador was informed about the progress towards fulfilling the 12 priorities of the European Commission. The sides expressed the hope that the reforms implemented by Georgia will be recognized. Within the framework of the meeting, discussions focused on the security challenges facing the region.
