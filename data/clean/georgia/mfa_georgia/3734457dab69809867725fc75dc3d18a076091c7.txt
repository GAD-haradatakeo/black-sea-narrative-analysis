2 December 2025 On 2 December 2025, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, participated in an event dedicated to the National Day of the United Arab Emirates. On behalf of the Government and the people of Georgia, the Minister congratulated the friendly state and its people on this significant occasion, extending wishes for peace, success and prosperity. “2025 has been a particularly notable year in our bilateral relations. It is gratifying to observe that relations between our two countries are developing dynamically and deepening in every possible direction, and that our successful political dialogue is being translated into concrete economic projects,” stated Maka Botchorishvili.
