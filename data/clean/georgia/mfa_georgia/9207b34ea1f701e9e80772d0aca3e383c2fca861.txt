12 July 2025 The Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade of the Republic of North Macedonia, Timčo Mucunski. The meeting took place on the sidelines of the Dubrovnik Forum and focused on key aspects of bilateral relations between the two countries, as well as prospects for cooperation within both bilateral and multilateral formats. The two ministers reaffirmed the importance of maintaining regular high-level dialogue. They also exchanged views on the current security environment and challenges facing the region and the broader international community. Additionally, the meeting addressed the European Union’s enlargement policy, with both sides discussing opportunities for cooperation in this area.
