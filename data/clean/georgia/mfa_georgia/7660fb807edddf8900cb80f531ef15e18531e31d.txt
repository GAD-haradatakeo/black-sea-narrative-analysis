11 September 2025 On 11 September 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with Belen Sanz Luque, Regional Director for Europe and Central Asia of UN Women. The meeting focused on the successful cooperation between Georgia and UN Women, as well as its priority areas. Special attention was given to one of the organization’s main activities - peace and security - as well as ongoing regional projects and the related work agenda. The significance of commemorating the 25th anniversary of UN Security Council Resolution 1325 on women, peace, and security was also highlighted. The parties discussed the difficult security, human rights, and humanitarian situation in the Russian-occupied regions of Abkhazia and Tskhinvali and emphasized the importance of continued regular engagement by international organizations, including UN Women, in addressing these challenges.
