18 September 2025 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the newly appointed Ambassador of the Kyrgyz Republic to Georgia, Maksat Mamytkanov (resident in Baku). During the meeting, the parties discussed current issues in bilateral cooperation, with particular attention to deepening collaboration in trade and economic relations, transport, tourism, and reform initiatives. They also addressed cooperation within international organizations and active participation in joint initiatives. The importance of exchanging high-level and top-level visits was underscored. Deputy Minister Khvtisiashvili congratulated the Ambassador on the commencement of his diplomatic mission in Georgia and reaffirmed Georgia’s readiness for active cooperation. Prior to the meeting with the Deputy Minister, the Ambassador of the Kyrgyz Republic presented copies of his letters of credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Protocol Department at the Georgian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Zurab Darchiashvili congratulated the Ambassador on the start of his diplomatic mission and wished him success in his future activities.
