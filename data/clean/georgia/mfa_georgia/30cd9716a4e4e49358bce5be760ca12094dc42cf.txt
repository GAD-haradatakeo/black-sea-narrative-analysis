29 April 2022 The OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media, Theresa Ribeiro paid a visit to Georgia from 27 to 30 April 2022. It needs to be highlighted that this was Theresa Ribeiro's first visit to Georgia in her capacity as OSCE Representative. During the visit, the OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media met with the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia. During the meeting, the sides discussed issues of cooperation and the media environment in Georgia. Lasha Darsalia noted that Georgia highly appreciates the activities and recommendations of the OSCE Representative and his team. The sides also discussed the importance of the South Caucasus media conference. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia stressed that Georgia is ready to continue supporting this event. It should be noted that during the visit, the OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media also held meetings with the Chairman of the Parliament of Georgia, the Prosecutor General, the Public Defender and representatives of the National Communications Commission.
