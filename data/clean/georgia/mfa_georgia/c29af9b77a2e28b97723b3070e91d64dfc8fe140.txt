13 February 2018 13.02.2018, Kuwait - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with Saudi Arabian Foreign Minister Adel Al-Jubeir on the sidelines of the Global Coalition to Defeat ISIS Conference held in Kuwait. The two ministers spoke about co-operation between Georgia and Saudi Arabia. Discussing bilateral relations, the sides highlighted trade-economic co-operation and the exchange of bilateral visits. The positive dynamics of co-operation in torusim and the increasing number of Saudi Arabian visitors to Georgia was also highlighted. Mikheil Janelidze brought to the attention of his Saudi Arabian counterpart Georgia’s economic development trends, transit and investment potential and the government’s efforts towards the development of tourism and infrastructure. Exchanging views over international security, the tw ministers recognized the importance of the international community’s joint efforts in further strengthening stability.
