22 October 2018 23.10.2018, Tbilisi - Archbishop Paul Richard Gallagher, the secretary for relations with States of the Holy See visited Khurvaleti village, which is located at the occupation line between Georgia and its eastern breakaway Tskhinvali region. Gallagher spoke with locals and received information about grave humanitarian situation in and near the occupied territories due to illegal actions of Russia. He also laid a wreath at the memorial of Georgian fallen heroes in Tbilisi. Gallagher has already met with Georgian prime and foreign ministers and visited the historic town of Mtskheta in eastern Georgia. He is due to give a lecture at the Tbilisi State University.
