1 December 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of North Macedonia, Bujar Osmani, within the framework of the OSCE ministerial meeting. Darchiashvili congratulated his colleague on the forthcoming North Macedonian chairmanship of the OSCE in 2023, wished him success in this direction and expressed the hope that the issue of Georgia will remain high on the political agenda of the OSCE chairmanship. He thanked North Macedonia for its support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, which was expressed many times within both bilateral and international forums. At the meeting, the ministers also reviewed issues of bilateral relations and cooperation in international formats. Special attention was paid to relations with North Macedonia in the process of integration of the two countries into the European Union and the importance of sharing experience in this regard. The sides welcomed the intensified dialogue between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and North Macedonia and agreed to continue close coordination.
