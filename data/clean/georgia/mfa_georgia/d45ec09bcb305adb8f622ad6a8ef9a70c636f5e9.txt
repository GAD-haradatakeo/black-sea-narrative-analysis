2 July 2018 02.07.2018, Berlin – According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, the Georgian government actively works with U.S. partners to ensure that the issue of Georgia is kept high on the agenda, including during the meeting between the U.S. and Russian Presidents “We actively work with U.S. partners to ensure that Georgia-related themes are one of the important directions of the high-level agenda. We had active communication at all levels and expect it to be so. We had an unprecedented level of communication with the U.S. in all areas, to which attest the fact that problems relating to Georgia are duly reflected in a series of acts, bills, resolutions, statements of the U.S. The fact that the legislative act makes mention of the term “occupation” indicates clearly that Georgia is high on the political agenda. Despite the current geopolitical situation, persisting challenges and hot spots, we actively work with our partners, including the main partner – the U.S. to ensure that the issue of Georgia is duly considered” – the Minister said.
