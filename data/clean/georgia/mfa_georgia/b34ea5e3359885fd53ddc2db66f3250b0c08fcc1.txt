9 September 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, on behalf of the Government of Georgia, expresses its deep sorrow over the earthquake, the strongest in decades, which hit Mexico on 8 September leaving 61 people dead. The states of Chiapas and Oaxaca located closest to the earthquake's epicenter were hit the hardest. Conveying its condolences to the families of disaster victims, the Government of Georgia expresses its solidarity with the government and people of Mexico. Documents Similar News
