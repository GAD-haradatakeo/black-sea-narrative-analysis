23 October 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili met with the young professionals who were selected to participate in the paid internship program at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, implemented within the framework of the Prime Minister’s initiative. Minister Botchorishvili congratulated the interns on their success in the competitive selection process, introduced them to the structure and key areas of activity of the Ministry, and spoke about the significance of the diplomatic profession. She wished the participants success in their upcoming assignments and expressed confidence that they will make a meaningful contribution to the development of Georgia’s civil service in the future. The state program for paid internships in civil service institutions was established by the decision of the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze. More than five hundred young applicants submitted their candidacies for the competition announced by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
