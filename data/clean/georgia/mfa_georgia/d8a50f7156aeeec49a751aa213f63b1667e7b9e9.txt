9 April 2021 The Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani along with his deputies and other members of the cabinet laid a wreath on the 9 April Memorial. According to Zalkaliani, "9April is a significant date in Georgia’s history – the date, which marks the start of our fight for liberty and independence. This fight for liberty and independence is not yet over as Georgia’s territories remain occupied, but even against such background, we still continue our relentless pursuit of European and Euro-Atlantic integration" - the Minister said. „It is hard to overestimate the contribution of those people who laid down their lives for the cause of freedom. In tribute to their memory, we have to stand as united and consolidated as on 9 April, 32 years ago”- Zalkaliani added.
