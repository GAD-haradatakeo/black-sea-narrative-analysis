19 January 2021 Within the framework of the 113th session of the executive council of the UN World Tourism Organisation the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with the Spanish PM, Pedro Sanchez. The Minister expressed the wish of the Georgian PM to host the head of the Spanish government in Tbilisi at his earliest convenience. David Zalkaliani also expressed gratitude towards the Spanish PM for the ‘solidarity plan’ proposed by Spain, which includes close cooperation between Spain and its partner states in terms of distributing the COVID-19 vaccines. Particular emphasis was also made on the successful cooperation between Georgia and Spain, along with the high levels of support that Georgia receives from Spain in terms of its European and Euro-Atlantic integration and Georgia’s territorial integrity.
