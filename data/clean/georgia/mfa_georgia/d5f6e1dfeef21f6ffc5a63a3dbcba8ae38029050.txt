23 March 2021 Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili received the UK Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Georgia, MarkClayton. The sides discussed issues relating to bilateral relations, the situation in the region and security challenges. Special attention was paid to defence and security cooperation between the two countries. Mark Clayton brought to the attention of Vakhtang Makharoblishvili the United Kingdom’s Integrated Review of Security, Defence, Development and Foreign Policy – “Global Britain in a Competitive Age”, providing the most fundamentally revised approaches since the cold war.
