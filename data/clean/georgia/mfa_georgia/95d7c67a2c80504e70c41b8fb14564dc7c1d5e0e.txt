4 October 2018 04.10.2018, Tbilisi –Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Hungary to Georgia Viktória Horváth. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of partner relations between the two countries. Special mention was taken of Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade Péter Szijjártó’s visit to Georgia in November 2018 to participate in the Defence and Security Conference. Special attention was paid to the successful cooperation in the area of education. David Zalkaliani thanked the Ambassador for the exchange programmes and scholarships offered to Georgian students. The sides positively appraised active people-to-people contacts and increased tourist flows to both countries that is further facilitated by the operation of direct flights between Kutaisi and Budapest. Tbilisi-Budapest direct flights will be launched from this month.
