3 June 2021 Georgia has been elected to the Executive Council of the World Tourism Organisation (UNWTO) for the period 2021-2025, at the 66th meeting of the UNWTO Commission for Europe in Athens, Greece. The Executive Council consists of 35 member states and is the governing body of the UNWTO, which ensures the effective work of the organization and reviews key issues of travel and tourism industry policy on the global agenda. As a member of the Executive Council, Georgia will have the opportunity to be involved in setting and implementing the goals of the organization, as well as in ensuring its efficient and transparent functioning.
