9 July 2021 On 9 July 2021, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a bilateral meeting betweenthe Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia and the Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister, Vasyl Bodnar. Discussions focused on the strategic partnership between Georgia and Ukraine. Positive assessment was given to the increasing dynamics of high-level meetings, including Georgian President Salome Zourabichvili’s visit to Ukraine, Ukrainian Prime Minister Denys Shmyhal’s visit to Georgia andVice Prime Minister/Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani’s visit to Ukraine. Talking points also included political, trade-economic, defence and security developments. Special attention was paid to cooperation between the two countries on European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The sides highlighted the importance of cooperation within the format of Associated Trio. Heavy focus was put on Black Sea security issues, including on strengthening interaction with partners. The Deputy Ministers reaffirmed their support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of the two countries. The sides also exchanged views over the COVID-19 situation in the country.
