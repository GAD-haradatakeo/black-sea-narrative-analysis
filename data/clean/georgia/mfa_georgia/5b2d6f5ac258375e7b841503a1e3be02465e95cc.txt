4 December 2025 On the sidelines of the OSCE Ministerial Council taking place in Vienna, Georgia’s Minister for Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic, Juraj Blanár. The meeting highlighted the close, friendly, and partnership-based relations between Georgia and Slovakia, and emphasized the priority importance of deepening political dialogue and enhancing the dynamics of bilateral visits. The parties reviewed the prospects for enhancing bilateral economic cooperation, as well as the positive agenda for collaboration within multilateral formats. In addition to issues on the bilateral agenda, the ministers also discussed Georgia-EU relations. Maka Botchorishvili expressed her gratitude for Slovakia’s support for Georgia’s European future.
