5 September 2018 5.09.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held his first meeting of Georgia Chief of Mission Sanja Celebic Lukovac International Organization for Migration. The sides discussed the issues relating to ongoing and future co-operation highlighting the importance of involvement of the International Organization for Migration on such areas as labour migration, consular document management and relations with Diasporas. At the end of the meeting the sides agreed to further deepen co-operation. Documents Similar News
