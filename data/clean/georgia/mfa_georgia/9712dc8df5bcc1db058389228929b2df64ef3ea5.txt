10 September 2019 On 10 September 2019, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with his Ukrainian counterpart, MinisterofForeign AffairsVadymPrystaiko. Congratulating VadymPrystaiko on his recent appointment, Zalkaliani wished him success in his future activities. The sides reaffirmed their commitment to further deepening strategic partnership between the two countries, as well as close cooperation towards European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The two ministers expressed their unconditional support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and Ukraine pledging to undertake joint efforts to address common challenges both in bilateral and multilateral formats.
