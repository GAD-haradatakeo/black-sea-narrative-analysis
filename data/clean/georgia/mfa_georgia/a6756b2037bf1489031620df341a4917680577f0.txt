6 November 2018 06.11.2018, Tbilisi - UN ResidentCoordinator/UNDP ResidentRepresentative in Georgia, Louisa Vinton presented copies of her credentials to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani. The sides discussed the issues relating to UN/UNDP activities in Georgia. David Zalkaliani updated the UNDP ResidentRepresentative on the Georgian Government’s recent reforms and provided her with detailed information on the difficult humanitarian and human rights situation in the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. Special attention was paid to the need to create international human rights monitoring mechanisms on the ground, as well as to the importance of active involvement of the international mechanisms, including the UN. In the conflict resolution process. The Georgian Foreign Minister briefed Louisa Vinton on the Georgian Government’s peace initiative “A Step towards a Better Future”. At the end of the meeting, David Zalkaliani reaffirmed the government’s support for the UN Office in Georgia.
