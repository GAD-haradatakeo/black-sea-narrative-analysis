22 May 2020 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani spoke today over the phone with his Azerbaijani counterpart, Elmar Mammadyarov. The parties discussed the situation in both countries in the time of pandemic, the measures taken by the governments to prevent the spread of the virus and thanked each other for mutual assistance, including in facilitating the repatriation of Georgian and Azerbaijani nationals to their countries. The Foreign Ministers of the two countries discussed their bilateral relations based on strategic partnership, including the ongoing economic cooperation, regional transport and energy projects.
