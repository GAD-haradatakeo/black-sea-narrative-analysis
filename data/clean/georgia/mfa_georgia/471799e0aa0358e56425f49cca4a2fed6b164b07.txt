16 May 2018 16.05.2018, Astana - – The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze the foreign minister for the Republic of Kazakhstan Kairat Abdrakhmanov signed the Co-operation Programme between the Foreign Ministries of Georgia and the Republic of Kazakhstan for 2018-2019. The programme envisages promotion of co-operation in various areas of bilateral relations and intensification of co-operation between the Ministries. Within the framewok of his visit to Kazakhstan, Mikheil Janelidze will hold meetings with representatives of the executive and legislative branches of the government, as well as with the President, Prime Minister, Chairmen of the Parliament and the Senate.
