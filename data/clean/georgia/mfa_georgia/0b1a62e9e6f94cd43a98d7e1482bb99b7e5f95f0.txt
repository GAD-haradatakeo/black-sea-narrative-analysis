Lasha Darsalia has paid a working visit to France 10 May 2021 From 5 to 7 May 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia paid a working visit to Paris to hold meetings with French President Emmanuel Macron's Special Envoy for the Architecture of Security and Trust with Russia, the President of the Senate Commission for Foreign Affairs, Defence and Armed Forces and the President of the Foreign Affairs Commission of the National Assembly, high ranking officials of the French Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry for Armed Forces. Lasha Darsalia also met with representatives of the media and expert communities. See All
