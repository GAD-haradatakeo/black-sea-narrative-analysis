7 December 2017 We have looked carefully at the statement made by the President of our strategic partner, the United States of America, regarding Jerusalem. We are closely monitoring the developments as this issue is being widely discussed internationally, including at the United Nations. Georgia supports the efforts of the international community aimed at the peaceful resolution of the Israeli-Palestinian conflict which should be achieved through an agreement based on dialogue.
