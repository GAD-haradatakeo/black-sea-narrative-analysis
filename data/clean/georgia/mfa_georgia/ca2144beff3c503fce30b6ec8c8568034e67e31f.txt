19 May 2022 On 19 and 20 May, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili will participate in the 132nd Ministerial Meeting of the Council of Europe. The Georgian Foreign Minister will address the participants of the session within the framework of the Ministerial. Ilia Darchiashvili will hold meetings with the foreign ministers of the member states of the organization, as well as with the high-ranking officials of the Council of Europe.
