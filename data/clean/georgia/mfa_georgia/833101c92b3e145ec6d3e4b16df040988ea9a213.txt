21 July 2017 21.07.2017, Tbilisi –Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze and Ambassadorial 2017 participants visited the high-tech and multi-functional Joint Operations Center of the Ministry of Internal Affairs. They were hosted by Minister of Internal Affairs Giorgi Mgebrishvili. Visitors got acquainted with work specifics of the center, process of handling traffic movement and detection of administrative violations. MIA Joint Operations Center provides the following services: prevention of crime, quick response, establishment of the violators and detention, detection of administrative offences and foiling of the crime, maintenance of traffic movement, extraction of incontrovertible evidence, introduction of the latest technologies and monitoring its implementation.
