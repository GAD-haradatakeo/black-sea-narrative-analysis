26 November 2025 On 26 November 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with Kemlin Furley, the Representative of the Office of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in Georgia, who is concluding her tenure in the country at the end of the year. During the meeting, the parties reviewed the activities of the UNHCR Office in Georgia. Lasha Darsalia and Kemlin Furley also discussed the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions and the conditions of internally displaced persons. The importance of UNHCR’s engagement in supporting the population affected by conflict was underscored, and both parties expressed their readiness to continue cooperation in this area.
