13 October 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia stresses the utmost significance of holding October 15 Gubernatorial elections in Venezuela in a peaceful, free, transparent, democratic and competitive environment. The upcoming elections are of a particular importance given the ongoing crises in Venezuela. Therefore, it is critical that the people of Venezuela are given all the possibilities to freely exercise their choice. It is of great concern that regional and international observer missions will not be allowed to monitor the elections. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia is concerned that the rights of the people of Venezuela are being neglected and joins international community’s call upon the Government of Venezuela to respect the rule of law, fundamental human rights and freedoms and ensure the freedom of expression.
