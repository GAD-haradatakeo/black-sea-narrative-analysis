3 November 2022 On 3 November 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the State of Qatar to Georgia, Hassan Ahmed Ali Mohammed Al-Mutawa. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides noted with satisfaction that relations between the countries are intensive in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Special attention was paid to the importance of further deepening trade, economic and tourism cooperation. The sides expressed their readiness to further promote the existing relations.
