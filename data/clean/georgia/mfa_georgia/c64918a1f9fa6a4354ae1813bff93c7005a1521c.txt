23 March 2020 “Acting upon instruction of the Prime Minister, we negotiated with the Georgian Airlines a schedule of flights for the next two weeks. During this week, only two flights will be operated: Tbilisi-Berlin-Batumi flight today and Aegean Airlines flight to Tbilisi – tomorrow. Tbilisi-Vienna-Tbilisi flight will be performed on 26 March, Tbilisi-Amsterdam-Tbilisi flight – on 31 March, and Tbilisi-Larnaka-Tbilisi flight – on 10 April. This is the schedule for the next few days. We also work on planning flights for the further periods to bring home our citizens stage-by-stage, gradually. Gradual evacuation is necessary to allow our healthcare system to prepare for the intake of passengers who are subject to 14-day mandatory quarantine procedures ” – David Zalkaliani said at a press briefing following the Inter-Agency Coordination Council.
