4 December 2025 On 4 December 2025, on the margins of the OSCE Ministerial Council meeting, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Tajikistan, Sirojiddin Muhriddin. The parties discussed various aspects of bilateral and multilateral cooperation, including political, economic, and cultural-humanitarian spheres, the enhancement of the bilateral legal framework, and collaboration within international organizations. They noted with satisfaction the positive dynamics in trade between Georgia and the Republic of Tajikistan throughout 2025.
