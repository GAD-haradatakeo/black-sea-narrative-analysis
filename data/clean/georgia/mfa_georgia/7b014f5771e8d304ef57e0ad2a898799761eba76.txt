29 July 2024 On 29 July 2024, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with Omar Barakat Al-Nahar, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan to Georgia. During the meeting, Ambassador Al-Nahar presented copies of his credentials. The discussion covered a broad range of bilateral relations between Georgia and Jordan, as well as the current situation in the Middle East. Emphasis was placed on the importance of further strengthening cooperation in political, economic, humanitarian, and other areas.
