13 March 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the State of Israel to Georgia, Hadas Meitzad. The meeting highlighted the enduring friendship between the Georgian and Israeli peoples, built on mutual respect and multifaceted cooperation. Discussions focused on regional and global security dynamics, with particular emphasis on the importance of fostering peace and stability. The parties also reviewed the strong bilateral ties across various sectors, including tourism, culture, and trade and economic cooperation. Regarding tourism, both sides noted the sector’s positive momentum, emphasizing Israel’s position as one of the leading sources of visitors to Georgia.
