15 March 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey, Hakan Fidan, during the Azerbaijan-Georgia-Turkey tripartite ministerial in Baku. Ilia Darchiashvili and Hakan Fidan highly praised the tripartite cooperation format and the successful ninth ministerial meeting. The ministers emphasized the significance of this format for fostering sustainable development in the region and positively assessed the strategic partnership between Georgia and Turkey. They exchanged information regarding the current bilateral agenda and discussed the regional situation. Both ministers expressed their readiness to maintain the existing close coordination.
