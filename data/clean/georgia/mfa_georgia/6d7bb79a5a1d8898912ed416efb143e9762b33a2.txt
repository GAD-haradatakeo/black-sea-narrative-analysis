5 July 2023 On 5 July 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Justice of Hungary, Judit Varga. At the meeting, the sides discussed the current issues relating to relations between the two countries and prospects for further deepening of cooperation in bilateral and multilateral formats. The sides noted with satisfaction the active dynamics of high-level visits between the two countries. The key focus of discussions was on the process of Georgia's integration into the European Union. Ilia Darchiashvili provided detailed information to the Minister of Justice about the progress on Georgia's implementation of the European Commission’s 12 priorities. The Minister of Foreign Affairs expressed the hope that the efforts made by Georgia will be assessed fairly and based on merit, and Georgia will be granted the candidate status by the end of the year.
