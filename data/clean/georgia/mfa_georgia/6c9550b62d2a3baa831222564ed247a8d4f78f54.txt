8 October 2025 On 7-8 October 2025, a Georgian delegation conducted an official visit to the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Within the framework of the visit, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Saud Al-Sati. The discussion focused on issues of bilateral cooperation, prospects for deepening sectoral collaboration between the two countries, and the organization of the first high-level session of the Georgia–Saudi Arabia Intergovernmental Coordinating Council in Tbilisi. The sides also exchanged views on the potential conclusion of a Free Trade Agreement between Georgia and the Gulf countries, as well as on current regional and international developments and challenges. During the visit, the Georgian delegation also held meetings at the Ministry of Investment of Saudi Arabia (MISA), the General Authority of Foreign Trade (GAFT), and the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC). The discussions centered on ways to enhance bilateral trade, explore Georgia’s investment opportunities, and assess the prospects for concluding a Free Trade Agreement with the Gulf countries.
