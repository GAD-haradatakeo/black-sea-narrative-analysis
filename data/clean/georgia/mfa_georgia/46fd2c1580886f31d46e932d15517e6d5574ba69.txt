6 June 2022 On 6 June 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Sweden to Georgia, Ulrik Tideström. Congratulating the Ambassador on the occasion of the National Day – 6 June, the Minister wished peace and prosperity to the friendly Swedish people. Highlighting the close and friendly relations based on mutual trust and common European values, the sides welcomed the 30th anniversary of the re-establishment of diplomatic relations between the two countries and expressed their readiness to deepen mutually beneficial cooperation in all areas of common interest. The sides emphasized the need for closer political dialogue amid the challenges facing the European security. The Georgian Foreign Minister thanked Sweden, as one of Georgia's largest donors for its many years of support to the country in the implementation of European reforms, as well as for Sweden’s active participation in the EU Monitoring Mission in Georgia. The Minister also stressed the importance of Sweden's support for granting Georgia the status of EU candidate country. The Swedish Ambassador, for his part, reaffirmed Sweden's continued unconditional support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the country's European agenda.
