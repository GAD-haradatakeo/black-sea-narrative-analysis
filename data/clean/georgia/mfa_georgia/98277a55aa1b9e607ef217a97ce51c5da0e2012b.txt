10 October 2023 On 9 October 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held his first meeting with the Ambassador of the Czech Republic to Georgia, Petr Kubernát. The sides highly appreciated the close relations between Georgia and the Czech Republic, which have significantly deepened in various directions over the recent years. Special Attention was focused on the high-level visits exchanged between the two countries at the executive and legislative levels. The sides emphasized Georgia's foreign policy priorities. Petr Kubernát reaffirmed the Czech Republic's firm support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Discussions focused on Georgia's implementation of the European Commission’s 12 recommendations. The minister provided the ambassador with comprehensive information about the steps taken by Georgia in this regard. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed the hope that Georgia’s efforts will be evaluated accordingly and the country will be granted a candidate status.
