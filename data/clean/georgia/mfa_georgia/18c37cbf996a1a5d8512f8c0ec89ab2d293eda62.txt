18 March 2020 18.03.2020 – Six Georgian students prevented from returning to Georgia from the Arab Republic of Egypt due to the outbreak of coronavirus, will be able to come back to their country thanks to the active involvement of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The assistance came after the Georgian students had appealed to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and used the hotline of the Georgian Embassies to Egypt and Armenia. By decision of the Prime Minister-led Inter-Agency Coordination Council, Georgian citizens staying abroad and unable to return to Georgia due to coronavirus-related restrictions in force in various countries, will be assisted by the government of Georgia. Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs are working under special schedules. The Ministry and all embassies are operating a 24-hour hotline.
