17 February 2025 On 17 February 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Sovereign Order of Malta to Georgia, Teodorico Nanni. During the meeting, the parties discussed the existing relations between Georgia and the Sovereign Order of Malta, as well as various areas of cooperation. The Minister of Foreign Affairs expressed gratitude for the significant humanitarian and charitable activities carried out by the Sovereign Order of Malta in Georgia.
