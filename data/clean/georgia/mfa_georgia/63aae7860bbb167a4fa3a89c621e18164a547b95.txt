8 October 2020 On October 9, 2020, at 13:00 (Tbilisi Time), an online Webinar “Study in Austria!”, organized in collaboration with International Centre for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD), will take place on the Facebook page of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. Ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Austria, Ketevan Tsikhelashvili and Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Vladimer Konstantinidi, will participate in the webinar. The webinar will be carried out by Salome Saladze, Georgia’s Young Ambassador to Austria. Throughout the webinar Georgian students (and potential students) will gain detailed information on all topics related to studying in Austria, including procedures and regulations, applications and registrations, issues related to residence, visa, insurance, grants and employment opportunities.
