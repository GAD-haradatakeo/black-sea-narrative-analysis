10 September 2019 10.09.2019 - Ambassador Designate of the Kingdom of Norway to Georgia, HeleneSand Andresen presented copies of her credentials to Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani. Congratulating the Norwegian diplomat on her oppointment, David Zalkaliani wished her success in her future activities. The sides discussed the partner relations and fruitful cooperation between the two countries. The Norwegian Ambassador reaffirmed her commitment to the further strengthening of the potential of Georgia-Norway relations and expressed the hope that the bilateral political, economic and sectoral cooperation between the two countries will further develop. Speaking about Georgia’s EU and NATO integration process, the Minister highlighted the reforms carried out by Georgia that bring the country closer to European standards. Zalkaliani briefed the Ambassador on the difficult situation in the occupied territories. HeleneSand Andresen reaffirmed that Norway as a friend of Georgia unconditionally supports Georgia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity, European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations and democratic transformation.
