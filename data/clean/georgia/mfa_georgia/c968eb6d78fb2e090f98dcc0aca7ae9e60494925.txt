31 July 2024 On 30 July 2024, a memorandum of cooperation was signed in the Independence Hall of the National Library of the Parliament of Georgia. The agreement was formalized by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Vladimir Konstantinidi, and Konstantine Gamsakhurdia, Director of the National Library of the Parliament of Georgia. The memorandum outlines a commitment to collaborate closely on establishing Georgian libraries in diaspora organizations and Sunday schools abroad. Additionally, it includes plans for joint diaspora events, scientific conferences, and meetings. The National Library, in partnership with the Ministry of Education, Science, and Youth of Georgia, will also play a key role in implementing the "Support for Sunday Schools" program of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
