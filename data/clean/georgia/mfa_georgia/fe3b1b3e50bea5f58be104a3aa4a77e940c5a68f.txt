Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Lasha Darsalia has met with the Representative of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees in Georgia 26 November 2025 On 26 November 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with Kemlin Furley, the Representative of the Office of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in Georgia, who is concluding her tenure in the country at the end of the year.
