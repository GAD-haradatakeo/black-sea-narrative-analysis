24 June 2021 The Foreign Ministers of the Associated Trio – David Zalkaliani, Aureliu Ciocoi and Dmytro Kuleba held a meeting in the European Parliament within the framework of their joint visit to Brussels. The Ministers met with the chair of the Foreign Relations Committee, David McAllister, chairs of the Association Parliamentary Committee, Marina Kaljurand, Siegfried Muresan, Vitold Vashikovski, as well as speakers of the European Parliament, Dragos Tudorache, Michael Gahler, and MEPs, Andrius Kubilius and Petras Austrevicius. The MEPs welcomed the establishment of the Associated Trio as well as the actual visit of the three Minister to Brussels. The Ministers discussed the goals of the establishment of the Association Trio and its future activity, which is associated with the tight coordination of the three states in terms of European integration. During the meeting the head of the Georgian Foreign Ministry discussed the issue of human rights violations in Georgia’s occupied regions, as well as the existing security environment, the illegal arrests of Georgian citizens and the significance of active engagement in the process of the international community, and especially the EU, for the purposes of improving the regional security environment and peaceful resolution of the Russo-Georgian conflict.
