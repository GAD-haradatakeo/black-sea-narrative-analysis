19 July 2021 The 17th Batumi International Conference - Enduring Power of Attraction - When the EU Makes Difference has been officially launched.David Zalkaliani, Vice Prime-Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia will open the conference. As the Minister noted Batumi International Conference was conceived as a forum to discuss Georgia’s European integration. From the very beginning representatives of the EU institutions, member states, governments and parliamentarians, scholars and think-tanks gathered to discuss the challenges of European integration, the opportunities that have been presented all the way and new ideas to advance our ever close integration with the European Union. According to the Minister, the conference has been gaining new pace and more distinguished audience over the years, the scope of topics on the agenda has also extended from regional developments, economic opportunities and security challenges of the Black Sea region to EaP perspectives. The Minister focused his attention on the agenda of the Conference, and in particular, on the leaders’ meeting which will be held between the president of the European Council, the Presidents of Georgia, Republic of Moldova and Ukraine. It will be the first joint meeting of the associated partners and the President of the European Council. “I believe that we can see this momentous event as a manifestation of the mutual commitment of the European Union and associated partners” – Zalkaliani said.
