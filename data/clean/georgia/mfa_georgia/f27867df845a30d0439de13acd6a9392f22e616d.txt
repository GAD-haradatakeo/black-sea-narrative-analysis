22 June 2023 On 22 June 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a telephone conversation with the newly appointed Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Bulgaria, Mariya Gabriel. The minister congratulated her Bulgarian colleague on appointment as Minister of Foreign Affairs and wished her success in her future work. The foreign ministries talked about the issues on the bilateral agenda. The long-standing friendly relations between Georgia and Bulgaria and the importance of further deepening the political dialogue were emphasized. Special attention was paid to the European and Euro-Atlantic integration agenda of Georgia. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Bulgaria pledged her support to Georgia in the implementation of the country's foreign policy priorities. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked his Bulgarian colleague for her strong support for the territorial integrity of Georgia and the country's European and Euro-Atlantic integration.
