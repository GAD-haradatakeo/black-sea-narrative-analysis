15 July 2025 On 15 July 2025, a farewell meeting was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia in Tbilisi between Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili and the Ambassador of the Commonwealth of Australia to Georgia, Miles Armitage (with residence in Ankara) on the occasion of the conclusion of the Ambassador’s term. During the meeting, the parties discussed the friendly relations between Georgia and Australia, key developments in the region, Georgia’s role within the regional context, including the country’s transit potential. At the conclusion of the meeting, the Georgian side expressed its gratitude to Ambassador Armitage for the productive cooperation and wished him success in his future endeavors. Documents Similar News
