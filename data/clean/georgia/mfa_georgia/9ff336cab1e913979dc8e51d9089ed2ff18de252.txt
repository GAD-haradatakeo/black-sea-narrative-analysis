26 January 2019 26.01.2019, Spain – According to Zalkaliani, Spain has raised no claim regarding co-operation with Georgia on visa-liberalization issues. Moreover, the Spanish side highly appraises Georgia’s progress in complying with its obligations. “The steps taken by Georgia towards visa liberalization has been positively appraised at the meeting. It is important that Georgia is the leader in terms of fulfilling its readmission commitments. We have joint plans on which we plan to co-operate in the futue. Spain has no claim regarding visa-liberalization, which gives me the hope that we will further deepen our relations and visa-free travel as our key achievement will be maintained because it has not only practical but also political importance on which Georgia places high value” – the Foreign Minister said.
