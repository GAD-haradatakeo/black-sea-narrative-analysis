25 August 2017 ​25.08.2017, Frankfurt, Germany – As part of his visit to Germany, Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with representatives of business communities in Frankfurt. Mikheil Janelidze spoke extensively about Georgia’s liberal economic climate and the investment and business opportunities in the country. The Georgian Foreign Minister brought to the attention of German businessmen the ongoing economic reforms in Georgia and important results already achieved. He highlighted Georgia’s leading position in international ratings The meeting witn the Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze was attended by the President of the Frankfurt society of trade, industry and science, the President of the Politechnical Society, the President of the Frankfurt School of Finance and Management, representatives of the Goethe University, Standard Bank and other financial organizations.
