“Outside the borders of Georgia 52 election districts are open in 38 countries” - Announced David Zalkaliani. 31 October 2020 By the information provided by the minister of foreign affairs, outside the Georgian borders in 38 countries, 52 election districts are open. Furthermore, because of the existing restrictions caused by the pandemic, every country has an existing plan, because of said restrictions, every country has its own set of regulations, along with the essential regulations provided by the country they are in. The minister noted that two countries, namely the Republic of Azerbaijan and the Republic of Greece (Thessaloniki), did not allow Georgia to open the voting districts. According to Zalkaliani, the city of Thessaloniki is a red zone, which remains under severe lockdown policies, which was the fundamental reason for the restriction of opening any voting districts in the area.
