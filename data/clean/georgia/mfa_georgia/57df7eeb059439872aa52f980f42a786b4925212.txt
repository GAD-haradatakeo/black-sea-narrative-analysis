17 November 2020 On Tuesday 17 November, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani attended a virtual ministerial meeting of International Religious Freedom or Belief Alliance Ministers’ Forum (IRFBA), hosted by U.S Secretary of State Mike Pompeo. The event brought together Alliance member-states to plan their collective action for the next year in the religious freedom space. Speaking before the audience of the forum, David Zalkaliani highighted the occupation regimes’ continued vioulation of religious rights and freedoms of ethnic Georgians in the occupied regions of Tskhinvali and Abkhazia. He stressed the the occupation regimes continue to bar international experts’ access to the religious sites on the ground and prevent assessment of the damge and any restoration effort.
