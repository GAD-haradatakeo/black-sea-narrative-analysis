28 June 2023 On June 28, 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, received the Ambassador of the Republic of Estonia, Riina Kaljurand. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides discussed the existing relations between the two countries in bilateral and multilateral formats and directions of their further development. The minister highlighted the reforms implemented by Georgia on the path of European integration, in terms of implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations and the progress achieved in this regard. In this context, special emphasis was put on the importance of the partners' support for Georgia's foreign policy priorities.
