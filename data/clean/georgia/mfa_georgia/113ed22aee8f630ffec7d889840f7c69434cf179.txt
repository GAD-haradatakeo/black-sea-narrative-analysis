“I strongly believe that our shared interests will be fulfilled through materializing the common commitment towards European integration” - Moldovan Foreign Minister 25 May 2018 “Gaining the independence established a beacon for the the Georgian statehood for the ensuing decades and the ideals of freedom and independence that drove the architects of independence 100 years ago resonate with us today as both Republic of Moldova and Georgia continue to struggle for liberty and unhindered independence” – reads the letter of the Minister of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of the Republic of Moldova, Tudor Ulianovschi to the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze, on the occasion of the 100th anniversary since the foundation of the first democratic republic of Georgia.
