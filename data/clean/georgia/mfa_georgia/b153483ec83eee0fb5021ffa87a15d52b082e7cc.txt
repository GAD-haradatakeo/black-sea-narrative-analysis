23 October 2019 23.10.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Li Hui, China’s Special Envoy in Euroasia Region, on the sidelines of the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum. Highlighting the importance of Georgia strategic location and the successful Georgia-China cooperation within the framework of One Belt – One Road initiative, the Minister thanked the Chinese side for high-level participation in the Silk Road Forum. The sides praised dynamic tourism and trade cooperation between the two countries which has particularly increased following the entry into force of the Free Trade Agreement. Special attention was paid to Georgia’s investment climate attracting China as our key investment partner. Zalkaliani emphasized the importance of conducting various Georgia awareness-raising campaigns in China.
