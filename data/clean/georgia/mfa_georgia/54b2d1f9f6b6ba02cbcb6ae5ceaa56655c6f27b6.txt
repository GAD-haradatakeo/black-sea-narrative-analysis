18 February 2022 Within the framework of the Munich Conference, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with his Indian counterpart, Subrahmanyam Jaishankar. The Ministers reaffirmed the close and friendly relations between the two countries clearly evidenced by the first historic visit of the Indian Foreign Minister to Georgia. The Georgian side once again thanked India for transferring to Georgia the sacred relics of Queen Ketevan. Speaking about bilateral relations, the Ministers paid special attention to the prospects for the development of trade and economic relations between the two countries and stressed the need for effective use of the existing potential.
