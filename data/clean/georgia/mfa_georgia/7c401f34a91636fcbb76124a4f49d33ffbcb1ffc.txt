2 October 2022 During the panel discussion, the honorary consuls of Georgia drew special attention to the priority directions of cultural diplomacy and the prospects for the development of this area in different countries of the world. The participants of the conference emphasized the need to promote Georgian culture, as well as the Georgian language worldwide as one of the clearest indicators of the identity of the Georgian people; to introduce the Georgian cinema to an international audience; to using Tbilisi as a platform for holding various large-scale events.
