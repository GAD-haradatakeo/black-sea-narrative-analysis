26 May 2018 26.05.2018, Tbilisi - France and Georgia are launching a high-level dialogue named after Georgian prince Dimitri Amilakhvari- the French Minister of Europe and Foreign Affairs, Jean-Yves Le Drian said at a joint press conference of the foreign ministers of the two countries. At the meeting between the French Foreign Minister, Jean-Yves Le Drian and the Georgian Prime Minister, Giorgi Kvirikashvili, also attended by the Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janlidze, discussions focused on the issues of bilateral relations, as well as on regional and multilateral co-operation in the areas of economy, culture and education. Following the meeting, the Georgian Minister of Education and Science and the French Minister of Europe and Foreign Affairs signed the Inter-governmental agreement on the implementation of the French-Georgian University Project.
