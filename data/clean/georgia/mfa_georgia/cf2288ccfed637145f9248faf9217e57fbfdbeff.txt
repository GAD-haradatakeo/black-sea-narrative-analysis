23 January 2021 The Armenian Foreign Minister, Ara Ayvazyan paid an informal visit to Georgia. The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani hosted his colleague in Tsinandali. The head of the Armenian Foreign Ministry was received by the Georgian Vice-PM and Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, who is to for an introductory meeting. During the informal meeting the Foreign Ministers of Georgia and Armenia discussed the agenda of bilateral relations between the two states, expressing their commitment to further deepen the current cooperation in various areas. Discussions also focused on the situation in the region with special focus on the importance of long-term stability. The two Ministers highlighted the close relations between the two countries. Talking points also included various aspects of economic and tourism cooperation, as well as the necessity of joint action to improve the pandemic situation and effectively eliminate the COVID-19 consequences.
