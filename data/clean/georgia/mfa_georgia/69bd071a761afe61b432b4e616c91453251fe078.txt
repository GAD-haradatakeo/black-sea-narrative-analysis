18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Commonwealth of Australia to Georgia, Sally Ann-Vincent (with residence in Ankara). Discussions during the meeting focused on current issues of bilateral cooperation and the importance of strengthening economic ties. The sides also reviewed opportunities for holding political consultations and exchanging high-level visits. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed gratitude to the Australian side for its support within international formats. The Deputy Minister wished the Ambassador success in her new position and expressed hope for fruitful cooperation. Within the framework of the visit, the Ambassador of the Commonwealth of Australia presented copies of her Letters of Credence to the Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Zurab Darchiashvili. The parties also discussed the friendly relations between the two countries and current issues of cooperation. Zurab Darchiashvili wished the Ambassador success in her future endeavors.
