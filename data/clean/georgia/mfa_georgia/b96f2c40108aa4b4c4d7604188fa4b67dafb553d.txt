The 9th round of political consultations held between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Korea 27 March 2024 On 27 March 2024, the 9th round of political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Korea was held in Tbilisi. From the Georgian side, the delegation was led by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, and from the Korean side, by the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Korea, Chung Byung-Won.
