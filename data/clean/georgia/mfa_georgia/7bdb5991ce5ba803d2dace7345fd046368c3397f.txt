17 March 2025 The Georgian Foreign Minister, Maka Botchorishvili visited the Republic of India. Maka Botchorishvili will participate in the Raisina Dialogue, a high-level international conference organized by the Ministry of External Affairs of India and the influential analytical research organization - Observer Research Foundation (ORF), which is held annually in New Delhi. As part of the conference, the Foreign Minister will address panel discussions on the Caucasus region, geopolitical dynamics, global security challenges and economic trends; she will also hold bilateral meetings with Indian External Affairs Minister Subrahmanyam Jaishankar and officials from other countries participating in the conference. The Raisina Dialogue is a high-level international conference, which will be opened by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi. At the conference, world leaders, diplomats, business leaders and academics will discuss geopolitical dynamics, global security challenges, economic trends and multilateral cooperation in these areas.
