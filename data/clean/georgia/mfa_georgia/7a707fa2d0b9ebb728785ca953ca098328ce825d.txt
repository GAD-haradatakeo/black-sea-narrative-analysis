26 June 2017 26.06.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze attended the lunch organized by the Amercian Chamber of Commerce. AmCham President Michael Cowgill delivered a welcome speech. Mikheil Janelidze spoke before the audience about the friendly and partner relations between Geprgia and the U.S. He also highlighted the existing trade and economic co-operation. The aim of the lunch was to increase the involvement of both local and international businesses in the reforms and projects launched by the country.
