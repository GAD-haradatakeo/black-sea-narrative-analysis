15 May 2018 15.05.2018, Paris – The Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani participated in the High-Level Political Meeting of the Proliferation Security Initiative, which marks its 15th anniversary this year. David Zalkaliani highlighted the importance of Georgia’s participation in the Proliferation Security Initiative and the challenges facing the region in terms of the trafficking of weapons of mass destruction. He paid special attention to Georgia’s efforts to combat the illegal trafficking of weapons of mass destruction and their delivery systems. Representatives of the countries participating in the PSI reaffirmed the importance of close-coperation and shared vision that will make its positive impact on the international security.
