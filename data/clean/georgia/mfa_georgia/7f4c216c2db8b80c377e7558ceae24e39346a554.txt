31 August 2022 On 31 August 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the representative of the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) in Georgia, Ghassan Khalil. Discussions focused on the issues related to the activities of the United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) in Georgia. Special attention was paid to the fruitful cooperation that has evolved between the sides over the years. Lasha Darsalia provided Ghassan Khalil with information regarding the current situation in Georgia’s Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions occupied by Russia.
