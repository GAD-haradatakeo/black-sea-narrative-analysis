31 October 2022 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia held a meeting with the Head ofthe OSCE/ODIHRElection ObservationMission(EOM),Ambassador Albert Jonsson. The sides discussed the report and recommendations on the local self-government elections in Georgia prepared by the OSCE Office for Democratic Institutions and Human Rights(ODIHR). The First Deputy Minister thanked the representatives of the OSCE Democratic Institutions and Human Rights Office for sending the observation mission to the local self-government elections and emphasized the importance of their involvement in the transparent conduct of the election processes. At the meeting, Lasha Darsalia also emphasized Georgia's efforts and commitment to consider ODIHR recommendations. The sides discussed the situation in the regions of Georgia occupied by Russia.
