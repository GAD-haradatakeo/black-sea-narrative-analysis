7 September 2022 Within the framework of his visit to North Macedonia, on 7 September, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the Secretary of State for European Affairs of North Macedonia, Fatmir Isak. The sides discussed the possibilities of deepening cooperation between Georgia and North Macedonia in the direction of European integration; North Macedonia’s experience in terms of sectoral rapprochement with the European Union was also noted.
