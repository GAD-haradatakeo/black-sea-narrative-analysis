Ilia Darchiashvili has met with his Icelandic colleague 29 November 2023 Within the framework of the ongoing OSCE ministerial meeting in Skopje, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with his Icelandic counterpart, Bjarni Benediktsson.
