16 October 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns the terrorist attack on 16 October 2017 in the capital city Mogadishu of the Federal Republic of Somalia that resulted in the death of more than two hundred people and left hundreds of others injured. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism in all its forms and expresses the hope that perpetrators of this brutal act will be brought to justice. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the Government of Somalia and the families of those who were killed by the terrorist attack, and wishes speedy recovery to those who have been injured.
