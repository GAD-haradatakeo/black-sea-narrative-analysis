30 July 2021 On 30 July 2021, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Qatar to Georgia, Mubarak Bin Nasser Al-Khalifa. Within the framework of the meeting, the sides discussed issues on the bilateral agenda, noting the dynamic rise in cooperation. Particular attention was paid to attracting investments to Georgia from Qatar and the necessity to further deepen trade and economic cooperation.
