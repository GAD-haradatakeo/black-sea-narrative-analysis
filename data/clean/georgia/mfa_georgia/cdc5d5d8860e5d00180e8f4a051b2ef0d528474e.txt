20 March 2019 20.03.2019, Argentina – Within the framework of the United Nations High-level Conference on South-South Cooperation, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with the Ministerof Foreign Affairs and Human Mobilityof Ecuador, José Valencia. The meeting was attended by Georgia’s candidate to the World Food Organization, Davit Kirvalidze. The two ministers discussed co-operation between Georgia and Ecuador in bilateral and international formats. The sides welcomed the theupcoming round of politica consultations between Georgia and Ecuador due to be held in April, which, from the Georgian side, will be led by Deputy Foreign Minister Vakhtang Makharoblishvili. The Georgian Foreign Minister expressed his gratitude for Ecuador’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and provided José Valencia with information on the developments in the occupied regions of Georgia on the government’s measures aimed at prevention of human rights violations on the ground. Zalkaliai introduced to his Ecuadorian colleague Georgia’s candidate to the World Food Organization, Davit Kirvalidze. Highlighting his professionalism and Georgia’s programme aimed at increasing the FAO’s effectiveness, the further development of agricultural sector and achievement of UN goals in all priority areas The sides highlighted the huge potential of developing sectoral co-operation and the effective use of economic opportunities by the Georgiana and Ecuadorian business communities.
