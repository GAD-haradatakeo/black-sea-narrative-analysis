14 April 2022 On 14 April 2022, another round of consultations was held in a virtual format between the Departments of International Organizations of the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Korea. During the meeting, the sides discussed the key issues of fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Korea, within the framework of international organizations, primarily, the United Nations, and the priority areas for future cooperation. The sides highlighted the successful cooperation with Korea in promoting the implementation of the 16th Sustainable Development Goal. The importance of mutual support for candidates within international organizations was also stressed. During the consultations, the Georgian side briefed the representative of the Korean Foreign Ministry on the difficult situation in the Russian-occupied regions of Georgia. The sides expressed concern over the situation in Ukraine, condemned Russia's ongoing aggression against Ukraine and noted the importance of further consolidation of support for Ukraine within the international organizations. At the end of the meeting, the sides reiterated their readiness to continue regular consultations on issues of close partnership and cooperation within the international organizations.
