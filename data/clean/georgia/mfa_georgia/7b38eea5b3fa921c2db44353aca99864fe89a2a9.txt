1 March 2023 On 1 March 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Turkmenistan to Georgia, Dovletmurat Muratov. Discussions focused on the issues of relations between Georgia and Turkmenistan. The sides discussed prospects for further development of cooperation, both in bilateral and multilateral formats, reviewed regional issues and international projects. Special attention was paid to the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries and activating trade and economic relations. The sides also discussed the issues relating to the visit of the President of Turkmenistan to Georgia, the Georgia-Turkmenistan Intergovernmental Economic Cooperation Commission, and the political consultations due to be held between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Turkmenistan.
