27 September 2023 On 27 September 2023, the newly appointed Ambassador of the Republic of India (with residence in Yerevan), Nilakshi Saha Sinha, presented copies of his credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili. Following the presentation ceremony, the sides held a meeting to discuss the traditionally friendly relations between Georgia and the Republic of India, as well as mutually beneficial cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Special attention was paid to the necessity of exchanging high-level visits and further intensifying trade and economic relations between the two countries. Discussions focused on cooperation in the fields of tourism, transport, culture and education. At the meeting, the sides highlighted the historical significance of the visit of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of India, Subrahmanyam Jaishankar to Tbilisi in 2021 and the handover of the holy relics of Great Martyr Queen Ketevan in Georgia. Nilakshi Saha Sinha confirmed that India continues to support the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and expressed his readiness to actively promote the deepening of relations between the two countries in all areas.
