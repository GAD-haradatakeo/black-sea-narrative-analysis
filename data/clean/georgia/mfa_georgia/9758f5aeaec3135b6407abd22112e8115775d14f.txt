5 April 2019 05.04.2019, Washington – On 5 April, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Under Secretary forArms Control and International SecurityAndreaThompson. The sides discussed the issues on the agenda of Georgia-U.S. partnership. The U.S. side reaffirmed its firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity, and Euro-Atlantic integration course. High appraisal was given to the decisions made during the NATO Ministerial Meeting. Under the decisions, the Alliance develops Black Sea security co-operation with Georgia.. Discussions also focused on the security environment in the region and gross violations of human rights in Georgia's occupied territories.
