18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Commonwealth of Australia, Sally-Anne Vincent. The discussion focused on the friendly relations between the two countries, current issues of bilateral cooperation, and prospects for further strengthening their partnership. Particular emphasis was placed on Australia’s significant role in promoting closer engagement with the countries of the Pacific region. The sides also discussed future initiatives aimed at enhancing trade and expanding economic cooperation. The parties exchanged information on key developments taking place in their respective regions. They further underlined the importance of exchanges of high-level visits and the advancement of cooperation within international organizations.
