14 January 2021 The heads of the Polish and Bulgarian diplomatic institutions, Zbigniew Rau and Ekaterina Zakharieva, congratulate their Georgian counterpart, David Zalkaliani, upon being re-appointed to the position of the Foreign Minister of Georgia via congratulatory letters. “I am convinced that with your leadership the Ministry will do its utmost to continue achieving outlined goals in terms of integration into Western Structures, strengthening Georgia’s position on the international arena, especially in the light of the ambitious agenda of the association agreement and its consistent implementation, along with effective deepening of cooperation with NATO,” – stated Zbigniew Rau in his letter, where the Polish Foreign Minister, once again, confirms the unwavering position of continuation of good relations between Poland and Georgia and strengthening of bilateral ties of friendship. According to the head of the Ministry, Poland will consistently support Georgia’s independence, sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognised borders. “We staunchly support Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations and I would like to underscore with particular emphasis that Poland will continue active support of Georgia on all international platforms, including the EU, NATO, OSCE, the Council of Europe, etc. as Poland did before,” – stated Rau in his address. The support for Georgia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity and Euro-Atlantic aspirations was also confirmed by Ekaterina Zakharieva, expressing hope that with the active joint efforts of our two Ministries Georgia and Bulgaria will further deepen their friendly relations.
