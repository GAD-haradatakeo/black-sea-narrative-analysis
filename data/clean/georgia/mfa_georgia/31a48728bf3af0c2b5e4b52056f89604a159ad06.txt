20 May 2022 Within the framework of the Council of Europe Ministerial Meeting, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of North Macedonia, Bujar Osmani. The Ministers noted with satisfaction the dynamics of relations between the two countries, high-level meetings and visits, which contributes to deepening cooperation in various fields. In this regard, the Ministers welcomed the visit of the President of Georgia to North Macedonia. The ministers exchanged information on the countries' European and Euro-Atlantic integration process. Special mention was made of the importance of sharing experience and further intensifying coordination in this regard. The North Macedonian Foreign Minister reiterated his strong support for Georgia's membership in NATO and the European Union. While on the theme of regional security, he stressed the importance for North Macedonia to assume the OSCE Chairmanship in 2023, highlighting that the occupation of Georgian territories will be high on the OSCE agenda during the North Macedonian chairmanship. Bujar Osmani reaffirmed North Macedonia’s strong support for the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia. At the meeting, the ministers focused on such promising areas of cooperation as trade, economy, people-to-people contacts, and agreed to conduct bilateral visits and facilitate the intensification of sectoral contacts.
