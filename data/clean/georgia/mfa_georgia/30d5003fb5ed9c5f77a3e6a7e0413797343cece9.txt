11 July 2025 On 12 July 2025, within the framework of the Dubrovnik Forum, Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs Maka Botchorishvili held a meeting with Christophe Kamp, the OSCE High Commissioner on National Minorities (HCNM). The meeting highlighted the long-standing, successful, and results-oriented cooperation between Georgia and the HCNM, grounded in close and constructive dialogue. Georgia’s state policy on national minorities, along with the effective implementation of relevant government strategies tailored to the specific needs of ethnic minorities, was positively assessed. Minister Botchorishvili reaffirmed Georgia’s strong commitment to fulfilling its international obligations in this area. Special attention was given to the OSCE’s involvement in the peaceful resolution of conflicts and the organization's significant role in the Geneva International Discussions. At the conclusion of the meeting, both parties expressed their readiness to continue close cooperation with the Office of the OSCE High Commissioner on National Minorities and other relevant OSCE institutions.
