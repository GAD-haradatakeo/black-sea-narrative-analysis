16 December 2018 16.12.2018, Telavi -“ I am happy to be in Georgia to attend the swearing-in ceremony of the president of Georgia. The Georgian people have a great reason for celebration today – the more especially as the new president is a women that points to the democratic development of Georgia. Co-operation between Georgia and Ukraine is based on the vector of European integration and democracy” - the Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister Vasyl Bodnar said when commenting on the inauguration of the 5th president of Georgia Salome Zurabishvili. The inauguration ceremony of Salome Zurabishvili has been attended by 150 high level guests from 55 countries in Telavi, Kakheti region. Documents Similar News
