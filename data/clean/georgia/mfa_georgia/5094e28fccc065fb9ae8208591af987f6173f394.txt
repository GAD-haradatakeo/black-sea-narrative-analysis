21 October 2019 21.10.2019, London – Within the framework of his visit to London, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Jonathan Djanogly, Chair of All-Party Parliamentary Group onGeorgia(APPG) in the UK Parliamentand other members of the Group. Welcoming the signing of the Georgia-UK Strategic Cooperation Agreement, the sides highly appraised the inter-parliamentary cooperation between the two countries. The Minister thanked British MPs for their support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations that is manifested in the UK Parliament’s statements and resolutions in support for Georgia.
