24 September 2019 25.09.2019, New York – On the sidelines of the 74th session of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Moldovan colleague, Ministerof Foreign Affairs Nicu Popescu. The two ministers highly appraised the bilateral relations between Georgia and Moldova and the European integration agenda. Discussing the security situation in the region, Zalkaliani briefed his Moldovan colleague on the recent developments in Georgia’s occupied territories and the occupation regime’s destructive activities that further aggravate the security climate in the region and make worse the living standards of the local population. The Ministers welcomed the fruitful cooperation between Georgia and Moldova within the framework of Eastern Partnership and expressed the readiness to further cooperate for the strengthening of bilateral relations. ​ Documents Similar News
