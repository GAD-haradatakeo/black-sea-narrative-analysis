23 September 2021 Within the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, on 22 September 2021, the Vice Prime Minister/Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with his Cypriot counterpart, Nikos Christodoulides. The Ministers spoke in detail about the friendly relations between the two countries based on common values ​​and stressed the importance of strengthening relations between Georgia and Cyprus in both bilateral and multilateral formats. During the conversation, the sides also exchanged information on current developments in the region and security challenges. David Zalkaliani informed his colleague about the difficult situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. The diplomats discussed prospects for deepening economic cooperation between Georgia and Cyprus. The importance of strengthening the tourism sector in the post-pandemic period was also highlighted.
