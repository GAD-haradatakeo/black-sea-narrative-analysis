29 September 2018 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs expresses its deepest condolences over the loss of hundreds of human lives triggered by an earthquake and a following tsunami in the Island of Sulawesi of the Republic of Indonesia that took place on 28th of September. Devastating earthquake and tsunami caused tragic consequences and destruction to the infrastructure of the region. Georgia stands together with friendly Indonesia in this moment of sorrow. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia once again conveys its condolences to the Government and the People of Indonesia and offers its sympathy to the families of the victims.
