6 September 2018 6.09.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani positively appraised a draft report presented by Andrejs Mamikins, Rapporteur on Implementationof the EU-Georgia AssociationAgreementand other statements made by MPs of the European Parliament in this regard. According to the Minister, this is the result of the great efforts exerted by the Georgian population and government on the EU integration path. “Assessments of the European Parliament were very objective, precise and truly reflecting the successful reforms the Georgian government is implementing. It is highly important that Georgia has the support of the European Parliament. We have the support both at the legislative and the executive level. In this light I’d like to recall European Commissioner Hahn’s visit and his high appraisal of Georgia’s European integration process and the steps we are taking in this regard” – David Zalkaliani told journalists following the government session. According to the Minister, opponents’ criticism regarding the European Parliament’s resolution is derived from their narrow political interests. “Unfortunately they are disregarding the interests of Georgia. Being guided by their narrow party interests, they are trying to mislead the society. Their main goal is to get as much political benefit as possible from this process and to leave the country with as little benefit as possible”. David Zalkaliani said that despite opponents’ attempts, the Georgian people take a healthy look at the situation and objectively assess the tangible results made on the EU integration path. “There are concrete assessments and results in terms of EU integration, on the one hand, and on the other, misleading statements of opponents guided by their narrow political interests. But our people see everything objectively” – David Zalkaliani said. Documents Similar News
