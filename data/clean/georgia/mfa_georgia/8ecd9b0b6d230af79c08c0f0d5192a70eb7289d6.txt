8 February 2022 On 8 February 2022, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia, Maaike van Koldam. The sides discussed important issues of cooperation between the two countries with a focus on the current political processes in Georgia. The sides exchanged views on the situation in the region and security challenges. Special attention was paid to the involvement of the Netherlands in the de-escalation of the crisis in Ukraine, including to the recent joint visit of the Prime Minister and Foreign Minister of the Netherlands to Ukraine. Russia's aggression against Georgia and the situation in the occupied territories were also highlighted. Talks also focused on Georgia's foreign policy priorities, such as EU integration, and on the Associated Trio the initiative as a mechanism for Georgia, Ukraine and Moldova to join the EU.
