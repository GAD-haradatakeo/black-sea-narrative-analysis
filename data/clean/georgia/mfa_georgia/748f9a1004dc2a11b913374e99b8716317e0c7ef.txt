22 September 2021 On 21 September 2021, during his visit to the United States, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the Republic of Sierra Leone, David John Francis. At the meeting, the two ministers spoke in detail about the relations between the two countries and the prospects for deepening cooperation in various fields. The sides also discussed topical issues of cooperation in a multilateral format. Special attention was paid to the importance of coordination of activities and mutual support of priority initiatives.
