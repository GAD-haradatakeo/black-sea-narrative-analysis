25 October 2018 The seventh meeting of the States parties to the Optional Protocol to the Convention against Torture and other Cruel, Inhuman or Degrading Treatment or Punishment (OPCAT) was convened on 25 October 2018, where Nika Kvaratskhelia, a candidate from Georgia was elected among thirteen members of the Subcommittee on Prevention of Torture (SPT) to serve for a four-year term from 2019 to 2022. The SPT is composed of 25 independent and impartial experts from countries which have ratified or acceded to the OPCAT and exercises control over the implementation of the Optional Protocol to the Convention against Tortureand other Cruel, Inhuman or Degrading Treatment or Punishment.
