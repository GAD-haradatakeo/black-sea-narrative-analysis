13 June 2021 The Secretary General of OSCE, Helga Maria Schmid is to visit Georgia from 14 to 16 June 2021. Within the framework of the two-day visit the Secretary General of OSCE will hold meeting with the Georgian President, Salome Zourabichvili, Georgian PM, Irakli Gharibashvili, Georgian Speaker of Parliament, Kakha Kuchava, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, and State Minister for Civil Equality Issues of Georgia, Tea Akhvlediani. During her visit to Georgia, Helga Schmid will also visit the village Ergneti near the occupation line, participating in the meeting of IPRM.
