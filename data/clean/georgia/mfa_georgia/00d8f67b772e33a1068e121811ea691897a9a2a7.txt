10 October 2023 Within the framework of his visit to the Czech Republic, on 9 October, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the head of the General Directorate of European Affairs of the Czech Government Administration, Stefan Czerny. Discussions focused on Georgia’s European integration process and on the implementation of the European Commission’s 12 recommendations. During the meeting, the representative of the Czech Republic once again confirmed the support for the European perspective of Georgia.
