14 February 2022 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia pays a visit to the Hague, on 14-16 February 2022. On 14 February, the Deputy Foreign Minister met with the Foreign Affairs and Defence Advisor to the Prime Minister of the Netherlands, Geoffrey Van Leeuwen. The sides expressed their satisfaction at the friendly relations between Georgia and the Kingdom of the Netherlands, based on common European values ​​and interests. Discussions also focused on prospects for Georgian-Dutch cooperation. Emphasis was placed on the importance of exchanging high-level visits to further intensify the political dialogue between the two countries. The sides highlighted that in 2022 the two countries will mark the 30th anniversary of establishment of diplomatic relations. The sides exchanged views over the situation in the region and security challenges, the recent developments around Ukraine. Geoffrey Van Leeuwen was provided with detailed information on the dire humanitarian situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. The sides highlighted the need for the international partners’ consolidated position to maintain the issues relating to Georgia high on the international agenda and the importance of the Netherlands support in this regard. The Dutch side reaffirmed its strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for the country's integration into European and Euro-Atlantic structures.
