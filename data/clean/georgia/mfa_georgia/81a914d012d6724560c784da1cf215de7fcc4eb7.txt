16 December 2025 On 16 December 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvitsishvili, hosted a reception in honour of the newly appointed ambassadors. The event was attended by the ambassadors of the Federative Republic of Brazil, the Republic of Panama, the State of Kuwait, the People’s Democratic Republic of Algeria, the State of Qatar and Iceland to Georgia. The Deputy Minister congratulated the ambassadors on the commencement of their diplomatic missions in Georgia and wished them success in their future endeavours.
