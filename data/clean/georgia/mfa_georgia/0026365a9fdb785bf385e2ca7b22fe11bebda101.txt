8 December 2021 On 8 December 2021, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili, met with the Secretary General of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC), Jagan Chapagain. The sides discussed issues relating to cooperation between the IFRC and Georgia and IFRC’s future activities in the country. The Deputy Minister thanked the IFRC Secretary General for his ongoing work, in particular for the measures taken by the IFRC in response to the COVID-19 pandemic.
