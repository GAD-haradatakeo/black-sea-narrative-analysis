20 April 2021 On 20 April 2021, the Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili participated in the ninth plenary session of the Euronest Parliamentary Assembly held online. The meeting was led by the Co-Presidents: Andrius Cubilius, a member of the European Parliament and Maka Bochorishvili, Chair of the European Integration Committee of the Parliament of Georgia. The First Deputy Foreign Minister’s remarks were devoted to Easter Partnership. He highlighted the importance the forthcoming EaP Summit scheduled for 2021 bears in terms of further strengthening the EaP. Speaking about his country’s priorities and expectations with respect to the EaP summit, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili expressed the hope the Summit’s final joint declaration will be ambitious, will reflect a strategic vision towards the region and will incorporate Georgia’s as well as other partners’ interests and aspirations. Speaking before the plenary session, the First Deputy Minister emphasized the need to use to the maximum all existing and new possibilities of rapprochement and integration with the EU. He also paid special attention to the difficulties that emerged due to the pandemic. Expressing his thanks to the EU for its efforts in the fight against the pandemic he highlighted the significance of solidarity, including in the context of the supply of vaccines.
