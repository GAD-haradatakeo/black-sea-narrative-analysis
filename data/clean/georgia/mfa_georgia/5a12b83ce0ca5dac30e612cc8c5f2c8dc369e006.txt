18 July 2019 18.07.2019, Washington - The Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani, upon invitation from the Us State Secretary Mike Pompeo, is participating at the Ministerial to promote the Religious Freedom organized in Washington D.C. The head of the Georgian Foreign Ministry will address the participants of the conference today. In his speech the Minister will focus attention on the situation in Georgia in terms of religious freedom, will also emphasize the role of Georgia as a point of historical connection between different cultures and religions as well as the importance of the tradition of tolerance. In his speech David Zalkaliani will pay special attention to the complicated situation created in the occupied regions of Georgia in terms of protection of the human rights, including the facts of violation of religious freedom and damaging of the religious monuments of cultural heritage.
