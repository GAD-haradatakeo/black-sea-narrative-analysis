18 May 2023 On 18 May 2023, the non-resident ambassadors of Uruguay, Chile, El Salvador, the Republic of Korea, Vietnam, Laos and Egypt handed over copies of their credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Khatuna Totladze. During the meeting held following the credentials ceremony, the sides discussed the main areas of cooperation and plans for the future. The deputy minister thanked the ambassadors for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia. The sides confirmed their readiness to deepen and further intensify the existing cooperation between the countries.
