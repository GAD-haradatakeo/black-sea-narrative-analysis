5 July 2021 The Georgian First DeputyForeign Minister, Lasha Darsalia held a meeting with the Japanese Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Georgia, Akira Imamura. The sides discussed Georgia-Japan bilateral relations and the situation in the region. Highlighting the importance of presence of Georgia’s high-level delegation at the Tokyo Olympic Games, the sides underlined that this will give a positive impetus to the further development of friendly relations between the two countries. Lasha Darsalia briefed Akira Imamura on the results of the last round of the Geneva International Discussions, as well as on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories.
