18 December 2019 18.12.2019, Tbilisi – Meeting on “NATO, Black Sea Security and Georgia” was held within the framework of the Ambassadors’ Conference -2019. First Deputy Foreign Minister, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili spoke before the participants of the meeting. Speakers of the panel discussions included Director of the NATO Integration Department, David Nardaia, Georgia’s Permanent Representative to NATO, Alexandre Maisuradze, Ambassador of Georgia to Romania, Nikoloz Nikolzishvili and Ambassador of Georgia to Ukraine, Teimuraz Sharashenidze. Discussions focused on Georgia’s NATO prospects, Georgia’s contribution to the Alliance’s military exercises and enlargement policy. Conversations also included such key challenges in the present-day world as hybrid war waged by Russia, cyber-security and energy security. The Ambassadors provided detailed information on their host countries’ activities in support for Georgia’s NATO integration and highlighted the challenges facing the country on its path to its ultimate goal – membership of the Alliance.
