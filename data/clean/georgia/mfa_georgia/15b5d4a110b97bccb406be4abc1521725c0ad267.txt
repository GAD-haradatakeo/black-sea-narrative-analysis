10 April 2022 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia condemns the so-called presidential elections in the occupied Tskhinvali region/South Ossetia being held on the 10th of April. This represents another illegal action of the Russian Federation against the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, which contradicts to the fundamental principles and norms of international law. This kind of provocative actions aim to legitimize the illegal occupation of Georgian regions and ethnic cleansing conducted against Georgians. In the circumstances of illegal occupation and effective control of Georgia’s indivisible regions by the occupation force, the so-called presidential elections cannot have any legal effect. Moreover, there shall not be any legal consequences of so-called referendum on joining the Russian Federation recently announced by the occupation regime in Tskhinvali, which is another illegal step towards annexation. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia calls on the Russian Federation to respect the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia, to fulfill its international obligations, inter alia the EU-mediated 12 August 2008 Ceasefire Agreement and to reverse the illegal decision on recognition of the so-called independence of Georgia’s occupied territories. We appeal to the international community to give due assessment and to react decisively to the illegal steps of the Russian Federation violating Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
