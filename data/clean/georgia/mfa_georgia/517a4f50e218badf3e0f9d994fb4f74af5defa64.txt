2 September 2025 Levan Diasamidze, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Kazakhstan, presented his Letters of Credence to the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Kassym-Jomart Tokayev. The official ceremony of presentation was held at the Presidential Palace. During the event, the Ambassador, on behalf of the Government of Georgia, expressed gratitude to the President for his support of Georgia and for his contribution to strengthening bilateral relations between the two countries. On behalf of the Government of Georgia, the Ambassador extended an invitation to the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan to pay an official visit to Georgia.
