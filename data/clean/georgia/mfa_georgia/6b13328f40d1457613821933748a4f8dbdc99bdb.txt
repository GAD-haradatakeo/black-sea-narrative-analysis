30 April 2019 30.04.2019 - Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani attended the event on the occasion of the King’s Day organized by the Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia. Speaking before the attending audience Zalkaliani highlighted the important support of the Kingdom of the Netherlands for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. According to him, Georgia and the Netherlands share joint vision for strong, united and future-oriented Europe. The Minister wished peace, welfare and prosperity to the Dutch nation. The event organized by the Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia was attended by foreign diplomats accredited to Georgia, members of the executive and legislative branches of the government, cultural and art workers.
