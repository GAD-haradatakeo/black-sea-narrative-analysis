23 February 2022 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Alexander Khvtisiashvili met with Nick Clements, Assistant Director for Australasia, Wider Europe and MENA at the Department for International Trade. During the meeting, the sides discussed the dynamics of trade and economic relations between the two countries. Emphasis was placed on the importance of fully using the economic potential in the post-pandemic recovery process. Alexander Khvtisiashvili spoke about the main directions and priorities of the government’s economic policy, the existing challenges and ways to overcome them. The Deputy Minister paid special attention to the attractive business environment and investment opportunities of the country.
