6 August 2021 David Zalkaliani speaks over the phone with his Greek counterpart The Vice Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Davit Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with his Greek counterpart, Minister of Foreign Affairs Nikos Dendias. Davit Zalkaliani expressed his sorrow over the large-scale fires in Greece and expressed solidarity with the Greek people and government on behalf of the Georgian government. The sides highlighted the historical ties and friendly relations between the two countries. Amid the large-scale fires in Greece, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Georgian Embassy to Greece remain in constant contact with the diaspora organizations on the ground.
