30 March 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to Georgia, AbdullahAlmutairi. The sides discussed issues on the bilateral agenda and cooperation perspectives between the two countries. The importance of high- and highest-level visits was also highlighted during the meeting. The Ambassador highly appraised the Georgian Government’s measures in the fight against the pandemic. The sides reaffirmed their commitment to further deepen relations between the two countries.
