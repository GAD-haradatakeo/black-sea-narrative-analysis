26 November 2018 26.11.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held his first meeting with the RepresentativeofUNICEFinGeorgiaDr. GhassanKhalil. Discussing the successful co-operation between Georgia and UNICEF, the sides highlighted the organization’s future plans in Georgia. David Zalkaliani provided the RepresentativeofUNICEFinGeorgiawith detailed information on the difficult human rights and humanitarian situation in the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. Special accent was placed on restrictions on free movement and on the ban on native language education for ethnic Georgians. In this light, the sides highlighted the importance of active engagement of international organizations, including UNICEF, in the occupied territories. At the end of the meeting, the Minister reaffirmed the Georgian Government’s support for UNICEF in Georgia.
