9 July 2018 09.07.2018 – As a result of intensive co-operation between the Georgian Embassy to the Republic of Korea and the Justice Ministry of the Republic of Korea and Immigration Service, the Korean government made a decision to simplify visa regime for the citizens of Georgia holding ordinary passports. The citizens of Georgia will obtain multiple-entry Korean visa, valid for 1-5 years and they will be issued for the persons having higher education and representatives of various spheres like private sector, heath and etc. Such new-type multiple visas will facilitate technological, educational and business co-operation and the development of tourism between the two countries.
