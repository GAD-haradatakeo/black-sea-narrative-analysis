16 April 2021 US investors have set up a $500,000 fund to finance Georgian startups as part of a memorandum of understanding signed between the Georgian Innovation and Technology Agency (GITA) and TECH Friends of Georgia on 15 April 2021, under the aegis of the Consulate General of Georgia in San Francisco. The signing ceremony held in a virtual format was opened by Georgia’s Consul General Levan Beridze. The Georgian Minister of Economy and Sustainable Development, Natia Turnava and Norm Coleman Senator of Minnesota (2003-2009). Senator Norm Coleman who is a close friend and supporter of Georgia closely cooperates with Georgian private companies and support Georgian business in the U.S. The memorandum aims to strengthen cooperation between Georgian and Californian startups and to organise various activities in the field of innovation and technology to help Georgian startups gain access to necessary resources and finances, as well as international experience.
