16 September 2022 On 16 September 2022, the Georgian Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili signed a book of condolences opened at the UK Embassy in Tbilisi over the death of Queen Elizabeth II. In his note, Darchiashvili expressed his condolences to the royal family and the people of Great Britain. “We were saddened by the demise of HM The Queen Elizabeth II, Queen of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. At this time of sorrow, we fully join the world in commemorating Her Majesty’s incredible legacy. Her reign symbolizing a strong statehood, national integrity and unwavering duty serves as an inspiration for us and will be undoubtedly remembered by the generations to come. While expressing the most sincere condolences on behalf of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, our prayers are with the Royal Family, the British people and the Commonwealth Realms” - the message of the Foreign Minister said.
