12 May 2020 Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia held the meeting with the UN Resident Coordinator Sabine Machl and FAO representatives. The sides discussed the issues of cooperation between Georgia and the UN including in the context of the crisis caused by the spread of the Coronavirus (Covid-19). The discussion focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the importance of effective engagement of the international community in addressing the existing challenges.
