25 September 2021 On 24 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of State, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Côte d'Ivoire, Kandia Kamissoko Carmara. The sides discussed bilateral relations between the two countries and expressed readiness to expand the cooperation agenda. In this context, the importance of deepening trade and economic relations was especially emphasized. The conversation also focused on the need to share experience in various fields and to strengthen inter-agency cooperation.
