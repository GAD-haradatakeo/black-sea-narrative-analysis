30 April 2018 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia welcomes the results of the summit meeting held between the President of the Republic of Korea, Moon Jae-in and the Chairman of the State Affairs Commission of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, Kim Jong-un in Panmunjeom, on 27 April 2018. Georgia supports the dialogue and the process of negotiations, as well as the active efforts over the recent period that have created a prerequisite for achieving a substantial progress. The Panmunjeom Declaration that serves the shared goal of the two countries and the international community as a whole – securing the safe and peaceful development - is also appraised highly. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs expresses the hope that the tangible results achieved during the meeting of 27 April will provide a solid basis for and contribute to the prosperity of the Korean Peninsula and the region, as well as of the Korean people.
