16 December 2018 16.12.2018, Telavi -“Georgia is Azerbaijan’s friend and strategic partner. Azerbaijan, along with Georgia, celebrates the election of new president and wishes happiness and prosperity to the Georgian people”, Chairman ofAzerbaijan’s Milli Majlis Ogtai Asadov said when commenting on the inauguration of the 5th president of Georgia Salome Zurabishvili. Ogtai Asadov came to Georgia today to attend the inauguration ceremony of the fifth president of Georgia, Salome Zurabishvili. The inauguration ceremony of Salome Zurabishvili has been attended by 150 high level guests from 55 countries in Telavi, Kakheti region. Documents Similar News
