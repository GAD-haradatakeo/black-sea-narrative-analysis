29 May 2020 On 29 May, Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia held an online meeting with State SecretaryAudun Halvorsen of theNorwegian MinistryofForeign Affairs. Discussions focused on the difficult human rights, humanitarian and security situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and on various issues of cooperation between Georgia and Norway in international formats. The Deputy Foreign Minister briefed the Norwegian side on the Russian Federation’s recent provocations, including the so-called illegal borderization process, deliberate disinformation campaigns and instruments of hybrid warfare. The parties emphasized the need for Russia to comply with the ERU-mediated Ceasefire Agreement of 12 August 2008 and the necessity of immediate creation of internationalsecurity and human right mechanisms on the ground. The importance of making progress on main issues on the agenda of the Geneva International Discussions was also highlighted.
