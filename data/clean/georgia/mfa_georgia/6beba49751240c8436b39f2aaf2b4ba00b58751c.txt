4 November 2019 4.11.2019, Sweden – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with SIDA'sDirector-General, CarinJämtin. Zalkaliani expressed his gratitude to theSwedish International Development Cooperation Agency for many years of cooperation with Georgia and the organization’s support via its projects for the reforms ongoing in the country that are aimed at strengthening democracy, human rights and economic development. The parties positively appraised SIDA President Peter Ericsson’s recent visit to Georgia during which Georgia-SIDA cooperation aspects and issues relating to the implementation of the Cooperation Plan for 2014-2020 were discussed. TheSwedish International Development Cooperation Agency has been cooperating with Georgia for many years and is currently carrying out up to 25 projects in the areas of business promotion, environmental protection, sustainable development and human rights protection.
