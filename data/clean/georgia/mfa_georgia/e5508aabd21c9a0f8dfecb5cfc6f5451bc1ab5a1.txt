9 July 2021 The Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Hungary to Georgia, Viktória Horváth, who completes her term of office in Georgia. Within the framework of the meeting, David Zalkaliani gave a highly positive assessment to the Georgia-Hungary relations, which have significantly deepened over the recent years, to which attests the exchange of high-level visits and successful sectoral cooperation.
