15 November 2019 15.11.2019, Tbilisi - Turkish Deputy Foreign Minister YavuzSelim Kıran paid a visit to Georgia on 14 and 15 November 2019. On 15 November, he held a meeting with Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia. The parties discussed important issues on the agenda of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and the Republic of Turkey. Special accent was put on the need to intensify strategic partnership. The parties also highlighted the importance of cooperation within the international organizations. Within the framework of the visit, consular consultations were held between the consular departments of the two foreign ministries. Discussions, aside consular issues, focused on the ways to further develop cooperation between the law enforcement and justice authorities. The importance of conducting annual meetings on consular issues was also highlighted. YavuzSelim Kıran was received for a brief meeting by David Zalkaliani as well. On 14 November, the Turkish Deputy Foreign Minister participated in the events to mark the 75th anniversary of deportation by Soviet authorities of the population from Georgia in the 1940s.
