3 October 2018 2.10.2018, Tbilisi – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani attended the reception on the occasion of the German Unity Day held under the auspices of the German Embassy to Georgia. The Georgian Prime Minister, Mamuka Bakhtadze and the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federal Republic of Germany to Georgia, Hubert Knirsch spoke before the attending audience. Mamuka Bakhtadze spoke about the close bilateral relations between Georgia and Germany He singled out for special mention the Federal Chancellor Angel Merkel’s visit as a further clear proof Germany’s support for Georgia. The Prime Minister also highlighted he importance of Georgia’s status as the Guest of Honour at the 2018 Frankfurt Book Fair that will raise awareness of Georgia and its culture in Germany. The German Ambassador, for his part, underlined the fruitful co-operation between Georgia and Germany in the areas of politics, economy, education, culture and other areas The reception was attended by representatives of the Georgian Government, members of the diplomatic corps accredited to Georgia, journalists and other invited guests.
