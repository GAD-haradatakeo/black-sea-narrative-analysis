27 March 2020 Some persons or political groups have recently become active in social networks promising our citizens abroad to get them back home and making them fill in various forms. We hereby ask you to take into consideration the fact that the rapid spread of coronavirus across the world has created serious barriers to international travel. Most countries have closed down their borders or have suspended or restricted international traffic whether by air, sea, road or railway. Hence, any promise or offer of assistance by certain individuals are completely unfounded, entail risks and may create further problems to the citizens concerned. We therefore call on you to take a more cautious approach to such kind of offers and to plan your route exclusively through the help of the Georgian diplomatic representations in order to avoid further complications.
