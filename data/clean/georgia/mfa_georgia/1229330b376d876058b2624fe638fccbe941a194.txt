4 July 2018 04.07.2018, Berlin – Members of the Group of Friends for South Caucasus under the German Bundestag will visit Georgia – they announced it during their meeting with the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani. They thanked the Minister for extensive and in-depth review highlighting the impressive progress Georgia has made in terms of European and democratic development of the country. according to the members of the Group, Georgia is an unchallenged leader of the region in terms of human rights, democracy and many other factors. David Zalkaliani emphasized Germany’s particular role in Georgia’s institutional development as well as in its efforts aimed at European and Euro-Atlantic development. He paid special attention to inter-parliamentary co-operation that will give an additional impetus to the development of bilateral relations. In this context special mention was taken of the group of friendship with Germany under the Parliament of Georgia headed by the Speaker of the Parliament, Irakli Kobakhidze. Discussions also focused on the grave human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and Georgia’s peaceful policy for the conflict resolution. David Zalkaliani also briefed the German MPs on the Otkhozoria-Tatunashvili list developed by the Government in order to ensure that all perpetrators of human rights violations are brought to justice. The Foreign Minister also spoke extensively on the Government’s new programme aimed at Georgia’s rapid economic development, social security, education reforms and modernization.
