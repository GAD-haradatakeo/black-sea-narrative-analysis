10 October 2022 A memorandum of understanding was signed between the Levan Mikeladze Diplomatic Training and Research Institute under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the United Nations University for Peace. The document was signed by the director of the Levan Mikeladze Diplomatic Training and Research Institute, Irine Dakhundaridze and the permanent observer at the University for Peace to the United Nations Office and Other International Organizations in Geneva, Ambassador David Fernández Puyana. Within the framework of his visit to Georgia, David FernándezPuyanaheld meetings with the representatives of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the senior officials of the Diplomatic Institute to discuss the prospects for future cooperation.
