31 August 2022 On August 31, 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the UN Deputy Secretary General for Political Affairs , Miroslav Yencha, who is on a visit in Georgia. The sides discussed a wide range of issues related to the UN-Georgia cooperation. Special attention was paid to active cooperation with the UN Secretariat and the UN team in Georgia in various priority areas. The First Deputy Minister provided information to Miroslav Yencha about the current situation in Georgia’s Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions occupied by Russia and emphasized the importance of providing unhindered access to international human rights mechanisms on the ground. Discussions focused on the Geneva International Discussions and the role of the United Nations as one of the co-chairs. Lasha Darsalia thanked Miroslav Yencha for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and for the involvement of the United Nations in the process of peaceful resolution of the Russia-Georgia conflict.
