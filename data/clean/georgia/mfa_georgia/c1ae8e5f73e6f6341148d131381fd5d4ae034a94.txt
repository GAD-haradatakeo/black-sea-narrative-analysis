1 April 2021 On 1 April the Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani, held a video conference with the Executive Secretary of the UN ESCAP and Deputy General Secretary of UN, Armida Salsiah Alisjahbana. The sides discussed topical issues relating to Georgia’s cooperation with ESCAP. More specifically, the Executive Secretary spoke about cooperation in the areas of energy and statistics. It was highlighted that ESCAP developed a roadmap for Georgia to achieve the 7th goal of sustainable development. Georgia is and will remain engaged in the researches and thematic pilot projects to be carried out under ESCAP. Georgia will share with ESCAP Member States its experience in implementing successful reforms including in the areas of civil acts and property registration.
