7 March 2024 A memorandum of understanding was signed between the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, the Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University and the National Archives of Georgia. The document aims to highlight the activities of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Democratic Republic of Georgia, member of the National Council and Constituent Assembly, Ambassador of Georgia to France Akaki Chkhenkeli. To mark the 150th anniversary of the Georgian public figure, various events are scheduled under the memorandum. These include the release of a book-album showcasing Akaki Chkhenkeli's correspondence, an exhibition featuring his archival and photographic materials, and the establishment of a scholarship in his honor. Additionally, an international scholarly conference is planned to introduce both Georgian and international audiences to the achievements of the prominent Georgian diplomat in Europe and highlight his contributions to Georgia. Furthermore, in collaboration with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and various organizations, several activities are scheduled throughout the year. These include the inauguration of a square dedicated to Akaki Chkhenkeli and the installation of his bust in Tbilisi. Similar events are also planned to take place in other regions. The trilateral memorandum was signed by Ilia Darchiashvili, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Jaba Samushia, the Rector of Ivane Javakhishvili Tbilisi State University, and Teona Iashvili, the Director General of the National Archives.
