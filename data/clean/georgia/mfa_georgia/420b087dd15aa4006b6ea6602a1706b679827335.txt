28 May 2022 A thematic panel on the role of the Diaspora was held to highlight Georgian culture abroad, within the framework of the Diaspora Forum. At the session - "Cultural Diplomacy", the speakers spoke about the Georgian Diaspora’s special role in ensuring the international recognition of the country’s rich creative potential, as well as in increasing awareness about Georgian culture. Discussions also focused on the Georgian young people’s potential in foreign countries, and on the opportunities to use their resources for the benefit of the country. Speakers of the Digital Diaspora panel discussed innovative opportunities for the increased use use of information and communication technologies and social media, including in the context of developing relationships with the Diaspora. Discussions also focused on the innovative approaches and new technologies that enable sustainable cooperation with the Diaspora, continuous communication, service delivery and involvement of the Diaspora in the development process of the country. The forum dedicated to the Diaspora Day - "Georgian Diaspora for the European Future of Georgia" was opened in Tbilisi on 27 May 2022, organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
