21 June 2017 21.06.2017 Tbilisi -On 21 June 2017 the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia hosted the working level meeting on legal issues between Georgian inter-agency team and NATO Legal experts. During the meeting the sides have discussed the existing legal framework of NATO-Georgia cooperation including the issues pertaining to Georgia’s participation in international operations and exercises, host nation support, PfP SOFA, as well as laws and regulations related to counter-terrorism, cyber security and hybrid threats. The participants have further discussed the Substantial NATO-Georgia Package projects and legal status of NATO-Georgia Joint Training and Evaluation Centre and Defence Institution Building School.
