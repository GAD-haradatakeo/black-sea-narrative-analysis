2 December 2017 2.12.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze took part in the event to mark the national day of Romania. Highlighting friendship between Georgia and Romania, Janelidze welcomed that the two countries are celebrating 25 years of diplomatic relations this year. Minister Janelidze thanked Romania for supporting Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations, as well as its commitment to Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. He said economic cooperation between Georgia and Romania has great potential of development especially in transport and energy.
