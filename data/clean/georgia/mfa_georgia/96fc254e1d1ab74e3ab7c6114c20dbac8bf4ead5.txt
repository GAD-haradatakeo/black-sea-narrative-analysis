18 December 2023 On 18 December 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, had a telephone conversation with his Polish counterpart, Radosław Sikorski. The Minister of Foreign Affairs congratulated his colleague on his recent appointment and wished him success in his future work. The sides confirmed their commitment to close cooperation to further deepen the ties between the two countries. The Polish Foreign Minister congratulated his Georgian counterpart on granting Georgia the EU candidate status. It was noted that this decision is a recognition of Georgia's aspirations, efforts and the progress achieved in this regard. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked his colleague for Poland's firm support of Georgia's European aspirations. The sides emphasized the importance of the Eastern Partnership format, which Georgia successfully uses to advance on its EU path.
