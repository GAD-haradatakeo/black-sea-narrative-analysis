29 May 2018 29.05.2018, Tbilisi – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted a meeting with representatives of the diplomatic corps accredited to Georgia where the Deputy Foreign Minister, David Dondua briefed foreign diplomats on the Georgian Government’s position regarding the recognition by Syria of the so-called independence of Georgia’s occupied territories. The Deputy Minister called upon the international community to duly assess to duly assess the illegal decision of the Assad regime made as a result of manipulation by the Russian Federation, by which the Syrian regime disregarded the international legal obligation to respect the territorial integrity and inviolability of internationally recognized borders of a sovereign state and declared its support for Russia’s military aggression against Georgia.
