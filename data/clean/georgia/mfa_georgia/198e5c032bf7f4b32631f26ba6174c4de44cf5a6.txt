22 May 2017 22.05.2017, Tbilisi - On 22nd of May, 2017, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Mr. David Dondua held a meeting with the UNDP Resident Representative in Georgia Mr. Niels Scott. The situation in the occupied territories of Georgia and Georgia-UN cooperation in the process of peaceful conflict resolution were discussed at the meeting. The parties focused on the programmes being implemented by the UNDP with the aim to improve the human rights and humanitarian situation of the people living in the occupied territories.
