4 April 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili started his first working day by holding a meeting with the Deputy Foreign Ministers. At the Ministry, he was received by the former Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, who congratulated his colleague on the appointment and wished him success in his diplomatic activities. At the meeting with the Deputy Ministers, discussions focused on main issues related to the activities of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. As it was mentioned at the meeting, the team of the Ministry will continue to focus on the fulfillment of strategic goals and objectives of Georgia’s foreign policy. The work will also continue towards restoring Georgia's territorial integrity and achieving de-occupation, promoting Georgia's European and Euro-Atlantic integration process, deepening relations with the strategic partners, encouraging trade and investment, and strengthening the Georgian diaspora. It was highlighted that the Ministry’s future efforts will be aimed at effectively addressing the foreign policy challenges facing the country. Ilia Darchiashvili was appointed Minister of Foreign Affairs on 4 April. He replaced David Zalkaliani, who was appointed Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the United States.
