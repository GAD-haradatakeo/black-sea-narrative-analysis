27 May 2021 On 27 May, the Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, participated in a joint, annual high-level conference of the Georgian national platform of the civil society forum of the Eastern Partnership Initiative and the Government of Georgia – “European Choice of the Georgian People”, which was conducted online. On the first thematic high-level session of the conference, “Georgia’s Application for Accession – a path towards 2024 – Challenges and Reality”, in his address, David Zalkaliani, underscored the most significant role of the civil society in terms of democratisation of the country and its development. The Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs noted that integration and full-fledged membership of Georgia in the EU is the choice of the vast majority Georgian people. This is an irreversible process, solidified by the European aspiration of the Georgian people, in addition to the successful reforms, undertaken by Georgia within the framework of the Association Agreement. David Zalkaliani expressed gratitude towards the aforementioned platform for organising such a significant event, underscoring the openness and readiness of the Government of Georgia to continue active cooperation with NGOs, including in terms of the processes associated with European integration, a significant aspect of Georgia’s path towards application for accession by 2024. Along with David Zalkaliani, the thematic session was attended and addressed by MPs from the EU integration committee, the chairwoman, Maka Bochorishvili, chairman of the above-mentioned platform, Lasha Tughushi and Director of the European Research Centre of the Georgian Strategic and International Relations Research Fund, expert, Kakha Gogolashvili.
