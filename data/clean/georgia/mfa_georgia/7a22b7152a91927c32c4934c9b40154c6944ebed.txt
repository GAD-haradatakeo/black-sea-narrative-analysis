20 December 2024 On 19 December 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili held an introductory meeting with Didier Trebucq, the United Nations Resident Coordinator in Georgia. The discussion focused on the fruitful cooperation between Georgia, the UN Resident Coordinator’s office, and the UN team operating in Georgia. They reviewed key priority areas of collaboration and plans for the future. Particular attention was given to the activities of the UN office in Georgia, including assistance to vulnerable populations, promoting inclusive economic growth, and fostering cooperation with civil society on these issues. The conversation also addressed the security, humanitarian, and human rights situation in the Russian-occupied Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions.
