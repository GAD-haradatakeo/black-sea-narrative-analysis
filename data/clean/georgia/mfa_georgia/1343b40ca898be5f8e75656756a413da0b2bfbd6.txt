27 May 2017 27.05.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze played host to participants of the Diaspora Forum and spoke to them about the importance of their active involvement in the implementation of the state diaspora strategy. The Minister welcomed the idea of organizing working meetings within the framework of the Forum. According to Mikheil Janelidze, proposals set forth during these meetings will be incorporated into government programmes and projects. As Mikheil Janelidze said, interaction with the Georgian Diaspora is of priority importance to the Georgian Government. On closing of the Diaspora Forum, participants summarized the issues discussed within various working groups and considered various possibilities of further co-operation. The thematic working groups dealt with such issues as the use of Diaspora potential in public diplomacy, promotion of their involvement in the economic development of the country, activities to maintain national identity, etc. Following the meeting, Diaspora representatives visited Kojori where they laid a wreath at the Memorial of Junkers.
