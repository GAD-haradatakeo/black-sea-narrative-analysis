12 October 2023 On 12 October 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vladimer Konstantinidi, and members of the Parliamentary Committee on Diaspora Affairs held an online meeting with Georgian citizens living in Israel and representatives of diaspora organizations. At the meeting, the deputy minister of foreign affairs and the members of the parliamentary committee were informed about the situation on the ground, as well as about the needs of the citizens of Georgia. It was noted that the Embassy of Georgia in the State of Israel works in an emergency mode and is in constant contact with the citizens of Georgia. Documents Similar News
