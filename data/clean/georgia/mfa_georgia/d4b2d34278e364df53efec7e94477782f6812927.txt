23 September 2021 On 22 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the Republic of Honduras, Lisandro Rosale Banegas. The sides discussed a wide range of bilateral relations and prospects for future cooperation in the political, economic and cultural spheres. Special attention was focused on the importance of strengthening inter-parliamentary cooperation. The sides also discussed topical issues of cooperation in a multilateral format. The need to continue bilateral consultations was also stressed at the meeting.
