Ambassador-designate of Brazil to Georgia has presented copies of his credentials to Lasha Darsalia 26 March 2025 On 26 March 2025, the Ambassador-designate of the Federative Republic of Brazil to Georgia, Carlos Ricardo Martins Célia, presented copies of his credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia. The Deputy Minister congratulated the Ambassador on the commencement of his diplomatic mission and wished him success in his future endeavors. See All
