12 September 2019 12.09.2019, Tbilisi – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted second meeting of the government commission on Georgia’s chairmanship of the Council of Europe. The meeting was chaired by Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia. The Commission discussed organizational issues relating to Georgia’s chairmanship and the ongoing preparatory works. The Commission members outlined an action plan, which will be discussed at the next meeting of the commission.
