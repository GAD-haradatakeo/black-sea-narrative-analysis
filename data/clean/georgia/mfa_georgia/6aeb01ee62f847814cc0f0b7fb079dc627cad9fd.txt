6 April 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the EU High Representative for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy, Josep Borrell. Wishing the Minister success in his future endeavors, the High Representative noted the close cooperation with Georgia, as a valuable partner of the European Union. The Minister underlined that with the official application for EU membership, Georgia's European integration process advanced to a qualitatively higher level and informed the High Representative about the Georgian Government’s steps following the EU application. According to the Minister, Georgia remains committed to its reform agenda. He also highlighted the country’s progress on implementation of the Association Agreement. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed the hope that Georgia, as well as the associate members of the European Union, will have the strong and continuous support of the European Union. The meeting with the High Representative focused on regional security challenges.
