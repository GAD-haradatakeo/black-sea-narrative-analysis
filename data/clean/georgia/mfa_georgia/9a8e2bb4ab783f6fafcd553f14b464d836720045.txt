18 August 2017 18.08.2017, Tbilisi – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the terrorist act that hit Barcelona on 17 August 2017 leaving several people dead and injured. We are shocked by this tragedy and in these trying times express our support and solidarity with the Spanish people and the Government. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia strongly condemns terrorism and violence in all their forms as a crime against humanity which is targeted against peace and stability. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the families of terror victims and wishing speedy recovery to those who have been injured in the attack. Documents Similar News
