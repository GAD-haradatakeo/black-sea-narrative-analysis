24 May 2018 24.05.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Giorgi Sharvashidze opened the second exhibition “European Democratic Republic” dedicated to the 100th anniversary since the foundation of the first Democratic Republic of Georgia at the Information Centre on NATO and EU. Following the opening ceremony Giorgi Sharvashidze and the head of the TSU library, Zurab Gaiparashvili announced the winners of the student essay competition and awarded them with special prizes.
