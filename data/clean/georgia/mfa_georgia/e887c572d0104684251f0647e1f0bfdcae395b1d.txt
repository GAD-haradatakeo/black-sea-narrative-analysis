17 June 2021 The Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the UK to Georgia, Mark Clayton. The British diplomat congratulated Lasha Darsalia on his career advancement and wished him success in his new role. Exchanging views over the results of the NATO Summit held on 14 June the sides commended the communique messages regarding Georgia’s NATO membership. Special attention was focused on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the progress in the Geneva International Discussions. In this context, Lasha Darsalia highlighted the importance of the international community’s consolidated position.
