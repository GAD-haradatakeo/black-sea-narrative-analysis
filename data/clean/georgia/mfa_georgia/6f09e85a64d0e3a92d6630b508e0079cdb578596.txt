26 October 2021 On 26 October, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia held his meeting with the Acting Representative of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies in the South Caucasus. The meeting was also attended by the President of the Georgia Red Cross Society, Natia Loladze. At the beginning of the meeting, Lasha Darsalia congratulated Davron Mukhamadiev on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. At the meeting, the sides discussed various areas of cooperation between Georgia and the IFRC. The First Deputy Minister welcomed the fruitful work of the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies in Georgia.
