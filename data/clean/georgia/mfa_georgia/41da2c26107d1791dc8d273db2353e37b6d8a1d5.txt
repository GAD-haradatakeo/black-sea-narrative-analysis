25 March 2020 Exactly 63 years ago, on 25 March 1957, by signing the Treaty of Rome the European states laid the groundwork for what is today the European Union - a symbol of peace and prosperity. This unique project is based on the unity of Europe, as well as solidarity and shared responsibility. These exact values give us strength and hope, that we will successfully eliminate the global threat of COVID-19. Today, as never before, it is vital to demonstrate the European solidarity, which will prove devotion and loyalty to the declared values. We should not allow that today's sudden reality is used to foment nihilism towards the values upon which the EU has been founded.
