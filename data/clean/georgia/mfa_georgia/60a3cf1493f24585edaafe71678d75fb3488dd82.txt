30 October 2023 From 30 October to 7 November, 2023, the UN Special Rapporteur on the situation of human rights defenders, Mary Laurel, is visiting Georgia. Within the framework of the visit, on 30 October, the Special Rapporteur met with the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia. At the meeting, the First Deputy Minister emphasized the importance of UN human rights protection mechanisms and confirmed Georgia's readiness to continue close cooperation with them. During the visit, the Special Rapporteur will also meet with representatives of the country's executive and legislative bodies and civil society. Based on the visit, the special rapporteur will prepare a relevant report, which she will present to the UN Human Rights Council in March 2025, during the spring session.
