2 February 2022 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the United Kingdom to Georgia, Mark Clayton. The sides focused on the ongoing processes in the region, the situation on the Ukraine border and security challenges. The sides also discussed the impact of regional processes on Georgia. The Ambassador briefed the Deputy Foreign Minister on London's position on regional security challenges and reiterated the United Kingdom's strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. Against the background of the rapidly changing regional environment and complex geopolitical challenges, emphasis was placed on the importance of the international community’s uniform and coherent position.
