12 July 2025 Within the framework of the 2025 Dubrovnik Forum, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Mauro Luiz Iecker Vieira, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Federative Republic of Brazil. The discussion focused on the friendly relations between Georgia and Brazil and the prospects for deepening bilateral cooperation. Special emphasis was placed on the importance of fostering ties between Georgia and the countries of Latin America and the Caribbean, with particular regard to Brazil’s role in this context. The ministers exchanged views on the potential for further development of bilateral relations, highlighting the possibility of high-level visits. They also explored opportunities for enhancing cooperation in the fields of economy, tourism, culture, humanitarian affairs, and people-to-people exchanges. The conversation addressed the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions and the broader security environment.
