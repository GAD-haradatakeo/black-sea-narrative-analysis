13 July 2021 On 12 July 2021, the Georgian First DeputyForeign Minister, Lasha Darsalia held a meeting with the Headof the European Union Monitoring Mission (EUMM) in Georgia, MarekSzczygiel. The aim of the meeting was to discuss issues relating to Georgia-EUMM cooperation. The sides discussed security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions, as well as across the occupation line. Putting a special focus on the importance of the Incident Prevention and Response Mechanism (IPRM) meetings, the sides expressed the hope that tangible results will be achieved at Ergneti meetings on 13 June. Highlighting the EUMM’s role in monitoring the implementation of the EU-mediated 12 August Ceasefire Agreement, the First Deputy Foreign Minister expressed his gratitude for the Mission’s important contribution to ensuring peace and stability in the country and underlined the significance of granting EU monitors an unhindered access to the occupied territories.
