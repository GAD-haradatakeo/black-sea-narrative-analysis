4 May 2020 Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 18,646 citizens of Georgia in the host countries. The Government has organized the repatriation 9,486 citizens to Georgia, as of 4 May. When taking decisions with respect to Georgian citizens intending to return to Georgia in the nearest future, Georgia’s diplomatic representations and consular offices abroad are guided by general criteria and key priorities determined by the Coordination Council. The Georgian MFA website is updated every day to provide the latest information regarding support measures undertaken by the Georgian government with respect to Georgian citizens abroad.
