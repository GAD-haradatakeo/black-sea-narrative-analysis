26 January 2026 The first round of bilateral political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Bosnia and Herzegovina was held on 26 January, in Tbilisi. On the Georgian side the consultations were led by First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Giorgi Zurabashvili, while the delegation of Bosnia and Herzegovina was headed by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Josip Brkić. The parties welcomed the establishment of the political consultations format and reviewed key issues on the bilateral agenda, including prospects for the further development of bilateral dialogue, as well as the deepening of trade, economic and sectoral cooperation. Particular attention was devoted to issues of European and Euro-Atlantic integration. The First Deputy Minister briefed the delegation of Bosnia and Herzegovina on the situation in Georgia’s occupied regions, expressed gratitude for its support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, and underscored the particular importance of the continued support of partners in this regard.
