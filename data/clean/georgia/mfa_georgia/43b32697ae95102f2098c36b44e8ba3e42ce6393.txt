25 September 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Political Affairs, International Economic Cooperation, and Digital Transition of San Marino, Luca Beccari. The parties underlined the friendly relations established between the two countries and emphasized the importance of fully utilizing the potential of bilateral cooperation, particularly in the fields of trade, economy, tourism, culture, and beyond. The discussion also addressed the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, highlighting the significance of the policy of peaceful conflict resolution.
