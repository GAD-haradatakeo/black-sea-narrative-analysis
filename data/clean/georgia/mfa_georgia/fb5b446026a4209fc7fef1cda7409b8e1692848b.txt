3 December 2017 President of the Swiss Confederation Doris Leuthard has paid an official visit to Georgia, on 3 and 4 December. Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze, Head of the Administration of the President Giorgi Abashishvili and other officials took part in the official welcoming ceremony at the International Tbilisi Airport. President of Georgia Giorgi Margvelashvili will officially host his Swiss counterpart at the Paresidential Palace. The Georgian and Swiss Presidents will hold a tête-à-tête meeting, as well as a meeting in an extended format.
