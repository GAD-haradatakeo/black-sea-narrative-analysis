The pervading spirit of the FAC meeting in Luxembourg is that Georgia is part of Europe - Ilia Darchiashvili 24 April 2023 “The main message we received from our partners is that almost everyone supports Georgia’s European integration, European future, our common coexistence in the European family - and this is especially important for us, and we will continue our efforts to bring our country even closer to the European Union”, Foreign Minister Ilia Darchiashvili said following his participation in the European Union’s Foreign Affairs Council meeting in Luxembourg.
