24 May 2017 24.05.2017, Tbilisi – Foreign Diplomats will visit Kvareli within the framework of the diplomatic tour that will be held at the initiative of the Georgian Foreign Ministry, on 25 May. Within the framework of the tour, both resident and non-resident ambassadors will travel to Kvareli, Kakheti Region. The diplomatic tour will help the diplomats get familiarized with the economic and tourist potential of various regions of Georgia. Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze will deliver a welcoming speech for the foreign diplomats. The Kakheti Development Agency will offer a presentation on culture and traditions of the Kakheti region, whereas a representative of the Georgian Tourism Administration will highlight the tourism potential of Georgia.
