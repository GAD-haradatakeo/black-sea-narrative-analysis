24 October 2017 24.10.2017, London –The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze participated in the meeting organized by Chatham House, the Royal Institute of International Affairs. The meeting, which was devoted to discussions of Georgia’s foreign policy priorities was attended by representatives of leading British think-tanks, diplomatic corps and media. The Vice Prime Minister focused on Georgia’s foreign policy aspirations, the success made on the European and Euro-Atlantic integration path and various directions of Georgia’s democratic and economic development.
