19 July 2021 Speaking at the opening session of the Batumi International Conference, the President of the Republic of Moldova Maia Sandu focused her attention on Moldova’s EU aspirations and EU expectations. According to her, the Moldovan people’s expectations rest on EU support and the progress made on the EU integration path. However, as Maia Sandu noted, far more important than EU support is the homework done at home. According to the Moldovan President, EU institutions have been helping Moldova over many years and therefore “the commitment we first of all have to our people this is the commitment we have for the EU” – Maia Sandu said. “They help us even more now that we have political commitment to do what’s right for the country, to do what’s right for the citizens of Moldova” – she added.
