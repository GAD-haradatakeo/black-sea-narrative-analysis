18 May 2021 On 18 May 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Austrian Ambassador to Georgia, Thomas Mühlmann. Discussions focused on close cooperation between Georgia and Austria within the framework of international organizations. The Georgian side paid special attention to Russia’s destructive activities in Georgia, including the difficult humanitarian situation and grave human rights violations in Georgia’s occupied territories. Thomas Mühlmann reaffirmed Austria’s firm and unequivocal support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders.
