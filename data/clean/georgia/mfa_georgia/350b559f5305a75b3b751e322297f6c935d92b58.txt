23 May 2018 23.05.2018 – “Dear friends, Finland celebrated 100 years last year, now it’s your turn. You are great nation and you can be proud of your country. Happy birthday Georgia!” Finnish Foreign Minister Timo Soini says in his video address congratulating Georgia on the 100th anniversary since the foundation of the first Democratic Republic. According to the Finnish Foreign Minister, Finland and Georgia are more than partners, they are friends. They cooperate in many areas, including education and security. Finnish Foreign Minister reaffirms his government’s firm support for Geortgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity highlighting Georgia’s progress in the European integration process. “Our support on Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity stands firm. Georgia has come a long way in reforms and closer integration with the EU, the visa freedom, association agreement and Free trade agreement are great achievements. We also see tourism growing, over 4000 fins visited Georgia last year. We also welcome you in Finland” - Timo Soini says. Documents Similar News
