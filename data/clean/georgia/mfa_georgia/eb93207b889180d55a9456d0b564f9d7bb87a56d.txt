25 September 2019 Within the framework of the UN General Assembly, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with OSCE Secretary General ThomasGreminger.The sides discussed the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the recent developments across the occupation line in the Tskhinvali region. The sides expressed their concern over the ongoing illegal borderization, closure of the so-called checkpoints, increasing number of kidnappings of civilians, deployment of an illegal post in the Georgia-controled territory and other destructive activities of the occupation regimes that have become particularly aggressive recently. It was highlighted that the sides should employ maximum efforts and all mechanisms at their disposal establish security and achieve peacesful resolution of the conflict on the ground. Thanking the OSCE Secretary General for OSCE’s active involvement in the Geneva International Discussions, the Georgian delegation also took special note of the role of the co-chairs who have considerably contributed to preventing the situation on the ground from further escalating.
