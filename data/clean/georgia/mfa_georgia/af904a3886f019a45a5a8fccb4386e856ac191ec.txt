27 October 2023 On 27 October 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the Secretary General of the Organization for Democracy and Economic Development (GUAM), Altai Efendiev, who is participating in the Tbilisi Silk Road Forum. The meeting was also attended by the Ambassador of Georgia to Ukraine, the permanent representative of Georgia to GUAM, Giorgi Zakarashvili. At the meeting, the sides discussed the current issues of cooperation within the framework of GUAM, including prospects for interaction with political, inter-parliamentary, sectoral and SUAM+ partners, as well as activities on the Organization’s 2023 agenda. Particular attention was paid to the importance of coordinated interaction between GUAM’s member states within the framework of international organizations, and the possibilities of strengthening cooperation in the areas of transport and connectivity.
