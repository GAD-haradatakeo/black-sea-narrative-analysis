2 March 2023 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, took part in the event dedicated to Estonia's Independence Day and addressed the audience. He congratulated the Estonian government and the Estonian people on the Independence Day and wished the country peace and prosperity. In his speech, the deputy minister emphasized the particularly friendly and close relations between Georgia and Estonia and thanked Estonia for its firm support of Georgia's European and Euro-Atlantic integration and for sharing experience in the process of implementing reforms in various fields. The Deputy Foreign Minister expressed the hope that with joint efforts and common vision, Georgia and Estonia will further strengthened their friendly ties and relations across a broad spectrum of areas.
