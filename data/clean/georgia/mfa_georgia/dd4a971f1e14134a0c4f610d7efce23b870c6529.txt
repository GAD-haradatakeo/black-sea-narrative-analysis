17 February 2018 17.02.2018, Munich – Georgia is an important partner of Italy and Italy highly appraises Georgia’s progress and achievements - Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation Angelino Alfano said during his meeting with Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidzeheld on the sidelines of the 54th Munich Security Conference in Munich. Mikheil Janelidze welcomed the Italian chairmanship of OSCE expressing the hope that the Georgia-Russia conflict will remain atop the OSCE agenda. Angelino Alfano reaffirmed Italy’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European aspirations. In this context Mikheil Janelidze brought to the attention of his Italian counterpart the objectives of Georgia’s European integration set forth in the roadmap.
