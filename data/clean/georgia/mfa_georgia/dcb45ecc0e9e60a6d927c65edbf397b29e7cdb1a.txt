29 March 2022 On 29 and 30 March, 2022, the OSCE Chairman-in-Office, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Poland, Zbigniew Rau will pay a visit to Georgia. During the visit, the Polish Foreign Minister will hold a meeting with the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani, after which the Foreign Ministers of the two countries will make joint statements. Zbigniew Rau will also hold meetings with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili, the President of Georgia, Salome Zourabichvili, the First Deputy Chairman of the Parliament, Giorgi Volski. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Poland will pay tribute to the memory of the heroes who died in the battle for the unity of Georgia at the Heroes' Square and will make an entry in the guest book. Zbigniew Rau will also pay a wreath at the memorial of President Lech Kaczynski in Tbilisi. Zbigniew Rau will visit the occupation line and get acquainted with the situation on the ground.
