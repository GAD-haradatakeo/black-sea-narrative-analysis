UN General Assembly adopts resolution on status of internally displaced persons and refugees from Abkhazia, Georgia and the Tskhinvali region / South Ossetia, Georgia 9 June 2022 On 8 June 2022, in New York, the UN General Assembly, in its 76th session, adopted a resolution initiated by Georgia and other countries across the world (a total of 61 countries) on the Status of Internally Displaced Persons and Refugees from Abkhazia, Georgia and the Tskhinvali Region / South Ossetia, Georgia.
