21 February 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its sorrow for the loss of life and injury caused by the fire on 20 February 2019, in the capital of Bangladesh, Dhaka, that killed over 70 people and left up to 40 injured. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the families of disaster victims, to the Bangladeshi people and the Government of the People’s Republic of Bangladesh wishing speedy recovery to all those who have been affected by the fire.
