4 September 2021 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Finland Pekka Havisto thanked his Georgian counterpart Davit Zalkaliani for his contribution to the evacuation operations carried out by Georgia in Kabul, Afghanistan. The international evacuation operation in Kabul required good and agile cooperation internationally. Tbilisi suddenly became an important focal point for the evacuation of Finnish citizens and Afghans with Finnish protection. The flexibility shown by the Georgian Authorities in the use of Tbilisi airport was extremely valuable. The excellent cooperation between the Georgian and Finnish authorities during the evacuation operation was exemplary and serves as a manifestation of our friendly relations. I want to extend to you our sincerest gratitude and I look forward to an opportunity to meet you again in person” – reads the letter of the Finnish diplomat. The letter was presented to David Zalkaliani by the Finnish Ambassador to Georgia, Kirsti Narinen, on 4 September 2021. Documents Similar News
