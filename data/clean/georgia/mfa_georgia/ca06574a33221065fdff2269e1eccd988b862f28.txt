30 August 2019 30.08.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the President DelfimSantiagodas Neves of the National Assembly of São Tomé and Príncipeand the parliamentary delegation. The sides discussed the need to further develop cooperation both in bilateral and multilateral formats between Georgia and the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe. Discussions also focused on the importance of exchanging high-level visits and of intensifying inter-parliamentary relations. The President of the National Assembly reaffirmed the firm support of the Democratic Republic of São Tomé and Príncipe for Georgia’s sovereigny and territorial integrity. David Zalkalianihighlighted the challenges facing the country, including the difficult security and human rights situation in the occupied territories and the ongoing borderization process across the occupation line.
