17 September 2018 17.09.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister David Dondua held a meeting with OSCE High Commissioner on National Minorities Lamberto Zannier. Discussions focused on the protection of rights of minorities in Georgia and the need of their integration into the country’s political, economic and social life and the projects involving the participation of the Office of OSCE High Commissioner. Within the framework of his visit, Zannier will hold meetings with the President of Georgia, Giorgi Margvelashvili, the Chairman of Parliament, Irakli Kobakhidze and other representatives of the executive and legislative authorities of Georgia. The OSCE High Commissioner will also hold meetings with the Public Defender, Nino Lomjaria and representatives of national minorities. The OSCE High Commissioner also plans to visit the Abkhazia region that will give him a good opportunity to explore the situation on the ground. It is Lamberto Zannier’s first visit in his capacity as High Commissioner. The OSCE High Commissioner on National Minorities, Lamberto Zannier will leave Georgia on 21 September.
