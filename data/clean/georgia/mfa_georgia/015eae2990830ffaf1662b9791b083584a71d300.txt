30 June 2022 At the meeting, the sides discussed the current agenda of cooperation between the two countries. Speaking about the security environment in the region, the ministers emphasized the role of Georgia as a reliable partner of NATO in terms of maintaining regional peace and stability. Attention was focused on the steps and progress made by Georgia on the path of Euro-Atlantic integration over the recent years. Ilia Darchiashvili highly appreciated the decisions made at the NATO Summit in Madrid and the Alliance’s clear support for Georgia's Euro-Atlantic course. The Ministers took stock of the Georgia-Norway relations in both bilateral and multilateral formats and welcomed the positive dynamics of development of cooperation between Georgia and the Nordic countries. They emphasized the important assistance provided by Norway to Georgia. Ilia Darchiashvili informed his colleague about the process of Georgia's integration into the European Union and the EU Council’s recent decision to grant Georgia the membership perspective, which is an important tangible result on the country's European integration path.
