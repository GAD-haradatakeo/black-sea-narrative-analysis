24 November 2025 Within the framework of a visit to the Vatican, the Minister forForeign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, together with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze, and members of the Government, attended a concert by the Georgian State Chamber Choir held at the Basilica of Santa Maria Maggiore. The performance was dedicated to a jubilee occasion celebrated by the Vatican. As part of the visit, the Prime Minister held a meeting with Pope Leo XIV. On behalf of the Government and the people of Georgia, the Prime Minister congratulated the Pontiff on his election as the head of the Catholic Church and expressed the hope that the visit would contribute to further strengthening the relations between Georgia and the Holy See. The Prime Minister also met with the Secretary of State of the Holy See, Cardinal Pietro Parolin. During the meeting, the parties emphasized the importance of the friendly relations between Georgia and the Holy See, founded on rich historical heritage and shared Christian values.
