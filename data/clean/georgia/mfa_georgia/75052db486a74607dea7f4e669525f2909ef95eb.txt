4 February 2023 The Visa Waiver Agreement between the Government of the Republic of Paraguay and the Government of Georgia for Holders of Ordinary Passports came into force, on 3 February 2023. The entry into force of the Agreement represents yet another important step towards deepening friendly relations between Georgia and the Republic of Paraguay. Visa-free travel between the two countries will contribute to the strengthening of people-to-people, cultural, tourist and business ties and the further development of mutually beneficial cooperation in various fields.
