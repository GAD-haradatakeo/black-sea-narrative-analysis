10 February 2023 The Georgian Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili, visited the Republic of Turkey, where he made a note in the book of condolences for the victims of the February 6 earthquake. “I extend my condolences to the people of Turkey over this devastating earthquake. Our thoughts are with those, who have lost loved ones and suffered terrible losses due to this tragedy. I would like to console the people of Turkey, reconfirming that in these trying times they do not stand alone; the Georgian people are by their side during these most difficult days” – wrote the Minister.
