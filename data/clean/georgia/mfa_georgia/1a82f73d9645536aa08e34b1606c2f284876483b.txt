22 November 2018 22.11.2018, Tbilisi – “The Russian president’s yet another meeting with the representative of the Abkhazian occupation regime, Raul Khajimba represents blatant violation of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and an illegal attempt to legitimize the occupation regimes and control over these regimes in direct violation of the commitments under 12 August 2008 “ – the representative of the Georgian Foreign Ministry, Vladimer Konstantinidi said when commenting on President Vladimir Putin’s meeting with Abkhaz representativeRaul Khajimbain Sochi on 21 November. According to Vladimer Konstantinidi, such attempts of Russia cannot lead to any result as the international community will never accept the illegal recognition of the so-called independence of Georgia’s integral regions. “The international community is perfectly aware of and duly assesses Russia’s destructive policy with respect to the occupied territories that poses a threat to regional security. There should be no expectation that the facts of increasing militarization, human rights violations, kidnappings, tortures and other violations will go unpunished” - the representative of the Georgian Foreign Ministry. According to him, the international community continues to call on Russia to withdraw its illegal decision on recognition of the so-called independence of the occupied territories and firmly supports Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as the Georgian government’s non-recognition policy with respect to the occupied territories.
