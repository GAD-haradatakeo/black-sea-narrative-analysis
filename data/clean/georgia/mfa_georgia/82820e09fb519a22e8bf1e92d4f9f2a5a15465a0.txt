17 June 2020 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani received German Ambassador to Georgia, Hubert Knirsch, French Ambassador to Georgia, Diego Colas and Regional Portfolio Manager at the UK Embassy to Georgia, Benjamin Luckock. The aim of the meeting was to update the Georgian side on the New Quarantine Framework, which was put in place on 15 June, in Germany. The Robert Koch Institute published a list of coronavirus-risk countries, which means that anyone entering Germany from a risk area will be ordered to quarantine. Georgia, owing to its positive pandemic situation, was not included into this list. It was also noted that citizens of non-EU Member States, including Georgia, cannot travel to Germany as tourists. However, from 15 June, they can enter Germany for business, educational, medical or other specific purposes. Based on EU recommendations, the German government will prepare individual travel recommendations with respect to individual countries and regions.
