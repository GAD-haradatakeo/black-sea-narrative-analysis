3 February 2018 03.01.2018, Kvareli - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a summing-up meeting with Georgian experts and media representatives. Mikheil Janelidze discussed in detail the activities and deliverables of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs over the past year, along with steps taken in terms of deepening the bilateral relations with the partners, Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration and other areas of the foreign policy. The Foreign Minister highlighted the Ministry’s plans and objectives for 2018. The meeting proceeded in a Q&A format. The Deputy Foreign Ministers were also present providing the experts and journalists with detailed information on various issues of interest to them. The first meeting of the foreign minister with experts and media representatives was held in 2017. Since then Mikheil Janelidze has been regularly inviting the interested audience to bring to their attention the activities of the Ministry. The summing-up meeting 2018 was held within the framework of the EU-funded project "Facility for the Implementation of the Association Agreement in Georgia" supporting the implementation of the EU-Georgia Association Agreement.
