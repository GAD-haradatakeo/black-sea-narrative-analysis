27 September 2022 27 September 2022 marks 29 years since the fall of Sokhumi. 27 September is the day of the common tragedy suffered by Georgian and Abkhaz people, which has led to the Russian occupation of Georgia’s Abkhazia region, and has resulted in the death of tens of thousands of people and displacement of hundreds of thousands fleeing their homes. Today we are paying tribute to the memory of our soldiers and civilians who fell in the fight for the unity of Georgia and express our condolences to their families and relatives. There is no alternative to peaceful resolution of the conflict - de-occupation and restoration of trust and reconciliation between the communities torn apart by the barbed wire fences provides the only solid basis for ensuring peaceful coexistence and common prosperity in a united and strong state.
