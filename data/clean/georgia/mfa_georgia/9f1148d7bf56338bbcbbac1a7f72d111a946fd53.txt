16 February 2026 On 16 February 2026, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, will pay a working visit to the Slovak Republic. Within the framework of the visit, Minister Botchorishvili will hold a meeting with the Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of the Slovak Republic, Juraj Blanár.
