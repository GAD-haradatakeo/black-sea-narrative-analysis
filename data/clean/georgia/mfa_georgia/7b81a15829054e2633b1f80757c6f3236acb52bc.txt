2 March 2024 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, is participating in the Antalya Diplomacy Forum, which will be held on 1-3 March 2024. Within the framework of the event, the Georgian diplomat took part in the panel - "Peace, Development and Connectivity in the South Caucasus". The participants discussed the ongoing processes in the South Caucasus and the future perspectives for the development of regional cooperation. It should be noted that within the framework of the visit, the First Deputy Minister will hold bilateral meetings with colleagues from Slovenia, Oman, Argentina, Sri Lanka, Ghana, as well as the co-chairs of the Geneva International Discussions, Toivo Klaar and Cihan Sultanoglu. The Antalya Diplomacy Forum is a high-level event and is held under the auspices of the President of the Republic of Turkey, Recep Tayyip Erdoğan, and at the initiative of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The Antalya Forum serves as a platform for sharing ideas through scheduled panels, discussing international challenges, and formulating action plans to address them. Leaders of different countries, heads of government, ministers, diplomats, representatives of analytical circles and mass media take part in the forum.
