12 August 2021 The Interagency Working Group on Development, Implementation and Monitoring of Georgia's EU Integration Action Plans and EU Assistance Coordination held its first meeting in a digital format, on 12 August. Salome Shapakidze, Director General of the Directorate General for European Integration at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs chaired the meeting, which brought together representatives of the ministries and other state agencies involved in the European integration process. The Working Group discussed issues related to Georgia's integration into the European Union, including the fulfillment of obligations under the Georgia-EU Association Agreement and the progress in implementing the EU assistance. The Working Group was established within the framework of Georgia’s European Integration Commission and aims to strengthen the supervision over and monitoring of the European integration process of Georgia.
