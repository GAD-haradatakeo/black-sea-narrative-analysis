3 February 2025 On 3 February 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the State of Qatar to Georgia, Hassan bin Ahmed Al-Mutawa. The discussion centered on key priorities of the bilateral agenda, with particular emphasis on the positive dynamics of economic relations and prospects for their further expansion. Both parties underscored the priority importance of strengthening cooperation with the Gulf states, both bilaterally and within multilateral frameworks. Furthermore, the meeting addressed the significance of tourism development for Georgia, highlighting Qatar as a promising market in this sector. The participants also emphasized the successful operations of Qatar Airways in Georgia, recognizing its pivotal role in fostering economic cooperation and enhancing people-to-people ties.
