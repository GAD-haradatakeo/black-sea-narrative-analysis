20 November 2019 20.11.2019, Brussels – The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani has raised the issue of the abduction of Georgian doctorVazhaGapridashviliwiththe High Representative of the EU for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy, FedericaMogherini, the acting OSCEChairperson-in-Office, MiroslavLajcak, the Deputy Secretary General of the UN, RosemaryDiCarlo. Meeting with journalists at the NATO Headquarters in Brussels, David Zalkaliani said that the illegal detention of Vazha Gapridashvili was one of the key issues of discussions with the Deputy Secretary General of NATO,MirceaGeoana. According to the Foreign Minister, all diplomatic formats have been activated to achieve an immediate release of the Georgian citizen. “I called on the OSCE chairman to take active steps on behalf of OSCE for the release of doctor. I also spoke to FedericaMogherini about the necessity of employing all resources available to the EU for the immediate release of Gaprindashvili. I communicated with the Deputy Secretary General of the UN and the UN together with its institutions is also engaged in efforts to resolve this issue. I discussed the issue with the NATO Deputy Secretary General as well. All the formats have been used and activated for the immediate release of the doctor. To put aside politics, the action is totally unacceptable from a humanitarian point of view. The doctor entering the occupied territory to assist the patient has been illegally taken hostage. He must be immediately released. We have relevant plans for any kind of scenario” - Zalkaliani said. Documents Similar News
