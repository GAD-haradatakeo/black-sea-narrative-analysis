19 December 2023 The deputy minister congratulated the Maltese diplomat on his appointment and wished him success in his future activities. At the meeting, the sides emphasized the importance of friendly and partner relations between Georgia and Malta, both at the bilateral level and within the framework of international organizations. Teimuraz Janjalia congratulated the Ambassador on Malta's chairmanship in the OSCE in 2024 and expressed hope that the issue of Georgia will remain high on the agenda. Special attention was focused on the importance of mutual exchange of high-level visits and regular political dialogue between the foreign agencies. The growing trade and economic relations and the need to fully utilize the existing potential were also emphasized. Noel Camilleri spoke about the deepening of cultural-educational and people-to-people ties between the two countries. The sides highlighted the process of Georgia's European integration and the progress achieved in this regard, especially after Georgia was granted the candidate status. The ambassador expressed his readiness to actively promote the bilateral relations between the two countries. He confirmed Malta's firm position towards Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity and emphasized that Malta actively continues to support Georgia's European integration.
