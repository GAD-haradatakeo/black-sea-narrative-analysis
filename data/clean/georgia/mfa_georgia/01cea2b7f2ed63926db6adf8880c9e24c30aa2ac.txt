4 August 2017 4.08.2017, Tbilisi - The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep sorrow over the death of the Junior Sergeant from Georgia, Mdinari Bebiashvili who was killed in an attack on Georgian soldiers. Junior Sergeant Mdinari Bebiashvili served in the 23rd infantry Battalion of the Georgian Armed Forces participating in the Resolute Support Mission, where Georgia as a future member and reliable partner of NATO makes its own contribution towards strengthening Euro-Atlantic security and anti-terrorism efforts. The death of Mdinari Bebiashvili will be a great loss to the Georgian Armed Forces. The Ministry wishes rapid recovery to those Georgian soldiers who were wounded in the Bargam attack.
