17 March 2022 The international community strongly condemns the so-called “parliamentary election” held in Georgia’s Abkhazia region on 12 March. The United States, the European Union, the European Parliament, the Council of Europe, NATO and the leading European countries made statements in this regard. Georgia's strategic partners do not recognize the legitimacy and results of the so-called parliamentary elections held on 12 March in the occupied Abkhazia region. Their position with regard to the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions remains unchanged. They reaffirm their strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders. The international community calls on Russia to fulfill its obligations under the 2008 ceasefire agreement, to withdraw its forces to the pre-conflict positions, to reverse its recognition of Georgia’s Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions as independent states and to ensure free access to humanitarian aid in the regions.
