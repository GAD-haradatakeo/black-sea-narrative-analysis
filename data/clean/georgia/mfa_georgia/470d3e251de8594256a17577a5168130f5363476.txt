24 May 2023 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the State Minister of Foreign Affairs of Qatar, Soltan bin Saad Al Muraikhi. The sides discussed issues on the agenda of bilateral relations between the countries and future priority directions in both bilateral and multilateral format. The State Minister of Foreign Affairs of Qatar once again emphasized his support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The dynamics of bilateral economic relations between Qatar and Georgia was positively assessed and plans were drawn up for the future deepening of investment and trade cooperation.
