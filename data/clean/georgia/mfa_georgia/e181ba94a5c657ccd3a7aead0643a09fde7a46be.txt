2 October 2020 I would like to express my deepest gratitude towards Ann Linde for visiting Georgia. As the global Pandemic continues to rage on, this visit, once again, reconfirms the special attitude of Sweden towards Georgia, along with all of the support! Our bilateral relations are connected to Georgia’s development and significant aspects of our European integration – It was, in fact, Sweden who was among one of the initiators of the Eastern Partnership Initiative. Additionally, along with political support, Sweden continues to show practical assistance to Georgia in terms of our reforms. My Swedish colleague’s visit really does coincide with a difficult period, especially with the escalating situation in the region. Naturally, we have discussed the current affairs. The existing conflicts in the region, once again, have us convinced of the significance of peace and stability for the development of our countries along with the entire region.
