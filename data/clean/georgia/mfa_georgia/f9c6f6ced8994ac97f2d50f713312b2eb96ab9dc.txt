5 April 2023 On 5 April 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Vladimer Konstantinidi held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Paraguay to Georgia (with residence in Ankara), Ceferino Valdez Peralta. Discussions focused on the issues on the agenda of bilateral and multilateral relations and on future prospects for cooperation. Special attention was paid to the positive dynamics of the political dialogue between Georgia and Paraguay and the progress achieved in this regard over the recent years. It was highlighted that there is an opportunity to further deepen the existing ties and legal framework in the fields of trade and economy, culture and education. The sides emphasized the positive cooperation between the two countries within the framework of international organizations and the importance of continuing close coordination in this regard. From 3 to 5 April, within the framework of the visit of the Paraguayan delegation to Georgia, a memorandum of understanding was signed between the Caucasus University of Georgia and San Sebastian University.
