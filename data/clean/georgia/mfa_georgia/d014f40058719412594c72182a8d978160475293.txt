14 December 2019 14.12.2019, Tbilisi – According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, there is no such high-ranking official, or international organization, or high-level format that has not reacted to the illegal detention of Vazha Gaprindashvili. Ministerial meetings have adopted joint statements calling for the immediate release of Vazha Gaprindashvili. “The country continues to further mobilize the international community. I remain in constant communication with the society, including with the family of the detained doctor. All international mechanisms have been put in action. There is no such high-ranking official, or international organization, or high-level format that has not reacted to the illegal detention of Vazha Gaprindashvili. Ministerial meetings have adopted joint statements calling for the immediate release of Vazha Gaprindashvili” – Zalkaliani said. According to him, despite the decision taken yesterday by the occupation regime, the country continues its efforts to achieve greater involvement of the international community and ensure that Vazha Gaprindashvili returns to his family as early as possible. “Based on the information available to me prior to the occupation regime’s so-called decision, my expectation was that Vazha Gaprindashvili would be released. He was not, however. But this should not give us the grounds for discontinuing our efforts to further engage the international community. We are already working towards this engagement starting from yesterday, in order to increase pressure and to ensure that Vazha Gaprindashvili returns to his family” – Zalkaliani said.
