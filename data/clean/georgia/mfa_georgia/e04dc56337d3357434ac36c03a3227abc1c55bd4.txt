25 August 2017 25.08.2017, Frankfurt - Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with Minister of European and Federal Affairs of Hessen Lucia Puttrich. The Sides discussed the existing close relations between Georgia and Germany and prospects for their further consolidation. Special attention was paid to the importance of Germany’s support for Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration and Georgia’s reforms in this process. While on the theme of bilateral relations, the sides highlighted the importance of raising awareness about Georgia in the land of Hessen. The Georgian Vice Premier and the Hessenian State Minister focused on the priorities of economic co-operation between the two countries. The German side expressed its interest in Georgia’s economic and investment potential. Mikheil Janelidze spoke about the situation in Georgia’s occupied Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions. Lucia Puttrich emphasized Georgia’s progress in various areas including in the implementation of the Association Agreement. This year has been declared as a year of friendship of Germany and Georgia. The sides welcomed the activities planned as part of the year of friendship. The sides also underlined the importance of Georgia’s status as the Guest of Honour at the 2018 Frankfurt Book Fair.
