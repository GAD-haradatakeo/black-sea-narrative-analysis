4 October 2019 Within the framework of the North Atlantic Council’s visit to Georgia, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with Deputy Secretary General of NATO RoseGottemoeller. The sides discussed Georgia’s progress on the Euro-Atlantic integration path and the ongoing comprehensive reforms in the country. David Zalkaliani updated RoseGottemoeller in detail on the renewed borderization process and the difficult humanitarian situation in the occupied territories. Conversations included a broad spectrum of issues related to Georgia’s integration with NATO.
