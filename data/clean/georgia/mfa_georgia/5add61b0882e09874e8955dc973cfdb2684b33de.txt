23 January 2023 On 23 January 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Bulgaria to Georgia, Veselin Valkanov. Highlighting the traditionally friendly relations of Georgia and Bulgaria, the sides focused on important issues relating to bilateral and multilateral cooperation and the perspectives of their further deepening. In light of the challenges facing the region, special attention was paid to the importance of the Black Sea, both in terms of security and connectivity. The necessity of further deepening cooperation between Georgia and Bulgaria as Black Sea countries was emphasized.
