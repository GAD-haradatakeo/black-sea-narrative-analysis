Another round of political consultations held between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and Saudi Arabia 17 February 2023 Within the framework of the meeting, the sides emphasized the Georgian Prime Minister’s successful visit to Riyadh in December last year and outlined plans for the timely implementation of the agreements reached within the visit. Special attention was paid to the prospects for the development of trade and economic relations between the two countries, successful cooperation in multilateral formats and the importance of people-to-people contacts.
