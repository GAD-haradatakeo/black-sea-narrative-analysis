15 February 2026 Within the framework of the Munich Security Conference, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili participated in a discussion dedicated to European Union enlargement. The meeting held in a roundtable format brought together the European Commissioner for Enlargement, Marta Kos, as well as the foreign ministers of European Union member states and candidate countries.
