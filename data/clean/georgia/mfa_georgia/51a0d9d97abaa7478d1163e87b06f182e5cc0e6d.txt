15 March 2018 15.03.2018, Baku – On the side-lines of the quadripartite meeting of the foreign ministers of Azerbaijan, Turkey, Iran, and Georgia, the Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze took part in the opening ceremony of the sixth global forum in Baku. The global forum was opened by President of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev. The Turkish Prime Minister and the Latvian President from 1997 to 2007 also spoke before the participants of the meeting that was attended by the world’s leading politicians and representatives of non-governmental organizations. A special address on behalf of the Roman Pope, UN Secretary General, British Prime Minister and Italian President was communicated among the participants of the Forum. In his speech, the President of Azerbaijan paid special attention to the issues relating to regional co-operation, highlighting, in this context, the importance of the quadripartite meeting of the foreign ministers of Azerbaijan, Turkey, Iran, and Georgia.
