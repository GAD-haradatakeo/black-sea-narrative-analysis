25 April 2018 25.04.2018, New York - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister, Mikheil Janelidze participated in the meeting organized by the Consulate General of Georgia in New York. The event was dedicated to the presentation of Georgia’s tourist potential. The meeting was attended by the local tour operators, tourism journalists and representatives of the business communities. Making a brief survey of Georgia’s tourism potential and the government’s efforts for its further development, the Georgian Foreign Minister emphasized that Georgia is transforming itself into an attractive tourism destination. At the end of the meeting, Georgia’s image video was presented to the attending audience. The meeting was attended by the Minister of Finance of Georgia, Mamuka Bakhtadze. Documents Similar News
