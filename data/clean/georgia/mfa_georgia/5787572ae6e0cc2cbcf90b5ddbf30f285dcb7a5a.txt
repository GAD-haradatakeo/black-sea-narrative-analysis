14 September 2018 14.09.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Secretary-General of the International Maritime Organization Kitack Lim. The Secretary General Georgia participated in the Georgia International Maritime Forum held in Batumi, from 12 to14 September 2018. Discussions focused on prospects for further deepening co-operation between Georgia and IMO. David Zalkaliani highlighted the importance of fruitful co-operation between the UN institutions, including IMO and the competent authorities of Georgia. According to Kitack Lim, Georgia with its achievements and reforms is role model in the region, as well as among the Black Sea countries.
