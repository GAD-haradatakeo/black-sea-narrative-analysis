7 May 2018 7.05.2018, Tbilisi – “I welcome the vision of the Ministry of Education regarding substantial reforms of the education sector. The education system of Georgia should be based on the best European practices” – The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze said following the presentation of the education reform action plan. The Minister paid special attention to the importance of vocational education and the effective use of European model in carrying out research programmes. Georgian Prime Minister Giorgi Kvirikashvili also spoke before the attending audience highlighting the ongoing reforms in the country and the importance of engaging young people in these processes. Georgian Minister of Education and Science Mikheil Chkhenkeli presented the action plan “12 Stages to Quality in Education” providing detailed information on the reforms planned in the education sector and prospects for deepening contacts with international organizations.
