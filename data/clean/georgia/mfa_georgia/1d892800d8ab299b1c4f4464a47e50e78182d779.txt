26 September 2024 On the sidelines of the 79th session of the UN General Assembly, Ilia Darchiashvili met with Ervin Ibrahimović, Montenegro's Deputy Prime Minister for International Relations and Minister of Foreign Affairs. During the meeting, the two ministers reviewed key aspects of bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Montenegro, emphasizing the potential for further development of relations. They highlighted the importance of high-level visits to strengthen ties. The ministers welcomed the successful and dynamic cooperation between the legislative bodies of Georgia and Montenegro. Minister Darchiashvili expressed his gratitude to his counterpart for Montenegro’s steadfast support for Georgia’s sovereignty, territorial integrity, and European integration. Both ministers underscored the importance of sharing experiences on the path toward European Union integration and welcomed the active use of various formats for collaboration in this regard.
