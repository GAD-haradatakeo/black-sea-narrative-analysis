28 March 2022 On 28 March2022, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Switzerland to Georgia Heidi Grau. The Minister congratulated the Ambassador on her appointment and expressed the hope that she will actively contribute to the further deepening of the close and friendly relations between the two countries. The Ambassador informed the Minister about her plans to deepen the political dialogue, as well as economic and sectoral cooperation. Emphasis was placed on the importance of scheduled visits and bilateral political consultations during the year, and on joint activities to mark the 30th anniversary of the resumption of diplomatic relations between the two countries. In addition to the agenda of bilateral relations, the sides discussed the fruitful cooperation established between the two countries within the framework of international organizations. The Minister and the Ambassador discussed the security situation in the region and Russia's large-scale war against Ukraine. The Minister briefed the Ambassador on Georgia's efforts to provide political and humanitarian assistance to Ukraine, special note was taken of Georgia’s support for Ukraine on various international platforms. Speaking about the security challenges facing Georgia, special attention was paid to the situation in the occupied regions of Georgia. David Zalkaliani noted that the ongoing war in Ukraine extends beyond the region and poses a threat to European security as a whole. In this regard, the importance of maintaining a strong and consistent position of the international community was underlined.
