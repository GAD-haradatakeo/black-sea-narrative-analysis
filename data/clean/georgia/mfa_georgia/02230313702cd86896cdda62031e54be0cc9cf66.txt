19 February 2020 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia has prepared the “2019 Compilation Report on the human rights violations in the Russia-occupied territories of Georgia”, which, to a great extent is based on the documents adopted within the frameworks of the international organizations. The Report aims to contribute to the provision of regular and updated information to the international community on the human rights situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. “2019 Compilation Report on the human rights violations in the Russia-occupied territories of Georgia” has been prepared in English and is available on the following link.
