3 September 2024 On 2 September 2024, Marge Mardisalu-Kahar, the ambassador-designate of Estonia to Georgia, presented her copies of credentials to Georgia's Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia. Teimuraz Janjalia congratulated the Estonian ambassador on her appointment and wished her success in her new role. During the meeting, both sides emphasized the traditionally strong and cooperative relations between Georgia and Estonia. They discussed the current bilateral and multilateral agenda and highlighted the importance of maintaining regular political dialogue.
