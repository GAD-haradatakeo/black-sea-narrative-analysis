24 February 2023 Within the framework of the UN General Assembly in New York, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with his Hungarian counterpart, Péter Szijjártó. Discussions focused on the current processes in the region, Georgia’s European integration agenda and Hungary's full support in this regard. The ministers talked about the existing cooperation between the countries, as well as their plans to conduct working and high-level meetings between the sectoral agencies and the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of the two countries.
