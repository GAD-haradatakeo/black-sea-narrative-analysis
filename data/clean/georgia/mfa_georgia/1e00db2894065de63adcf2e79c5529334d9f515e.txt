5 March 2024 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee of the Danish Parliament (Folketinget) Lise Beck and the accompanying delegation. The Minister of Foreign Affairs welcomed the visit of Danish parliamentarians to Georgia, which is a clear expression of Denmark's support for our country. The sides emphasized the opening of the diplomatic representation of Denmark in Georgia, which advanced the bilateral cooperation to a qualitatively newer level. The minister paid particular attention to Denmark's support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. The members of the parliamentary delegation emphasized the common values and interests of the two countries. Attention was focused on the process of Georgia's integration into the European Union. The sides noted the decision of the European Council to grant Georgia a candidate status, which brought Georgia-EU relations to a new stage. The Minister of Foreign Affairs highlighted the efforts and progress made by Georgia on the path of European integration.
