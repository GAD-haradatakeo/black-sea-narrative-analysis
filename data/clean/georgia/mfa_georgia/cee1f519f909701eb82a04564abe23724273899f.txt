6 May 2023 On 5 May 2023, the Minister of Foreign Affairs Ilia Darchiashvili held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of Norway to Georgia, Helene Sand Andresen. The sides talked about various issues of cooperation between Georgia and Norway. They noted the growing dynamics of bilateral relations between the two countries. Special note, in this regard, was taken of the Norwegian Foreign Minister's visit to Georgia and the importance of opening the Norwegian embassy in Georgia. The role of Helene Sand Andresen as the first resident ambassador in deepening relations between the two countries was also emphasized. The sides confirmed Norway's strong support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its Euro-Atlantic integration course. It was highlighted that Norway is one of the largest international donors of Georgia, whose contribution to the process of socio-economic modernization is highly appreciated by Georgia.
