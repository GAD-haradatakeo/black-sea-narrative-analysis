29 March 2019 29.03.2019, Tbilisi - Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with UNHCRDirector of the Bureau for Europe Pascale Moreau. The sides discussed issues relating to UNHCR activities. David Zalkaliani informed the UNHCRDirector on the severe human rights situation in occupied Abkhazia and Tskhinvali (South Ossetia) regions, as well as the alarmingly increasing number of illegal detentions and deprivation of life, the latest case of which is the death of Georgian citizen Irakli Kvaratskhelia in suspicious circumstances in Abkhazia. Conversations included cases of interference with property rights, restrictions on access to education in the native language, freedom of movement and residence, as well as continued discrimination on the grounds of ethnic origin in both regions. In this light the sides highlighted the importance of engagement of international organizations, including the UNHCR on the ground.
