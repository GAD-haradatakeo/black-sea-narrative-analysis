14 April 2020 Today, on 14 April, Tbilisi-Amsterdam-Tbilisi flight will be performed bringing home 184 Georgian citizens, including transit passengers, from the U.S., Belgium, Brazil, Germany, Denmark, Japan, Indonesia, Canada, Malta, Thailand and Norway. Among passengers there are under-age children and people with chronic or other grave health conditions. All returnees will be put into 14-day quarantine upon arrival in Georgia. Documents Similar News
