24 November 2017 24.11.2017, Brussels – “We have a very ambitious association agenda and we plan to do more than it is stipulated by the Association Agreement” - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze told journalists, on the sidelines of the 5th Eastern Partnership Summit in Brussels. The Georgian Prime Minister spoke anout the great potential and importance of the summit as well. According to him, the gathering of the EU and Eastern Partnership countries bears a particular weight for Georgia as, in addition to setting forth various plans for the future, “Georgia during this Summit presented itself as a successful country whose European choice and aspirations are strongly supported by the EU and the EU member states”. Mikheil Janelidze said that the Government of Georgia makes every effort to ensure that Georgia is ready for political decisions and is at the lead of European transformation. “With this Georgia will secure for itself a European future” – the Minister said.
