3 October 2018 3.10.2018, Tbilisi – On 3 October 2018, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with Jürgen Hardt, a member of the German Parliament, Spokesman for Foreign Policy of the CDU/CSU Parliamentary Group, who arrived in Georgia for a two-day visit to meet high-ranking representatives of the legislative and executive authorities. David Zalkaliani highly appraised the bilateral relations between Georgia and Germany highlighting the importance of the exchange of high- and highest level visits. He singled out for special mention the Federal Chancellor Angel Merkel’s visit as a further clear proof Germany’s support for Georgia, David Zalkaliani discussed in detail Georgia’s foreign policy priorities, including the process of European and Euro-Atlantic integration, the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and the government’s new peace initiative. In this light, David Zalkaliani expressed his gratitude for Germany’s firm support for Georgia. Discussions focused on the further development of Georgia-Germany co-operation. David Zalkaliani underlined the importance of Georgia’s status as the Guest of Honour at the 2018 Frankfurt Book Fair that will raise awareness of Georgia and its culture in Germany. Jürgen Hardt, for his part, reaffirmed Germany’s strong support for Georgia. According to him, Georgia is one of the closest and special partners in the region. The member of the German Parliament highlighted Georgia’s progress on the European integration path expressing his readiness to further promote bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Germany and to bring Georgia closer to the European structures.
