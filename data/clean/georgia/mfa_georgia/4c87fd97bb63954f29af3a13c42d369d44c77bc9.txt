21 September 2018 Georgia closely watches the processes ongoing on the Korean peninsula and welcomes the progress of the constructive dialogue aimed at complete denuclearization of the region. The third meeting of the Leaders of the Republic of Korea and theDemocratic People's Republic of Korea held from 18 to 20 September 2018, in Pyongyang, takes on a particular importance, in this context. Georgia supports solution of all problematic issues through diplomatic means and expresses the deep confidence that the high level dialogue will contribute to the strengthening of security and economic development in the region.
