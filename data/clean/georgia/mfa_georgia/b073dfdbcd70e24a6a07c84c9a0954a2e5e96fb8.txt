27 June 2017 27.06.2017, Vienna - On the sidelines of the Annual Security Review Conference (ASRC) in Vienna, Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the Head of the U.S. Delegation to the Conference, Deputy Assistant Secretary of State Kathleen Kavalec. The sides discussed issues relating to bilateral co-operation including within the format of OSCE.
