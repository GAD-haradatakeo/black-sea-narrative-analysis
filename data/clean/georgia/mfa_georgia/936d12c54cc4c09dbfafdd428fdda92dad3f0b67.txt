14 June 2022 On 14 June 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Head of the EU Monitoring Mission, Marek Schiegel. At the meeting, the Foreign Minister stressed the important role of the EU Monitoring Mission in preventing the escalation of the situation in the occupied regions of Georgia, including the efforts made within the framework of the Ergneti Incident Prevention and Response Mechanism. The Minister thanked the EU and EUMM monitors for their efforts to strengthen peace and security in the country and noted that the EU Monitoring Mission is an important mechanism for maintaining peace and stability on the ground, especially in the face of severe humanitarian and security situation further exacerbated by the occupying forces.
