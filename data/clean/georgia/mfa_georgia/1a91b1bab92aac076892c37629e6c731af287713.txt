24 July 2021 On 24 July, Georgia received 15,000 doses of the AstraZeneca vaccine as a donation from Lithuania. The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexander Khvtisiashvili and the Deputy Ambassador of Lithuania to Georgia attended the delivery of the vaccines at the Tbilisi International Airport. The Vice Prime Minister of Georgia, Minister of Foreign Affairs Davit Zalkaliani thanked the Lithuanian government for the donation. "15 000 doses of AstraZeneca Vaccine, allocated by the Lithuania government, have been delivered to Tbilisi, Georgia. A huge thank you to my colleague Gabrielius Landsbergis and all those involved in this process for their firm support and timely aid!", - wrote Georgian FM Davit Zalkaliani on Twitter.
