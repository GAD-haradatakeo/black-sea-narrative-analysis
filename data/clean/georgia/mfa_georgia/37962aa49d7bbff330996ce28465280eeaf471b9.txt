2 August 2018 02.08.2018, Tbilisi – Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Davit Zalkaliani held a meeting with Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Latvia to Georgia, Ingrīda Levrence. The sides noted with satisfaction the historically friendly relations and partnership between the two countries. The Minister singled out for special mention Latvia’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as its European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. The upcoming joint visit of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Latvia Edgars Rinkēvičs together with the foreign ministers of other countries on 6-7 August, ten years after the Russia-Georgia was considered as a clear proof of support and solidarity with Georgia. The sides positively appraised the trade-economic relations between the two countries and an increasing number of Latvian visitors to Georgia which is further encouraged by the introduction of direct flights. Concrete areas of were identified, including transport and logistics, education and environment where there is huge potential for the development of co-operation.
