15 October 2019 15.10.2019, Armenia – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met his Armenian counterpart, Foreign Minister ZohrabMnatsakanyan. The meeting was held within the framework of Georgian Prime Minister Giorgi Gakharia’s visit to the the Republic of Armenia. Discussing the issues relating to bilateral and multilateral cooperation between Georgia and Armenia, the sides paid special attention to the need of further deepening trade-economic relations and tourism cooperation. Discussions also focused on the security challenges facing the region. Noting with satisfaction the positive dynamics in the relations between the two countries, the two ministers highlighted the centuries-long friendship and good-neighbourly relations between Georgia and Armenia.
