8 April 2019 On 9 April 2019, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs will host representatives of the diplomatic corps accredited to Georgia to mark the 30th anniversary of 9 April 1989 – Day of Georgia’s National Unity. The foreign diplomats will have the opportunity to receive full information regarding the tragedy of 9 April 1989. The Director of the Strategic Research Centre, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Irakli Menagarishvili will share with the attending audience his views on the developments around 9 April. An exhibition of archival photos will also be held within the framework of the meeting. Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani will speak before the guests. 30 years have passed since 9 April 1989 – the day when the Soviet troops violently dispersed peaceful protesters rallying on Rustaveli Avenue.Thecrackdown resultedin casualties among civilians.
