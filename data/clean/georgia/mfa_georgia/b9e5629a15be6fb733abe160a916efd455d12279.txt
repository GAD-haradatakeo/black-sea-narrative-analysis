25 April 2018 25.04.2018, New York – The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the State Secretary for Foreign Policy, Bering Liisberg. Discussions focused on issues relating to co-operation within the international organizations. The Danish side was provided with information regarding the Georgian Government’s new peace initiative and the situation in the occupied territories. The sides stressed the importance of keeping the issue of Georgia under the spotlight of international community, as well as on the agenda of the UN Human Rights Council and the General Assembly. Talking points also included aspects of bilateral co-operation. The First Deputy Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani expressed his appreciation for Denmark’s support of Georgia on its path of European integration.
