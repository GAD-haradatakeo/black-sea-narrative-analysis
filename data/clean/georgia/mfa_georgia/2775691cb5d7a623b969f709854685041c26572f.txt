20 June 2017 19.06.2017 Luxembourg – Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with his Bulgarian counterpart Ekaterina Zaharieva on the sidelines of the eighth Eastern Partnership Foreign Ministerial Meeting held in Luxembourg. Mikheil Janelidze thanked Bulgaria for the support of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic aspirations. For her part, the Bulgarian Foreign Minister took special mention of Georgia’s impressive progress on the European integration path. The two Ministers highlighted friendly and partner relations between the two countries, including active cooperation in the trade and economic areas, and especially the fact that Bulgaria is among Georgia’s top ten trade partners.
