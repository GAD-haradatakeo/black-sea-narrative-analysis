17 August 2019 We express our concern over the fact that 7 Georgian citizens were kidnpped by the occupation forces from the territory near the Shida Kartli occupation line. Against the background of the escalation that arose due to the erection of artificial barriers in the village of Gugutiantkari, yet another fact of illegal detention and kidnapping of Georgian citizens by the occupation forces represents a clear attempt to further destabilize the situation. An increasing number of provocations and incidents across the occupation line further aggravates the security environment and the already difficult humanitarian situation in the conflict-affected villages. We immediately set up a hotline and contacted the co-chairs of the Geneva International Discussions. The EUMM is also engaged in the process. We call on the Russian Federation to immediately stop illegal activities across the occupation line and respect its international obligations. And we call on the Russian Federation to start unconditional implementation of the 12 August Ceasefire Agreement as withdrawal of the Russian troops from the Georgian territory is the only way to establish sustainable peace and security on the ground.
