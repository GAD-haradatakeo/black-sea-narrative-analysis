2 December 2019 2.12.2019, France - Within the framework of his visit to France, the Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani visited the Leuville Estate and the Leuville cemetery of Georgians. Paying tribute to the memory of the members of the government of the First Democratic Republic, David Zalkaliani laid wreathes of flowers on the graves of the Head of Government of the first Republic, Noe Zhordania and the Foreign Minister Evgeni Gegechkori. Zalkaliani also met with the granddaughter of the first President, Iren Tsitsishvili. According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, the Leuville Estate is a symbol of historically strong friendship between Georgia and France. Documents Similar News
