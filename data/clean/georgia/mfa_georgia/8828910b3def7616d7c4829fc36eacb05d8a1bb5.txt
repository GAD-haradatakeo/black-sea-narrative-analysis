14 October 2021 An honorary consulate of Georgia opened in the Slovenian city of Portoroz on 14 October, during the visit of the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani to Slovenia. Zalkaliani congratulated Honorary Consul Andrej Malgai on his appointment and emphasised the importance of the event. He spoke of the successful cooperation between Georgia and Slovenia in the fields of politics, economy, tourism and culture. The Foreign Minister highlighted the role of the Honorary Consulate in deepening trade, investment and interpersonal relations between Georgia and Slovenia. The Ambassador of Georgia to the Republic of Slovenia, Irakli Koplatadze and the Honorary Consul, Andrei Malgai addressed the audience.
