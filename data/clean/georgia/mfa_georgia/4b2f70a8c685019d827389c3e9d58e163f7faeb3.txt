26 September 2023 As part of his working visit to Berlin, on 26 September 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia held meetings with the European Policy DG of the Federal Chancellery of Germany, Edgar Lenski and the Minister of State at the Federal Foreign Office, Tobias Lindner. The main goal of the Deputy Minister's visit to Berlin is to present Georgia’s progress on the EU integration path and to secure Germany’s support in this regard. The sides highlighted Georgia’s progress on the implementation of the 12 priorities defined by the European Commission and the critical importance of granting Georgia the candidate status at the end of the year. Teimuraz Janjalia emphasized the need for the support of EU member states, including the Federal Republic of Germany, at this important stage for Georgia. The sides noted with satisfaction that the development of bilateral relations between the two countries reached an unprecedented level this year. The sides exchanged opinions on regional and global current issues. Special attention was paid to the ongoing events in the region and the challenges arising from the war waged by Russia in Ukraine. The Deputy Minister thanked his German colleagues for their firm support of the territorial integrity and sovereignty of Georgia, as well as for the foreign policy goals of our country.
