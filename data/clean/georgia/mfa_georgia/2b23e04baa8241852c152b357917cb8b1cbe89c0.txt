20 October 2025 The Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of Georgia to the Republic of Bulgaria, Zurab Khamashuridze, presented his credentials to the President of Bulgaria, Rumen Radev. Following the credentials ceremony, the Ambassador held a meeting with the President. The parties discussed the dynamics of bilateral cooperation between Georgia and Bulgaria and explored prospects for its further development. The meeting also addressed Georgia’s European integration process. Attention was focused on the security environment in the region.
