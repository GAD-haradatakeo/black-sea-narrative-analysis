5 March 2025 On 5 March 2025, non-resident ambassadors accredited to Georgia from the Kingdom of Belgium, the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, the Republic of Cyprus, the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Republic of Tajikistan, and the Republic of Guatemala presented copies of their Letters of Credence to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Zurabashvili. After the presentation of the Letters of Credence, the Deputy Minister held meetings with the ambassadors, congratulating them on the commencement of their diplomatic missions and wishing them success in their future endeavors. The meetings focused on bilateral and multilateral cooperation issues and the prospects for further developing relations. The parties confirmed their readiness to deepen and intensify cooperation between the countries.
