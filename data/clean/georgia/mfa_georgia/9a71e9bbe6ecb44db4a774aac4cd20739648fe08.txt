7 December 2018 7.12.2018, Milan - On the sidelines of the 25th OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with his Slovak counterpart MiroslavLajčák. The Ministers welcomed Slovak’s upcoming chairmanship of OSCE and its priorities, including the conflicts in the OSCE area. The Georgian delegation highlighted the importance of OSCE’s participation in the peaceful resolution of the Georgia-Russia conflict. Special attention was paid to the grave humanitarian situation in Georgia's occupied territories. The Ministers discussed the issues relating to bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Slovakia.
