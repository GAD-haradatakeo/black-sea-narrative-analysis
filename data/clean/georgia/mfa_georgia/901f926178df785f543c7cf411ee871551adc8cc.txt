11 July 2024 On 11 July 2024, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili met with a delegation from the Foreign Relations Committee of the Parliament of the Kingdom of the Netherlands. During their meeting, both parties expressed satisfaction with the longstanding friendly relations and extensive cooperation between the two countries. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili thanked the Dutch delegation for their support of Georgia's sovereignty, territorial integrity, and its European and Euro-Atlantic integration efforts. The discussion focused on Georgia's integration into the European Union. The deputy minister highlighted the country's unwavering commitment to its European path and updated the Dutch MPs on the measures undertaken in line with the Association Agreement obligations and the European Commission's recommendations. The Georgian side underscored the importance of continued support from partner countries, including the Netherlands, to advance to the next stage of the accession process successfully. They also discussed the security situation in the region and related challenges. Additionally, the conversation covered Georgia's internal political developments and the upcoming parliamentary elections.
