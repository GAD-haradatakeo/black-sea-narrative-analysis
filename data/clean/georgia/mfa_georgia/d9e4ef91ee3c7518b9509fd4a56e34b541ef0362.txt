16 May 2023 On 15 May 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the participants of the Global Strategy Program (GSP) of the Royal College of Defense Studies (RCDS) of Great Britain. Within the framework of the meeting, the First Deputy Minister discussed the priorities of Georgia's foreign policy and talked about the challenges facing the country. Particular attention was paid to the progress achieved by the country on the path of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration and the reform agenda. Lasha Darsalia informed the RCDS representatives about the difficult situation in the occupied territories of Georgia, which is the result of the full-scale military aggression carried out by Russia in Georgia in 2008.
