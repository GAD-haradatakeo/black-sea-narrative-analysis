15 July 2025 On 14 July 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, together with the Chargé d’Affaires of Georgia to the Arab Republic of Egypt, Mikheil Tigishvili, held a meeting with Mohamed Sayed, the owner of “Checkpoint Wear,” one of Egypt’s largest sportswear manufacturers. Mr. Sayed has expressed a firm intention to invest in Georgia and to establish a premium-quality sportswear production facility in the country. Checkpoint Wear specializes in the production of high-end sports apparel for the American and European markets, supplying major brands and organizations such as the NBA, NFL, MLS, NHL, UMBRO, ZARA, and FILA, among others. The meeting focused on Mr. Sayed’s plans to launch a new manufacturing plant in Georgia and to export the locally produced goods not only to traditional markets but also to emerging ones in the South Caucasus, Central Asia, and the Russian Federation.
