8 October 2017 8.10.2017, Tbilisi – The Foreign Ministers of Georgia, Ukraine, Moldova and Azerbaijan signed a joint communique within the framework of the special meeting of the GUAM Council of Foreign Ministers. The Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze welcomed the adoption of the document saying that the Joint Communique reflects the priorities to be discussed during the anniversary meeting of the GUAM Council. “The Organization has been progressing over the past 20 years. And today we have gathered to discuss the future of GUAM. The Joint Communique and discussions we have held during the day serve this very purpose” – Mikheil Janelidze said. Documents Similar News
