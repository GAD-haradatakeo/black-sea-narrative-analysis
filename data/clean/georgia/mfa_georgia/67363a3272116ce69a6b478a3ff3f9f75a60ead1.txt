3 October 2017 03.10.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze took office as the OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media Harlem Désir. Discussions during the meeting focused on close and fruitful co-operation between Georgia and the Office of OSCE Representative on Freedom of the Media. Mikheil Janelidze spoke about the steps taken by the Government towards media freedom and the progress achieved in this regard. Special attention was paid to the constitutional amendments designed to further improve the pluralist environment.
