18 June 2021 Within the framework of the Antalya Diplomatic Forum, the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, held a meeting with the Deputy Secretary General of the UN, Miroslav Yenca. The topics of discussion during the meeting were the following: The dire humanitarian situation on the ground in the occupied regions of Georgia and human rights violations; The significant role of the UN within the Geneva International Discussions Format; The issue of illegal arrests of Georgian citizens by the occupational regime; Particular attention was paid to the topic of Zaza Gakheladze.
