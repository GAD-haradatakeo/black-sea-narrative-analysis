3 August 2017 3.08.2017, Tbilisi – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia closely watches the ongoing processes in Venezuela expressing its concern over an increasing number of violations of the Venezuelan people’s rights and freedom of election. The elections of the Constituent Assembly on 30 July 2017 provoked broad international criticism. Georgia fully shares the international community’s strict condemnation of the acts of violence resulting in the death of people in Venezuela. It is an alarming fact that the so-called elections conducted on 30 July are intended to terminate the democratically elected national assembly and further, to rewrite the constitution of Venezuela in an attempt to disproportionately expand the powers of the executive authorities.
