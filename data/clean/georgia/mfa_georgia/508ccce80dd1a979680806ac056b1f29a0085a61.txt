28 May 2020 On 27 May 2020, the Committee of Ministers of the Council of Europe held its first video conference under the Greek presidency. During the conference, the CoE Presidency’s symbolic key was handed over to Georgia’s Permanent Representative to the Council of Europe, Irakli Giviashvili. The traditional handover was performed by the Greek Ambassador to the Council of Europe who spoke about the term of Georgian presidency which coincided with the 20th anniversary of Georgia’s accession to the Organization. The Greek Ambassador thanked Georgia’s Permanent Representative to the Council of Europe for the successful activities and important results achieved under the Georgian Presidency For his part, Irakli Giviashvili thanked his colleagues for firm support. He also expressed his gratitude to the Secretariat of the Committee of Ministers and various structures of the Organization for excellent cooperation that greatly contributed to the success of the Georgian Presidency.
