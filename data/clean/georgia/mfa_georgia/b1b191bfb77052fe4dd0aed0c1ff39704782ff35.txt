1 July 2021 Discussions focused on the pandemic situation in Georgia and the Czech Republic, the ongoing vaccination process, regulations for entry into the country, the EU digital Covid certification system and the ways to use and join it, as well as the mutual recognition of vaccination certificates. During the meeting, the Czech side reaffirmed its willingness to support Georgia in the fight against the pandemic and during the vaccination process.
