4 April 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Ilia Darchiashvili had a telephone conversation with his Azerbaijani counterpart Jeyhun Bairamov. The Azerbaijani side congratulated the Georgian Minister on his appointment and wished him success in his future endeavors. Focusing on the strategic partnership between Georgia and Azerbaijan, the two ministers highlighted once again the close and increasingly dynamic cooperation between the two countries. The ministers reviewed the situation in the region and the agenda of bilateral relations. The sides highly appraised Georgia's role in promoting the sustainable stability and development of the South Caucasus region. The Ministers expressed their readiness to actively cooperate for developing further the traditionally friendly and partnership relations between the two countries.
