16 February 2020 16.02.2020, Munich – “We had a very fruitful meeting. I am in love with Tbilisi, Georgian people. Georgia is a wonderful country. We, Germans, as part of Europe, must support Georgia in addressing the challenges it is facing. We should not let Russia think that everything has ended and that Georgia should accept the occupation of Abkhazia and South Ossetia and the existence of thousands of IDPs” - Claudia Roth,Vice-Presidentof the German Bundestag said following the Munich Security Conference. According to Claudia Roth, what is taking place in the Georgian territories is totally unacceptable. “Refugees from the occupied territories have lost everything. I met people in the villages who were crying for their inability to return to their homes. I also have information about abductions of citizens. We Germans as Europeans must be more vocal. We should not focus only on Ukraine as the reality in Georgia is the same as in Ukraine” – she said.
