28 June 2021 “Georgia is an active and significant member of the global anti-terrorism coalition. We participate, along with our partners, in the fight against this challenge and even today, as the peacekeeping mission in Afghanistan draws to a close, Georgia is responsible for the significant function, with coalition members using Georgia as a transit country to transport cargo and personnel from Afghanistan” – stated the Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani, in his address made to the meeting of the global coalition held in Rome. According to the Minister Georgia is a significant contributor to the process of ensuring global security.
