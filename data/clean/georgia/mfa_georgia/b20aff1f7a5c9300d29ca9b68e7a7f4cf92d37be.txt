17 February 2021 On 17 February 2021, the Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the heads of the US, German, UK, French and EU missions to Georgia. Within the framework of the meeting David Zalkaliani provided information to ambassadors regarding the militarisation of occupied Georgian regions by Russia along with the dire humanitarian situation on the ground. The minister made particular emphasis on the recent activation of destructive actions by the Tskhinvali region occupational regime, including, in terms of the provocation of the Russian Federation – the illicit arrest of a Georgian citizen, Zaza Gakheladze, sentencing him to more than 12 years in prison. The illegally arrested Georgian citizen must be freed immediately. Additionally, during the discussion regarding the steps taken on behalf of the Russian federation in terms of annexation of Georgia’s occupier regions and more frequent cases of human rights violations, the significance of engagement of the international community was underscored. In this regard, particular emphasis was made by both parties on the importance of active cooperation in all international formats and organisations.
