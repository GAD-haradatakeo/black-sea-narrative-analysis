21 February 2022 The Council of Europe Commissioner for Human Rights, Dunja Mijatović begins her working visit to Georgia, on 21 February. During the visit, meetings will be held with the President of Georgia, the Prime Minister, the Speaker of the Parliament, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs, the Minister of Internal Affairs, the Minister of Environment and Agriculture, the State Minister for Reconciliation and Civic Equality, the Chairman of the Government of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia, the Deputy Ministers of Refugees, Labour, Health and Social Affairs. At the end of the visit, the Commissioner for Human Rights will issue a press release followed in a few months by a report together with relevant recommendations. Documents Similar News
