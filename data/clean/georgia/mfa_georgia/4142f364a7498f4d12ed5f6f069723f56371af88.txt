1 May 2025 On 1 May 2025, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Turkey, Archil Kalandia, presented his credentials to the President of the Republic of Turkey, Recep Tayyip Erdoğan. The official credential presentation ceremony took place at the Presidential Palace of Turkey. Following the ceremony, Ambassador Kalandia and President Erdoğan held a tête-à-tête meeting, during which they discussed the strategic partnership between Georgia and Turkey.
