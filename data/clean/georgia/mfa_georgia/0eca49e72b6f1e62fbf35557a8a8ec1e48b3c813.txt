19 July 2017 19.07.2017, Tbilisi –The second panel held within Ambassadorial 2017 was dedicated to democratic development of Georgia. Speaker of the Parliament of Georgia Irakli Kobakhidze and Minister of Justice Tea Tsulukiani spoke before the attending audience. The panel was moderated by Georgian Vice Premier, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze. The Speaker of the Parliament of Georgia extensively spoke about the Georgian Parliament’s main goals and the ongoing process of making amendments to the Constitution. The Justice Minister focused on the priorities of the Georgian chairmanship of the Open Government Partnership, as well as on the ongoing human rights and justice reforms which have already translated into tangible results in terms of developing the independent justice system.
