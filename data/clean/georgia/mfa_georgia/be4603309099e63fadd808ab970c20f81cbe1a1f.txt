26 January 2023 Within the framework of his visit to the United Kingdom, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili met with Jonathan Djanogly, the Chairman of the UK All-Party Parliamentary Group on Georgia. At the meeting, the sides welcomed the strong inter-parliamentary relations between Georgia and the United Kingdom and the support the British Parliament demonstrates to Georgia. Discussions also focused on the visit of the UK All-Party Parliamentary Group on Georgia. The sides welcomed the format of Wardrop's strategic dialogue and highly appraised the meetings taking place within the dialogue, as well as its parliamentary dimension.
