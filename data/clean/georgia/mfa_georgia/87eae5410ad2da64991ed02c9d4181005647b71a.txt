18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan to Georgia, Omar Barakat Mnawer Al Nahar. The meeting addressed current issues of Georgia-Jordan relations and the prospects for future cooperation. The traditionally close and friendly ties between the two countries were highlighted. Both parties expressed their readiness to continue active cooperation in both bilateral and multilateral formats. Within the framework of the visit, the Ambassador of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, Omar Barakat Mnawer Al-Nahar, presented copies of his Letters of Credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Directorate of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. The parties welcomed the friendly relations and close cooperation existing between Georgia and Jordan.
