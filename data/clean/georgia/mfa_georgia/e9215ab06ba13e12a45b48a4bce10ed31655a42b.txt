4 November 2019 4.11.2019, Sweden – Within the framework of his visit to the Kingdom of Sweden, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with the Swedish MinisterforDefence, Peter Hultqvist. The Ministers highly appraised defence cooperation between the two countries including by Sweden’s active involvement in defence education, exchange of experts, etc. David Zalkaliani thanked the Swedish Defence Minister for Georgia’s active support within the international forums and formats, as well as at the level of bilateral relations. The Parties paid special attention to the process of Georgia’s integration with the EU and NATO. Defence and security cooperation with the EU in the form of high-level strategic dialogue on security issues was also appraised as successful. The Parties also gave high appraisal to Georgia’s participation in the EU-led missions in Mali and Central Africa. Within the scope of his visit to Sweden, Zalkaliani also met with the Swedish Minister of Justice and Migration to discuss the issues of bilateral cooperation and Georgia’s successful reforms in the process of visa liberalization.
