27 March 2020 On 27 March 2020, Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg, at the latter’s initiative. Stoltenberg informed the Minister that meetings ofNATO MinistersofForeignAffairs will be held in a restricted format on 3 and 4 April. According to the Secretary General, Georgia tackles the pandemic successfully and will hopefully continue to do so. For his part, Zalkaliani updated Stoltenberg on the Georgian Government’s measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19. Jens Stoltenberg reaffirmed NATO’s support for and solidarity with Georgia in its efforts to counter COVID-19. Conversations also included various areas of NATO-Georgia cooperation. The NATO Secretary General welcomed the democratization processes ongoing in Georgia. In the words of Stoltenberg, NATO will continue to undertake additional measures in support for Georgia. The parties stressed that the existing situation will not prevent NATO and Georgia from continuing their cooperation.
