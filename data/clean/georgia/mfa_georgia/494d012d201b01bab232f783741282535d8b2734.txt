24 June 2022 On 24 June 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs and Trade of Hungary,Péter Szijjártó.will visit Georgia. During the visit, the Hungarian Foreign Minister will hold meetings with the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Garibashvili, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili and the Minister of Economy and Sustainable Development Levan Davitashvili.
