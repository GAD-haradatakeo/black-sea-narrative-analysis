21 May 2018 On the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the restoration of Georgia’s independence, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of the Netherlands, Stef Blok sent a congratulation letter to the Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze. “One hundred years ago, your country laid the social and democratic foundations on which modern-day Georgia rests” – reads the letter. According to the Foreign Minister of the Netherlands, Georgia is steadily increasing its links with Europe – “A path the Netherlands fully supports”. According to the letter, the Netherlands would like to reiterate its support for Georgia’s territorial integrity and diplomatic efforts to ensure a peaceful resolution to this issue. This includes the work undertaken through the Geneva International Discussions and bilateral efforts such as the government’s recent peace initiative “A Step to a Better Future”.
