5 December 2019 05.12.2019, Bratislava – Discussing the agenda of bilateral relations, the parties positively appraised the progress of cooperation between the two countries, the exchange of high-level visits and mutual support. Highlighting the importance of Poland’s support to Georgia both in bilateral and multilateral formats, Zalkaliani thanked his Polish counterpart for Poland’s strong support of Georgia within OSCE. The two ministers also discussed the recent developments in Georgia’s occupied regions and the illegal detention of Vazha Gafrindashvili by the occupation regime. The parties expressed their readiness to actively cooperate both for the strengthening of strategic partnership, as well as for the implementation of Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic agenda and goals.
