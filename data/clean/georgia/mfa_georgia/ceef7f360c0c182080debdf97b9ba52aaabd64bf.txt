17 November 2021 On 15 November 2021, within the framework of the Eastern Partnership Ministerial Meeting in Brussels, the Vice Prime Minister /Foreign Minister of Georgia, David Zalkaliani, had a conversation with his Azerbaijani and Armenian counterparts, Jeyhun Bairamov and Ararat Mirzoyan. While discussing the situation in the region, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia reiterated the importance of strengthening peace and stability in the region and de-escalating the tense situation between the neighboring countries. David Zalkaliani reaffirmed Georgia’s readiness to spare no effort in the future to contribute to the process of strengthening long-term peace and stability in the region. Documents Similar News
