Georgia and Latvia have signed a Joint Statement on the Occasion of the 25th Anniversary of Diplomatic Relations between the Republic of Latvia and Georgia 28 September 2018 Georgia and Latvia signed a joint statement on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of Diplomatic Relations between the Republic of Latvia and Georgia, within the framework of the 73rd session of the UN General Assembly in New York. The declaration was signed during the bilateral meeting between the Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and his Latvian counterpart, Edgars Rinkēvičs. See All
