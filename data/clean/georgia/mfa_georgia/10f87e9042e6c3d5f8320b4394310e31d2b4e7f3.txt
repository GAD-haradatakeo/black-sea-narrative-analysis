3 September 2024 On 3 September 2024, Ahmad Kamrizamil bin Mohd Riza, the Ambassador-designate of Malaysia to Georgia (residing in Baku), presented copies of his credentials to Khatuna Totladze, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia. Following the credential presentation, the two officials discussed various aspects of bilateral relations, including cooperation in the fields of economy, trade, tourism, and education, and explored opportunities for further development. They also focused on improving the bilateral legal framework. Khatuna Totladze expressed gratitude to Malaysia for its support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders.
