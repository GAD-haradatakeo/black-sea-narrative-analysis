28 September 2024 Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, visited the newly opened reception area at the Consulate General of Georgia in New York. The modern space, designed to meet international standards, features three independent service windows, enabling the consulate to deliver faster and more efficient services to Georgian citizens residing within its consular jurisdiction.
