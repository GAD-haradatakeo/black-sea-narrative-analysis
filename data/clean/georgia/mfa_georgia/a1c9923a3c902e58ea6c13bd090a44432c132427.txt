21 October 2022 On October 21, 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia Lasha Darsalia met with the Head of the Delegation of the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) in Georgia Anne Montavon. The representative of the Delegation of the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) in Georgia based in Sokhumi, Rodrigo Stanger Martins Pinto De Rezende participated in the meeting. The difficult security, humanitarian and human rights situation in the occupied regions of Georgia was discussed at the meeting, which took place upon Rodrigo Stanger Martins Pinto De Rezende’s return from Sokhumi. In this regard, the important role of the work of the Delegation of the ICRC in Georgia was highlighted in terms of improvement of the humanitarian situation of the people affected by the Russia-Georgia conflict. Hereby, the attempts by the Russian occupation regime to hamper and politicize the humanitarian activities of international organizations on the ground were stressed, underlining that those actions further harm the dire humanitarian and human rights situation on the ground. The First Deputy Minister expressed his appreciation for the humanitarian efforts of the ICRC Delegation in Georgia, particularly in supporting people in Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions of Georgia.
