16 February 2026 On 16 February 2026, within the framework of her bilateral visit to the Slovak Republic, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign and European Affairs of Slovakia, Juraj Blanár. The parties discussed current issues in bilateral relations and prospects for deepening cooperation. Emphasis was placed on the active dynamics of political dialogue and bilateral visits. The discussion focused on deepening trade and economic relations and fully leveraging the existing potential. The Ministers also addressed issues related to Georgia’s European integration. Maka Botchorishvili emphasized that Slovakia’s steadfast support for Georgia’s European aspirations is particularly valuable. The discussion also addressed security challenges in the region, with specific reference to the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories.
