12 July 2025 Within the framework of the 2025 Dubrovnik Forum, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Igli Hasani, Minister for Europe and Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Albania. The ministers discussed the current agenda of bilateral relations between Georgia and Albania, as well as prospects for cooperation within international formats. They underlined the importance of fostering political dialogue and exchanging high-level visits. In the context of bilateral cooperation, special emphasis was placed on strengthening ties in the areas of trade, economy, tourism, and people-to-people contacts. The parties also exchanged views on the European Union enlargement policy and the prospects for cooperation in this regard. In this context, they discussed the reforms undertaken by both countries and the challenges that remain.
