18 June 2024 On 18 June 2024, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili met with Ben Malunga Phiri, head of the Friendship Group with Georgia, along with other members of the delegation in the Parliament of the Republic of Malawi. The parties highlighted the positive and growing dynamics of their bilateral relations, discussing the development prospects of Georgia-Malawi cooperation. Special attention was given to the bilateral legal framework and sharing Georgia's expertise with Malawi in agriculture, food security, education, information technology, and tourism.
