30 November 2023 On 30 November 2023, within the framework of the ongoing OSCE ministerial Council in Skopje, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with his Norwegian counterpart, Espen Bart Eide. Ilia Darchiashvili congratulated his colleague on his recent appointment as Foreign Minister and wished him success in his future work. During the meeting, the sides highly appraised the friendly and close cooperation between the two countries in both bilateral and multilateral formats. In this regard, they stressed the importance of opening the Norwegian embassy in Tbilisi this year and noted that the opening of the diplomatic representation of Norway in Georgia is another clear proof of successful cooperation between the two countries. Ilia Darchiashvili stressed that Georgia highly appreciates Norway as one of the major international donors, which has contributed to institutional modernization and economic development in the country.
