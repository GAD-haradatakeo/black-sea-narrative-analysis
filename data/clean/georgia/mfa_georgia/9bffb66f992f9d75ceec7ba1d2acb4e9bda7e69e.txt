30 March 2021 The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Estonian, Latvian and Lithuanian Ambassadors in Georgia. Discussions focused on close cooperation between the Baltic countries and Georgia within the framework of international organizations. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister updated the ambassadors on the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories.
