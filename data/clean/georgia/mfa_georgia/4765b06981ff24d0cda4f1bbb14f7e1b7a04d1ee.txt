1 June 2017 One of Saudi Arabia’s leading magazines SAUDDI GAZETTE has published yet another article about Georgia titled “Georgia – the Pearl of the Caucasus”. The article describes Tbilisi, the capital of Georgia as one of the places famous for its picturesque nature, unique culture and rich heritage, which is an attractive tourist destination because of its warm hospitality, exotic cuisine and affordable prices.
