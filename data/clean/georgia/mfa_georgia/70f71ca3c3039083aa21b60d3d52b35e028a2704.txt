13 April 2023 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia participated in the first Black Sea Security Conference held in Bucharest within the framework of the Crimean Platform. The conference was jointly organized by Romania and Ukraine. The focus of discussions during the Conference was on the existing security challenges fa the Black Sea region, including hybrid threats. The necessity of cooperation on cyber security, energy and economic spheres was emphasized. In his speech, Lasha Darsalia spoke about the prospects of cooperation and the possibility of joint actions to strengthen the Black Sea security. Discussions also focused on the steps taken by Georgia on the path of European and Euro-Atlantic integration.
