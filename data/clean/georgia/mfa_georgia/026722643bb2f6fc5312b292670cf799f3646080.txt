19 December 2022 On 19 December 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held his first meeting with the Ambassador of the United Arab Emirates to Georgia, Ahmed Ibrahim Ahmed Taher Alnuaimi. The Minister congratulated the Ambassador on the start of his diplomatic mission in Georgia and wished him success in his future activities. During the meeting, the sides noted with satisfaction that relations between the two countries are intensively developing in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The importance of exchanging high-level visits was also noted. Special attention was paid to the need of further deepening the relations in the fields of trade, economy and tourism.
