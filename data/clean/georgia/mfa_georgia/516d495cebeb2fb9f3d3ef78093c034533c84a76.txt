15 November 2024 On 15 November 2024, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with Claudio Cajado Sampaio, Head of the Friendship Group with Georgia at Chamber of Deputies of the National Congress of Brazil, who was on an official visit to Georgia. During the meeting, the friendly relations between Georgia and Brazil were underscored, and key aspects of cooperation were discussed in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The importance of high-level exchange visits and the role of parliamentary diplomacy in fostering bilateral relations were highlighted. The meeting also noted the successful cooperation between the two countries within international organizations and emphasized the importance of continued mutual support. Claudio Cajado reaffirmed Brazil's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
