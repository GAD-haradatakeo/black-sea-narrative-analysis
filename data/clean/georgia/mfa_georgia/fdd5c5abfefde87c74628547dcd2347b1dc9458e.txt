4 April 2018 4.04.2018, Washington – Within the framework of his working visit to Washington, Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with the U.S.Assistant Secretary of State for European and Eurasian Affairs, Wess Mitchell. Wess Mitchell reaffirmed the U.S. support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its Euro-Atlantic integration and democratic and economic development. The sides discussed the Georgian government’s peace initiatives and the engagement policy with respect to the occupied territories aimed at improving the humanitarian and social-economic situation of people living in the Abkhazia and Tskhinvali regions, as well as intensifying the contacts across the dividing lines.
