13 May 2017 12.05.2017, New York – Within the framework of his visit to New York, Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze held a meeting with Managing Director of Jigsaw Think Tank, Scott Carpenter. Discussions focused on Georgia’s successful reforms and the progress the country has achieved in terms of Internet freedom. It was emphasized that Georgia ranks as a free country for Interned freedom. The sides spoke about the importance of modern technologies in countering propaganda and misinformation. The need of strengthening cyber-security and ensuring the smooth functioning of key information systems, against the backdrop of large-scale cyber-terrorism was also highlighted. Jigsaw is a think-tank created by Google. Based in New York City, Jigsaw is dedicated to understanding global challenges and applying technological solutions, from countering extremism, online censorship and cybe-attacks, to protecting access to information.
