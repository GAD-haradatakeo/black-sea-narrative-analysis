28 November 2017 28.11.2017, Tbilisi - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze gave a summary of Georgia-Slovenia relations in bilateral and multilateral formats during a joint press-conference with his Slovenian counterpart Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, Karl Erjavec. According to Mikheil Janelidze, over the 25 years of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Slovenia, the two countries enjoy excellent political relations and successful co-operation in the areas of economy, defence, education and culture. Mikheil Janelidze said that the favourable background to the increasing dynamics of relations and the implementation of investments is provided by the visit of representatives of the Slovenian Companies to Georgia and the conduction of a business forum. The Georgian Foreign Minister thanked his Slovenian counterpart for his strong support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for its European and Euro-Atlantic integration. According to the Minister, Slovenia expressed the readiness to render expert and practical support to Georgia in terms of conducting reforms and ensuring integration with the EU. “We welcome political, practical and expert support from the EU and its member states, including Slovenia.
