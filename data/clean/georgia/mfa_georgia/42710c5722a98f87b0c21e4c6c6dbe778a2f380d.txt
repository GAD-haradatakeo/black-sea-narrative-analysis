22 January 2026 On 22 January 2026, the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Carlos Ricardo Martins Ceglia, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Federative Republic of Brazil to Georgia. During the meeting, the parties discussed the dynamics of ongoing cooperation between the two countries, as well as prospects for further deepening their partnership in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The discussion also addressed the regional and global security environment and related challenges. Particular emphasis was placed on firm support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. It was noted that Brazil is an important partner of Georgia and the only state from the Latin American region that maintains a diplomatic representation in Georgia.
