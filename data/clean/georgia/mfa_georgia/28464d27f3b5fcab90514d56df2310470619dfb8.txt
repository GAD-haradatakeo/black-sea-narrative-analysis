12 May 2022 On 11 May, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador of Poland to Georgia Mariusz Maszkiewicz. Discussing the close Georgia-Poland bilateral and multilateral relations, the sides paid special attention to the issues of cooperation during the Polish chairmanship in the OSCE, as well as various aspects of regional security. The conversation focused on the current situation in the region in the light of Russia’s large-scale aggression against Ukraine.
