5 December 2018 “U.S. and NATO Allies strongly support the sovereignty, territorial integrity, and NATO aspirations of Georgia and Ukraine. We value their contributions and cooperation and are encouraged by progress made toward meeting NATO standards and values,” Pompeo said Mike Pompeo, United States Secretary of State wrote on Twitter. The North Atlantic Council held a meeting with Georgia and Ukraine within the framework of the NATO Ministerial on 4 December, in Brussels. Discussions during the meeting focused on issues relating to Georgia’s Euro-Atlantic integration, regional security and defence reforms. Documents Similar News
