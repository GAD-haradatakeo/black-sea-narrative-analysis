6 September 2022 Within the framework of the Georgia-EU Association Council in Brussels, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with MEP Sven Mikser, the European Parliament’s rapporteur on Georgia. The sides welcomed Georgia's fruitful cooperation with the European Parliament and the European Parliament's firm support for Georgia's European integration, as well as for its sovereignty and territorial integrity. Discussions focused on the progress in the implementation of the association agenda by Georgia. In this regard, the sides emphasized the importance of reports by the European Parliament’s rapporteurs. The minister expressed the hope that the European Parliament will properly assess Georgia's progress on the path of European integration and will contribute to the process of granting the country a candidate status. The sides highlighted the importance of the European Commission’s historical decision to grant Georgia a European perspective, and Sven Mikser was informed about progress in the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations.
