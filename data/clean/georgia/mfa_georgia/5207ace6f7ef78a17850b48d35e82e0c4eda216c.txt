12 June 2018 12.06.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze met with the chairman of the State Committee on Work with Diaspora, Fuad Muradov, who arrived in Georgia on 12 June 2018. The sides discussed a broad spectrum of issues relating to bilateral co-operation between Georgia and Azerbaijan highlighting the need to deepen contacts with their fellow citizens living abroad and the importance of stronger co-operation with the Diaspora organizations.
