28 February 2022 The Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani held a telephone conversation with the UK Minister of State for Europe and North America, James Cleverly. The sides discussed the ongoing war in Ukraine and the fragile geopolitical environment posing a serious challenge for the European security architecture. David Zalkaliani expressed his concern over Russia's military aggression against Ukraine and stressed the importance of the international community’s consolidated and consistent position regarding the violation of the fundamental principles of international law. The United Kingdom's Secretary of State reaffirmed his unconditional support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity. James Cleverly highlighted that the United Kingdom is clearly aware of the challenges Georgia is confronted with and that the country is not alone in the face of growing threats. The British side put a special accent on the role of Georgia as a reliable partner making a significant contribution to peace and security. The Ministers discussed the difficult humanitarian situation in the occupied territories of Georgia as a result of Russia's intervention into Georgia in 2008. The sides noted that the Kremlin's aggressive actions against Ukraine has echoes of the 2008 war and is yet another manifestation of Russia's fight for spheres of influence.
