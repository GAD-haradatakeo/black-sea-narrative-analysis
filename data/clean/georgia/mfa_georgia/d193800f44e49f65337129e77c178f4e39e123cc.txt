The meeting was co-chaired by First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and First Deputy Defence Minister Lela Chikovani. 09.09.2017, Washington - The United States Department of State has hosted a working group on security of the Georgian-US strategic partnership commission. The meeting was co-chaired by First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and First Deputy Defence Minister Lela Chikovani. Georgian and US state officials discussed bilateral issues of defense and security, Georgia’s contribution in the fight against terrorism, the human rights situation in the Russian occupied regions and the further plans for strengthening Georgia’s defense compatibility with the NATO allies. The US officials praised Georgian reforms in defense and security and thanked the country for its contribution to the global peacekeeping.
