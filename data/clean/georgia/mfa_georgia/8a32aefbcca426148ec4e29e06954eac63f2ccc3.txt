6 March 2023 On 6 March, under the aegis of the Levan Mikeladze Diplomatic Training and Research Institute, a panel discussion on the Priorities of Georgia's foreign policy was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, which was attended by 80 students of the University of Oslo. The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia addressed the attending audience. The purpose of the meeting was to familiarize the Norwegian students visiting Georgia with the current processes in the context of international politics.
