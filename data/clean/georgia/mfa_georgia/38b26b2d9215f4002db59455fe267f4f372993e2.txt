20 May 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kingdom of Denmark, Jeppe Kofod, who announced Denmark’s decision to open an embassy in Georgia. At the meeting, the Ministers praised Georgia-Denmark bilateral cooperation, which has gained a special momentum over the recent years. In this regard, the sides highlighted the importance of the exchange of visits, including the Danish Foreign Minister’s visit to Georgia in March this year. The two ministers made special note of Denmark’s growing interest in and support for Georgia and the Eastern Neighborhood, duly reflected in Denmark’s foreign policy documents.
