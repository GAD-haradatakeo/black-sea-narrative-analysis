31 October 2025 Within the framework of the 43rd session of the UNESCO General Conference, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Bochorishvili, held a meeting with the Director-General of UNESCO, Audrey Azoulay. During the meeting, the parties positively assessed the ongoing cooperation between Georgia and UNESCO in the fields of culture, education, and science, and discussed prospects for further strengthening their partnership. Minister Bochorishvili expressed gratitude to the Director-General for the fruitful cooperation and for UNESCO’s continued support of projects aimed at preserving Georgia’s cultural heritage. Particular attention was devoted to the issue of cultural heritage in Georgia’s occupied territories.
