Ilia Darchiashvili: The Prime Minister's visit is of particular importance as Hungary is a prominent supporter of our country's European and Euro-Atlantic integration 27 October 2022 "The official visit of the Prime Minister of Georgia to Hungary has ended today. Hungary is an outstanding supporter of our country's European and Euro-Atlantic integration. Accordingly, this visit bears a special significance" - the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili is quoted as saying while evaluating the visit of the Head of the Government of Georgia, Irakli Gharibashvili to Hungary.
