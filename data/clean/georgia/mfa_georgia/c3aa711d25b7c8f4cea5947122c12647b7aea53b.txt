27 September 2022 On 27 September 2022, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia held a meeting with Javier Colomina, NATO Secretary General’s Special Representative for South Caucasus. The sides discussed all important aspects of NATO-Georgia cooperation. Discussions focused on the importance of timely implementing the decisions regarding Georgia made at the Madrid summit. The sides paid special attention to the difficult security environment in the region and the importance of NATO's unwavering support for Georgia's sovereignty and territorial integrity.
