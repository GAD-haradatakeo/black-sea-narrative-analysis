13 June 2018 13.06.2018 - the Georgian Foreign Ministry hosted an interim meeting held within the annual bilateral political dialogue – Wardrop Dialogue – between Georgia and the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. The Wardrop Dialogue serves as a venue for both sides to discuss a wide spectrum of Georgia-UK co-operation. Meetings in the frames of the Dialogue are held once a year in London and Tbilisi alternatively. The intermediate round held in Tbilisi was led by Director of European Affairs Department of the Georgian Foreign Ministry Nino Baratashvil and the Co-Director of the Eastern Europe and Central Asia Directorate of the Foreign and Commonwealth Office. Participants of the meeting discussed bilateral agenda of political-economic, educational and cultural issues, as well as people-to-people contacts. The participants also spoke about he aims and tasks of the Wardrop Dialogue. Talking points also included issued to be discussed within the working groups.
