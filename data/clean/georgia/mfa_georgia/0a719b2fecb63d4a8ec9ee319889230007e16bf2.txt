5 April 2020 To ensure the phased return of Georgian citizens from abroad, the Coordination Council has organized the following additional flights: 🔺24 April – Barcelona-Tbilisi 🔺27 April – Warsaw-Tbilisi Additional flights were scheduled on 5 April: 🔹16 April - Paris-Tbilisi 🔹18 April – Tel Aviv-Tbilisi 🔹21 April – Rome-Tbilisi The aforesaid flights will be operated by Georgian Airways. Tickets can be bought 2 days before the flight date, via the Company webpage, ticket price: €199. Flights will be performed under the regulations of the WHO. Each passenger arriving in Georgia will be put into 14-day mandatory quarantine. Given below is the list of other upcoming flights as determined by the Coordination Council: 🔹7 April - Athens-Tbilisi 🔹10 April - Larnaka-Tbilisi 🔹12 April - Berlin-Tbilisi 🔹14 April – Amsterdam -Tbilisi Amid the COVID-19 outbreak, the Georgian Government has already organized the return of up to 5000 Georgian citizens from such countries as China, Italy, Iran, Poland, Bulgaria, Germany, Greece, Belarus, Netherlands, Egypt, Azerbaijan and Armenia.
