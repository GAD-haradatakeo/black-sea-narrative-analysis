14 September 2021 The 56th Meeting of the GUAM Council of National Coordinators was held under the Georgian Chairmanship in GUAM Secretariat, on 14 September 2021, by video conference. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, GUAM National Coordinator Teimuraz Janjalia took part in the online meeting. The meeting was also attended by Deputy Foreign Minister of the Republic of Azerbaijan Mahmud Mammad-Guliyev, State Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of the Republic of Moldova Vladimir Kuk, Deputy Foreign Minister of Ukraine Dmitry Senik and GUAM Secretary General Aliyev Effendi. During the online meeting, the sides discussed issues on the agenda of the organization, including political, sectoral and inter-parliamentary aspects of cooperation. A special accent was put on the activities planned for the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, including on the importance of holding a meeting of GUAM Foreign Ministers. The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister provided the participants with detailed information on the situation in the occupied territories of Georgia. He focused on the facts of gross human rights violations in the occupied territories of the Russian Federation and the dire humanitarian situation on the ground. Teimuraz Janjalia also paid special attention to strengthening interaction with GUAM partners and talked about the prospects of cooperation with other countries, which is one of the main priorities of the Georgian GUAM Chairmanship this year.
