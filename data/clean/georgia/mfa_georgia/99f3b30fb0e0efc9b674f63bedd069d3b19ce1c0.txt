25 August 2022 On 25 August 2022, the Ambassador-designate of the Islamic Republic of Iran to Georgia, Mahmud Adib, presented copies of his credentials to the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Alexandre Khvtisiashvili. Following the presentation ceremony, the sides held a meeting to discuss the historical relations between Georgia and Iran, and the new challenges and opportunities facing the region.
