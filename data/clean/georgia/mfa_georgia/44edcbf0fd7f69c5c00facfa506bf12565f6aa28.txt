23 December 2019 Within the framework of the trilateral foreign ministerial meeting of Azerbaijan, Turkey and Georgia, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with his Turkish counterpart, Foreign MinisterMevlüt Çavuşoğlu. Reaffirming their support for the strengthening of political and economic cooperation, the two ministers highly appraised the Georgian Prime Minister, Giorgi Gakharia’s recent visit to the Republic of Turkey and his-high-level meeting with the Turkish President. The Ministers also discussed the upcoming meeting of the High-Level Strategic Cooperation Council. At the meeting with his Turkish colleague, Zalkaliani also spoke about the illegal detention of Vazha Gaprindashvili and thanked Turkey for its firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
