18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Giorgi Zurabashvili, met with the newly appointed non-resident Ambassador of Ireland to Georgia, Clare Brosnan. During the meeting, the parties discussed current issues of bilateral relations. The First Deputy Minister noted with satisfaction that Georgia and Ireland are reliable partners bound by traditionally close ties. Special attention was devoted to the prospects and possible directions for the development of cooperation. The importance of further deepening high-level bilateral dialogue and enhancing the dynamics of reciprocal visits was underscored. Giorgi Zurabashvili expressed gratitude to the Ambassador for Ireland’s support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity.
