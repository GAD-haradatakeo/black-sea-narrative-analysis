13 November 2018 At his meeting with the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani, the Head the Parliamentary Friendship Group with Georgia and the former President of the Storting (Norwegian Parliament), Olemic Thommessen said that Norway firmly supports Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity and considers as unacceptable Russia’s aggression against Georgia. Discussions during the meeting focused on the difficult human rights situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. David Zalkaliani provided Olemic Thommessen with information on the murder of Tatunashvili and Otkhozoria and the importance of the international community’s strong action in this regard. David Zalkaliani also highlighted the so called Tatunashvili-Otkhozoria list adopted by the Georgian Parliament. David Zalkaliani highlighted Georgia’s peace policy with respect to the conflict resolution, which is aimed at sharing the benefits of Georgia’s European integration with the population on the other side of the occupation line. Special attention was paid to the great potential of trade-economic co-operation between the two countries. According to Olemic Thommessen, with its reforms, especially in the judicial system and fight against corruption, Georgia is an attractive country for Norwegian business.
