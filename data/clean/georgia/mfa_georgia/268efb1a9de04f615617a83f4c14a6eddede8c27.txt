22 March 2025 The Consulate General of Georgia will commence operations in Kraków, Poland. The necessary procedures for its establishment were finalized in cooperation with the Polish authorities on 11 February 2025. Ioseb Khakhaleishvili has been appointed as the Consul General and will officially assume his duties next week. The Consulate General will serve the following administrative regions: Lubusz, Lesser Poland (Małopolskie), Subcarpathian (Podkarpackie), and Świętokrzyskie. The office will be located in Kraków, with an official inauguration ceremony scheduled for the near future.
