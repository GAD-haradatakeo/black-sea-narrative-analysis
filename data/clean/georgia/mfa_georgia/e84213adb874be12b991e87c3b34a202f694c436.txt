30 December 2025 On 30 December 2025, a telephone conversation took place between the Minister for Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Yermek Kosherbayev. The ministers positively assessed the dynamics of bilateral relations throughout 2025. During the conversation, particular emphasis was placed on the importance of deepening cooperation in the fields of trade and economy, as well as transit and logistics. The parties also underscored the significance of exchanging high-level visits, which contribute to the further development of political dialogue between the two countries, and discussed plans for future engagement.
