29 December 2023 On 29 December 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Teimuraz Janjalia, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Lithuania to Georgia, Andrius Kalindra. At the meeting, the sides took stock of the existing cooperation between the countries in 2023 and discussed the agenda of relations for 2024. The Lithuanian Ambassador congratulated the Deputy Minister on granting Georgia the EU candidate status and wished the country the success on its EU path. In this context, Teimuraz Janjalia emphasized Georgia's progress on implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations and the importance of the support of partner countries, including Lithuania. Special attention was paid to the importance of high-level visits and regular political dialogue between the foreign ministries. Teimuraz Janjalia emphasized the importance of active cooperation and participation in regional formats. The sides expressed their willingness to continue active work on issues of bilateral and multilateral agenda in order to further strengthen the relations between the two countries. 2024 marks the 30th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Lithuania.
