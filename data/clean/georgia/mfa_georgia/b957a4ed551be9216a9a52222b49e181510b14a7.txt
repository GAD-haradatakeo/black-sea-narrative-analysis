30 April 2020 Today Madrid-Tbilisi charter flight will bring home Georgian citizens, including persons with special needs, chronic diseases, pregnant women, students, minors and other emigrants who have been left without shelter because of the pandemic. Transit passengers from Uruguay will also be aboard the plane. As a result of consultations of the Georgian embassy to Argentina with the Argentine Foreign Ministry and the National Migration Agency, Georgian citizens were granted the right to cross Uruguay’s border with Argentina currently closed due to coronavirus pandemic. Flights from various cities, including Berlin, Vienna, London, Athens, U.S., Larnaca, Amsterdam, Beijing, Rome, Paris, Tel Aviv, Barcelona, Warsaw have already been performed, as organized by the Coordination Council. Georgia’s diplomatic representations abroad have assisted 17,072 citizens of Georgia on the ground. The Government has organized the repatriation 8,728 citizens to Georgia, as of 30 April. The Georgian MFA website is updated every day to provide the latest information regarding support measures undertaken by the Georgian government with respect to Georgian citizens abroad.
