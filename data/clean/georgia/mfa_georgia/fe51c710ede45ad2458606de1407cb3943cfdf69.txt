24 September 2021 On 23 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister of Georgia/Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kyrgyz Republic, Ruslan Kazakbayev. The sides discussed the issues of bilateral and multilateral relations, including the development of cooperation in the fields of trade, economy, transport and tourism. The two ministers spoke about prospects for information exchange and sharing of experience in priority areas.
