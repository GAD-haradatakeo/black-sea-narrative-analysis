18 January 2018 18.01.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister David Dondua signed the condolence book, which has been opened at the Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Iran to Georgia. David Dondua conveyed his condolences to the Iranian people and government over the death of people as a result of the collision of the Iranian oil tanker. The Iranian oil tanker sank after burning for more than a week following a collision in the East China Sea, with a crew of 32 aboard. Documents Similar News
