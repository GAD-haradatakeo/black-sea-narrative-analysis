"The first official visit of the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Liechtenstein is a demonstration of important support for Georgia" - Ilia Darchiashvili 12 June 2023 "The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Principality of Liechtenstein paid her first official visit to Georgia, which is an event of historical significance for our bilateral relations. At the same time, we consider this visit as a demonstration of support for our country at this important juncture, against the background of the security challenges facing the region” - the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili said at the briefing following his meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Liechtenstein, Dominique Hasler.
