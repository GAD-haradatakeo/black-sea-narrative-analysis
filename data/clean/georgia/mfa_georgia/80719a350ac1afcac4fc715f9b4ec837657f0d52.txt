6 June 2023 On 2 June 2023, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Teimuraz Janjalia attended the event dedicated to the Italian National Day. The deputy minister congratulated the Italian people on their national day and spoke about the traditionally friendly relations between Georgia and Italy. The deputy minister thanked the Italian side for supporting the sovereignty and territorial integrity of Georgia and drew special attention to the fruitful cooperation between the two countries in various fields. The Deputy Minister emphasized Italy’s role in advancing Georgia’s European and Euro-Atlantic integration path. He noted that Georgia always relies on Italy's assistance both in the direction of integration into the European Union and NATO,. The deputy minister spoke about fruitful parliamentary cooperation between the two countries, as well as positive dynamics in the field of economy and trade. He also focused on educational ties and highlighted the importance of Italian language teaching in Georgian schools and scholarships allocated by the Italian side to Georgian students.
