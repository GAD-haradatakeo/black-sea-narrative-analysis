Another Round of Political Consultations Held Between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Azerbaijan 21 July 2025 On 21 July 2025, Tbilisi hosted the latest round of political consultations between the Ministries of Foreign Affairs of Georgia and the Republic of Azerbaijan at the level of Deputy Ministers.
