7 December 2017 7.12.2017, Vienna - Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze is on his working visit in Austria where he will participate in the meeting of the 24th OSCE Ministerial Council. Mikheil Janelidze will speak before the participants of the plenary session and will hold bilateral meetings withOSCE Secretary General Thomas Greminger, OSCE High Commissioner on National Minorities Lamberto Zannier, senior officials of various OSCE institutions and foreign ministers of OSCE Member States. On the sidelines of the 24th OSCE Ministerial Council, Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze will hold a meeting with a group of Georgia’s friends within OSCE.
