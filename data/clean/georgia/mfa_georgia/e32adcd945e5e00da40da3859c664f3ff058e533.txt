2 February 2021 The Parties welcomed the busy agenda and strategic cooperation both at bilateral and multilateral levels between the two countries. Special attention was paid to the common values and interests on which the relations between Georgia and the UK are based. Highlighting the importance of the Strategic Partnership and Cooperation Agreement which came into effect from 1 January 2021, the Parties underscored the role of the Wardrop Strategic Dialogue as a main platform of bilateral relations and sectoral cooperation. Vakhtang Makharoblishvili and Mark Clayton also discussed the possibility of conducting high-level visits during 2021. Discussing the global pandemic situation in Georgia and the UK, the sides emphasized the importance of the vaccination process and the international community’s concerted efforts in the fight against the novel virus. The First Deputy Foreign Minister and the UK Ambassador also discussed the situation in the region and security challenges. In this light, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili stressed the significance of the UK’s involvement. At the end of the meeting, Vakhtang Makharoblishvili wished the British diplomat success in his activities.
