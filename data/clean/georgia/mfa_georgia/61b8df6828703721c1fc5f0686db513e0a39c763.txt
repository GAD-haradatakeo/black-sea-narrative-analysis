18 December 2024 On 18 December 2024, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili participated in an event dedicated to Qatar's National Day and addressed the audience. He congratulated the government and people of Qatar on their National Day, extending his best wishes for peace, prosperity, and success. In his remarks, the Deputy Minister emphasized the close and friendly partnership between Georgia and Qatar, which has been strengthened by significant steps taken by both countries in recent years. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili highlighted the bilateral relations between the two nations, particularly underscoring the cooperation at the parliamentary level, the importance of tourism, and the deepening of people-to-people connections. “I am confident that in the near future, our relations will reach a new qualitative level, and through joint efforts, the friendship and business ties between our two countries will be further strengthened for the benefit of our peoples,” stated Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili during his speech.
