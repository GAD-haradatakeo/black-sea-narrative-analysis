15 December 2022 On 15 December 2022, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with the representative of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in Georgia, Kemlin Furley. The meeting was also attended by the employees of the UNHCR office in Georgia and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. At the meeting, the sides reviewed a wide range of issues relating to cooperation between Georgia and UNHCR. The first deputy minister thanked the UNHCR representative for fruitful cooperation. The sides discussed issues relating to UNHCR's activities and future plans in Georgia, including in the occupied region of Abkhazia.
