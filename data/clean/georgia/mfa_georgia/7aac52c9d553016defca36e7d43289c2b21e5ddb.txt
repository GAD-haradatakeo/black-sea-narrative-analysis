26 June 2024 On 27 June 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan, Jeyhun Bayramov, will pay an official visit to Georgia. During his visit, Minister Bayramov is scheduled to meet with several key Georgian officials, including the Prime Minister of Georgia, Irakli Kobakhidze, the Chairman of the Parliament of Georgia, Shalva Papuashvili, and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili. The foreign ministers' meeting will focus on significant topics on the agenda of bilateral cooperation and regional collaboration. Following their discussions, press announcements will be made. Additionally, within the framework of his visit, Jeyhun Bayramov will lay a wreath at the memorial honoring the heroes who died for the unity of Georgia.
