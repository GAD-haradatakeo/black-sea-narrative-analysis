18 April 2019 18.04.2019, Tbilisi – Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani held a meeting with UNICEF Regional Director for Europe and Central Asia, Afshan Khan. Discussions focused on issues relating to UNICEF activities in Europe. The Minister thanked UNICEF representatives for the projects implemented in Georgia. The Minister provided Afshan Khan with detailed information regarding the difficult situation in Georgia’s occupied territories, including in terms of children’s rights. The sides expressed their concern on restrictions on access to healthcare services, to education in one’s native language, free movement and residence, as well as other human rights violations in both regions.
