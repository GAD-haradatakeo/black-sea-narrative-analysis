Comment of the Press and Information Department regarding the statement of the press speaker of the High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy 18 May 2017 18.05.2017, Tbilisi - The Spokesperson of the High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy Federica Mogherini reaffirmed in her statement that the European Union fully supports Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as the Georgian Government’s peace policy, including within the framework of the Geneva International Discussions.
