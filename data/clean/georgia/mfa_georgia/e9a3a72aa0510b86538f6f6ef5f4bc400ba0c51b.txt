26 November 2025 On 26 November 2025, at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili met with the delegation of the Kingdom of Thailand headed by Isara Sereewatthanawut, Special Adviser on International Trade to the Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand and Secretary-General of the King Prajadhipok’s Institute. The twenty-member delegation included senators of the Thai Parliament, heads of governmental agencies, and executives of companies representing various sectors. The President of the Georgian Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Giorgi Pertaia, also took part in the meeting. The discussion focused on existing relations and cooperation between Georgia and Thailand across various areas. The importance of high-level visits was underscored, and the parties agreed on specific actions aimed at further strengthening people-to-people contacts, increasing tourism flows, and raising awareness of business and investment opportunities. They also discussed the participation of both sides in high-level events planned in Thailand and Georgia.
