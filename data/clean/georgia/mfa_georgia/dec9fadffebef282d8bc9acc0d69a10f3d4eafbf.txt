5 February 2021 Air Service agreement was signed on 4 February 2021, in Seoul, between the Government of Georgia and the Government of the Republic of Korea. From the Korean side the Agreement was signed by the Foreign Minister, Kang Kyung-wha, and from the Georgian side – by the Georgian Ambassador to the Republic of Korea, Otar Berdzenishvili. The Agreement will contribute to the development of international air services, establishment of mutual understanding and cooperation between the two nations and the promotion of tourism and trade-economic relations between Georgia and the Republic of Korea.
