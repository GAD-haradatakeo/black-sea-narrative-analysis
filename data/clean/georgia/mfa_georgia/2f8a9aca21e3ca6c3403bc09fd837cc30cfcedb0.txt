18 October 2017 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia is deeply concerned over gubernatorial elections held on October 15 in Venezuela. The Venezuelan government failed to ensure free, fair and transparent electoral environment. Moreover, impartial independent international observer missions were denied to monitor the electoral process. It is of great concern that conducting these elections in an undemocratic environment threatens stable and peaceful development of the country and neglects fundamental rights of the Venezuelan people to freely exercise their right to vote. Georgia joins international community’s call upon the Government of Venezuela to respect the rule of law, fundamental human rights and freedoms and ensure the freedom of expression.
