26 August 2025 On 26 August 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with Qasim Mohiuddin, Ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan to Georgia, with residence in Baku. The parties discussed current issues of bilateral cooperation, including prospects for high-level visits, as well as opportunities to deepen relations in the fields of trade and economy, transport and logistics, tourism, education, and sports.
