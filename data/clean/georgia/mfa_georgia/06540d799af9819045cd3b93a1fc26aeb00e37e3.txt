1 October 2025 On 1 October, 2025, Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Khatuna Totladze, held an introductory meeting with the Ambassador of the State of Israel to Georgia, Walid Abu Haya. During the meeting, the sides highlighted the historically friendly ties and partnership relations between the two countries, which continue to develop dynamically. They also reviewed existing cooperation and prospects for further development in various fields, placing particular emphasis on the successful relations in tourism, as well as cultural and people-to-people exchanges. The parties also discussed the regional security environment and the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. The Israeli side once again reaffirmed its support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity. In addition, the parties exchanged views on the situation in the Middle East and underscored the importance of diplomatic efforts to prevent any escalation.
