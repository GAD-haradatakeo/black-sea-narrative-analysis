23 September 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili and the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Iceland, ThórdísKolbrúnGylfadóttir, held a meeting within the framework of the UN General Assembly, during which the ministers discussed the issues of bilateral relations, as well as cooperation within the international organizations. The Ministers welcomed the 30th anniversary of diplomatic relations between Iceland and Georgia and emphasized the special importance of the anniversary date for the further strengthening of relations.
