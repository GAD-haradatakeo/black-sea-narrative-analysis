6 October 2025 On 6 October 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to the Russian Federation, Chrystyna Marty Lang, who, through the mediation mechanism, represents the interests of Georgia in the Russian Federation. The meeting was also attended by the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Swiss Confederation to Georgia, Heidi Grau. During the meeting, the sides noted the close and friendly relations between Georgia and the Swiss Confederation, based on shared values and mutual respect. They discussed key issues on the current bilateral cooperation agenda and explored prospects for deepening collaboration with Switzerland, as the country prepares to assume the OSCE Chairmanship. In this context, it was noted that at the beginning of next year, the Head of the Federal Department of Foreign Affairs of the Swiss Confederation, Ignazio Cassis, is expected to pay an official visit to Georgia. The parties also discussed the regional security environment and existing challenges. The Minister paid particular attention to the grave humanitarian situation and the dire human rights conditions in Georgia’s occupied territories. Maka Botchorishvili expressed gratitude to the Swiss side for its firm support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as for Switzerland’s continued role as a mediator in relations with the Russian Federation. The discussion highlighted Georgia’s dynamic economic development amid ongoing regional processes, as well as the country’s growing engagement in regional infrastructure and energy initiatives that contribute to Europe-Asia energy and trade connectivity and integration.
