Political consultations held between Georgia and Canada 5 December 2022 On 5 December 2022, another round of political consultations between Georgia and Canada was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, which was co-chaired by the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, and the Assistant Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Canada for Europe, the Arctic, the Middle East and the Maghreb, Ambassador Sandra McCardell.
