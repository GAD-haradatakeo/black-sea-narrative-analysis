25 January 2019 25.01.2019, Madrid – Within the framework of his visit to Spain, the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani participated in the event organized by the Argentine Republicin Madrid where he held a meeting with the Argentinian Tourism Minister Gustavo Santos. The meeting was attended by the Secretary General of the World Tourism Organization Zurab Pololikashvili. David Zalkaliani and Gustavo Santos discussed the issues on the agenda of bilateral relations between Georgia and Argentina. The priority importance of developing co-operation with Latin America was also highlighted. The Ministers noted with satisfaction the Georgia-Argentina Tourism Co-operation Memorandum due to be signed in 2019. Documents Similar News
