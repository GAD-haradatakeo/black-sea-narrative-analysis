7 September 2022 On 6 September 2022, within the framework of the Georgia-EU Association Council in Brussels, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, held a meeting with the member of the European Parliament, Petras Auštrevičius. At the meeting, special attention was paid to Georgia’s European integration process, the progress achieved on this path and the challenges involved. The sides highlighted the special importance of the European Parliament’s support of and fruitful cooperation with Georgia in this regard. The sides noted the historical significance of the European Union’s decision granting Georgia a European perspective. The Georgian Foreign Minister informed the European parliamentarian about the progress in the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations. Ilia Darchiashvili expressed the hope that Georgia’s progress and the reforms implemented in different directions will be properly recognized by the European partners. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked Petras Auštrevičius for the European Parliament’s strong support of of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity, as well as of the European integration process of Georgia.
