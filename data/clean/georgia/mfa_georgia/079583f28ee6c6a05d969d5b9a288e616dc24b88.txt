28 March 2019 Ms. Pascale Moreau, Director of Bureau for Europe of the Office of the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) is currently visiting Georgia. On 28 March Director of the UNHCR Bureau for Europe visited the villages of Khurvaleti and Odzisi, adjacent to the occupation line. Topics pertaining the cooperation between the UNHCR and Georgia will be discussed at the meeting with Mr. David Zalkaliani, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia on 29 Marh 2019.
