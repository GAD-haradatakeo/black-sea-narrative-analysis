Georgian citizens living in Spain will be provided with online medical consultations 21 March 2020 Georgian citizens living in Spain will be provided with online medical consultations 21.03.2020 - Online consultations on coronavirus are underway for Georgian citizens living abroad. Consultations were jointly organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Ministry of IDPs from the Occupied Territories, Labour, Health and Social Affairs of Georgia and the National Public Health Centre, by decision of the Prime Minister-led Inter-Agency Coordination Council.
