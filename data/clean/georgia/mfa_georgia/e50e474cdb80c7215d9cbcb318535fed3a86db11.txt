29 March 2023 On 29 March 2023, the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, held a meeting with Director General for European Issues of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Czech Republic Jaroslav Kurfürst, who is visiting Georgia. During the meeting, discussions focused on the security environment in the region, with special emphasis on the priority of the Black Sea security and the role of the European Union in this context. Lasha Darsalia informed the Czech delegation about the security, human rights and humanitarian situation in the occupied regions of Georgia and emphasized the special importance of the further consolidation of partners' support. The first deputy minister also shared with the Czech side detailed information on Georgia’s political and humanitarian support to Ukraine. Special attention was paid to the progress of reforms carried out by Georgia on the path of integration into the European Union and the process of fulfilling the European Commission’s 12 recommendations. The First Deputy Minister informed the Czech side about the ongoing work and the progress achieved in this regard. The sides discussed the process of Georgia's integration into NATO, as well as Georgia's priorities and expectations regarding the NATO summit planned for this year.
