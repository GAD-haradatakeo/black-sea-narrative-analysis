2 November 2023 Within the framework of the Berlin conference, Ilia Darchiashvili participated in the meeting of the working group on EU enlargement. The meeting was attended by Foreign Ministers of Latvia, Sweden, Austria, Montenegro, Slovenia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Kosovo, as well as representatives of Moldova, Hungary and Albania. The minister spoke about the process of Georgia's integration into the European Union and the implementation of the European Commission’s recommendations. Ilia Darchiashvili informed the participants of the meeting about the reforms carried out by Georgia and expressed the hope that the efforts of Georgia will be duly reflected in the European partners’ decision regarding the candidate status.
