17 January 2018 17.01.2017, Tbilisi - Children living near the occupation line in Georgia celebrated the New Year in Tbilisi, on 17 January 2018. Georgian Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze and Deputy Foreign Minister Khatuna Totladze hosted children at the National Youth Palace where a special program entertained the kids from the Kareli and Gori Municipalities. "I’m very happy to host these very special guests and share the happy minutes with them. We are very well aware of the difficulties these children face because of the occupation. The Government of Georgia works around the clock to solve this problem” – the Minister said. As part of the Programme, the children visited the Experimentorium - Museum of Entertaining Science putting on display up to 80 exhibits of physics, chemistry and biology and took part in the azot show. The event was organized by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia in team with the educational resource centres of the Georgian Ministry of Education and Science and the National Youth Palace.
