23 September 2021 On 22 September 2021, in the framework of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly, the Vice Prime Minister/Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the President of the UN General Assembly, Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Maldives, Abdulla Shahid. Davit Zalkaliani congratulated Abdulla Shahid on his election as President of the UN General Assembly. The sides discussed the agenda of the 76th session of the UN General Assembly. David Zalkaliani briefed the President on Georgia's priorities regarding the 76th session of the General Assembly. The meeting focused on the situation in the occupied regions of Georgia and the important role of the UN in the process of peaceful settlement of the conflict. The President of the General Assembly thanked Georgia for its active cooperation in the format of the UN General Assembly and expressed hope that Georgia will continue to make a significant contribution to the implementation of the goals of the organization.
