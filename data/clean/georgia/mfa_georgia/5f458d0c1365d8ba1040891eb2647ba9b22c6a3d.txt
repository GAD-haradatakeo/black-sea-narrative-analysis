27 September 2019 7.09.2019, New York –On the sidelines of the 74thsession of the UN General Assembly,Georgian Foreign Minister David Zalkalianiheld his first meeting with his Singaporean counterpart VivianBalakrishnan. The two minuters spoke about the ways to further develop bilateral relationsin including by means of exchanging experience in such areas as liberal business and investment climate, reforms and tourism. Noting with satisfaction the 25th anniversary of diplomatic relations relation between the two countries, Zalkaliani highly appraised multilateral cooperation with Singapore. The Georgian delegation expressed its close interest in further developing contacts with the countries of South-East Asia, including through communication with respective regional organizations.
