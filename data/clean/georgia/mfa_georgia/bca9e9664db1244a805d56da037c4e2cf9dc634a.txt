24 May 2017 24.05.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister Davit Zalkaliani received Ambassador of the Argentine Republic to Georgia (residing in Ankara) Julian Luis Tettamanti. The Ambassador presented copies of his credentials to the First Deputy Minister. David Zalkaliani congratulated the Ambassador on the 25th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Georgia and Argentina and expressed his gratitude to the government of Argentina being among the first to recognize the independence of Georgia. The sides discussed important issues on the agenda of bilateral relations and agreed on further steps to deepen cooperation in the fields of economy, tourism and culture.
