26 September 2025 On 25 September 2025, Tamar Liluashvili, Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia, presented her Letters of Credence to the President of the Italian Republic, Sergio Mattarella. Following the credential presentation ceremony at the Presidential Palace, the Ambassador of Georgia and the President of Italy discussed the dynamics of bilateral cooperation between the two countries and future plans.
