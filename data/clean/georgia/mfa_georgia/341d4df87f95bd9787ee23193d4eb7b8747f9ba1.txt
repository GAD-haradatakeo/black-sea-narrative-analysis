Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Netherlands 7 April 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili met with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Netherlands, Wopke Hoekstra on the sidelines of the NATO Ministerial.The Ministers welcomed the 30th anniversary of diplomatic relations between the Netherlands and Georgia and praised the close and friendly relations between the two countries.
