18 September 2025 On 18 September 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Alexandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the Arab Republic of Egypt, Amal Afif. The meeting highlighted the close and friendly relations between Georgia and Egypt, which continue to develop successfully in both bilateral and multilateral formats. The parties discussed opportunities for high-level and sectoral cooperation, particularly in trade, education, and culture. Emphasis was placed on the productive collaboration within international organizations. The ministers exchanged views on ongoing developments and security challenges in the broader region. The Ambassador reaffirmed Egypt’s support for Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders. During the visit, the Ambassador presented copies of her letters of credence to Zurab Darchiashvili, Director of the Diplomatic Protocol Department at the Georgian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Both sides welcomed the existing friendly relations and expressed readiness to further deepen mutually beneficial cooperation.
