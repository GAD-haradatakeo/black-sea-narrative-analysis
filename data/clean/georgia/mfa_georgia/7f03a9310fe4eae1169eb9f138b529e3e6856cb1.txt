18 May 2017 18.05.2017, Tbilisi – Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister David Jalagania met with State Secretary at the Ministry of Economic Development and Technology Ales Cantarutti. At the meeting the sides discussed the issues of bilateral trade-economic cooperation. The Deputy Minister provided the guests with information on the investment and business environment in Georgia. In addition, he spoke about the four-point plan of the reforms developed by the Government, as well as about ongoing and planned activities within the framework of this plan.
