17 February 2024 On 17 February 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ilia Darchiashvili, met with the director of the National Endowment for Democracy, Damon Wilson, within the framework of the Munich Security Conference. The discussion during the meeting covered topics such as the geopolitical landscape in the area, Georgia's progress towards European integration, and the expanding regional significance of Georgia within this framework.
