28 May 2018 28.05.2018, Tbilisi - The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze hosted participants of the Diaspora Forum and thanked them for their active involvement and efforts aimed at promoting Georgia abroad. According to the Georgian Foreign Minister, one of the goals of the Georgian Minister is to be as close as possible with its citizens both in Georgia and beyond its borders. Mikheil Janelidze said that a series of steps were taken towards this goal over the past year but also of great importance are recommendations and ideas of diaspora representatives. “I am excited about the idea of establishing a joint diaspora chamber of commerce, which, I hope, will be one of the first projects to bring togather this tremendous resource and potential, and then to spill over into other areas, including education and culture". Mikheil Janelidze extensively spoke about Georgia’s progress on the Euro-Atlantic integration path.
