When you arrive in Batumi you can see the country is developing in the right direction and Georgia has a very good future ahead of it – Mikheil Janelidze 22 April 2018 22.04.2018, Batumi – The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister, Mikheil Janelidze visited the Adjara region where, within the framework of the NATO Week, he held meetings with representatives of the local self-government bodies, non-governmental organizations, students and professors.
