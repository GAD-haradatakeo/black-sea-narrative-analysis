3 June 2022 On 3 June 2022, the First Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia received the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Kingdom of the Netherlands Maaike van Koldam. The First Deputy Minister and the Ambassador discussed the security situation in the region and Russia's large-scale war against Ukraine. Lasha Darsalia briefed the Ambassador on Georgia's political support and humanitarian assistance to Ukraine. Special emphasis was placed on the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories and on the ongoing international negotiations in Geneva. In this context, Lasha Darsalia stressed the importance of the consolidated position of the international community.
