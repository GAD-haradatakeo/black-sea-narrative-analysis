29 March 2020 On 29 March 2020, Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia held the meeting with UN Resident Coordinator Sabine machl, UNDP Resident Representative Louisa Vinton, and WHO Resident Representative Silviu Domente. The sides discussed the issues of cooperation between Georgia and the UN amid the situation with regard to spread of new Coronavirus (Covid-19). They touched upon the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories in this regard and underlined the importance of effective engagement of the international community, including the UN in addressing the challenges the conflict-affected population faces. At the end of the meeting, Deputy Foreign Minister thanked the UN Representatives for their efforts and reaffirmed Georgia’s support to the UN activities.
