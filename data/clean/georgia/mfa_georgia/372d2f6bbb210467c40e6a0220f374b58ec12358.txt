25 June 2024 On 25 June 2024, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Ilia Darchiashvili, attended an informal event at the invitation of the Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, Xavier Bettel. The meeting was initiated by Xavier Bettel. The informal meeting included the participation of foreign ministers from EU member, partner, and candidate countries. During the event, Minister Ilia Darchiashvili had the opportunity to engage with a number of his fellow ministers, providing them with updates on Georgia's European integration agenda and the progress in implementing recommendations. Additionally, the foreign ministers attending the event had an audience with Grand Duke Henri of Luxembourg. The Grand Ducal Palace responded to the meeting on social media platform "Twitter".
