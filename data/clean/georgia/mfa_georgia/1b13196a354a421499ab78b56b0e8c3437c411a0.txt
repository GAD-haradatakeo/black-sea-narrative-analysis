1 February 2018 1.02.2018, Tbilisi - Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Georgia to the Republic of Austria Konstantine Zaldastanishvili was posthumously awarded Decoration of Honour for his services to the Republic of Austria and for his special contribution towards the deepening of relations between Georgia and Austria (Grand Decoration of Honour in Gold with Sash). Ambassador of the Republic of Austria to Georgia Arad Benkö presented the award to the spouse of the late Ambassador Konstantine Zaldastanishvili. Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani and Austrian Ambassador Arad Benkö spoke about the high professionalism of Kote Zaldastanishvili who had distinguished himself through his tremendous contribution to the development of Georgian diplomacy and the relations between Georgia and Austria. Grand Decoration of Honour in Gold with Sash is Austria's highest national honour for Austrian and foreign leading figures, presidents and prime ministers, and is only awarded to foreign ambassadors in exceptional cases.
