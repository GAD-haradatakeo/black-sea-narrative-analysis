3 May 2023 On 3 May 2023, Ilia Darchiashvili held a meeting with the Minister of Interior and Health of the State of Israel, Moshe Arbel. The sides welcomed the dynamic relations and close cooperation between Georgia and Israel. The positive experience of the two countries in the field of security cooperation was also noted. The conversation focused on migration policy and the importance of using the possibility of circular migration. The sides agreed to expand close cooperation even further. Documents Similar News
