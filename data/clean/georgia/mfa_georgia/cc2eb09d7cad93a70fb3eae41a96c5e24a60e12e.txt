21 October 2021 The Deputy Foreign Minister, Teimuraz Janjalia participated in the 4th Association Exchange Forum in Kiev, on 21 October 2021, to share experience between experts on the implementation of Association Agreements (AA) from Ukraine, Moldova and Georgia, to discuss recent achievements on European Integration Path and to outline new visions, proposals and recommendations regarding the further strengthening of this process. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Ukraine, Dmytro Kuleba delivered opening remarks before the audience. In the framework of the panel discussions, Teimuraz Janjalia reviewed the reforms implemented by Georgia under the Association Agreement.
