23 September 2021 The Georgian delegation is participating in the 65th General Conference of the International Atomic Energy Agency in Vienna. The General Conference is attended by high-level delegations of the Member States, international and non-governmental organizations. Tamar Karseladze, the Head of the Delegation and the Head of New Threats and Arms Control Division under the Department of Security Policy and Euro-Atlantic Integration, spoke before the participants of the General Conference on the importance of the Agency in terms of nuclear non-proliferation and radiation protection and safety. In her speech, she paid special attention to the threats emanating from the occupied territories of Georgia and the importance of international verification and monitoring mechanisms on the ground. On closing, the head of the Georgian delegation thanked the partner states and organizations for the implementation of projects that contribute to the strengthening of the Georgian regulatory body and the improvement of the national system of nuclear and radiation safety.
