23 January 2025 On 23 January 2025, Georgia’s Minister of Foreign Affairs, Maka Botchorishvili, held a meeting with Robert Gershner, the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Austria to Georgia. During the meeting, discussions focused on significant aspects of bilateral cooperation, emphasizing the traditionally friendly and partnership relations between the two countries. Both parties underscored the importance of enhancing political dialogue between the two countries. The meeting also highlighted the necessity of deepening economic and sectoral cooperation between Georgia and Austria. The parties also touched upon Georgia's relations with the European Union, noting the significance of Austria's support on the path of European integration. The Minister expressed gratitude to the Austrian side for their unwavering support for Georgia's territorial integrity and sovereignty.
