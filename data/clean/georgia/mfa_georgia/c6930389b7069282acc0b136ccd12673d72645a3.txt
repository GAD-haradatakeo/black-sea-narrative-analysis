The Irish Parliament has adopted a resolution on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the First Democratic Republic of Georgia 22 June 2018 22.06.2018 -The Joint Committee on Foreign Affairs and Trade, and Defence of the Irish Parliament adopted at its meeting on 21 June a resolution on the occasion of the 100th anniversary of the First Democratic Republic of Georgia. The resolution welcomes the increase of political and sectoral cooperation between Georgia and Ireland and supports further development of trade, economic and investment ties. See All
