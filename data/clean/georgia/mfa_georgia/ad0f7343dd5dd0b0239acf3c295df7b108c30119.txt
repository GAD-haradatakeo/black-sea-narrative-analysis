18 January 2019 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia expresses its deep concern over the terrorist attack on the police cadettrainingacademyin the capital of the Republic of Colombia, on 17 January 2019, leaving over 20 people dead and tens of people - wounded. Georgia strongly condemns terrorism in all its forms and supports the Colombian Government’s efforts directed against violence. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Georgia conveys its condolences to the government and people of Colombia, as well as to the families of terror victims and wishes speedy recovery to those who have been injured as a result of this deadly act.
