21 September 2025 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, has departed for the United States. Together with the President of Georgia, Mikheil Kavelashvili, Minister Botchorishvili will participate in the 80th Session of the General Debate of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA). Within the framework of the visit, the Minister of Foreign Affairs will hold high-level meetings with her ministerial counterparts.
