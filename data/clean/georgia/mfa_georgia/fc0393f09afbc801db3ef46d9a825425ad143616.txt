21 February 2018 20.02.2018, Tbilisi – Georgian First Deputy Foreign Minister David Zalkaliani met with UNICEF's Representative to Georgia Laila Omar Gad. The sides discussed the Georgian Government’s reforms to ensure the protection of human rights. Special attention was paid to problems relating to children’s rights and people with disabilitise.
