22 September 2022 The Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia held a meeting with the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Hungary, PéterSzijjártó within the framework of the UN General Assembly. The ministers noted the active dynamics of communication between the two countries, which is especially important in the process of effective implementation of the challenges facing the region, as well as the goals and objectives of Georgia. The Hungarian side once again expressed its commitment to help Georgia on its path to European integration. The Hungarian side once again confirmed its strong support for Georgia aspiring to achieve European integration goals. The ministers also reviewed issues of cooperation in international formats.
