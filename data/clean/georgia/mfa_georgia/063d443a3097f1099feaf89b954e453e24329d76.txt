8 April 2021 On 8 April 2021, The Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Japan to Georgia, Imamura Akira. The sides discussed issues relating to Georgia-Japan bilateral relations and the situation in the region.
