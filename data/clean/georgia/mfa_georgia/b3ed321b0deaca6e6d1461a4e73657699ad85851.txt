3 May 2023 The First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs, Lasha Darsalia, participated in the opening session of the SPS Information Day in Tbilisi, on 3 May 2023. The event was organized with support from Georgia’s Ministries of Foreign Affairs and of Education and Science, at the Information Centre on NATO and EU. James Appathurai, NATO’s Deputy Assistant Secretary General for Emerging Security Challenges spoke before the participants of the meeting. In his speech, the First Deputy Minister talked about Georgia's Euro-Atlantic aspirations. He also noted that the result-oriented practical cooperation between NATO and Georgia within the framework of SPS strengthens the country's resilience and helps Georgia get closer to NATO standards.
