27 April 2021 On 27 April 2021, the Georgian Deputy Foreign Minister, Lasha Darsalia met with the acting UNDP Resident Representative in Georgia, Anna Chernyshova. Discussions focused on a broad spectrum of fruitful cooperation between Georgia and the UNDP. The sides discussed the UNDP-implemented projects in Georgia. Special note was taken of the UNDP’s important role in contributing to the sustainable development goals at the national level. Talking points also included the situation in Georgia’s occupied territories. At the end of the meeting, the sides expressed their commitment to continued close cooperation. Documents Similar News
