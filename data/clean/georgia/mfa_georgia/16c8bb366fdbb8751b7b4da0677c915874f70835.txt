3 July 2025 On 3 July 2025, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Maka Botchorishvili, held a farewell meeting with the Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Hungary to Georgia, Anna Mária Sikó, on the occasion of the completion of her diplomatic mission in the country. Minister Botchorishvili expressed her appreciation to Ambassador Sikó for her significant contribution to the advancement of bilateral relations during her tenure, which has played an instrumental role in fostering and deepening the strategic partnership between Georgia and Hungary. The meeting underscored the successful and close cooperation between the two countries across various sectors and emphasized the importance of the intergovernmental summit as a key platform for high-level political dialogue between Georgia and Hungary. Minister Botchorishvili reiterated Georgia’s gratitude for Hungary’s steadfast support and expressed confidence that the productive partnership between the two nations will continue to strengthen in the years ahead.
