23 February 2022 On 23 February 2022, the Vice Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, David Zalkaliani met with the Ambassadors of the EU Member States to Georgia and the Head of the European Union Delegation to Georgia, Carl Hartzell. Discussions focused on the situation around Ukraine and the impact of the ongoing processes on both global and regional security. The Vice Prime Minister/Foreign Minister reaffirmed Georgia's strong support for Ukraine's sovereignty and territorial integrity. According to him, Russia's recognition of Ukraine’s Donetsk and Lugansk regions as independent states is a gross violation of the fundamental principles of international law. As David Zalkaliani highlighted, in the current situation, paramount importance needs to be attached to the international partners’ united position and strong support for Ukraine.
