12 January 2026 On 12 January 2026, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, met with the Vice President of the Republic of Botswana and Minister of Finance, Ndaba Nkosinathi Gaolathe. During the meeting, the parties discussed issues of bilateral cooperation, including in the fields of trade, investment, and tourism. Cooperation between Georgia and Botswana within the framework of international organizations was noted, and the importance of continuing close interaction in this regard was emphasized. Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili expressed his gratitude to Botswana for its support of Georgia’s sovereignty and territorial integrity within its internationally recognized borders. It is noteworthy that, within the framework of a working visit to Tbilisi, the Vice President of Botswana will hold meetings with representatives of Georgia’s Ministry of Economy and Sustainable Development, Ministry of Finance, Ministry of Justice, and the National Bank of Georgia.
