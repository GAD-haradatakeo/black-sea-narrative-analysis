26 August 2021 Georgia was elected a member of the Council of Administration of the World Postal Union (UPU) and the Postal Operations Council (POC) for another 2022-2025 term. Elections were held within the framework of the 27th Congress of the World Postal Union on 9-27 August, 2021, in Abidjan (Côte d'Ivoire). Georgia was first elected a member of UPU and POC in 2016. As a member of the Council of Administration of the World Postal Union (UPU) and the Postal Operations Council (POC), Georgia will have the opportunity to continue its efforts to effectively implement its mandate and to promote, alongside other members of the Postal Union, a better and more modern international postal sector.
