28 March 2023 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Aleksandre Khvtisiashvili, held a meeting with the director of the Eurasian Division of the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD), William Thompson and the project manager, Daniel Quadbeck. Discussions focused on the prospects of further deepening cooperation between Georgia and OECD. The deputy minister emphasized the active cooperation of the OECD with the government agencies of Georgia. Special note was taken of the fruitful experience of working on small and medium business development strategy, as well as on issues relating to taxation, environmental protection and education. The Deputy Minister spoke about the geopolitical challenges facing the region, as well as the measures taken by the Government of Georgia to promote the country's economic growth and transport and logistics capabilities of Georgia. According to William Thompson, many successful initiatives have been implemented by the Georgian government in terms of supporting small and medium-sized businesses. It was emphasized that active work is underway to hold a large-scale OECD event: "Eurasia Week" in Georgia.
