29 May 2018 29.05.2018, Tbilisi – An inter-agency meeting was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs where the recognition by Syria of the so-called independence of Georgia’s occupied territories was discussed with focus on the need of coordinated activities in this regard. During the meeting held at the level of deputy ministers, the Deputy Foreign Minister, David Dondua briefed the representatives of other ministries on the steps to be taken in a coordinated manner in terms of the non-recognition policy.
