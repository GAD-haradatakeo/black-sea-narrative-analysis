24 September 2021 On 24 September 2021, UNDP’s new resident representative in Georgia, Nick Beresford, presented letters of his credentials to the First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia. Lasha Darsalia congratulated UNDP’s new representative on the launch of his mission in Georgia and wished him success in his future endeavors. During the meeting, the sides discussed a wide range of issues on the agenda of cooperation between Georgia and UNDP. Special attention was paid to UNDP's efforts to implement the Sustainable Development Agenda, contribute to economic development, democratic governance and environment protection in Georgia. The First Deputy Minister briefed the UNDP representative on the dire humanitarian situation in the Russian-occupied regions of Georgia.
