8 February 2019 08.02.2019, U.S. - “Georgia continues to be an important ally in regional stability and defense cooperation” – Under Secretary of State Andrea Thompson twitted following her meeting with the Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani. David Zalkaliani held a meeting with the U.S. Under Secretary of State for Arms Control and International Security,Andrea Thompson on 7 February, where the sides highly appraised U.S.-Georgia strategic partnership. According to them, co-operation between the two countries is at an all-time high in all priority areas. Documents Similar News
