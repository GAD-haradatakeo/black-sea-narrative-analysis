27 April 2018 27.04.2018 , Tbilisi – The Georgian Vice Prime Minister, Foreign Minister Mikheil Janelidze attended the event on the occasion of the King’s Day organized by the Embassy of the Kingdom of the Netherlands to Georgia. Mikheil Janelidze and Dutch Ambassador to Georgia Johannes Douma spoke before the audience attending the event. The Minister congratulated the Dutch people on the day of national unity. Accoridng to Mikheil Janelidze, Georgia appreciates the support that the Netherlands renders to the country in various bilateral and multilateral formats. Mikheil Janelidze highlighted excellent diplomatic relations between the two countries over the past 25 years and successful co-operation in the areas of economy, trade, investments and tourism. “Thanks to the efforts of our Embassies, trade and economic ties between our states have become closer and gained further impetus. I am delighted to underscore that Holland ranks fourth on the list of top countries investing in Georgia” - the Minister said. Emphasizing the importance of deepening people-to-people contacts, the Minister also focused on the cultural events due to take place within the framework of the 100th anniversary since the foundation of the first democratic republic of Georgia.
