19 June 2021 The Georgian Vice Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs, David Zalkaliani held a meeting with his Ukrainian counterpart, Dmytro Kuleba, within the framework of the Antalya Diplomatic Forum. During the meeting the parties positively assessed the bilateral relations between Georgia and Ukraine, across all strategic issues, including in terms of the recent increase in high-level visits, which, along with improving said bilateral ties, also supports the two states in terms of overcoming common challenges on the international level, outlining joint positions of the two countries.
