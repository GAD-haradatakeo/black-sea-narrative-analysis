7 May 2025 The Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Vladimer Konstantinidi, awarded qualification certificates to junior diplomats. In his remarks, the Deputy Minister emphasized the importance of the preparatory program for entry-level civil servants in the diplomatic service and wished the young diplomats success in their future careers. The preparatory program for entry-level civil servants in the diplomatic service is conducted annually by the Diplomatic Training and Research Institute. The aim of the program is to equip participants with the professional skills necessary for a successful career in the diplomatic service.
