17 June 2023 On 17 June 2023, the Georgian Foreign Minister, Ilia Darchiashvili held a telephone conversation with his Turkish counterpart, Foreign Minister Hakan Fidan. Ilia Darchiashvili once again congratulated Hakan Fidan on his appointment and wished him success in his future activities. During the telephone conversation, the ministers once again confirmed the readiness to further strengthen the strategic partnership between Georgia and Turkey and emphasized the importance of exchanging high-level visits between the two countries. Ilia Darchiashvili thanked his Turkish colleague for his firm support of Georgia's territorial integrity and the country's Euro-Atlantic aspirations. Once again, the importance of cooperation between the two countries was emphasized for the positive management of peace processes in the region and for ensuring stable and sustainable development of the region. The sides highly evaluated the last meeting between the Prime Minister of Georgia and the President of Turkey at the presidential inauguration ceremony in Ankara and expressed the hope that the high-level meetings will be maintained with the current intensity, which will have a positive impact on the strategic partnership between the countries. The ministers also positively assessed Georgia-Turkey cooperation in the Georgia-Turkey-Azerbaijan tripartite format and expressed their readiness to meet within the tripartite ministerial.
