14 March 2025 Maka Botchorishvili, the Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, held a meeting with the United States Ambassador to Georgia, Robin Dunnigan. The discussion focused on the agenda for strengthening cooperation between Georgia and the United States, underscoring the need for positive momentum in their partnership, which is rooted in shared values and interests. The Georgian government has consistently reaffirmed its commitment to advancing this dynamic partnership. Both sides emphasized that the strategic relationship developed over several decades forms a solid foundation for further deepening bilateral collaboration. The meeting also addressed the current security environment and challenges both regionally and globally, highlighting the significance of continued U.S. support and cooperation in this context.
