20 November 2018 20.11.2018, Tbilisi – The Ministry of Foreign Affairs hosted the first meeting of the Inter-agency Council for elaboration of Georgia’s Foreign Policy Strategy and Action Plan for 2019-22, which was chaired by the Deputy Foreign Minister Lasha Darsalia. The meeting was attended by senior officials from the Ministries of Defence, Internal Affair, Regional Development and Infrastructure, IDPs from theOccupiedTerritories,Labour, Health and Social Affairs, Agriculture and Environment Protection, Finance, Economy and Sustainable Development, Education, Science, Culture and Sport, Reconciliation and Civic Equality, as well as from the National Security and Intelligence Services. Speaking about general trends of foreign policy planning Lasha Darsalia updated the attending audience on Georgia’s Foreign Policy Strategy and Action Plan for 2019-22. He highlighted the importance of inter-agency coordination and involvement of expert communities in the development of the document.
