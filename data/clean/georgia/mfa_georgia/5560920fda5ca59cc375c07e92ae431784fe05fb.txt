10 February 2020 The Georgian Foreign Minister, David Zalkaliani met with the Special Representative of the OSCE Chairperson-in-Office for theSouth Caucasus Rudolf Michalka. The Special Representative of the OSCE Chairperson-in-Office updated the Foreign Minister on the priorities of the Albanian OSCE Chairmanship for 2020. The Georgian side, for its part, expressed its commitment to actively cooperate with the OSCE Chairmanship in implementing the priorities. Zalkaliani expressed the hope that the OSCE Chairperson-in-Office will pay a visit to Georgia in the near future. Discussions also focused on issues relating to Georgia-OSCE cooperation. The Georgian Foreign Minister provided Rudolf Michalka with detailed information on the difficult human rights and humanitarian situation in the occupied territories.
