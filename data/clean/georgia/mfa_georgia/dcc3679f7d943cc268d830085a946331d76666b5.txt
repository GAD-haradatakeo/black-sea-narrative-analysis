13 February 2025 On 13 February 2025, the Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Georgia, Lasha Darsalia, met with a delegation from the Milli Majlis of the Republic of Azerbaijan. During the meeting, the parties positively assessed the strategic partnership between the two countries and emphasized the friendly relations that bind Georgia and the Republic of Azerbaijan. Lasha Darsalia and the visiting delegation reviewed the key aspects of bilateral cooperation, focusing on the unprecedented dynamics of high-level visits between the two countries. Additionally, it was noted that the parliamentary agenda for the previous year had been marked by substantial activity and intensification of relations, with an optimistic outlook for the further expansion of this collaboration. The meeting also covered the current regional situation, with a particular focus on the successful cooperation within the trilateral Georgia-Turkey-Azerbaijan format.
