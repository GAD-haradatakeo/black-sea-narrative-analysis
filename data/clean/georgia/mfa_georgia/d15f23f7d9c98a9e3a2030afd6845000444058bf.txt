25 May 2018 22.05.2018, New York - The UN headquarters hosted the reception to mark the 100th anniversary of the creation of the first Democratic Republic of Georgia. The presentation of the post stamp issued by the United States on the occasion of the 850th anniversary of the birth of Georgia’s iconic 12th century poet, Shota Rustaveli was also held as part of the reception. It is the first case for the UN to issue a post stamp depicting the figure living in the Middle Ages that is yet another recognition of Georgia, its centuries-long history and its contribution to the treasury of world civilization. Also a concert tittled „Georgian Overtones” of Georgian pianist Giorgi Mikadze and ensemble Basiani will be held within the event.
