On 31 January 2024, the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 2206 (2015) concerning South Sudan held a briefing to Member States during which the Coordinator of the Panel of Experts briefed participants on the Panel’s interim report dated 1 December 2023 ( S/2023/922 ). During the briefing, the Coordinator of the Panel of Experts provided an overview of the findings and recommendations in the interim report and highlighted a number of recent developments since its publication. The briefing was attended by Committee members and representatives of regional States and interested Member States. The meeting represented an opportunity for the broader UN membership to exchange views on the work of the Committee and the Panel of Experts, as well as the implementation of relevant Security Council resolutions.
