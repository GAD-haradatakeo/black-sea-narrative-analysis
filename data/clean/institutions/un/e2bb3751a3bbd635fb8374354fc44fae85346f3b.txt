On 31 May 2022, the Security Council Committee pursuant to resolution 751 (1992) concerning Somalia updated its Implementation Assistance Notice (IAN) 2: “Summary of procedural approvals and notification requirements related to the partial lifting of the arms embargo on Somalia”. The full text of the Implementation Assistance Notice is accessible on the Committee’s website at the following URL: www.un.org/securitycouncil/sanctions/751/notices .
