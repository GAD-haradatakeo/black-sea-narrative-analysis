On 6 February 2024, the Security Council Committee pursuant to resolution 2713 (2023) concerning Al-Shabaab issued its Implementation Assistance Notice (IAN) #4: “ Guidance to Member States on the application of the humanitarian exemption established by resolution 2664 (2022) to the asset freeze established under resolution 1844 (2008)” . The full text of Implementation Assistance Notice #4 is accessible on the Committee’s website at the following URL: https://www.un.org/securitycouncil/sanctions/2713 .
