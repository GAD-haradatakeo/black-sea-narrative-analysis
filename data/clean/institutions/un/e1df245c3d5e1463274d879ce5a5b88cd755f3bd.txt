On 14 February 2022, members of the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 2206 (2015) concerning South Sudan were briefed by the Coordinator of the Panel of Experts on South Sudan in connection with the Panel’s workplan for mandate in pursuance of Security Council resolution 2577 (2021) of 28 May 2021. Members of the Committee welcomed the Panel’s appointment on 20 January 2022 ( S/2022/42 ) and the Coordinator’s briefing, and looked forward to receiving the Panel’s final report and monthly updates, in accordance with resolution 2577 (2021).
