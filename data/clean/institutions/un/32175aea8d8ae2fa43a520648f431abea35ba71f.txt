On 24 June 2022, the Coordinator and members of the Panel of Experts briefed the Security Council Committee established pursuant to resolution 2127 (2013) concerning the Central African Republic on the Panel's final report submitted pursuant to resolution 2588 (2021). During the briefing, the Coordinator provided an overview of the report's key findings and recommendations with the focus on the political process, international humanitarian law and human rights, natural resources and arms embargo. Members of the Committee thanked the Panel for its briefing and emphasized the need for further reporting from the Panel on the situation in the country in accordance with its mandate.
