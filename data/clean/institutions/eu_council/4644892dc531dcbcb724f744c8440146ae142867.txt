General Secretariat Press release 4 March 2022 17:00 Forward look: 7-20 March 2022 Overview of the main topics and events at the Council of EU and European Council for the coming fortnight Informal European Council, 10-11 March EU leaders will meet in France to discuss the European Growth and Investment Model for 2030 Employment, Social Policy, Health and Consumer Affairs Council, 14 March 2022 Ministers of employment and social affairs aim to reach a general approach on a proposed directive to improve the gender balance on listed company boards Economic and Financial Affairs Council, 15 March 2022 Economic and finance ministers will take stock of the economic and financial aspects of the sanctions imposed following Russia's aggression in Ukraine. The Council may discuss possible additional sanctions Environment Council, 17 March 2022 Environment ministers will aim to reach a general approach on new rules for batteries and waste batteries. They will hold an exchange of views on the “Fit for 55” package and on a proposal to reduce deforestation and forest degradation Indicative calendar of meetings under the French presidency (1 January - 30 June 2022) French presidency website Press contacts Press office +32 2 281 63 19 If you are not a journalist, please send your request to the public information service .
