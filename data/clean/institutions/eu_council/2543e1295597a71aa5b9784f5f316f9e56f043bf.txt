Eurogroup Media advisory 14 January 2022 17:25 Media advisory – Eurogroup meeting and Economic and Financial Affairs Council of 17 and 18 January 2022 Indicative programme All times are approximate and subject to change Eurogroup meeting - Monday 17 January Chaired by Mr Paschal Donohoe, President of Eurogroup 13.45 Arrivals ( live streaming ) +/- 14.50 Joint doorstep by Paschal Donohoe, President of the Eurogroup and Bruno Le Maire, French Minister for Economic Affairs, Finance and Recovery and Chair of the ECOFIN. 15.00 Eurogroup (roundtable) Economic adjustment and resilience - recent euro area performance compared to international peers Thematic discussion on corporate vulnerability and structural developments post Covid-19: challenges and policy responses 2022 euro area recommendation Economic governance review – euro area aspects Miscellaneous 18.00 Eurogroup in inclusive format (roundtable) Reporting from the December Euro Summit Banking union - state of play including an update on ESM ratification +/- 19.15 Press conference with Eurogroup President Paschal Donohoe, Commissioner Paolo Gentiloni and ESM Managing Director Klaus Regling ( live streaming ). Videos and photos from the event Economic and Financial Affairs Council - Tuesday 18 January Chaired by Bruno Le Maire, French Minister for Economic Affairs, Finance and Recovery 08.30 Arrivals ( live streaming ) +/- 08.50 Doorstep by Bruno Le Maire, French Minister for Economic Affairs, Finance and Recovery 09.00 Breakfast (roundtable) 10.00 Beginning of the Economic and Financial Affairs Council Adoption of the agenda Approval of non-legislative "A" items +/- 10.10 Start of public session Priorities of the French Presidency (+/- 10.10 public session ) Directive on ensuring a global minimum level of taxation for multinational groups in the Union Any other business: Current financial services legislative proposals +/- 11.30 Non-legislative activities Economic recovery State of play regarding the implementation of the Recovery and Resilience Facility (RRF) European Semester in 2022 Preparations for the meeting of G20 finance ministers and central bank governors in February 2022: G20 EU terms of reference Any other business: G7 Presidency priorities, EIB investment report At the end of the meeting (+/- 13.30) - Press conference with French Minister Bruno Le Maire and Commissioner Dombrovskis ( live streaming ). Videos and photos from the event Arrangements for the press conferences Both press conferences will take place in a hybrid format: EU accredited journalists will be able to participate and ask questions either remotely or in person at the Europa building press room .
