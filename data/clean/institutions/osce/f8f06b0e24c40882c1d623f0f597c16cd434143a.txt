The Co-Chairs of the Geneva International Discussions (GID) welcome the holding of the 56th round of the GID in Geneva on 5 October 2022. As mandated by the Six-point Agreement of 12 August 2008 and the implementing measures of 8 September 2008, the GID process continues to serve as the only platform where the consequences of conflict are addressed and other important issues are discussed. This round was the first to be held since December 2021. The channels of communication between the Co-Chairs and the respective participants remained operational during this period. The Co-Chairs reiterated their appreciation of the participants’ commitment to the GID format.
