This attack on Ukraine puts the lives of millions of people at grave risk and is a gross breach of international law and Russia’s commitments. We call for the immediate cessation of all military activities.” Contacts Communication and Media Relations Section OSCE Secretariat press@osce.org Bartosz Kempiński Press and Communication Polish OSCE 2022 Chairmanship Phone: +43 660 207 4782 bartosz.kempinski@msz.gov.pl Subscribe to OSCE Sign up to receive OSCE Press Releases and Media Advisories by email as soon as they have been published.
