During his first visit to Moldova in his capacity as the OSCE Chairman-in-Office, Rau will focus on advancing the Transdniestrian Settlement Process within the principles endorsed by all 57 participating States at the OSCE Ministerial Council in Stockholm in December 2021. OSCE Chairman Rau will hold talks in Chisinau with President Maia Sandu, Prime Minister Natalia Gavrilita, Deputy Prime Minister, Minister of Foreign Affairs and European Integration of the Republic of Moldova, Nicolae Popescu, and Deputy Prime Minister for Reintegration Oleg Serebrian. For more information about Poland's 2022 OSCE Chairpersonship, please visit the OSCE website: https://www.osce.org/chairmanship .
